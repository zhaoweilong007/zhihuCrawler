 <h2>千万别犯写邮件的大忌。</h2>
 <p data-pid="p2YNV41Z">（有时候自己凉了都不知道怎么凉的）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="X1t-aOzV">想必不少中国学生在腐国读书时遇到过email-communication的经历。</p>
 <p data-pid="WWomQvIB">作为一个non-native speaker，因为不熟悉email-communication的各种规范禁忌，</p>
 <p data-pid="gKzmRXur">在和申请学校联系、和教授套磁、向外教要邀请信或者申请internship时，</p>
 <p data-pid="lrmvq8MG"><b>真 · 凉了都不知道怎么凉的。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MzmLiYzX"><b>那么在腐国（or any other country）进行email-communication时千万不要范哪些禁忌呢？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="4UDkhOzA">When writing a formal E-mail······</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="6NfoQwdj">
  <b>• 千万不要用不正式的邮箱发邮件！</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="jtP5KdZI"><b>第一印象相当重要！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LuQ-8W6f">请避免使用任何狂拽酷炫杀马特昵称的邮箱地址······比如你用了wildandcrazygeek@gmail.com给教授或者实习公司发邮件，那你有很大可能 凉了（嗯）</p>
 <p data-pid="Cat_BQKO">建议用<b><i>学校邮箱</i></b>发邮件，但请确保学邮确实能收到对方的回执！（有些中国大学的学邮存在收不到国外邮件或者发不出去的现象，一个名字较为正式的私人邮箱地址可以作为备用邮箱）</p>
 <p data-pid="k-GNbSBm">如果你给申请的公司或套磁的教授写信，学邮往往能让对方快速了解你来自哪个大学，从而形成一个较好的第一印象。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="uMm3wc-T">
  • <b>一定要在邮件主题中简要地写明来意！千万不要在邮件主题中简单地丢下一个“Help”或者“Hello”</b> <b>或者“Question”</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="yggMFBom">请简要申述邮件内容，如给导师写信问问题：“Query about case study in Clinical Skills lecture.” （如果要询问某一节课的问题，最好把该节课的具体时间附上）</p>
 <p data-pid="bW6ydATd">如在申请工作或实习时，写明你要应聘的岗位: “Application for Library Assistant post ref: LIB/6291.”</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pYOGiRG_">
  • <b>要先打招呼！</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="KCwyYSwx">给比较熟络的导师写信时可以address them by their first name，否则用family name（如Dear Dr. Miles）</p>
 <p data-pid="A3Yi7_Ze">如果不知道收信人姓名，可以使用:</p>
 <p data-pid="zL_iV01Y">1) Dear Sir or Madam</p>
 <p data-pid="rwYhOsPm">2) 对方的职位title：如Dear Sales Team, Dear Admissions Office, Dear Advisory Board…etc. (p.s.如果用To whom it may concern则显得太过正式，也会暴露你对收信人没有做过调查和了解，因此建议各位在写email之前尽可能了解到对方的信息)</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pbaYQkWL">
  • <b>邮件开头怎么写？（最好客气一些！）</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="GZ0FTp5R">在address完对方的title以后，尤其是需要有求于人的，可以在邮件的开头（第一句）加上I hope you are well/ hope this email finds you well/hope this email finds you in good health以示客气，大意为“希望您最近过的不错”，是常用的客套话。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="KkFohb6K">
  • <b>千万不要用net speak/neologism！</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="_WDTwzX9">一切正式writing中都应该避免像YOLO（you only live once）或ROFL（rolling on the floor laughing）等等非正式用语。</p>
 <p data-pid="GLFNT7RK">请确保使用完成的、语法正确的、没有拼写错误的句子。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="_NTVIZO2">
  <b>• 千万不要把邮件写的过长或者附上超大附件！</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="TWYgoUWu">教授、学校的工作人员、公司的HR等等都是大忙人</p>
 <p data-pid="fgIJ3eaH">在委婉地表明来意时不宜过于客套导致邮件冗长让人读得心累</p>
 <p data-pid="izSAk6L2"><b>附件太大下载起来（也许）会很慢</b></p>
 <p data-pid="whXXmps6">可能给追求效率的对方带来麻烦</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="KfqZ6-XO">
  <b>• 如果向对方提出要求时，一定要加上please &amp; thank you</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="vhx7QkPF">礼貌永远是保险栓。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="UU2atjVG">
  <b>• 结尾该怎么写？</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="WLiCiwFq"><b>一、留出互动（邮件往来）的余地</b></p>
 <p data-pid="FW_3g12X">邮件最后可以添加一句Should you have any questions, please do not hesitate to get in touch with me, 或者let me know if you have any issues with (…),保证对方能够对你有充分的了解，也让对方看出你对申请的重视。</p>
 <p data-pid="eRz8iYle">（如果稍微有点着急希望收到对方回信的话，可以适当地加一句I look forward to hearing from you!）</p>
 <p data-pid="hfzfsxg1"><b>二、a PROPER sign off</b></p>
 <p data-pid="JrHqkMcs">- 给导师写信时</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="NiXJ2qUm">关系非常友好的– best wishes</li>
  <li data-pid="YffWS7ii">礼尚往来的– kind regards，</li>
  <li data-pid="qWKmMght">礼貌且热心的– best regards，（简单点的可直接使用best或regards）</li>
  <li data-pid="Ys24lHWc">非常专业，职业上较为正式的– yours sincerely, yours faithfully…</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="gXlDvXqT">- 给（不知道对方具体姓名的，Dear Sir or Madam）应聘公司HR写信时- “Yours faithfully,”</p>
 <p data-pid="Zv-yEHqg">- 给（知道对方姓名的，Dear Dr. Smith）应聘公司HR写信时- “Yours sincerely,”</p>
 <p data-pid="zkDDDNEG">（!!! Sign off后面要跟上自己的全名）</p>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="CYji13vU"><b>(以下是比较实用的两个实例！关于如何向申请实习的公司发邮件等场景会慢慢更新～)</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nQWHZ1Sy"><b>--------------------------------------我是分割线--------------------------------------</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="UgKUG6AE">
  <i><b>实例一：如何向教授要邀请信？</b></i>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="VC09aqII">Professor [Name]/ Dear Dr. [Name],</p>
 <p data-pid="EeNNFLkz">(I hope this email finds you well.)</p>
 <p data-pid="UZV63WpE"><b>邮件开头的常用客套话，也可省略</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LdzMZQTz">My name is […]. I took your [Course name] course last semester.</p>
 <p data-pid="5IiTUXmZ"><b>简要介绍自己及与教授的联系</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dGeAUWyr">You may remember me [e.g.coming to your office hours after every test to go over some of my wrong answers/ discuss essay prompts / etc.]</p>
 <p data-pid="Bc474gI1"><b>如果和教授在课下有过一定接触，可以写进来；</b></p>
 <p data-pid="DGV_aWhI">You may not remember me [e.g. because I tend to put my nose to the grindstone and study on my own.]</p>
 <p data-pid="W5gLjsGg"><b>如果和教授从没有课下的接触,</b> <b>可以简要地为自己开脱；</b></p>
 <p data-pid="R-OFPpes">[Course name] was a very challenging subject for me and I was proud of the [Grade, e.g. A-] I received in your class.</p>
 <p data-pid="g4PTuvZK"><b>写出自己本课的成绩（一般会选择成绩较好的课的任课老师写推荐信）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="H03UznoP">I am currently in the process of applying to [University/College/School name] and I am trying to gather a few letters of recommendation.</p>
 <p data-pid="hI2ZmRkq"><b>另起一段，写明来意：准备申请某学校，因此需要邀请信。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="k0YxoVx5">Because I enjoyed [ e.g. your class and teaching style so much/ your research/ etc. ], I decided to start by asking you. Would you be able to write me a strong letter of recommendation for my [University/College/School name] application?</p>
 <p data-pid="_P6ShTN9"><b>如果和教授有过一定接触，可以这么写；</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xWI2A2-Z"><i>Because of the way I study it has been difficult for me to develop a relationship with my professors. I enjoyed [your class and teaching style very much/ your research/ etc.], so I decided to start by asking you. Would it be possible for us to meet and discuss the possibility of you writing a strong letter of recommendation for my [medical school] application? </i></p>
 <p data-pid="0_IbtACF"><b><i>如果和教授从没有接触，可以先提出面谈邀请信的事宜，让对方先了解你；且用语更加委婉）</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="9KXbkAdN">I would be more than happy to meet with you at your convenience. Of course I would also provide my personal statement, CV, and any other material that you would like.</p>
 <p data-pid="xe4xPZhR"><b>另起一段，陈述把自己现有的资料（除了PS、CV外，还可以提供自己做过的research/project/paper的资料）提供给导师，保证对方有内容可写而不是仅凭上课对你的记忆。*有时也有自己先写好推荐信、让教授过目修改的操作（需和教授讨论）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="4vVgxtq-">If you don’t feel comfortable writing me a strong letter of recommendation, please don’t feel obligated to say yes. I completely understand.</p>
 <p data-pid="xJKjLkhc"><b>另起一段，如果和导师有过接触（但还是不太熟），可以参考如上表述；don’t destroy the professor’s negative face</b> <b>给对方留出选择余地。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dhYdwQJn"><i>I realize that writing a letter of recommendation would be a burden on your time and it means a lot to me that you would take the time to read and consider my request. </i></p>
 <p data-pid="I_BiftdB"><b><i>但如果你是课堂上的隐形人，和导师完全没接触，就要把自己的要求写的更加的委婉，给对方留出选择余地的同时表达出迫切和感激。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="UbzvJC-j">Sincerely,</p>
 <p data-pid="OoMVlKXS">[Your Full Name]</p>
 <p data-pid="LmYBuxIL"><b>结尾附上自己的全名（可以加上自己的联系电话、Student Number等有关信息）</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <blockquote data-pid="08kMnHG-">
  <b>实例二：如何和教授套磁？i.e.套磁信该怎么写？</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="qCkbwd9N">Dear Professor XXX,</p>
 <p data-pid="kTIXUU13">(I hope this email finds you well/ I hope this email finds you in good health)</p>
 <p data-pid="iqBoGi3g"><b>同邮件开头的常用客套话，也可省略</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="g8AGzGSm">My name is XXX and I am writing to you as a prospective student in your Bioengineering Ph.D. program at Stanford, commencing in the Fall of 2012. I attended your presentation in Langer Lab Seminar this November and I am particularly interested in your research that is directed towards designing novel approaches in decoupling biochemical and mechanical properties of cell scaffolds using the microribbon-like hydrogels, and mixed co-culture enhanced cartilage matrix production.</p>
 <p data-pid="Zin4UnZm"><b>套磁第一步：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="1cBmCFlC"><b>1)</b> <b>简要阐明自己的身份</b></p>
 <p data-pid="KM5bdkST"><b>2)</b> <b>套磁的动机(a prospective student in your Bioengineering Ph.D. program at Stanford)</b></p>
 <p data-pid="slz9y-y4"><b>3)</b> <b>自己与教授认识的契机(attended your presentation in Langer Lab Seminar this November and I am particularly interested in your research that…）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="DtwBsSgQ">Currently, I am a graduate Materials Science student at the University of XXXX and currently carrying out my master research at the University of XXX, focusing on novel formulations for ultrasound-mediated drug delivery mechanisms in gastrointestinal tract. As a side project of interest, I also brainstormed and designed a research plan of developing a biomimetic scaffold for interstitial cells in GI smooth muscle to study its electrical-signal properties for the purposes of treating obesity and GI disorder. <b><u>I would like to pursue doctoral researches in an area that combines both engineering and biology, and am fascinated by the research that is being conducted in your group.</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="C_yoA1J1"><b>套磁第二步：建立“连系”</b></p>
 <p data-pid="GaxCBB3c"><b>1)</b> <b>简要阐述自己的研究领域、背景</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dMxbCJP_"><b>2)</b> <b>之后的研究方向是如何与目标教授相关的</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Tq3JIXq2">During my undergraduate experience in Oxford and summer internships, I have acquired a wide range of laboratory techniques including scanning electron microscopy, confocal microscopy, rheology, electrospinning and tissue dissection. I seek your guidance on opportunities for research in your group as a new PhD student in the Fall of 2016, and your opinion on how my research interests align with those of your lab. I am attaching my CV and would be grateful for the opportunity to speak with you in the future.</p>
 <p data-pid="SF_dq66D"><b>套磁第三步：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="7qVkzzMt"><b>1)</b> <b>大概阐述自己的技能</b></p>
 <p data-pid="V4NLooAa"><b>2)</b> <b>以及这些技能如何辅佐自己胜任职位</b></p>
 <p data-pid="a3fOSCx1"><b>3)</b> <b>附上CV！！！说再多的话教授就不会看了，顶多扫一眼你的CV，感兴趣了就会联系你，静候佳音即可。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="2LIXoQAB">Thank you for your time and consideration.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cfp1TI_k">Best regards,</p>
 <p data-pid="HGlvoSwf">XXX</p>

 <p>我们先来个大长句总结一下<span><span>，</span></span>那就是——</p>
 <p>因为悠久而曲折的历史<span><span>，</span></span>以及过于重要的地理位置<span><span>，</span></span>造成对国力的认知不明<span><span>，</span></span>所以在<span><span>「</span></span>伟大复兴<span><span>」</span></span>的道路上<span><span>，</span></span>土耳其<strong><span><span>「</span></span>打仗基本没赢过<span><span>、</span></span>装 X 基本没输过<span><span>」</span></span></strong>——然后我们就可以开始正文了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>我记得在陈丹青的<span><span>《</span></span>局部<span><span>》</span></span>里<span><span>，</span></span>他说过一句话<span><span>「</span></span>我搞了一辈子艺术<span><span>，</span></span>但是这个历史观念却没建立起来<span><span>，</span></span>等我前几年亲自去了一趟伊斯坦布尔的时候<span><span>，</span></span>我才知道我去的并不是土耳其这一个国家<span><span>，</span></span><strong>它既是古希腊<span><span>、</span></span>又是古罗马<span><span>、</span></span>也是东罗马<span><span>、</span></span>还是奥斯曼<span><span>、</span></span>也是土耳其</strong><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这一点<span><span>，</span></span>其实在我们中国历史的视角下<span><span>，</span></span>是很难感同身受的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>土耳其这个国家<span><span>，</span></span>其实因为三个至今都存在的问题<span><span>，</span></span>而搞得自己非常尴尬<span><span>，</span></span>那就是——<span><span>「</span></span><strong>奥斯曼主义<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>、</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>泛伊斯兰主义<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>、</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>泛突厥主义<span><span>」</span></span></strong><span><span>，</span></span>我们一起来看一看这三个问题的源头<span><span>。</span></span></p>

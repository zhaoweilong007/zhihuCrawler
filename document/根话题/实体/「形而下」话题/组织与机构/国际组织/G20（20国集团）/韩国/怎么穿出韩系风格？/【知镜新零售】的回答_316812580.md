 <blockquote data-pid="skHAUb62">
  <b>穿搭技巧 </b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="IsztR9qX"><b>「 羽绒服穿搭 」</b></p>
 <p data-pid="j_nsYKXp">有人说羽绒服是时尚的灾难，因为它臃肿不堪，难以搭配出好看的造型。日常选择大多数人会选黑色，以为会低调显瘦，其实不然。看看kimehwa给我们的示范，白色也不显得臃肿。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-de44dfd46a16c8e0168014f775b595e3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-de44dfd46a16c8e0168014f775b595e3" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-de44dfd46a16c8e0168014f775b595e3_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ZJhyAuLD">羽绒和卫衣的经典搭配，深蓝色和深绿色让她搭配出新的撞色效果。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-fee3c3e1b7d355404fa7fac4ccecfeec_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-fee3c3e1b7d355404fa7fac4ccecfeec" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-fee3c3e1b7d355404fa7fac4ccecfeec_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="3iHpGbEk">这一套黑色系让她穿得一点也不沉闷，萌萌哒。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-52d68fc4d2736aa42b91a7ed595531bf_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-52d68fc4d2736aa42b91a7ed595531bf" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-52d68fc4d2736aa42b91a7ed595531bf_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

# 印度人为什么总给人一种很奇葩的感觉？
- 点赞数：8278
- 更新时间：2019年12月20日11时12分32秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/26897956/answer/943158918
<body>
 <p data-pid="kecxpvwP">我们看下Quora一则印度网友的回答</p>
 <p data-pid="CzhYmgEn">Quora网友问：</p>
 <p data-pid="Rfc8PSgJ">Why is China afraid of India when China beat India?</p>
 <p data-pid="Bax5k0K-">为什么中国打败了印度，却还害怕印度？</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="py3tAr7T">最高赞的回答来自一位印度网友：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="asRfUm5W">
  1. China is afraid of India because India too is a nuclear state. If China threatens or uses nuclear material against India will respond with equal measure inflicting greater damage.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="xUu7diop">中国害怕印度，因为印度也是一个核国家。如果中国威胁或使用核武器对付印度，印度将以同样的手段反制并造成更大的损害。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="2scSrAfY">
  2. China is afraid of India because geography of position is more advantageous to India. Hence China has been provoking India to start the war, so that they can meet India in open Tibet where China holds advantage. India is unfazed and unperturbed.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Ty2F74VV">中国害怕印度，因为地理位置对印度更有利。因此，中国一直在挑衅印度发动战争，以便他们能够在中国占优势的XZ与印度开战。但印度没有受到影响，也没有动摇。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="CP-lO1dt">
  3. China is afraid of India because Chinese equipments, arms and ammunition are more indigenously made through reverse engineering which are not time tested. However Indian defence equipments, arms and ammunitions are either manufactured in India with western design or purchased from developed nations, which are more reliable and time tested.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="pfigG6cT">中国之所以害怕印度，是因为中国的装备、武器和弹药更多地是通过逆向工程山寨的，而逆向工程没有经过时间检验。然而，印度的国防装备、武器和弹药要么是按西方设计制造的，要么是从发达国家购买的，这些装备、武器和弹药更可靠，也更经得起时间考验。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="7uzEWAa9">
  4. China is afraid of India because it won all the wars against Pakistan, whereas it has only one victory against India in 1962 when India was not prepared. China is also not sure of the full strength of India.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="xJgG26AP">中国之所以害怕印度，是因为印度赢得了所有对巴基斯坦的战争，而1962年，在印度没有准备的情况下，中国对印度只有一次胜利。中国也不确定是否有足够的实力对付印度。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="9T3lldvF">
  5. China is also afraid of India because of its diplomatic reach, it's friendship with powerful nations who will support India either openly by providing arms and ammunitions or moral support, and also because of its powerful military.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ILQ32U7b">中国还担心印度，因为它具有外交影响力，与强大国家的友谊，这些国家将通过提供武器和弹药或道义上的支持公开支持印度</p>

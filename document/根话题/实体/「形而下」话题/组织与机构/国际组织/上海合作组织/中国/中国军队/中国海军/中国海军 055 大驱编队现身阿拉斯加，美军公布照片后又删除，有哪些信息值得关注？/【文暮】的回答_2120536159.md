 <p data-pid="zerFyeTe"><b>第三，这是价值守护</b>。<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2119649386%7D" class="internal">台湾</a>问题，海洋方面的障碍不可回避，我们有信心、有能力解决来自海洋方向的挑战，捍卫核心<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%88%A9%E7%9B%8A&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2119649386%7D" class="internal">利益</a>。我们到时也能够把作战地点选到我们希望的地方。</p>
 <p data-pid="ixMaxFeE"><b>第四，这是</b><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%A4%96%E4%BA%8B%E7%AD%96%E5%BA%94&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2119649386%7D" class="internal">外事策应</a>。其实时间点在两边电话沟通之前。正如<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%9C%9D%E9%B2%9C%E6%88%98%E4%BA%89&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2119649386%7D" class="internal">朝鲜战争</a>谈判是靠打出来的，就像教员说的，美国人是不讲道理的，他和你讲道理，说明他拿你没办法了。我猜测当时的计划是，谈拢了不公布，没谈拢就公布，结果美国自己公布又赶紧删了，可见对外问题统一口径多么重要。</p>
 <p data-pid="4LUj8EWx"><b>第五，这是</b><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%B3%95%E5%BE%8B%E5%AE%9E%E8%B7%B5&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2119649386%7D" class="internal">法律实践</a>。国际法赋予的权利，我们能够专业、文明地予以行使。请美方不要大惊小怪。</p>

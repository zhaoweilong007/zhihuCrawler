 <p data-pid="05qrdYCd">在中国，亚洲人扮演各种各样的角色，亚洲人被表现成正常人。在中国电影中也有许多英俊的亚洲人，比如小战或王艺博等。</p>

 <p data-pid="Gff4i3JR">所以女孩们不会有来自四面八方的成见。但在美国，这是不存在的。你只能看到亚洲男人扮演非常刻板的角色，这会影响人们对亚洲男人的看法。</p>
 <p data-pid="fjJO9Cs4">但欧洲女孩不像美国女孩那样受到这种影响。欧洲人有不同的观点和看法。我认为欧洲人对亚洲男人的看法与美国大不相同，我知道很多欧洲女孩认为亚洲男人很有魅力。</p>
 <p data-pid="-YxEuJRa">我个人认为杭州有很多帅哥。而且他们也非常友好和热情，这对我来说比英俊更重要。</p>
 <p data-pid="7AKUYYnD">I've heard American girls don't like Asian guys that much. I think it's because American movies always show Asian guys as being not very handsome or not very cool so it makes American girls more biased against Asian guys. All the roles for Asian men in Hollywood are really bad. For example</p>
 <p data-pid="70ABC_HX">Foreign exchange students with poor English skills</p>
 <p data-pid="69IeF5zq">Immigrants with poor English skills Foreigners with poor English skills Mystical wise men with poor English skills</p>
 <p data-pid="McVeibsT">Chinese restaurant owners with poor English skills</p>
 <p data-pid="y0eWxSTU">Landlords with poor English skills</p>
 <p data-pid="PO5MsJLt">And if by some miracle the character is written to speak decent English he usually winds up being:</p>
 <p data-pid="o7AweFsK">A geek/nerd</p>
 <p data-pid="H_OkzFDI">A kung fu expert</p>
 <p data-pid="vCYzaKJL">Physically weak</p>
 <p data-pid="6c2W4yUd">Sexually unappealing</p>
 <p data-pid="PWd2UqeD">Effeminate</p>
 <p data-pid="c7gAJUBO">Socially awkward</p>
 <p data-pid="YgqbQVpe">For example Han from Two broke girls. Every time Han gets to say something on 2 Broke Girls, the undercurrent is that it’s funny because of his broken English. Plus he’s really short, has no sex appeal or experience with women, is geeky and generally very uncool. It's the typical representation of Asian guys in American tv.</p>
 <p data-pid="0nqOBCt8">Even my favorite Bruce Lee is not being treated well by Americans. Bruce Lee was a very talented man who still had to work very hard to succeed in America. He had to work much harder than other white actors to be successful. But in recent movie Once upon a time made by Quentin Tarantino he is being depicted as an arrogant asshole who gets beaten up and thrown around by some guy. So Americans have no respect even for such a legend as Bruce Lee.</p>
 <p data-pid="oBQoOQXf">The situation in American movies or tv is incomparable to the situation here in China. Here Asian men get to play all kinds of roles, Asian men are being shown as normal men. Also there are many handsome Asian guys in Chinese movies as Xiao Zhan or Wang Yibo and many others. So girls don't have stereotypical views pushed onto them from all sides. But in America this is non existent. You can only see Asian men in very stereotypical roles, which affects the way people think about Asian men.</p>
 <p data-pid="V5luYcht">But European girls are not affected by this like American girls. European people have different views and opinions. I think the view of Asian men in Europe is much different from America and I know many girls who think Asian men are attractive. And I personally think there are many handsome Chinese guys here in Hangzhou. And they are also very friendly and welcoming, which to me is even more important than being handsome.</p>

 <p data-pid="WTWHqIsf">将原球坐标作一合适的微小坐标变换<b>[注2]</b>: <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cto+x%2B%5Cxi" alt="x\to x+\xi" eeimg="1"> ，可在t&gt;0后观察到运动方程：</p>

 <p data-pid="aDeqC-gy"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidetilde%7B%5CPhi%7D%28r%2C%5Comega_k%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bl%2Cm%7D%5Cleft%5B+A%5E%7B%281%29%7D_%7Blm%7D+h%5E%7B%281%29%7D_%7Bl%7D%28kr%29+%2B+A%5E%7B%282%29%7D_%7Blm%7D+h%5E%7B%282%29%7D_%7Bl%7D%28kr%29+%5Cright%5DY_%7Blm%7D%28%5Ctheta%2C%5Cvarphi%29+%5Ctag%7B3%7D" alt="\widetilde{\Phi}(r,\omega_k)=\sum_{l,m}\left[ A^{(1)}_{lm} h^{(1)}_{l}(kr) + A^{(2)}_{lm} h^{(2)}_{l}(kr) \right]Y_{lm}(\theta,\varphi) \tag{3}" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="VmpBXr0F">显然 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidetilde%7B%5CPhi%7D%28r%2C%5Comega%29%25%3D%5Cwidetilde%7B%5CPhi%7D%28k%2C-%5Comega%29" alt="\widetilde{\Phi}(r,\omega)%=\widetilde{\Phi}(k,-\omega)" eeimg="1"> 是实的并且没有角动量也不会发散，所以只有一对非零系数<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A_%7B00%7D%5E%7B%281%29%7D%3DA_%7B00%7D%5E%7B%282%29%7D%3DA_k%2F2" alt="A_{00}^{(1)}=A_{00}^{(2)}=A_k/2" eeimg="1">, 即剩下零阶的球Bessel函数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cwidetilde%7B%5CPhi%7D%28r%2C%5Comega_k%29%3DA_kj_0%28kr%29" alt="\widetilde{\Phi}(r,\omega_k)=A_kj_0(kr)" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="JSOp3oyw">应当注意上式在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3ER_%5Codot" alt="r>R_\odot" eeimg="1"> 的积分会炸因为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CPhi%28r%3ER_%5Codot%2Ct%3D0%29" alt="\Phi(r>R_\odot,t=0)" eeimg="1"> 只满足零频的波动方程，所以数值上可以取一个足够大的截断，出于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%5Cmathrm%7Bau%7D%5Capprox200R_%5Codot" alt="1\mathrm{au}\approx200R_\odot" eeimg="1"> 的考虑，我们计算中取了 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R_%5CLambda%3D10%5E8R_%5Codot" alt="R_\Lambda=10^8R_\odot" eeimg="1">。 所以(4)式在数值上可以近似为：</p>

 <p data-pid="mpG9ITca">又考虑到匀质球自身的万有引力作用，在半径r处产生压强：</p>
 <p data-pid="8riBx7qC"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+p_%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bgrav%7D%7D%28r%29%3D%5Cint_r%5ER%5Cfrac%7BG%5Cfrac%7B4%5Cpi%7D%7B3%7D%5Crho%5E2+r%5E3%7D%7Br%5E2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cpi%7D%7B3%7DG%5Crho%5E2%28R%5E2-r%5E2%29" alt=" p_{\mathrm{grav}}(r)=\int_r^R\frac{G\frac{4\pi}{3}\rho^2 r^3}{r^2}=\frac{2\pi}{3}G\rho^2(R^2-r^2)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="_UlqaSKv">故从最大横切面处分开该球至少要克服引力:</p>
 <p data-pid="1xOAStRe"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+F_G%3D2%5Cpi%5Cint_0%5ER+p_%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bgrav%7D%7D%28r%29+r%5Cmathrm%7Bdr%7D+%3D%5Cfrac%7BG%5Cpi%5E2%5Crho%5E2+R%5E4%7D%7B3%7D" alt=" F_G=2\pi\int_0^R p_{\mathrm{grav}}(r) r\mathrm{dr} =\frac{G\pi^2\rho^2 R^4}{3}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="50H2MoC-">如果匀质球抗拉临界强度是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_c" alt="S_c" eeimg="1"> , 那么撕裂的要求至少是:</p>
 <p data-pid="VjnxdQ57"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+F_%7B%5Cmathrm%7Btide%7D%7D-F_G%3E%5Cpi+R%5E2+S_c%5C%5C+%5CLongrightarrow+%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B15M_%5Codot%7D%7B4%5Cmathrm%7BAU%5E2%7D%5Ccdot+R_%5Codot%7D+-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi+%5Crho%7D%7B3%7D+%5Cright%29G%5Crho+R%5E2%26%3ES_c+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D" alt="\begin{aligned} F_{\mathrm{tide}}-F_G>\pi R^2 S_c\\ \Longrightarrow \left(\frac{15M_\odot}{4\mathrm{AU^2}\cdot R_\odot} -\frac{\pi \rho}{3} \right)G\rho R^2&amp;>S_c \end{aligned}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="EmnfO91Z">这里可见撕裂的必要条件是左边系数必须大于零，那么存在<b>临界密度</b>：</p>
 <p data-pid="EGi8T40A"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Crho_c%3D%5Cfrac%7B3%5Ctau%7D%7B4%5Cpi+G%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B45M_%5Codot%7D%7B4%5Cpi%5Cmathrm%7BAU%5E2%7D%5Ccdot+R_%5Codot%7D%5Capprox+0.4558%5Cmathrm%7Bkg%2Fm%5E3%7D+%5Ctag%7B6%7D" alt=" \rho_c=\frac{3\tau}{4\pi G}=\frac{45M_\odot}{4\pi\mathrm{AU^2}\cdot R_\odot}\approx 0.4558\mathrm{kg/m^3} \tag{6}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="RpD88xmA">使得球体密度<i><b>小于</b></i>这个密度才有撕裂的可能。现取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho_0%3C%5Crho_c" alt="\rho_0<\rho_c" eeimg="1"> ， 对应撕裂的<b>临界半径</b>是：</p>
 <p data-pid="K3hgjyfL"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+R_c%3D%5Csqrt%7BS_c+G%5E%7B-1%7D+%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B15M_%5Codot%7D%7B4%5Cmathrm%7BAU%5E2%7D%5Ccdot+R_%5Codot%7D+-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi+%5Crho_0%7D%7B3%7D+%5Cright%29%5E%7B-1%7D%5Crho_0%5E%7B-1%7D+%7D+%5Ctag%7B7%7D" alt=" R_c=\sqrt{S_c G^{-1} \left(\frac{15M_\odot}{4\mathrm{AU^2}\cdot R_\odot} -\frac{\pi \rho_0}{3} \right)^{-1}\rho_0^{-1} } \tag{7}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Fd9d1xuq">使得球体半径<i><b>大于</b></i>该半径才有撕裂的可能。</p>
 <p data-pid="9COLZ5BI">可针对某一密度作出相图：</p>

 <p data-pid="xp7idmdZ">-------------------注释比正文重要-----------------</p>
 <p data-pid="khOuM5wO"><b>注1</b>：爱因斯坦场方程 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=G_%7B%5Cmu%5Cnu%7D%3D8%5Cpi+G+T_%7B%5Cmu%5Cnu%7D" alt="G_{\mu\nu}=8\pi G T_{\mu\nu}" eeimg="1"> 的左边是一定无散的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnabla%5E%5Cmu+G_%7B%5Cmu%5Cnu%7D%3D0" alt="\nabla^\mu G_{\mu\nu}=0" eeimg="1"> , 然鹅让太阳消失使得t=0的时候物质流不守恒，等式右边的散度 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnabla%5E%5Cmu+T_%7B%5Cmu%5Cnu%7D%5Cne0" alt="\nabla^\mu T_{\mu\nu}\ne0" eeimg="1"> 甚至还发散， 所以破坏了引力场动力学在t=0的结构。实际上，这个问题类似于在电动力学中让单个电荷突然消失然后问你“怎么解释这个电场纵波辐射/高斯定律”, 因为电荷流守恒是电动力学(麦克斯韦方程组)中的核心， 破坏电荷守恒甚至会在一定程度上破坏电动力学拉氏量里面耦合项的规范不变性 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnabla_%5Cmu%5Cxi%5Ccdot+J%5E%5Cmu%3D%5Cnabla_%5Cmu%28%5Cxi+J%5E%5Cmu%29-%5Cxi%5Cnabla_%5Cmu+J%5E%5Cmu" alt="\nabla_\mu\xi\cdot J^\mu=\nabla_\mu(\xi J^\mu)-\xi\nabla_\mu J^\mu" eeimg="1">。换句话说，对于很多物理方程，Conservation of matter really matters, 守恒律是一个隐含的必要条件。<b>强行破坏这个条件，可以导致解出来不科学的波动模式。</b> 比如本文说了这么长其实是一个<b>不科学的引力纵波</b>。正确的取走太阳的做法应该是沿着地球轨道法向，趁太阳不注意快速地抽离掉太阳，但这样引入的巨大动量便是另一个完全不同问题了（ 或者增加一个额外维将太阳偷走然后只考虑原本3+1维的这个切片）。 无论如何，地球受到的潮汐力都会取决于太阳被顺走的"力度"。</p>

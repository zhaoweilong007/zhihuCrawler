 <p data-pid="-CCV9PHN">维基百科了一下恐怖袭击的定义：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="KJoFP7XZ">
  <b>恐怖活动</b>（英语：terror）是指<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E6%2581%2590%25E6%2580%2596%25E4%25BB%25BD%25E5%25AD%2590" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">恐怖份子</a>制造的危害社会稳定、危及<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E5%25B9%25B3%25E6%25B0%2591" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">平民</a>的生命与财产安全的一切形式的活动，通常表现为针对平民的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/w/index.php%3Ftitle%3D%25E5%25A4%25A7%25E8%25A6%258F%25E6%25A8%25A1%25E5%2582%25B7%25E5%25AE%25B3%26action%3Dedit%26redlink%3D1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">大规模伤害</a>、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E8%25A2%25AD%25E5%2587%25BB" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">袭击</a>公共交通工具和<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E7%25B6%2581%25E6%259E%25B6" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">绑架</a>等形式，与恐怖活动相关的事件通常称为“<b>恐怖事件</b>”、“<b>恐怖袭击</b>”等。是一种较极端的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%25E9%259D%259E%25E5%25AF%25B9%25E7%25A7%25B0%25E4%25BD%259C%25E6%2588%2598" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">非对称作战</a>方式。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="MP6ZlSc1">重点标记【针对平民的大规模伤害】。</p>

# 哪些句子拯救了你的英文邮件？
- 点赞数：23169
- 更新时间：2022年01月17日09时08分52秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/34147404/answer/140038805
<body>
 <p data-pid="aAYAdGH2">（2022年1月17日更新）</p>
 <p data-pid="mSrEtw1Z">作为一名天天和老外同事打交道的美资公司工程师，这些年写过的英文邮件没有一万封也有五千封了，看过的邮件没有十万也五万封了，看了一些回答特别是某位自称外企人的，我只想呵呵，和书上写的好像哦，你试试这样写看看，看看别人什么反应。我来说说实际美国人是怎么写邮件的。</p>
 <p data-pid="iFPYGrll" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1tBe4CG_"><b>我举的例子都是美国人写的邮件原文，比那些书上写的要清新脱俗得多。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0AAU7MnA">先来一封我们CEO最近发的邮件，全篇没有装逼词，但是就是显得很有水平。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="LWmeS-R8">
  <b>Happy New Year—I hope you and your family had a great holiday and enjoyed some well-deserved time off. Thank you again for a terrific effort and a strong result last year!</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6P9toUoA">再来一封老美写的开发客户的邮件，我以前兼职做过外贸，有一次打电话给美国客户，因为英语不好所以别人以为我是采购商，反而发一封邮件给我和我做生意o(╯□╰)o。这邮件也是写得清新自然，没有书上写的套话。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="y_cFymDC">
  <b>Hello Steven,</b> <b>It was my pleasure to talk with you earlier today – thank you for contacting XXXX.</b> <b>I am writing to share more information with you about XXXX and our digital signage systems. The attached corporate brochure will provide you with an overview of our most popular system features – you can also learn more by visiting our website at <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.janusdisplays.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">X</a>XXX.com. As you will see, XXX has been providing industry-leading digital signage systems for the past 25 years and we are honored to have our systems in place with over 2,500 hotels, resorts and casinos around the world. </b> <b>We would be very interested in exploring a relationship with XXXX in order to explore digital signage opportunities in China. As I’ve mentioned, our primary customer focus is with hotels, resorts and casinos. Our digital display systems are designed to meet the needs of the hospitality market. We are fortunate to have earned preferred-vendor status with many of the leading hotel brands including: Hyatt, Hilton, Sheraton, Westin, Marriott, Four Seasons, Ritz Carlton, Renaissance and many more. Please let me know if you have relationships with any hotels and we can develop a plan to pursue the opportunity together. </b> <b>Please review our information at your earliest convenience and let me know if you have any questions. I look forward to our next communication.</b> <b>Regards,</b> <b>xxxx</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="d1P-Bwow"><b><u>一、基本邮件礼仪(Email etiquette)</u></b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cS4Mhdep">英文邮件的礼仪成千上万条，大多其实都已经超出了“英文”的范畴，而是不管什么语言都要注意的，那我就不说了，我只说和印象最深刻的，而且和中文邮件有明显区别的，那就是感谢和道歉。</p>
 <p data-pid="-LNbAn4o"><b>1）感谢</b></p>
 <p data-pid="H32v57AO">中文邮件不喜欢太多客套话，废话少说，直入正题，但是美国人写邮件，哪怕是和熟悉的人，回邮件第一句话，都要感谢，所以他们的邮件都是 thank来thank去的，中文邮件没这种习惯。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OclSvhz5">开头，感谢别人回复自己的邮件（也可以用thank you，用在稍微正式场合，比如和陌生人发邮件）</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="J9O-ofLH"><b>Thanks for the quick reply.</b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="Oj2UbiEo"><b>Thanks for getting back to me.</b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="0v5mwKQ4"><b>Thanks for the update on the situation.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="V-U5MZC3"><b>Thanks for the updated information.</b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="efB6hJS9"><b>Thanks for gathering the information this week on this issue.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mm1yTek_">别人发邮件提供信息给你，中文邮件是懒得回的，英文邮件也习惯感谢，简单回复一个。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="h3ZvYyGD"><b>Thank you.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="parcqSvX"><b>Well noted. </b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="aXHLY3fQ"><b>Noted/Received with thanks.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="BUlF38l_"><b>Good information.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FXLpieE2"><b>2）道歉</b></p>
 <p data-pid="adS6OqMn">如果是回邮件回得晚了，先道歉，貌似中文很少有这个习惯(以下sorry也可以替换成apologize，更正式一点，发给重要的人或者群发）</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="wjqs_--1"><b>Sorry for the late reply.（中国人普遍这这一种，也有老外用，不过下面两种更多）</b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="UpSNE5OU"><b>Sorry I haven’t got back to you sooner . </b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="eFe199__"><b>Sorry for the delay getting back to you.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6VKZmST3">如果是临时通知别人是什么事情</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="17NhYHG2"><b>Apologies for the late/short notice.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="W3GjocmZ">如果是上一封邮件没写清楚导致收件人误解了</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="rLg1kAbd"><b>I apologize if this was not made clear </b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VNDkIK7h">提前给别人预警如果自己的措辞过于强硬。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="dxzZgnSY"><b>I apologize if this may make you feel uncomfortable/bad.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="dYPOTP4t"><b>I apologize if this may sound a little harsh.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oBOCCS_g"><b>3）收尾套路</b></p>
 <p data-pid="axRELWLc">最后收尾的套路，一般都是问别人意下如何。(不要用please tell me ...这种句式，从来没见过老外用的）</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="6m4FuS6S"><b>Please let me know what you think.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="3T9KVOoa"><b>Please let me know your thoughts.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="e9oblmu2"><b>Let me know if you have any questions or concerns. (最常见）</b><br></li>
  <li data-pid="tgMbU33x"><b>Please let me know if there are questions.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="6BT_7iGB"><b>Looking forward to your input/insight(用insight/input代替opinion)</b></li>
  <li data-pid="f1wtlCBk"><b>That’s my idea, what's your idea?(也有直接这样说的）</b></li>
  <li data-pid="1eMsDu6w"><b>Please review the lengthy e-mail and provide feedback on how we can proceed.(如果邮件太长了）</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="A6eQNB22">或者，干脆下面的话结尾，</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="eXkVANzb">
  <b>I'll let you know more tomorrow(表示今天没说完，明天再说更多)</b>
  <br><b>Please call me if you like.(有什么事打我电话）</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8sSDuLQH"><b>4）称呼</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zRxBcalE">普通工作邮件，直接称呼名字字就好了，比如Hi Steven，不需要用Mr.， 甚至写给总裁的邮件，也是直接说Hi Bill，不需要Hi President Johnson。<b>否则真的真的太令人不自在了。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="khmpAYZp">只有德国人是个例外，喜欢加Mr./Ms. 我以前在德资企业工作，发给女性的邮件居然要称呼Ms. Zhang。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="I1NsTqSL">学术场合才需要加抬头，比如Prof. Johnson. 政客的Congressman Johnson.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7UvaQzC2">记住，一般不加Dear，只有公司HR群发邮件才说Dear all, 发给个人的，一般就Hi Steven或者直接说Steven,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yhn4n2Xi">抓住这几点就够了，其他礼仪说多了都是正确的废话。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7MMicYsZ">另外再看看我这篇文章，弄清楚什么时候该用正式用语，什么时候可以用非正式。</p>
 <h2><a href="https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/24030595" class="internal">英语 | 到底什么是正式场合和非正式场合</a></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3vDbxbmX"><b>5）告别邮件主题</b></p>
 <p data-pid="puhFeT5V">离职时发邮件感谢同事，我见过老外用以下两种</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="8Ep9P2vG"><b>Thank you</b></li>
  <li data-pid="p1E_Ye71"><b>Farewell</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="6WJWNn3v">两种都可以。注意farewell并非永别，也可以表示<b>以后很难见面</b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="a51dC1h4"><b><u>二、邮件基本术语</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="_NgXGidh">很多国人凭自己的想象力在创造一些表达，以下才是地道的用语。</p>
 <p data-pid="MzbS1XSu"><b>1）抄送别人怎么说？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="C0GbAp81">中国人喜欢说cc，美国人直接说copy.</p>
 <p data-pid="u0zWNkY5">cc是 carbon copy（复写纸）的简称，以前还没有电子邮件时候，用复写纸抄送，所以本质上还是copy.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="CR8fuMsQ"><b>I have put John on copy. (记住put on copy的固定搭配）</b></li>
  <li data-pid="7MyU1eRY"><b>You can copy Steven going forward.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zQdBA8Q9">如果是分别抄送给某些人，但是又不想让他们互相知道对方的联系方式，可以用暗送(BCC也就是blind cc),比如同时发给几个供应商，邮件开头直接写</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="IKgmGLkE">
  <b>BCC vendors</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JioZ6lPL"><b>2）群发邮件增加联系人怎么说？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="AzrqGhJ_">我看到台湾香港人大陆人都是用add xxx in the loop.从来没见过美国人这样说的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jGzbidYd">一般都是在邮件开头，无称呼，直接说</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="_LJ96bUn"><b>Looping in Steven.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="QFcNvY3i"><b>Adding/Added Steven.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OIvDDPbt">甚至直接用 “+”都可以</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="WEKBSYOF"><b>+ Steven</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="y7qBDELh">把某个人删除（涉及到保密的信息）,用remove</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="BJdE9NMA"><b>Removed Mike from this email</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gQmhGiX1"><b>3)转发邮件怎么说？</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="76Q5wif_">
  <b>forward the email to someone(大部分中国人只会用这种)</b>
  <br><b>pass me the email(老外也经常用这种)</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gQ_0dgUG"><b>4)回复邮件怎么说？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="uqk0y2JZ">get back比 reply常用</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Y5dt08uV">
  <b>I will get back to you tomorrow.</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="13DNRNZy"><b>5）附件怎么说？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="l1ykNtDy">附件的英文是attachment.但是如果你说please find the attachment实在是恶俗至极。</p>
 <p data-pid="O_mZDATt">一般直接用动词attached, enclosed. 我比较喜欢用attached.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="D0qTwmdY">
  <b>Attached/Enclosed please find the report.</b>
  <br><b>I attached the report and let me know what you think.</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hN7sxYV-"><b>6)文件怎么说?</b></p>
 <p data-pid="23gLD3jk">excel, pdf, word 如果你说file也是恶俗至极，正确的说法是document.（excel可以说spreadsheet, ppt可以说presentation)</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="anyjOurK">
  <b>Attached please find the document.</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Iuj0jl33"><b><u>三、和陌生人发邮件如何自我介绍？</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="FJ0pr92Q">不要说 This is xxx和I am这种句式，开头也不要太多客套话，老外开头都是直接说My name is xxx</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="a85bwhk8">
  <b>Hi Mike,</b>
  <br><b>My name is Steven Gates. I work in the R&amp;D department and am in charge of ...</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="xSfBj3Ml"><b><u>四、群发邮件如何称呼？</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="ePpkYtUK">书上百分之一万告诉你用Dear all, Hi all这种，实际，只有群发给整个公司的邮件，比如公司新闻之类的才用这种称呼，其他时候用下面的，</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="fS509zcf"><b>Team, (最常用）</b></li>
  <li data-pid="zUdlukYZ"><b>Gents, (如果全部是男的）</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1EFEsNt-">全部都是女的，我还真不知道怎么称呼，我也没在女人堆里待过。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3y37Jjk6"><b>五、邮件里插话怎么说？</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fFglSvzP">本来你只是在cc列表里作为旁观者，但是你也想进来插一句话怎么说？用chime in</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="LQEo4y6_">
  <b>Just to chime in, xxxx</b>
  <br><b>I'd like to chime in with my idea, xxxx</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="XewQ8wKw">如果邀请别人发表意见怎么说，用 please chime in here with your thoughts/comments/idea.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="tr-hhEDJ">
  <b>Steven, please chime in here with correct information.</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FPmtPfoq"><b><u>六、邮件如何更加礼貌？</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="FQ3sItDW">参考史蒂芬这个3000赞的回答</p>
 <p data-pid="ysonmA0s"><b><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/30964795/answer/137634710" class="internal">在日常英语对话中有哪些细节中国人不会注意，但是外国人却觉得很重要，并可能认为中国人很无礼？ - 史蒂芬的回答 - 知乎</a></b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vhWNsCf7">另外补充</p>
 <p data-pid="jDKDVZFi">1) 问别人一个问题时，前面加个wondering表示想知道，比直接问一个问题更加委婉。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="-qeDCENg"><b>I was wondering </b>what your thoughts are changing this design.</li>
  <li data-pid="aWiGJ1g0"><b>I am wondering</b> if you are using the same material.</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_DTEko9N">2) 请求别人做一件事，用<b>If you could xxx, that would be great.</b> 更加礼貌</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FehzWZiH">3) 请别人帮忙做一件事时，在前面加<b>(by) any chance</b>，意思是如果有时间/有机会，很委婉。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="Zr89lo1s"><b>By any chance</b>, could you help check the data? 有时间可不可以帮忙看看数据？</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="KvYTg8lX"><b><u>六、起承转合常用词</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="P0FNpoUM">我就不说However, Nevertheless, on one hand, on the other hand, firstly这种老师教的恶俗至极的老八股了。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qtib5gnD"><b>1）lastly/finally</b></p>
 <p data-pid="tTw9HR2E">很少看到邮件用说firstly的，一般有话就直说, 但是lastly很常见，在说完前面一大堆之后，说最后一件事的时候，说lastly,finally表示最后你想说什么</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="yCtDaGx9"><b>Lastly, could you confirm that there is no additional change? 最后一件事，你可不可以确认一下不会再改了？</b></li>
  <li data-pid="e_Dv5R2Z"><b>Finally, the most important thing is ...(不知道怎么翻译这个finally)</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qMw-L-KN"><b>2）as/on a side note </b></p>
 <p data-pid="UH3ng60w">原意是边注，引申为补充说明某件事情，特别是你想提醒别人做某件事，这样更加委婉，可以翻译成“友情提示”。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="qAdTegfF"><b>Also, on a side note, can you xxxx?</b></li>
  <li data-pid="qZ_CZJhp"><b>As a side note, I'm beginning to think our current measurement method doesn't seem to be accurate enough .</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="UmC-tcrt">类似的还有 on a related point（相关说明）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="e1lJrPZZ"><b>3) specifically </b></p>
 <p data-pid="WuYssOEO">特别说明一件事时，用这个词开头，另写一个自然段。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="y2Iw8Ci0"><b>Specifically , we are interested in xxxx.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="kb2b7RYP"><b>Specifically , we'd like to xxxx.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="EYsYyLcn"><b>4) all said/ with all that said</b></p>
 <p data-pid="gfYXgoTH">中文意思就是“说了那么多”，用于总结。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="MQxFxtLr"><b>All said, I think we are in an enviable position going into the 2017 sales season with xxxx. 说了那么多，我想我们处在一个令人羡慕的市场地位。</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pM6XpE3T"><b>5) that being said</b></p>
 <p data-pid="IzhD-XU7">中文意思是“话虽如此',</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="HSCsJNV7"><b>That being said, we still need to ...(话虽如此，我们还是需要...)</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cRZ2T90q"><b>6) on another front</b></p>
 <p data-pid="uZ-Nm3GP">代替on another side</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="p9MzdsWC"><b><u>七、邮件里常用的词汇</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="wQDkoxzS">待我逐一讲解。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8D40dMtZ"><b>1) highlight</b></p>
 <p data-pid="H0KhJVg5">这真是个妙词，本意是高亮显示，也就是给字体加点背景颜色，引申为强调以引起注意。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1_-8f376">以下都叫highlight，前者是真正的highlight，后者虽然是画圈，但是也是框起来引起人注意，也叫highlight. 下面区域就叫highlighted area.</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6bfad9c740b00fbdca561ddb36efce9b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="420" data-rawheight="191" data-original-token="v2-6bfad9c740b00fbdca561ddb36efce9b" class="content_image" width="420">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bSNCbFuK">所以，任何事情只要你想强调，以引起别人注意，都可以用highlight.就连足球比赛精彩回顾也叫highlight.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="MAnYwusP">I have to <b>highlight</b> the issue that the deadline is close whereas up to date we haven't come up with a solution<br></li>
  <li data-pid="TwV0Gtsc">One of my concerns is that the factory is not <b>highlighting</b> the challenges in production.</li>
  <li data-pid="_zv59S5h">Can you provide some pictures and videos <b>highlighting </b>the xxx area of the product?(就是要你特写拍一下某个区域)</li>
  <li data-pid="49d4vntQ">I would like you to put a report together highlighting what caused the issue and how you expect to correct the issue.</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mXxsoWM3"><b>2) issue</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3eYMlwUx">邮件里很少说problem,一般都用issue代替，更加正式，表示值得注意的问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="V6P5gKv6">比如品质问题是quality issue.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="iwUHkqEM">例句参照上面。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="W5ooLH2K"><b>3) bullet points</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="SbbZH3pQ">现在左边这个黑色的点就是bullet point，这是它的本意，中文叫做项目符号，引申为重点内容。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="MsKEy6DK"><b>Let's discuss all the bullet points tomorrow.</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TkVhx85A"><b>4) callout</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Uj56HZ6t">记住下面这这种有箭头或者线条的标注文字叫做callout，不叫remark, comment, note.</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f9fd62df77f09f00adf6bee999e8cbf5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="259" data-original-token="v2-f9fd62df77f09f00adf6bee999e8cbf5" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="600" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-f9fd62df77f09f00adf6bee999e8cbf5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ObVfjLEo">把call out分开变成动词，就是标注，学工科的都知道工程图很多尺寸公差是需要标注的。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="NN7_JUtC">
  <b>The tolerance/spec wasn't called out.</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8Yn5zLvR"><b>5) with regard to/ regarding</b></p>
 <p data-pid="qDOZAHod">关于....的话题，不要用about</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="2kZIkcha">
  I'll have a business trip regarding the quality issue.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="qlk3hJJM"><b>6) echo</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ImbQ_DLT">本意是“回声”，也就是唱KTV或者打电话经常出现的回声，引申为“附和，邮件经常用这个词来表示“我只是重复一下某人观点”，谦虚的说法</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="J4zLEaHp">
  To echo John's <b>direction</b>, our plan is xxxx（John的邮件在前面，如果在John的邮件上再回复就可以这样说。
  <br>
  Just to confirm and <b>echo</b> John, please xxxx.
  <br>
  I can only <b>echo</b> David's email. I also think xxx.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="JBAqmpsy"><b>7) address</b></p>
 <p data-pid="m72KKzO-">不要只知道是" 地址“的意思，邮件和日常工作中经常用这个词表达”考虑、讨论（以尝试解决）“，意思介于think/talk about和solve之间，并不承诺一定能解决，只是表示"需要引起注意并解决”，固定搭配address the issue/ problem/question/ concern</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="eeMIA0rP">This issue needs to <b>be addressed. </b>（这个问题需要引起注意并解决）<br></li>
  <li data-pid="mMdu0gJu">The products <b>address </b>the needs of beginners. （这个产品考虑到了新用户的需求）<br></li>
  <li data-pid="xOS69nSF">A question we‘ll need to <b>address</b> is whether we can afford XXXX<br></li>
  <li data-pid="GbkYm0hE">I <b>addressed </b>the quality issue in the meeting(我在会议中讨论了质量问题）</li>
  <li data-pid="TtAlDPwD">All other questions/issues will be addressed by Kevin.(其他问题将由Kevin来讨论）</li>
  <li data-pid="_lhWLhHw">Items to be <b>addressed </b>Wednesday（星期三要讨论的事项）<br></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="X6G2w4nv"><b>8) bring up</b></p>
 <p data-pid="6CvUZOD-">表示把一个问题/话题提出来，这个是最常用的表达，隐含提出来供大家讨论的意思，比mention的意思更丰富。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="zRNPRtDv">I also <b>brought up</b> a<br>
   few concerns with John about XXXX.<br></li>
  <li data-pid="-hBHb4dJ">One thing I'd like to <b>bring up</b> is XXXX<br></li>
  <li data-pid="Zv8XZo0J">We are looking to save some cost on XXX project and one of the ideas <b>brought up</b> by the team was to look at XXXX.</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Y_j1WZet"><b>9 )involve</b></p>
 <p data-pid="H9NVAqeR">把某人(某事）包含到某事来，很难用某个中文单词翻译，意思就是把他牵扯进来（非贬义），比include的语气要弱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="VmNe1AzQ">We would<b> involve </b>members from purchasing team as well.<br></li>
  <li data-pid="mNCEVdfP">When there is delivery issue, please <b>involve</b> the sales team.</li>
  <li data-pid="eeTotYHi">I would like for the root causes and<br>
   corrective actions to <b>involve</b> some engineering controls in the process instead<br>
   of just increased inspections.</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="h8Z3KmSn"><b>10) direction </b></p>
 <p data-pid="dq8yWCs8">字面意思是“方向”，引申义为“指示”，不一定是上级对下级，任何“做某件事的方法”都可以叫direction.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="jlmE8hIa">I will follow David's <b>direction.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="FgoiPGPX">Thanks for your <b>direction.</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pGRJy0v9"><b>11) concern</b></p>
 <p data-pid="NrbOzQd3">字面意思是“担心”，和worry的意思差不多，还隐含“担心而值得注意的事”，邮件中很少用worry这个词，一般都用concern.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="XeJmUzHz">
  quality concern
  <br>
  delivery concern
  <br>
  cost concern
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Clhu2R24"><b>12) input</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ildG-ZoV">本意是「输入」的意思，但是实际工作中，经常做「提供信息/建议」理解。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="8drsVpLT">
  比如你给别人提供建议/信息，他会说Thanks for your <b>input. </b>
  <br>
  比如《纸牌屋》里，幕僚长对副总统说 We
  <br>
  value your <b>input</b>, Mr. Vice President.
  <br>
  比如你想问别人对这件事的看法，你可以说 I'd like your <b>input </b>on it.
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="iel7q9Sn"><b>13) insight </b></p>
 <p data-pid="ySpnoayf">翻译成「洞见」，不接地气，但也找不出更好的表达（或者是「高见」？）</p>
 <p data-pid="10cTozHX">工作中，经常用来拔高别人，可以看做是opinion和input的升级版.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="6JqdWVGu">
  比如 provide some <b>insight</b> for it, 
  <br>
  Look forward to your <b>insight.</b>
  <br>
  That's valuable <b>insight</b>.
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9UIPiHSC"><b>14) dig into</b></p>
 <p data-pid="fRPeWxb6">"深入研究“、”深挖“的意思。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="8XuPfDyE">We need to <b>dig into</b> the root cause.<br></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cQRSwXWf"><b>15) expedite</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Q8sYEyx-">加快，邮件中多用这个词代替accelerate和speed up</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="hk7GdJuF"><b>expedite</b> the order (加快订单）</li>
  <li data-pid="DdU6xMTT"><b>expedite </b>the process (加快流程）</li>
  <li data-pid="gxhNMTQP">We appreciate whatever<br>
   you are able to do in order to help<b> expedite</b> these samples.<br></li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="v1kTnc8M"><b>16）due</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zg2Jo5-L">很多人知道due to，但是不知道 due原来还有“预期”的意思,代替expect，due date是“预计日期”，而不是通常理解的"截止日期“，比如预产期也是due date.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="w4fRGYOP">The parts are <b>due</b> in Shanghai on Friday = The parts are expected to arrive in Shanghai on Friday.</li>
  <li data-pid="U5v3Du8T">Our annual report is <b>due</b> to be shared early next year.(财报明年年初公布）</li>
  <li data-pid="6ujP2Z9B">The samples are <b>due</b> to ship(或者be shipped) on Monday.（样品预计星期一寄出）</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gk4M9f0r"><b>17）part</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wLnNXcGX">制造业用part代替product，没为什么</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ZhDLDDeG">
  The <b>parts </b>were made in January.
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pGBTA7qO"><b>18) timing</b><br>
  时间安排，和time有相同和不同的地方，,很难翻译，用来代替time。看字典里怎么解释的。<br>
  Timing is used to refer to<b> the time at which something happens</b> or is planned to happen, or to the <b>length of time</b> that something takes.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="tALKeNmq">Because the manufacturing<b> timing</b> is too long, we would come up with a better idea(这里和time是一样的意思）<br></li>
  <li data-pid="d93YXcvC">My July visit <b>timing</b> has not yet been decided(我七月份来访的时间还没确定）</li>
  <li data-pid="Kv7SozfK">That's perfect <b>timing</b>.(真是个好时候）</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="UnnsnTmN"><b>19） check in</b><br>
  不要以为只是酒店入住登记的意思，在邮件里经即可表示”获取信息“也可以表示“汇报“<br>
  老外有时候发邮件主题就是check in两个字，邮件的内容就是问一个问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="apuDeIxn">字典上的释义<br>
  to talk with (someone) in order to report or find out new information &lt;I have to go to a meeting now, but I'll check in with you later.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="xo8FliVM">I wanted to <b>check in</b> to ensure xxx. 我发邮件过来只是想了解一下状况，确保xxxx</li>
  <li data-pid="8Xqk7ZmU">David <b>checked in</b> with me on what we can do. 大卫找我问我我们能够做什么。<br></li>
  <li data-pid="aSeI0ESN">I'll <b>check in</b> with our team to confirm expected ship date and let you know. 我要去问一下我们团队。<br></li>
  <li data-pid="aukInOJr">I'll <b>check in</b> with you tomorrow. 我明天再告诉你。</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="F9bPqtoy">口语里也常说I'll check in with you later（我稍后再和你联系）I'm<br>
  just calling to check in（我打电话来也没什么事，了解你的近况而已）。你问别人在吗，别人说what's up？你说没什么事就是I'm<br>
  just checking in.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="a5fWH2Ij"><b>20）coordinate</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HKATXDI6">coordinate虽然有中文「协调」的意思，但日常使用中却并没有协调矛盾和利益那么复杂，因此很多人不会用，也容易误解。<br></p>
 <p data-pid="gAMbaVk7">我印象最深刻的一次是，部门组织了团建活动，结束后老板发了一封邮件，其中有说到Thank you Linda for coordinating all this.</p>
 <p data-pid="XGr87D_6">这个活动是他的助理Linda全程安排的。看到老板用coordinate这个词我还觉得还挺新颖的，换做我只会说arrange或者organize（感谢Linda组织这次活动）。</p>
 <p data-pid="0XLHseXB">其实Linda所做的工作就是打电话订酒店、买票、安排交通，通过邮件和微信通知我们行程，并没有需要协调各方利益，她所起到的作用就是「联络沟通」各个群体，最后让团队活动得以顺利进行。</p>
 <p data-pid="HHWoN2qn">由此可以看出coordinate就是联络沟通以<b>使事情顺利进行。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="FOJQ8WmZ">如果我们只记住「协调」的意思，可能永远不会用coordinate这个词，我们更应该记住是「联络」的意思。</p>
 <p data-pid="VR7HPKLq">project coordinator/ sales coordinator其实就是项目跟单、销售跟单，主要工作是打打电话、发发邮件追踪各方进度，是很初级的职位。</p>
 <p data-pid="JIKxWdoT"><b>例1：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="yMHVRfJ1">可以和酒店联系一下，把入住时间改为星期五吗？</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="WOuuCALq">翻译一：Could you please contact the hotel and change the check-in date to Friday?<br></li>
  <li data-pid="oLi7ITkD">翻译二：Could you please <b>coordinate</b> with the hotel and change the check-in date to Friday?</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5s-MzFiq"><b>例2：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zhxVsyLi">请和实验室联系一下尽快安排测试。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="YkLi2jLd">翻译一：Please contact the lab to start the test asap.</li>
  <li data-pid="-a9Ss8E3">翻译二：Please <b>coordinate</b> with the lab to start the test asap.</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="FRVxVnfR"><b>例3：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0XxvVWuw">可以叫贵公司的美国办事处帮忙提供一下邀请函吗？</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="4H9MJdfJ">翻译一：Could you ask your US office to offfer an invitation letter?</li>
  <li data-pid="VG-vrJED">翻译二：Could you help <b>coordinate</b> with your US office to offfer an invitation letter?</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="vWtnSMvk"><b>例4：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3lL7QVJC">让Linda来处理签证的事。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="TroCuE6j">翻译：Have Linda <b>coordinate</b> the visa thing.</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="kOFcxK1s">这里的coordinate是指让Linda去和领事馆、中介、当事人联络。</p>
 <p data-pid="0fCFX2oC">大家平时可以多用coordinate来代替contact和communicate，甚至 handle（如果是处理联络沟通的事情）。</p>

 <p data-pid="ymB5PmrB"><b><u>八、英语邮件如何显得更加专业，给人留下好的印象。</u></b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="8oQiiBPF">主题必须要“标题党”，必须包含必要信息，让人有打开的欲望。有些台湾人甚至在标题上加-&gt;Steven表示这封邮件是发给谁的。比如<b> XXXX Delay notice -&gt; Steven</b></li>
  <li data-pid="18kNuGVr">一定不要用任何简写，比如c.u(see you)之类，除了pls(please),thx(thanks),当然能不用尽量不用。</li>
  <li data-pid="sPHp0uBR">一定不要用gonna, wanna这种形式，恶俗至极，请变成原形 going to, want to</li>
  <li data-pid="NDTyBjcf">回复别人邮件，要把过往邮件的内容recap一遍，防止别人忘记。</li>
  <li data-pid="OnSjOoog">多用bullet point, table 让邮件更加清晰。</li>
  <li data-pid="itfGhq4Y">多用callout，一目了然。</li>
  <li data-pid="8Ib9Azq6">有附件的话，把附件做一个summary贴在正文。</li>
  <li data-pid="0t91TYoe">附件的命名一定要规范，让人知道里面是什么，也方便别人保存</li>
  <li data-pid="bOi3b6Wi">任何邮件，当天必须回、必须回</li>
 </ul>

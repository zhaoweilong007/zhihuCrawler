# 哪些句子拯救了你的英文邮件？
- 点赞数：6792
- 更新时间：2018年03月09日23时14分12秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/34147404/answer/90867998
<body>
 <p data-pid="5nFGUgOD">之前跟印度人来往过一些邮件，觉得他们的用语看起来非常礼貌，就总结了一些技巧：</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jIfcQqSg"><b>1. 在邮件正文开始前不要上来就问，而是添加一句问候，寒暄一下：</b><br>
  Hope this email finds you well.<br>
  I hope you are well.<br>
  I hope all is well.<br>
  We send you our best wishes.<br>
  Greetings from all of us at XXX (organization)</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9GcTd0LG"><b>2. 寒暄之后，开门见山资讯问题：</b><br>
  I'm writing to inquire about xxx</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="KNc-N433"><b>3. 礼貌的提出咨询问题：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="JCaKwlgx">Would you please do xxx<br>
  Should you be so kind as to do xxx<br>
  Please could you tell me if xxx<br>
  I would like to know xx<br>
  I would be grateful/thankful if you could xxx<br>
  I would appreciate knowing xxx<br>
  I would appreciate information about xxx</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-Vk8VJKs"><b>4. 提出问题后继续表达感谢：</b><br>
  I would appreciate your help.<br></p>
 <p data-pid="2MFhhBbq">---</p>
 <p data-pid="FtwIVTAK"><b>合在一起使用效果：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="apLKyAHG">Dear AAA,</p>
 <p data-pid="0AMireeg">Hope this email finds you well.<br>
  I am writing to inquire about the project xxx.<br>
  Would you please send the latest version of xxx lib to me ?<br>
  I would appreciate your help.</p>
 <p data-pid="xkvw5yqw">Yours sincerely,</p>
 <p data-pid="pq_whN0a">BBB</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

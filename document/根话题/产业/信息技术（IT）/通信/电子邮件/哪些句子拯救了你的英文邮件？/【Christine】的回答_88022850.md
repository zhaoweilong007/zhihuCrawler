# 哪些句子拯救了你的英文邮件？
- 点赞数：47336
- 更新时间：2016年09月02日08时31分20秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/34147404/answer/88022850
<body>
 <p data-pid="egcatbYB">1. 請別人幫忙（發文件等等）</p>
 <p data-pid="4sD3hbq7">At your convenience, would you please send me.....</p>
 <p data-pid="-nn82IsZ">最後加上Thank you for your assistance.</p>
 <p data-pid="d4TFWA_x">2. 尋求意見：</p>
 <p data-pid="tT_458Zm">Our team would appreciate your insights/input on.....</p>
 <p data-pid="YkKXlkl3">Due to the short time frame for this proposal, prompt reply is greatly appreciated.</p>
 <p data-pid="1BBb9HN9">3. 取消會議：</p>
 <p data-pid="b1p8oCc2">I want to be mindful of your time and we don’t have any updates at this point; therefore I suggest that we cancel/reschedule today's meeting. If you disagree or need assistance, please let us know.</p>
 <p data-pid="PLL52zuj">My apologies for the late notice, but i need to reschedule tomorrow's call. There remain a few open items we need to address, therefore i will send out a new invite once we agree upon a time.</p>
 <p data-pid="bdkSqK_h">4. 對方發郵件問問題時，回答完加上：Hope this answers your question. Let me know if further detail/explanation would be helpful.</p>
 <p data-pid="9dOXV0Dl">5. 會議總結：</p>
 <p data-pid="BK1-pR74">Thank you for making time on a very busy Friday afternoon to join our discussion. i hope we were able to provide clarity on......Attached is a summary of what we discussed today.</p>
 <p data-pid="qd53TO6K">6. 道歉：</p>
 <p data-pid="3mS42BPt">My sincere apology for this unfortunate situation; I will address to my team immediately.</p>
 <p data-pid="VKxps1Oe">Again，please accept my most sincere apology.</p>
 <p data-pid="VkKP1ZRf">7. 跟客户分享项目进展以后，建议电话详谈</p>
 <p data-pid="PRtzzNwP">Once these final steps are finished, I would suggest having a brief call to discuss our findings and recommendations for next steps. Would you please provide a few times the week of XXX that work within your schedule and I will send the calendar invite?</p>
 <p data-pid="eX3bWQss">8. 文件打了草稿，请求上司过目</p>
 <p data-pid="g6aL0f0C">Attached is the drafted xx file. It is still a work in process but I would like to see if anything immediately stood out to you as odd or worthy of follow-up.</p>
 <p data-pid="r9DTSJKB">9. 分享2个不错的automatic reply,去旅游或者外地开会的时候可以用:</p>
 <p data-pid="jh7abPbI">Thank you for your e-mail. I will be out of the office on vacation from XXX - XXX with no access to e-mails. I apologize for any delay in response to your e-mail. I will respond to your e-mail as quickly as I can when I return to the office on Monday, April 18th. Thanks!</p>
 <p data-pid="-BzjdKRq">Thank you for your e-mail. I am out of the office in meetings on XXX and XXX with limited access to e-mail. If you need immediate assistance, please contact me via my mobile phone. Thank you!</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="xVPzmtN7">10. 指点下属</p>
 <p data-pid="2vnhKQ7A">Thanks for preparing the XXX. I made the following updates to the question responses. We can discuss my thought process when I am in the office on Monday.</p>
 <p data-pid="rdUdEMVk">XXX, good job! I have reviewed the XXX documents and have the following review comments:</p>
 <p data-pid="TyXt5DOp">1...</p>
 <p data-pid="-1S_YVAC">2....</p>
 <p data-pid="2AIQJGIn">(将做好的文件交给老板检查以后，通常会有Review Comments，就是列出需要改的部分）</p>
 <p data-pid="U6vbexxq">11. 邮件问好 (美国人好喜欢用hope 哈哈）</p>
 <p data-pid="6Ipoyrht">周一/二可用：</p>
 <p data-pid="2asnYPBG">I hope that your week is off to a great start.</p>
 <p data-pid="nrAFpJbj">Hope you had a great weekend.</p>
 <p data-pid="UI5Rl79-">万能的：</p>
 <p data-pid="3a5tFz7R">Hope you’re well/Hope you are doing well.</p>
 <p data-pid="Rs8coAqn">I hope this message finds you well.</p>
 <p data-pid="bIpHw-UT">PS. If you like this answer, thank you; if not, don't. I REALLY don't give a damn of your opinion.</p>

# 哪些句子拯救了你的英文邮件？
- 点赞数：6474
- 更新时间：2015年08月21日09时15分45秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/34147404/answer/58837331
<body>
 <p data-pid="2YcuryOb">再更新一些吧。请原谅可能的单词拼写错误因为outlook有自动纠错功能所以没有检查的习惯 (∩_∩)</p>
 <p data-pid="p8zFm4KC">1/连接词的使用</p>
 <p data-pid="zzEhfsz0">快抛弃BTW吧，在一个邮件里说起另外一件事情，你可以用in addition to/additionally/furthermore</p>
 <p data-pid="rswFl_2t">2/多使用被动语态</p>
 <p data-pid="CvdWfDcD">邮件或者正式的文件里，被动语态会显得更专业。比如需要某样东西，把I need xxx改成xxx is required.是不是看上去专业多了？</p>
 <p data-pid="D105Qygp">还有一个例子可以省掉主语，把句子结构改成It is+动词，比如It is proposed that/It is confirmed that...</p>
 <p data-pid="QugnrA1n">3/多注意native speaker的用词，注意用他们常用的同义词替换你常用的。比如because of 可以用due to替代;比如请别人核实问题的时候不要只会用check, clarify/verify/cross verify都非常好用;比如表示在某件事之前除了before你还可以用prior to;表示在某件事之后除了用after还可以用upon(当然用法有一些区别，举例：Let's talk about this after you come back改成Let's talk about this upon your return)</p>
 <p data-pid="BU7jfp4w">类似的同义词，如果用的时候不确定用法是否有区别，随时查字典即可。</p>
 <p data-pid="EQhBoOqT">4/两个投诉句式</p>
 <p data-pid="xz_Hk2g0">There were some small issues which we probably need your attention to look upon.</p>
 <p data-pid="oOE_ldQz">Due to the discrepancies in xxx issue we need your attention to resolve the problem.</p>
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="Egboazt6">以下是原答案</p>
 <p data-pid="FYKqUxbq">----------------------------------------------------------------------在最近的邮件里随便翻了翻，由于专业限制只能找一些通用的。有赞就继续更。 (∩_∩)</p>
 <p data-pid="2OL4HkTF">用在句首的:</p>
 <p data-pid="UMbJcHRd">带到之前讨论</p>
 <p data-pid="jWJEye9K">Pertain to ...(issue)</p>
 <p data-pid="GKP_f89z">As communicated earlier...</p>
 <p data-pid="VvN_XC7h">回复晚了</p>
 <p data-pid="a-Q08rs6">Sorry for the delayed response...</p>
 <p data-pid="mxdj9s1y">很久没联系</p>
 <p data-pid="YQTN-IJw">Hope everything is well with you.</p>
 <p data-pid="yqtrlfs1">I hope this email finds you doing well.</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="e_lJyhGp">句中:</p>
 <p data-pid="-H9nmoD4">请求对方做某件事</p>
 <p data-pid="EHFkxwV5">Please arrange...accordingly so we can proceed with.../move forward</p>
 <p data-pid="kNvXxynr">Please follow up with...</p>
 <p data-pid="Dfl6bDNt">更委婉的请求</p>
 <p data-pid="bsJKcM6O">If possible could you please...</p>
 <p data-pid="89pVIXeu">I will have to bother you on...</p>
 <p data-pid="PUSCGcdX">和对方确认某件事</p>
 <p data-pid="2BDcxux7">Could you please clarify.../give a clarification on...</p>
 <p data-pid="AIpL_q2_">Kindly please confirm on our proposed...(schedule/plan)</p>
 <p data-pid="0b_yZjt0">询问进展</p>
 <p data-pid="NFyk-t1T">Do you have any updates on...</p>
 <p data-pid="qltMzaMX">Have you got any response from...</p>
 <p data-pid="MtqUEYpJ">通知对方某件事</p>
 <p data-pid="Hz_TjLGc">Please be informed that...</p>
 <p data-pid="7E7fVZWj">Please kindly note that...</p>
 <p data-pid="YtjGSi1w">征询对方意见</p>
 <p data-pid="yg-JCLWo">Please advise.</p>
 <p data-pid="uaql0-y0">Please let me know your thought on this.</p>
 <p data-pid="XoqMBJv-">Please give your comments if any.</p>
 <p data-pid="Vf3e3j75">会议邀请</p>
 <p data-pid="nshN0vKT">You are all invited to attend the meeting on...</p>
 <p data-pid="dLNvSRfs">Please check your calendar and let me know your preference/if there is any conflict.</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="c1dhIKZb">结束：</p>
 <p data-pid="eqZRzj71">...is attached here for your reference.</p>
 <p data-pid="a8GJBxcs">Thanks for your kind support.</p>
 <p data-pid="lYdo6wts">Please let me know should you have any further questions.</p>
 <p data-pid="AnC2lq5K">Your prompt response on this will be highly appreciated.</p>

# 有哪些高质量的自学网站？
- 点赞数：66392
- 更新时间：2019年10月24日09时35分28秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/41476832/answer/91127489
<body>
 <p data-pid="M5POv5zh">一、艺术与音乐</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="sY-vnJSf">
  1、 Dave Conservatoire—一个完全免费的音乐学习网站，口号是“让每一个人都可以接受世界级的音乐教育”，有视频，有练习。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.daveconservatoire.org/" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://www.</span><span class="visible">daveconservatoire.org/</span><span class="invisible"></span></a>
  <br>
  2、 Drawspace—如果你想学习绘画，或者提高自己的绘画技能，就来Drawspace吧。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.drawspace.com/" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://www.</span><span class="visible">drawspace.com/</span><span class="invisible"></span></a>
  <br>
  3、 Justin Guitar —超过800节免费的吉他课程，有自己的app，还有电子书、DVD等实用内容。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.justinguitar.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Justin Guitar</a>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="CNEXygOT">二、数学、数据科学与工程</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="DsnQ-mHn">
  4、 Codecademy —Codecademy 还用多介绍吗？学编程，来Codeacademy就对了。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.codecademy.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">https://www.codecademy.com</a>
  <br>
  5、 Stanford Engineering Everywhere —有很多免费的工程类课程资源。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//see.stanford.edu/" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://</span><span class="visible">see.stanford.edu/</span><span class="invisible"></span></a>
  <br>
  6、 Big Data University—教你如何做大数据分析。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//bigdatauniversity.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Big Data University</a>
  <br>
  7、 Better Explained—从整体图景着手，通过图像向你解释抽象的数学（还有其他领域）概念。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//betterexplained.com/" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://</span><span class="visible">betterexplained.com/</span><span class="invisible"></span></a>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="UUd9WJCx"><b>三、设计、网页设计与开发</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ND5t9dOj">
  9 、 HTML Dog — 学 习 HTML, C SS 和JavaScript编程技能。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//htmldog.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">htmldog.com</a>
  <br>
  10、 Skillcrush — 提供职业网页设计与开发课程。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//skillcrush.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Skillcrush | Learn to code. Land the job. Change your life.</a>
  <br>
  11、 Hack Design— 全球顶级设计师教你网页/App设计，完全免费。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//hackdesign.org/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Design lessons for everyone, curated by top designers</a>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="a71ywBQv">四、综合</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="NG4Sh4aE">
  12、 Scratch - Imagine, Program, Share —为小孩子设计，通过游戏学编程。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//scratch.mit.edu/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Scratch - Imagine, Program, Share</a>
  <br>
  13、 E-learning for kids — 向 5-12 岁的儿童提供小学程度基础课程。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.e-learningforkids.org/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">e-learning for kids</a>
  <br>
  14、 SchoolTube — 面向中小学生，称有5 0 万视频。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.schooltube.com/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">SchoolTube</a>
  <br>
  15、 HippoCampus—主要面向中学生。
  <br><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//www.hippocarnpus.org/" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://www.</span><span class="visible">hippocarnpus.org/</span><span class="invisible"></span></a>
 </blockquote>

 <blockquote data-pid="g3nWOJqA">

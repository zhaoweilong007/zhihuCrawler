# 你在快手上见过哪些罕见的民间手艺？

- 更新时间：2019年04月10日17时53分39秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="o3lEfQFC">李先鹏，快手人称：琉璃哥。山东人，样貌酷似黄家驹。</p>
 <p data-pid="NK2MlruF">18岁开始就苦心钻研琉璃文化（08年的时候琉璃烧制技艺入选为国家级非物质文化遗产），他烧制的琉璃，色彩流云漓彩，品质晶莹剔透、光彩夺目。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1071474606472212480" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="民间手艺：琉璃金牛" data-poster="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-4ac91c0d43abe887827e60d6a2ce56e4.jpg" data-lens-id="1071474606472212480"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-4ac91c0d43abe887827e60d6a2ce56e4.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">民间手艺：琉璃金牛<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1071474606472212480</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="TomnEXob">琉璃哥说：刚开始以为这个技术很简单，制作快，很容易上手。但是经过自己的尝试，发现并不是这样，所谓的简单其实就是熟能生巧罢了。琉璃是在1000多度的高温下烧制而成的，其中辛苦，只能自己体会。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1071469920537292800" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="民间手艺：琉璃白菜" data-poster="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-c4c7520e89491819493fb2ce28677f7c.jpg" data-lens-id="1071469920537292800"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-c4c7520e89491819493fb2ce28677f7c.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">民间手艺：琉璃白菜<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1071469920537292800</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="91qcFfsL">过去，琉璃甚至比玉器还要珍贵。现在，这项技术对于现在的年轻人来讲大多数人都不愿意干，怕辛苦又赚不了钱。</p>
 <p data-pid="MiGgaLOA">但是前辈们留给我们的技艺不能断。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1071475071712874496" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="民间手艺：琉璃盘子" data-poster="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-61acd1b54134daf8c60437dda5ec0af2.png" data-lens-id="1071475071712874496"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-61acd1b54134daf8c60437dda5ec0af2.png"><span class="content"><span class="title">民间手艺：琉璃盘子<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1071475071712874496</span></span></a>

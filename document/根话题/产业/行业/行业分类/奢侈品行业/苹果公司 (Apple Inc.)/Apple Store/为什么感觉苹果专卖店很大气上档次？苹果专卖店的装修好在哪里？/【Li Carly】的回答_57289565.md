 <p data-pid="f5ImSDx2"><u><b>3. 然后看看Apple对员工的培训。</b></u></p>
 <p data-pid="eFGyXwYV">网上曾经有流出过Apple部分培训手册的内容。其中，苹果店的员工们不仅业务知识要过硬，还要了解顾客的心理和学会沟通倾听的技巧，由此提升用户体验，让每位顾客都能满意开心地离开, 他们把这套标准化de服务准则叫做Apple Approach:</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="vderXkKg"><b>APPLE Approach</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="w57_WAZb">Approach customers with a personalized, warm welcome.<br></li>
  <li data-pid="9wTMRLRA">向顾客致以热烈的欢迎。<br></li>
  <li data-pid="AoEgpXVP">Probe politely to understand the customer’s needs (ask closed and open-ended questions).<br></li>
  <li data-pid="lqwxIrZe">礼貌地了解询问客户的需求 。<br></li>
  <li data-pid="r3NOaZbe">Present a solution for the customer to take home today.<br></li>
  <li data-pid="iNM1xWUE">为顾客提供即刻即时的解决方案和产品。<br></li>
  <li data-pid="epgFq5HD">Listen for and resolve any issues or concerns.<br></li>
  <li data-pid="jI5maAdk">倾听并解决任何问题或疑虑。 <br></li>
  <li data-pid="eAzf-iAg">End with a fond farewell and an invitation to return.<br></li>
  <li data-pid="OHNDXJFc">结束与道别，并邀请他们下次再来。<br></li>
 </ul>

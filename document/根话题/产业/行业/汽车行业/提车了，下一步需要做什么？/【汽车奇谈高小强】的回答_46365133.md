 <p><strong>一<span><span>、</span></span>玻璃贴膜</strong></p>

 <p><strong>看完以上文字依然觉得你是需要贴膜的<span><span>，</span></span>务必记住以下两点<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>1<span><span>、</span></span> 前挡膜纯属浪费钱<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>前挡需要的是清晰的视野和高透光度<span><span>，</span></span>但跟手机贴膜一样<span><span>，</span></span>最顶级的膜<span><span>，</span></span>通透效果都不如不贴膜<span><span>！</span></span><span><span>！</span></span><span><span>！</span></span>有人喜欢<strong>用侧挡使用的 50% 透光率<span><span>（</span></span>侧挡膜中几乎最高透光率<span><span>）</span></span>的膜贴到前挡<span><span>，</span></span>夜晚开车等于你大灯亮度降低一半<span><span>！</span></span></strong><span><span>！</span></span><span><span>！</span></span>而高透光度的前挡膜<span><span>（</span></span>透光率 85% 以上<span><span>）</span></span>首先价格非常贵<span><span>（</span></span>零售价 1000 一张以上<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>而且高透光度<span><span>，</span></span>意味着没什么挡光隔热功能<span><span>，</span></span>问题来了<span><span>，</span></span>你花大钱贴了个<span><span>「</span></span>高透光度<span><span>」</span></span>的膜<span><span>，</span></span>又达不到你想要的<span><span>「</span></span>遮光隔热<span><span>」</span></span>效果<span><span>，</span></span>是不是秀逗了<span><span>？</span></span>太阳晃眼就配个太阳镜<span><span>，</span></span>几百块可以买个货真价实的意大利雷朋<span><span>，</span></span>比前挡贴膜帅多了安全多了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>2<span><span>、</span></span> 金属反光膜千万别用<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>很多人觉得照镜子一样的反光膜很帅<span><span>，</span></span>这就是金属反光膜<span><span>，</span></span><strong>金属反光膜最大的问题在于内反光</strong><span><span>，</span></span>晚上开车会发现特别多车内仪表按键灯光的倒影<span><span>，</span></span><strong>非常影响安全</strong><span><span>。</span></span>很多人贴了反光膜以后发现<strong> GPS 很难搜星<span><span>，</span></span>手机信号</strong>变差<span><span>，</span></span>这是因为金属膜的金属屏蔽效应<span><span>。</span></span>所以要外面看不到里面<span><span>，</span></span>就老老实实贴深色膜吧<span><span>，</span></span>反光膜纯属坑爹<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>二<span><span>、</span></span>封釉<span><span>、</span></span>打蜡<span><span>、</span></span>镀膜</strong></p>

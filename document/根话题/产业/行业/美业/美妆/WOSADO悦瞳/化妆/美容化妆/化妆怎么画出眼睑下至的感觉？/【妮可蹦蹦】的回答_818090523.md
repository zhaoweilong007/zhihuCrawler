 <p data-pid="319sqq7F">谢邀。</p>
 <p data-pid="wbaObxwY">前几天在家里捣腾新的妆容，微博发了这个《假装自己只有三岁》的大眼娃娃的造型：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-ee4428663e09fa9540b614285f7c1bc1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="2048" data-rawheight="1534" data-original-token="v2-abcc2f529411ef9b6f83f5bd0c243792" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-124007c33d82c1a1d59ee429270e9e3d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2048" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-ee4428663e09fa9540b614285f7c1bc1_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="2YxaJBR0">好多同学留言说想我出照片里的眼妆教程，哈哈，这个眼妆，就是用到了逆天改命的徒手<b>眼睑下至大法</b>！</p>
 <p data-pid="K6OUzUi-">所谓眼睑下至，就是<b>将原本比较平直的下眼睑从外眼角到内眼角向下拉出一条弧度</b>，营造出狗狗眼或者说是娃娃眼的无辜感：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-ddc6db242e03754e80370fa85da5ff5a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="890" data-rawheight="320" data-original-token="v2-590909cd2aba3d0f44ce867d539fc1ce" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-88f0a6bd269e06719e07e50da19ae768_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="890" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ddc6db242e03754e80370fa85da5ff5a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="uVmfW0Ed">这种眼睛可以说是杀人于无形，就算你前一秒卖了男友的游戏币，后一秒也会让他说爱你的程度。</p>

 <p data-pid="PrrdZI4j"><b>我用到的产品</b></p>
 <p data-pid="lvt9f8nF">眼影：charlotte tilbury pillow talk</p>
 <p data-pid="hytKBMZH">眼线胶笔：addiction眼线笔#灰黑色</p>
 <p data-pid="jUB4q-d0">睫毛膏：艾杜纱睫毛打底膏</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="CnQyw6uJ"><b>step1.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Ve0Yinfn"><b>用一个日常的打底色铺满上眼皮，淡淡一层就好。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dl4898H8">下手太重不仅显得老气还容易让整个眼妆看起有种艳俗感。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-fc6c3bf5f53849212f6a1aa1fb61858c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-fc6c3bf5f53849212f6a1aa1fb61858c" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5pX9gG-H"><b>step2.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="cGKK4om2"><b>下眼影从眼尾向前延长到三分之二的范围，并适当的向下加宽。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="4lnZa3ca">这个步骤的重点在于范围和颜色的过渡，为眼睑下至做好基础打底工作。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c5b6e06b43cdbf68ed832c4bcd675137_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-c5b6e06b43cdbf68ed832c4bcd675137" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jKzMQ9_e"><b>step3.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Zx47Zk47"><b>用精准度比较高的小型的铅笔刷，沾取同色系的深色眼影，沿着眼尾的地方向外延伸。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="KlfOqSyJ">不需要太多晕染，营造出棕色眼线的感觉就ok。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-152d8ec9be3b31fd344de162db0aa705_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-152d8ec9be3b31fd344de162db0aa705" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fBp_OEP_"><b>step4.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LZxaMj6O"><b>加深下眼睑，范围从眼尾到眼球正下方，重点：在眼角处向上晕染，衔接上眼影。</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-27da3a31e5a287b122efa8b0ad2d3583_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-27da3a31e5a287b122efa8b0ad2d3583" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="iNeRUzUV">这样能自然打造出加宽版的下眼睑弧度：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b2bdb2fedde811b969f942080fdfb769_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="264" data-rawheight="238" data-original-token="v2-1ff792c1a20a25bbeffcb8a3392b007e" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3e60f8a3a7147852e61996aed3544aef_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="264">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="q7sVGtU0"><b>step5.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="RkSXmv-R">下眼线是整个妆教的一大重点。</p>
 <p data-pid="MA2gyAyd">选择一款你喜欢的<b>眼线胶笔或者眼线膏</b>，这种软软糯糯，边缘模糊的眼线工具能够更加自然的更改眼型。眼线液笔这种线条太过死板的就不建议用了～</p>
 <p data-pid="DbKC0hkv"><b>在下眼尾画一条短短的眼线，再次强调眼睑下至的弧度：</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3bbec1e71d428921a491be6bb694428c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="576" data-rawheight="524" data-original-token="v2-4a6757240f96a35fccd6959b9c156528" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-7b7ac3fd41a87b553a4795cc3d001ca9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="576" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3bbec1e71d428921a491be6bb694428c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hdxzZkRv"><b>step6.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8g1YoLFU">卧蚕这个东西也需要一些技巧（再次建议看视频！）</p>
 <p data-pid="Cme7WXyP">可以用鼻影或修容色从眼尾向里强调卧蝉线：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e34c9c6e311dbf49520ca5b48f407e84_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-e34c9c6e311dbf49520ca5b48f407e84" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ekc6CE8L">再选择颜色比较接近于肤色（例如香槟色，米黄色）的珠光色，提亮卧蝉：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-5016ffc92b5cdd0f6cb0b54d49bba47a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-5016ffc92b5cdd0f6cb0b54d49bba47a" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5Rxr08Ep"><b>step7.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_gXp1Lvu">最后一步就是睫毛～</p>
 <p data-pid="IjptP4zc">先用日常的睫毛夹将这个睫毛夹一下，不用很精细的操作，夹到自然卷翘的程度就行。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e6ca7c2840d3973ad58f1c50dbf8357f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-e6ca7c2840d3973ad58f1c50dbf8357f" class="content_image" width="320">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="RAvxg0m2">然后再用局部的睫毛夹来加强夹翘后半段睫毛：</p>
 <figure data-size="small">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-68b9fa2ce03167286101e127f1c472d0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="small" data-rawwidth="734" data-rawheight="821" data-original-token="v2-6c4c53f19bb3e67f116e5cc9bfd41929" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-1973c0ae4232f5affced3df985098377_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="734" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-68b9fa2ce03167286101e127f1c472d0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-a0909cb3c7d1fc4de64cfb304b7a6eda_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="320" data-rawheight="180" data-original-token="v2-a0909cb3c7d1fc4de64cfb304b7a6eda" class="content_image" width="320">

 <p data-pid="19uIAuiP">刷睫毛膏也是重点刷后半段的睫毛，有明显弧度变化的睫毛造型可以让大眼更灵动哦～</p>

 <p data-pid="fC5Yqydq">艾杜纱的刷头是我个人非常喜欢的，扁平的梳子型，膏体量控制的很好，<b>易上手又不会刷成苍蝇腿，下睫毛也能轻松照顾到。</b></p>

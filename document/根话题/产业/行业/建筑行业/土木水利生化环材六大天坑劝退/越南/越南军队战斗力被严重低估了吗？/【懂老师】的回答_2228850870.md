 <p><strong>举一个被反复打脸的例子<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>1950 年前后<span><span>，</span></span>中国援助越南<span><span>，</span></span>越不听劝告闷着头往前冲<span><span>，</span></span>几次战败<span><span>，</span></span>都没汲取经验<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>

 <p>可是这个时候<span><span>，</span></span>陈赓刚刚帮他们打了胜仗<span><span>，</span></span>他们就改变了之前的态度<span><span>，</span></span>拒绝了陈赓的意见<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <h2><strong>一<span><span>、</span></span>第一把钥匙<span><span>：</span></span>快速反应公式</strong></h2>
 <p><strong><span><span>（</span></span>一<span><span>）</span></span>首先<span><span>，</span></span>我们要全面了解资料分析涉及的求解对象有哪些</strong></p>

 <p><strong><span><span>（</span></span>二<span><span>）</span></span>其次<span><span>，</span></span>你得知道并理解计算对象对应的公式</strong></p>
 <p>在这里<span><span>，</span></span>一些基础性的公式我就不罗列了<span><span>，</span></span>在我的背公式小工具<span><span>（</span></span>后文会介绍)里都有<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>因为我这篇文章是用来进阶的<span><span>，</span></span>所以对于几个大家容易混淆的公式<span><span>，</span></span>我想重点辨析一下<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>① 两期比重差<span><span>（</span></span>出现频率<span><span>：</span></span>中<span><span>）</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>例<span><span>：</span></span>今年苹果数为 A<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长率为 a<span><span>，</span></span>水果总数为 B<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长率为 b<span><span>。</span></span>求苹果占水果比重比上年上升了几个百分点<span><span>？</span></span></p>

 <p>为什么要调整顺序<span><span>？</span></span>因为这可以大大缩短我们的做题时间<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>在实际做题中<span><span>，</span></span>出题者并非想让我们精确地把公式全部算出来<span><span>，</span></span>所以选项之间的差距往往会设计得恰到好处<span><span>，</span></span>让你可以估算解题<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><span><span>「</span></span>a-b<span><span>」</span></span>可以口算<span><span>；</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>A/B<span><span>」</span></span>不需要算<span><span>，</span></span>大概化成几分之几就可以了<span><span>；</span></span>对于<span><span>「</span></span>1/<span><span>（</span></span>1+a<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>如果 a&lt;10%,那么可以忽略不算<span><span>，</span></span>如果 a&gt;10%<span><span>，</span></span>那么就还需要根据<span><span>「</span></span>1/<span><span>（</span></span>1+a<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>」</span></span>的值调整最后结果<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>以上这几步都可以口算<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <blockquote>
  例题 1<span><span>：</span></span>2016 年山东省公务员录用考试<span><span>《</span></span>行测<span><span>》</span></span>真题第 117 题 
  <br><span><span>【</span></span>材料<span><span>】</span></span>2015 年一季度<span><span>，</span></span>某省省级及以上园区<span><span>（</span></span>以下简称园区<span><span>）</span></span>实现主营业务收入 7062.85 亿元<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长 11%......上缴税金 223.87 亿元<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长 14.1%<span><span>。</span></span>
  <br><span><span>【</span></span>问题<span><span>】</span></span>2015 年一季度<span><span>，</span></span>该省园区企业上缴税金占主营业务收入的比重比上年同期<span><span>：</span></span>
  <br>
   A.上升了 0.1 个百分点 B.上升了 3.1 个百分点 C.下降了 0.1 个百分点 D.下降了 3.1 个百分点
 </blockquote>
 <p>解<span><span>：</span></span>看问题<span><span>，</span></span>求的是两期比重差<span><span>。</span></span>脑海里立刻跳出公式<span><span>，</span></span>找数据<span><span>，</span></span>计算<span><span>。</span></span>a-b=14.1%-11%=3.1%<span><span>，</span></span>A/B≈223/7062≈2/70<span><span>，</span></span>算到这里就可以了<span><span>。</span></span>我们知道两期比重差为正数<span><span>，</span></span>且比 3.1% 小很多<span><span>，</span></span>所以选 A<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>② 平均数增长量<span><span>（</span></span>出现频率<span><span>：</span></span>低<span><span>）</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>例<span><span>：</span></span>今年苹果数为 A<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长率为 a<span><span>，</span></span>苹果树的数量为 N<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长率为 n<span><span>。</span></span>求平均每棵苹果树上的苹果数量比上年多多少个<span><span>？</span></span></p>

 <p>此类题型出现频率较低<span><span>，</span></span>做题时<span><span>，</span></span>一般要把全部式子算完<span><span>，</span></span>才能开始对比选项<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>③ 平均数增长率<span><span>（</span></span>出现频率<span><span>：</span></span>低<span><span>）</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>例<span><span>：</span></span>今年苹果数为 A<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长率为 a<span><span>，</span></span>苹果树的数量为 N<span><span>，</span></span>同比增长率为 n<span><span>。</span></span>求平均每棵苹果树上的苹果数量比上年增长多少<span><span>（</span></span>增长率<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>？</span></span></p>

 <p>大家应该注意到<span><span>，</span></span>公式里面没有<span><span>「</span></span>A<span><span>」</span></span>和<span><span>「</span></span>N<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>这就大大减少了计算量和做题时间<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <blockquote>
  例题 2<span><span>：</span></span>2018 年上海市公务员录用考试<span><span>《</span></span>行测<span><span>》</span></span>真题<span><span>（</span></span>B 类<span><span>）</span></span>第 86 题 
  <br><span><span>【</span></span>材料<span><span>】</span></span>......2017 年上半年<span><span>，</span></span>S 市出口手机 1.9 亿台<span><span>，</span></span>比去年同期减少 22.7%<span><span>；</span></span>价值 513.1 亿元人民币<span><span>，</span></span>下降 23.7%<span><span>。</span></span>......
  <br><span><span>【</span></span>问题<span><span>】</span></span>2017 年上半年<span><span>，</span></span>S 市平均每台出口手机的价值比去年同期约 ____<span><span>。</span></span>
  <br>

 <h2><strong>三<span><span>、</span></span>第三把钥匙<span><span>：</span></span>快速运算技巧</strong></h2>
 <p>这部分大家首先要明确几点<span><span>：</span></span>1.绝大部分题都不要求精确计算<span><span>，</span></span>不然不可能在 25-30 分钟内做完 20 题<span><span>（</span></span>机关里并不缺计算器<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>；</span></span>2.看完选项再计算<span><span>，</span></span>选项的特点决定了你使用哪种计算方式<span><span>；</span></span>3.技巧在精不在多<span><span>，</span></span>而且要熟练掌握才有用<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>注<span><span>：</span></span>网上资料分析的技巧很多很杂<span><span>，</span></span>我备考的时候学了很多技巧<span><span>，</span></span>但发现最后真正有用的也就那几个<span><span>。</span></span>这部分内容的目的就是让大家再少走弯路<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>以下的方法大部分是在我备考的那时候就已经有了<span><span>，</span></span>我不知道<span><span>、</span></span>也无法查证原创是谁<span><span>，</span></span>但为了表达敬意<span><span>，</span></span>我会说明我最早是在谁的课里看到的<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>因为后文的技巧都涉及一个知识点——选项差距的判断<span><span>，</span></span>所以这里先和大家补充一下这块的知识<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong><span><span>（</span></span>一<span><span>）</span></span>选项差距判定标准</strong></p>
 <p><span><span>「</span></span>选项差距大<span><span>」</span></span>指<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>① </strong>四个选项首位均不同<span><span>。</span></span>例如 2018 年北京市市考第 126 题求基期题<span><span>，</span></span>四个选项分别为<span><span>：</span></span>A.22 B.33 C.43 D.55<span><span>。</span></span>A 的首位为 2<span><span>、</span></span>B 的首位为 3<span><span>、</span></span>C 的首位为 4<span><span>、</span></span>D 的首位为 5,这就是首位不同<span><span>。</span></span>那么这道题就可以看作<span><span>「</span></span>选项差距大<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>② 存在首位相同的两个选项<span><span>，</span></span>但这两个选项的次位差<span><span>（</span></span>第二位的差<span><span>）</span></span>大于首位<span><span>。</span></span>例如 2017 年北京市市考第 126 题求基期题<span><span>（</span></span>居然和前一年题号<span><span>、</span></span>类型都一样<span><span>，</span></span>惊<span><span>！</span></span><span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>四个选项分别为<span><span>：</span></span>A.387 B.320 C.265 D.214<span><span>。</span></span>比较接近的选项有两组<span><span>，</span></span>分别是 A 和 B,C 和 D<span><span>。</span></span>A 和 B 首位都是 3<span><span>，</span></span>A 的第二位是 8<span><span>，</span></span>B 的第二位是 2<span><span>，</span></span>A 和 B 的次位差 8-2=6&gt;首位 3<span><span>；</span></span>C 和 D 的首位都是 2<span><span>，</span></span>C 和 D 的次位差 6-1&gt;首位 2<span><span>。</span></span>所以这道题可以看作选项差距大<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><span><span>「</span></span>选项差距小<span><span>」</span></span>指<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>存在首位相同的两个选项<span><span>，</span></span>且这两个选项的次位差小于或等于首位<span><span>。</span></span>例如 2012 年浙江省考第 128 题<span><span>，</span></span>选项为<span><span>：</span></span>A.85.40% B.84.70% C.83.50% D.82.70%<span><span>。</span></span>首位相同<span><span>，</span></span>均为 8<span><span>，</span></span>次位差均小于 8<span><span>（</span></span>其实存在有一组次位差小于 8 即可<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>所以这道题可以看作选项差距小<span><span>。</span></span></p>

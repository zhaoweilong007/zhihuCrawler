 <p><strong>4.2.1</strong> <strong>客观题</strong></p>

 <p>怎么抄<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第一<span><span>，</span></span>全面<span><span>。</span></span></strong>在字数限制的范围内将能踩到的点全部写上去<span><span>。</span></span>为了保证全面<span><span>，</span></span>语言一定要精练<span><span>、</span></span>精练<span><span>、</span></span>再精练<span><span>，</span></span>能用一个词表达的<span><span>，</span></span>不用一句话<span><span>，</span></span>这样才能尽可能地多写一些得分点<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>第三<span><span>，</span></span>逻辑清晰<span><span>、</span></span>层次分明<span><span>。</span></span></strong>一二三四标清楚<span><span>，</span></span>让阅卷人一眼就看出你答了几个点<span><span>。</span></span>有几个点就标几个点<span><span>，</span></span>不要怕要点多<span><span>，</span></span>多了不扣分<span><span>，</span></span>但少了就得不偿失了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>4.2.2</strong> <strong>应用文</strong></p>

 <p>按照以上三部分专项突破<span><span>，</span></span>基本可以保证拿到一个相对不错的分数<span><span>，</span></span>至于高分那就看运气了<span><span>。</span></span></p>

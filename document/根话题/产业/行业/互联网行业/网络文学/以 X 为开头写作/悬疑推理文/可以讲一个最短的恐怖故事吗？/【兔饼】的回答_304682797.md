# 可以讲一个最短的恐怖故事吗？

- 更新时间：2018年03月08日18时15分51秒

 <p data-pid="HZSoM2DD">180308 重大更新 故事10+2&amp;动画短片</p>
 <p data-pid="6qsr_AoH">————————原答案————————</p>
 <p data-pid="zdy3eKd3">Reddit上有一个曾经很火的帖子：</p>
 <p data-pid="ub1WLJ8u">用两句话讲能讲完的最恐怖故事是怎样的？</p>
 <p data-pid="_dIB6qdR">试着给大家翻译几个。</p>
 <p data-pid="OQRfOEzf">01</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img data-rawheight="640" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-76a4e44944a7c291aa4989d324fc1579_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="640" data-original-token="v2-76a4e44944a7c291aa4989d324fc1579" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="640" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-76a4e44944a7c291aa4989d324fc1579_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <blockquote data-pid="o7lDguoY">
  Growing up with cats and dogs, I got used to the sounds of scratching at my door while I slept. 
  <br>
  Now that I live alone, it is much more unsettling.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="UzARQkCK">小时候家里养了很多猫猫狗狗，所以我早就习惯了睡觉的时候有挠门的声音。</p>
 <p data-pid="7AOsbkqx">可现在我一个人住，听到这声音就让人心里发毛了。</p>
 <p data-pid="63bX6UNQ">02</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="VbL_5B2_">
  My grandmother told me that it was a gift to see the angel of death in front of people's houses, to know that he'd be collecting someone there soon. 
  <br>
  I thought it was a gift too, up until the day I began to see it in front of every house.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="7Eer8kpN">奶奶曾告诉我说，能看见告死天使站在将死之人的家门前，是一种天赐的福分。</p>
 <p data-pid="r10_FYPL">我相信了，直到我看见每一栋房子前都站着一个。</p>
 <p data-pid="n0gjorcT">03</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="_ZjsVJp_">
  I begin tucking him into bed and he tells me, “Daddy check for monsters under my bed.”
  <br>
   I look underneath for his amusement and see him, another him, under the bed, staring back at me quivering and whispering, “Daddy there's somebody on my bed.”
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="GEPKoYbd">我给儿子盖好被子，他对我说：“爸比，帮我看下床下面有没有妖怪。”</p>
 <p data-pid="JjR8vWQ3">我想逗逗他，弯下腰，却看见了他，另一个他，颤抖着悄声对我说：“爸比，有人在在我床上。”</p>
 <p data-pid="29DTq36T">04</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img data-rawheight="774" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-406f8e94b04c738593f9ab1fa0a47df8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="774" data-original-token="v2-406f8e94b04c738593f9ab1fa0a47df8" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="774" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-406f8e94b04c738593f9ab1fa0a47df8_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <blockquote data-pid="OrYvUOXR">
  I woke up to hear knocking on glass.
  <br>
  At first, I though it was the window until I heard it come from the mirror again.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="JgJIe5SN">我被敲玻璃的声音吵醒了。</p>
 <p data-pid="sLma1W14">一开始我以为是有人在敲窗户，然后再次声音再次响起，我听出它是从镜子里传来的。</p>
 <p data-pid="WU42oKSj">------------180127第一次更新---------</p>
 <p data-pid="_EQB20jW">05</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img data-rawheight="800" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-627f1d710f88bfb5e6862c697ccd546a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="800" data-original-token="v2-627f1d710f88bfb5e6862c697ccd546a" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="800" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-627f1d710f88bfb5e6862c697ccd546a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <blockquote data-pid="hhfUBXbq">
  There was a picture in my phone of me sleeping.
  <br>
  I live alone.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="YKc8bJYW">我手机里有一张我睡觉时的照片。</p>
 <p data-pid="_Tevi87c">我独居。</p>
 <p data-pid="qH7m_KjY">06</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="7QQZX1hu">
  My sister says that mommy killed her. 
  <br>
  Mommy says that I don't have a sister.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ZbXBIBb3">妹妹说妈妈杀了她。</p>
 <p data-pid="xM5KDrX0">妈妈说我没有妹妹。</p>
 <p data-pid="CKWjs6Rt">07</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img data-rawheight="800" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-da7cd54ebac4fd5a8b6289868c2a0e3c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="800" data-original-token="v2-da7cd54ebac4fd5a8b6289868c2a0e3c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="800" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-da7cd54ebac4fd5a8b6289868c2a0e3c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <blockquote data-pid="7Ar484e9">
  Don't be scared of the monsters, just look for them. 
  <br>
  Look to your left, to your right, under your bed, behind your dresser, in your closet but never look up, she hates being seen.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="8WLa_A5q">不用怕怪物，不信我们找找看。</p>
 <p data-pid="O-daQ587">你看看左边，看看右边，看看床底下，梳妆台后面，衣橱里面，但千万别抬头，她不喜欢被人看到。</p>
 <p data-pid="bERBnp-T">08</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="-Y34sMk9">
  The longer I wore it the more it grew on me. 
  <br>
  She had such pretty skin.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="FbbjGdfk">越穿越喜欢。</p>
 <p data-pid="h7DsHI5X">她皮肤真好。</p>
 <p data-pid="mTvPr69o">（grow on me 的双关）</p>
 <p data-pid="VxQ7ZqMg">09</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="A7gYHi-k">
  You hear your mom calling you into the kitchen.
  <br>
  As you are heading down the stairs you hear a whisper from the closet saying "Don't go down there honey, I heard it too."
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="wudTy30Z">你听到你妈叫你去厨房。</p>
 <p data-pid="-V6mfSwK">你正要下楼，却听到壁橱里传来一阵耳语“别下去宝贝，我也听见了。”</p>
 <p data-pid="hUtm-aVT">10</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="waiQiHcq">
  My daughter won't stop crying and screaming in the middle of the night. 
  <br>
  I visit her grave and ask her to stop, but it doesn't help.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="tqbnO9LM">我女儿半夜里不停地哭喊尖叫。</p>
 <p data-pid="43fNL2Oh">我去她坟头求她停下，但没有用。</p>
 <p data-pid="MLZL-zEn">————————20180216————————</p>
 <p data-pid="BkJOZfKa">好吧，这个就作为特别篇好了。</p>
 <p data-pid="Wqn8jhJB">这是我特别喜欢的一条。但个人水平有限，无法将那种你一个词一个词的读下去的同时，恐怖的形象一点点在你眼前展开的画面感通过翻译传达出来，所以就只放原版不翻译了。</p>
 <p data-pid="1NJ9tSrO">ex-1.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="HUI5Hv_q">
  The last thing I saw was my alarm clock flashing 12:07 before she pushed her long rotting nails through my chest, her other hand muffling my screams. 
  <br>
  I sat bolt upright, relieved it was only a dream, but as I saw my alarm clock read 12:06, I heard my closet door creak open.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Bt5vxIse">有兴趣的朋友可以在留言里尝试看看。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="EgaabxQV">————————20180308————————</p>
 <p data-pid="9RDLuJar">女生节重大更新，追加几个短故事</p>
 <p data-pid="YQsLt1yi">11.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="x_jB1Mbo">
  She asked why I was breathing so heavily.
  <br>
  I wasn't.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="h66nc20Z">她问我为什么喘气这么大声。</p>
 <p data-pid="NavNULun">我没有。</p>
 <p data-pid="g6Ff1vJB">12.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="lgDiXYPx">
  After working a hard day, I came home to see my girlfriend cradling our child. 
  <br>
  I didn't know which was more frightening, seeing my dead girlfriend and stillborn child, or knowing that someone broke into my apartment to place them there.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="l3N3JgvP">辛苦了一天回家，一开门看见女朋友正抱着我们的孩子。</p>
 <p data-pid="lO_LpcGt">我不知道哪个更恐怖：看着自己过世的女友和死产的孩子，还是意识到有人闯进我的公寓，把他们放在这里。</p>
 <p data-pid="STESWmED">13.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="dEpoIZvC">
  I followed the trail of blood.
  <br>
  But then realized I was walking in a circle.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="AFZUHbgh">我循着血迹一路前行。</p>
 <p data-pid="C1EKbRSO">然后发现我在兜圈子。</p>
 <p data-pid="PdtkH_w8">14.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="1fbGasUT">
  A few hours after I was buried alive, I was thrilled when I heard someone digging me out. 
  <br>
  My joy quickly faded when I realized the sound was coming from under me.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="DrSjAwsZ">被活埋了几个小时后，听到有人在挖土，我欣喜若狂。</p>
 <p data-pid="R-QiIP0V">但很快我就不那么高兴了，因为我意识到那声音是从我身下传来的。</p>
 <p data-pid="cEGRjnRO">15.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="6gl8Roe1">
  i know txting ur phone is stupid after u died but i miss u
  <br>
  ✓ Seen
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="-LpIAkSc">我知道在你死后还给你发短信很蠢但我真的好想你</p>
 <p data-pid="42rguoat">✓已读</p>
 <p data-pid="HCZulWuC">16.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="9V0cYJSd">
  I wake up and everything feels wrong; it's too quiet outside. 
  <br>
  I look out the window and see everyone standing still, looking at my house.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="aahopOjS">醒来后，一切都觉得很怪异：外面安静得不正常。</p>
 <p data-pid="KYx6MMwx">我向窗外看去，所有人都一动不动地站着，看向我的家。</p>
 <p data-pid="5Dqw7Z3x">17.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="oyikODQn">
  It's been watching me for hours now... 
  <br>
  Sometimes I catch glimpses of its reflection on the computer screen, but I dare not turn around...
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="fEyRmzQW">它已经看了我几个小时了…</p>
 <p data-pid="HVOwVR38">有一瞬间，我从电脑屏幕的反光中看到了它，但我实在不敢回头…</p>
 <p data-pid="f-2ToS4s">18.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="u_8lgS7L">
  I killed my neighbor's dog yesterday - in self defense I told them.
  <br>
  The truth was, the dog was just too good at digging up bones.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="wMpego7N">昨天我杀了邻居家的狗——我告诉别人这是自卫。</p>
 <p data-pid="gADlSPeH">实际上，只不过是那只狗太会从地里挖骨头罢了。</p>
 <p data-pid="nmY3sl7t">19.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="AmUUf447">
  I told him to get rid of everything that reminded him of ex-wife.
  <br>
  I forgot his daughter had her mother's eyes.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="NE0RHlAO">我叫他处理一切会让他想起他前妻的东西。</p>
 <p data-pid="EgS4jVTG">我忘了他女儿遗传了她妈妈的眼睛。</p>
 <p data-pid="I9Jr2a_1">20.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="n3vRbOn-">
  Mom hugged me tight as the police drove off with the butcher who had tried cutting us open the night before. 
  <br>
  "Your mother's skin is warm," she whispered into my ear.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="9DH3hM9j">妈妈紧紧地抱着我，看着警车押着那个昨晚想把我们开膛的屠夫离去。</p>
 <p data-pid="TS8HGjpm">妈妈在我耳边悄声说：“你妈的皮好暖和。”</p>
 <p data-pid="aOFmexRj">————————</p>

 <blockquote data-pid="YmObGkKU">

  <br>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Nu2MTVMn">你半夜醒来，发现有张脸正看着你。</p>
 <p data-pid="nRIYUw9Y">那是你的脸。</p>
 <p data-pid="Yw6AiHQY">ex-3.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ZXeJMfZj">

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="kTQYZfdC">你半夜醒来，发现他不在床上。</p>

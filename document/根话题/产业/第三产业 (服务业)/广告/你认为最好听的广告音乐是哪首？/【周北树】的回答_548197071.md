 <p data-pid="9UoO0i24">我最喜欢的广告歌曲，</p>
 <p data-pid="t8Cx__5U">来自日本「(株)関電工」在 2016 年发布的「光を灯す / future with bright lights」</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1054916202408767488" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="光を灯す" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-d8d3eea5d1637640109684d84f9d2747.jpg" data-lens-id="1054916202408767488"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-d8d3eea5d1637640109684d84f9d2747.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">光を灯す<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1054916202408767488</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="pm6QezMT">广告的创意是，在白纸上用特殊的电子电路笔·AgIC来画线，在墨水上通过的灯都会点亮。</p>
 <p data-pid="tB0f2rTf">関電工的企业理念是：“每个人都会点亮未来。”广告也完美的体现了这家企业的世界观。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aWUiKumS">广告音乐以「未来和希望」为主题，以艺人 YeYe 为中心所创作的原创歌曲。</p>

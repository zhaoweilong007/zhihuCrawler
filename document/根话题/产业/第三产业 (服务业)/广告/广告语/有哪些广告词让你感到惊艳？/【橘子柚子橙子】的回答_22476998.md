 <p data-pid="d9tjagDI">if you are, you breathe.</p>
 <p data-pid="Frbja47x">If you breathe, you talk.</p>
 <p data-pid="M5ETJdcB">If you talk, you ask.</p>
 <p data-pid="_Y0MjXWa">If you ask, you think.</p>
 <p data-pid="N1bZXTza">If you think, you search.</p>
 <p data-pid="ILKG-2Ks">If you search, you experience.</p>
 <p data-pid="hYoCLEms">If you experience, you learn.</p>
 <p data-pid="H1HnNwVM">If you learn, you grow.</p>
 <p data-pid="CyAfMrkg">If you grow, you wish.</p>
 <p data-pid="VAFfpl9u">If you wish, you find, and if you find, you doubt.</p>
 <p data-pid="bgow_DlD">If you doubt, you question.</p>
 <p data-pid="B4ALdxEe">If you question, you understand, and if you understand, you know.</p>
 <p data-pid="vq8pdqdC">If you know, you want to know more.</p>
 <p data-pid="94M6GLhU">If you want to know more, you are alive.</p>

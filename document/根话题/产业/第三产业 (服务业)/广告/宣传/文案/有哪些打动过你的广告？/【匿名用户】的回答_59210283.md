# 有哪些打动过你的广告？
- 点赞数：7367
- 更新时间：2015年08月14日22时36分00秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/22256995/answer/59210283
<body>
 <p data-pid="_YHC5m3V">If you are alive, you breath.</p>
 <p data-pid="W_0HminT">If you breath, you talk.</p>
 <p data-pid="fudqdT_X">If you talk, you ask.</p>
 <p data-pid="dcci15lH">If you ask, you think.</p>
 <p data-pid="L6pvGC5X">If you think, you search.</p>
 <p data-pid="CBZp8pA-">If you search, you experience.</p>
 <p data-pid="Lw3fmxNe">If you experience, you learn.</p>
 <p data-pid="cjAZOViB">If you learn, you grow.</p>
 <p data-pid="y4xNJ1LK">If you grow, you wish.</p>
 <p data-pid="M2jKvMD6">If you wish, you find.</p>
 <p data-pid="PuEUhQTP">If you find, you doubt.</p>
 <p data-pid="mbQzAQPs">If you doubt, you question.</p>
 <p data-pid="mh5j-NiS">If you question, you understand.</p>
 <p data-pid="K11pmmBw">If you understand, you know.</p>
 <p data-pid="4TtG2r79">If you know, you want to know more.</p>
 <p data-pid="8T_m_T3I">If you want to know more, you are alive.</p>
 <p data-pid="lUlr63Tm">Live curious.</p>
 <p data-pid="AcIES5Ev">by National Geographic</p>

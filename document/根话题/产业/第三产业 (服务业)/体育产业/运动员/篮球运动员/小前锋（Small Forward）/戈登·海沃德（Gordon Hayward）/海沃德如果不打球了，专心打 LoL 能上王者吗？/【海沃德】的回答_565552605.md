 <p data-pid="hMUePXiO">Thanks for inviting.</p>
 <p data-pid="wnTUvxUI">I really like playing League of Legends. It's something I do to relax. I began to play the game since I could remember. I am still looking for a person in NBA who plays LOL better than me. I haven't found that person.</p>
 <p data-pid="Z8UjB10e">So if I focus on playing League of Legends right now, what do you think?</p>
 <p data-pid="gPIRt8wu">Of course, I need time for hard training, and I also have a family to take care of. So for the LOL lovers, I suggest that there should be balance among family, game and work. For me, I have to take care of my princess during the game.</p>
 <p data-pid="HDb4xJnm">谢邀。</p>
 <p data-pid="VtmRR8jX">我确实很喜欢打LOL。电竞是我平时放松的方式。从我有记忆以来，我就在玩电竞游戏。我一直在找NBA里有没有比我打LOL更厉害的人，不过目前还没有。</p>
 <p data-pid="_cTLf_iI">所以如果我专心打LOL的话，能不能上王者，你们说呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="r6BhPqb1">当然，我需要时间辛苦训练，同时又有一个家庭要照顾，所以对知乎上的LOL爱好者，我的心得分享是要找到家庭、游戏和工作之间的平衡。对于我来说，我在打游戏的时候也要照顾我的小公主。</p>

# 有哪些拟态很厉害的生物？

- 更新时间：2018年05月30日14时25分10秒

 <p data-pid="uSUk83eP"><b>要说到拟态大王，必须是我们的章鱼哥！</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-468123e806473a63ffb536e9e40fa6c3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="500" data-rawheight="500" data-original-token="v2-468123e806473a63ffb536e9e40fa6c3" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="500" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-468123e806473a63ffb536e9e40fa6c3_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="GkKXiW1m">章鱼拥有逆天的拟态本领是在于它的体表分布着一种含有生物色素的细胞（chromatophores）</p>
 <p data-pid="zoOsVlJi">在色素细胞里面，色素的颗粒被包围在有弹性的囊中，称为细胞弹性囊（cytoelastic sacculus）</p>
 <p data-pid="N_fjH5Q-">它可以利用肌肉来改变这些囊的形状或大小，使细胞的透明度或是反射能力改变，并造成色彩变化。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/984857750559367168" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic4.zhimg.com/80/v2-9f03ae5b23a3afe7045bebcda7ba8c53_b.jpg" data-lens-id="984857750559367168"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/80/v2-9f03ae5b23a3afe7045bebcda7ba8c53_b.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/984857750559367168</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="MixYkg3h">以视频中章鱼为例，它从灰白色到黑色再到拟态礁石斑驳的颜色就在瞬间进行</p>
 <p data-pid="kbnCNJxQ">更厉害的地方在于，章鱼除了体表颜色的变化， 还可以让皮肤的结构与周围环境相仿</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/984863444763385856" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/80/v2-119df813879faecf1a2c6c3909c616e9_b.jpg" data-lens-id="984863444763385856"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/80/v2-119df813879faecf1a2c6c3909c616e9_b.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/984863444763385856</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="7n904mZe">片中的章鱼在拟态时，把光滑的皮肤模拟出珊瑚的棱角</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/984866074315141120" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-01b61183098f6eb5075ab7159aeff5c6_b.jpg" data-lens-id="984866074315141120"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-01b61183098f6eb5075ab7159aeff5c6_b.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/984866074315141120</span></span></a>

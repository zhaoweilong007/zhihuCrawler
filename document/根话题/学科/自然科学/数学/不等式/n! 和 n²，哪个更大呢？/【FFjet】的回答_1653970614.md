# n! 和 n²，哪个更大呢？

- 更新时间：2020年12月31日08时32分13秒

 <p data-pid="-zlKPs2D">为啥感觉都做那么麻烦。。实际上根本不用数归，直接用定义就出来了</p>
 <p data-pid="dtsI6OE0">首先我们知道 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%21%3D1%5E2%2C2%21%3C2%5E2%2C3%21%3C3%5E2" alt="1!=1^2,2!<2^2,3!<3^2" eeimg="1"> ，下面我们证明 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeq4" alt="n\geq4" eeimg="1"> 时有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%21%3En%5E2" alt="n!>n^2" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="mFCK_X49">直接作商，得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bn%21%7D%7Bn%5E2%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%5Ccdot+2%5Ccdot+3%5Ccdots+%5Cleft%28+n-1+%5Cright%29+n%7D%7Bn%5Ccdot+n%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%5Ccdot+2%5Ccdot+3%5Ccdots+%5Cleft%28+n-1+%5Cright%29%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cge+%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cleft%28+n-1+%5Cright%29%7D%7Bn%7D%3E1+" alt="\frac{n!}{n^2}=\frac{1\cdot 2\cdot 3\cdots \left( n-1 \right) n}{n\cdot n}=\frac{1\cdot 2\cdot 3\cdots \left( n-1 \right)}{n}\ge \frac{2\left( n-1 \right)}{n}>1 " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Yy0HWwo9">证毕。</p>

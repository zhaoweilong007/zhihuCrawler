 <p data-pid="z3CFen6b"><b>2.2.3 正定</b></p>
 <p data-pid="YU_IEvwN">正定是对二次函数有效的一个定义，对方程无效。</p>
 <p data-pid="8TOzB2vI">对于二次型函数， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dx%5E+TAx" alt="f(x)=x^ TAx" eeimg="1"> ：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="-NB4cbWm"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3E0%2Cx%5Cne+0%2Cx%5Cin+%5Cmathbb+%7BR%7D" alt="f(x)>0,x\ne 0,x\in \mathbb {R}" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f" alt="f" eeimg="1"> 为正定二次型， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 为正定矩阵</li>
  <li data-pid="7Nj-MAUz"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%5Cgeq+0%2Cx%5Cne+0%2Cx%5Cin+%5Cmathbb+%7BR%7D" alt="f(x)\geq 0,x\ne 0,x\in \mathbb {R}" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f" alt="f" eeimg="1"> 为半正定二次型， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 为半正定矩阵</li>

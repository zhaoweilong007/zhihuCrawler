# 小偷能逃出无数个警察的包围圈吗？
- 点赞数：6732

<body>
 <p data-pid="k5FTPAtm">答案可能有点反直觉——小偷能逃出警察的包围。尽管逃出的时候离他任意近的范围内都有警察，却没有任何一个警察能够和他完全重合。接下来我会构造一个逃跑的策略。</p>

 <p data-pid="Ox79vCL7">如图，不妨设警察与小偷的速度为1，警察所在的圆为单位圆， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O" alt="O" eeimg="1"> 为圆心， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB" alt="AB" eeimg="1"> 是一条直径。棕色曲线是半条心脏线，以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OA" alt="OA" eeimg="1"> 为极轴，其极坐标方程为</p>
 <p data-pid="MCWub4hR"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%3D%5Csin%5E2%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D%2C0%5Cle%5Ctheta%5Cle%5Cpi" alt="r=\sin^2\frac{\theta}{2},0\le\theta\le\pi" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="XoAN-B5z">对于心脏线上的点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> ，设其在圆周上的投影为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T%5E%5Cprime" alt="T^\prime" eeimg="1"> ，那么我们可以计算一下弧长：</p>
 <p data-pid="M8gt4wDH"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BTB%7D%3D%5Cint_%7B%5Cangle+AOT%7D%5E%5Cpi%5Csqrt%7Br%5E2%2B%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bdr%7D%7Bd%5Ctheta%7D%5Cright%29%5E2%7Dd%5Ctheta%3D%5Cleft.-2%5Ccos%5Cfrac%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%7C_%7B%5Cangle+AOT%7D%5E%5Cpi%3D2%5Csin%5Cfrac%7B%5Cangle+BOT%7D%7B2%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{TB}=\int_{\angle AOT}^\pi\sqrt{r^2+\left(\frac{dr}{d\theta}\right)^2}d\theta=\left.-2\cos\frac{\theta}{2}\right|_{\angle AOT}^\pi=2\sin\frac{\angle BOT}{2}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="klMkFhPY"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT%5E%5Cprime+B%7D%3D%5Cangle+BOT" alt="\overset{\frown}{T^\prime B}=\angle BOT" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="BDIqC6J8">可以发现始终有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BTB%7D%5Clt%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT%5E%5Cprime+B%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{TB}\lt\overset{\frown}{T^\prime B}" eeimg="1"> ，也就是说如果小偷在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 而警察在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T%5E%5Cprime" alt="T^\prime" eeimg="1"> ，那么小偷一定能先赶到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 点处。我们可以据此构造小偷的策略：</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="xDTsyhaA">将圆周上的警察从1开始编号，由于警察是可数无穷多，因此每个警察都会有一个有限的编号</li>
  <li data-pid="YYqp3TA4">小偷走到心脏线上某点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_1" alt="T_1" eeimg="1"> 处，观察1号警察的位置 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1" alt="P_1" eeimg="1"> 。如果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1" alt="P_1" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 在直线 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OT_1" alt="OT_1" eeimg="1"> 的同侧，则将心脏线 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_1B" alt="T_1B" eeimg="1"> 段以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OT_1" alt="OT_1" eeimg="1"> 为对称轴进行翻转，然后沿翻转后的曲线前进，记 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1" alt="B_1" eeimg="1"> 为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 的对称像；否则，继续沿原曲线前进，记 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1%3DB" alt="B_1=B" eeimg="1"></li>
  <li data-pid="EvIMKuF7">此时由于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_1B_1%7D%5Clt%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%7D%5Cle%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1B_1%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_1B_1}\lt\overset{\frown}{T_1^\prime B_1}\le\overset{\frown}{P_1B_1}" eeimg="1"> ，可以在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_1B_1%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_1B_1}" eeimg="1"> 上找到一个点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_2" alt="T_2" eeimg="1"> ，使得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_1B_1%7D%2B2%5Cangle+T_2OB_1%5Clt%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1B_1%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_1B_1}+2\angle T_2OB_1\lt\overset{\frown}{P_1B_1}" eeimg="1"> 。小偷沿曲线前进到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_2" alt="T_2" eeimg="1"> 处</li>
  <li data-pid="5vI8ZiIq">回到第2步，观察2号警察的位置，以此类推</li>
 </ol>

 <p data-pid="enCXPGXf">下面说明这个策略的有效性。考虑1号警察，由于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_1B_1%7D%2B2%5Cangle+T_2OB_1%5Clt%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1B_1%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_1B_1}+2\angle T_2OB_1\lt\overset{\frown}{P_1B_1}" eeimg="1"> ，当小偷到达 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_2" alt="T_2" eeimg="1"> 时有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_2B_1%7D%2B2%5Cangle+T_2OB_1%5Clt%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_2B_1}+2\angle T_2OB_1\lt\overset{\frown}{P_1^\prime B_1}" eeimg="1"> ，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1%5E%5Cprime" alt="P_1^\prime" eeimg="1"> 为1号警察此时的位置。无论之后如何翻转路线，小偷到达圆周所需时间都是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_2B_1%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_2B_1}" eeimg="1"> ，且到达时的点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T_%5Cinfty" alt="T_\infty" eeimg="1"> 必然在圆弧 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BB_1B_1%5E%5Cprime%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{B_1B_1^\prime}" eeimg="1"> 上，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1%5E%5Cprime" alt="B_1^\prime" eeimg="1"> 是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1" alt="B_1" eeimg="1"> 关于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OT_2" alt="OT_2" eeimg="1"> 的反射像。此时有</p>
 <p data-pid="ajubovSl"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_2B_1%7D%5Clt%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%7D-2%5Cangle+T_2OB_1%3D%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%7D-%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BB_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%7D%3D%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%5E%5Cprime%7D" alt="\overset{\frown}{T_2B_1}\lt\overset{\frown}{P_1^\prime B_1}-2\angle T_2OB_1=\overset{\frown}{P_1^\prime B_1}-\overset{\frown}{B_1^\prime B_1}=\overset{\frown}{P_1^\prime B_1^\prime}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="b-XZ_eiI">因此1号警察来不及赶到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1%5E%5Cprime" alt="B_1^\prime" eeimg="1"> 点，从而</p>
 <p data-pid="Ka1wSXhb"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cinfty+T_%5Cinfty%7D%5Cge%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cinfty+B_1%5E%5Cprime%7D%5Cge%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BP_1%5E%5Cprime+B_1%5E%5Cprime%7D-%5Coverset%7B%5Cfrown%7D%7BT_2+B_1%5E%5Cprime%7D%3D%5Cdelta_1%5Cgt0" alt="\overset{\frown}{P_1^\infty T_\infty}\ge\overset{\frown}{P_1^\infty B_1^\prime}\ge\overset{\frown}{P_1^\prime B_1^\prime}-\overset{\frown}{T_2 B_1^\prime}=\delta_1\gt0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Imzdi5a6">当然，警察也可以向反方向运动，但只要 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cangle+T_1OB_1%5Clt90%5E%5Ccirc" alt="\angle T_1OB_1\lt90^\circ" eeimg="1"> ，依然是来不及。</p>
 <p data-pid="INLzXOQ_">也就是说，当小偷到达圆周时，与1号警察之间的距离严格大于零。同样的推理适用于后面的所有警察。尽管这个距离 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdelta_i" alt="\delta_i" eeimg="1"> 会越来越小，但对每一个单独的警察来说都是一个固定的正数。因此，最终没有任何一个警察能够在小偷到达圆周时与其重合。小偷就这么从也许处处稠密的警察中找到了缝隙，逃出生天。</p>

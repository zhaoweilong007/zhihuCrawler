# 解微分方程为什么会出现个 e？
- 点赞数：10205
- 更新时间：2017年10月01日19时35分49秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/33177784/answer/237992320
<body>
 <p data-pid="3G0dnNA7">你搞不懂的原因就是------你懂的太多了...</p>
 <p data-pid="rf6UWeo4">想象你生活在那个微积分初创的年代,<b>你还不知道什么通解公式之类的玩意儿</b>,自然常数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> 还未曾知晓...</p>
 <p data-pid="Pf8wzCz7">你是一个站在时代前沿的数学家,你想知道微分方程: <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5Cprime%2Bpy%2Bq%3D0" alt="y\prime+py+q=0" eeimg="1"> 的解.</p>
 <p data-pid="nUWjV9xX">你觉得可以有一个性质良好的函数作为解,比如在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D+" alt=" \mathbb{C} " eeimg="1"> 上解析...</p>
 <p data-pid="Gjzolh_n">于是你可以在原点将这个函数展开: <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%28x%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+a_nx%5En" alt="y(x)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="V2JahnEH">因为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmathbb%7BC%7D+" alt=" \mathbb{C} " eeimg="1"> 上解析嘛,所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D+" alt=" \mathbb{R} " eeimg="1"> 上光滑,求导得: <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%27%28x%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+na_nx%5E%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%28n%2B1%29a_%7Bn%2B1%7Dx%5En" alt="y'(x)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty na_nx^{n-1}=\sum_{n=0}^\infty (n+1)a_{n+1}x^n" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="m6eLynnO">然后代入得:</p>
 <p data-pid="6rNVAnY6"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%28n%2B1%29a_%7Bn%2B1%7Dx%5En%2Bp+%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+a_nx%5En+%2Bq+%26%3D+0%5C%5C+q%2B%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%28%28n%2B1%29a_%7Bn%2B1%7D%2Bp+a_n%29x%5En+%26%3D+0%5C%5C+%28a_1%2Bp+a_0%2B+q%29%2B%5Csum_%7Bn%3D1%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%28%28n%2B1%29a_%7Bn%2B1%7D%2Bp+a_n%29x%5En+%26%3D+0%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D" alt="\begin{aligned} \sum_{n=0}^\infty (n+1)a_{n+1}x^n+p \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n +q &amp;= 0\\ q+\sum_{n=0}^\infty ((n+1)a_{n+1}+p a_n)x^n &amp;= 0\\ (a_1+p a_0+ q)+\sum_{n=1}^\infty ((n+1)a_{n+1}+p a_n)x^n &amp;= 0\\ \end{aligned}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="2MySMtnl">要使得等式恒成立,所有项数都应该是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="ZB9Vjzoj"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+0%3D%26%28n%2B1%29a_%7Bn%2B1%7D%2Bp+a_n%5C%5C+0%3D%26a_1%2Bp+a_0%2B+q%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%5Cright." alt="\left\{\begin{aligned} 0=&amp;(n+1)a_{n+1}+p a_n\\ 0=&amp;a_1+p a_0+ q\\ \end{aligned}\right." eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Zci8jDen">上面一个递推式直接迭代可以解得:<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_n%3DC%5Cfrac%7B+p%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%7Bn%21%7D" alt="a_n=C\frac{ p^{n-1}}{n!}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="67JBksHp">也就是说解可以写成: <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%28x%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+C%5Cfrac%7B+p%5E%7Bn-1%7D%7D%7Bn%21%7Dx%5En%3D%5Cfrac%7BC%7D%7Bp%7D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+p%5En%5Cfrac%7Bx%5En%7D%7Bn%21%7D" alt="y(x)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty C\frac{ p^{n-1}}{n!}x^n=\frac{C}{p}\sum_{n=0}^\infty p^n\frac{x^n}{n!}" eeimg="1"> 的形式...</p>
 <p data-pid="-FuJM74d">后来发现每次都要写这么一坨级数太烦了,经过研究发现定义 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bexp%7D%28x%29%3A%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty%5Cfrac%7Bx%5En%7D%7Bn%21%7D" alt="\mathrm{exp}(x):=\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{x^n}{n!}" eeimg="1"> 能减少很多麻烦.</p>
 <p data-pid="NdjPGwdr">然后进一步定义欧拉数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%3A%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bexp%7D%281%29%3D%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%21%7D%3D1%2B1%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%21%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%21%7D%2B%5Ccdots" alt="e:=\mathrm{exp}(1)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{1}{n!}=1+1+\frac{1}{2!}+\frac{1}{3!}+\cdots" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="UHhjmpWE">这个函数性质很好,可以把加法变乘法:<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bexp%7D%28x%2By%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bexp%7D%28x%29%5Cmathrm%7Bexp%7D%28y%29" alt="\mathrm{exp}(x+y)=\mathrm{exp}(x)\mathrm{exp}(y)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="-A0znP44">&gt;&gt;级数绝对收敛时算符可以交换</p>
 <p data-pid="QnHl6YvF"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+%5Cexp%28x%29+%5Ccdot+%5Cexp%28y%29+%3D%26%5Cleft%28%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%5Cfrac%7Bx%5En%7D%7Bn%21%7D%5Cright%29+%5Ccdot+%5Cleft%28%5Csum_%7Bm%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%5Cfrac%7By%5Em%7D%7Bm%21%7D%5Cright%29+%5C%5C+%3D%26%5Csum_%7Bn%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%5Csum_%7Bm%3D0%7D%5E%5Cinfty+%5Cfrac%7Bx%5En%7D%7Bn%21%7D+%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7By%5Em%7D%7Bm%21%7D+%5C%5C+%3D%26%5Csum_%7Bk+%3D+0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D+%5Cleft%28%5Csum_%7Bn%2Bm%3Dk%7D+%5Cfrac%7Bx%5En+y%5Em%7D%7Bm%21n%21%7D%5Cright%29+%5C%5C+%3D%26%5Csum_%7Bk%3D0%7D%5E%7B%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%28x+%2B+y%29%5Ek%7D%7Bk%21%7D+%5C%5C+%3D%26%5Cmathrm%7Bexp%7D%28x%2By%29+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D" alt="\begin{aligned} \exp(x) \cdot \exp(y) =&amp;\left(\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{x^n}{n!}\right) \cdot \left(\sum_{m=0}^\infty \frac{y^m}{m!}\right) \\ =&amp;\sum_{n=0}^\infty \sum_{m=0}^\infty \frac{x^n}{n!} \cdot \frac{y^m}{m!} \\ =&amp;\sum_{k = 0}^{\infty} \left(\sum_{n+m=k} \frac{x^n y^m}{m!n!}\right) \\ =&amp;\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{(x + y)^k}{k!} \\ =&amp;\mathrm{exp}(x+y) \end{aligned}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="FYBbhqTn">人们知道有这种性质的可以叫指数函数,于是,最后定义自然指数函数为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cexp%28x%29+%3D+%5Cexp%281%29%5Ex+%3D+e%5Ex" alt="\exp(x) = \exp(1)^x = e^x" eeimg="1"> .</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="2GdzJN2b">所以不是为什么出现了个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> ,出现的是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex" alt="e^x" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="EYlDMSkV">至于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex" alt="e^x" eeimg="1"> 为什么会出现,楼上说的很明白了.</p>
 <p data-pid="IllrRQGI"><b>因此指数函数是求导算子的特征函数------</b>算子作用于函数后的不变量, <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex" alt="e^x" eeimg="1"> 求导仍是本身.</p>
 <p data-pid="qUu6n1TO">这个和线性代数里矩阵与特征值是相似的...</p>
 <p data-pid="Sgi8I6M2">特征值是矩阵变换后的基,特征函数也是算子变换后的基...</p>
 <p data-pid="OVf3ms1h">至于基为什么这样...唉...捉鸡啊...这可以另开一个问题了...</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="zS4QOLzi">再举一个例子,把傅里叶变换看成一个算子,其特征函数(之一)为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdisplaystyle+e%5E%7B-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7Dx%5E2%7D" alt="\displaystyle e^{-\frac{1}{2}x^2}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="ysqvEXUK">所以傅里叶变换里这个东西经常出现...</p>
 <p data-pid="x6rMardE">没有也正常,因为傅里叶变换的特征函数可以长得很不一样,比如 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B4%7D+%5Cleft%28%5Csqrt%7B2+%5Cpi+%7D+%5Cdelta+%28x%29%2B1%5Cright%29+%2C%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%5Cleft%7C+x%5Cright%7C+%7D%7D" alt=" \frac{1}{4} \left(\sqrt{2 \pi } \delta (x)+1\right) ,\frac{1}{\sqrt{\left| x\right| }}" eeimg="1"> 这俩也是.</p>

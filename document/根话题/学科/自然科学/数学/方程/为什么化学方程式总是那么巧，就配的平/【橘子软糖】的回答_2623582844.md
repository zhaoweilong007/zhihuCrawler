# 为什么化学方程式总是那么巧，就配的平?

<body>
 <p data-pid="aa7LLdK1">因为配不平的方程式不会发生，也就不会出现在试卷上（逃</p>
 <p data-pid="7Nyg6n5v">因为所有的方程式都可以配平，只需要系数全零就可以（逃</p>
 <figure data-size="small">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-b10f31f57895dd88c277e69c0bcd8bdd_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="small" data-rawwidth="1423" data-rawheight="1345" data-original-token="v2-e56cec05f073bd2e248ebbeff1de1550" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-226d66fafe1622979268056847f078e9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1423" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-b10f31f57895dd88c277e69c0bcd8bdd_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="IpqqyY51">我们称化学方程式：</p>
 <p data-pid="MoUyrr2E"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_i%5Cnu_i%5Csum_jc_%7Bij%7DE_j%3D0%5C%5C" alt="\sum_i\nu_i\sum_jc_{ij}E_j=0\\" eeimg="1"> （其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cnu_i" alt="\nu_i" eeimg="1"> 表示化学方程式中第 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i" alt="i" eeimg="1"> 个物质前的系数， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csum_i%5Cnu_i%5E2%5Cneq0" alt="\sum_i\nu_i^2\neq0" eeimg="1"> ，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_j" alt="E_j" eeimg="1"> 表示某元素， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c_%7Bij%7D" alt="c_{ij}" eeimg="1"> 则表示第 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=i" alt="i" eeimg="1"> 个物质中某元素的原子的数目，若不存在则为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> ）</p>
 <p data-pid="Vdf-O5H3">是配平的，当且仅当：</p>
 <p data-pid="wEg6KPK5"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cforall%7Bj%7D%2C%5C+%5Csum_i%5Cnu_ic_%7Bij%7D%3D0%5C%5C" alt="\forall{j},\ \sum_i\nu_ic_{ij}=0\\" eeimg="1">也即：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-177410235105938093a2c819d8bc68f7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1253" data-rawheight="346" data-original-token="v2-8cfbfc290ec198281b74905d8e0423f0" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7e7403f1da5f6addbe0ac809365ab119_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1253" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-177410235105938093a2c819d8bc68f7_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="YHUa21kv">以上两式也就是质量守恒的数学表达（电荷守恒可以通过添加等量的异号离子使之变为电中性）。</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="D5t8dnVN">例如</p>
 <p data-pid="_PgtND8_"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cce%7BN2%5C+%2B%5C+3H2%5C+-%5C+2NH3%7D%3D0%5C%5C" alt="\ce{N2\ +\ 3H2\ -\ 2NH3}=0\\" eeimg="1"> 是配平的，等价于：</p>
 <p data-pid="8eM-o3oo"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+2%260%261%5C%5C+0%262%263+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+1%5C%5C3%5C%5C-2+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%3D%5Cvec%7B0%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{bmatrix} 2&amp;0&amp;1\\ 0&amp;2&amp;3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1\\3\\-2 \end{bmatrix}=\vec{0}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="mRYrPgyB">不难看出，配平的过程其实就是找 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7Bj%5Ctimes%7Bi%7D%7D%5Ccdot%5Cvec%5Cnu%7D%3D%5Cvec0" alt="\mathbf{C_{j\times{i}}\cdot\vec\nu}=\vec0" eeimg="1"> 的非平凡解。存在非零解的充要条件是</p>
 <p data-pid="XtztLmLO"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%5Crm%7B%7Drank%28%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7Bj%5Ctimes%7Bi%7D%7D%7D%29%3Ci%7D%5C%5C" alt="\color{red}{\rm{}rank(\mathbf{C_{j\times{i}}})<i}\\" eeimg="1"> 具体来说：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="OTBFH9xJ"><b>如果</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j%3Ci" alt="j<i" eeimg="1"> ，也即<b>元素的数目小于物质的数目</b>，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7Bj%5Ctimes%7Bi%7D%7D%5Ccdot%5Cvec%5Cnu%7D%3D%5Cvec0" alt="\mathbf{C_{j\times{i}}\cdot\vec\nu}=\vec0" eeimg="1"> 一定有非零解，<b>方程一定可以配平</b>，许多无机反应属于这一类。</li>
  <li data-pid="QBF4CDnR">如果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=j%5Cgeq%7B%7Di" alt="j\geq{}i" eeimg="1"> ，比如一些化合、分解、异构化的反应属这一类，这类方程式是不一定可以配平的。</li>
 </ul>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="Eo8sM33f">接下来用两个例子简要说明一下如何判断方程式能否配平：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="dFlTFBe_">系数矩阵：</p>
 <p data-pid="9R5jTaRr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7B3%5Ctimes3%7D%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+1%260%262%5C%5C+3%262%268%5C%5C+0%261%261+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\mathbf{C_{3\times3}}=\begin{bmatrix} 1&amp;0&amp;2\\ 3&amp;2&amp;8\\ 0&amp;1&amp;1 \end{bmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"> 秩：</p>
 <p data-pid="K-t-bepC"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crm%7B%7Drank%28%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7B3%5Ctimes3%7D%7D%29%3D2%3C3%5C%5C" alt="\rm{}rank(\mathbf{C_{3\times3}})=2<3\\" eeimg="1"> 因此这个方程是可以配平的。</p>
 <p data-pid="jQhnMUOb">上面这个例子有点太过于显然了，那么下面这个呢：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-4808cd2e594995335ad917478b58fe70_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1314" data-rawheight="230" data-original-token="v2-ac83d603c30435ef60a1bb9afcc5a1fd" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-75dfa999a08e5bb13c1ee4ede4e37de8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1314" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-4808cd2e594995335ad917478b58fe70_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p data-pid="g7WIJCsy">乍一看这不就是个磷的歧化吗，怎么就不能配平呢？不妨考察一下系数矩阵：</p>
 <p data-pid="7C59OmO2"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7B4%5Ctimes4%7D%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+2%260%261%261%5C%5C+4%260%261%260%5C%5C+0%262%264%263%5C%5C+0%261%260%264+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\mathbf{C_{4\times4}}=\begin{bmatrix} 2&amp;0&amp;1&amp;1\\ 4&amp;0&amp;1&amp;0\\ 0&amp;2&amp;4&amp;3\\ 0&amp;1&amp;0&amp;4 \end{bmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"> 不难发现：</p>
 <p data-pid="ozjFgvJr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crm%7B%7Drank%28%5Cmathbf%7BC_%7B4%5Ctimes4%7D%7D%29%3D4%5Cnless4%5C%5C" alt="\rm{}rank(\mathbf{C_{4\times4}})=4\nless4\\" eeimg="1"> 因此这个方程式是不可配平的。不过只需要多填一种物质：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-fe8db5cb045eb9c36844b22d79f83b0b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1294" data-rawheight="228" data-original-token="v2-bbbca5a7049d9c265ae819fdc9e26a2e" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3474c5876217722fd4930259b0749117_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1294" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-fe8db5cb045eb9c36844b22d79f83b0b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p data-pid="qyRxa8uR">公司楼下有家馒头店：</p>

 <p data-pid="ig3iut3E">老板统计了一周每日卖出的馒头（为了方便计算和讲解，缩小了数据）：</p>
 <p data-pid="u8SQ9PCr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7Cc%7D+%5Cqquad%5Cqquad%26%5Cqquad%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE%5Cqquad%5C%5C%5Chline%5Ccolor%7BSkyBlue%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%B8%80%7D%26+3+%5C%5C+%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%BA%8C%7D%26+7+%5C%5C+%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7Borange%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%B8%89%7D%264%5C%5C%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7BGoldenrod%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E5%9B%9B%7D%266%5C%5C+%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%BA%94%7D%265%5C%5C%5Cend%7Barray%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{array}{c|c} \qquad\qquad&amp;\qquad销售\qquad\\\hline\color{SkyBlue}{周一}&amp; 3 \\ \hline \color{blue}{周二}&amp; 7 \\ \hline \color{orange}{周三}&amp;4\\\hline \color{Goldenrod}{周四}&amp;6\\ \hline \color{green}{周五}&amp;5\\\end{array}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Psx2H45i">均值为：</p>
 <p data-pid="DYoShuI0"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BX%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B3%2B7%2B4%2B6%2B5%7D%7B5%7D%3D5%5C%5C" alt="\overline{X}=\frac{3+7+4+6+5}{5}=5\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="VbvnkcQ2">按道理讲均值是不错的选择（参见“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/818.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">如何理解最小二乘法？</a>”），但是如果每天准备5个馒头的话，从统计表来看，至少有两天不够卖，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=40%5C%25" alt="40\%" eeimg="1"> 的时间不够卖：</p>
 <p data-pid="05TtSxDy"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7Cc%7D%5Cqquad%5Cqquad%26%5Cqquad%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE%5Cqquad%26%5Cquad%E5%A4%87%E8%B4%A7%E4%BA%94%E4%B8%AA%5C%5C%5Chline%5Ccolor%7BSkyBlue%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%B8%80%7D%26+3+%5C%5C%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%BA%8C%7D%26+7%26%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%E4%B8%8D%E5%A4%9F%7D+%5C%5C+%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7Borange%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%B8%89%7D%264%5C%5C+%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7BGoldenrod%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E5%9B%9B%7D%266%26%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%E4%B8%8D%E5%A4%9F%7D%5C%5C%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%BA%94%7D%265%5C%5C%5Cend%7Barray%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{array}{c|c}\qquad\qquad&amp;\qquad销售\qquad&amp;\quad备货五个\\\hline\color{SkyBlue}{周一}&amp; 3 \\\hline \color{blue}{周二}&amp; 7&amp;\color{red}{不够} \\ \hline \color{orange}{周三}&amp;4\\ \hline \color{Goldenrod}{周四}&amp;6&amp;\color{red}{不够}\\\hline \color{green}{周五}&amp;5\\\end{array}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="MyKe6VEE">你“甜在心馒头店”又不是小米，搞什么饥饿营销啊？老板当然也知道这一点，就拿起纸笔来开始思考。</p>
 <p data-pid="r2dF0XzH"><b>2 老板的思考</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ujGJMB7e">老板尝试把营业时间抽象为一根线段，把这段时间用<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 来表示：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a03c893a0e32dedcf58aad215a596000_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="628" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-34bd0f592979215d7cf2b6ac22822632" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-25bcae9a680b1bfbe56d4a6714a1f097_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="628" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a03c893a0e32dedcf58aad215a596000_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="uo1b02is">然后把<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7BSkyBlue%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%B8%80%7D" alt="\color{SkyBlue}{周一}" eeimg="1"> 的三个馒头（“甜在心馒头”，有褶子的馒头）按照销售时间放在线段上：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-063eefa202cdc1201967561c69643bed_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="628" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-b8ff68f865cf0462ae7980cb689db935" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-84ace0ee8618fd90a22cab1b5a47c255_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="628" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-063eefa202cdc1201967561c69643bed_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="1NgeMIFR">把<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 均分为四个时间段：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-8eccfd812ec54eefe8c6a21127512778_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="588" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-cfd9125f93b1a48b41e63be57af2573d" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0e2701a6ede004ea5d52253eff24d2b4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="588" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-8eccfd812ec54eefe8c6a21127512778_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="VSmiKBwK">此时，在每一个时间段上，要不卖出了（一个）馒头，要不没有卖出：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-1fec3572490eecdb1f620546fecf4b87_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="588" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-3cc15dbb5a4792b71a58fc770e05ea43" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f05bf66cc5dad673ec42b0ad11f233a7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="588" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-1fec3572490eecdb1f620546fecf4b87_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="h_3dii7i">在每个时间段，就有点像抛硬币，要不是正面（卖出），要不是反面（没有卖出）：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-632be81e6b73e6eb3637bfff70e60a3d_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="500" data-rawheight="210" data-original-token="v2-632be81e6b73e6eb3637bfff70e60a3d" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-632be81e6b73e6eb3637bfff70e60a3d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="500" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-632be81e6b73e6eb3637bfff70e60a3d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="XBxAoWIX"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 内卖出3个馒头的概率，就和抛了4次硬币（4个时间段），其中3次正面（卖出3个）的概率一样了。</p>
 <p data-pid="jWA8rr7L">这样的概率通过二项分布来计算就是：</p>
 <p data-pid="_8knMKmR"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbinom%7B4%7D%7B3%7Dp%5E3%281-p%29%5E1%5C%5C" alt="\binom{4}{3}p^3(1-p)^1\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="G_b6eshy">但是，如果把<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%E5%91%A8%E4%BA%8C%7D" alt="\color{blue}{周二}" eeimg="1"> 的七个馒头放在线段上，分成四段就不够了：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b2359584d6ac471f2eea581c33d1cf77_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="588" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-6f8a5a2322226d26b514f944f0e57802" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-887f68fe65b26e57d7a5f1199dca5df3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="588" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-b2359584d6ac471f2eea581c33d1cf77_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="RL7KFApz">从图中看，每个时间段，有卖出3个的，有卖出2个的，有卖出1个的，就不再是单纯的“卖出、没卖出”了。不能套用二项分布了。</p>
 <p data-pid="kbOJnSoT">解决这个问题也很简单，把<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 分为20个时间段，那么每个时间段就又变为了抛硬币：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-f665f9b2df9793c5417afa62e5684b14_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-57f9a35aec83d1758a9791c69941d576" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-7bf81392e67cd5d975b864f036d8891b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="600" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-f665f9b2df9793c5417afa62e5684b14_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="oUm-uiA2">这样，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 内卖出7个馒头的概率就是（相当于抛了20次硬币，出现7次正面）：</p>
 <p data-pid="Cv2uKsRY"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbinom%7B20%7D%7B7%7Dp%5E7%281-p%29%5E%7B13%7D%5C%5C" alt="\binom{20}{7}p^7(1-p)^{13}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Y1YrxrWB">为了保证在一个时间段内只会发生“卖出、没卖出”，干脆把时间切成<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 份：</p>
 <p data-pid="dno2ZuyF"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7B7%7Dp%5E7%281-p%29%5E%7Bn-7%7D%5C%5C" alt="\binom{n}{7}p^7(1-p)^{n-7}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="U9sRAyKD">越细越好，用极限来表示：</p>
 <p data-pid="o19w3U8n"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7B7%7Dp%5E7%281-p%29%5E%7Bn-7%7D%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\binom{n}{7}p^7(1-p)^{n-7}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="a6Cg8QN5">更抽象一点，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 时刻内卖出<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> 个馒头的概率为：</p>
 <p data-pid="_MzzH1J_"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7Bk%7Dp%5Ek%281-p%29%5E%7Bn-k%7D%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\binom{n}{k}p^k(1-p)^{n-k}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="xUA20lAn"><b>3</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p" alt="p" eeimg="1"> <b>的计算</b></p>
 <p data-pid="c9S8n-v3">“那么”，老板用笔敲了敲桌子，“只剩下一个问题，概率<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p" alt="p" eeimg="1"> 怎么求？”</p>
 <p data-pid="-mrQ3yOC">在上面的假设下，问题已经被转为了二项分布。二项分布的期望为：</p>
 <p data-pid="85d3AZeO"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%28X%29%3Dnp%3D%5Cmu%5C%5C" alt="E(X)=np=\mu\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="zG6L9MpW">那么：</p>
 <p data-pid="WT0Tp0kc"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5C%5C" alt="p=\frac{\mu}{n}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="nu0p5y4R"><b>4 泊松分布</b></p>
 <p data-pid="aTCGY1T8">有了<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D" alt="p=\frac{\mu}{n}" eeimg="1"> 了之后，就有：</p>
 <p data-pid="I8TPmSIA"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7Bk%7Dp%5Ek%281-p%29%5E%7Bn-k%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7Bk%7D%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5Ek%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E%7Bn-k%7D%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\binom{n}{k}p^k(1-p)^{n-k}=\lim_{n\to\infty}\binom{n}{k}\left(\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^k(1-\frac{\mu}{n})^{n-k}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5xiWlyHX">我们来算一下这个极限：</p>
 <p data-pid="_lPKuD97"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%7D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7Bk%7D%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5Ek%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E%7Bn-k%7D%26%3D+%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bn%28n-1%29%28n-2%29%5Ccdots%28n-k%2B1%29%7D%7Bk%21%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%5Ek%7D%7Bn%5Ek%7D%5Cleft%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7Bn-k%7D%5C%5C+%26%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%5Ek%7D%7Bk%21%7D%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7Bn%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Ccdots%5Cfrac%7Bn-k%2B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cleft%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7B-k%7D%5Cleft%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5En%5Cend%7Balign%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align}\lim_{n\to\infty}\binom{n}{k}\left(\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^k(1-\frac{\mu}{n})^{n-k}&amp;= \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{n(n-1)(n-2)\cdots(n-k+1)}{k!}\frac{\mu^k}{n^k}\left(1-\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^{n-k}\\ &amp;=\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\mu^k}{k!}\frac{n}{n}\cdot\frac{n-1}{n}\cdots\frac{n-k+1}{n}\left(1-\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^{-k}\left(1-\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^n\end{align}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="XPTlhHrh">其中：</p>
 <p data-pid="1xWIahBP"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7Bn%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Ccdots%5Cfrac%7Bn-k%2B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cleft%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7B-k%7D%3D1%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{n}{n}\cdot\frac{n-1}{n}\cdots\frac{n-k+1}{n}\left(1-\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^{-k}=1\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="09kE-o0f"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty%7D%5Cleft%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5En+%3D+e%5E%7B-%5Cmu%7D%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{n \to \infty}\left(1-\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^n = e^{-\mu}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="0SmgwDt_">所以：</p>
 <p data-pid="x8yqebWw"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cbinom%7Bn%7D%7Bk%7D%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5Ek%281-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E%7Bn-k%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%5Ek%7D%7Bk%21%7De%5E%7B-%5Cmu%7D%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\binom{n}{k}\left(\frac{\mu}{n}\right)^k(1-\frac{\mu}{n})^{n-k}=\frac{\mu^k}{k!}e^{-\mu}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="DFTzK3mk">上面就是泊松分布的概率密度函数，也就是说，在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T" alt="T" eeimg="1"> 时间内卖出<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> 个馒头的概率为：</p>
 <p data-pid="mvRsRxpr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%28X%3Dk%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmu%5Ek%7D%7Bk%21%7De%5E%7B-%5Cmu%7D%5C%5C" alt="P(X=k)=\frac{\mu^k}{k!}e^{-\mu}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="wkwzENBC">一般来说，我们会换一个符号，让<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu%3D%5Clambda" alt="\mu=\lambda" eeimg="1"> ，所以：</p>
 <p data-pid="pNlKhIgI"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%28X%3Dk%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Clambda%5Ek%7D%7Bk%21%7De%5E%7B-%5Clambda%7D%5C%5C" alt="P(X=k)=\frac{\lambda^k}{k!}e^{-\lambda}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="lLzReyMi">这就是教科书中的泊松分布的概率密度函数。</p>
 <p data-pid="pFXN-w6X"><b>5 馒头店的问题的解决</b></p>
 <p data-pid="m-1J8fae">老板依然蹙眉，不知道<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmu" alt="\mu" eeimg="1"> 啊？</p>
 <p data-pid="DcfrdzkV">没关系，刚才不是计算了样本均值：</p>
 <p data-pid="t8ylifjg"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BX%7D%3D5%5C%5C" alt="\overline{X}=5\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="0aIG6rYN">可以用它来近似：</p>
 <p data-pid="JRuaTixW"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BX%7D%5Capprox%5Cmu%5C%5C" alt="\overline{X}\approx\mu\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="4NKU5CFP">于是：</p>
 <p data-pid="pNirWOl-"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%28X%3Dk%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B5%5Ek%7D%7Bk%21%7De%5E%7B-5%7D%5C%5C" alt="P(X=k)=\frac{5^k}{k!}e^{-5}\\" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="Iuq4APpb">可以看到，如果每天准备8个馒头的话，那么足够卖的概率就是把前8个的概率加起来：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">

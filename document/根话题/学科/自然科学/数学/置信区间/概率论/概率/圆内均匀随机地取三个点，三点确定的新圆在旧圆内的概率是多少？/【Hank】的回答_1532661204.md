 <p data-pid="60UziZmw"><b>1.总体思路</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hYTsR9vp">在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维空间中取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1" alt="n+1" eeimg="1"> 个点，总的样本空间是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%28n%2B1%29" alt="n(n+1)" eeimg="1"> 维空间中的某个区域 ，其上的概率密度为常数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7BV_n%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D" alt="\frac{1}{V_n^{n+1}}" eeimg="1"> ，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_n" alt="V_n" eeimg="1"> 是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维单位球的体积。满足题中条件的样本构成样本空间的一个子集 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q" alt="Q" eeimg="1"> ，我们只要求出其 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维体积即可得到对应的概率。现在的问题是要直接在原来的坐标系下描述这个集合比较困难，想求体积就必须换一个方式来刻画（换元），从而得到较为规则的积分区域和较为容易计算的积分。我的方法与 <a class="member_mention" href="https://www.zhihu.com/people/9c17dcb2e59bf98c5ff986be8612b0a7" data-hash="9c17dcb2e59bf98c5ff986be8612b0a7" data-hovercard="p$b$9c17dcb2e59bf98c5ff986be8612b0a7">@Ao Sun</a> 的方法类似，但又稍有不同：</p>
 <p data-pid="lNqCO09S">注意到任意两个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维球之间都构成位似关系，且一般有两个位似中心，对应的位似比一正一负。考虑正的那个位似中心，我们发现<b>两球为包含关系当且仅当该位似中心在大球内部</b>。因此我们可以用球内的位似中心 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_0" alt="\mathbf{x}_0" eeimg="1"> ，位似比 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda" alt="\lambda" eeimg="1"> 以及球面上的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1" alt="n+1" eeimg="1"> 个点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_1%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="\mathbf{x}_1,\cdots,\mathbf{x}_{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 来表示满足条件的点：</p>
 <p data-pid="KTvRYVkS"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_i%3D%5Clambda+%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_i%2B%5Cleft%281-%5Clambda%5Cright%29%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_0%2Ci%3D1%2C2%2C%5Ccdots%2Cn%2B1" alt="\mathbf{y}_i=\lambda \mathbf{x}_i+\left(1-\lambda\right)\mathbf{x}_0,i=1,2,\cdots,n+1" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="0s8_VfWd">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_0%5Cin+B_n%2C%5Clambda%5Cin%5B0%2C1%5D%2C%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_i%5Cin+S_n" alt="\mathbf{x}_0\in B_n,\lambda\in[0,1],\mathbf{x}_i\in S_n" eeimg="1"> . 除去测度为零的退化情况外，该表示方法恰好一一对应了所有满足条件的情形，因此我们可以将 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Q" alt="Q" eeimg="1"> 的体积写成</p>
 <p data-pid="Egy3SugN"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_Q%3D%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_0%5Cin+B_n%7DdV%5Cint_0%5E1d%5Clambda%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_1%5Cin+S_n%7DdS_1%5Ccdots%5Cint_%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D_%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Cin+S_n%7DdS_%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_1%5Ccdots+d%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7Bx_0%7Dd%5Clambda+dS_1%5Ccdots+dS_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5Cright%7C" alt="V_Q=\int_{\mathbf{x}_0\in B_n}dV\int_0^1d\lambda\int_{\mathbf{x}_1\in S_n}dS_1\cdots\int_{\mathbf{x}_{n+1}\in S_n}dS_{n+1}\left|\frac{d\mathbf{y}_1\cdots d\mathbf{y}_{n+1}}{d\mathbf{x_0}d\lambda dS_1\cdots dS_{n+1}}\right|" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="UG_duh8P"><b>2.初遇难关</b></p>

 <p data-pid="YzZvRdGk">这当然不是手算出来的，否则我已经吐血翻白眼不省人事了。观察二维和三维的两个式子，很容易发现带 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda" alt="\lambda" eeimg="1"> 的因子有很简单的规律：前 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 行都带 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1-%5Clambda" alt="1-\lambda" eeimg="1"> ，后面有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E2-1" alt="n^2-1" eeimg="1"> 行带 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda" alt="\lambda" eeimg="1"> ，于是贡献的因子为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clambda%5E%7Bn%5E2-1%7D%5Cleft%281-%5Clambda%5Cright%29%5En" alt="\lambda^{n^2-1}\left(1-\lambda\right)^n" eeimg="1"> 。然而剩下的部分才是重头戏所在，我们看这个因子</p>

 <p data-pid="yMD5MxK_">一个大胆的猜想浮上心头：<b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维情形下的这个因子，是否恰好是球面上 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1" alt="n+1" eeimg="1"> 个点构成的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维单形（三角形、四面体等在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维空间中的对应物）的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维体积的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%21" alt="n!" eeimg="1"> 倍？</b>写成数学语言就是</p>
 <p data-pid="LVeuzwxH"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft.%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_1%5Ccdots+d%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7Bx_0%7Dd%5Clambda+dS_1%5Ccdots+dS_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5Cright%7C%5Cmiddle%2F%5Clambda%5E%7Bn%5E2-1%7D%281-%5Clambda%29%5En%5Cright.%3Dn%21V_%7BX_1X_2%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5C%5C%3D%5Cleft%7C%5Cdet%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+x_%7B1%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7B1%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots+++%26+x_%7B1%2Cn%7D+%26+1+++%5C%5C+x_%7B2%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7B2%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots+++%26+x_%7B2%2Cn%7D+%26+1++%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots++%26+%5Cddots+++%26+%5Cvdots++%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+x_%7Bn%2B1%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots%5C++%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2Cn%7D+%26+1+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%5Cright%7C" alt="\left.\left|\frac{d\mathbf{y}_1\cdots d\mathbf{y}_{n+1}}{d\mathbf{x_0}d\lambda dS_1\cdots dS_{n+1}}\right|\middle/\lambda^{n^2-1}(1-\lambda)^n\right.=n!V_{X_1X_2\cdots X_{n+1}}\\=\left|\det\begin{bmatrix} x_{1,1} &amp; x_{1,2}  &amp; \cdots   &amp; x_{1,n} &amp; 1   \\ x_{2,1} &amp; x_{2,2}  &amp; \cdots   &amp; x_{2,n} &amp; 1  \\ \vdots &amp; \vdots  &amp; \ddots   &amp; \vdots  &amp; \vdots \\ x_{n+1,1} &amp; x_{n+1,2}  &amp; \cdots\  &amp; x_{n+1,n} &amp; 1 \\ \end{bmatrix}\right|" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="KGwbsuE6"><b>4.小心求证</b></p>
 <p data-pid="13WxfHe9">要证明上面的猜想，说简单不简单，说难其实也不难。由于我们要求的是两个体积元之比，它们各自都有良好的定义，因此这个比值与我们采用的坐标系统无关。只要我们使用 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%28n%2B1%29" alt="n(n+1)" eeimg="1"> 个参数来描述，并且在某点附近不退化，那么尽管对应的雅可比行列式会随着坐标系选取而改变，该点处两个体积元的比值却是一个不变量。因此，我们可以选择一个用起来最舒服的坐标系来计算。</p>

 <p data-pid="P3VS1tk8"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cleft.%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_1%5Ccdots+d%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7Bx_0%7Dd%5Clambda+dS_1%5Ccdots+dS_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5Cright%7C%5Cmiddle%2F%5Clambda%5E%7Bn%5E2-1%7D%281-%5Clambda%29%5En%5Cright.%5C%5C%3D%26%5Cleft%7C%5Cdet%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+x_%7B1%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7B2%2C1%7D++%26+%5Ccdots+++%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2C1%7D+++%5C%5C+x_%7B1%2C2%7D+%26+x_%7B2%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots+++%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2C2%7D++%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots++%26+%5Cddots+++%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+x_%7B1%2Cn%7D+%26+x_%7B2%2Cn%7D++%26+%5Ccdots%5C++%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2Cn%7D%5C%5C1%261%26%5Ccdots%261+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%5Cright%7C+%5C%5C%3D%26%5Cleft%7C%5Cdet%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+x_%7B1%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7B1%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots+++%26+x_%7B1%2Cn%7D+%26+1+++%5C%5C+x_%7B2%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7B2%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots+++%26+x_%7B2%2Cn%7D+%26+1++%5C%5C+%5Cvdots+%26+%5Cvdots++%26+%5Cddots+++%26+%5Cvdots++%26+%5Cvdots+%5C%5C+x_%7Bn%2B1%2C1%7D+%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2C2%7D++%26+%5Ccdots%5C++%26+x_%7Bn%2B1%2Cn%7D+%26+1+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%5Cright%7C+%3Dn%21V_%7BX_1X_2%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\left.\left|\frac{d\mathbf{y}_1\cdots d\mathbf{y}_{n+1}}{d\mathbf{x_0}d\lambda dS_1\cdots dS_{n+1}}\right|\middle/\lambda^{n^2-1}(1-\lambda)^n\right.\\=&amp;\left|\det\begin{bmatrix} x_{1,1} &amp;  <p data-pid="RnFw3fcE"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C%5Cfrac%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_1%5Ccdots+d%5Cmathbf%7By%7D_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Bd%5Cmathbf%7Bx_0%7Dd%5Clambda+dS_1%5Ccdots+dS_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5Cright%7C%3D%5Clambda%5E%7Bn%5E2-1%7D%281-%5Clambda%29%5En+n%21V_%7BX_1X_2%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D" alt="\left|\frac{d\mathbf{y}_1\cdots d\mathbf{y}_{n+1}}{d\mathbf{x_0}d\lambda dS_1\cdots dS_{n+1}}\right|=\lambda^{n^2-1}(1-\lambda)^n n!V_{X_1X_2\cdots X_{n+1}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5gW95fzu">将其代入最初的积分式，把所有变量积掉，我们就得到了一个看起来比较简单的式子：</p>
 <p data-pid="dx_Rd88G"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_Q%3DV_nS_n%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Cfrac%7B%28n%5E2-1%29%21n%21%7D%7B%28n%5E2%2Bn%29%21%7Dn%21%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D" alt="V_Q=V_nS_n^{n+1}\frac{(n^2-1)!n!}{(n^2+n)!}n!\overline{V}_{X_1\cdots X_{n+1}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="CaMwt1xN"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_n%3D%5Cfrac%7BV_Q%7D%7BV_n%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bn%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D%28n%21%29%5E2%5Cpi%5E%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%28n%5E2-1%29%21%7D%7B%28n%5E2%2Bn%29%21%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%21%7D%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D" alt="P_n=\frac{V_Q}{V_n^{n+1}}=\frac{n^{n+1}(n!)^2\pi^\frac{n}{2}(n^2-1)!}{(n^2+n)!\left(\frac{n}{2}\right)!}\overline{V}_{X_1\cdots X_{n+1}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="UPXZXdnp">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_n%2CS_n" alt="V_n,S_n" eeimg="1"> 为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维单位球的体积和表面积， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D" alt="\overline{V}_{X_1\cdots X_{n+1}}" eeimg="1"> 为单形 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="X_1\cdots X_{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 的平均体积。看起来是不是有点结果那味了？有一部分因子已经出来了。</p>
 <p data-pid="-4JhQugX">于是乎，我们将原问题转化为了一个全新的问题：<b>在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维单位球表面独立、（按面积）均匀地取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1" alt="n+1" eeimg="1"> 个随机点，它们构成的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维单形的体积期望是多少？</b>这个问题看起来简化了一些，毕竟"体积"比之"外接球球心"来说要好处理得多，但依然不容易，解决之路依然漫长。</p>
 <p data-pid="9KhVLENp"><b>5.准备工作</b></p>
 <p data-pid="YryfBv2l">在介绍新问题的解决方法之前，我们需要先做一些准备工作，以便接下来的过程能够被顺畅地阅读。</p>
 <p data-pid="qkQtmQs0">首先定义Beta函数，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%2Cb" alt="a,b" eeimg="1"> 为正实数：</p>
 <p data-pid="uo3U0FZv"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%28a%2Cb%29%3D%5Cint_0%5E1x%5E%7Ba-1%7D%281-x%29%5E%7Bb-1%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5CGamma%28a%29%5CGamma%28b%29%7D%7B%5CGamma%28a%2Bb%29%7D" alt="B(a,b)=\int_0^1x^{a-1}(1-x)^{b-1}dx=\frac{\Gamma(a)\Gamma(b)}{\Gamma(a+b)}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="jqTFoRLG">再定义Beta分布的概率密度函数，这是一个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D" alt="[0,1]" eeimg="1"> 上的分布：</p>
 <p data-pid="-XpUPCRM"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta%28x%3Ba%2Cb%29%3D%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E%7Ba-1%7D%281-x%29%5E%7Bb-1%7D%7D%7BB%28a%2Cb%29%7D%2C0%5Cle+x%5Cle+1" alt="\beta(x;a,b)=\frac{x^{a-1}(1-x)^{b-1}}{B(a,b)},0\le x\le 1" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="H8l9YV8f">之所以要特别提出Beta分布，是因为其与球内的分布有着十分密切的联系，以至于接下来我们会不止一次遇到这个分布。</p>
 <p data-pid="bZwtXl5l">一个Beta分布密度函数在另一个Beta分布下的期望值为：</p>
 <p data-pid="IqJnufKj"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E_%7Bx%5Csim+Beta%28a%2Cb%29%7D%5Cleft%5B%5Cbeta%28x%3Bc%2Cd%29%5Cright%5D%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cbeta%28x%3Ba%2Cb%29%5Cbeta%28x%3Bc%2Cd%29dx%3D%5Cint_0%5E1%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E%7Ba%2Bc-2%7D%281-x%29%5E%7Bb%2Bd-2%7D%7D%7BB%28a%2Cb%29B%28c%2Cd%29%7Ddx%3D%5Cfrac%7BB%28a%2Bc-1%2Cb%2Bd-1%29%7D%7BB%28a%2Cb%29B%28c%2Cd%29%7D" alt="E_{x\sim Beta(a,b)}\left[\beta(x;c,d)\right]=\int_0^1\beta(x;a,b)\beta(x;c,d)dx=\int_0^1\frac{x^{a+c-2}(1-x)^{b+d-2}}{B(a,b)B(c,d)}dx=\frac{B(a+c-1,b+d-1)}{B(a,b)B(c,d)}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="XGYX32xn">在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 维单位球内随机取一个点，其半径 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r" alt="r" eeimg="1"> 是取值在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D" alt="[0,1]" eeimg="1"> 上的随机变量，累积分布函数为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=F_n%28r%29%3Dr%5En" alt="F_n(r)=r^n" eeimg="1"> ，概率密度函数为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_n%28r%29%3Dnr%5E%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Cbeta%28r%3Bn%2C1%29" alt="f_n(r)=nr^{n-1}=\beta(r;n,1)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="TkMw6Db-"><b>6.叠 叠 乐</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZkJl0-Cv">现在我们已经做好了准备工作，可以开始向着答案进军了。作为第一步，考虑一个更简化的问题：<b>将其中一个点固定到原点处</b>，则形成的单形的体积期望是多少？这个问题比原问题的简单之处在于，有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 条棱是经过球心的，因此它们张成的平面等也经过球心，使得计算有章可循。</p>
 <p data-pid="G6ixXCoE">考虑依次在球面上选取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个随机点，现在已经选取了 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt="m" eeimg="1"> 个的情况。记这 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt="m" eeimg="1"> 个点为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%2CX_2%2C%5Ccdots%2CX_m" alt="X_1,X_2,\cdots,X_m" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow%7BOX%7D_1%2C%5Coverrightarrow%7BOX%7D_2%2C%5Ccdots%2C%5Coverrightarrow%7BOX%7D_m" alt="\overrightarrow{OX}_1,\overrightarrow{OX}_2,\cdots,\overrightarrow{OX}_m" eeimg="1"> 张成一个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt="m" eeimg="1"> 维的子空间 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=W" alt="W" eeimg="1"> 。现在考虑第 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2B1" alt="m+1" eeimg="1"> 个点，我们将其位矢分解为垂直和平行分量 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverrightarrow%7BOX%7D_%7Bm%2B1%7D%3D%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%7B%5Cperp%7D%2B%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_W" alt="\overrightarrow{OX}_{m+1}=\mathbf{r}_{\perp}+\mathbf{r}_W" eeimg="1"> ，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_W%5Cin+W%2C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cin+W%5E%5Cperp" alt="\mathbf{r}_W\in W,\mathbf{r}_\perp\in W^\perp" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bd70abc896aae78e26bf8114b21886b7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="368" data-rawheight="430" data-original-token="v2-c41172efc904e5a5380209c3777e234c" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-981370c89f74d2bd96c51cb7d7f47bcb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="368">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="idbfdp_A">那么 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2B1" alt="m+1" eeimg="1"> 维单形 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bm%2B1%7D" alt="OX_1\cdots X_{m+1}" eeimg="1"> 的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m%2B1" alt="m+1" eeimg="1"> 维体积为</p>
 <p data-pid="jbUyLN5S"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bm%2B1%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7CV_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_m%7D%7D%7Bm%2B1%7D" alt="V_{OX_1\cdots X_{m+1}}=\frac{\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|V_{OX_1\cdots X_m}}{m+1}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5Ec4biCM">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_m%7D" alt="V_{OX_1\cdots X_m}" eeimg="1"> 为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt="m" eeimg="1"> 维单形 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=OX_1%5Ccdots+X_m" alt="OX_1\cdots X_m" eeimg="1"> 的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt="m" eeimg="1"> 维体积。这其实就是底乘高的高维推广。注意到分子中的两项是独立的：无论前 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=m" alt="m" eeimg="1"> 个点如何分布，由于球对称性，都不会影响 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C" alt="\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|" eeimg="1"> 的分布。对于独立的随机变量，积的期望等于期望的积，因此我们有</p>
 <p data-pid="JOhZXU1I"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bm%2B1%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B%5Coverline%7B%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%7D%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_m%7D%7D%7Bm%2B1%7D" alt="\overline{V}_{OX_1\cdots X_{m+1}}=\frac{\overline{\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|}\overline{V}_{OX_1\cdots X_m}}{m+1}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="b225eVOZ">下面我们来求 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7B%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%7D" alt="\overline{\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|}" eeimg="1"> 的值。为此我们首先考虑 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_%7Bm%2B1%7D" alt="X_{m+1}" eeimg="1"> 在球内而不是球面上均匀分布的情况。</p>
 <p data-pid="H23vxJB0">对于任意 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%3Dr%3C1" alt="\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|=r<1" eeimg="1"> 的情形，其 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_W" alt="\mathbf{r}_W" eeimg="1"> 满足 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_W%5Cright%7C%3C%5Csqrt%7B1-r%5E2%7D" alt="\left|\mathbf{r}_W\right|<\sqrt{1-r^2}" eeimg="1"> ，且体积元 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D%3Dd%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp+d%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_W" alt="d\mathbf{r}=d\mathbf{r}_\perp d\mathbf{r}_W" eeimg="1"> ，因此若取总体为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_%7Bn-m%7D%5Cotimes+B_m" alt="B_{n-m}\otimes B_m" eeimg="1"> 上的均匀分布，则</p>
 <p data-pid="m22G69SH"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+P%5Cleft%28r%3C%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%3Cr%2Bdr%2C%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D%5Cright%7C%3C1%5Cright%29%3D%26P_%7Bn-m%7D%5Cleft%28r%3C%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%3Cr%2Bdr%5Cright%29P_m%5Cleft%28%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_W%5Cright%7C%3C%5Csqrt%7B1-r%5E2%7D%5Cright%29%5C%5C%5Cpropto%26f_%7Bn-m%7D%28r%29drF_m%5Cleft%28%5Csqrt%7B1-r%5E2%7D%5Cright%29%5C%5C%3D%26nr%5E%7Bn-m-1%7D%5Cleft%281-r%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%7Bm%2F2%7Ddr+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D" alt="\begin{align*} P\left(r<\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|<r+dr,\left|\mathbf{r}\right|<1\right)=&amp;P_{n-m}\left(r<\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|<r+dr\right)P_m\left(\left|\mathbf{r}_W\right|<\sqrt{1-r^2}\right)\\\propto&amp;f_{n-m}(r)drF_m\left(\sqrt{1-r^2}\right)\\=&amp;nr^{n-m-1}\left(1-r^2\right)^{m/2}dr \end{align*}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="yb28oflb">从而有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r" alt="r" eeimg="1"> 的概率密度</p>
 <p data-pid="k_zubRRX"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+f_%7Bn%2Cm%7D%5Cleft%28r%5Cright%29%3D%26%5Cfrac%7BP%5Cleft%28r%3C%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%3Cr%2Bdr%5Cmid%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D%5Cright%7C%3C1%5Cright%29%7D%7Bdr%7D%5C%5C%3D%26%5Cfrac%7BP%5Cleft%28r%3C%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D_%5Cperp%5Cright%7C%3Cr%2Bdr%2C%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D%5Cright%7C%3C1%5Cright%29%7D%7BP%5Cleft%28%5Cleft%7C%5Cmathbf%7Br%7D%5Cright%7C%3C1%5Cright%29dr%7D%5C%5C%5Cpropto%26r%5E%7Bn-m-1%7D%5Cleft%281-r%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%7Bm%2F2%7D+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D" alt="\begin{align*} f_{n,m}\left(r\right)=&amp;\frac{P\left(r<\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|<r+dr\mid\left|\mathbf{r}\right|<1\right)}{dr}\\=&amp;\frac{P\left(r<\left|\mathbf{r}_\perp\right|<r+dr,\left|\mathbf{r}\right|<1\right)}{P\left(\left|\mathbf{r}\right|<1\right)dr}\\\propto&amp;r^{n-m-1}\left(1-r^2\right)^{m/2} \end{align*}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="A9HqRlRF">于是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5E2" alt="r^2" eeimg="1"> 的概率密度为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_%7Bn%2Cm%7D%28r%5E2%29%3Df_%7Bn%2Cm%7D%28r%29%5Cfrac%7Bdr%7D%7Bd%28r%5E2%29%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bf_%7Bn%2Cm%7D%28r%29%7D%7B2r%7D%5Cpropto+%28r%5E2%29%5E%7Bn%2F2-m%2F2-1%7D%5Cleft%281-r%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%7Bm%2F2%7D" alt="f_{n,m}(r^2)=f_{n,m}(r)\frac{dr}{d(r^2)}=\frac{f_{n,m}(r)}{2r}\propto (r^2)^{n/2-m/2-1}\left(1-r^2\right)^{m/2}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="9UEm89V2">至于常数系数我们不关心，因为从上面的式子我们已经足以看出 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5E2" alt="r^2" eeimg="1"> 服从Beta分布，即</p>
 <p data-pid="lZ3R1CLH"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%5E2+%5Csim+Beta%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bn-m%7D%7B2%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7Bm%7D%7B2%7D%2B1%5Cright%29" alt="r^2 \sim Beta\left(\frac{n-m}{2},\frac{m}{2}+1\right)" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="3xANEMI5">接下来我们想办法将其转化为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_%7Bm%2B1%7D" alt="X_{m+1}" eeimg="1"> 在球面上均匀分布的情况。对于函数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D%29" alt="g(\mathbf{x})" eeimg="1"> ，如果</p>

 <p data-pid="WcuMecgB">其中每一项的正负号是我们主要关心的点。在什么情况下这个符号会变成负的？稍微想象一下就能得到答案： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BO%5Cleft%5C%7BX_1%2C%5Ccdots%2CX_%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Cright%5C%7D%5Csetminus%5C%7BX_i%5C%7D%7D" alt="V_{O\left\{X_1,\cdots,X_{n+1}\right\}\setminus\{X_i\}}" eeimg="1"> 的符号是负的，当且仅当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O%2CX_i" alt="O,X_i" eeimg="1"> 在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%5C%7BX_1%2C%5Ccdots%2CX_%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Cright%5C%7D%5Csetminus%5C%7BX_i%5C%7D" alt="\left\{X_1,\cdots,X_{n+1}\right\}\setminus\{X_i\}" eeimg="1"> 构成的超平面异侧。我们可以以此作为出发点。</p>
 <p data-pid="4VoWU3vW">将 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%5Ccdots+X_n" alt="X_1\cdots X_n" eeimg="1"> 构成的平面作为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n%3DH%3E0" alt="x_n=H>0" eeimg="1"> 的平面，则我们关心的是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="X_{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 在垂直方向上的坐标，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3Dx_%7Bn%2B1%2Cn%7D" alt="h=x_{n+1,n}" eeimg="1"> 。若 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3EH" alt="h>H" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D" alt="V_{OX_1\cdots X_n}" eeimg="1"> 贡献为负号，反之为正号。现在我们考虑 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="X_{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 的对踵点，也就是以球心为中心的对称点。显然 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="X_{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-X_%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="-X_{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 处的概率密度是相同的，而对于两种情况的平均体积来说，若 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%7Ch%5Cright%7C%3E%5Cleft%7CH%5Cright%7C" alt="\left|h\right|>\left|H\right|" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D" alt="V_{OX_1\cdots X_n}" eeimg="1"> 的贡献正负抵消，为零，否则为一。若能得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H" alt="H" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h" alt="h" eeimg="1"> 的分布，以及"底面积" <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=S_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D" alt="S_{X_1\cdots X_n}" eeimg="1"> 的概率分布，就能计算出 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D" alt="V_{OX_1\cdots X_n}" eeimg="1"> 对体积期望的总贡献。而 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%2B1" alt="n+1" eeimg="1"> 个单形对总体积的贡献必然是相等的，因此我们可以写出体积期望的表达式：</p>
 <p data-pid="KZGbOwHG"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%3D%28n%2B1%29%5Cint_0%5E1P%5Cleft%28%5Cleft%7Ch%5Cright%7C%3Ct%5Cright%29f_H%28t%29%5Cfrac%7BtE%5Cleft%28S_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D%5Cmid+H%3Dt%5Cright%29%7D%7Bn%7Ddt" alt="\overline{V}_{X_1\cdots X_{n+1}}=(n+1)\int_0^1P\left(\left|h\right|<t\right)f_H(t)\frac{tE\left(S_{X_1\cdots X_n}\mid H=t\right)}{n}dt" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="lj1NCKD_">同样的，我们可以由此写出 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D" alt="V_{OX_1\cdots X_n}" eeimg="1"> 的体积期望：</p>
 <p data-pid="QMqlIbBu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coverline%7BV%7D_%7BOX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D%3D%5Cint_0%5E1f_H%28t%29%5Cfrac%7BtE%5Cleft%28S_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D%5Cmid+H%3Dt%5Cright%29%7D%7Bn%7Ddt" alt="\overline{V}_{OX_1\cdots X_n}=\int_0^1f_H(t)\frac{tE\left(S_{X_1\cdots X_n}\mid H=t\right)}{n}dt" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="X3ir262E">两个式子看起来非常相似，不过我们仍需进行进一步的计算才能发现两者之间的联系。</p>
 <p data-pid="UiJX7SL8">球面上 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3Cx_n%3Ch%2Bdh" alt="h<x_n<h+dh" eeimg="1"> 的面元为</p>
 <p data-pid="3JdOTMtD"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dS%3D%5Cleft%281-h%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7B2%7DS_%7Bn-1%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bdh%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B1-h%5E2%7D%7D%3D%5Cleft%281-h%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%5Cfrac%7Bn-3%7D%7B2%7DS_%7Bn-1%7Ddh" alt="dS=\left(1-h^2\right)^\frac{n-1}{2}S_{n-1}\cdot\frac{dh}{\sqrt{1-h^2}}=\left(1-h^2\right)^\frac{n-3}{2}S_{n-1}dh" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="vv-pN__G">从这个形式中我们能发现 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%5E2%5Csim+Beta%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29" alt="h^2\sim Beta\left(\frac{1}{2},\frac{n-1}{2}\right)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="So8sMMUj">于是</p>
 <p data-pid="8O3dtmLX"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_%7Bh%5E2%7D%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7Bx%5E%7B-1%2F2%7D%281-x%29%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7Bn-3%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7D%7BB%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%7D%2C0%5Cle+x%5Cle+1" alt="f_{h^2}(x)=\frac{x^{-1/2}(1-x)^{\frac{n-3}{2}}}{B\left(\frac{1}{2},\frac{n-1}{2}\right)},0\le x\le 1" eeimg="1"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%7Ch%7C%29%3D2%7Ch%7Cf_%7Bh%5E2%7D%28h%5E2%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%7Ch%7C%5Cleft%28h%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%7B-1%2F2%7D%5Cleft%281-h%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%5Cfrac%7Bn-3%7D%7B2%7D%7D%7BB%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%2C%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%5Cleft%281-h%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%5Cfrac%7Bn-3%7D%7B2%7D%5CGamma%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%5Cpi%7D%5CGamma%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%7D" alt="f(|h|)=2|h|f_{h^2}(h^2)=\frac{2|h|\left(h^2\right)^{-1/2}\left(1-h^2\right)^\frac{n-3}{2}}{B\left(\frac{1}{2},\frac{n-1}{2}\right)}=\frac{2\left(1-h^2\right)^\frac{n-3}{2}\Gamma\left(\frac{n}{2}\right)}{\sqrt{\pi}\Gamma\left(\frac{n-1}{2}\right)}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Ap6Op0hQ"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5Cleft%28%5Cleft%7Ch%5Cright%7C%3Ct%5Cright%29%3D%5Cint_0%5Etf%28x%29dx%3D%5Cfrac%7B2%5CGamma%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bn%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B%5Cpi%7D%5CGamma%5Cleft%28%5Cfrac%7Bn-1%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%7D%5Cint_0%5Et%5Cleft%281-x%5E2%5Cright%29%5E%5Cfrac%7Bn-3%7D%7B2%7Ddx" alt="P\left(\left|h\right|<t\right)=\int_0^tf(x)dx=\frac{2\Gamma\left(\frac{n}{2}\right)}{\sqrt{\pi}\Gamma\left(\frac{n-1}{2}\right)}\int_0^t\left(1-x^2\right)^\frac{n-3}{2}dx" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="ew7u4Uc7">这玩意看起来有点复杂，而剩下 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f_H%28t%29" alt="f_H(t)" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=E%5Cleft%28S_%7BX_1%5Ccdots+X_n%7D%5Cmid+H%3Dt%5Cright%29" alt="E\left(S_{X_1\cdots X_n}\mid H=t\right)" eeimg="1"> 也不像是什么简单东西，而我们还需要对这些复杂的东西做积分。这看上去非常得难，不过做到最后我们会发现，其实我们是在自己吓自己。</p>
 <p data-pid="PFZqhBrp"><b>8.故技重施</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MNY39HGK">那么我们要怎么去求剩下的那些分布函数呢？回顾一下本回答的开头，我们采用了一套新的坐标体系来描述这些点，并且通过体积元之比将两者联系起来。下面我们用同样的方法，选取一套包含了 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H" alt="H" eeimg="1"> 的坐标系来描述球面上的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个点：</p>
 <p data-pid="1JqhM3Q0">首先，选定随机变量 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H%5Cin%5B0%2C1%5D" alt="H\in[0,1]" eeimg="1"> ，其分布函数未知；</p>
 <p data-pid="zUdXVd31">接下来选定球面上的随机单位向量 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D" alt="\hat{\mathbf{n}}" eeimg="1"> ，其分布为球面上均匀分布；</p>
 <p data-pid="rUJsjkSr">在赤道面 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_n%3D0" alt="x_n=0" eeimg="1"> 上选定 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个随机点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1%2CP_2%2C%5Ccdots%2CP_n" alt="P_1,P_2,\cdots,P_n" eeimg="1"> ，其分布未知，是否独立未知；</p>
 <p data-pid="w6Q-fk0c">通过某个正交旋转，将赤道面变换为与 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D" alt="\hat{\mathbf{n}}" eeimg="1"> 垂直的大圆，得到上面 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个点的像 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P_1%5E%5Cprime%2CP_2%5E%5Cprime%2C%5Ccdots%2CP_n%5E%5Cprime" alt="P_1^\prime,P_2^\prime,\cdots,P_n^\prime" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="z98xvAmc">将大圆沿 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D" alt="\hat{\mathbf{n}}" eeimg="1"> 的方向平行移动距离 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H" alt="H" eeimg="1"> ，且同时随着球面而缩小，得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%2CX_2%2C%5Ccdots%2CX_n" alt="X_1,X_2,\cdots,X_n" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="UCwO0vnw">如此，我们就得到了球面上的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个随机点，且它们构成的超平面与原点的距离为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=H" alt="H" eeimg="1"> 。</p>

 <p data-pid="JzGpVR78">而我们的任务就是找到这样一个分布，使得上面的采样方法与直接在球面上独立地取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个随机点等价。容易证明的是，除去退化情形外，上述表示方法恰好构成一一对应，因此我们所要求的概率密度实际上就是两个坐标空间中的体积元之比。</p>
 <p data-pid="N658zA93">对于正交旋转的选择，一个很自然的想法是只进行 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%3C%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bz%7D%7D%2C%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%5Cright%3E" alt="\left<\hat{\mathbf{z}},\hat{\mathbf{n}}\right>" eeimg="1"> 面内的旋转，而保持所有其它分量不变，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bz%7D%7D%3D%5Cleft%280%2C0%2C%5Ccdots%2C1%5Cright%29" alt="\hat{\mathbf{z}}=\left(0,0,\cdots,1\right)" eeimg="1"> 为垂直于赤道面的单位矢量。这个旋转的表达式不难求得，为</p>
 <p data-pid="oVH5vrUC"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D%5E%5Cprime%3D%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D-%5Cleft%28%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D%5Ccdot%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%5Cright%29%5Cfrac%7B%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%2B%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bz%7D%7D%7D%7B1%2B%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%5Ccdot%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bz%7D%7D%7D" alt="\mathbf{p}^\prime=\mathbf{p}-\left(\mathbf{p}\cdot\hat{\mathbf{n}}\right)\frac{\hat{\mathbf{n}}+\hat{\mathbf{z}}}{1+\hat{\mathbf{n}}\cdot\hat{\mathbf{z}}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="MaRtOe-R">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D%5E%5Cprime%2C%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D" alt="\mathbf{p}^\prime,\mathbf{p}" eeimg="1"> 分别为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5E%5Cprime%2CP" alt="P^\prime,P" eeimg="1"> 的位矢。最终得到的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1"> 点位矢为</p>
 <p data-pid="iorSeBu4"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathbf%7Bx%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7B1-H%5E2%7D%5Cleft%28%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D-%5Cleft%28%5Cmathbf%7Bp%7D%5Ccdot%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%5Cright%29%5Cfrac%7B%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%2B%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bz%7D%7D%7D%7B1%2B%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D%5Ccdot%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bz%7D%7D%7D%5Cright%29%2BH%5Chat%7B%5Cmathbf%7Bn%7D%7D" alt="\mathbf{x}=\sqrt{1-H^2}\left(\mathbf{p}-\left(\mathbf{p}\cdot\hat{\mathbf{n}}\right)\frac{\hat{\mathbf{n}}+\hat{\mathbf{z}}}{1+\hat{\mathbf{n}}\cdot\hat{\mathbf{z}}}\right)+H\hat{\mathbf{n}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="UjOc7Iqh">这个变换的性质非常不错，可以帮我们大大减少计算难度。首先，其是一个仿射变换，因此位矢的变化保持线性关系；其次，其在超平面内是“正交”的：</p>

# 考试砸了回到家，母亲打我的概率是 1/2，父亲打我的概率也是 1/2，那我被打的概率是多少？

 <p data-pid="GZfGNxD8">设随机变量<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D0%2C%26%E7%88%B6%E4%BA%B2%E4%B8%8D%E6%89%93%E4%BD%A0%3B%5C%5C1%2C%26%E7%88%B6%E4%BA%B2%E6%89%93%E4%BD%A0%2C%5Cend%7Bcases%7D" alt="X=\begin{cases}0,&amp;父亲不打你;\\1,&amp;父亲打你,\end{cases}" eeimg="1"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Y%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D0%2C%26%E6%AF%8D%E4%BA%B2%E4%B8%8D%E6%89%93%E4%BD%A0%3B%5C%5C1%2C%26%E6%AF%8D%E4%BA%B2%E6%89%93%E4%BD%A0%2C%5Cend%7Bcases%7D" alt="Y=\begin{cases}0,&amp;母亲不打你;\\1,&amp;母亲打你,\end{cases}" eeimg="1">则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%2C+Y%5Csim+B%281%2C1%2F2%29" alt="X, Y\sim B(1,1/2)" eeimg="1"> 。设你会挨打的概率是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=p" alt="p" eeimg="1"> , 则</p>
 <p data-pid="b_d5FBA2"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5C%7BX%3D0%2CY%3D0%5C%7D%3DP%5C%7BX%3D1%2CY%3D1%5C%7D%3D1-p%2C+" alt="P\{X=0,Y=0\}=P\{X=1,Y=1\}=1-p, " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="GwRRHUhK"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5C%7BX%3D0%2CY%3D1%5C%7D%3DP%5C%7BX%3D1%2CY%3D0%5C%7D%3Dp-1%2F2." alt="P\{X=0,Y=1\}=P\{X=1,Y=0\}=p-1/2." eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="nHwVe8bO">因此 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2F2%5Cle+p%5Cle+1" alt="1/2\le p\le 1" eeimg="1"> . 设 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%3D%5Crho_%7BXY%7D" alt="\rho=\rho_{XY}" eeimg="1"> 为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%2CY" alt="X,Y" eeimg="1"> 的相关系数，则我们有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Crho%7D%7B4%7D%3D%5Cmathrm%7BCov%7D%28X%2CY%29%3DE%28XY%29-EX%5Ccdot+EY%3D%5Cfrac34-p%2C+%5Cquad+p%3D%5Cfrac%7B3-%5Crho%7D%7B4%7D." alt="\frac{\rho}{4}=\mathrm{Cov}(X,Y)=E(XY)-EX\cdot EY=\frac34-p, \quad p=\frac{3-\rho}{4}." eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Oqin6KGt">由 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2F2%5Cle+p%5Cle+1" alt="1/2\le p\le 1" eeimg="1"> 可知 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1%5Cle%5Crho%5Cle1" alt="-1\le\rho\le1" eeimg="1"> .</p>
 <p data-pid="D2-Rz7ig"><b>结论</b>：父母行动越一致，你被打的概率越低。</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="bqPY2bB1">如果父母喜欢同时行动，那么你只有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%2F2" alt="1/2" eeimg="1"> 几率会被打；</li>
  <li data-pid="9JfBfWkj">如果两人行动完全不相关，此时两人的行动恰好也是独立的（由联合分布律可知），挨打几率为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3%2F4" alt="3/4" eeimg="1"> ；</li>
  <li data-pid="wYZ5e5aQ">如果两人喜欢对着干，相关系数为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1" alt="-1" eeimg="1"> ，那你就惨了， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=100%5C%25" alt="100\%" eeimg="1"> 被打。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="jwksUhvw">注意<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=100%5C%25" alt="100\%" eeimg="1">被打并不代表一定会被打。举个例子，从 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B0%2C1%5D" alt="[0,1]" eeimg="1"> 上取点，父亲在小于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0.5" alt="0.5" eeimg="1"> 时打你，母亲在大于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0.5" alt="0.5" eeimg="1"> 时打你，此时 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho%3D-1" alt="\rho=-1" eeimg="1"> ，你虽然不被打几率是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%5C%25" alt="0\%" eeimg="1"> ，但仍可能发生。实际上此时有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%5C%7BX%3D1-Y%5C%7D%3D1" alt="P\{X=1-Y\}=1" eeimg="1"> ，而必定被打指的是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3D1-Y" alt="X=1-Y" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="ECvixJbh">最后注意如果父母打你几率不正好都是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac12" alt="\frac12" eeimg="1"> ，相关系数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Crho_%7BXY%7D" alt="\rho_{XY}" eeimg="1"> 一般不能取满 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B-1%2C1%5D" alt="[-1,1]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>

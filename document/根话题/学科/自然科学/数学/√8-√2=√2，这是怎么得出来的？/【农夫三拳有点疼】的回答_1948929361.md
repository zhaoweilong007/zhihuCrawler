 <p data-pid="_KDtCX8H">要证明 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B8%7D-%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7B2%7D" alt="\sqrt{8}-\sqrt{2}=\sqrt{2}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="pXpvjHZo">只需证 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%2B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7B8%7D" alt="\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{2}=\sqrt{8}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="arpgv2BN">严格证明这个等式需要用到戴德金分割</p><a data-draft-node="block" data-draft-type="link-card" href="https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/382110302" data-size="small" data-image="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a7ff7348fba97cef15909f538e0b3e1a_720w.jpg" data-entity-type="article" class="internal">【数分笔记】§1 戴德金定理、单调有界定理</a>
 <p data-pid="ziT4AKNR">令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D%5C%7Ba%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%3Aa+%5Cleq0%E6%88%96a%5E2%3C2%5C%7D" alt="A=\{a\in\mathbb{Q}:a \leq0或a^2<2\}" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%27%3D%5C%7Ba%27%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%3Aa%27%3E0%E4%B8%94%7Ba%27%7D%5E2%3E2%5C%7D" alt="A'=\{a'\in\mathbb{Q}:a'>0且{a'}^2>2\}" eeimg="1"> ，则构成一个分割 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28A%7CA%27%29" alt="(A|A')" eeimg="1"> ；令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%3D%5C%7Bb%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%3Ab%5Cleq0%E6%88%96b%5E2%3C2%5C%7D" alt="B=\{b\in\mathbb{Q}:b\leq0或b^2<2\}" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%27%3D%5C%7Bb%27%5Cin%5Cmathbb%7BQ%7D%3Ab%27%3E0%E4%B8%94%7Bb%27%7D%5E2%3E2%5C%7D" alt="B'=\{b'\in\mathbb{Q}:b'>0且{b'}^2>2\}" eeimg="1"> ，则构成一个分割 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28B%7CB%27%29" alt="(B|B')" eeimg="1"> .</p>
 <p data-pid="Ys-p49iE">令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%3D%5C%7Bc%3Ac%3Da%2Bb%2Ca%5Cin+A%2Cb%5Cin+B%5C%7D" alt="C=\{c:c=a+b,a\in A,b\in B\}" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%27%3D%5C%7Bc%27%3Ac%27%3Da%27%2Bb%27%2Ca%27%5Cin+A%27%2Cb%27%5Cin+B%27%5C%7D" alt="C'=\{c':c'=a'+b',a'\in A',b'\in B'\}" eeimg="1"> ，显然 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%2CC%27" alt="C,C'" eeimg="1"> 都不是空集，下证 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%2CC%27" alt="C,C'" eeimg="1"> 满足分割定义的两个条件：</p>
 <p data-pid="vMn1-gY-">（1） 任一有理数都属于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C" alt="C" eeimg="1"> 与 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%27" alt="C'" eeimg="1"> 之一，而且只属于其中之一；</p>
 <p data-pid="bfR84O-2">（2）集 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C" alt="C" eeimg="1"> 内的任一有理数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c" alt="c" eeimg="1"> 必小于集 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%27" alt="C'" eeimg="1"> 内任一有理数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%27" alt="c'" eeimg="1"> ；</p>
 <p data-pid="_s4vdIxA">任取有理数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%27" alt="c'" eeimg="1"> ，满足 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%27%3E0" alt="c'>0" eeimg="1"> 且 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bc%27%7D%5E2%3E8" alt="{c'}^2>8" eeimg="1"> . 取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%27%3D%5Cfrac%7Bc%27%7D%7B2%7D" alt="a'=\frac{c'}{2}" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%27%3D%5Cfrac%7Bc%27%7D%7B2%7D" alt="b'=\frac{c'}{2}" eeimg="1"> ，这样就有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%27%5Cin+A%27" alt="a'\in A'" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%27%5Cin+B%27" alt="b'\in B'" eeimg="1"> .所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%27%3Da%27%2Bb%27%5Cin+C%27" alt="c'=a'+b'\in C'" eeimg="1"> . 同理，任取有理数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c" alt="c" eeimg="1"> ，满足 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Cleq0" alt="c\leq0" eeimg="1"> 或 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5E2%3C8" alt="c^2<8" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=c%5Cin+C" alt="c\in C" eeimg="1"> . 故 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=C%2CC%27" alt="C,C'" eeimg="1"> 满足分割的条件（1）. 而分割的条件（2）显然成立. 综上， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28C%7CC%27%29" alt="(C|C')" eeimg="1"> 是一个分割，分界点为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B8%7D" alt="\sqrt{8}" eeimg="1"> .　 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csquare" alt="\square" eeimg="1"></p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="OFvz7K-2">看到有知友提到这种做法有卖弄之嫌，属于高射炮打蚊子之类的. 这里统一回复一下，题主给出的是一个很基础的问题，对于基础的问题，最好要用基础的方法，其它的“证明”可能或多或少会用到一些幂函数的性质（例如√(ab)=√a√b），如果允许使用幂函数的性质了，这个问题也就很显然了. 这篇回答介绍的方法“分割”，即“从有理数域出发定义无理数”，虽然看起来比较复杂，但是从逻辑上讲，这才是更基础的做法. 一般数学系大一刚入学时就会讲这个的，这里给对数学感兴趣的朋友推荐一下陈纪修老师的《数学分析》，里面比较系统地介绍了实数理论.</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="VvPnGMNd">心累，知乎社区环境真是和以前不一样了. 我刚过来时这题才几百浏览量，而这道题又是数学分析第一章的课后题（参考《吉米多维奇》第13题），我上大一时老师就讲过这道题. 当时我并不知道题主的接受情况，何况题主一开始还给这道题标上了“高等数学”和“数学证明”这样的标签（见问题日志）.</p>

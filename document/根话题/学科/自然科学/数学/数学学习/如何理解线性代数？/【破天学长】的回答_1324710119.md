# 如何理解线性代数？
- 点赞数：5998
- 更新时间：2022年10月19日10时48分18秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/20534668/answer/1324710119
<body>
 <p data-pid="MVaNwfj7">本人本科线代满分，考研线代部分满分，在不同的阶段，我对线性代数也有很多不同角度、不同层次的理解。</p>
 <p data-pid="tWxYmLGX">但是，至少在现在这个阶段，我感觉最直观的方式莫过于从<b>线性变换的角度来理解线性代数！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3Xgrw9ou">考研数学中的线性变换可以看作是作用于向量上的<b>放缩、旋转（镜面反射）！而线性代数在某种程度上可以看作是通过不同的表现形式来展示线性变换！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YePRPkjQ">既如此，我们从以下三个方面来介绍：</p>
 <p data-pid="LVrDewV6"><b>引言</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kOmnYflT"><b>一、理解线性变换的本质——放缩和旋转（镜面反射）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wGvgmNTy"><b>二、从线性变换的角度理解线性代数</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0wSCabi1"><b>引言</b></p>
 <p data-pid="FguSh436">从加法、减法，到乘法、除法，再到幂次；从长度到面积，再到体积，我们学习数学地思路总是从简单到复杂、从特殊到一般地归纳思路，既如此，我们以正比例函数入手，层层递进，逐步深化剖析线性变换，并在直观理解线性代换的基础上，进一步的尝试理解线性代数中的行列式、矩阵之秩、方程组的解、相似变换（特征值与特征向量）和二次型（合同变换）。</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="cm9J6WXE">一、理解线性变换的本质——放缩和旋转</p>
 <p data-pid="GZSbleb2"><b>0 正比例函数（放缩）</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="2XZGNZQF">给定正比例函数如下</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="JVul7ZUE"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dkx+%5Ctag%7B1%7D+" alt="y=kx \tag{1} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="ijfSmbG1">我们不妨定义<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1">为自变量（一维向量），<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y" alt="y" eeimg="1">为因变量（一维向量），<b>线性比例系数</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"><b>（一阶方阵）为线性代换。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rGYH1TbH">显然，正比例函数的本质就在于<b>线性变换</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> ！</p>
 <p data-pid="nYPzcQIu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"><b> 放缩了自变量 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"><b> ，映射为因变量 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y" alt="y" eeimg="1"><b> 。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HrmiDVcC">所以正比例函数的线性变换的本质是<b>线性放缩！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="9_7NDyMg"><b>如果</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%5Cne0" alt="k\ne0" eeimg="1"> ，那么有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cxrightarrow%7By%3Dkx%7D+y%5Ctag%7B2%7D" alt="x\xrightarrow{y=kx} y\tag{2}" eeimg="1"></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="AkEW6ICT">那么必然有逆变换， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5Cxrightarrow%7By%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bk%7Dx%7D+x%5Ctag%7B3%7D" alt="y\xrightarrow{y=\frac{1}{k}x} x\tag{3}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="gT1HO1Xs">如果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D0" alt="k=0" eeimg="1"> ，那么有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5Cxrightarrow%7By%3D0%5Ccdot+x%7D+y%3D0%5Ctag%7B4%7D" alt="x\xrightarrow{y=0\cdot x} y=0\tag{4}" eeimg="1"></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="CMidzI00">但将不存在逆变换,即无法实现如下 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbbox%5Bpink%2C2pt%5D%7By%3D0%5Crightarrow+x%7D%5Ctag%7B5%7D" alt="\bbox[pink,2pt]{y=0\rightarrow x}\tag{5}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="t3koOrxp"><b>这就是所谓的“降维打击”，从一维降为零维，那么，就再也无法从零维升为一维了！</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ShlUxPFY">
  <b>刘慈欣在《三体》中提到的二向箔就是</b>迫使三维宇宙及其中的所有物质向二维宇宙坍塌的一种降维打击原理的宇宙规律武器！
  <br>
  降维打击不可逆的特性是最令人生畏的地方！
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="4yVx9iSV"><b>1 特殊的线性变换——对角矩阵（放缩）</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="cN-3x_CB">我们再增加一个正比例函数，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+y_%7B1%7D%3Dk_%7B1%7Dx_%7B1%7D%5C%5C+y_%7B2%7D%3Dk_%7B2%7Dx_%7B2%7D%5C%5C++%5Cend%7Barray%7D%5Ctag%7B6%7D" alt="\begin{array}{} y_{1}=k_{1}x_{1}\\ y_{2}=k_{2}x_{2}\\  \end{array}\tag{6}" eeimg="1"></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="up5OUYtA">写成矩阵形式，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++y_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++k_%7B1%7D%260%5C%5C++++0%26k_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B7%7D" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      y_{1}\\    y_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      k_{1}&amp;0\\    0&amp;k_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{7}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5ufNubQS">不妨设 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++y_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\vec{y}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      y_{1}\\    y_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1">, <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLambda%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++k_%7B1%7D%260%5C%5C++++0%26k_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\Lambda=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      k_{1}&amp;0\\    0&amp;k_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"> , <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\vec{x}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"> ，则</p>
 <p data-pid="O03CFfWN"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3D%5CLambda%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%5Ctag%7B8%7D" alt="\vec{y}=\Lambda\vec{x}\tag{8}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="YogqRJ3y"><b>显然 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLambda" alt="\Lambda" eeimg="1"><b> 为对角矩阵，也即是我们的“主角”——线性变换！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ISLaHQcd"><b>此时，线性变换仍然是将</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"> <b>放缩为</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"> ，与正比例函数不同的是，<b>放缩是独立的沿着每个分量分别放缩的！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="oOoTf_ZZ">同样的，如果存在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7B1%7D+%3D0" alt="k_{1} =0" eeimg="1"> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=or" alt="or" eeimg="1"> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7B2%7D+%3D0" alt="k_{2} =0" eeimg="1"> ，那么，必然会对二维向量造成“<b>降维打击</b>”，不妨设<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7B1%7D+%3D0" alt="k_{1} =0" eeimg="1">，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cxrightarrow%7B%5Cvec%7By%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++0%260%5C%5C++++0%26k_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ccdot+%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%7D+%5Cvec%7By%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++0%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B9%7D" alt="\vec{x}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\xrightarrow{\vec{y}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      0&amp;0\\    0&amp;k_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\cdot \vec{x}} \vec{y}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      0\\    y_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{9}" eeimg="1"></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="59fSYnxP">于是，同样实现不了如下逆变换 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbbox%5Bpink%2C2pt%5D%7B%5Cvec%7By%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++0%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Crightarrow%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%7D%5Ctag%7B10%7D" alt="\bbox[pink,2pt]{\vec{y}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      0\\    y_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\rightarrow\vec{x}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)}\tag{10}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="NBZ9rpgp">我们拓展到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个正比例函数，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+y_%7B1%7D%3Dk_%7B1%7Dx_%7B1%7D%5C%5C+y_%7B2%7D%3Dk_%7B2%7Dx_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+y_%7Bn%7D%3Dk_%7Bn%7Dx_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C++%5Cend%7Barray%7D%5Ctag%7B11%7D" alt="\begin{array}{} y_{1}=k_{1}x_{1}\\ y_{2}=k_{2}x_{2}\\ ...\\ y_{n}=k_{n}x_{n}\\  \end{array}\tag{11}" eeimg="1"></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="HUI1i7JG">矩阵形式即<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++y_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+y_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++k_%7B1%7D%260%26...%5C%5C++++0%26k_%7B2%7D%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26+k_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+x_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B12%7D" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      y_{1}\\    y_{2}\\ ...\\ y_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      k_{1}&amp;0&amp;...\\    0&amp;k_{2}&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp; k_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\ ...\\ x_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{12}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="oXlN_G_E">得到类似的矩阵简写形式</p>
 <p data-pid="0Q0q_eVy"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3D%5CLambda%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%5Ctag%7B13%7D" alt="\vec{y}=\Lambda\vec{x}\tag{13}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WnME2rAk"><b>此时，线性变换（对角矩阵</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CLambda" alt="\Lambda" eeimg="1"> <b>）仍然是将</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"> <b>放缩为</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"> ，而且，<b>放缩是独立的沿着</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> <b>个分量分别放缩的！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="i924sKIg"><b>3 特殊的线性变换——正交矩阵（旋转和镜面反射）</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="n0RL_TZX">二阶旋转矩阵</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="Bg0gD-tf"><b>给定二阶旋转矩阵</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%28%5Ctheta%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++cos%5Ctheta%26-sin%5Ctheta%5C%5C++++sin%5Ctheta%26cos%5Ctheta%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="R(\theta)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      cos\theta&amp;-sin\theta\\    sin\theta&amp;cos\theta\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"> ，易知旋转矩阵的每行（列）彼此正交，且 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=det%5C+R+%3D1" alt="det\ R =1" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="_eNUvSdc">将旋转矩阵作为二元一次方程组的系数矩阵，则</p>
 <p data-pid="gttvOLZH"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++y_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++cos%5Ctheta%26-sin%5Ctheta%5C%5C++++sin%5Ctheta%26cos%5Ctheta%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B14%7D" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      y_{1}\\    y_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      cos\theta&amp;-sin\theta\\    sin\theta&amp;cos\theta\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{14}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="nWTUMHeq">即</p>
 <p data-pid="uUkJYQ7L"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3DR%28%5Ctheta%29%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%5Ctag%7B15%7D" alt="\vec{y}=R(\theta)\vec{x}\tag{15}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="z6Zg8T63">不妨设 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctheta%3D45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D" alt="\theta=45^{\circ}" eeimg="1"> ，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++1%5C%5C++++0%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\vec{x}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      1\\    0\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"> ,则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%2845%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++cos45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%26-sin45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%5C%5C++++sin45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%26cos45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%7B2%7D%26-%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%7B2%7D%26%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="R(45^{\circ})=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      cos45^{\circ}&amp;-sin45^{\circ}\\    sin45^{\circ}&amp;cos45^{\circ}\\     \end{array}   \right)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}&amp;-\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\   \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}&amp;\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="9R3SNdrk">则</p>
 <p data-pid="0jtxhcuG"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3DR%2845%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%29%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%5Ctag%7B15%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D+++++++++++%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++++++%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%7D%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\vec{y}=R(45^{\circ})\vec{x}\tag{15}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}           \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\         \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="UA2S0I_L"><b>此时，线性变换（旋转矩阵 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%2845%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%29" alt="R(45^{\circ})" eeimg="1"><b> ）是将</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"><b>绕着坐标轴原点逆时针旋转 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D" alt="45^{\circ}" eeimg="1"><b>，变成了 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"><b> ；反过来如果你</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"><b>绕保持不动，而将坐标轴绕着坐标轴原点顺时针旋转</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=45%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D" alt="45^{\circ}" eeimg="1"><b>，此时，静止的</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"><b>在新的坐标轴中的表示就变成了</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"><b>。</b></p>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="8hG3WXtg">不同的参考系，由不同的表示方法，但本质一样！</p>
 <p data-pid="rdLqXAm6"><b>于是，我们以后规定，旋转矩阵线性变换（旋转矩阵 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R" alt="R" eeimg="1"><b> ）只是旋转了坐标系，而不改变向量 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"><b> (包括线性相关性、向量模数等),但是，向量的表示是依赖于坐标系的，所以，在新的坐标系中，同一个向量变成了 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"><b> .</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="JyYSzxkp"><b>二阶镜面反射矩阵</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="scYpYpkb"><b>正交矩阵行列式为1的式旋转矩阵，为-1的称之为镜面反射矩阵！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="m3WY0uqB"><b>给定镜面反射矩阵</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++-1%260%5C%5C++++0%261%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="M=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      -1&amp;0\\    0&amp;1\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"> ，易知镜面反射矩阵的每行（列）彼此正交，且 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=det%5C+M+%3D-1" alt="det\ M =-1" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="8t1oi5NV">将镜面反射矩阵作为二元一次方程组的系数矩阵，则</p>
 <p data-pid="sqb5c5eD"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++y_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++-1%260%5C%5C++++0%261%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B16%7D" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      y_{1}\\    y_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      -1&amp;0\\    0&amp;1\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{16}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="W5zVeRr_">即</p>
 <p data-pid="zux3rL0P"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3DM%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%5Ctag%7B17%7D" alt="\vec{y}=M\vec{x}\tag{17}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="WyPCSJgG">不妨设<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++1%5C%5C++++0%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\vec{x}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      1\\    0\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"> ，则</p>
 <p data-pid="-cfG5U5W"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D%3DM%5Cvec%7Bx%7D%5Ctag%7B18%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D+++++++++++-1%5C%5C+++++++++0%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29" alt="\vec{y}=M\vec{x}\tag{18}=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}           -1\\         0\\     \end{array}   \right)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="NN6tbYbs">正如下</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-085078d726ff7ecb4f8848674487544c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="788" data-rawheight="442" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-3be84ecb10c136ffbeeac9cac9ae1139" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0e1a38a414fa9a53ad3e129eb0026a3d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="788" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-085078d726ff7ecb4f8848674487544c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="by0Q70Fb">同理，镜面反射向量与镜面反射坐标系本质一样，只不过是互为参考系。</p>
 <p data-pid="zlZGBq0P"><b>我们同样规定，镜面反射矩阵线性变换（镜面反射矩阵 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=M" alt="M" eeimg="1"><b> ）只是反射了坐标系，而不改变向量 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"><b> (包括线性相关性、向量模数等),但是，向量的表示是依赖于坐标系的，所以，在新的坐标系中，同一个向量变成了 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"><b> .</b></p>
 <ul>

 <p data-pid="HpwnKhIO">在这里，我们很容易得到结论（具体过程不予赘述，实则因为过程繁琐）：</p>
 <p data-pid="XutH4WRa">一个高阶正交矩阵可由有限个同阶单轴旋转矩阵和有限个镜面反射矩阵乘积组成，所以，对于系数矩阵为正交矩阵的<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 元一次方程组，有<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++y_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++y_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+y_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++k_%7B11%7D%26k_%7B12%7D%26...%26k_%7B1n%7D%5C%5C++++k_%7B21%7D%26...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26+...%26k_%7Bnn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+x_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B16%7D" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      y_{1}\\    y_{2}\\ ...\\ y_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      k_{11}&amp;k_{12}&amp;...&amp;k_{1n}\\    k_{21}&amp;...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp; ...&amp;k_{nn}\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\ ...\\ x_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{16}" eeimg="1"></p>

 <p data-pid="o7QQkxS2"><b>通过正交矩阵</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=T+" alt="T " eeimg="1"> <b>使得原坐标系绕坐标原点旋转或镜面反射，得到在新坐标系中向量 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx%7D" alt="\vec{x}" eeimg="1"><b> 的新的坐标表示 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7By%7D" alt="\vec{y}" eeimg="1"><b> 。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8SD31izo"><b> 4 一般的线性变换——任意 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"><b> 阶方阵</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wZgkwmaP"><b>通过以上分析，我们知道，特殊的 </b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"><b> 阶方阵就是一个线性变换，即包括放缩和旋转（镜面反射）变换！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="tAfrgjey">那么，一般的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 阶方阵是否也能够用放缩和旋转（镜面反射）变换表示？</p>

 <p data-pid="gE9pC93E">任意的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 阶方阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> (行列不相等，也可以，但，本文为简化仅借用方阵情况讨论)，必有</p>

 <p data-pid="Ysobrikg">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U" alt="U" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V" alt="V" eeimg="1"> 均为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 阶正交矩阵， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CSigma" alt="\Sigma" eeimg="1"> 是主对角线元素全为非负且降序的对角矩阵即</p>
 <p data-pid="mOARd7uV"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CSigma%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++k_%7B1%7D%260%26...%5C%5C++++0%26k_%7B2%7D%26...%26%5C%5C+...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26k_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B19%7D" alt="\Sigma=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      k_{1}&amp;0&amp;...\\    0&amp;k_{2}&amp;...&amp;\\ ...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp;k_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{19}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="lJQCHEqj">其中， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7Bi%7D%5Cgeq+k_%7Bi%2B1%7D%5Cgeq0%2Ci%3D1%2C2%2C...%2Cn-1" alt="k_{i}\geq k_{i+1}\geq0,i=1,2,...,n-1" eeimg="1"> 。</p>

eeimg="1">属性（包括向量的模数和彼此之间的线性关系），而在新坐标系<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O_%7B2%7D" alt="O_{2}" eeimg="1">中<b>向量</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bx_%7Bi%7D%7D" alt="\vec{x_{i}}" eeimg="1">变成 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%7BT%7D%5Cvec%7Bx_%7Bi%7D%7D" alt="V^{T}\vec{x_{i}}" eeimg="1"> <b>——在新坐标系</b> <img  <p data-pid="_PBlmbZL">二、从线性变换的角度理解线性代数</p>
 <p data-pid="7NpNNLS-"><b>1 行列式</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZTSByfkp"><b>行列式的绝对值是线性变换中每一个分量放缩系数的乘积！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nH4RFZA2">给定任意 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 阶方阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> ，根据式（18），有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=detA%3Ddet%28U%5CSigma+V%5E%7BT%7D%29%3DdetU%5Ccdot+det%5CSigma%5Ccdot+detV%5E%7BT%7D%3D%28-1%29%5Et%5Cprod_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Dk_%7Bi%7D%5Ctag%7B22%7D" alt="detA=det(U\Sigma V^{T})=detU\cdot det\Sigma\cdot detV^{T}=(-1)^t\prod_{i=1}^{n}k_{i}\tag{22}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="u96wjHvF">其中， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t%3D0" alt="t=0" eeimg="1"> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=or" alt="or" eeimg="1"> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1" alt="1" eeimg="1"> ，这是由正交矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U" alt="U" eeimg="1"> 、 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V" alt="V" eeimg="1"> 确定的。</p>
 <p data-pid="OWLvKChk">由式（22）可知，行列式是线性变换过程中的放缩系数之积；若存在至少一个放缩系数为0，即“降维打击”时，行列式为0。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kdI7kVUx"><b>2 矩阵之秩</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vqjc3QDF"><b>矩阵之秩就是放缩系数不为0的个数！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="4_ewoU2z">给定任意 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 阶方阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> ，根据式（18），有</p>
 <p data-pid="SWYUUWIf"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%28A%29%3Dr%28U%5CSigma+V%5E%7BT%7D%29%3Dr%28%5CSigma%29%3D++%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+++++i+%26+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7D++k_%7Bi%7D%3E0%2C+k_%7Bi%2B1%7D%3D0%2Ci%5Cleq+n-1%5C%5C++++n++++++%26+%5Ctext%7Bif+%7D+k_%7Bn%7D%3E0+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C%5Ctag%7B23%7D" alt="r(A)=r(U\Sigma V^{T})=r(\Sigma)=  \begin{cases}     i &amp; \text{if }  k_{i}>0, k_{i+1}=0,i\leq n-1\\    n      &amp; \text{if } k_{n}>0 \end{cases}\\\tag{23}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="O_i4utyW">显然，不存在“降维打击”的线性代换，即为满秩矩阵。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FZogT8Ys"><b>3 方程组的解</b></p>
 <p data-pid="EZKFes6g">给出方程组 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++a_%7B11%7D%26a_%7B12%7D%26...%26a_%7B1n%7D%5C%5C++++a_%7B21%7D%26...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26+...%26a_%7Bnn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7B1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7B2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+.x_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B24%7D%3DO" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      a_{11}&amp;a_{12}&amp;...&amp;a_{1n}\\    a_{21}&amp;...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp; ...&amp;a_{nn}\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{1}\\    x_{2}\\ ...\\ .x_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{24}=O" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="xcmzqPt5">即</p>
 <p data-pid="JDRQPqt6"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AX%3DO%5Ctag%7B25%7D" alt="AX=O\tag{25}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wyFxOEhe">由式（18）可知 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U%5CSigma+V%5E%7BT%7DX%3D0%5Ctag%7B26%7D" alt="U\Sigma V^{T}X=0\tag{26}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="SUTf1-CY">由于正交矩阵不改变向量属性，于是，变形有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CSigma%28V%5E%7BT%7DX%29%3DU%5E%7B-1%7DO%3DO%5Ctag%7B27%7D" alt="\Sigma(V^{T}X)=U^{-1}O=O\tag{27}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="IlEWB6sr">有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%7BT%7DX%3D%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7BV1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7BV2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+.x_%7BVn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Ctag%7B28%7D" alt="V^{T}X=\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{V1}\\    x_{V2}\\ ...\\ .x_{Vn}\\     \end{array}   \right)\tag{28}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="U62Le3tD">则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++k_%7B1%7D%260%26...%5C%5C++++0%26k_%7B2%7D%26...%26%5C%5C+...%26...%26...%5C%5C+...%26...%26k_%7Bn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%5Cleft%28+++++%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bcc%7D++++++x_%7BV1%7D%5C%5C++++x_%7BV2%7D%5C%5C+...%5C%5C+.x_%7BVn%7D%5C%5C+++++%5Cend%7Barray%7D+++%5Cright%29%3DO%5Ctag%7B29%7D" alt="\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      k_{1}&amp;0&amp;...\\    0&amp;k_{2}&amp;...&amp;\\ ...&amp;...&amp;...\\ ...&amp;...&amp;k_{n}\\     \end{array}   \right)\left(     \begin{array}{cc}      x_{V1}\\    x_{V2}\\ ...\\ .x_{Vn}\\     \end{array}   \right)=O\tag{29}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="2xQ_Wff6">若 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 满秩，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k_%7Bi%7D%3E0" alt="k_{i}>0" eeimg="1"> ,显然， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_%7BVi%7D%3D0" alt="x_{Vi}=0" eeimg="1"> ,故， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X%3DO" alt="X=O" eeimg="1"> ，原方程组仅有零解；</li>
  <li data-pid="1tShrqlh">若 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%28A%29%3Dr%3Cn" alt="r(A)=r<n" eeimg="1"> ,显然， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+++++k_%7Bi%7D%3E0%26+%5Ctext%7B+%7D++i%3D1%2C2%2C...%2C+r%5C%5C++++k_%7Bi%7D%3D0++++++%26+%5Ctext%7B%7D+i%3Dr%2B1%2C...%2Cn+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D" alt="\begin{cases}     k_{i}>0&amp; \text{ }  i=1,2,..., r\\    k_{i}=0      &amp; \text{} i=r+1,...,n \end{cases}" eeimg="1"> ,于是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_%7BVi%7D%3D0%EF%BC%8Ci%3D1%2C...%2Cr" alt="x_{Vi}=0，i=1,...,r" eeimg="1"> ;第 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=r%2B1" alt="r+1" eeimg="1"> 个分量到第 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 个分量，无论取什么值，都将会经受“降维打击”，坍缩为0,既然如此，我们就给定由 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n-r" alt="n-r" eeimg="1"> 个线性无关的向量来表示解空间，故 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=V%5E%7BT%7DX" alt="V^{T}X" eeimg="1"> 有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n-r+" alt="n-r " eeimg="1"> 个线性无关的解，所以， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1"> 有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n-r+" alt="n-r " eeimg="1"> 个线性无关的解。</li>
 </ul>

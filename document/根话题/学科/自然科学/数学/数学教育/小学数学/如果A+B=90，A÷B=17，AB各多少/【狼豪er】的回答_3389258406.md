# 如果A+B=90，A÷B=17，AB各多少?
- 点赞数：5154
- 更新时间：2024年02月07日14时06分48秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/592023780/answer/3389258406
<body>
 <p data-pid="yhtORBfv">你好啊小朋友，这道题是非常难的，我们很难通过常规方法来求出它的解，但是哥哥这里有一种简单巧妙的方法以解决该问题.</p>
 <p data-pid="PbWUBdxA">观察题目,我们发现可以用线性代数的方法来解决此问题.</p>
 <p data-pid="rNNGuES9">对于给定的方程组：</p>
 <p data-pid="1yeer7Ij"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cbegin%7Bmatrix%7DA%2BB%3D90++%5C%5CA%7B%5Cdiv%7D+B%3D17+%5C%5CAB%5Cne+0+%5Cend%7Bmatrix%7D%5Cright." alt="\left\{\begin{matrix}A+B=90  \\A{\div} B=17 \\AB\ne 0 \end{matrix}\right." eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="vOC_4KwC">我们发现，这个方程组不可以直接用标准的线性代数中的矩阵变换来解，因为矩阵变换和线性代数主要处理线性方程组，而第二个方程 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A+%5Cdiv+B+%3D+17" alt="A \div B = 17" eeimg="1"> 是一个非线性方程.</p>
 <p data-pid="Eqoq03NB">不过，我们可以通过变形来尝试解决问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="337SgUsi">变形第二个方程</p>
 <p data-pid="pj0gXp7f"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cdiv%7BB%7D%3D17" alt="A\div{B}=17" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="1nWNBYZq">得到</p>
 <p data-pid="veBye7mb"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D17B" alt="A=17B" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="srf3nW1t">整理，得到</p>
 <p data-pid="wiMsm_rr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A-17B%3D0" alt="A-17B=0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="oU3ISzOD">那么我们就得到了这么一个方程组，</p>
 <p data-pid="uHg6Agfu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%5C%7B%5Cbegin%7Bmatrix%7DA%2BB%3D90++%5C%5CA-+17B%3D0+%5Cend%7Bmatrix%7D%5Cright." alt="\left\{\begin{matrix}A+B=90  \\A- 17B=0 \end{matrix}\right." eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="h90m5jZG">首先，我们将这个方程组表示为矩阵形式 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AX+%3D+B" alt="AX = B" eeimg="1"> ，其中：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="TUIo3SLF"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 是系数矩阵，</li>
  <li data-pid="CYoXtWis"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1"> 是未知数矩阵，</li>
  <li data-pid="OGavoNqp"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 是常数矩阵.</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="d06KnPsC">所以，我们有：</p>
 <p data-pid="Yl0-mCuC"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+1+%26+1+%5C%5C+1+%26+-17+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%2C+%5Cquad+X+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+A+%5C%5C+B+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D%2C+%5Cquad+B+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+90+%5C%5C+0+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D" alt="A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 &amp; 1 \\ 1 &amp; -17 \end{bmatrix}, \quad X = \begin{bmatrix} A \\ B \end{bmatrix}, \quad B = \begin{bmatrix} 90 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="cRIqTwI3">为了找到未知数矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1"> ，我们需要计算系数矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 的逆矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B-1%7D" alt="A^{-1}" eeimg="1"> ，然后将其乘以常数矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> .</p>
 <p data-pid="j5FhBtze">即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%3D+A%5E%7B-1%7DB" alt="X = A^{-1}B" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="v3QNcuLT">首先，我们计算 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 的逆矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B-1%7D" alt="A^{-1}" eeimg="1"> 。为了计算一个矩阵的逆，我们使用公式：</p>
 <p data-pid="35FyaxP7"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B-1%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Ctext%7Bdet%7D%28A%29%7D+%5Ccdot+%5Ctext%7Badj%7D%28A%29" alt="A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\text{det}(A)} \cdot \text{adj}(A)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="V7XIHGb5">其中， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdet%7D%28A%29" alt="\text{det}(A)" eeimg="1"> 是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 的行列式， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Badj%7D%28A%29" alt="\text{adj}(A)" eeimg="1"> 是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 的伴随矩阵。</p>
 <p data-pid="zELMovBr">对于矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> ，行列式 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdet%7D%28A%29" alt="\text{det}(A)" eeimg="1"> 为：</p>
 <p data-pid="yaLHrGc_"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Bdet%7D%28A%29+%3D+%281%29%5Ctimes%28-17%29+-+%281%29%5Ctimes%281%29+%3D+-17+-+1+%3D+-18" alt="\text{det}(A) = (1)\times(-17) - (1)\times(1) = -17 - 1 = -18" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="lrg2sR-7">伴随矩阵 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Badj%7D%28A%29" alt="\text{adj}(A)" eeimg="1"> 是由 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 的每个元素的代数余子式构成的矩阵的转置。对于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2+%5Ctimes+2" alt="2 \times 2" eeimg="1"> 矩阵，伴随矩阵简单地由交换对角线元素和改变非对角线元素的符号组成：</p>
 <p data-pid="wgNETpMu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctext%7Badj%7D%28A%29+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+-17+%26+-1+%5C%5C+-1+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D" alt="\text{adj}(A) = \begin{bmatrix} -17 &amp; -1 \\ -1 &amp; 1 \end{bmatrix}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="KPX3O1cR">因此， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B-1%7D" alt="A^{-1}" eeimg="1"> 为：</p>
 <p data-pid="uytCjGZy"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B-1%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B-18%7D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+-17+%26+-1+%5C%5C+-1+%26+1+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+%5Cfrac%7B17%7D%7B18%7D+%26+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%26+-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D" alt="A^{-1} = \frac{1}{-18} \begin{bmatrix} -17 &amp; -1 \\ -1 &amp; 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{17}{18} &amp; \frac{1}{18} \\ \frac{1}{18} &amp; -\frac{1}{18} \end{bmatrix}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="agn_frtG">最后，我们用 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5E%7B-1%7D" alt="A^{-1}" eeimg="1"> 乘以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 来找到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1"> ：</p>
 <p data-pid="dASy05OD"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+%5Cfrac%7B17%7D%7B18%7D+%26+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%26+-%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+90+%5C%5C+0+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+%5Cfrac%7B17%7D%7B18%7D+%5Ctimes++90+%2B+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5Ctimes+0+%5C%5C+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5Ctimes+90+-+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B18%7D+%5Ctimes+0+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D+%3D+%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D+85+%5C%5C+5+%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D" alt="X = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{17}{18} &amp; \frac{1}{18} \\ \frac{1}{18} &amp; -\frac{1}{18} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 90 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{17}{18} \times  90 + \frac{1}{18} \times 0 \\ \frac{1}{18} \times 90 - \frac{1}{18} \times 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 85 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="NZeJGfbI">因此，A 和 B 的值分别为 85 和 5.</p>
 <p data-pid="FYwUo2yu">由于原方程组给的是分式，我们还需要代回去检验一下是不是增根.</p>
 <p data-pid="O2c2sg1w">检验: <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%2BB%3D85%2B5%3D90" alt="A+B=85+5=90" eeimg="1"> 且 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%5Cdiv%7BB%7D%3D85%5Cdiv%7B5%7D%3D17" alt="A\div{B}=85\div{5}=17" eeimg="1"> 且 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=AB%3D85%5Ctimes5%3D425%5Cne0" alt="AB=85\times5=425\ne0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="QuGhTGgQ">经检验， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D85%EF%BC%8CB%3D5" alt="A=85，B=5" eeimg="1"> 是原方程组的解，且符合题意.</p>
 <p data-pid="Kr8Dn9xv">所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A%3D85%EF%BC%8CB%3D5." alt="A=85，B=5." eeimg="1"></p>

# 如何记住所有的三角函数公式？
- 点赞数：7386
- 更新时间：2022年01月23日13时45分31秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/63652417/answer/1986480381
<body>
 <p data-pid="AyUek0n4">不用记那么多，记一个就行了。</p>
 <p data-pid="jwFzKlSd">考虑欧拉公式 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bi%5Ctheta%7D%3D%5Ccos%28%5Ctheta%29+%2Bi%5Csin%28%5Ctheta%29+++++%2Ci%3D%5Csqrt+%7B-1%7D" alt="e^{i\theta}=\cos(\theta) +i\sin(\theta)     ,i=\sqrt {-1}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="aHSNT5xy">两边平方，虚实对应：</p>
 <p data-pid="wqzd0A6j"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D+%7B1%7D+%28e%5E%7Bi%5Ctheta%7D%29%5E2+%3De%5E%7Bi2%5Ctheta%7D+%3D%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D%7B%5Ccos2%5Ctheta%7D+%2Bi%5Ccolor+%7Bblue%7D%7B%5Csin2%5Ctheta%7D%5C%5C++%3D%28%5Ccos%5Ctheta+%2Bi%5Csin%5Ctheta%29%5E2%5C%5C++%3D%28%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D%7B%5Ccos%5E2%5Ctheta-%5Csin%5E2%5Ctheta%7D%29+%2B+i%5Ccolor+%7Bblue%7D%7B2%5Csin%5Ctheta+%5Ccos+%5Ctheta%7D%5C%5C++%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt="\begin{array} {1} (e^{i\theta})^2 =e^{i2\theta} =\color {red}{\cos2\theta} +i\color {blue}{\sin2\theta}\\  =(\cos\theta +i\sin\theta)^2\\  =(\color {red}{\cos^2\theta-\sin^2\theta}) + i\color {blue}{2\sin\theta \cos \theta}\\  \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="WQ-SWT-o">这就是倍角公式。</p>
 <p data-pid="mPlz-yf3">更一般地， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccolor+%7B%7D%7B%5Ccos%28%5Cphi%5Ctheta%29+%2Bi%5Csin%28%5Cphi%5Ctheta%29+%3D%28%5Ccos%5Ctheta+%2B+i%5Csin%5Ctheta%29%5E%5Cphi%7D" alt="\color {}{\cos(\phi\theta) +i\sin(\phi\theta) =(\cos\theta + i\sin\theta)^\phi}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="FNdntnAv">这就是传说中的 <b>棣莫弗定理。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Y6-SLcq0">此外，我们可以用旋转矩阵描述n倍角：</p>
 <p data-pid="AKxbyudP"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+%5Ccos%5Ctheta+%26-%5Csin%5Ctheta%5C%5C+%5Csin%5Ctheta+%26+%5Ccos+%5Ctheta+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D+" alt="R=\begin{pmatrix} \cos\theta &amp;-\sin\theta\\ \sin\theta &amp; \cos \theta \end{pmatrix} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="w9SJfa-v">几何意义就是<b>逆时针旋转二维坐标系</b>，转多少次就几次幂，转一圈就是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2%5Cpi" alt="2\pi" eeimg="1"> ，所以当<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctheta%3D2k%5Cpi+%2C+k%5Cin+%5CZ+" alt="\theta=2k\pi , k\in \Z " eeimg="1"> 时， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=R%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D+1+%26+0%5C%5C+0+%26+1%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D" alt="R=\begin{pmatrix} 1 &amp; 0\\ 0 &amp; 1\\ \end{pmatrix}" eeimg="1"> ，此时为单位矩阵（就是把两个坐标基向量拼在一起），互相垂直（专业术语叫正交），这两个基向量的线性组合可以张成整个平面，线性代数会学。</p>
 <p data-pid="_0MBPpK_">那半角怎么算呢?也很简单，用上面二倍角的公式代换一下：</p>
 <p data-pid="FuzZbPen"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D++%5Ccos%5Ctheta+%3D++%5Ccos%5E2%5Cfrac+%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D+-+%5Csin%5E2%5Cfrac+%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D+%5C%5C+%5Csin%5Ctheta++%3D++2%5Csin%5Cfrac+%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D+%5Ccos+%5Cfrac+%7B%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D++%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+" alt="\begin{cases}  \cos\theta =  \cos^2\frac {\theta}{2} - \sin^2\frac {\theta}{2} \\ \sin\theta  =  2\sin\frac {\theta}{2} \cos \frac {\theta}{2}  \end{cases} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="X4anv7aK">到这步别急着解，其实还有一个条件： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E2%2By%5E2%3D1" alt="x^2+y^2=1" eeimg="1"> ，解得：</p>
 <p data-pid="3hQCpNd0"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+%5Csin+%5Cfrac+%7B%5Ctheta%7D2%3D+%5Csqrt%7B+%5Cfrac%7B1%2B%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D+%7D++%5C%5C+%5Ccos+%5Cfrac+%7B%5Ctheta%7D2%3D%5Csqrt%7B+%5Cfrac%7B1-%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%7B2%7D+%7D%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D" alt=" \begin{cases} \sin \frac {\theta}2= \sqrt{ \frac{1+\cos\theta}{2} }  \\ \cos \frac {\theta}2=\sqrt{ \frac{1-\cos\theta}{2} }\\ \end{cases}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="7ou4eMf7">（感谢评论区提醒，这两个写反了，但我手机没latex改不了，朋友门注意交换一下）</p>
 <p data-pid="oi4QxaAA">倍角你会，但如果要任意缩小一个角呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="lyoq6i1j">还是<b>棣莫弗定理</b>，这里介绍“单位根”：</p>
 <p data-pid="VIb8euBe">解方程 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%5E%7Bn%7D%3D1" alt="x^{n}=1" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="-J9ddLz8">根据代数基本定理，这东西有n个复根，他们分别是：</p>
 <p data-pid="an2Z6AOS"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+e%5E%7B%5Cfrac+%7B2k%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7Di%7D%3D%5Ccos%28%5Cfrac%7B2k%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%2Bi%5Csin%28%5Cfrac%7B2k%5Cpi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%2Ck%3D0%2C1%2C2%2C...%2Cn-1." alt=" e^{\frac {2k\pi}{n}i}=\cos(\frac{2k\pi}{n})+i\sin(\frac{2k\pi}{n}),k=0,1,2,...,n-1." eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="2J5HEiER">这些根都落在复平面的单位圆上，也就是把一个360度分成了n份。</p>
 <p data-pid="fKME4Ogt">为什么叫单位根，因为这些根离原点的距离都是1</p>
 <p data-pid="Usm7NVwA">不禁让人联想起想起割圆法(其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D1" alt="x=1" eeimg="1"> 是一个不动的根)。</p>
 <p data-pid="ucTLxmOi">再来看大家最喜闻乐道的和差角公式</p>
 <p data-pid="n3ToFtHk"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+e%5E%7Bi%28%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%29%7D%3De%5E%7Bi%5Calpha%7D%5Ccdot+e%5E%7Bi%5Cbeta%7D+%5C%5C+%3D%5B%5Ccos%28%5Calpha%29%2B+i%5Csin%28%5Calpha%29%5D%5B%5Ccos%28%5Cbeta+%29+%2B+i%5Csin%28%5Cbeta%29%5D%5C%5C+%3D%5B%5Ccolor+%7Bblue%7D%7B%5Ccos%28%5Calpha%29%5Ccos%28%5Cbeta%29-+%5Csin%28%5Calpha%29%5Csin%28%5Cbeta%29%7D%5D%2B+i%5B%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D%7B%28%5Csin%28%5Calpha%29%5Ccos%28%5Cbeta%29%2B+%5Ccos%28%5Calpha%29%5Csin%28%5Cbeta%29%7D%5D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt=" \begin{array}{} e^{i(\alpha+\beta)}=e^{i\alpha}\cdot e^{i\beta} \\ =[\cos(\alpha)+ i\sin(\alpha)][\cos(\beta ) + i\sin(\beta)]\\ =[\color {blue}{\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)- \sin(\alpha)\sin(\beta)}]+ i[\color {red}{(\sin(\alpha)\cos(\beta)+ \cos(\alpha)\sin(\beta)}] \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="NhD21o_i">差角公式就直接把 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbeta" alt="\beta" eeimg="1"> 变成负的，在数学里面只需要加法不用减法。</p>
 <p data-pid="2_oVPcOT"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5B%5Ccolor+%7Bblue%7D%7B%5Ccos%28%5Calpha%29%5Ccos%28%5Cbeta%29-+%5Csin%28%5Calpha%29%5Csin%28-%5Cbeta%29%7D%5D%2B+i%5B%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D%7B%28%5Csin%28%5Calpha%29%5Ccos%28%5Cbeta%29%2B+%5Ccos%28%5Calpha%29%5Csin%28-%5Cbeta%29%7D%5D" alt="[\color {blue}{\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)- \sin(\alpha)\sin(-\beta)}]+ i[\color {red}{(\sin(\alpha)\cos(\beta)+ \cos(\alpha)\sin(-\beta)}]" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="yjUrUBYU"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bi%28%5Calpha%2B%5Cbeta%2B%5Cgamma%29%7D" alt="e^{i(\alpha+\beta+\gamma)}" eeimg="1"> ，你初中学三角函数在百度上搜了觉得很高大上的“三角和公式”（好牛逼），现在会随便推了吧？</p>
 <p data-pid="pbMUVQ2z">还可以这样 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bi%28x_1%2Bx_2%2B...%2Bx_n%29%7D%3De%5E%7Bi%5Cvec+x%7D+" alt="e^{i(x_1+x_2+...+x_n)}=e^{i\vec x} " eeimg="1"> （后面有机会补充高斯积分）</p>
 <p data-pid="OiTj8HJ0">我们再回过头看欧拉公式，两边同时取倒：</p>
 <p data-pid="zOqMr-7w"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+e%5E%7Bix%7D%3D%5Ccos%28x%29%2Bi%5Csin%28x%29+%5C%5C+%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%29%5E%7B-1%7D%3De%5E%7Bi%28-x%29%7D+%3D%5Ccos%28-x%29%2Bi%5Csin%28-x%29%5C%5C+%3D%5Ccos%28x%29-i%5Csin%28x%29++%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt="\begin{array}{} e^{ix}=\cos(x)+i\sin(x) \\ (e^{ix})^{-1}=e^{i(-x)} =\cos(-x)+i\sin(-x)\\ =\cos(x)-i\sin(x)  \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="GIabB9RF">于是我们得到了一个很漂亮的方程组：</p>
 <p data-pid="vMwVR50-"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D+e%5E%7Bix%7D%3D%5Ccos%28x%29%2Bi%5Csin%28x%29+%5C%5C+e%5E%7B-ix%7D%3D%5Ccos%28x%29-i%5Csin%28x%29%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Bcases%7D+" alt="\begin{cases} e^{ix}=\cos(x)+i\sin(x) \\ e^{-ix}=\cos(x)-i\sin(x)\\ \end{cases} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="S_Lz0THG">实部相同，虚部相反——共轭。</p>
 <p data-pid="AzmWio9f">两边乘起来试试，得到我们最熟悉的东西：</p>
 <p data-pid="IJhgK8CK"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D+%7Be%5E%7Bix%7D%5Ccdot+e%5E%7B-ix%7D%3D1%7D%3D%28%5Ccos%28x%29%2Bi%5Csin%28x%29%29%28%5Ccos%28x%29-i%5Csin%28x%29%29++%5Ccolor+%7Bgreen%7D%7B%3D%5Ccos%5E2%28x%29%2B%5Csin%5E2%28x%29+%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt="\begin{array}{} \color {red} {e^{ix}\cdot e^{-ix}=1}=(\cos(x)+i\sin(x))(\cos(x)-i\sin(x))  \color {green}{=\cos^2(x)+\sin^2(x) } \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="twTaPIvf">当然，我们也可以直接把3个三角函数表示出来：</p>
 <p data-pid="CLy1Q0gw"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D+%7B%5Ccos%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7Be%5E%7Bix%7D%2Be%5E%7B-ix%7D%7D%7B2%7D%7D+%5C%5C+%5Ccolor+%7Bblue%7D+%7B+%5Csin%28x%29%3D+%5Cfrac+%7Be%5E%7Bix%7D-e%5E%7B-ix%7D%7D%7B2i%7D%7D+%5C%5C+%5Ccolor+%7Bgreen%7D%7B%5Ctan%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac+%7Be%5E%7Bix%7D-e%5E%7B-ix%7D%7D%7Bi%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%2Be%5E%7B-ix%7D%29%7D%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D+" alt=" \begin{array}{} \color {red} {\cos(x)=\frac{e^{ix}+e^{-ix}}{2}} \\ \color {blue} { \sin(x)= \frac {e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2i}} \\ \color {green}{\tan(x)=\frac {e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{i(e^{ix}+e^{-ix})}} \end{array} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Uyi5sAcY">不急，之后解<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%28x%29%3D2" alt="\cos(x)=2" eeimg="1"> 这种超越方程你就知道怎么用了。</p>
 <p data-pid="KmQrnvKV">很多人高中必修一学<b>函数奇偶性</b>的时候都遇到过类似的形式： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac+%7Be%5E%7Bx%7D%2Be%5E%7B-x%7D%7D%7B2%7D" alt="f(x)=\frac {e^{x}+e^{-x}}{2}" eeimg="1"> ，只不过当时大家都嫩，只知道 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Df%28-x%29" alt="f(x)=f(-x)" eeimg="1"> ，但从没想过把它的奇偶性和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos%28x%29" alt="\cos(x)" eeimg="1"> 产生关联：</p>
 <p data-pid="ISfH-dZY"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7Dix%29%3D%5Cfrac+%7Be%5E%7B%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7Dix%7D%2Be%5E%7B-%5Ccolor+%7Bgreen%7D%7Bi%7Dx%7D%7D%7B2%7D%3D%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D+%7B+%5Ccos%28x%29%7D" alt="f(\color{green}ix)=\frac {e^{\color{green}ix}+e^{-\color {green}{i}x}}{2}=\color {red} { \cos(x)}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="zPZ3xMZ1">OKOK，变来变去有啥用呢？最大的用就是让你不再觉得sin和cos只是两根弯弯曲曲的东西。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pUgTUvyI">
  横看成岭侧成峰，远近高低各不同。——苏轼
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H5ca8VWK">做道题休息一下吧，算一下 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos1%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%2B%5Ccos2%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%2B...%2B%5Ccos360%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D" alt="\cos1^{\circ}+\cos2^{\circ}+...+\cos360^{\circ}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5SejNmGN">看起来有点吓人。</p>
 <p data-pid="W_kjLDyx">我们想把 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bi%5Ctheta%7D%3D%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%5Ccos%5Ctheta%7D%2Bi%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%5Csin%28%5Ctheta%29%7D" alt="e^{i\theta}=\color{red}{\cos\theta}+i\color{blue}{\sin(\theta)}" eeimg="1"> 的实部和虚部提取出来单独研究，那么引入两个函数</p>
 <p data-pid="5KrI3h3H">他们的作用是取实部和取虚部：</p>
 <p data-pid="H2Nz0as2"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+%5CRe%28e%5E%7Bi%5Ctheta%7D%29%3D%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D%7B%5Ccos%28%5Ctheta%29%7D+%2C+%5CIm%28e%5E%7Bi%5Ctheta%7D%29%3D%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B%5Csin%28%5Ctheta%29%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt="\begin{array}{} \Re(e^{i\theta})=\color {red}{\cos(\theta)} , \Im(e^{i\theta})=\color{blue}{\sin(\theta)} \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="akY1sAVb">看看这个函数有啥性质</p>
 <p data-pid="ZfvEd2ou"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+e%5E%7Bix%7D%2Be%5E%7Biy%7D%3D%5CRe%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%29%2B%5CRe%28e%5E%7Biy%7D%29%2Bi%5B%5CIm%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%29%2B%5CIm%28e%5E%7Biy%7D%29%5D%5C%5C+%5CRe%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%2Be%5E%7Biy%7D%29%3D%5CRe%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%29%2B%5CRe%28e%5E%7Biy%7D%29%5C%5C+%5CIm%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%2Be%5E%7Biy%7D%29%3D%5CIm%28e%5E%7Bix%7D%29%2B%5CIm%28e%5E%7Biy%7D%29+%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt="\begin{array}{} e^{ix}+e^{iy}=\Re(e^{ix})+\Re(e^{iy})+i[\Im(e^{ix})+\Im(e^{iy})]\\ \Re(e^{ix}+e^{iy})=\Re(e^{ix})+\Re(e^{iy})\\ \Im(e^{ix}+e^{iy})=\Im(e^{ix})+\Im(e^{iy}) \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="ugxWPx6h">太棒了，线性！</p>
 <p data-pid="-f5JF5OE"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D++%5Ccolor+%7Bred%7D%7B%5CRe%28x%29%2B%5CRe%28y%29%3D%5CRe%28x%2By%29%5C%5C+%5CIm%28x%29%2B%5CIm%28y%29%3D%5CIm%28x%2By%29%5C%5C+%7D%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt="\begin{array}{}  \color {red}{\Re(x)+\Re(y)=\Re(x+y)\\ \Im(x)+\Im(y)=\Im(x+y)\\ }\end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="A1T5g2Aw">回到题目， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ccos+k%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%3D%5CRe%28e%5E%7Bi+%5Ccdot+k%5Cfrac+%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B180%7D%7D%29" alt="\cos k^{\circ}=\Re(e^{i \cdot k\frac {\pi}{180}})" eeimg="1"> ，那么</p>
 <p data-pid="92ImL8Ll"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%7D+%5Ccos1%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%2B%5Ccos2%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D%2B...%2B%5Ccos360%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D++%3D%5Csum%5Climits%5E%7B360%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7D+%5Ccos+k%5E%7B%5Ccirc%7D+%3D%5CRe%28%5Csum%5Climits%5E%7B360%7D_%7Bk%3D1%7De%5E%7Bi%5Ccdot+k%5Ccdot+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpi%7D%7B180%7D+%7D+%29+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Barray%7D" alt=" \begin{array}{} \cos1^{\circ}+\cos2^{\circ}+...+\cos360^{\circ}  =\sum\limits^{360}_{k=1} \cos k^{\circ} =\Re(\sum\limits^{360}_{k=1}e^{i\cdot k\cdot \frac{\pi}{180} } ) \\ \end{array}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="13ldrRxr">里面是个几何级数，展开：</p>

 <p data-pid="1G1KoyxS">下面我们要拿欧拉公式搞点事情，从另外一个角度看三角函数：</p>

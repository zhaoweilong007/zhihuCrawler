# 老师说链式法则里某个 dy/dx 不能理解为 dy 除以 dx，为什么？
- 点赞数：7352
- 更新时间：2020年08月26日09时18分50秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/66601481/answer/1431205499
<body>
 <p data-pid="ZEqDi7gY">这事儿刚开始是比较不好理解。</p>
 <p data-pid="mn_U44Ek">这事儿得从这位大佬开始讲起：</p>
 <h2>莱布尼茨的发明</h2>
 <p data-pid="UL1_lO7z">我们知道导数是这样定义的：</p>
 <p data-pid="mMypETVI"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%5Cprime%28x%29+%3D%5Clim_%7Bh%5Cto0+%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%2Bh%29-f%28x%29%7D%7Bh%7D" alt="f^\prime(x) =\lim_{h\to0 }\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="N6zuXIFp">但在数学大佬的眼里，这玩意不<b>美观</b>啊！！在许多时候，数学家眼里 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%E7%BE%8E%3D%E7%AE%80%E6%B4%81" alt="美=简洁" eeimg="1"> 。但这式子不简洁。</p>
 <p data-pid="zXqjdWg0">从导数的定义上看它不可避免的问题就是要求<b>商</b>，但无奈又不能把极限分别写进分子和分母里去！于是Leibniz就想出了这么个玩意：</p>
 <p data-pid="5qBkuewm"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dx}" eeimg="1"> ，并解释道：它是" <b>由 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 的变化而引起的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y" alt="y" eeimg="1"> 的无穷变化量</b>"( <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dy" alt="dy" eeimg="1"> )与“<b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 的无穷小变化量</b>”<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28dx%29" alt="(dx)" eeimg="1">的<b>商</b>。那么用这个记法就可以很好地体现出导数定义里 <b>商 </b>的形式了。</p>
 <h2>上述解释的关键在于： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dy" alt="dy" eeimg="1"> 必须是因 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 改变了 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx" alt="dx" eeimg="1"> 而引起的变化，这个记号才有意义</h2>
 <p data-pid="WjraiEtX">翻译几下就是：<b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dy" alt="dy" eeimg="1"> 不能独立于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 而变化！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rDN7mtut">反例其实很好找：</p>
 <p data-pid="6Rd-K6cY"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%5E%5Cprime%28x%29%5Ccdot+z%5E%5Cprime%28t%29%3D%5Clim+_%7Bh+%5Crightarrow+0%7D+%5Cfrac%7By%28x%2Bh%29+-y%28x%29%7D%7Bh%7D+%5Clim+_%7Bh+%5Crightarrow+0%7D+%5Cfrac%7Bz%28t%2Bh%29+-z%28t%29%7D%7Bh%7D+%3D%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bdz%7D%7Bdt%7D" alt="y^\prime(x)\cdot z^\prime(t)=\lim _{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{y(x+h) -y(x)}{h} \lim _{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{z(t+h) -z(t)}{h} =\frac{dy}{dx}\cdot\frac{dz}{dt}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="wEG0XOoh">此时 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dy" alt="dy" eeimg="1"> 是和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t" alt="t" eeimg="1"> 无关的， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dz" alt="dz" eeimg="1"> 是和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 无关的。</p>
 <p data-pid="ITsVvLtm">因此如果写成这样就肯定错了：</p>
 <p data-pid="Od-jmenk"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cxcancel%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx+%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bdz%7D%7Bdt%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdt%7D%5Ccdot%5Cfrac%7Bdz%7D%7Bdx%7D+%7D%7D" alt="\xcancel{\color{red}{\frac{dy}{dx }\cdot\frac{dz}{dt} = \frac{dy}{dt}\cdot\frac{dz}{dx} }}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <h2>反过来，如果满足我们上述的条件，那么实际上它是可以作为“商”来处理的</h2>
 <blockquote data-pid="XbsZ6zTA">
  注意：下面所有的推导都必须保证这个“商”所对应的极限必须存在！
 </blockquote>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="5t5RsgYp"><b>比如我们熟悉的反函数求导：</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="s_YXb5cj"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdx%7D%7Bdy%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cquad%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D%5Cquad%7D" alt="\frac{dx}{dy} = \frac{1}{\quad\frac{dy}{dx}\quad}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="pmTr_wjq"><b>再比如链式法则：</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="Ug6FKGlR"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdu%7D%5C%3B%5Cfrac%7Bdu%7D%7Bdx%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du}\;\frac{du}{dx}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="dSLDdo5p">
  <b>注意：链式法则是可以任意增长的（有限次）。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="1SCf8vVL">比如我们令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3Dx%28t%29" alt="x=x(t)" eeimg="1"> ，</p>
 <p data-pid="-AXu0cjf"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdt%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdu%7D%5C%3B%5Cfrac%7Bdu%7D%7Bdx%7D%5C%3B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Bdx%7D%7Bdt%7D%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dt} = \frac{dy}{du}\;\frac{du}{dx}\;\color{red}{\frac{dx}{dt}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="TwgaIhbt">甚至于：</p>
 <p data-pid="n4O6f6sL"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y+%3D+f_1%5Ccirc+f_2%5Ccirc+%5Ccdots%5Ccirc+f_n%28x%29" alt="y = f_1\circ f_2\circ \cdots\circ f_n(x)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="EbjUgLGf">我们可以这样处理：</p>
 <p data-pid="1qviIYlw"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D+%7Bdf_n%7D+%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+df_n%7D+%7D%7Bdx%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{\color{Red} {df_n} }\frac{\color{Red}{ df_n} }{dx}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Bmq7kCDo"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+df_%7Bn-1%7D%7D+%7D%5C%3B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+df_%7Bn-1%7D%7D+%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BBlue%7D%7B+df_%7Bn%7D%7D+%7D%5C%3B%5Cfrac%7B%5Ccolor%7BBlue%7D%7B+df_n%7D+%7D%7Bdx%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{\color{Red}{ df_{n-1}} }\;\frac{\color{Red}{ df_{n-1}} }{\color{Blue}{ df_{n}} }\;\frac{\color{Blue}{ df_n} }{dx}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="mqrLS5Rv"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvdots" alt="\vdots" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="G6FlVLHs"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdf_1%7D%5C%3B%5Cfrac%7Bdf_1%7D%7Bdf_2%7D%5Ccdots%5Cfrac%7Bdf_n%7D%7Bdx%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{df_1}\;\frac{df_1}{df_2}\cdots\frac{df_n}{dx}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="D6HQhcKZ">这个过程中，我们就可以看成不断地同时“乘”、“除” <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=df_k" alt="df_k" eeimg="1"> ，因为每一步的添加项都<b>没有破坏分子是因其自变量改变而产生的无穷小量这一条件</b>。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="noYnX45A">
  目前深度学习的所谓Back propagation其实就是这个法则(当然需要用到偏导数）。
 </blockquote>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="tKF2eb2O"><b>还有由参数方程确定的函数的求导问题</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="bbPk55pk"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft+%5C%7B++%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D++x+%3D+x%28t%29%5C%5C+y+%3D+y%28t%29+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Cright.+" alt="\left \{  \begin{align*}  x = x(t)\\ y = y(t) \end{align*} \right. " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="rFMcb0pr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7Bdx%7D%3D%7B%5Cleft+%28+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+dt%7D+%7D+%5Cright+%29++%5CBig%2F+%5Cleft+%28+%5Cfrac%7Bdx%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+dt%7D%7D+%5Cright+%29++%7D" alt="\frac{dy}{dx}={\left ( \frac{dy}{\color{Red}{ dt} } \right )  \Big/ \left ( \frac{dx}{\color{Red}{ dt}} \right )  }" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="uS2oiLFg">其反函数类似：</p>
 <p data-pid="syRwzu7s"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Bdx%7D%7Bdy%7D%3D%7B%5Cleft+%28+%5Cfrac%7Bdx%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+dt%7D+%7D+%5Cright+%29++%5CBig%2F+%5Cleft+%28+%5Cfrac%7Bdy%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7BRed%7D%7B+dt%7D%7D+%5Cright+%29++%7D" alt="\frac{dx}{dy}={\left ( \frac{dx}{\color{Red}{ dt} } \right )  \Big/ \left ( \frac{dy}{\color{Red}{ dt}} \right )  }" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="QASyYUzQ">这里与复合函数链式法则完全相同，<b>由于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx%2Cdy" alt="dx,dy" eeimg="1"> 都是由 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=t" alt="t" eeimg="1"> 的变化而产生的无穷小量</b>，因此仍然可以同时“乘”、“除” <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dt" alt="dt" eeimg="1">。</p>

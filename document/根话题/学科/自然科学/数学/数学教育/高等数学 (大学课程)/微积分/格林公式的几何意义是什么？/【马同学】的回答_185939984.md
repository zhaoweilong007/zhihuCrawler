 <p data-pid="j3kxbLVY"><b>3 物理的解答</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8352AaLk"><b>3.1 旋转方向与有向路径</b></p>
 <p data-pid="fEirf-Ue">首先，规定逆时针旋转为正方向：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f37a323f6c666cb6b19effb9c650c17b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="530" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-f37a323f6c666cb6b19effb9c650c17b" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="530" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-f37a323f6c666cb6b19effb9c650c17b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="8tVS7wId">旋转有了方向之后，此点走过的路径也就有了方向，我们称为“有向路径”。</p>
 <p data-pid="Pe6CJu1r">根据旋转的正方向，就可定义点走过的路径的正方向：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7fce93bf17c220127756fcda72578cb8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="530" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-7fce93bf17c220127756fcda72578cb8" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="530" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-7fce93bf17c220127756fcda72578cb8_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="zTrDcub6">点要是反着转，那么走过的路径自然就是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5E-" alt="L^-" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="70EZTLq9"><b>3.2 做功分析</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zgoOV_QE">根据微积分的思想，我们把路径切成无数个微小的曲线段：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-f176f6eaf903472b8a2a7457179cca8a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="530" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-f176f6eaf903472b8a2a7457179cca8a" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="530" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-f176f6eaf903472b8a2a7457179cca8a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="LpP5X1gk">根据我们已知的两个知识（已知的意思，其实是我不想解释了）：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="IkADfVK4">根据微积分“以直代曲”的思想，这些微小的曲线段可以用切线来代替</li>
  <li data-pid="mpQHxLKA">根据物理知识，我们知道，力只在路径方向做功</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="XWCsl_LV">结合上述两点，我们可以得到，每个微小的曲线段上做的功为：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Sh6ntlo-">那么，很明显，整段封闭曲线做功可以表示为如下：</p>
 <p data-pid="3_-SAFaa"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{r_{}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="v3we0XLY">“哇，清晰多了！”同学搓搓手，递上一只大前门香烟：“老师，可是怎么计算呢？”</p>
 <p data-pid="57bmiE7i">格林抽出笔来，刷刷地写道：“就这么算！”</p>
 <p data-pid="hnOO4oMP"><b>4 数学计算</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dyljmCBx"><b>4.1 矢量形式转为标量形式</b></p>
 <p data-pid="iwW3Cv1x">矢量形式 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{r_{}}" eeimg="1"> 不太好计算，让我们转为标量形式。</p>
 <p data-pid="61N1tR8Y">根据我们一元微积分的知识，我们知道 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D" alt="d\vec{r_{}}" eeimg="1"> 在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7Bi_%7B%7D%7D%2C%5Cvec%7Bj_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{i_{}},\vec{j_{}}" eeimg="1"> 方向的分量为：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-395cfeeb3ed386be672286cb187ac205_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="425" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-395cfeeb3ed386be672286cb187ac205" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="425" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-395cfeeb3ed386be672286cb187ac205_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="bkWk1MWB">那么，有 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%3DP%5Cvec%7Bi_%7B%7D%7D%2BQ%5Cvec%7Bj_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{F_{}}=P\vec{i_{}}+Q\vec{j_{}}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Cvec%7Br%7D%3Ddx%5Cvec%7Bi%7D%2Bdy%5Cvec%7Bj%7D" alt="d\vec{r}=dx\vec{i}+dy\vec{j}" eeimg="1"> ，所以， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D%3DPdx%2BQdy" alt="\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{r_{}}=Pdx+Qdy" eeimg="1"> ，所以：</p>
 <p data-pid="Ox6cvf0i"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D%3D%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7DPdx%2BQdy" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{r_{}}=\oint _{L^+}Pdx+Qdy" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="rEAnycdM"><b>4.2 非常简单的加减运算</b></p>
 <p data-pid="V1I_5h2z">我们给出一个简单的力场，这个力场的特点是：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="rb2z-fi2">只有水平方向的力</li>
  <li data-pid="NzQ5l9Zh">在同一个垂直高度上，力的大小一样</li>
  <li data-pid="yXBkygwK">随着垂直高度的增加，力逐渐减小</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="WdoEmKIV">画出来就是这样的（矢量的方向表示力的方向，矢量的长度表示力的大小）：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-a5d093caa04f8319a81796b498b399ee_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="671" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-a5d093caa04f8319a81796b498b399ee" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="671" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-a5d093caa04f8319a81796b498b399ee_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ZYujzDCR">计算在此力场中，某点围绕正方形路径一圈所做的功，已知：</p>
 <ul>

  <li data-pid="FRPutI9_">上边受力大小为1，下边受力大小为4</li>
  <li data-pid="U52-8YQa">力与左右两边垂直，所以在这两边不做功</li>
 </ul>

 <p data-pid="TxVLApR3"><b>4.3 计算微小矩形边界上的功</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wl_lOf_B">怎么计算微分矩形上做的功呢？让我取一个微分矩形出来，我把矩形的边和顶点、以及矩形的区域都标注出来了：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-35115e54056bc99c3bba03b986e8bc58_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="664" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-35115e54056bc99c3bba03b986e8bc58" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="664" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-35115e54056bc99c3bba03b986e8bc58_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="zj4iAfNz">下面是代数推断了，我觉得过程还是很清晰明了的。</p>
 <p data-pid="2PuXaf48">首先，注意到在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L_1%5E%2B%2CL_3%5E%2B" alt="L_1^+,L_3^+" eeimg="1"> 上 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dy" alt="dy" eeimg="1"> 为0（因为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y" alt="y" eeimg="1"> 方向没有变化）， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L_2%5E%2B%2CL_4%5E%2B" alt="L_2^+,L_4^+" eeimg="1"> 上 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=dx" alt="dx" eeimg="1"> 为0，然后我们继续推下去：</p>
 <p data-pid="tGQdZtsI"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7DPdx%2BQdy%26+%3D%5Cint+_%7BL_1%5E%2B%7DPdx%2B%5Cint+_%7BL_2%5E%2B%7DQdy%2B%5Cint+_%7BL_3%5E%2B%7DPdx%2B%5Cint+_%7BL_4%5E%2B%7DQdy%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cint+_%7Ba_0%7D%5E%7Ba_1%7DP%28x%2Cb_0%29dx%2B%5Cint+_%7Bb_0%7D%5E%7Bb_1%7DQ%28a_1%2Cy%29dy%2B%5Cint+_%7Ba_1%7D%5E%7Ba_0%7DP%28x%2Cb_1%29dx%2B%5Cint+_%7Bb_1%7D%5E%7Bb_0%7DQ%28a_0%2Cy%29dy%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cint+_%7Bb_0%7D%5E%7Bb_1%7D%5BQ%28a_1%2Cy%29-Q%28a_0%2Cy%29%5Ddy%2B%5Cint+_%7Ba_0%7D%5E%7Ba_1%7D%5BP%28x%2Cb_0%29-P%28x%2Cb_1%29%5Ddx%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cint+_%7Bb_0%7D%5E%7Bb_1%7D%5Cint+_%7Ba_0%7D%5E%7Ba_1%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+Q%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7Ddxdy-%5Cint+_%7Ba_0%7D%5E%7Ba_1%7D%5Cint+_%7Bb_0%7D%5E%7Bb_1%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+P%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7Ddydx%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Cint+_%7Bb_0%7D%5E%7Bb_1%7D%5Cint+_%7Ba_0%7D%5E%7Ba_1%7D%5B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+Q%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+P%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7D%5Ddxdy%5C%5C+%26+%3D%5Ciint+_%7BD_1%7D%5B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+Q%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+P%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7D%5Ddxdy+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D" alt="\begin{align*} \oint _{L^+}Pdx+Qdy&amp; =\int _{L_1^+}Pdx+\int _{L_2^+}Qdy+\int _{L_3^+}Pdx+\int _{L_4^+}Qdy\\ &amp; =\int _{a_0}^{a_1}P(x,b_0)dx+\int _{b_0}^{b_1}Q(a_1,y)dy+\int _{a_1}^{a_0}P(x,b_1)dx+\int _{b_1}^{b_0}Q(a_0,y)dy\\ &amp; =\int _{b_0}^{b_1}[Q(a_1,y)-Q(a_0,y)]dy+\int _{a_0}^{a_1}[P(x,b_0)-P(x,b_1)]dx\\ &amp; =\int _{b_0}^{b_1}\int _{a_0}^{a_1}\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}dxdy-\int _{a_0}^{a_1}\int _{b_0}^{b_1}\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}dydx\\ &amp; =\int _{b_0}^{b_1}\int _{a_0}^{a_1}[\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}]dxdy\\ &amp; =\iint _{D_1}[\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}]dxdy \end{align*}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="GAPJErFL">微分矩形的边界做功求出来了，结合我们之间的结论，边界的做功＝微分矩形做功之和我们可以得到最终的结论：</p>
 <p data-pid="erF3a5Uf"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D%3D%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7DPdx%2BQdy%3D%5Ciint+_%7BD%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+Q%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D-%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+P%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7Ddxdy" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{r_{}}=\oint _{L^+}Pdx+Qdy=\iint _{D}\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}dxdy" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="SiV06Vh8">其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=D" alt="D" eeimg="1"> 为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=L%5E%2B" alt="L^+" eeimg="1"> 围成的区域。</p>
 <p data-pid="fZnEU-62">同学之前听得屏息凝视，现在才有机会长出了口气：“真是精彩啊！”</p>
 <p data-pid="XvndJWPW">格林反问道：“你知道 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{n_{}}" eeimg="1"> 会得到什么吗？”</p>
 <p data-pid="OyqV4ici"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D" alt="d\vec{n_{}}" eeimg="1"> 是法向量。</p>
 <p data-pid="V3Uyusl0"><b>5 通量</b></p>
 <p data-pid="W-jz3S4h"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Br_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{r_{}}" eeimg="1"> 代表力在运动方向做功，但是力并不会在与运动的垂直方向做功，那么 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{n_{}}" eeimg="1"> 代表了什么？</p>
 <p data-pid="-0D4V_Eu">如果把 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{F_{}}" eeimg="1"> 看作流速，或者电流密度，那么 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{n_{}}" eeimg="1"> 就在流体力学、电磁学中被称为通量。</p>
 <p data-pid="dTaYcZZ8">关于通量更详细的可以看我另外一个回答 <a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/21912411/answer/177976053" class="internal">散度和旋度的物理意义是什么</a> ，其中回答了为什么是法向量方向。</p>
 <p data-pid="tgFq9X6T">比如，对于我们头顶上的太阳：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-488106bcf9de8ce9065ede41ee1a586b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="590" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-488106bcf9de8ce9065ede41ee1a586b" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="590" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-488106bcf9de8ce9065ede41ee1a586b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="p9TsLJGe">我们要计算穿过（包括射出和进入）太阳表面的能量总量：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a34abd8a9e3e3f315e847f2a35507ee1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="589" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-a34abd8a9e3e3f315e847f2a35507ee1" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="589" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-a34abd8a9e3e3f315e847f2a35507ee1_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="-rp6nxI4">这就是通量，记作：</p>
 <p data-pid="Zwy5Qhtg"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{n_{}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="IEP_PPaC">太阳内部时时都在发生核聚变，以及其他的能量活动：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-dd878688f7cc9a65bb4c3954b3e9eb79_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="511" data-rawheight="402" data-original-token="v2-dd878688f7cc9a65bb4c3954b3e9eb79" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="511" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-dd878688f7cc9a65bb4c3954b3e9eb79_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Ym0YEQse">根据能量守恒，内部的能量总量，必然等于穿过太阳表面的能量总量。</p>
 <p data-pid="ZxwPU1Uz">也就是说，通量和内部能量总量相等。</p>
 <p data-pid="wdZ71Svv">定了这个基调之后，然后按照之前分析做功的方式，最终我们可以得到：</p>
 <p data-pid="QXCzPwcY"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D%3D%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7DPdy-Qdx%3D%5Ciint+_%7BD%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+P%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+Q%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7Ddxdy" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{n_{}}=\oint _{L^+}Pdy-Qdx=\iint _{D}\frac{\partial P}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y}dxdy" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="qKHUtIG7">格林说完之后，突然发现，自己发现了不得了的东西，对于数学有重要的意义，相当于把封闭曲线的线积分转为了二重积分。所以，赶快去发表论文吧。</p>

 <p data-pid="noxoWi_F">做功的形式（电磁学、流体力学也可以把 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D" alt="\vec{F_{}}" eeimg="1"> 看作流速，下面就称为环流量）：</p>

 <p data-pid="CWdM5knb"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7D%5Cvec%7BF_%7B%7D%7D%5Ccdot+d%5Cvec%7Bn_%7B%7D%7D%3D%5Coint+_%7BL%5E%2B%7DPdy-Qdx%3D%5Ciint+_%7BD%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+P%7D%7B%5Cpartial+x%7D%2B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cpartial+Q%7D%7B%5Cpartial+y%7Ddxdy" alt="\oint _{L^+}\vec{F_{}}\cdot d\vec{n_{}}=\oint _{L^+}Pdy-Qdx=\iint _{D}\frac{\partial P}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y}dxdy" eeimg="1"></p>

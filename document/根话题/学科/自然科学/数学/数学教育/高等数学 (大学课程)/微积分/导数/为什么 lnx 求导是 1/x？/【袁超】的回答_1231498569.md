# 为什么 lnx 求导是 1/x？
- 点赞数：19616
- 更新时间：2020年05月19日22时21分11秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/38810755/answer/1231498569
<body>
 <p data-pid="K-kvcWmh">其实，我想对题主说，你提的问题一点都不弱智。恰恰相反，这是个非常有意义的问题。我们学习一门知识，尤其是数学知识，要知其然，更要知其所以然。今天，我尝试从<b>“如何去定义“</b>的角度出发来解释这个问题，不玩公式推导的符号游戏。希望能带来新的启发。</p>
 <p data-pid="7SALdrFR">为解决对数函数的问题，在未给出定义以前，我们应该先研究指数函数。最开始，我们有指数的基本性质： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5E0%3D1%2Ca%5E1%3Da%2Ca%5E2%3Da%5Ccdot+a" alt="a^0=1,a^1=a,a^2=a\cdot a" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="9ZDF02et">根据基本性质，你应该能够推导出一些指数函数的运算法则，例如：</p>
 <p data-pid="NARNhtvG"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5E%7Bm%2Bn%7D+%3D+a%5Ema%5En+%5C%5C+a%5E%7Bmn%7D+%3D+%7B%28a%5Em%29%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%3D%7B%28a%5En%29%7D%5Em%5C%5C+a%5E%7Bm%2Fn%7D%3D%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Ba%5Em%7D+" alt="a^{m+n} = a^ma^n \\ a^{mn} = {(a^m)}^{n}={(a^n)}^m\\ a^{m/n}=\sqrt[n]{a^m} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="lONEYwMz">上述公式中的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bm%2Fn%7D" alt="{m/n}" eeimg="1"> ，，使得指数函数在<b>实数域</b>上也有了定义。 （经<a class="member_mention" href="https://www.zhihu.com/people/a269e4342020219729574a4e050ead40" data-hash="a269e4342020219729574a4e050ead40" data-hovercard="p$b$a269e4342020219729574a4e050ead40">@朱俊辉</a> 指正）</p>
 <p data-pid="2PLq_Bsr">我们先研究指数函数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Ex" alt="a^x" eeimg="1"> 的一阶导，根据极限：</p>
 <p data-pid="NCwvw0d-"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bd%7D%7D%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bd%7Dx%7Da%5Ex%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto0%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%5E%7Bx%2B%5CDelta+x%7D-a%5Ex%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%7D%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto+0%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%5Exa%5E%7B%5CDelta+x%7D-a%5Ex%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%7D+%5C%5C%3Da%5Ex%5Cboxed%7B%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto+0%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7Ba%5E%7B%5CDelta+x%7D-1%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%7D%7D" alt="\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}a^x=\lim_{\Delta x\to0}{\frac{a^{x+\Delta x}-a^x}{\Delta x}}=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}{\frac{a^xa^{\Delta x}-a^x}{\Delta x}} \\=a^x\boxed{\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}{\frac{a^{\Delta x}-1}{\Delta x}}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="68tQYRWB">注意框起来的部分，一个关于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a" alt="a" eeimg="1"> 的函数，我们姑且叫它<b>“谜之函数”</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> ，一个迷一样的函数。</p>
 <p data-pid="T95H0Xqr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bd%7D%7D%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bd%7Dx%7Da%5Ex%3Da%5Ex+%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D%5C%5C" alt="\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}a^x=a^x \mathrm{M(a)}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="KK6cQMbF"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cfrac%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bd%7D%7D%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bdx%7D%7Da%5Ex%5Cbigg%7C_%7Bx%3D0%7D%3Da%5E0%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D%3D%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D%5C%5C" alt=" \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{dx}}a^x\bigg|_{x=0}=a^0\mathrm{M(a)}=\mathrm{M(a)}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="QPoEzhLV">我们对 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> 所知甚少，但至少应该了解它的<b>几何含义</b>： 指数函数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Ex" alt="a^x" eeimg="1"> 在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3D0" alt="x=0" eeimg="1"> 点处的斜率。</p>
 <figure data-size="small">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-82835dd726709cd57267129f7bf2d6f5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="small" data-rawwidth="882" data-rawheight="610" data-original-token="v2-46fd2fa6d35e64339b7965101e4c1a2c" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-b827de44a2520226df5f902620d7b683_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="882" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-82835dd726709cd57267129f7bf2d6f5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="1NJBTv5H">为了搞清楚 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> 究竟是什么，我们假设有一个<b>“迷之数”</b> <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> ， 使得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28%7De%5Cmathrm%7B%29%7D%3D1" alt="\mathrm{M(}e\mathrm{)}=1" eeimg="1"> 。虽然，我们不清楚 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> 究竟是什么，但有了这样的假设，非常自然的：</p>
 <p data-pid="yGUBagzt"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7De%5Ex%3De%5Ex%5C%5C+%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7D%7Be%5Ex%7D%5Cbigg%7C_%7Bx%3D0%7D%3D1+" alt="\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}e^x=e^x\\ \mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}{e^x}\bigg|_{x=0}=1 " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="-htpO-Ba">那么，<b>数</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"><b> 一定就存在的吗？</b>不妨来验证一下，考虑 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Da%5Ex" alt="f(x)=a^x" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%280%29%3D%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="f'(0)=\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="vQ5dBLbv">将 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=kx" alt="kx" eeimg="1"> 带入到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1">： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28kx%29%3Da%5E%7Bkx%7D%3D%7B%28a%5Ek%29%7D%5Ex%3Db%5Ex" alt="f(kx)=a^{kx}={(a^k)}^x=b^x" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%3Da%5Ek" alt="b=a^k" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="sqMxrDzk"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7Db%5Ex%3D%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7Df%28kx%29%3Dkf%27%28kx%29%5C%5C+%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7Db%5Ex%5Cbigg%7C_%7Bx%3D0%7D%3Dkf%27%280%29%3DkM%28a%29" alt="\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}b^x=\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}f(kx)=kf'(kx)\\ \mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}b^x\bigg|_{x=0}=kf'(0)=kM(a)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="PccC1NtL">对比我们上面的假设可以发现，数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e+" alt="e " eeimg="1"> 确实是存在的，只需令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D%7D" alt="k=\frac{1}{\mathrm{M(a)}}" eeimg="1"> ，此时的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b" alt="b" eeimg="1"> 即为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="-ThD9qdM">我们绕了一大圈，还没搞清楚 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> ，又引入了数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> ，这么做究竟是为什么呢？回顾一下，考虑一个指数函数是非常自然的，更进一步，定义在实数域上的指数函数也很显然。我们在计算指数函数的导数过程中，遇到了“棘手”的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> ，但是，通过引入假设的数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> ，似乎又没那么难了。而且我们证明了，数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> 是一定存在的。</p>
 <p data-pid="ZKmcXGkt">有了指数函数，定义其<b>反函数</b>称为对数函数。你应该记得什么是反函数，几何意义是将函数图像以轴 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dx" alt="y=x" eeimg="1"> 对称，计作 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%5E%7B-1%7D" alt="f^{-1}" eeimg="1"> 。以数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> 为底的指数函数具有良好性质，其反函数是以数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e" alt="e" eeimg="1"> 为底的对数函数，记作： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx" alt="\mathrm{ln}x" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="dCPAYu_G">一个问题：已知 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx" alt="\mathrm{ln}x" eeimg="1"> 是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex" alt="e^x" eeimg="1">的反函数，那么 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bln%28x%29%7D%7D%3D%3F" alt="e^{\mathrm{ln(x)}}=?" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="YS7kRnUU">答案： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 。令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=w_1%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx_1" alt="w_1=\mathrm{ln}x_1" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=w_2%3De%5E%7Bx_2%7D" alt="w_2=e^{x_2}" eeimg="1"> ，取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=w_2" alt="w_2" eeimg="1"> 的反函数 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_2%3De%5E%7Bw_2%7D" alt="x_2=e^{w_2}" eeimg="1"> 。因为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx" alt="\mathrm{ln}x" eeimg="1"> 是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5Ex" alt="e^x" eeimg="1">的反函数，所以，当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=w_1%3Dw_2" alt="w_1=w_2" eeimg="1"> ， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_2%3De%5E%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx_1%7D%3Dx_1" alt="x_2=e^{\mathrm{ln}x_1}=x_1" eeimg="1"> ，推出 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx%7D%3Dx" alt="e^{\mathrm{ln}x}=x" eeimg="1"> 。可以这样想，反函数是关于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dx" alt="y=x" eeimg="1"> 对称，那么反函数的反函数则就是其本身了。</p>
 <p data-pid="7OyHPcLP">最后，我们解决 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7Dln%7Dx" alt="\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}ln}x" eeimg="1"> ，令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=w%3D%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx" alt="w=\mathrm{ln}x" eeimg="1"> ：</p>
 <p data-pid="zKaaRksd"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7De%5Ew%3D%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7De%5E%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx%7D%3D%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7Dx%3D1%5C%5C+" alt="\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}e^w=\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}e^{\mathrm{ln}x}=\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}x=1\\ " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="1980X1mq">根据隐函数微分法：</p>
 <p data-pid="tqvHOPmp"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7D%7De%5Ew%3D%28%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdw%7D%7De%5Ew%29%28%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bdw%7D%7Bdx%7D%7D%29%3De%5Ew%28%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bdw%7D%7Bdx%7D%7D%29%3D1%5C%5C" alt="\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}}e^w=(\mathrm{\frac{d}{dw}}e^w)(\mathrm{\frac{dw}{dx}})=e^w(\mathrm{\frac{dw}{dx}})=1\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="ezWKbyoJ">整理可得： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bd%7D%7Bdx%7Dln%7Dx%3D%28%5Cmathrm%7B%5Cfrac%7Bdw%7D%7Bdx%7D%7D%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5Ew%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Be%5E%7B%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Dx%7D%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%7D" alt="\mathrm{\frac{d}{dx}ln}x=(\mathrm{\frac{dw}{dx}})=\frac{1}{e^w}=\frac{1}{e^{\mathrm{ln}x}}=\frac{1}{x}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="33xONVku">证毕。</p>
 <p data-pid="-KnAt6gB">最后，聪明的小伙伴可能已经发现了，那个“迷之函数” <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7BM%28a%29%7D" alt="\mathrm{M(a)}" eeimg="1"> 其实就等于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cmathrm%7Bln%7Da" alt="\mathrm{ln}a" eeimg="1"> 。相关证明留作课后习题，请读者自行完成。</p>

 <p data-pid="p_644AT_">今天这篇文章再换一个角度来谈论微分和导数，让我们从微分出现的原因说起。</p>
 <p data-pid="5CvnhL-A"><b>1 微分出现的原因</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HW1sbqZ9">出于种种原因，我们可能想去求曲线的长度、曲面梯形的面积：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-40b25732167e042a77def0e720e29be0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="651" data-rawheight="198" data-original-token="v2-9c60371f2b7440072635d1097565eb2e" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-be00aad80bfc11f6faaad4558510a44a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="651" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-40b25732167e042a77def0e720e29be0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="JU5pGY29">求解思路是这样的，以求曲线长度为例，将曲线分为多个部分，每一部分都用切线来近似曲线：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3926f3f5e32bd6f21314142e6b325124_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="425" data-rawheight="229" data-original-token="v2-a9d83d143867490f6ca8f7e512fb736b" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-04f5897b57836d575f932e7604b65bee_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="425" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3926f3f5e32bd6f21314142e6b325124_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="xBPJXOiV">划分的越细，直到划分为无穷多份，最终这些切线的长度加起来就是曲线的长度：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-01dcf20142e698520e673472ff54d623_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="440" data-rawheight="222" data-original-token="v2-01dcf20142e698520e673472ff54d623" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-01dcf20142e698520e673472ff54d623_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="440" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-01dcf20142e698520e673472ff54d623_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="5RRnI5DY">求曲边梯形面积也是类似的，用小矩形来近似曲边梯形的面积，随着小矩形的增多最终得到曲边梯形的面积：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9885eba2c1901c1df4e9860368feb1a3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="362" data-rawheight="184" data-original-token="v2-fd235393219e971289e9ac037448eb0d" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2abf71e8f3827647e9717d481370e79f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="362">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Zzo0DfJt">上面的思想就是微积分的核心思想，“以直代曲”。曲线长度、曲边梯形就是“曲”，切线、小矩形就是用来近似（代替）的“直”，这种“直”就是 <b>微分</b>。关于这里还不了解的可以看“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/847/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">微分是什么？</a>”这篇文章。</p>
 <p data-pid="uDi7e2AA"><b>2 微分与导数</b></p>
 <p data-pid="AodAuIJn">先不谈曲边梯形，本节先来回答曲线的微分是什么，也就是可以近似曲线的直线是什么？</p>
 <p data-pid="-MQXI1Bh"><b>2.1 几何分析</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3OYRqpmL">下面结合几何来理清一下求解的思路。假设有曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 和直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1"> ，如果两者完全相等，那么有：</p>
 <p data-pid="NKvBlTQ2"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dg%28x%29%5Cquad+%E6%88%96%5Cquad+f%28x%29-g%28x%29%3D0%5C%5C" alt="f(x)=g(x)\quad 或\quad f(x)-g(x)=0\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="PoRNK7uG">很显然这在整个曲线和直线上是做不到的，但肯定可以做到在某点上相等，比如让它们在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1"> 点相等，从几何上看就是曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 和直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1"> 在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1"> 点相交：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-26cbc5e6c0384f5dfdaa510e968bcc48_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="480" data-rawheight="251" data-original-token="v2-9f1971a563596d8c4cfcd01caaaabf2c" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-52d1f27fab87a9589583fa46857c8265_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="480" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-26cbc5e6c0384f5dfdaa510e968bcc48_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="2zOIWGci">下面要更进一步，不过不要太贪心，只希望两者在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1"> 附近尽量相等。比如像下图一样，在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_0-%5CDelta+x%2Cx_0%2B%5CDelta+x%29" alt="(x_0-\Delta x,x_0+\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 的区间内，曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 和直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1"> 相差很小：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-084d724742ebf8603411b73303387bb4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="486" data-rawheight="251" data-original-token="v2-ce5816cc36138eb5e03dc73652e88630" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-cd18942d761fa4a4d39291594ea01439_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="486" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-084d724742ebf8603411b73303387bb4_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="c42WRQJv">这点如果可以做到，那就可以按照上一节说的，将曲线分成n份，每份都用各自的微分来近似。</p>
 <p data-pid="pBkcyPtZ"><b>2.2 代数分析</b></p>
 <p data-pid="D1qSqMIb">通过几何分析，思路已经理清楚了，要找的微分（直线）需要满足以下两点：</p>
 <p data-pid="aw3g4hc1">（1）曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 和直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1"> 要交于<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1"> 点。假设直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1"> 的斜率为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> ，那么根据点斜式，可得直线函数：</p>
 <p data-pid="seR_sq28"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29%3Df%28x_0%29%2Bk%28x-x_0%29%5C%5C" alt="g(x)=f(x_0)+k(x-x_0)\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="IquhQ5e3">这里面就是<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> 还未知，求出了<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> 就得到了想要的直线，也就得到了微分。</p>
 <p data-pid="1eTD4w3K">（2）曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 和直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1"> 要在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_0-%5CDelta+x%2Cx_0%2B%5CDelta+x%29" alt="(x_0-\Delta x,x_0+\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 的区间内尽可能相等。用代数表示即为：</p>
 <p data-pid="BEIuJqW3"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29-g%28x%29%5Capprox+0%2C%5Cquad+x%5Cin%28x_0-%5CDelta+x%2Cx_0%2B%5CDelta+x%29%5C%5C" alt="f(x)-g(x)\approx 0,\quad x\in(x_0-\Delta x,x_0+\Delta x)\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="2N8ZeWnZ">有约等号的式子是没法计算的，引入高阶无穷小<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o%28%5CDelta+x%29" alt="o(\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 可以将约等号去掉：</p>
 <p data-pid="91OeKCXq"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29-g%28x%29%3Do%28%5CDelta+x%29%2C%5Cquad+x%5Cin%28x_0-%5CDelta+x%2Cx_0%2B%5CDelta+x%29%5C%5C" alt="f(x)-g(x)=o(\Delta x),\quad x\in(x_0-\Delta x,x_0+\Delta x)\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="-frdJHN0">高阶无穷小<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o%28%5CDelta+x%29" alt="o(\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 的意思就是在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x" alt="\Delta x" eeimg="1"> 这个范围内无限的接近于 0。至于为什么它无限接近于 0，以及为什么是高阶无穷小，在文章的最后会进行补充说明。</p>
 <p data-pid="1AQwCQ9K"><b>2.3 解出直线的斜率</b><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="vJgcZv50">经过上面的分析我们有了：</p>
 <p data-pid="1kZR7HN9"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cleft.%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+++++g%28x%29%3Df%28x_0%29%2Bk%28x-x_0%29%5C%5C+++++f%28x%29-g%28x%29%3Do%28%5CDelta+x%29+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%5Cright%5C%7D%5Cimplies+f%28x%29-f%28x_0%29-k%28x-x_0%29%3Do%28%5CDelta+x%29+%5C%5C" alt=" \left.\begin{aligned}     g(x)=f(x_0)+k(x-x_0)\\     f(x)-g(x)=o(\Delta x) \end{aligned}\right\}\implies f(x)-f(x_0)-k(x-x_0)=o(\Delta x) \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5RbVKSST">这已经足够让我们解出直线的斜率<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> 了。注意到<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x%3Dx-x_0" alt="\Delta x=x-x_0" eeimg="1"> ，可以进行如下变形：</p>
 <p data-pid="56Q87VqZ"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+++++f%28x%29-f%28x_0%29-k%28x-x_0%29%3Do%28%5CDelta+x%29+++++++++%26%5Cimplies+f%28x%29-f%28x_0%29-k%5CDelta+x%3Do%28%5CDelta+x%29%5C%5C+++++++++%26%5Cimplies+%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29-f%28x_0%29-k%5CDelta+x%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%3D%5Cfrac%7Bo%28%5CDelta+x%29%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D+%5C%5C" alt=" \begin{aligned}     f(x)-f(x_0)-k(x-x_0)=o(\Delta x)         &amp;\implies f(x)-f(x_0)-k\Delta x=o(\Delta x)\\         &amp;\implies \frac{f(x)-f(x_0)-k\Delta x}{\Delta x}=\frac{o(\Delta x)}{\Delta x} \end{aligned} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="-Cy3NQc9">两侧取极限可得：</p>
 <p data-pid="odqpkitN"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29-f%28x_0%29-k%5CDelta+x%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Bo%28%5CDelta+x%29%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%5Cimplies+%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29-f%28x_0%29-k%5CDelta+x%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%3D0+%5C%5C" alt=" \lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\frac{f(x)-f(x_0)-k\Delta x}{\Delta x}=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\frac{o(\Delta x)}{\Delta x}\implies \lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\frac{f(x)-f(x_0)-k\Delta x}{\Delta x}=0 \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="5UhRl8ik">略微作一下化简可得：</p>
 <p data-pid="h_f2-HiE"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto+0%7D%5Cfrac%7Bf%28x%29-f%28x_0%29%7D%7B%5CDelta+x%7D%5C%5C" alt="k=\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}\frac{f(x)-f(x_0)}{\Delta x}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="zTPPrnHW">至此我们就求出了<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> ，也就找到了<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 的 <b>微分</b>（直线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=g%28x%29" alt="g(x)" eeimg="1">的形式还需要变一下才是真正的微分，关于这点可以参考“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/849/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">dx，dy是什么？</a>”这篇文章）。</p>
 <p data-pid="9sJhUqHY">这个<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1"> 在微积分中又有一个专门的名字，称为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 的 <b>导数</b>。<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1">如果存在（因为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k" alt="k" eeimg="1">是通过极限求得的，这个极限有不存在的可能），那么就称<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1">可导。</p>
 <p data-pid="yVnkZsV8">上面的思路还可以进一步思考，如果要找最接近曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 的多项式曲线呢？这会得到泰勒公式，就留给大家作课后习题吧。</p>
 <p data-pid="NcoEznQc"><b>3 补充说明</b></p>
 <p data-pid="GZ1OtJYU">这里补充说明两点：</p>
 <p data-pid="6v0rHrvc">（1）高阶无穷小<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o%28%5CDelta+x%29" alt="o(\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 为什么表示在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28x_0-%5CDelta+x%2Cx_0%2B%5CDelta+x%29" alt="(x_0-\Delta x,x_0+\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 的区间内无限接近于 0 ？这是因为它满足：</p>
 <p data-pid="uaZ5Tj8Q"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Clim_%7B%5CDelta+x%5Cto0%7Do%28%5CDelta+x%29%3D0%5C%5C" alt="\lim_{\Delta x\to0}o(\Delta x)=0\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="j9gadXc9">上面这个极限式意味着<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x" alt="\Delta x" eeimg="1"> 越小，也就是越接近于<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_0" alt="x_0" eeimg="1"> 点，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o%28%5CDelta+x%29" alt="o(\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 越接近于 0。用动画表示就是：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2d33d2cc98e29b143f2ae2148dd28b4c_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="589" data-rawheight="425" data-original-token="v2-2d33d2cc98e29b143f2ae2148dd28b4c" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-2d33d2cc98e29b143f2ae2148dd28b4c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="589" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-2d33d2cc98e29b143f2ae2148dd28b4c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Aa_SuT9o">上图中绿线就是曲线<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> ，蓝线为微分，可以看到随着<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CDelta+x" alt="\Delta x" eeimg="1"> 缩小，两者之间的距离<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=o%28%5CDelta+x%29" alt="o(\Delta x)" eeimg="1"> 也无限接近于 0。</p>
 <p data-pid="8mTr5dOy">（2）为什么一定要高阶无穷小？同阶无穷小行不行？关于这点可以参考“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/2115/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">为什么算出来的圆周率 π 等于 4 ？</a>”这篇文章。</p>

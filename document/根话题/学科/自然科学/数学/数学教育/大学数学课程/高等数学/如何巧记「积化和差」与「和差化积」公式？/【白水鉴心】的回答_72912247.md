 <p data-pid="HyTBAPTh"><b>前后相随，表里如一。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JFP177EI">前后相随（前后项数统一）：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="RBDMgVVx">积是一项，化和差后要 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdiv2" alt="\div2" eeimg="1"> ；</li>
  <li data-pid="dkDX7vUZ">和差是两项，化积后要成 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ctimes+2" alt="\times 2" eeimg="1"> 。</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vuwzuX54">表里如一（内外项数统一）：</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="zLUGcp-m">括号内变量都是先 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%2B+%5Cbeta" alt="\alpha + \beta" eeimg="1"> ，再 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+-+%5Cbeta" alt="\alpha - \beta" eeimg="1"> 。</li>
  <li data-pid="0AoA3ugS">化和差后是两项， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cpm+%5Cbeta" alt="\alpha \pm \beta" eeimg="1"> 两项不变；</li>
  <li data-pid="1-jU942a">化积后是一项， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Calpha+%5Cpm+%5Cbeta" alt="\alpha \pm \beta" eeimg="1"> 要 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cdiv2" alt="\div2" eeimg="1"> 变一项。</li>
 </ul>

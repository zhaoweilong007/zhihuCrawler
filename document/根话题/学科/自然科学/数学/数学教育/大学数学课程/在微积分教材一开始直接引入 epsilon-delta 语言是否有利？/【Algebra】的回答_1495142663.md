# 在微积分教材一开始直接引入 epsilon-delta 语言是否有利？
- 点赞数：5125
- 更新时间：2021年10月16日23时39分45秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/422341327/answer/1495142663
<body>
 <p data-pid="y9RsWOxO">不合适，非常不合适！抛弃了数学史的数学课本都会让初学者感到莫名其妙。</p>
 <p data-pid="gUyDGXww">下面，我会用通俗的语言来告诉大家，当年柯西和魏尔斯特拉斯要弄出这么奇怪的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon-%5Cdelta" alt="\varepsilon-\delta" eeimg="1"> 语言。</p>
 <p data-pid="BTgPL0pg">首先，假设我们忘掉微积分里的所有知识，考虑一个简单的二次函数： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3Dx%5E2" alt="y=x^2" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-424e396c99f81a37ccbb569d39afcb16_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1565" data-rawheight="1339" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-34b5bae12afde8e0949e4fb81346ad22" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-24e22723053be3eb02178ebf5f90a190_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1565" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-424e396c99f81a37ccbb569d39afcb16_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="CYB_DZVK">现在让我们忘掉微积分，忘掉导数，如果让你求该函数在点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%28+%7B1%2C1%7D+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[\left( {1,1} \right)\]" eeimg="1"> 处的切线，你有什么好的办法？</p>
 <p data-pid="wLWqkAr8">如果你把这个问题丢给一个没学导数的初中生，他应该能反应过来：</p>
 <p data-pid="fsrg7p3s">切线不就是与该曲线仅有一个交点的直线嘛！</p>
 <p data-pid="T608vLaY">因为过<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%28+%7B1%2C1%7D+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[\left( {1,1} \right)\]" eeimg="1">点的直线方程为： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5By+-+1+%3D+k%5Cleft%28+%7Bx+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[y - 1 = k\left( {x - 1} \right)\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="V8EsD9Mp">将其与二次函数方程联立： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%5C%7B+%7B%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7B%2A%7B20%7D%7Bc%7D%7D+%7By+-+1+%3D+k%5Cleft%28+%7Bx+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5C%5C+%7By+%3D+%7Bx%5E2%7D%7D+%5Cend%7Barray%7D%7D+%5Cright.%5C%5D" alt="\[\left\{ {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} {y - 1 = k\left( {x - 1} \right)}\\ {y = {x^2}} \end{array}} \right.\]" eeimg="1"> ，这步骤很简单，初中生都可以做到。</p>
 <p data-pid="LWRvDVHR"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7Bx%5E2%7D+%3D+k%5Cleft%28+%7Bx+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29+%2B+1+%5CRightarrow+%7Bx%5E2%7D+-+kx+%2B+%5Cleft%28+%7Bk+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29+%3D+0%5C%5D" alt="\[{x^2} = k\left( {x - 1} \right) + 1 \Rightarrow {x^2} - kx + \left( {k - 1} \right) = 0\]" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="FkbtJsE9">在这我顺便插一句话，我非常非常赞同 <a class="member_mention" href="https://www.zhihu.com/people/915baad335094372f80eeadf7f454501" data-hash="915baad335094372f80eeadf7f454501" data-hovercard="p$b$915baad335094372f80eeadf7f454501">@xinggu</a> 的<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/419254184/answer/1452350272" class="internal">这篇回答</a>里的这段话：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="T2SWm_Zd">
  初次讲解求抛物线 C 与直线 l 的公共点坐标，称职的老师会这样讲：“什么是 C 与 l 的公共点呢？就是既在 C 上，又在 l 上的点，所以它们的坐标应该怎么样呢？因为它们在 C 上，所以满足 C 的方程，又在 l 上，所以也满足 l 的方程。我们就有两个方程了，交点坐标满足这两个方程，对不对？也就是这两个方程所构成的方程组的解。大家想，是不是这样？那么有几个未知数呢？有一个横坐标x，还有一个纵坐标y。现在有两个方程，两个未知数，我们可以试着求解了... ”
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="at2tmdYA">当然了，关于方程 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7Bx%5E2%7D+-+kx+%2B+%5Cleft%28+%7Bk+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29+%3D+0%5C%5D" alt="\[{x^2} - kx + \left( {k - 1} \right) = 0\]" eeimg="1"> ，我们没必要去算它的解，我们先思考思考，这是一个一元二次方程，方程的解对应着直线与抛物线的交点的横坐标。</p>
 <p data-pid="ilj-o8cM">初中老师都教过我们， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5CDelta++%3D+%7Bb%5E2%7D+-+4ac%5C%5D" alt="\[\Delta  = {b^2} - 4ac\]" eeimg="1"> 关系到一元二次方程解的个数。如果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5CDelta++%3E+0%5C%5D" alt="\[\Delta  > 0\]" eeimg="1"> ，那么显然此时直线与抛物线有两个交点。</p>
 <p data-pid="3RtbfwNr">既然我们在寻找切线，那必然是只有一个交点的情形啦，也就是说，只要令<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5CDelta++%3D+0%5C%5D" alt="\[\Delta  = 0\]" eeimg="1"> 即可： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5CDelta++%3D+%7Bb%5E2%7D+-+4ac+%3D+%7Bk%5E2%7D+-+4%5Cleft%28+%7Bk+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29+%3D+0%5C%5D" alt="\[\Delta  = {b^2} - 4ac = {k^2} - 4\left( {k - 1} \right) = 0\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="s-A90rWX">也就是： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5CDelta++%3D+%7Bk%5E2%7D+-+4k+%2B+4+%3D+%7B%5Cleft%28+%7Bk+-+2%7D+%5Cright%29%5E2%7D+%3D+0%5C%5D" alt="\[\Delta  = {k^2} - 4k + 4 = {\left( {k - 2} \right)^2} = 0\]" eeimg="1">，解得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bk+%3D+2%5C%5D" alt="\[k = 2\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="cdpGoQxo">将它画出来：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f19ed29fa865994937eeec1d933af045_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1564" data-rawheight="1472" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-1d1b1b0a24346c439f1ff206640b8d2d" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-cf459bca09998a63eb69e9482b3eb697_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1564" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-f19ed29fa865994937eeec1d933af045_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="_jcQDQZH">嗯，应该是这样！</p>
 <p data-pid="W4oTEOL7">但是！这种做法有一个漏洞……</p>
 <p data-pid="U-Z0P0tk">就是大家看一下下图那条红色的虚线，也与抛物线仅有一个交点：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0d21227aa12058c05b3e7b856ed21d41_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1563" data-rawheight="1452" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-e01eebf47e0a7e787ddaa1141c9017a7" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e6dc86bb1dd061f7127c789f5251ec17_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1563" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-0d21227aa12058c05b3e7b856ed21d41_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="plwbzFqu">这就发现了，我们上面的推导是有漏洞的，之前我们假设切线的斜率是存在的，进而寻找与抛物线仅有一个交点的直线，可是偏偏这样一条斜率不存在的直线也与抛物线的交点仅有一个。</p>
 <p data-pid="i-KxB6m3">直观上，我们很难把这条红色的虚线也当成抛物线的切线，所以我们之前的：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ADDXLyDk">
  切线不就是与该曲线仅有一个交点的直线嘛！
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="xqRVK5oD">这句话是错误的，我们得重新寻找一个合适的定义切线的方式。</p>
 <p data-pid="3x00hd0v">遇到困难的问题，我们得想想最基本的问题，看看它能不能给我们一些启发。最简单的例子就是圆的切线了：</p>
 <p data-pid="0FCT6MkH">我们回忆一下，如果一条直线与圆有两个交点，我们叫它“割线”，像这样：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-77f80ad658ef6006cae8806b61802037_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="952" data-rawheight="1329" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-cd715a444b4c1eac728c83902cfc9ea1" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-754482e45536133c84215a2483b2cbec_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="952" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-77f80ad658ef6006cae8806b61802037_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="lEqH7J2R">如果我们稍微的动一动，不断地偏转直线，使得两个交点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 、 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 不断的靠近一点：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c69096b698ec5f5cf98bd300e5ae0878_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="825" data-rawheight="953" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-68669e41bd90ae52a8adf4bdac121df1" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-392796472b1b3654412b4ee2cef45554_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="825" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-c69096b698ec5f5cf98bd300e5ae0878_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="mufKMcwe">我敢说，随着直线的偏转，使得直线与圆交点<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 、 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1">慢慢变为一点的时候，直线也就从“割线”变成了“切线”。</p>
 <p data-pid="ZTNtptVP">所以，接下来，我们按照上面的思路，给出作抛物线的切线方式：</p>
 <p data-pid="FlFF45bw">过抛物线上的一点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 与另一点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 作割线，再通过偏转直线的方法，使得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 逐渐的向 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 靠拢，当<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 、 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1">两点重合的时候，对应的直线就是切线。</p>
 <p data-pid="y9QatTqN">还是回到刚刚的问题，下面我们用这个思路，求一下抛物线上任意一点的切线。</p>
 <p data-pid="kgIYAil_">首先抛物线上的所有点都满足 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D%2C%7B%7Bx_0%7D%5E2%7D%7D+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[\left( {{x_0},{{x_0}^2}} \right)\]" eeimg="1"> 的形式，不妨把它定为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 点，再在抛物线上任选一非 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 的点 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> ，为了方便书写，不妨假设 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 的坐标为： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%28+%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29%2C%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5E2%7D%7D+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[\left( {\left( {{x_0} + h} \right),{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right)}^2}} \right)\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="FtR9_1Qc">则这条割线的斜率 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bk+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%7B%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5E2%7D+-+%7B%7Bx_0%7D%5E2%7D%7D%7D%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29+-+%7Bx_0%7D%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[k = \frac{{{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right)}^2} - {{x_0}^2}}}{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right) - {x_0}}}\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="Pk285c3W">下面按照上面的思路，不断地让 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 点靠近 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> ：</p>
 <p data-pid="l4mfdmwX">（几何画板的点我不会写下标，所以我只能把原本的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B_1%2CB_2%2CB_3+%5Ccdots+" alt="B_1,B_2,B_3 \cdots " eeimg="1"> 写成 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B%2CC%2CD+%5Ccdots+" alt="B,C,D \cdots " eeimg="1"> ）</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-1891f679fae6b122f47ecf602efa3b40_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1561" data-rawheight="1330" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-36ec7506c94ef6141e111b01384e617c" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-e5551e4564ae14e522cc3f1644ed0585_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1561" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-1891f679fae6b122f47ecf602efa3b40_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="rQzmaF-M">首先化简： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bk+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%7B%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5E2%7D+-+%7B%7Bx_0%7D%5E2%7D%7D%7D%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29+-+%7Bx_0%7D%7D%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%7B2%7Bx_0%7Dh+%2B+%7Bh%5E2%7D%7D%7D%7Bh%7D+%3D+2%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%5C%5D" alt="\[k = \frac{{{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right)}^2} - {{x_0}^2}}}{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right) - {x_0}}} = \frac{{2{x_0}h + {h^2}}}{h} = 2{x_0} + h\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="2CVTa7j-">随着 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=B" alt="B" eeimg="1"> 点逐渐的向 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=A" alt="A" eeimg="1"> 靠近， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h" alt="h" eeimg="1"> 的绝对值也越来越小，割线的斜率 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B2u+%2B+h%5C%5D" alt="\[2u + h\]" eeimg="1"> 也越来越趋向于真正切线的斜率，显然：</p>
 <p data-pid="cHziN703">当两点重合，也就是是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h" alt="h" eeimg="1"> 变成 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 的时候，此时真正切线的斜率 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bk+%3D+2%7Bx_0%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[k = 2{x_0}\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="64POcY-9">取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bx_0%7D%3D1" alt="{x_0}=1" eeimg="1"> ，则 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D2" alt="k=2" eeimg="1">， 与我们之前的结果一样，这说明我们的推导应该是合理的。</p>
 <p data-pid="d4brCoMz">当然了，这个方法不但比之前用 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5CDelta+%5C%5D" alt="\[\Delta \]" eeimg="1"> 算方便不少，还更可以推广到更一般的情况。</p>
 <p data-pid="_Pw6I7Sa">对一个曲线来说，假设其方程为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5By+%3D+f%5Cleft%28+x+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[y = f\left( x \right)\]" eeimg="1"> ，那么首先算出其割线的斜率：</p>
 <p data-pid="ZWOFE_J_"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bk+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%7Bf%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29+-+f%5Cleft%28+x_0+%5Cright%29%7D%7D%7Bh%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[k = \frac{{f\left( {{x_0} + h} \right) - f\left( x_0 \right)}}{h}\]" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="zXHnoMNM">然后化简，最后再令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3D0" alt="h=0" eeimg="1"> ，得到的结果就是它在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bx_0%7D" alt="{x_0}" eeimg="1"> 点切线的斜率。</p>
 <p data-pid="RXejcu0L">我们把最后得到的这个玩意叫作 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bf%5Cleft%28+x+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[f\left( x \right)\]" eeimg="1"> 在<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7Bx_0%7D" alt="{x_0}" eeimg="1"> 点的导数，如果再抽象一点，一开始就不设什么 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7Bx_0%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{x_0}\]" eeimg="1"> ，纯粹的把它当成一个函数看，我们把它叫作函数<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bf%5Cleft%28+x+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[f\left( x \right)\]" eeimg="1">的导函数，记作： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bf%27%5Cleft%28+x+%5Cright%29%5C%5D" alt="\[f'\left( x \right)\]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="i-G01n_P">看上去很完美，不是吗？</p>
 <p data-pid="7yrcAiJT">但是！（又来但是了，但这真的是必不可少的）</p>
 <p data-pid="0UQ6J7f1">这种新方法虽然一举解决了成千上万种曲线的切线问题，但数学家发现，这里面有一个很严重的逻辑漏洞！</p>
 <p data-pid="EKVuZ7EO">我们回过头看一下，我们开始先列出式子：</p>
 <p data-pid="nl-Uc1Bj"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5Bk+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%7B%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5E2%7D+-+%7Bx_0%7D%5E2%7D%7D%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%7D+%5Cright%29+-+%7Bx_0%7D%7D%7D+%3D+%5Cfrac%7B%7B2%7Bx_0%7Dh+%2B+%7Bh%5E2%7D%7D%7D%7Bh%7D+%3D+2%7Bx_0%7D+%2B+h%5C%5D" alt="\[k = \frac{{{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right)}^2} - {x_0}^2}}{{\left( {{x_0} + h} \right) - {x_0}}} = \frac{{2{x_0}h + {h^2}}}{h} = 2{x_0} + h\]" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="LJ636O1N">这一步里的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h" alt="h" eeimg="1"> 显然是不等于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 的，毕竟 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 不可以作除数。</p>
 <p data-pid="QRWYWF9j">但是下一步，我们又“<b>出尔反尔</b>”的令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h%3D0" alt="h=0" eeimg="1"> ，得到最后切线的斜率是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=k%3D2x_0" alt="k=2x_0" eeimg="1"> 的结论。</p>
 <p data-pid="eufqWzMG">所以，这个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=h" alt="h" eeimg="1"> 到底是不是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0+" alt="0 " eeimg="1"> ？成为了当时数学家最纠结的问题。</p>

 <p data-pid="qRZwmTnH">以上灵感基本来源于张景中院士的《数学家的眼光》。</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="NhRuKeIg">以上，是人类为什么要建立<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon-%5Cdelta" alt="\varepsilon-\delta" eeimg="1"> 语言的背景，你只需要知道建立这个东西就是为了补全微积分这摇摇欲坠的大厦。</p>
 <p data-pid="3Q9HVZfd">从牛顿莱布尼兹建立微积分，到柯西与魏尔斯特拉斯真正打好地基，这之间一百三十多年无数数学家前赴后继。这段历史肯定没法讲，如果感兴趣，可以去看《微积分的历程》。</p>
 <p data-pid="PFdhkyuQ">接下来我打算简单讲一下建立那么拗口的语言的合理性。</p>
 <p data-pid="XvS7FKHB">每次我给学弟学妹讲 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon-N" alt="\varepsilon-N" eeimg="1"> 和<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon-%5Cdelta" alt="\varepsilon-\delta" eeimg="1"> 语言的时候，我都会先举一个非常显然，但却不太好讲的例子：</p>
 <p data-pid="bU5quTb8">为什么数列 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%5C%7B+%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D+%5Cright%5C%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[\left\{ {\frac{1}{n}} \right\}\]" eeimg="1"> 的极限是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0+" alt="0 " eeimg="1"> ？</p>
 <p data-pid="rJlx4hgL">我给学弟学妹讲这里的时候，为了更好的让他们理解，我就直接抛出这个问题</p>
 <p data-pid="tPDGotVW">也见到了很多种说法</p>
 <p data-pid="P7yM_nea">有的说：因为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1"> 随着 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 的增大会离 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 越来越近，</p>
 <p data-pid="-Y0_Kr1J">不好意思，反例是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1"> 随着 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 的增大也会离 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1" alt="-1" eeimg="1"> 越来越近，那你凭什么认为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">的极限是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 而不是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1" alt="-1" eeimg="1"> 呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="zIWbp_Zv">立刻就会有人说：肯定不会是负数啊，因为当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%3E0" alt="x>0" eeimg="1"> 时，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bx%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{x}}\]" eeimg="1"> 恒大于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> ，所以<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">的极限不可能是负数啊</p>
 <p data-pid="Bf-bRSuf">不好意思，我会立刻举出一个新的例子 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%5C%7B+%7B%5Cfrac%7B%7B%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5En%7D%7D%7D%7Bn%7D%7D+%5Cright%5C%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[\left\{ {\frac{{{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}}}{n}} \right\}\]" eeimg="1">，它就不是恒大于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> ，但极限还是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> .</p>
 <p data-pid="oq87wk8v">之前的那伙人就说，这个例子 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%7B%5Cfrac%7B%7B%7B%7B%5Cleft%28+%7B+-+1%7D+%5Cright%29%7D%5En%7D%7D%7D%7Bn%7D%7D" alt="{\frac{{{{\left( { - 1} \right)}^n}}}{n}}" eeimg="1"> ，随着<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 的增大并不会离 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-1" alt="-1" eeimg="1"> 越来越近啊</p>
 <p data-pid="uoWZCIcd">这个时候我会让他们停一下，纠结这些细枝末节的东西是没有什么意义的，你上面所举例的东西至多对一两个例子成立，我稍微换一个例子你就得换一套说辞，根本没有抓住最本质的东西。</p>
 <p data-pid="oOrnGs01">能不能找到一种方法，直击要害，一网打尽所有的数列极限问题？</p>
 <p data-pid="ZngQmSjz">这样的对话一般会持续很久很久，在此过程中，我会不断地、反复地向他们强调，你们所举的原因只是特例，不是什么根本性的原因</p>
 <p data-pid="mFyWjD8c">我希望在这样辩论的过程中，让他们好好想想极限究竟意味着什么。</p>
 <p data-pid="EHau-B_i">一般很久以后，才会有人一拍桌子：因为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1"> 随着 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 的增大，可以与 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离任意小！</p>
 <p data-pid="hu_EkwzW">对了，关键就是这个任意小！</p>
 <p data-pid="qU4nJs9M">什么叫任意小？</p>
 <p data-pid="3L9X1bdC">就是说你随便取一个数，比方说 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0.01" alt="0.01" eeimg="1"> 吧，则随着 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 大于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=100" alt="100" eeimg="1"> 以后，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">与<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离永远小于<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0.01" alt="0.01" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="A3cI9pJP">取 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0.001%EF%BC%8C0.0000001" alt="0.001，0.0000001" eeimg="1"> 什么的都可以，总是在某一项之后，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">与<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离要小于它们。</p>
 <p data-pid="vIuq4oSP">那就任取呗，反正就是看<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">与<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离是不是要多小有多小？</p>
 <p data-pid="9B9ckoSy">那随便取一个大于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 的数呗，试试是不是某一项以后<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">与<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离都小于这个数？</p>
 <p data-pid="Pm_47Y0E">哦，那就设一个希腊字母 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon" alt="\varepsilon" eeimg="1"> ，假设这个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0" alt="\varepsilon>0" eeimg="1"> ，看看是不是某一项以后，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">与<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离要小于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon" alt="\varepsilon" eeimg="1"> ？</p>
 <p data-pid="SNrM8zUI">再抽象一下，对任意的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon%3E0" alt="\varepsilon>0" eeimg="1">，是否存在一个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=N" alt="N" eeimg="1"> 为正整数，当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3EN" alt="n>N" eeimg="1"> 时，使得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%7C+%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D+-+0%7D+%5Cright%7C+%3C+%5Cvarepsilon+%5C%5D" alt="\[\left| {\frac{1}{n} - 0} \right| < \varepsilon \]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="R6kS2UU3">经过检验，发现是存在的，所以我们就认为数列 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%5C%7B+%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D+%5Cright%5C%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[\left\{ {\frac{1}{n}} \right\}\]" eeimg="1"> 的极限是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0+" alt="0 " eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="3ATlSITj">总结一下： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon" alt="\varepsilon" eeimg="1"> 实际上赋予了一种动态性。</p>
 <p data-pid="jEKyXPxu">其他数列同理，抽象一下我们给出数列极限定义：</p>
 <p data-pid="TKOqUdJ7"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bn+%5Cto+%5Cinfty+%7D+%7Ba_n%7D+%3D+A+%5CLeftrightarrow+%5C%5D" alt="\[\mathop {\lim }\limits_{n \to \infty } {a_n} = A \Leftrightarrow \]" eeimg="1"> 对 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon++%3E+0%5C%5D" alt="\[\forall \varepsilon  > 0\]" eeimg="1">， 总<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cexists+N+%5Cin+%7BN_+%2B+%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[\exists N \in {N_ + }\]" eeimg="1">， 当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3EN" alt="n>N" eeimg="1"> 时，有： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%7C+%7B%7Ba_n%7D+-+A%7D+%5Cright%7C+%3C+%5Cvarepsilon+%5C%5D" alt="\[\left| {{a_n} - A} \right| < \varepsilon \]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="9ePqJQ8k">老实说，我觉得函数的<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cvarepsilon-%5Cdelta" alt="\varepsilon-\delta" eeimg="1"> 语言同理：</p>
 <p data-pid="9o2hmBib"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cmathop+%7B%5Clim+%7D%5Climits_%7Bx+%5Cto+%7Bx_0%7D%7D+f%5Cleft%28+x+%5Cright%29+%3D+A+%5CLeftrightarrow+%5C%5D" alt="\[\mathop {\lim }\limits_{x \to {x_0}} f\left( x \right) = A \Leftrightarrow \]" eeimg="1">对 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cforall+%5Cvarepsilon++%3E+0%5C%5D" alt="\[\forall \varepsilon  > 0\]" eeimg="1">， 总<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cexists+%5Cdelta%3E0%5C%5D" alt="\[\exists \delta>0\]" eeimg="1">， 当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B0+%3C+%5Cleft%7C+%7Bx+-+%7Bx_0%7D%7D+%5Cright%7C+%3C+%5Cdelta+%5C%5D" alt="\[0 < \left| {x - {x_0}} \right| < \delta \]" eeimg="1"> 时，有： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%7C+%7Bf%5Cleft%28+x+%5Cright%29+-+A%7D+%5Cright%7C+%3C+%5Cvarepsilon+%5C%5D" alt="\[\left| {f\left( x \right) - A} \right| < \varepsilon \]" eeimg="1"> 。</p>

 <p data-pid="UTWxGIGK">关于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[{\frac{1}{n}}\]" eeimg="1">与<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离要小于任意数，还有一个重要的思想是“一直保持下去”</p>
 <p data-pid="vYr53wqp">什么意思呢？就是只有一部分满足是不够的，要自某一项以后，数列的每一项都满足这一条件。</p>
 <p data-pid="5O5oV1lC">比方说数列 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%5C%7B+%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D+%5Cright%5C%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[\left\{ {\frac{1}{n}} \right\}\]" eeimg="1"> 与数列 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5C%5B%5Cleft%5C%7B+1+%5Cright%5C%7D%5C%5D" alt="\[\left\{ 1 \right\}\]" eeimg="1"> 组成的交错数列： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1%E3%80%811%E3%80%81%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%7D%E3%80%811%E3%80%81%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B3%7D%7D%E3%80%811%E3%80%81+%5Ccdots+" alt="1、1、{\frac{1}{2}}、1、{\frac{1}{3}}、1、 \cdots " eeimg="1"> ，</p>
 <p data-pid="slSAUKN_">虽然它确实对任意的正数，在某一项以后，确实某一些项与 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离可以任意小，但不能做到全部都与 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 之间的距离任意小，所以它的极限不是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 。（当然了，事实上它没有极限）</p>

# 为什么很多「神童」长大后反而不那么优秀？
- 点赞数：14178

<body>
 <p data-pid="Amo01vVt">因为真正的神童是下面这样子的：</p>
 <p data-pid="WX3DrFKq">朗道：</p>
 <p data-pid="qOCh-qC1">13岁就中学毕业；</p>
 <p data-pid="XK9znXLx">14岁巴库大学学习数学、物理学和化学</p>
 <p data-pid="7AmiX9ft">16岁在巴库大学毕业</p>
 <p data-pid="rSGuYXRT">19岁引入了<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%2587%258F%25E5%25AD%2590%25E5%258A%259B%25E5%25AD%25A6" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">量子力学</a>中的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E5%25AF%2586%25E5%25BA%25A6%25E7%259F%25A9%25E9%2598%25B5" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">密度矩阵</a>概念（1927）</p>
 <p data-pid="itU6ItWp">22岁建立金属的电子抗磁性的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%2587%258F%25E5%25AD%2590%25E7%2590%2586%25E8%25AE%25BA" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">量子理论</a>（1930）</p>
 <p data-pid="_q3v3TNr">24岁到哈尔科夫从事研究和教学工作</p>
 <p data-pid="WklQtE1H">27岁提出<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%2593%2581%25E7%25A3%2581%25E4%25BD%2593" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">铁磁体</a>的磁畴结构和<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E5%258F%258D%25E9%2593%2581%25E7%25A3%2581%25E6%2580%25A7" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">反铁磁性</a>的解释（1935）</p>
 <p data-pid="vSKs_D2X">28岁提出<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E4%25BA%258C%25E7%25BA%25A7%25E7%259B%25B8%25E5%258F%2598" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">二级相变</a>理论（1936～1937）</p>
 <p data-pid="JAb0AgNv">29岁到莫斯科担任苏联科学院物理问题研究所的理论部负责人，并在莫斯科大学任教，提出原子核的统计理论（1937）</p>
 <p data-pid="B-rJeq-m">32岁提出液态氦Ⅱ超流动性的量子理论（1940～1941）</p>
 <p data-pid="OkgJSpkX">32岁开始陆续编写大名鼎鼎的朗道十卷，很多人一辈子都看不完，俗称朗道势垒。</p><a data-draft-node="block" data-draft-type="mcn-link-card" data-mcn-id="1267809717398913024"></a>

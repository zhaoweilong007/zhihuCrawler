# 你见过哪些在厕所隔间门上的奇葩句子？
- 点赞数：13840
- 更新时间：2015年08月25日17时48分00秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/29753074/answer/45760063
<body>
 <p data-pid="CvsK2lBT">在美国西部某小路旁脏兮兮的加油站厕所门上看到过小诗一首——</p>
 <p data-pid="4wlH5rJy">Some come here to sit and think.</p>
 <p data-pid="pZjEmXr0">Some come here to shit and stink.</p>
 <p data-pid="sFSPBgnz">But I come here to scratch my balls,</p>
 <p data-pid="RZPAGa37">And read the writings on the walls.</p>
 <br><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/people/bfc027dc03d66cddb7acdfa19ba2b78c" data-hash="bfc027dc03d66cddb7acdfa19ba2b78c" class="member_mention" data-hovercard="p$b$bfc027dc03d66cddb7acdfa19ba2b78c">@张影影</a>
 <p data-pid="Hpf2ym-b">的翻译——</p>
 <p data-pid="fF0nNrae">熙熙沉思，攘攘屙屎，唯我到此，挠蛋赏诗。</p>

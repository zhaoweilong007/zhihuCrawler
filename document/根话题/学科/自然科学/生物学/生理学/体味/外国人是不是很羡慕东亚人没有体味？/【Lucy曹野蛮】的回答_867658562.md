# 外国人是不是很羡慕东亚人没有体味？
- 点赞数：15442
- 更新时间：2019年10月23日23时19分44秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/327285145/answer/867658562
<body>
 <p data-pid="TBMdsYeM">我是外国人，我在中国生活一年，我要回答这个问题</p>
 <p data-pid="EXY4-GMf">是的，我觉得外国人很嫉妒。自从我来到中国，我必须说我感觉好多了，如果我进入某个地方，我不必闻到别人的汗味。斯洛伐克男人很臭，尤其是在夏天，他们经常不使用除臭剂。我记得有一次我去斯洛伐克村的邮局，天气很热，因为那里的臭味我差点晕倒。真是难以置信。但在中国，我从来没有遇到过。</p>
 <p data-pid="mOUWjmLZ">另一方面，当斯洛伐克男人闻起来难闻，不使用除臭剂时，斯洛伐克女孩太喜欢用香水了。如果一个女孩从你身边走过，在她离开很久之后，香水还在空气中飘荡。所以你可以想象汗水和浓烈的香水的结合…这让你很恶心。</p>
 <p data-pid="1keTgFoV">谢谢中国不臭！</p>
 <p data-pid="o2a-63TG">我中文不好，以上用翻译软件，我的英文回答:</p>
 <p data-pid="odp9EncM">Yeah I think foreigners are jealous of that. Since I came to China I must say I feel much better I don't have to smell someone's sweat if I enter somewhere. Slovak guys are very smelly especially in summer and they often don't use deodorant. I remember one time when I went to the post office in Slovak village, it was a very hot day, and I almost fainted because of the bad smell in there. It was really incredible. But in China it never happened to me. On the other hand when Slovak guys smell bad and don't use deodorant, Slovak girls love to use perfume way too much. If a girl walks past you, long after she is gone, the perfume is still hanging in the air. So you can imagine the combination of a sweat a very strong perfume... it makes you really sick. Thank you China for not being smelly</p>

 <p data-pid="SvhqzFtQ">只用青椒、大蒜和盐就能做，0厨艺也没问题，</p>
 <p data-pid="gjJw8WiD">做完了保存一周不坏，配饭配面配一切的万能酱，</p>
 <p data-pid="YEIOvBva">符合你要求咩？</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lv3IpvPX">事情是这样的，我写了个“宅家美食日记”系列的菜谱，初衷就是“<b>用有限的食材制作无限的美味</b>”，唔，但是有多有限呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="zNn_jXad">大约就是有限到，今早我拉开冰箱门，发现冰箱里只剩下了<b>十几个青辣椒对我笑得绿油油</b>.....</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3s_VHQrD">呵呵，以为我拿你没办法么？</p>

 <p data-pid="zvOZjt_G"><b>精华步骤：</b></p>
 <h2><b>辣椒焙干——所有原料切碎——下油爆香</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rlL47NGk">是的，为了避免太长劝退，我会尽量在详细做法前放上精华步骤，记住了就真的不难。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-vvjj3Ua"><b>详细做法：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xRonsNbG">1、螺丝椒洗干净，擦干水（很重要），切掉头柄，不需要去籽。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-5a5573fb655be720f131489bcbdef7fb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-5a5573fb655be720f131489bcbdef7fb" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-4b9dcaa493479c276816a39c5cb56925_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-5a5573fb655be720f131489bcbdef7fb_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="yl0OWiwR">2、找一个干净的平底锅，无油无水，把辣椒铺平在底部，最小火烘烤。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7ec7a1d259fdfb8421e726aeb2de786d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="693" data-original-token="v2-7ec7a1d259fdfb8421e726aeb2de786d" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9a9a4f162d25142ff9dcef905b909ef7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-7ec7a1d259fdfb8421e726aeb2de786d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zqx-HVnQ">是的，如果你觉得螺丝椒不好铺平，也可以横剖一刀，我就省掉这个步骤了。</p>
 <p data-pid="y3RnMWiN">烤到辣椒身上出现点点焦斑就好了，不要心疼它，这一步是为了蒸发掉辣椒多余的水分，让酱料更易储存；同时加热可以激发出辣椒的香味，让味道更柔和。</p>

 </figure>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-875a1d6ce597b1fee4618bf56b065aa6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="903" data-original-token="v2-875a1d6ce597b1fee4618bf56b065aa6" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-65cb590238610b9d44e909d98fea2756_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-875a1d6ce597b1fee4618bf56b065aa6_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tRwE_hxL">好了取出来放在一边晾凉。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-265eac26b0ab9a508e30923e0a11a762_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="785" data-original-token="v2-265eac26b0ab9a508e30923e0a11a762" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f8754f7ad2a3d1c8acd33bd47f370dff_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-265eac26b0ab9a508e30923e0a11a762_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yfyQ0Ulh">3、一头大蒜剥干净、小红椒去杆，一起切碎成燕麦片大小的颗粒。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3fc4eba748ddeb7f36095abc5b9e3ee2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1100" data-original-token="v2-3fc4eba748ddeb7f36095abc5b9e3ee2" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-be7bd2985cf2d2084867cfc4d1bca356_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-3fc4eba748ddeb7f36095abc5b9e3ee2_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="z6ibec6_">有搅蒜器的话更好，三两下搞定！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-8089e268c88e89b5db04908eed70365d_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="440" data-rawheight="248" data-original-token="v2-8089e268c88e89b5db04908eed70365d" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-8089e268c88e89b5db04908eed70365d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="440" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-8089e268c88e89b5db04908eed70365d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="N-1vw6QB">插一句，我真的万年安利这个张小泉的拉蒜器啊！谁用谁知道的那种好用，我还专门写答案“歌颂”过它：</p><a data-draft-node="block" data-draft-type="mcn-link-card" data-mcn-id="1351129135595655168"></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hTUhvkRA">4、螺丝椒晾凉（为了不烫手而已），先剁碎成段，再切碎；</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d117baa93eaf82d05ba2c8ce355273c0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-d117baa93eaf82d05ba2c8ce355273c0" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d365c3790dbd1f99030d4c2d21b48e3f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-d117baa93eaf82d05ba2c8ce355273c0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LKgcbXry">我再次建议买个搅蒜器，做剁椒酱简直犹如神助，讲真没有这个搅蒜器我才懒得做酱。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bddbc1d1be68eb024e23d4c22d1a16a3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="822" data-original-token="v2-bddbc1d1be68eb024e23d4c22d1a16a3" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-b91b7e3e27ea8f5d88fe56a82c596c27_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-bddbc1d1be68eb024e23d4c22d1a16a3_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uEn4QiI8">5、起锅下油，油的量要能没过所有酱。</p>
 <p data-pid="sGYNUNjO">先下花椒、花生碎（白芝麻）和蒜末小火爆香。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-291bb9724d9a00c10a73db5e91a0d6d5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-291bb9724d9a00c10a73db5e91a0d6d5" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-fee009f1ca6d8c05e678eda30fa5d543_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-291bb9724d9a00c10a73db5e91a0d6d5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PNZ3zviM">6、再下青椒熬制，同时加入盐和一点点糖。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-e6bdf9871b3df22399dcbc150c540f9d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-e6bdf9871b3df22399dcbc150c540f9d" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-b3e1af1d783d4826b4a2cb96033a8ad9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-e6bdf9871b3df22399dcbc150c540f9d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="L0DDffTV">盐的量要比你平时做菜重得多，毕竟是酱料，大概尝起来是你觉得咸到微微齁的程度就可以了。糖加一点点，提鲜用的，手指头捏一小簇就行。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1208739382037135360" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="宅在家里能做些什么有趣的事儿？" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-5b202a999a73c6bd8fb936d3dfaa4ed1.png" data-lens-id="1208739382037135360"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-5b202a999a73c6bd8fb936d3dfaa4ed1.png"><span class="content"><span class="title">宅在家里能做些什么有趣的事儿？<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1208739382037135360</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Yq1_HJt0">熬到油变绿了，就说明原料已经happy together了。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-97b82d73b220a0a1f9f7bf5a66cd3877_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="810" data-original-token="v2-97b82d73b220a0a1f9f7bf5a66cd3877" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ca856627d7d40d339a847bea79dddd2b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-97b82d73b220a0a1f9f7bf5a66cd3877_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="a1sST6Y5">7、找个干净瓶子or盒子盛起来，放凉了搁冰箱，你就拥有了一周的小菜。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-769c6505e1bce903dfae773b62a10a1d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-769c6505e1bce903dfae773b62a10a1d" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6d0f6237aa6b7ef0484089b87e05e18f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-769c6505e1bce903dfae773b62a10a1d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="67sRDlT0">隔夜味道会更好，因为会更融合。吃的时候用干净勺子挖出来一点，不要污染整体。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-1f1eaea51bd09f083a3b003587ad35ea_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-1f1eaea51bd09f083a3b003587ad35ea" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-345b2aa0b181accae93c9afe3c88eda4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-1f1eaea51bd09f083a3b003587ad35ea_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Kp0zkAIB">这个酱啊，真真儿简单，除了烘干那里需要点耐心之外，其余基本不要厨艺，而且味道绝不是你想的那种单调的辣，而是清新有辣味，浸在油里香气扑鼻的那种。</p>

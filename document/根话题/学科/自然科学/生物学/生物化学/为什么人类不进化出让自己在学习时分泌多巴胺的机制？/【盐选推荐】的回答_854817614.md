# 为什么人类不进化出让自己在学习时分泌多巴胺的机制？

<body>
 <p>这个问题很有趣<span><span>。</span></span>我们首先得了解多巴胺的实质是什么<span><span>，</span></span>以及它在我们大脑中的作用机制<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>多巴胺大概是多数人最耳熟能详的神经科学术语了<span><span>。</span></span> 更广为流传的一个概念大概是<span><span>「</span></span>多巴胺是快乐物质<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>这个误解导致很多不明真相的人以为<span><span>，</span></span>直接往大脑里注射多巴胺<span><span>，</span></span>就能快乐<span><span>。</span></span>在知乎上搜索<span><span>「</span></span>多巴胺<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>光是看问题就可见一斑<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>人生的意义是不是就是多巴胺<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>未来人类是否会用多巴胺取代所有娱乐活动<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>大脑怎样才能分泌更多的多巴胺让自己开心<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span>如果你明白多巴胺到底是怎么运作的<span><span>，</span></span>就不会有这样的误解了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>多巴胺的个人简历</strong></p>
 <p>英文名<span><span>：</span></span>dopamine<span><span>，</span></span> 缩写 DA</p>
 <p>籍贯<span><span>：</span></span>大脑里有好几个区域生产多巴胺<span><span>，</span></span>但最主要的在基底核<span><span>（</span></span>basal ganglia<span><span>）</span></span>里<span><span>。</span></span>基底核里有两个区域<span><span>：</span></span> 黑质 <span><span>（</span></span>substantia nigra<span><span>）</span></span>和 腹侧被盖区 <span><span>（</span></span>ventral tegmental area<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>负责生产多巴胺<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>常居地<span><span>：</span></span>黑质—&gt; 纹状体 <span><span>（</span></span>striatum<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>；</span></span> 腹侧被盖区—&gt; 前额皮层<span><span>（</span></span>prefrontal cortex<span><span>）</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>多巴胺的三大功能</strong></p>
 <p>不卖关子了<span><span>，</span></span>多巴胺主要有三个功能<span><span>，</span></span> <strong>运动控制<span><span>、</span></span>行为选择和强化学习</strong> <span><span>。</span></span>你可能有点懵逼<span><span>，</span></span>怎么感觉它们三个风马牛不相及呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>我第一次学的时候也觉得莫名其妙<span><span>。</span></span>我琢磨了很久该怎么捋多巴胺的功能<span><span>，</span></span>改了又改<span><span>，</span></span>最后我决定按图索骥——从多巴胺在大脑里的实际分布来讲<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>多巴胺从何而来<span><span>？</span></span>大脑里的绝大多数多巴胺产于一个叫基底核的中脑区域<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>其中<span><span>，</span></span>又有两大产地<span><span>：</span></span>黑质 <span><span>（</span></span>substantia nigra<span><span>）</span></span>和 腹侧被盖区 <span><span>（</span></span>ventral tegmental area<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>它们俩挨得特别近<span><span>，</span></span>但我们一定要将它们分开讲<span><span>，</span></span>因为产地决定了多巴胺的最终去处<span><span>，</span></span>也很大程度上决定了它们的功能<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>#额外说几句#</p>
 <p>基底核的英文名是<span><span>「</span></span>Basal ganglia<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>如果你对神经解剖学有一些了解的话<span><span>，</span></span>就会发现这个翻译不对劲<span><span>。</span></span>Ganglia<span><span>（</span></span>单数为 ganglion<span><span>）</span></span>的中文翻译应该是神经<span><span>「</span></span>节<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>指的是周边神经系统的神经核团<span><span>（</span></span>神经核团 = 神经细胞的胞体的聚集<span><span>，</span></span>a cluster of neurons<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>而<span><span>「</span></span>核<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>nucleus<span><span>）</span></span>是指的中枢神经系统的神经核团<span><span>。</span></span>那为什么不叫基底节<span><span>，</span></span>而叫基底核呢<span><span>？</span></span>原因出在英文本身<span><span>，</span></span>它本身就是错的<span><span>；</span></span>基底核属于中枢神经系统<span><span>，</span></span>而不是周边神经系统<span><span>，</span></span>所以它本应该叫 basal nuclei<span><span>，</span></span>而不是 basal ganglia<span><span>。</span></span>这个错误的英语名字现在已经成为习惯<span><span>，</span></span>但在被翻译成中文的时候<span><span>，</span></span>被纠正了<span><span>。</span></span>这种<span><span>「</span></span>英文错的<span><span>，</span></span>中文翻译反而是对的<span><span>」</span></span>的例子实在少见<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>咱们换一种方式来解释<span><span>：</span></span>多巴胺是从农村进城的打工仔<span><span>。</span></span>这群打工仔主要来自一个省份<span><span>，</span></span>叫做基底核<span><span>。</span></span>细分一下<span><span>，</span></span>多巴胺的籍贯来自这个省份两个紧挨的村儿<span><span>，</span></span>一个叫做黑质<span><span>，</span></span>另一叫做腹侧被盖区<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>因为一些未知原因<span><span>，</span></span>来自黑质的多巴胺<span><span>，</span></span>走不远<span><span>，</span></span>出村后全体都会去同省的另一个城市<span><span>，</span></span>叫纹状体<span><span>（</span></span>striatum<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>去的多了<span><span>，</span></span>就形成了通路<span><span>，</span></span>这个通路被称为<span><span>「</span></span>黑质纹状体通路<span><span>（</span></span>nigrostriatal pathway<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>这群多巴胺主要就是负责自主运动的调节和控制<span><span>，</span></span>比如维持一个姿势或执行一个动作<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但黑质这个村儿啊<span><span>，</span></span>有个问题<span><span>，</span></span>因为一些未知原因<span><span>，</span></span>村上的建筑物<span><span>（</span></span>也就是负责生产多巴胺的神经细胞<span><span>）</span></span>特别容易一片儿一片儿的受损甚至彻底坍塌<span><span>（</span></span>也就是细胞死亡<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>这些细胞一死<span><span>，</span></span>人就会出现无法控制运动的现象<span><span>，</span></span>比如手抖<span><span>、</span></span>路都走不了<span><span>，</span></span>严重就会出现帕金森<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>帕金森</strong> 是一种通常在老年人的大脑中出现的疾病<span><span>，</span></span>其最明显的症状对动作失去控制权<span><span>，</span></span>比如身体僵硬<span><span>、</span></span>动作迟缓<span><span>、</span></span>或是四肢在不动的时候出现不自主的颤抖等等<span><span>。</span></span>现阶段帕金森是不可治愈的<span><span>，</span></span>但可以减缓症状<span><span>。</span></span>现在最常见的帕金森治疗方式就是摄入 L-DOPA<span><span>。</span></span>L-DOPA 是多巴胺的一个前体<span><span>。</span></span>那为啥不直接摄入多巴胺呢<span><span>？</span></span>因为多巴胺不能经过血脑屏障<span><span>，</span></span>你无论是口服还是直接打入血液<span><span>，</span></span>都没办法进入大脑<span><span>，</span></span>那没啥用<span><span>，</span></span>但 L-DOPA 可以<span><span>。</span></span>这个 L-DOPA 必须长期使用<span><span>，</span></span>而且还有很多副作用<span><span>，</span></span>更重要的是<span><span>，</span></span>它治标不治本<span><span>。</span></span>等大脑里负责生产多巴胺的神经细胞大片死亡<span><span>，</span></span>那注入再多的 L-DOPA 都没用了<span><span>。</span></span>而患有帕金森的大脑最明显的标志<span><span>，</span></span>就是黑质里的神经细胞大片死亡<span><span>，</span></span>进而导致黑质纹状体通路里的多巴胺水平降低<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>从另一个村儿——腹侧被盖区——出来的多巴胺<span><span>，</span></span>就走得比较远了<span><span>，</span></span>都要出省<span><span>。</span></span>绝大多数会走得特别远<span><span>，</span></span>去前额皮层<span><span>（</span></span>prefrontal cortex<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>途径前扣带回<span><span>（</span></span>anterior cingulate<span><span>）</span></span>和眼窝前额皮质<span><span>（</span></span>orbitofrontal cortex<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>这形成<span><span>「</span></span>中脑皮层通路<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>mesocortical pathway<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>走这个路子的多巴胺主要和 <strong>做决策</strong> 有关<span><span>。</span></span></p>

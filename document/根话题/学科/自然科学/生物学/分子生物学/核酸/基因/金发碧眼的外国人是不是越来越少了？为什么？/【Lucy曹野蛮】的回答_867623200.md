 <p data-pid="ygzOfeDo">是的，现在白人不仅和其他白人有孩子。所以基因开始混合。混血儿也不太可能有金发。由于越来越多的白人与非白人生孩子，因此出生的金发孩子越来越少。我也不认为红头发的人更多。红发是极其罕见的，所以红发的人比金发的人要少得多。</p>
 <p data-pid="N7TbQiWh">最近，白人看起来更具民族色彩（皮肤更黑，头发更黑）也是非常流行的，所以可能会有更少的人选择把头发染成金色。所以也许这就是你这样想的原因。也许这种趋势在未来会改变，你会看到更多金发碧眼的人。</p>
 <p data-pid="DisQQ7da">Yeah these days white people don't only have children with other white people. So the genes are getting mixed. And mixed children are less likely to have blond hair. Since more and more white people have children with non white people, less children with blond hair are being born. Also I don't think there are more people with red hair. Red hair is extremely rare so there are much less people born with red hair than people born with blond hair.</p>
 <p data-pid="WQsys5gS">Recently it's also very fashionable for white people to look more ethnic (have darker skin, darker hair) so maybe less people will choose to dye their hair blond. So maybe that's why you feel this way. Maybe this trend will change in the future and you will see more blond people.</p>

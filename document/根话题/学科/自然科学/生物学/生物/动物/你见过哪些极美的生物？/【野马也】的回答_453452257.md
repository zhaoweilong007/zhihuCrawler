 <p data-pid="GXQiM6sF">另外附上一点介绍（以下内容来自百度百科）</p>
 <p data-pid="f_o-rwCt">短尾矮袋鼠（学名：<i>Setonix brachyurus</i>）。它是最小的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E8%25A2%258B%25E9%25BC%25A0/1724" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">袋鼠</a>之一，体高不到60厘米。短尾矮袋鼠是有袋目<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E5%2593%25BA%25E4%25B9%25B3%25E5%258A%25A8%25E7%2589%25A9/62197" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">哺乳动物</a>，看起来像老鼠一样。它们大多生活在澳大利亚西南海岸外的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%25B4%259B%25E7%2589%25B9%25E5%25B0%25BC%25E6%2596%25AF%25E5%25B2%259B/8802411" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">洛特尼斯岛</a>。因其胖嘟嘟的脸庞和“甜美”的笑容被称为“世界上最快乐的动物”。仅一种，无亚种分化。已被世界自然保护联盟列入<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%25BF%2592%25E5%258D%25B1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">濒危</a>物种红色名录。</p>
 <p data-pid="fIegN-nf">作为<b>濒危物种红色名录</b>上的物种，短尾矮袋鼠<b>禁止买卖。</b></p>

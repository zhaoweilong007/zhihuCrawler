# 猫咪这么可爱又柔弱的动物是如何在弱肉强食的进化史上幸存的？
- 点赞数：6405
- 更新时间：2023年10月18日15时38分50秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/358132366/answer/924321333
<body>
 <p data-pid="URSi0Ojp">猫是猫科动物，<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%8C%AB%E7%A7%91%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">猫科动物</a>在演化的过程中，</p>
 <p data-pid="dqjEkDIW">演化出了好几个很成功的种，</p>
 <p data-pid="b3jvHsDE">或者说现存猫科有一个算一个都是当地生态的顶尖<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%8E%A0%E9%A3%9F%E8%80%85&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">掠食者</a>（大多数的唯一，部分不唯一），</p>
 <p data-pid="FKWu8XDV">全是SSR（孤身一人在野外，送10只绝对忠臣的动物，是不是十个<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%A4%A7%E7%8C%AB&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">大猫</a>最爽）。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6FDDQAy1">它们又分为好几种特化型。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yCrLC6un">虎-<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%B1%B1%E5%9C%B0%E5%8A%9B%E9%87%8F&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">山地力量</a>特化型</p>
 <p data-pid="MaKbV8_n">狮-草原群战特化型</p>
 <p data-pid="-1dW5_p-"><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%8C%8E%E8%B1%B9&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">猎豹</a>-高速刺客特化型（根据评论指正调整名称）</p>
 <p data-pid="m3sZe1xr">猞猁-寒域特化型</p>
 <p data-pid="--U7vbeY"><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E9%9B%AA%E8%B1%B9&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">雪豹</a>-高原特化型</p>
 <p data-pid="un763k2E">渔猫-<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E4%B8%A4%E6%A0%96%E4%BD%9C%E6%88%98&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">两栖作战</a>特化型</p>
 <p data-pid="fIRZCMuE"><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%8C%AB-%E6%B2%99%E6%BC%A0&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">猫-沙漠</a>全能特化型（城市萌人特化型）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="snJll7cc">以上特化型都有基础配置+特化增强</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="A_gn6108">基础生存配置（王者模版）：</p>
 <p data-pid="KymanIcx">尖牙-具有强力的獠牙，犬齿咬合力<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E7%95%8C&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">动物界</a>顶尖（<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E9%AC%A3%E7%8B%97&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">鬣狗</a>是臼齿咬合力高，搏斗的时候不如犬齿）</p>
 <p data-pid="s9_q_e2M"><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%88%A9%E7%88%AA&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">利爪</a>-具有尖锐的爪，爪的锋利度和收缩自如也是动物界顶尖</p>
 <p data-pid="hiGxLLhG">强健-<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E9%AA%A8%E9%AA%BC%E8%82%8C&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">骨骼肌</a>多，脂肪少，爆发力高，擅长<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E8%B7%B3%E8%B7%83&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">跳跃</a>，前肢力量大，比普通动物多出了抓和拍等动作</p>
 <p data-pid="IPTQgmiJ">灵敏-身体柔韧度高，平衡性极好，可以做出各种高难度动作，急转急停空翻等</p>
 <p data-pid="ifMsQC5m">急速-猫科奔跑速度很快，除了有个陆地第一外，<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E8%99%8E%E7%8B%AE&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">虎狮</a>速度也排名前列</p>
 <p data-pid="Lq2QsMa1">夜视-弱光条件下动态视觉极佳，猎手必备属性</p>
 <p data-pid="L9BXK4h9">耳聪-接收声波范围大，听觉距离长，可以辨别出细小的声响</p>
 <p data-pid="1tBngJGo">强嗅-极强的嗅觉神经，可以辨别出远距离多种类的气味</p>
 <p data-pid="roY5Rxm8">潜行-掌有肉垫，自带迷彩，走路无声，习性善于寻找隐蔽的位置发起攻击</p>
 <p data-pid="Q8_Jnd1_">水行-猫科大多是游泳健将，即使在水中也可以正常进行搏杀（<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%AE%B6%E7%8C%AB&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">家猫</a>卸载了本模块）</p>
 <p data-pid="eqSx_bCB">登高-擅长攀爬各种地形，尤其擅长爬树，即使最笨重的虎也可以一口气爬很多米</p>
 <p data-pid="_Mt5B7F6">洁净-和那些满身泥的<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%9C%9F%E9%B3%96&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">土鳖</a>不同，猫科动物很爱干净，会经常自行清洁（家猫增加了埋便模块）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="44to7bH-">生物圈还真没多少动物能一口气拥有这么多标签</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Nh6kUBvG">而家猫，基本具有以上除了下水之外的所有配置，</p>
 <p data-pid="ZaCnF29n">还多了几个极度重要的标签【<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%BD%A2%E8%90%8C&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">形萌</a>】【声软】</p>
 <p data-pid="k0kgUCOx">这种生物，一身的本领，在野外环境已经可以很好的生存了，</p>
 <p data-pid="9mYdZvQN">而在城市生态中更是完全没有压力，如果不是人类有意控制数量，挺容易泛滥</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MUTuyD-c">另外我以前的回答也提到过，</p>
 <p data-pid="yLLkMznd">家里曾经闹老鼠，</p>
 <p data-pid="DuA0cU5m">全家人绞尽脑汁和老鼠斗智斗勇了很久，还是败下阵来，</p>
 <p data-pid="d1wqSTUD">最后请来一尊<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%B0%8F%E7%8C%AB&amp;search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_source=Entity&amp;hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A924321333%7D" class="internal">小猫</a>，居然就轻松摆平了</p>
 <p data-pid="2S6TkGCs">你说这生物多厉害</p>

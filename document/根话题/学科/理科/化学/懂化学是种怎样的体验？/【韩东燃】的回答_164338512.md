 <p data-pid="imrTW5ZQ">1、岳母要煮冰糖银耳，家里冰糖用完了，我说用白糖是一样的，岳母坚持要冰糖。学了化学之后，会对很多东西无语，该买冰糖还得买。。。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="IXb7b8tT">
  其实<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E7%2599%25BD%25E7%25B3%2596%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLnW7Wn19BuWTLrHPhrH9W0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWbLn1mvn10" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">白糖</a>、冰糖、红糖都是从甘蔗和甜菜中提取的，都属于<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E8%2594%2597%25E7%25B3%2596%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLnW7Wn19BuWTLrHPhrH9W0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWbLn1mvn10" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">蔗糖</a>的范畴。红糖是<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E8%2594%2597%25E7%25B3%2596%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLnW7Wn19BuWTLrHPhrH9W0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWbLn1mvn10" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">蔗糖</a>和糖蜜的混和物，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E7%2599%25BD%25E7%25B3%2596%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLnW7Wn19BuWTLrHPhrH9W0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWbLn1mvn10" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E7%2599%25BD%25E7%25B3%2596%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLnW7Wn19BuWTLrHPhrH9W0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWbLn1mvn10"  <p data-pid="6q8SghU5">不论结晶如何，融化到水里之后冰糖和白糖都是一模一样的东东，有人说冰糖味道更好那纯粹是心理感受，化学告诉你它们就是如假包换的一模一样滴~</p>
 <p data-pid="wFt-I_pL">2、学了化学和生物化学之后，我知道给人体内注射一颗艾滋病毒是不会得病的。</p>
 <p data-pid="-Dqp9a7y">3、虽然知道1块钱的钙片和很贵的钙片基本一样，还是得不断给家里人购买一两百块一瓶的美国钙片。</p>
 <p data-pid="YP4LNwlN">4、觉得尿液和粪便不脏，脏的是其他东西比如化学和生物制剂。所以便前肥皂洗手是必须，便后随便一冲就好了。</p>
 <p data-pid="g4ftcBB9">5、实验室的高纯白糖和盐是有同学会拿来吃的。有朋友指出实验室是不能进食的，这一点是正确的，不过每个实验室总有那么些个吃货是无法阻挡的。换句话说，即便是管理严格的哈佛实验室，你若翻遍抽屉，里面定然是能找出食物的——铤而走险，贪官会干，吃货也会干~</p>
 <p data-pid="e6y89ISD">6、觉得市面上的营养品保健品百分之八九十都是骗人的。</p>
 <p data-pid="tzuXTqAK">7、觉得自己有知识但无法帮助大家理解这些东西很可悲。</p>

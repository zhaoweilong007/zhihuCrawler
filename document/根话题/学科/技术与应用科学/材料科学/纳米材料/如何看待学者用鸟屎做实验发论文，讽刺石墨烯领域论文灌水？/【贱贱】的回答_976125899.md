# 如何看待学者用鸟屎做实验发论文，讽刺石墨烯领域论文灌水？
- 点赞数：20532
- 更新时间：2020年01月16日18时12分37秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/366538777/answer/976125899
<body>
 <p data-pid="UosIiOOl">一开口就知道是老阴阳了<sup data-draft-node="inline" data-draft-type="reference" data-numero="1" data-text="1" data-url="https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsnano.9b00184">[1]</sup></p>
 <p data-pid="c0sCT4hh">他真的做了很多人想做的一件事：</p>
 <p data-pid="MrDAt60B"><b>你就算放一坨屎去做材料，也能发文章，而且还是一区，比如ACS Nano之类的...</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="i-hBeX8m"><b>题目就很新奇：</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Eiwzw83l">
  Will Any <b>Crap</b> We Put into Graphene Increase Its Electrocatalytic Effect?
  <br>
  我们放入石墨烯中的<b>废物</b>会增加其电催化作用吗？
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="7735XnYC">Crap（废物）这个词不够确切，他明明用的是鸟屎，为什么不用Shit？差评！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-AdHDHXw">这全屏都是满满的嘲讽啊，不信我给大家截取几段：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="cg5Aueic">
  It apparently<b><i>did not matter</i></b>whether the doping atom/group was electron donating or electron withdrawing; the effect was <b><i>always</i></b>claimed to be electrocatalytic.
  <br>
  显然，掺杂原子/基团是给电子还是吸电子<b><i>无关紧要</i></b>；效果<b><i>总是</i>自称</b>是电催化的。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ZDS-W_p8">这个斜体是肿么回事...</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-927de9fd5ebfd746a8b61f28fe746e0f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="732" data-rawheight="137" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-00c0c2b91dbb221710924d8875cf65cc" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-412d1d62c226bae1842d837f231e30ac_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="732" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-927de9fd5ebfd746a8b61f28fe746e0f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="M20fJQwu">
  After doping with individual atoms, it was apparently again not enough for electrocatalysis (<b>one may be surprised that there is always room for improvement</b>) and two-element-doped graphene was claimed to be a better catalyst than graphene doped with one element due to a so-called <b>“synergistic effect”</b>.
  <br>
  ....（<b>人们可能会惊讶地<i>总是</i>有改进的余地</b>），并且声称由于所谓的<b>“协同效应”</b>掺有两种元素的石墨烯比而掺有一种元素石墨烯更好。
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="I4Xp-Xrb">
  One may exaggerate only a little by saying that if we spit on graphene it becomes a better electrocatalyst.
  <br>
  有人说，就算是我们在石墨烯上吐口水，它也会成为更好的电催化剂，这其实只浮夸了一丢丢。
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="6GuNNSsf">
  In summary, we demonstrated that bird dropping-treated graphenes indeed make graphene more electrocatalytic than nondoped graphene. Both bird-dropping-decorated graphenes and control nondoped graphenes show the same morphology. Graphenes decorated with bird droppings contain additional N, S, and P in the material. These decorated graphenes exhibit much better electrocatalytic properties toward both oxygen reduction and hydrogen evolution, and, in this case, it can be considered as a potential multifunctional catalyst for both ORR and HER.
  <br>
  总之，我们证明了鸟屎处理的石墨烯确实使石墨烯比未掺杂的石墨烯更具电催化性。鸟屎装饰的石墨烯和对照未掺杂的石墨烯都显示相同的形态。用鸟粪装饰的石墨烯在材料中包含额外的N，S和P。这些修饰的石墨烯在氧还原和氢释放方面均表现出更好的电催化性能，在这种情况下，可以将其视为ORR和HER的潜在多功能催化剂。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="KRqjKOeO"><b>标准的实验总结，你把鸟屎换成任何材料，可以一模一样发表。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="yElQLdoo">
  Because doping graphene with cheap bird droppings produces more electrocatalytic materials than many complex multielemental doping procedures, we do not see any justification for such efforts, and we believe that researchers should focus their energy on other research directions.
  <br>
  因为用便宜的鸟粪来掺杂石墨烯比许多复杂的多元素掺杂程序能产生更多的电催化材料，所以我们看不出有任何此类努力的理由，而且我们相信研究人员应将精力集中在其他研究方向上。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="wqQShras">我觉得这才是全文唯一语重心长的地方，哀其不幸，怒其不争，就算是坨屎，也能发高档次文章，别浪费生命了吧，屎人屎己。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="CEh-9jwl">
  To conclude in a positive (and a bit satiric) tone, we speculate that the chemical composition of chicken guano can be tailored by feedstock (chick feed), and, therefore, the quality of the resulting doped catalyst can be further improved.
  <br>
  总而言之，我们推测可以通过进料（雏鸡进料）调整鸡粪的化学成分，因此可以进一步提高所得掺杂催化剂的质量。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="M4vglyUL">尽管这么无聊，作者还是以一个科研人员的科学素养，对这个研究提出了展望：你可以通过喂鸡吃不同的东西，产生不同的屎（这样就可以发更多文章了）。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="OfstubwL">
  We believe that there is potential for the bird dropping-doped graphene for fuel cells and in a hydrogen economy, and we believe that bird droppings can become a high-value-added product such as guano was in the past.One can only hope that with such dramatic advantages, no wars (even trade wars) will be started over bird droppings this time.
  <br>
  我们认为燃料电池和氢经济领域中存在鸟滴掺杂石墨烯的潜力，并且我们认为鸟滴可以成为像鸟粪一样的高附加值产品。人们只能希望，凭借如此巨大的优势，这次不会因鸟粪而发动任何战争（甚至是mao衣战）。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="4o8g-LNp">最后，和所有文章做了点芝麻就上升到宇宙爆炸一样，鸟粪很高附加值，如此巨大优势，对mao衣战也是很重要的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5CdOcWga">我觉得这篇文章非常好，通俗易懂，受众广泛，</p>
 <p data-pid="7d8yRIa3">不夸张的说，别提什么AM, ACS Nano，比绝大多数Science Nature都有意义多了，都啥鸟玩意啊...</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OcQRdTxf">最后，这篇文章是个perspective，所以放飞一下很正常，</p>
 <p data-pid="PZ63cCVJ">正经研究论文这样写还是会被diss的，</p>
 <p data-pid="dhWkgIFx">（当然，他也有一些表征和数据而且还有SOM，就当research article看也没毛病，绝对不是一般的perspective）</p>

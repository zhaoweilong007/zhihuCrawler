# 199²⁰⁰和200¹⁹⁹哪个更大?

- 更新时间：2021年02月10日12时01分12秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="_wWbcB4M">先说结论， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=199%5E%7B200%7D%3E200%5E%7B199%7D" alt="199^{200}>200^{199}" eeimg="1"> ，而且类似的情况 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=9998%5E%7B9999%7D%3E9999%5E%7B9998%7D" alt="9998^{9999}>9999^{9998}" eeimg="1"> 也同样成立</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="za3nTeRr">已知 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%3Ca%3Cb" alt="e<a<b" eeimg="1"> ，证： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Eb%3Eb%5Ea" alt="a^b>b^a" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="tbMYUtM_">证明：</p>
 <p data-pid="C6MVee4N"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Eb%3Eb%5Ea" alt="a^b>b^a" eeimg="1"> 等价于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=e%5E%7Bb%2Alna%7D%3Ee%5E%7Ba%2Alnb%7D" alt="e^{b*lna}>e^{a*lnb}" eeimg="1">，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%2Alna+%3E+a%2Alnb" alt="b*lna > a*lnb" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="UboTqrt5">只需证明 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%2Alna+%3E+a%2Alnb" alt="b*lna > a*lnb" eeimg="1"> 即说明 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Eb%3Eb%5Ea" alt="a^b>b^a" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="2k3QDD1h">令 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3Dxlna-alnx" alt="f(x)=xlna-alnx" eeimg="1"> ,得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28a%29%3D0" alt="f(a)=0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="n1gVycrD"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x%29%3Dlna-%5Cfrac%7Ba%7D%7Bx%7D" alt="f'(x)=lna-\frac{a}{x}" eeimg="1"> ，当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%3Cx%3Cb" alt="a<x<b" eeimg="1"> 时， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%27%28x%29%3E0" alt="f'(x)>0" eeimg="1"> ，</p>
 <p data-pid="qg4Xlxf2">故 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29" alt="f(x)" eeimg="1"> 在 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Ba%2Cb%5D" alt="[a,b]" eeimg="1"> 上单调递增</p>
 <p data-pid="1LAwb1qP">由题得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b%3Ea" alt="b>a" eeimg="1"> ，故 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28b%29%3Ef%28a%29%3D0" alt="f(b)>f(a)=0" eeimg="1"> ，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28b%29%3E0" alt="f(b)>0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="RO8W60ho">所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=blna-alnb%3E0" alt="blna-alnb>0" eeimg="1"> ，也就是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5Eb%3Eb%5Ea" alt="a^b>b^a" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cHeAuOtu">将a=199,b=200代入，可知 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=199%5E%7B200%7D%3E200%5E%7B199%7D" alt="199^{200}>200^{199}" eeimg="1"> ，所以不管a,b取多少，只要满足大于e，就算是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=400%5E%7B500%7D%3E500%5E%7B400%7D" alt="400^{500}>500^{400}" eeimg="1"> 或者 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=9998%5E%7B9999%7D%3E9999%5E%7B9998%7D" alt="9998^{9999}>9999^{9998}" eeimg="1"> 也同样成立。</p>

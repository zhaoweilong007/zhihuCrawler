# 199²⁰⁰和200¹⁹⁹哪个更大?
- 点赞数：11829
- 更新时间：2021年08月02日14时44分50秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="J93NdcYc">鉴于评论中有大量的回答是：取对数，构造函数。有必要先声明</p>
 <p data-pid="cd_7u6zy">取对数构造函数再求导的方法是最基本也是最容易想到的方法，就好比用方程解应用题一样。已经有很多答案详实地、不知疲倦地展示了这种方法，我就不再浪费时间于此了。我用均值不等式来做完全是因为看到答案里面没人用，而均值不等式又属于非常基础的但有一定技巧的方法，而且觉得用均值不等式最省墨，并不是说这种方法要比构造函数的方法好，更不是说我不会用其他方法。。。（8.2补）</p>
 <p data-pid="Eu2hV4tg">-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------</p>
 <p data-pid="ySfX3let">看了琳琅满目的回答，有用高等数学方法的，有用初中比商法放缩的，有用不完全归纳当填空题做的，还有各种抖机灵反智用数值计算侮辱数学的，真可谓百般红紫斗芳菲，叫人应接不暇！可翻看了这么多，居然没有用均值不等式的！怎么可以没有均值不等式？！于是我琢磨了一下</p>
 <p data-pid="mESqBNfD"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%5E%7B199%7D%7D%3D%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%5E%7B198%7D%5Ccdot100%5Ccdot2%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B198%5Ctimes200%2B100%2B2%7D%7B200%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B199%5Ctimes200%7D%7B200%7D%3D199" alt="\sqrt[200]{200^{199}}=\sqrt[200]{200^{198}\cdot100\cdot2}<\frac{198\times200+100+2}{200}<\frac{199\times200}{200}=199" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="bgpR7hAr">所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200%5E%7B199%7D%3C199%5E%7B200%7D" alt="200^{199}<199^{200}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="fowZX8sA">so easy！</p>
 <p data-pid="whhXObyV">补充一下，均值不等式： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7Ba_%7B1%7D%2Ba_%7B2%7D%2Ba_%7B3%7D%2B......%2Ba_%7Bn%7D%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cgeq%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Ba_%7B1%7Da_%7B2%7Da_%7B3%7D......a_%7Bn%7D%7D" alt="\frac{a_{1}+a_{2}+a_{3}+......+a_{n}}{n}\geq\sqrt[n]{a_{1}a_{2}a_{3}......a_{n}}" eeimg="1"> ，算术平均值大于等于几何平均值， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a_%7B1%7D%3Da_%7B2%7D%3Da_%7B3%7D%3D......%3Da_%7Bn%7D" alt="a_{1}=a_{2}=a_{3}=......=a_{n}" eeimg="1"> 时等号成立</p>
 <p data-pid="SzVm1Hfo">====================【7.24更】=====================</p>
 <p data-pid="tUhCm8AL">感觉得加点东西才对得起大家的赞和回复，那我推广到一般情况吧</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XdZtDYEO">比较<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn%7D" alt="(n+1)^{n}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="n^{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 的大小，其中 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cin+N" alt="n\in N" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="13hdoAC-"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5Bn%2B1%5D%7B%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%3D%5Csqrt%5Bn%2B1%5D%7B%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn-1%7D%5Csqrt%7Bn%2B1%7D%5Csqrt%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%3C%5Cfrac%7B%28n-1%29%28n%2B1%29%2B2%5Csqrt%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%7Bn%2B1%7D%3Dn-1%2B%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D" alt="\sqrt[n+1]{(n+1)^{n}}=\sqrt[n+1]{(n+1)^{n-1}\sqrt{n+1}\sqrt{n+1}}<\frac{(n-1)(n+1)+2\sqrt{n+1}}{n+1}=n-1+\frac{2}{\sqrt{n+1}}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="rBTCXz1g">如果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n-1%2B%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5Cleq+n" alt="n-1+\frac{2}{\sqrt{n+1}}\leq n" eeimg="1"> ，就能得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5Bn%2B1%5D%7B%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn%7D%7D%3Cn+%5CRightarrow+%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn%7D%3Cn%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="\sqrt[n+1]{(n+1)^{n}}<n \Rightarrow (n+1)^{n}<n^{n+1}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="sWmuu2rU">通过解不等式 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n-1%2B%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%2B1%7D%7D%5Cleq+n" alt="n-1+\frac{2}{\sqrt{n+1}}\leq n" eeimg="1"> 得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeq3" alt="n\geq3" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="p0QZjz_7">从而证明了当 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeq3" alt="n\geq3" eeimg="1"> 时， <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn%7D%3Cn%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="(n+1)^{n}<n^{n+1}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="s0fmTr-0"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3D0%2C1%2C2" alt="n=0,1,2" eeimg="1"> 的情况，带入进去计算就能得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28n%2B1%29%5E%7Bn%7D%3En%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="(n+1)^{n}>n^{n+1}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="MaK2OHKz">-------------------------------------------------------------------------------</p>
 <p data-pid="2Sh0_j0z">感觉这就是初等的方法里能走得最远的结果。</p>
 <p data-pid="RWPWfCbR">其实构造 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=f%28x%29%3D%5Cfrac%7Blnx%7D%7Bx%7D" alt="f(x)=\frac{lnx}{x}" eeimg="1"> 才是可推广的标准化方法，能得到最强的结果，而且会对这个问题有更深入的理解。均值不等式技巧性强，属于抖机灵的解法。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PCIm6aqd">============下面再附赠一个初中水平的奇技淫巧的方法吧==============</p>
 <p data-pid="a8LxGlEc">要比较 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200%5E%7B199%7D" alt="200^{199}" eeimg="1">和<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=199%5E%7B200%7D" alt="199^{200}" eeimg="1"> ，就是要比较 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B199%7D%7B199%5Ctimes200%7D%7D" alt="200^{\frac{199}{199\times200}}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=199%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%5Ctimes200%7D%7D" alt="199^{\frac{200}{199\times200}}" eeimg="1"> ，那就是比较 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D" alt="\sqrt[200]{200}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B199%5D%7B199%7D" alt="\sqrt[199]{199}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="qP2zXc1V"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200%3D199%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D" alt="200=199\times\frac{200}{199}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="xrmeIEoa"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cqquad+%3D%5Cleft%28+%5Csqrt%5B199%5D%7B199%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7B199%7D+%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D%3D%5Cleft%28+%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D+%5Cright%29%5E%7B199%7D+%5Ctimes+%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D+++%5Cqquad++%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Ccdot%5Cquad%281%29" alt="\qquad =\left( \sqrt[199]{199}\right)^{199} \times\frac{200}{199}=\left( \sqrt[200]{200} \right)^{199} \times \sqrt[200]{200}   \qquad  \cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot\quad(1)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="0kP6gyRi">如果能比较 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D" alt="\sqrt[200]{200}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D" alt="\frac{200}{199}" eeimg="1"> 的大小，就能比较 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D" alt="\sqrt[200]{200}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B199%5D%7B199%7D" alt="\sqrt[199]{199}" eeimg="1"> 的大小</p>
 <p data-pid="1gpzGDw4"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200+%3D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cfrac%7B2%7D%7B1%7D%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B3%7D%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B4%7D%7B3%7D%E2%80%A6%E2%80%A6%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D%7D_%7B%E5%85%B1199%E9%A1%B9%7D%3D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B3%7D%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B4%7D%7B3%7D%E2%80%A6%E2%80%A6%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D%7D_%7B%E5%85%B1200%E9%A1%B9%7D%3E%5Cleft%28+%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7B200%7D" alt="200 =\underbrace{\frac{2}{1}\times\frac{3}{2}\times\frac{4}{3}……\times\frac{200}{199}}_{共199项}=\underbrace{\sqrt{2}\times\sqrt{2}\times\frac{3}{2}\times\frac{4}{3}……\times\frac{200}{199}}_{共200项}>\left( \frac{200}{199}\right)^{200}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="gzhgOSkH">所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D%3E%5Cfrac%7B200%7D%7B199%7D" alt="\sqrt[200]{200}>\frac{200}{199}" eeimg="1">，由 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%281%29" alt="(1)" eeimg="1"> 式 立马得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5B200%5D%7B200%7D%3C%5Csqrt%5B199%5D%7B199%7D" alt="\sqrt[200]{200}<\sqrt[199]{199}" eeimg="1"> ，所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200%5E%7B199%7D%3C199%5E%7B200%7D" alt="200^{199}<199^{200}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Wxw2MTL2">-------------------------------------------------------------------------</p>
 <p data-pid="5R1asp4f">当然这种方法也是可以推广到一般情况比较 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5Bn%2B1%5D%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="\sqrt[n+1]{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 和 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Bn%7D" alt="\sqrt[n]{n}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="pR_L88Op">要让 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B3%7D%7B2%7D%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7B4%7D%7B3%7D%E2%80%A6%E2%80%A6%5Ctimes%5Cfrac%7Bn%2B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D_%7B%E5%85%B1n%2B1%E9%A1%B9%7D%3E%5Cleft%28+%5Cfrac%7Bn%2B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7Bn%2B1%7D" alt="\underbrace{\sqrt{2}\times\sqrt{2}\times\frac{3}{2}\times\frac{4}{3}……\times\frac{n+1}{n}}_{共n+1项}>\left( \frac{n+1}{n}\right)^{n+1}" eeimg="1"> 成立，必须 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%3E%5Cfrac%7Bn%2B1%7D%7Bn%7D" alt="\sqrt{2}>\frac{n+1}{n}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="v5WcTDjk">所以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%3E%5Csqrt%7B2%7D%2B1" alt="n>\sqrt{2}+1" eeimg="1"> ，也就是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n%5Cgeq+3" alt="n\geq 3" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="LpFjbXzG">水平越来越低了，就一初中竞赛水平，当然是不能和高等数学的方法比的。。。。</p>

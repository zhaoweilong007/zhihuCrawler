 <p data-pid="glXkC-e0">这个过程称为“中心化”。“中心化”处理的原因是，这些数字后继会参与统计运算，比如求样本方差，中间就包含了<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_i-%5Coverline%7BX%7D" alt="X_i-\overline{X}" eeimg="1"> ：</p>
 <p data-pid="baFIMCuf"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Var%28X%29%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Csum+_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%28%5Ccolor%7BSalmon%7D%7BX_i-%5Coverline%7BX%7D%7D%29%5E2%5C%5C" alt="Var(X)=\frac{1}{n}\sum _{i=1}^{n}(\color{Salmon}{X_i-\overline{X}})^2\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="gdqvH31U">说明下，虽然<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/607.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">样本方差的分母是应该为n-1</a>，这里分母采用<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n" alt="n" eeimg="1"> 是因为这样算出来的样本方差<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=Var%28X%29" alt="Var(X)" eeimg="1"> 为<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/808.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">一致估计量</a>，不会太影响计算结果并且可以减小运算负担。</p>

 <p data-pid="TJSgCndy">那么应该怎么降维呢？分析一下，从线性代数的角度来看，二维坐标系总有各自的标准正交基（也就是两两正交、模长为1的基），<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{e_1},\boldsymbol{e_2}" eeimg="1"> ：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="1wgyXshk">在某坐标系有一个点，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dx%5C%5Cy%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{a}=\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}" eeimg="1"> ，它表示在该坐标系下标准正交基<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{e_1},\boldsymbol{e_2}" eeimg="1"> 的线性组合：</p>
 <p data-pid="xRTLhveX"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dx%5C%5Cy%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3Dx%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%2By%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{a}=\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}=x\boldsymbol{e_1}+y\boldsymbol{e_2}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="N8FEh7vR">只是在不同坐标系中，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x%2Cy" alt="x,y" eeimg="1"> 的值会有所不同（旋转的坐标表示不同的坐标系）：</p>

 <p data-pid="5ZR2AZRU">因为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{a}" eeimg="1"> 到原点的距离<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d" alt="d" eeimg="1"> 不会因为坐标系改变而改变：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="cxR-2bsg">而：</p>
 <p data-pid="TKoJauYg"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=d%5E2%3Dx%5E2%2By%5E2%5C%5C" alt="d^2=x^2+y^2\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="J8eJ9uJj">所以，在某坐标系下分配给<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> 较多，那么分配给<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y" alt="y" eeimg="1"> 的就必然较少，反之亦然。最极端的情况是，在某个坐标系下，全部分配给了<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x" alt="x" eeimg="1"> ，使得<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y%3D0" alt="y=0" eeimg="1"> ：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="_gUfOCln">那么在这个坐标系中，就可以降维了，去掉<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{e_2}" eeimg="1"> 并不会丢失信息：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="6OO2ObFc">如果是两个点<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dx_1%5C%5Cy_1%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%7Bb%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dx_2%5C%5Cy_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{a}=\begin{pmatrix}x_1\\y_1\end{pmatrix},\boldsymbol{b}=\begin{pmatrix}x_2\\y_2\end{pmatrix}" eeimg="1"> ，情况就复杂一些：</p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6076240315967ba679091d11e7d20416_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="405" data-rawheight="366" data-original-token="v2-6076240315967ba679091d11e7d20416" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6076240315967ba679091d11e7d20416_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="405">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="pEuC_G0X">为了降维，应该选择尽量多分配给<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=x_1%2Cx_2" alt="x_1,x_2" eeimg="1"> ，少分配给<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=y_1%2Cy_2" alt="y_1,y_2" eeimg="1"> 的坐标系。</p>
 <p data-pid="QpXpscJ-"><b>3 主元分析（PCA）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MR239Mpt">怎么做呢？假设有如下数据：</p>
 <p data-pid="FDhIdj23"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Barray%7D%7Bc%7Cc%7D+%5Cquad%26%5Cquad+X%5Cquad%26%5Cquad+Y%5Cquad%5C%5C++%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7BSkyBlue%7D%7Ba%7D%26+a_1%26+b_1+%5C%5C+++%5Chline+%5Ccolor%7BGoldenrod%7D%7Bb%7D%26+a_2%26+b_2%5C%5C%5Cend%7Barray%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{array}{c|c} \quad&amp;\quad X\quad&amp;\quad Y\quad\\  \hline \color{SkyBlue}{a}&amp; a_1&amp; b_1 \\   \hline \color{Goldenrod}{b}&amp; a_2&amp; b_2\\\end{array}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="qdJt9p7Z">上面的数据这么解读，表示有两个点：</p>
 <p data-pid="SzvBPEim"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7DX_1%5C%5CY_1%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cquad%5Cboldsymbol%7Bb%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7DX_2%5C%5CY_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{a}=\begin{pmatrix}X_1\\Y_1\end{pmatrix}\quad\boldsymbol{b}=\begin{pmatrix}X_2\\Y_2\end{pmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="gTcEmHtB">这两个点在初始坐标系下（也就是自然基<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D1%5C%5C0%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%2C%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D0%5C%5C1%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{e_1}=\begin{pmatrix}1\\0\end{pmatrix},\boldsymbol{e_2}=\begin{pmatrix}0\\1\end{pmatrix}" eeimg="1"> ）下坐标值为：</p>
 <p data-pid="QQauUlvv"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7DX_1%5C%5CY_1%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Da_1%5C%5Cb_1%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cquad+%5Cboldsymbol%7Bb%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7DX_2%5C%5CY_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Da_2%5C%5Cb_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{a}=\begin{pmatrix}X_1\\Y_1\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}a_1\\b_1\end{pmatrix}\quad \boldsymbol{b}=\begin{pmatrix}X_2\\Y_2\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}a_2\\b_2\end{pmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="QNp5uWX-">图示如下：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c024028b47b65f892af880cdec1ec048_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="467" data-rawheight="329" data-original-token="v2-c6220d19ba68a26c6bf2bca59fa7aeb8" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e85c6a25eb4dd1ab1cc458c278e1d53b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="467" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-c024028b47b65f892af880cdec1ec048_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="aZu7EzRl">随着坐标系的不同，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%2CX_2" alt="X_1,X_2" eeimg="1"> 的值会不断变化：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0f95dad90da3a9e48d3bf5b4841a8581_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="451" data-rawheight="501" data-original-token="v2-0f95dad90da3a9e48d3bf5b4841a8581" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0f95dad90da3a9e48d3bf5b4841a8581_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="451" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-0f95dad90da3a9e48d3bf5b4841a8581_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Xunw_In7">要想尽量多分配给<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%2CX_2" alt="X_1,X_2" eeimg="1"> ，借鉴最小二乘法（请参考<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/818.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">如何理解最小二乘法</a>）的思想，就是让：</p>
 <p data-pid="JWRxtVO9"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%5E2%2BX_2%5E2%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5E2+X_i%5E2%5C+%5C+%E6%9C%80%E5%A4%A7%5C%5C" alt="X_1^2+X_2^2=\sum_{i=0}^2 X_i^2\ \ 最大\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="fQV2223b">要求这个问题，先看看<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%2CX_2" alt="X_1,X_2" eeimg="1"> 怎么表示，假设：</p>
 <p data-pid="KNgJjOKu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7De_%7B11%7D%5C%5Ce_%7B12%7D%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cquad+%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7De_%7B21%7D%5C%5Ce_%7B22%7D%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{e_1}=\begin{pmatrix}e_{11}\\e_{12}\end{pmatrix}\quad \boldsymbol{e_2}=\begin{pmatrix}e_{21}\\e_{22}\end{pmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="24KSqvCA">根据点积的几何意义（<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/568.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">如何通俗地理解协方差和点积</a>）有：</p>
 <p data-pid="H5L6Boy8"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Ba%7D%5Ccdot%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Da_1%5C%5Cb_1%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Ccdot%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7De_%7B11%7D%5C%5Ce_%7B12%7D%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3Da_1e_%7B11%7D%2Bb_1e_%7B12%7D%5C%5C" alt="X_1=\boldsymbol{a}\cdot\boldsymbol{e_1}=\begin{pmatrix}a_1\\b_1\end{pmatrix}\cdot\begin{pmatrix}e_{11}\\e_{12}\end{pmatrix}=a_1e_{11}+b_1e_{12}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="_nNFdV5S"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_2%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Bb%7D%5Ccdot%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Da_2%5C%5Cb_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Ccdot%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7De_%7B11%7D%5C%5Ce_%7B12%7D%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3Da_2e_%7B11%7D%2Bb_2e_%7B12%7D%5C%5C" alt="X_2=\boldsymbol{b}\cdot\boldsymbol{e_1}=\begin{pmatrix}a_2\\b_2\end{pmatrix}\cdot\begin{pmatrix}e_{11}\\e_{12}\end{pmatrix}=a_2e_{11}+b_2e_{12}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="Zr-5wkiu">那么：</p>
 <p data-pid="itkr3qQA"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D++++X_1%5E2%2BX_2%5E2++++++%26%3D%28a_1e_%7B11%7D%2Bb_1e_%7B12%7D%29%5E2%2B%28a_2e_%7B11%7D%2Bb_2e_%7B12%7D%29%5E2%5C%5C+++++++%5C%5C+++++++%26%3Da_1%5E2e_%7B11%7D%5E2%2B2a_1b_1e_%7B11%7De_%7B12%7D%2Bb_1%5E2e_%7B12%7D%5E2%2Ba_2%5E2e_%7B11%7D%5E2%2B2a_2b_2e_%7B11%7De_%7B12%7D%2Bb_2%5E2e_%7B12%7D%5E2%5C%5C++++++%5C%5C++++++++%26%3D%28a_1%5E2%2Ba_2%5E2%29e_%7B11%7D%5E2%2B2%28a_1b_1%2Ba_2b_2%29e_%7B11%7De_%7B12%7D%2B%28b_1%5E2%2Bb_2%5E2%29e_%7B12%7D%5E2%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{aligned}    X_1^2+X_2^2      &amp;=(a_1e_{11}+b_1e_{12})^2+(a_2e_{11}+b_2e_{12})^2\\       \\       &amp;=a_1^2e_{11}^2+2a_1b_1e_{11}e_{12}+b_1^2e_{12}^2+a_2^2e_{11}^2+2a_2b_2e_{11}e_{12}+b_2^2e_{12}^2\\      \\        &amp;=(a_1^2+a_2^2)e_{11}^2+2(a_1b_1+a_2b_2)e_{11}e_{12}+(b_1^2+b_2^2)e_{12}^2\end{aligned}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="b_6RQfoI">上式其实是一个二次型（可以参看<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/271.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">如何通俗地理解二次型</a>）：</p>
 <p data-pid="TAQ-8lq8"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%5E2%2BX_2%5E2%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cunderbrace%7B%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Da_1%5E2%2Ba_2%5E2%26a_1b_1%2Ba_2b_2%5C%5Ca_1b_1%2Ba_2b_2%26b_1%5E2%2Bb_2%5E2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%7D_%7BP%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7DP%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5C%5C" alt="X_1^2+X_2^2=\boldsymbol{e_1}^\mathrm{T}\underbrace{\begin{pmatrix}a_1^2+a_2^2&amp;a_1b_1+a_2b_2\\a_1b_1+a_2b_2&amp;b_1^2+b_2^2\end{pmatrix}}_{P}\boldsymbol{e_1}=\boldsymbol{e_1}^\mathrm{T}P\boldsymbol{e_1}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="OzH_iDme">这里矩阵<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P" alt="P" eeimg="1"> 就是二次型，是一个对称矩阵，可以进行如下的奇异值分解（可以参看<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/306.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">如何通俗地理解奇异值分解</a>）：</p>
 <p data-pid="s1WrT5xs"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P%3DU%5CSigma+U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5C%5C" alt="P=U\Sigma U^\mathrm{T}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="eXoGNdCz">其中，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U" alt="U" eeimg="1"> 为正交矩阵，即<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=UU%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%3DI" alt="UU^\mathrm{T}=I" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="UJ2SPa7w">而<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CSigma" alt="\Sigma" eeimg="1"> 是对角矩阵：</p>
 <p data-pid="zFVlaSHn"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5CSigma%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Csigma_1%260%5C%5C0%26%5Csigma_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\Sigma=\begin{pmatrix}\sigma_1&amp;0\\0&amp;\sigma_2\end{pmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="-_gyu0sd">其中，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma_1%2C%5Csigma_2" alt="\sigma_1,\sigma_2" eeimg="1"> 是奇异值，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csigma_1+%3E+%5Csigma_2" alt="\sigma_1 > \sigma_2" eeimg="1"> 。</p>
 <p data-pid="cKTtnZZQ">将<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=P" alt="P" eeimg="1"> 代回去：</p>
 <p data-pid="rQG8oeJP"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7DX_1%5E2%2BX_2%5E2++%26%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7DP%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5C%5C++++%5C%5C++++%26%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7DU%5CSigma+U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5C%5C+++%5C%5C++++%26%3D%28U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%29%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5CSigma%28U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%29%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{aligned}X_1^2+X_2^2  &amp;=\boldsymbol{e_1}^\mathrm{T}P\boldsymbol{e_1}\\    \\    &amp;=\boldsymbol{e_1}^\mathrm{T}U\Sigma U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1}\\   \\    &amp;=(U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1})^\mathrm{T}\Sigma(U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1})\end{aligned}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="yOShX1YH">因为<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=U" alt="U" eeimg="1"> 是正交矩阵，所以令：</p>
 <p data-pid="AAwLeSKr"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Bn%7D%3DU%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{n}=U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="jOQZiGIg">所得的<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Bn%7D" alt="\boldsymbol{n}" eeimg="1"> 也是单位向量，即：</p>
 <p data-pid="vgJZdIYy"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dn_1%5C%5Cn_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cimplies+n_1%5E2%2Bn_2%5E2%3D1%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{n}=\begin{pmatrix}n_1\\n_2\end{pmatrix}\implies n_1^2+n_2^2=1\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="x2JXlUWe">继续回代：</p>
 <p data-pid="KYtT5sH6"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7DX_1%5E2%2BX_2%5E2++++%26%3D%28U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%29%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5CSigma%28U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%29%5C%5C+++%5C%5C++%26%3D%5Cboldsymbol%7Bn%7D%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5CSigma%5Cboldsymbol%7Bn%7D%5C%5C++++%5C%5C+++%26%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dn_1%26n_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Csigma_1%260%5C%5C0%26%5Csigma_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7Dn_1%5C%5Cn_2%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C++++%5C%5C++++%26%3D%5Csigma_1n_1%5E2%2B%5Csigma_2n_2%5E2%5Cend%7Baligned%7D%5C%5C" alt="\begin{aligned}X_1^2+X_2^2    &amp;=(U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1})^\mathrm{T}\Sigma(U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1})\\   \\  &amp;=\boldsymbol{n}^\mathrm{T}\Sigma\boldsymbol{n}\\    \\   &amp;=\begin{pmatrix}n_1&amp;n_2\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}\sigma_1&amp;0\\0&amp;\sigma_2\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}n_1\\n_2\end{pmatrix}\\    \\    &amp;=\sigma_1n_1^2+\sigma_2n_2^2\end{aligned}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="H20a953d">最初求最大值的问题就转化为了：</p>
 <p data-pid="FKy1G0Vk"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X_1%5E2%2BX_2%5E2%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D0%7D%5E2+X_i%5E2%5C+%5C+%E6%9C%80%E5%A4%A7%5Ciff%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7D%5Csigma_1n_1%5E2%2B%5Csigma_2n_2%5E2%5C+%5C+%E6%9C%80%E5%A4%A7%5C%5C%5C%5Cn_1%5E2%2Bn_2%5E2%3D1%5C%5C%5C%5C%5Csigma_1+%3E+%5Csigma_2%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C" alt="X_1^2+X_2^2=\sum_{i=0}^2 X_i^2\ \ 最大\iff\begin{cases}\sigma_1n_1^2+\sigma_2n_2^2\ \ 最大\\\\n_1^2+n_2^2=1\\\\\sigma_1 > \sigma_2\end{cases}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="J1bRXoYi">感兴趣可以用拉格朗日乘子法计算上述条件极值（参看<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.matongxue.com/madocs/987.html" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">如何通俗地理解拉格朗日乘子法以及KKT条件</a>），结果是当<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=n_1%3D1%2Cn_2%3D0" alt="n_1=1,n_2=0" eeimg="1"> 时取到极值。</p>
 <p data-pid="2fOhiy90">因此可以推出要寻找的主元1，即：</p>
 <p data-pid="y7sqqafY"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D1%5C%5C0%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%3DU%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%5Cimplies+%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3DU%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D1%5C%5C0%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{n}=\begin{pmatrix}1\\0\end{pmatrix}=U^\mathrm{T}\boldsymbol{e_1}\implies \boldsymbol{e_1}=U\begin{pmatrix}1\\0\end{pmatrix}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="9UTc7Qv6">总结下：</p>
 <p data-pid="Rv4fIzty"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_1%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7DP%3DU%5CSigma+U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5C%5C%5C%5C%E6%9C%80%E5%A4%A7%E5%A5%87%E5%BC%82%E5%80%BC%5Csigma_1%E5%AF%B9%E5%BA%94%E7%9A%84%E5%A5%87%E5%BC%82%E5%90%91%E9%87%8F%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{e_1}=\begin{cases}P=U\Sigma U^\mathrm{T}\\\\最大奇异值\sigma_1对应的奇异向量\end{cases}\\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="o1k849ET">同样的思路可以求出：</p>
 <p data-pid="97GKPeh3"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cboldsymbol%7Be_2%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bcases%7DP%3DU%5CSigma+U%5E%5Cmathrm%7BT%7D%5C%5C%5C%5C%E6%9C%80%E5%B0%8F%E5%A5%87%E5%BC%82%E5%80%BC%5Csigma_2%E5%AF%B9%E5%BA%94%E7%9A%84%E5%A5%87%E5%BC%82%E5%90%91%E9%87%8F%5Cend%7Bcases%7D%5C%5C" alt="\boldsymbol{e_2}=\begin{cases}P=U\Sigma U^\mathrm{T}\\\\最小奇异值\sigma_2对应的奇异向量\end{cases}\\" eeimg="1"></p>

# 发现 10 岁的弟弟悄悄抽烟，该如何正确引导？

 <p data-pid="0vWFDr2S">我的中学班主任发现我们班级那层厕所有人抽烟，然后为我们班级科普了“口唇期”和“吸吮反射”</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="KEL_Sp4k">
  <b>吸吮反射</b>（sucking reflex）是哺乳动物及人类<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E5%25A9%25B4%25E5%2584%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">婴儿</a>先天具有的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E5%258F%258D%25E5%25B0%2584" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">反射</a>之一，是<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E6%2596%25B0%25E7%2594%259F%25E5%2584%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">新生儿</a><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E6%2597%25A0%25E6%259D%25A1%25E4%25BB%25B6%25E5%258F%258D%25E5%25B0%2584" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">无条件反射</a>的一种。当用<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E4%25B9%25B3%25E5%25A4%25B4" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">乳头</a>或<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E6%2589%258B%25E6%258C%2587" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">手指</a>碰新生儿的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E5%258F%25A3%25E5%2594%2587" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">口唇</a>时，会相应出现口唇及舌的吸吮<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E8%25A0%2595%25E5%258A%25A8" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">蠕动</a>。出生后3-4个月自行消失，逐渐被主动的进食动作所代替。但在睡眠和其他一些场合，婴儿仍会在一段时期内表现出自发的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/index.php%3Ftitle%3D%25E5%2590%25B8%25E5%2590%25AE%25E5%258A%25A8%25E4%25BD%259C%26action%3Dedit%26redlink%3D1" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">吸吮动作</a>。 若<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E6%2596%25B0%25E7%2594%259F%25E5%2584%25BF%25E6%259C%259F" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">新生儿期</a>吸吮反射消失或明显减弱，提示脑内病变；若亢进则为<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//yixue.com/%25E9%25A5%25A5%25E9%25A5%25BF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">饥饿</a>表现。1岁后仍存在提示大脑皮层功能障碍。　　
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="hoH8Th0M">进而科普了吸烟是在口唇期结束后对安全期待的替代行为，简单讲就是“想妈妈了”。</p>
 <p data-pid="JhTcysAH">然后，我们班对在厕所抽烟同学都会报以关爱的眼神。</p>
 <p data-pid="FX0TKHwZ">骂这个孩子抽烟像个XX，不如通过科学严谨的方式证明这样是个XXX。</p>

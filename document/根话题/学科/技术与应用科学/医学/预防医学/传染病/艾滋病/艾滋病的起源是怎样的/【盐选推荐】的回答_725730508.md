# 艾滋病的起源是怎样的?

 <blockquote>
  今天<span><span>，</span></span>艾滋病早已被大多数人所熟悉<span><span>。</span></span>提起艾滋病<span><span>，</span></span>很多人都会想到死亡<span><span>、</span></span>传染病<span><span>、</span></span>滥交<span><span>、</span></span>卖血<span><span>、</span></span>鸡尾酒疗法<span><span>、</span></span>红丝带[1]这些关键词<span><span>，</span></span>也会充满恐惧<span><span>，</span></span>避之不及<span><span>。</span></span>
 </blockquote>
 <blockquote>
  然而<span><span>，</span></span>数据不会骗人<span><span>。</span></span>虽然和人类纠缠了几十年<span><span>，</span></span>感染了约 7800 万人<span><span>，</span></span>夺走了超过 3500 万人的生命<span><span>，</span></span>但在全人类的共同努力下<span><span>，</span></span>艾滋病的<span><span>「</span></span>战绩<span><span>」</span></span>开始一路下滑<span><span>。</span></span>
 </blockquote>
 <p><strong>1959 年·刚果·金沙萨</strong></p>
 <p>1959 年<span><span>，</span></span>刚果还是西欧国家的殖民地<span><span>。</span></span>它日后的首都<span><span>，</span></span>这个中部非洲最大的城市——金沙萨<span><span>，</span></span>当时还被称作利奥波德维尔<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>在当地<span><span>，</span></span>一名班图族<span><span>（</span></span>Bantu<span><span>）</span></span>男子被诊断出了镰刀型红细胞贫血症<span><span>（</span></span>sickle cell anemia<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>一种遗传因素导致的血液疾病<span><span>。</span></span>从他身上抽取的血样<span><span>，</span></span>存在了一个血清库中<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>同年<span><span>，</span></span>在刚果万里之外的加拿大<span><span>，</span></span>一名小男孩基坦·杜加刚满 6 岁<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <h3>1980 年·美国·洛杉矶</h3>
 <p>10 月<span><span>，</span></span>一场诡异事件发生了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>在加州洛杉矶的一所医院里<span><span>，</span></span>大夫们正在忙碌<span><span>。</span></span>他们接待了几位前来就诊的病患<span><span>。</span></span>事后证明<span><span>，</span></span>这些患者非比寻常<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>曾经<span><span>，</span></span>这些患者十分健康<span><span>，</span></span>有些还孔武健壮<span><span>。</span></span>如今<span><span>，</span></span>他们却出现了各种症状<span><span>。</span></span>他们的喉咙里布满了白色凝乳状真菌<span><span>，</span></span>几乎阻塞了食管<span><span>；</span></span>肺部出现了肿胀<span><span>，</span></span>呼吸困难得接近窒息<span><span>；</span></span>身上生出了紫黑色的斑斑块块<span><span>，</span></span>仿佛周身被毒虫咬过一般……</p>
 <p>诊断结果显示<span><span>，</span></span>他们的免疫系统几近崩溃<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>医生们用上了当时最先进的医疗手段<span><span>，</span></span>希望能挽救这几条濒死的生命<span><span>。</span></span>然而回天无力<span><span>，</span></span>一名 33 岁的患者在发病仅仅 4 个月后<span><span>，</span></span>就在痛苦之中与世界告别<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>几乎在同一时间<span><span>，</span></span>在当地的西达赛奈医学中心<span><span>（</span></span>Cedars-Sinai Medical Center<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>一名 20 多岁的华裔见习医生也接触到了这样的怪病案例<span><span>。</span></span>他叫大卫·何<span><span>，</span></span>中文名是何大一<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <h3>1981 年·美国·亚特兰大</h3>
 <p>在洛杉矶医院<span><span>，</span></span>这种可怕的疾病的成因仍然扑朔迷离<span><span>。</span></span>医学界开始了正本清源的事后调查<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>6 月<span><span>，</span></span>亚特兰大的美国疾病控制中心率先表态<span><span>。</span></span>在<span><span>《</span></span>发病率与死亡率周刊<span><span>》</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report<span><span>）</span></span>上<span><span>，</span></span>它发表了关于这些怪病的报告<span><span>。</span></span>这种怪病有了历史上第一次正式记载<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>1982 年<span><span>，</span></span>这种疾病有了名字<span><span>：</span></span>获得性免疫缺陷综合征<span><span>（</span></span>acquired immunodeficiency syndrome<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>根据其英文简称 AIDS<span><span>，</span></span>人们将它称作<span><span>「</span></span>艾滋病<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>几年后<span><span>，</span></span>国际病毒分类委员会正式确认了导致艾滋病的元凶<span><span>，</span></span>一种被命名为<span><span>「</span></span>人类免疫缺陷病毒<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>human immunodeficiency virus<span><span>）</span></span>的病毒<span><span>，</span></span>简称 HIV<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>科学家逐渐发现<span><span>，</span></span>HIV 感染人体后<span><span>，</span></span>会在人体内高速复制<span><span>，</span></span>破坏支撑人体免疫功能的 CD4-T 淋巴细胞<span><span>，</span></span>导致其数量逐渐下降<span><span>。</span></span>免疫机能崩溃<span><span>，</span></span>人体便无法抵御各种疾病的侵袭<span><span>，</span></span>最终怪病缠身<span><span>，</span></span>彻底崩溃<span><span>，</span></span>等待这副皮囊的便只剩了死亡<span><span>。</span></span></p>

# 什么样的菜是真正的米饭杀手？
- 点赞数：5226
- 更新时间：2021年10月12日09时30分40秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/308310091/answer/622409718
<body>
 <h3></h3>
 <p data-pid="Mftju9BX">------------------------------以下原回答-------------------------------------</p>
 <p data-pid="yQv-MPbo">评价菜好不好吃的标准有很多，但在小主这里，<b>下饭就是第一指标！</b><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ISPZDjIz">上周末，一个朋友搬了新家，请我们吃饭。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Qm4Xf3wp">餐桌上放眼望去可都是大鱼大肉，但把每个菜都轮着尝了以后，最中意的还是一盘简单的下饭菜：金针菇日本豆腐。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MiG0Wzsz">吃过饭以后，只有它一点没剩！简直就是一匹餐桌“黑马”，从众多油腻的肉菜中脱颖而出。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1uzOAATV">看来，小主的“下饭”指标是适用于大多数人的，嘻嘻。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="E2KZn4AP"><b> 金针菇烧日本豆腐 </b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-f2ef4d2424a4dea10b3fca9dc72563c7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-f2ef4d2424a4dea10b3fca9dc72563c7" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-f2ef4d2424a4dea10b3fca9dc72563c7_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vLxxa0dH">日本豆腐又叫玉子豆腐，虽然名字叫豆腐，但它的主料其实是鸡蛋，吃起来全是鸡蛋的香味。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MPqW8-Ct">口感细嫩柔滑，而且更不易碎，这是很多豆腐都比不上的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="86nVxVuH">而金针菇大家就很熟悉了，爽脆又顺滑，煎、炸、煮、烤都是一绝。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vLHAMJO3">这两种食材的鲜美交汇，让这道菜无须要繁多的调味，只需少许咸味就已完满。<br></p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1089525187548041216" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="最爱的下饭菜：金针菇烧日本豆腐" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-38233594419fa4477907440817dc1001.jpg" data-lens-id="1089525187548041216"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-38233594419fa4477907440817dc1001.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">最爱的下饭菜：金针菇烧日本豆腐<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1089525187548041216</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dK8D9QyT">滋味浓郁，又不油腻，没有肉也一样完美。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ebdfc5982d7f8ca640c5ffa6911e561e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-ebdfc5982d7f8ca640c5ffa6911e561e" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ebdfc5982d7f8ca640c5ffa6911e561e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fFze851N">掀开锅浓郁的汁香扑面而来，食欲瞬间被勾起。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wJur863G">豆腐很嫩，滑溜溜的，入口鲜香浓郁。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OBiQMl5A">金针菇密集的菌丝，扣着丝丝入味的鲜咸味道，爽滑多汁，让人忍不住大口吸溜起来。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ACLzLd6T"><b>&gt; 食材清单 &lt;</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5153313718bf225fc832329e2e5db10c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-5153313718bf225fc832329e2e5db10c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5153313718bf225fc832329e2e5db10c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q6lU5Re8">日本豆腐 3根 / 金针菇 一捆</p>
 <p data-pid="CZ1EltYR">姜末 适量 / 蒜末 适量</p>
 <p data-pid="6dHkhbBO">大葱末 适量 / 葱花 一小把</p>
 <p data-pid="ev92ZV9B">蚝油 2勺 / 酱油 2勺</p>
 <p data-pid="YsTul3om">淀粉 少许 / 食用油 适量</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ezKY8Qxw"><b>&gt; 烹饪步骤 &lt;</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SEO9AJJy"><b>1. </b>金针菇洗净，去除根部弄散，沥干水备用。日本豆腐打开包装，切块。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e0ed60eaec82f884f29229e1f40d12f0_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-e0ed60eaec82f884f29229e1f40d12f0" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-e0ed60eaec82f884f29229e1f40d12f0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gwgpiU3G"><b>2.</b> 锅中倒油烧热，加入日本豆腐煎至表皮焦黄，沥干捞起。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-5be9630bb1c33b8d819c043706932b5b_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-5be9630bb1c33b8d819c043706932b5b" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-5be9630bb1c33b8d819c043706932b5b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="iVFrZ4n3"><b>3.</b> 起锅热油，放入姜末、蒜末、大葱末爆香。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4d2d9d45c3fd6fb5f82d4a6d3ee563bc_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-4d2d9d45c3fd6fb5f82d4a6d3ee563bc" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4d2d9d45c3fd6fb5f82d4a6d3ee563bc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Pqbd06tW"><b>4.</b> 放入金针菇，翻炒至金针菇软身。淋入一勺酱油，一勺蚝油，翻炒均匀。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-9ba00d0f566ba55df07571c1ea028081_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-9ba00d0f566ba55df07571c1ea028081" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9ba00d0f566ba55df07571c1ea028081_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MyQGpngE"><b>5.</b> 加入日本豆腐，再加入一勺酱油及蚝油，稍微翻动一下，盖上盖焖几分钟（2分钟左右）</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-7044d72f7605a7baf3518289b1a8968e_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-7044d72f7605a7baf3518289b1a8968e" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-7044d72f7605a7baf3518289b1a8968e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9Yft62L9"><b>6.</b> 打开锅盖，倒入用淀粉加水调好的芡汁，略微翻均匀，再盖上焖一小会儿，待汁收得差不多即可。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0350f6c602e5c7c6a214a296510d7f69_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-0350f6c602e5c7c6a214a296510d7f69" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-0350f6c602e5c7c6a214a296510d7f69_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TciZOwjV">关火，上碟~ 撒上一把葱花，装饭开吃！！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-32bb109186ff3ae1a9a882ea69632571_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-32bb109186ff3ae1a9a882ea69632571" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-32bb109186ff3ae1a9a882ea69632571_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_8ianA3u">身为捞饭星人的我，当然不会放过香浓醇厚的汁。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZTpGZeB8">浇上几勺，就能让简单的白饭也能惹味，吃三碗饭都吃不够~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-168fb73044912e5695826f52a0fca42e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-168fb73044912e5695826f52a0fca42e" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-168fb73044912e5695826f52a0fca42e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="CBm9Widx">吃腻了山珍海味，回过头才发现，像这样寻常、简单的滋味，也许才是最耐吃、最给人以愉悦的味道。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

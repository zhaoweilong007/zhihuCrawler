# 有哪些你觉得是人间绝句的诗词？
- 点赞数：5009
- 更新时间：2020年09月12日12时06分09秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/287378875/answer/1468097762
<body>
 <p data-pid="p7t44KCa">.</p>
 <p data-pid="TGQa9IS7">1.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="aynlTKkY">
  <b>韶华不为少年留，恨悠悠，几时休。</b>
  <br>
  ——秦观《江城子·西城杨柳弄春柔》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YfgTwWWA">2.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="N8lJLPsx">
  <b> 且将新火试新茶，诗酒趁年华。</b>
  <br>
  ——苏轼《望江南·超然台作》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AaiVhWbC">3.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pkVosTCt">
  <b>棠梨花映白杨树，尽是死生别离处。</b>
  <br>
  ——白居易《寒食野望吟》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Hka6e5me">4.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ygJgQHkY">
  <b>林花谢了春红，太匆匆。</b>
  <br><b>无奈朝来寒雨，晚来风。</b>
  <br>
  ——李煜《相见欢》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wMFW38YR">5.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="YYJHOUy_">
  <b>长乐未央，长毋相忘。</b>
  <br>
  ——孔苑《长毋相忘》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="StddoVES">6.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="IW1Qvlz5">
  <b>细雨湿流光，芳草年年与恨长。</b>
  <br>
  ——冯延已《南乡子》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uMRRG0Gq">7.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="-h87u1pH">
  <b>水是眼波横，山是眉峰聚。</b>
  <br>
  ——王观《卜算子·送鲍浩然之浙东》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IHoN_1ys">8.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="4TDPjIwc">
  <b>肠断月明红豆蔻，月似当时，人似当时否？</b>
  <br>
  ——纳兰性德《鬓云松令》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ddf1yXvG">9.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="_PMoBU7y">
  <b>欲买桂花同载酒，终不似，少年游。</b>
  <br>
  ——刘过《唐多令》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IiITxRo9">10.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="mHmGSzKO">
  <b>醉后不知天在水，满船清梦压星河。</b>
  <br>
  ——唐温如《题龙阳县青草湖》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="phX_rb93">11.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="CUKgiz7u">
  <b>流光容易把人抛，红了樱桃，绿了芭蕉。</b>
  <br>
  ——蒋捷《一剪梅·舟过吴江》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="UsbKaG49">12.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="1zN1-biI">
  <b>似此星辰非昨夜，为谁风露立中宵。</b>
  <br>
  ——黄景仁《绮怀诗》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="UFuYxeCb">13.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="NbLb4IJW">
  <b>连雨不知春去，一晴方觉夏深。</b>
  <br>
  ——范成大《喜晴》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JWpOlMBR">14.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="QY0UqbcB">
  <b>千山鸟飞绝，万径人踪灭。</b>
  <br>
  ——柳宗元《江雪》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-655803244770eee2628ff8223f56cdb5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="577" data-rawheight="407" data-original-token="v2-655803244770eee2628ff8223f56cdb5" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-39c856ee95579ae435eaa0404607ddde_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="577" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-655803244770eee2628ff8223f56cdb5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="EGrWcx6n">15.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="HN8q3QPw">
  <b>满载一船明月，平铺千里秋江。</b>
  <br>
  ——张孝祥《西江月·黄陵庙》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kN1yrAY-">16.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pqgXYZXN">
  <b>秋色连波，波上寒烟翠。</b>
  <br>
  ——范仲淹《苏幕遮·怀旧》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-VruuoSA">17.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="zFCrdSfj">
  <b>柴门闻犬吠，风雪夜归人。</b>
  <br>
  ——刘长卿《逢雪宿芙蓉山主人》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XOwdumf_">18.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="2UaTjw_w">
  <b>落霞与孤鹜齐飞，秋水共长天一色。</b>
  <br>
  ——王勃《滕王阁序》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2BgEF3LL">19.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="BBcGA1Wi">
  <b>我见青山多妩媚，料青山见我应如是。</b>
  <br>
  ——辛弃疾《贺新郎·甚矣吾衰矣》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HC_DKpHi">20.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="vCScGOsD">
  <b>衣带渐宽终不悔，为伊消得人憔悴。</b>
  <br>
   ——柳永《蝶恋花》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TUJai_6O">21.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="zPTHZmxk">
  <b>人面不知何处去，桃花依旧笑春风。</b>
  <br>
  ——崔护《题都城南庄》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-e47ec6fc019a88ea0d1f25232ee7c8d4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="583" data-rawheight="450" data-original-token="v2-e47ec6fc019a88ea0d1f25232ee7c8d4" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-5cf6ddc62f3f025ccb7f0e951f133c54_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="583" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-e47ec6fc019a88ea0d1f25232ee7c8d4_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-rnfB-p3">22.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="06h7GdW8">
  <b>娉娉袅袅十三余，豆蔻梢头二月初。</b>
  <br>
  ——杜牧《赠别》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="sG626Z48">23.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="se9sPkl0">
  <b>依旧桃花面，频低柳叶眉。</b>
  <br>
  ——韦庄《女冠子》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tk9RbxCY">24.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="aDNdqvaJ">
  <b>可怜无定河边骨，犹是春闺梦里人。</b>
  <br>
  ——陈陶《陇西行》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="xBWnLy0u">25.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="MzQ_le75">
  <b> 何时仗尔看南雪，我与梅花两白头。</b>
  <br>
  ——徐珂《清稗类钞》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AE1KfVJS">26.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="lT9vB-G3">
  <b>昨夜星辰昨夜风，画楼西畔桂堂东。</b>
  <br>
  ——李商隐《无题》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hTgIHgWu">27.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="O_4DBhun">
  <b>酴釄落尽，犹赖有梨花。</b>
  <br>
  ——李清照《转调满庭芳·芳草池塘》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="skNgASnb">28.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="hypLuvql">
  <b>回头万里，故人长绝，满座衣冠胜雪。</b>
  <br>
  ——辛弃疾《贺新郎》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DorunfjF">29.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="YiXLKDB_">
  <b>满洞苔钱，买断风烟。笑桃花流落晴川。</b>
  <br>
  ——葛长庚《行香子·题罗浮》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LGEKZ9-X">30.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Pbji2BnN">
  <b>人闲桂花落，夜静春山空。</b>
  <br><b>月出惊山鸟，时鸣春涧中。</b>
  <br>
  ——王维《鸟鸣涧》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="faRipSwI">31.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="-0YYtGmV">
  <b> 人生自是有情痴，此恨不关风与月。</b>
  <br>
  ——欧阳修《玉楼春》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="G-io3S9n">32.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="VgpgS1TB">
  <b>春江潮水连海平，海上明月共潮生。</b>
  <br>
  ——张若虚《春江花月夜》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="a6YupLJO">33.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="X9wwZN5l">
  <b>寂寞空庭春欲晚，梨花满地不开门。</b>
  <br>
  ——刘方平《春怨》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="xIMgCFvX">34.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="s_7QD7WD">
  <b>十年生死两茫茫，不思量，自难忘；</b>
  <br><b>千里孤坟，无处话凄凉。</b>
  <br>
  ——苏轼《江城子》
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H1DXByKz">35.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ZX9nOSgB">
  <b>惜起残红泪满衣，它生莫作有情痴，天地无处着相思。</b>
  <br>
  ——况周颐《减字浣溪沙-听歌有感》
 </blockquote>

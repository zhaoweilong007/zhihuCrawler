# 你听过最残忍的诗是什么？
- 点赞数：19372
- 更新时间：2020年08月25日19时09分45秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/339919577/answer/1433027851
<body>
 <p data-pid="mWlMLDZZ"><b>《葬礼.蓝调》</b></p>
 <p data-pid="FnCZEBYi">诗人奥登写给他的同性情人Chester Kallman的诗，第一次接触这首诗是在电影《四个婚礼和一个葬礼》中，</p>
 <p data-pid="38kV4Oqf">婚礼中的嬉笑怒骂让人捧腹，但印象最深还是那场葬礼，</p>
 <p data-pid="BhxExomA">马修在葬礼上引用了如下的诗篇，是全片最感人的片段。</p><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/zvideo/1281623613557956608" data-draft-node="block" data-draft-type="link-card"></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="k_N6iits">Funeral Blues</p>
 <p data-pid="xCFVfPGD">Stop all the clocks, cut off the telephone,</p>
 <p data-pid="pDsdm5_c">Prevent the dog from barking with a juicy bone,</p>
 <p data-pid="e9hWXLxh">Silence the pianos and with muffled drum</p>
 <p data-pid="4NsqU2nu">Bring out the coffin, let the mourners come.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="z9-ygXCG">Let aeroplanes circle moaning overhead</p>
 <p data-pid="BM4psUbi">Scribbling on the sky the message He Is Dead.</p>
 <p data-pid="91Tu2iIr">Put crepe bows round the white necks of public doves,</p>
 <p data-pid="-mdwPVN3">Let the traffic policemen wear black cotton gloves.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="u_e-7HVz">He was my North, my South, my East and West.</p>
 <p data-pid="MabMnCsR">My working week and my Sunday rest,</p>
 <p data-pid="oWzxfIv4">My noon, my midnight, my talk, my song;</p>
 <p data-pid="LR3CuMQK">I thought that love would last forever; I was wrong.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FM27yexm">The stars are not wanted now: put out every one;</p>
 <p data-pid="eNFHaA2x">Pack up the moon and dismantle the sun;</p>
 <p data-pid="jXd-Q9S-">Pour away the ocean and sweep up the wood;</p>
 <p data-pid="TJhQLzZH">For nothing now can ever come to any good.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="e4Ylz5gM">-- W. H. Auden</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5GsLgMis">停止所有的时钟，切断电话 <br>
  给狗一块浓汁的骨头，让他别叫 <br>
  黯哑了钢琴，随着低沉的鼓 <br>
  抬出灵怄，让哀悼者前来。</p>
 <p data-pid="ms2yWmJr"><br>
  让直升机在头顶悲旋 <br>
  在天空狂草着信息他已逝去， <br>
  把黑纱系在信鸽的白颈， <br>
  让交通员戴上黑色的手套。</p>
 <p data-pid="KPlN4oj-"><br>
  他曾经是我的东，我的西，我的南，我的北， <br>
  我的工作天，我的休息日， <br>
  我的正午，我的夜半，我的话语，我的歌吟， <br>
  我以为爱可以不朽：我错了。</p>
 <p data-pid="eM4qrcWS"><br>
  不再需要星星，把每一颗都摘掉， <br>
  把月亮包起，拆除太阳， <br>
  倾泻大海，扫除森林； <br>
  因为什么也不会，再有意味。</p>

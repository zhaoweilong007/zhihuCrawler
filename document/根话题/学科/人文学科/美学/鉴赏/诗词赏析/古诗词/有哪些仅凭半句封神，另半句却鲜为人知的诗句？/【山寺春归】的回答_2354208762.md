# 有哪些仅凭半句封神，另半句却鲜为人知的诗句？

 <p data-pid="LjJQBlGB">1.</p>
 <p data-pid="FloIxSZV">十有九人堪白眼，百无一用是书生。</p>
 <p data-pid="-uJmOCZf">——黄景仁《杂感》</p>

 <p data-pid="w9iihViA">2.</p>
 <p data-pid="In-GDwVW">色不迷人人自迷，情人眼里出西施。</p>
 <p data-pid="kc-heK4q">——清·黄增《集杭州俗语诗》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3CvpO0Wj"><b>3. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="mkhEOwd7"><b>读书不觉已春深，一寸光阴一寸金。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vrHNnU-w"><b>——唐·王贞白《白鹿洞二首·其一》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DADfctQv">4.</p>
 <p data-pid="DEQBBzB6">春宵一刻值千金，花有清香月有阴。</p>
 <p data-pid="Lv2tRJqt">——北宋·苏轼《春宵》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="npZwjNqi">5.</p>
 <p data-pid="H9KbXQKC">安居不用架高堂，书中自有黄金屋。</p>
 <p data-pid="jW9PsXyO">——赵恒《劝学诗》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BdiL3sHR"><b>6. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="BcCaRvQ2"><b>得成比目何辞死，愿作鸳鸯不羡仙。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="WohwVRhe"><b>——唐·卢照邻《长安古意》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="65tNAsMt">7.</p>
 <p data-pid="3A56yl5N">有缘千里来相会，三笑徒然当一痴。</p>
 <p data-pid="GlCOOsbU">——清·黄增《集杭州俗语诗》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kfio7zzz">8.</p>
 <p data-pid="rqQzntSh">粗缯大布裹生涯，腹有诗书气自华。</p>
 <p data-pid="DUGvOxFK">——宋·苏轼《和董传留别》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="THnOsmNs">9.</p>
 <p data-pid="e4wz-BKM">十年磨一剑，霜刃未曾试。</p>
 <p data-pid="hIkX_fMp">——唐·贾岛《剑客》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6z037NqH">10.</p>
 <p data-pid="wvep5CKe">今朝有酒今朝醉，明日愁来明日愁。</p>
 <p data-pid="r-PHKbqL">——唐·罗隐《自遣》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Sj4fV9j_">11.</p>
 <p data-pid="NINEhj-1">诚知此恨人人有，贫贱夫妻百事哀。</p>
 <p data-pid="qRCnL7il">——元稹《遣悲怀三首其一》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VP1vZ9oC"><b>12. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="DlWR7kg5"><b>人生若只如初见，何事秋风悲画扇？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rgXzCr2z"><b> ——纳兰性德《木兰花令·拟古决绝词》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kehW7BUi">13.</p>
 <p data-pid="Q6rifohn">物以稀为贵，情因老更慈。</p>
 <p data-pid="jI114w1k">——白居易《小岁日喜谈氏外祖女孩满月》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dknwqlUJ">14.</p>
 <p data-pid="qFk4YGl9">近水楼台先得月，向阳花木易为春。</p>
 <p data-pid="Z_S-kNBp">——宋·苏麟《断句》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uvUJFADv">15.</p>
 <p data-pid="-qhOuLkP">枝上柳绵吹又少，天涯何处无芳草。</p>
 <p data-pid="iwr_PsCV">——宋·苏轼《蝶恋花·春景》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8-02WTUI"><b>16. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="QfpDlwLs"><b>衰兰送客咸阳道，天若有情天亦老。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="qRXlMPzi"><b>——唐·李贺《金铜仙人辞汉歌》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="17da9ui1">17.</p>
 <p data-pid="97u8pg5N">冲天香阵透长安，满城尽带黄金甲。</p>
 <p data-pid="IZcN-NJs">——唐·黄巢《不第后赋菊》</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d5e0d4340907bb84afa1998cc8d392d1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="2048" data-rawheight="1536" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-d5e0d4340907bb84afa1998cc8d392d1" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c1e650bb9f825e91c4d334c721e12bdb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2048" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-d5e0d4340907bb84afa1998cc8d392d1_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H-NNt_hy">18.</p>
 <p data-pid="_xajhqiS">别有幽愁暗恨生，此时无声胜有声。</p>
 <p data-pid="d0Q87SKE">——唐·白居易《琵琶行》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="L6rdXsGF">19.</p>
 <p data-pid="53ljqTiX">不到长城非好汉，屈指行程二万。</p>
 <p data-pid="8sAEzrT6">——毛泽东《清平乐·六盘山》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wgrTWncN">20.</p>
 <p data-pid="fCzHE6Sm">因过竹院逢僧话，偷得浮生半日闲。</p>
 <p data-pid="n7qQyEpB">——唐·李涉《题鹤林寺僧舍》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q328FKg6"><b>21. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="LvoV7Pwi"><b>殚竭心力终为子，可怜天下父母心。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="cyjWp-Qa"><b>——清·慈禧《祝母寿诗》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="UYdFsfUN">22.</p>
 <p data-pid="Ka5nYzLc">可怜夜半虚前席，不问苍生问鬼神。</p>
 <p data-pid="O1N35xGl">——李商隐《贾生》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lD5m0Zkh"><b>23.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="pmrQV6M0"><b>为人性僻耽佳句，语不惊人死不休。 </b></p>
 <p data-pid="YixlXot1"><b>——杜甫《江上值水如海势聊短述》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Iawqg5sx" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mhe-YeaT">24.</p>
 <p data-pid="W5g2TR_8">一失足成千古恨，再回首是百年身！</p>
 <p data-pid="PllFlJpJ">——魏秀仁《花月痕》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Li3ahB02">25.</p>
 <p data-pid="2Ji7CODS">多情自古空余恨，好梦由来最易醒。</p>
 <p data-pid="pGRwqmmD">——魏子安《花月痕·第十五回诗》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wLDSwxrt">26.</p>
 <p data-pid="-vKAlHgO">笑渐不闻声渐悄，多情却被无情恼。</p>
 <p data-pid="vRMIC--Z">——苏轼《蝶恋花·春景》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fPUMfQ-S">27.</p>
 <p data-pid="SeQWysdW">君子之交淡如水，小人之交甘若醴。</p>
 <p data-pid="uLcr3PB5">——《庄子·山木》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Y5bpb9Pt"><b>28.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="eRcpYUU_"><b>书到用时方恨少，事非经过不知难。 </b></p>
 <p data-pid="l4FkCBpa"><b>——《增广贤文》</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5JtAScuL">29.</p>
 <p data-pid="23qcQ287">出师未捷身先死，长使英雄泪满襟。</p>
 <p data-pid="g9JFfGUG">——杜甫《蜀相》</p>

 <p data-pid="r-kRmJ65"><b>——秦韬玉《贫女》</b></p>

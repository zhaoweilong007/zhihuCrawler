# 有哪些很惊艳但又很少人知道的古诗词？

<body>
 <p data-pid="xw9RZuFk"><b>1.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="cdM7Pskw">
  <b>酴釄落尽，犹赖有梨花。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="zpRVUaf3">——李清照《转调满庭芳·芳草池塘》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1HIRAuOX"><b>2.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="0pB0_s0d">
  <b>水是眼波横，山是眉峰聚。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="xhGRUiHd">——王观《卜算子·送鲍浩然之浙东》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Gl8qRTmv"><b>3.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pqw6AwkT">
  <b>清谈可以饱，梦想接无由。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="uLPwAUZj">——韩愈《洞庭湖阻风赠张十一署·时自阳山徙掾江陵》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qmaseA0z"><b>4.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="-hzgdJPI">
  <b>殷勤昨夜三更雨，又得浮生一日凉。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="7Keh87VN">——苏轼《鹧鸪天·林断山明竹隐墙》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="A3Yybro-"><b>5.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="YYtvPXJq">
  <b>钟鼎山林都是梦，人间宠辱休惊。只消闲处遇平生。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="yP_ZM8zG">——辛弃疾《临江仙》</p>

 <p data-pid="JYCCAE0U"><b>6.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="1fbqJLFS">
  <b>不知何处雨，已觉此间凉。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="la7a_Rnw">——王遇《句》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TOkP-PiR"><b>7.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="zjOaq-B7">
  <b>海棠未雨，梨花先雪，一半春休。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="fMLgKx8D">——王雱《眼儿媚·杨柳丝丝弄轻柔》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nxz6Oc9-"><b>8.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="cngB385v">
  <b>欲将心事付瑶琴。知音少，弦断有谁听？</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="qzJgIJCm">——岳飞《小重山》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nFse4086"><b>9.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="PTsrw-3m">
  <b>不是凤凰飞不到，更无一树是梧桐。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="1Ai8LZvj">——释行海《无题》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kVornXqk"><b>10.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="WkxS1jmt">
  <b>惜起残红泪满衣，它生莫作有情痴，天地无处着相思。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ABhUTB3e">——况周颐《减字浣溪沙-听歌有感》</p>

 <p data-pid="VL7yIeHR"><b>11.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Kk1rCVb_">
  <b>若似月轮终皎洁，不辞冰雪为卿热。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ycYvlQ9x">——纳兰性德《蝶恋花，辛苦最怜天上月》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SEXNM7H6"><b>12.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="wJgKygO5">
  <b>晚日寒鸦一片愁，柳塘新绿却温柔。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="7Prwus3I">——辛弃疾《鹧鸪天·代人赋》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bwtoZEAW"><b>13.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="OE4PuLG2">
  <b>我亦飘零久，十年来，深恩负尽，死生师友。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="N4SuvZdm">——顾贞观《金缕曲》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="017m0q2G"><b>14.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="pcHUZANS">
  <b>我有江南铁笛，要倚一枝香雪，吹彻玉城霞。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="7hnZFhIx">——张惠言《水调歌头·五首其一》</p>

 <blockquote data-pid="Gh0P4PP9">
  <b>晓来风，夜来雨，晚来烟。是他酿就春色，又断送流年。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Tr2z2LnP">——张惠言《水调歌头·春日赋示杨生子掞》</p>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="DK8ThPAW">——张旭《山中留客》</p>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="DQ5CxMK2">——苏轼《鹧鸪天·林断山明竹隐墙》</p>

 <p data-pid="LGmKi96P"><b>18.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="h_EBJxH1">
  <b>髣髴兮若轻云之蔽月，飘飖兮若流风之回雪。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="zqfqNl7c">——曹植的《洛神赋》</p>

 <p data-pid="r5ZLj108"><b>19.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="_FKYUuVC">
  <b>当时年少春衫薄，骑马倚斜桥，满楼红袖招。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="70LfwQg3">——韦庄《菩萨蛮·如今却忆江南乐》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ByRFdPFQ"><b>20.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="n87Mn2f-">
  <b>情知此后来无计，强说欢期。一别如斯，落尽犁花月又西。</b>
 </blockquote>

 <blockquote data-pid="0Ph4sSDJ">

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="--z6M06a">——陆游《雨霁出游书事》</p>

 <p data-pid="jPobUvRd"><b>22.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="9y9CR6VV">
  <b>黄花庭院，青灯夜雨，白发秋风。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="cZh0Xy8U">——张可久《人月圆•客垂虹》</p>

 <p data-pid="yiNwaHC8"><b>23.</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Msqx9N5b">
  <b>樱花落尽阶前月，象床愁倚薰笼。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="6bsRVWpJ">——李煜《谢新恩·樱花落尽阶前月》</p>

 <blockquote data-pid="d6DTeyY6">

 <p data-pid="cs6zPG7k">——李贺《将进酒》</p>

 <p data-pid="y_YV9B9W">——虞集《风入松·寄柯敬仲》</p>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="g3PMLGrx">——杜甫《宿府》</p>

 <blockquote data-pid="VoQpAmEp">
  <b>何时仗尔看南雪，我与梅花两白头。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="k4QyLRXK">——徐珂《清稗类钞》</p>

 <blockquote data-pid="kZc7mCsF">
  <b>伤心桥下春波绿，曾是惊鸿照影来。</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ZYsJ7msW">——陆游《沈园二首》</p>

 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="4ktX0KBO">——辛弃疾《生查子·独游雨岩》</p>

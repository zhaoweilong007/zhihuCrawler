# 你读过最惊艳的现代诗是什么？
- 点赞数：6380
- 更新时间：2020年10月16日20时36分35秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/337830799/answer/1505216967
<body>
 <p data-pid="6ocI-Dl7">我是在晋江看小说时看到的，在作话里。作者说这首诗让她感动了很久，我觉得也让我感动，内心有种说不出的感觉。</p>
 <p data-pid="EVk1hKMF"><b>《行者》</b></p>
 <p data-pid="EvX-Xe6_"><b>扎西拉姆•多多</b></p>
 <p data-pid="qkTPSrLg"><b>当你在穿山越岭的另一边</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wR8F-AwE"><b>我在孤独的路上没有尽头</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-fmUt7Xr"><b>你的表情总是很温暖</b></p>
 <p data-pid="W7CxskIo"><b>像一阵无心的阳光</b></p>
 <p data-pid="lnhB9ZcB"><b>扬起我心上的柔软尘埃</b></p>
 <p data-pid="y-umAVRX"><b>本来要哭出的眼泪就此忍住</b></p>
 <p data-pid="QgDIcj6E"><b>忍不住的</b></p>
 <p data-pid="AsaqGrhu"><b>是拔足向你奔去的</b></p>
 <p data-pid="BGPu1ECv"><b>愿望</b></p>
 <p data-pid="iVaoL-BL"><b>就像是一座山</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dxJ8Ipcm"><b>你端坐在我微仰目光的前方</b></p>
 <p data-pid="CvCtT_oe"><b>当我还在犹豫</b></p>
 <p data-pid="WRzXQVgY"><b>你已经敞开所有行走的秘道</b></p>
 <p data-pid="oeiSaY44"><b>指引我穿越</b></p>
 <p data-pid="lzZMeQ5V"><b>莫名的忧伤</b></p>
 <p data-pid="e0kipnGM"><b>山其实</b></p>
 <p data-pid="pmISFEKg"><b>不需要被仰望</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ztg8Hgg0"><b>他更愿意给你一个新高度</b></p>
 <p data-pid="5sQ6wXhf"><b>去自由而庄严地</b></p>
 <p data-pid="BssdfU7_"><b>回身俯看</b></p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="OpEjuodB">另一首诗前面很多人说过了，就是<b>博尔赫斯的《我用什么才能留住你？》</b></p>
 <p data-pid="RmZQzfcV"><b>What can I hold you with?</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZIDKopNb"><b>我用什么才能留驻你？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="uXA2_kcy"><b>Jorges Luis Borges</b></p>
 <p data-pid="t1C-4H06"><b>博尔赫斯</b></p>
 <p data-pid="h7NkoQZ8"><b>I offer you lean streets, desperate sunsets, the moon of the jagged suburbs.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-DsoO8f7"><b>我给你贫穷的街道、绝望的日落、破败郊区的月亮。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZPt-eJov"><b>I offer you the bitterness of a man who has looked long and long at the lonely moon.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="v5xITkzj"><b>我给你一个久久地望着孤月的人的悲哀。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="knNby0iH"><b>I offer you my ancestors, my dead men, the ghosts that living men have honoured in marble: my father’s father killed in the frontier of Buenos Aires, two bullets through his lungs, bearded and dead, wrapped by his soldiers in the hide of a cow;</b></p>
 <p data-pid="O_X113IK"><b>我给你我已死去的先辈，人们用大理石纪念他们的幽灵：在布宜偌斯艾利斯边境阵亡的我父亲的父亲，两颗子弹穿了他的胸膛。蓄着胡子的他死去了，士兵们用牛皮裹起他的尸体；</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8XL3XxaK"><b>my mother’s grandfather -just twentyfour- heading a charge of three hundred men in Perú, now ghosts on vanished horses.I offer you whatever insight my books may hold. whatever manliness or humour my life.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="w5nlYaYR"><b>我母亲的祖父——时年二十四岁——在秘鲁率领三百名士兵冲锋，如今都成了消失的马背上的幽灵。我给你我写的书中所能包含的一切悟力、我生活中所能有的男子气概或幽默。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="PRmASmBW"><b>I offer you the loyalty of a man who has never been loyal.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_JCmvSbk"><b>我给你一个从未有过信仰人的忠诚。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rL-2y8f2"><b>I offer you that kernel of myself that I have saved somehow -the central heart that deals not in words, traffics not with dreams and is untouched by time, by joy, by adversities.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="pWDZDThv"><b>我给你我设法保全的我自己的核心——不营字造句，不和梦想交易，不被时间、欢乐和逆境触动的核心。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="heDWUgRj"><b>I offer you the memory of a yellow rose seen at sunset, years before you were born.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Tmf2_4HD"><b>我给你，早在你出生前多年的一个傍晚看到的一朵黄玫瑰的记忆。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="cT8CmTiK"><b>I offer you explanationsof yourself, theories about yourself, authentic and surprising news of yourself.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wXb5tI4H"><b>我给你对自己的解释，关于你自己的理论，你自己的真实而惊人的消息。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="S970j71f"><b>I can give you my loneliness, my darkness, the hunger of my heart; I am trying to bribe you with uncertainty, with danger, with defeat</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vtjQosyc"><b>我给你我的寂寞、我的黑暗、我心的饥渴；我试图用困惑、危险、失败来打动你。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="cs7yjoh8">知道这首诗也是从小说中看到的（捂脸）。</p>

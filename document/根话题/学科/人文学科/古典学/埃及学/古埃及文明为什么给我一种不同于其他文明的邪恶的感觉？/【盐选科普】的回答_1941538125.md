<body>
 <p>古埃及<span><span>，</span></span>相当于埃及的童年时代<span><span>，</span></span>它有个特点<span><span>：</span></span>费朝代<span><span>，</span></span>特别费朝代<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这么说吧<span><span>，</span></span>我们要讲的这段历史<span><span>，</span></span>是从公元前 3000 多年<span><span>，</span></span>到公元前 30 年<span><span>。</span></span>在这段时间里<span><span>，</span></span>中国经历了夏<span><span>、</span></span>商<span><span>、</span></span>周<span><span>、</span></span>秦<span><span>、</span></span>汉<span><span>，</span></span>总共五个朝代<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>而古埃及就厉害了<span><span>，</span></span>人家经历了三十多个朝代<span><span>，</span></span>生生把改朝换代这种大事干成了流水线<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>其实并不是古埃及人有多勤快<span><span>，</span></span>这三十多个王朝<span><span>，</span></span>都是后人给他们划分的<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 </figure>
 <p>古埃及的大部分王朝都是按这个标准划分的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这么多王朝<span><span>，</span></span>我们怎么记呢<span><span>？</span></span>不必担心<span><span>，</span></span>虽然古埃及朝代多<span><span>，</span></span>但它的历史主要就是三件事<span><span>：</span></span></p>

 <p>统一</p>
 <p>国家比较团结<span><span>，</span></span>大家只听法老<span><span>（</span></span>古埃及国王的称呼<span><span>）</span></span>的<span><span>，</span></span>类似咱们的汉朝和唐朝<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bab247511c2dcc907ab817a29c224104_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="223" data-rawheight="316" data-original-token="v2-bab247511c2dcc907ab817a29c224104" class="content_image" width="223">
 </figure>
 <p>分裂</p>
 <p>地方势力逐渐强大<span><span>，</span></span>冒出一堆政权<span><span>，</span></span>类似咱们的南北朝和五代十国<span><span>。</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4e3ff301a8e7dad261796d8508ee9bcf_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="309" data-rawheight="316" data-original-token="v2-4e3ff301a8e7dad261796d8508ee9bcf" class="content_image" width="309">
 </figure>
 <p>外族统治</p>
 <p>自己人不行了<span><span>，</span></span>外面的强大民族过来接管<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>知道了这些<span><span>，</span></span>我们就可以正式开讲古埃及了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d6cf76d4e66bbff7812f842f0f367406_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="1303" data-rawheight="113" data-original-token="v2-d6cf76d4e66bbff7812f842f0f367406" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1303" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-d6cf76d4e66bbff7812f842f0f367406_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>首先我们要知道<span><span>，</span></span>埃及在哪里<span><span>。</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ac123a303f2a656a2dcdf4b06589fa01_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="721" data-rawheight="691" data-original-token="v2-ac123a303f2a656a2dcdf4b06589fa01" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="721" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ac123a303f2a656a2dcdf4b06589fa01_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>它就坐落在非洲的东北角上<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这里在古代可不是什么宝地<span><span>。</span></span>如果要用一个词总结这里的环境<span><span>，</span></span>就是两个字<span><span>：</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-be92b3b6cc8826cdb77c059596cdca19_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="968" data-rawheight="394" data-original-token="v2-be92b3b6cc8826cdb77c059596cdca19" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="968" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-be92b3b6cc8826cdb77c059596cdca19_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p>但是<span><span>，</span></span>就这么一个种啥啥不长的地方<span><span>，</span></span>却被神奇地划开了一道口子<span><span>。</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-97ed86341cff8f7e1a63d603a91d498f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="1243" data-rawheight="694" data-original-token="v2-97ed86341cff8f7e1a63d603a91d498f" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1243" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-97ed86341cff8f7e1a63d603a91d498f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p>尼罗河发源于南边的东非高原<span><span>，</span></span>流经埃及<span><span>，</span></span>最后注入地中海<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>它流经的地方土壤肥沃<span><span>，</span></span>能种庄稼<span><span>，</span></span>最早的古埃及人<span><span>，</span></span>就在河的沿岸定居<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>所以乍一看<span><span>，</span></span>尼罗河就像一棵加长版的西蓝花<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>一开始<span><span>，</span></span>上下埃及各玩各的<span><span>，</span></span>直到有一天<span><span>，</span></span>一个本地人统一了这两个地方<span><span>，</span></span>建立了古埃及的第一个王朝<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>还有一种说法是美尼斯统一了埃及<span><span>。</span></span>纳尔迈和美尼斯是什么关系呢<span><span>？</span></span>对此一直有争论<span><span>，</span></span>有的说是同一个人<span><span>，</span></span>有的说是父子<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但是大家都过惯了散养的日子<span><span>，</span></span>突然统一起来<span><span>，</span></span>需要一段磨合期<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>直到四百多年后<span><span>，</span></span>古埃及才真正稳定下来<span><span>。</span></span>大家都听法老的<span><span>，</span></span>法老让干啥就干啥<span><span>。</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-002de30dd488abf2d1eb6a8485c37536_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="1069" data-rawheight="555" data-original-token="v2-002de30dd488abf2d1eb6a8485c37536" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1069" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-002de30dd488abf2d1eb6a8485c37536_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p>这时候的埃及<span><span>，</span></span>是一个中央集权的专制国家<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>而且古埃及的法老很务实<span><span>，</span></span>他们觉得专制这种事<span><span>，</span></span>不能只是嘴上说说<span><span>，</span></span>还得付诸行动<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>那么<span><span>，</span></span>怎么做才能体现专制呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bf1ddd6a19861cc6026d8a934b96b584_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="1081" data-rawheight="537" data-original-token="v2-bf1ddd6a19861cc6026d8a934b96b584" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1081" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-bf1ddd6a19861cc6026d8a934b96b584_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p>是的<span><span>，</span></span>他除了操心人事制度<span><span>，</span></span>还要操心不在人世的制度<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这个事就很讲究了<span><span>，</span></span>首先<span><span>，</span></span>法老去世之后不能马上埋<span><span>，</span></span>得先脱水做成干尸<span><span>，</span></span>因为这样保质期能久一点<span><span>。</span></span>这就是木乃伊<span><span>。</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-9a5ca82f306910e09f78626021567920_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="1104" data-rawheight="283" data-original-token="v2-9a5ca82f306910e09f78626021567920" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1104" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-9a5ca82f306910e09f78626021567920_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p>然后<span><span>，</span></span>埋也不能随便埋<span><span>，</span></span>得建个像样的陵墓<span><span>。</span></span>这些陵墓的外观就像一个<span><span>「</span></span>金<span><span>」</span></span>字<span><span>。</span></span>这就是金字塔<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>最大的金字塔<span><span>，</span></span>就是法老胡夫执政时建造的胡夫金字塔<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>好了<span><span>，</span></span>法老的权力体现出来了<span><span>，</span></span>但这么大个国家<span><span>，</span></span>在那个交通基本靠腿的年代<span><span>，</span></span>该怎么来管呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>

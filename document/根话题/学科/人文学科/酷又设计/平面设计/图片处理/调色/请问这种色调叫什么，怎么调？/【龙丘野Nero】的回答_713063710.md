 <p data-pid="Ex0jw1sl">以下是原答案。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="N_pgLLd7">不请自来。</p>
 <p data-pid="ozt5fYI_"><b>这种色调是INS青橙色调的一种变体。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Cilr8kFV"><b>ps：根据下文思路做了个预设，放在文末。</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3995fe949923b991f2045b6adb699458_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1202" data-rawheight="944" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-3995fe949923b991f2045b6adb699458" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4a85ebf3eb8c02eac00407ac67ccfcb5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1202" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-3995fe949923b991f2045b6adb699458_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7013b9fd06be0af677dd580fe1136f7d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1195" data-rawheight="936" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-7013b9fd06be0af677dd580fe1136f7d" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-631067db35b27695a99a4aecfb5a9039_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1195" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-7013b9fd06be0af677dd580fe1136f7d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="GYyarbeq">用拾色器点一点，很容易看出<b>主色调</b>是<b>青色</b>和<b>橙色</b>，橙色<b>饱和度和明度较高</b>，青色<b>饱和度适中明度较低</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="5JlwqPBn">这样的作品风格，前几日大火的<b>日本摄影师rkrkrk</b>也经常使用。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-a854c0646562216fff4ae01fd64be115_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="619" data-rawheight="782" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-a854c0646562216fff4ae01fd64be115" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-10f6ead039fcd057ee13c72c4f42b663_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="619" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-a854c0646562216fff4ae01fd64be115_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="8c0TlqJn">要达成这样的效果，我们首先使用<b>HSL梯形调整</b>，使<b>冷暖统一</b>，再微调<b>冷暖色色相/饱和度和明度，</b>最后在<b>阴影加一点青色</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="SLo4JFKy">随便拿一张图片做演示，百度来的：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-af0edd6f07df9cac9d840de08d79e96b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1543" data-rawheight="1020" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-af0edd6f07df9cac9d840de08d79e96b" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5191a3d7432edcd448e2ff9cb545b106_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1543" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-af0edd6f07df9cac9d840de08d79e96b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ir2y-g4W">打开<b>Camera raw/Lightroom/泼辣修图</b>，点开<b>HSL面板—色相</b>。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4565e4d8ebc49314a303c18c507f8c45_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="281" data-rawheight="374" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-4565e4d8ebc49314a303c18c507f8c45" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3fcd9ad6ce4d900351f3bf861de357d9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="281">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="xgZKotey">大范围调整色相，使<b>冷色偏青</b>，<b>暖色偏橙</b>。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-21d696fccc938a8a4102655d3a2ffa80_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1547" data-rawheight="1025" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-21d696fccc938a8a4102655d3a2ffa80" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-02130f4c9e1a80cf8a08d3572dfe7b88_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1547" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-21d696fccc938a8a4102655d3a2ffa80_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-dd2864f8a99b11e62d829c53b3a5cbb8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="280" data-rawheight="367" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-dd2864f8a99b11e62d829c53b3a5cbb8" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-fd476763e62e0623614e9fa214d4d357_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="280">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="5moYe1_I">然后使<b>蓝色饱和度降低</b>，<b>提高橙色饱和度</b>。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0154f41ac894ce4f84a2e86c7189bc3d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1548" data-rawheight="1020" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-0154f41ac894ce4f84a2e86c7189bc3d" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-4f3cb1a0251e38c29f5e72ef711c8958_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1548" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-0154f41ac894ce4f84a2e86c7189bc3d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 </figure>

# 有哪些让你忍不住想吐槽/点赞/觉得蛋疼的设计或者设定？
- 点赞数：11832
- 更新时间：2020年03月06日15时55分48秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="CkOoddYc">这款专门为<b>帕金森患者</b>设计的<b>防抖勺子</b>，绝对<b>要赞一下</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="GSU5VmfQ">因为帕金森患者手部的<b>震颤抖动</b>，使得他们无法<b>独立吃饭</b>。这款勺子内装有<b>传感器</b>，并有和<b>防抖镜头</b>类似的算法，能感知和预测患者的<b>手部抖动</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="L-sW9rRo">同时装有<b>伺服运动机构</b>，能控制前端的勺子，进行反向抵消运动，<b>主动消除震颤</b>，尽可能保持平衡，尽量<b>避免勺子里面的食物洒落</b>。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/954738879627087872" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-dcadb8657a8a6ca9917e55045499fd76_b.jpg" data-lens-id="954738879627087872"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/80/v2-dcadb8657a8a6ca9917e55045499fd76_b.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/954738879627087872</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="10ngFxoc">产品名字叫<b>Liftware spoon</b>防抖勺子，型号是<b>Liftware Steady</b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OMogdfhV">再说下<b>另外一款</b>防抖勺子吧，用于<b>脑瘫，脊髓损伤，中风后遗症等</b>，造成的<b>手臂或胳膊活动受限</b>的情况<b>。</b>从外观上看前端是<b>柔性的结构，可一直使勺子面保持水平。型号是：Liftware Level。</b></p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/954703964596334592" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic4.zhimg.com/80/v2-ffe0ba6baacd8539ef4f8a13efc46fb7_b.jpg" data-lens-id="954703964596334592"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/80/v2-ffe0ba6baacd8539ef4f8a13efc46fb7_b.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/954703964596334592</span></span></a>

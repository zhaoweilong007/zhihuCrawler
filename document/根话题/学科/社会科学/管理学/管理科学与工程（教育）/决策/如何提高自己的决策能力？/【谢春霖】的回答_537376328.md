 <h2>先来看一下<strong>你为什么会纠结……</strong></h2>
 <p><strong>开始之前<span><span>，</span></span>我先问你几个问题<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <ol>
  <li>去星巴克喝咖啡<span><span>，</span></span>大杯拿铁 34 元<span><span>，</span></span>加 3 元升超大杯<span><span>，</span></span>你要不要升<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>和别人约会早到了半小时<span><span>，</span></span>你会打一局<span><span>《</span></span>王者荣耀<span><span>》</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>还是看一会书<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>好友问你借钱<span><span>，</span></span>借的话你舍不得<span><span>，</span></span>不借面子又挂不住<span><span>，</span></span>你是借还是不借<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>高考没进自己的理想大学<span><span>，</span></span>你会选择复读<span><span>，</span></span>还是去上一个不喜欢的大专院校<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>毕业后<span><span>，</span></span>你更愿意去大公司拿稳定的收入<span><span>，</span></span>还是去创业公司用时间换空间<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>你 30 岁了<span><span>，</span></span>是会继续在不喜欢的公司里打工<span><span>，</span></span>还是跳槽转行<span><span>，</span></span>或者下海创业<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>有两个男生追你<span><span>，</span></span>一个家财万贯<span><span>，</span></span>但是天生愚笨<span><span>，</span></span>长相一般<span><span>，</span></span>性格暴躁<span><span>，</span></span>另一个英俊帅气有才华<span><span>，</span></span>性格温柔<span><span>，</span></span>就是没钱<span><span>，</span></span>你选哪一个<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>你是一家大公司的 CEO<span><span>，</span></span>年度战略会议上<span><span>，</span></span>两个部门都说自己的业务重要<span><span>，</span></span>问你要预算<span><span>，</span></span>一个看似更有前景<span><span>，</span></span>但是近期不会赚钱<span><span>，</span></span>另一个看似很赚钱<span><span>，</span></span>但是发展空间有限<span><span>，</span></span>而公司预算有限<span><span>，</span></span>你会怎么选择<span><span>？</span></span></li>
 </ol>
 <p>都说选择大于努力<span><span>，</span></span>可应该怎么选才对<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>成功学会告诉你<span><span>，</span></span>成大事者不纠结<span><span>，</span></span>可如何才能不纠结<span><span>？</span></span>面对选择<span><span>，</span></span>你就是会犹豫<span><span>，</span></span>会纠结啊<span><span>，</span></span>到底该如何做出果断的决策呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>好<span><span>，</span></span>我现在再给你出一个选择题<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>两个骰子<span><span>，</span></span>一个 6<span><span>，</span></span>一个 2<span><span>，</span></span>请选一个点数更大的<span><span>。</span></span>请问你选哪个<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>你会说<span><span>，</span></span>这不是废话嘛<span><span>，</span></span>当然选 6……</p>
 <p>那么问题来了<span><span>，</span></span>面对这个选择<span><span>，</span></span>为啥你就不纠结了呢<span><span>？</span></span>这与前面的问题区别在哪<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>答案是<span><span>：</span></span>这个问题有一个统一的选择标准<span><span>，</span></span>而且选项可以被量化<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>选择标准是<span><span>：</span></span>比点数的大小<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>而骰子点数的大小<span><span>，</span></span>是可以被量化的<span><span>，</span></span>一个 2<span><span>，</span></span>一个 6<span><span>，</span></span>眼睛就能分辨<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>6 大于 2<span><span>，</span></span>因此<span><span>，</span></span>选 6<span><span>，</span></span>这个答案是显而易见的<span><span>，</span></span>我们完全不需要纠结<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>可我们经常遇到的并不是这类选择题<span><span>，</span></span>而是更像<span><span>「</span></span>石头剪子布<span><span>」</span></span>一样的选择题<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>石头＞剪子<span><span>，</span></span>剪子＞布<span><span>，</span></span>而布＞石头……</p>
 <p>你说哪个好<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>那你可能就不知道该怎么选了……</p>
 <p>这就像你要找一个终身伴侣<span><span>。</span></span>面对两位候选人<span><span>，</span></span>A 比 B 有钱<span><span>；</span></span>B 比 A 有才<span><span>；</span></span>A 又比 B 帅<span><span>；</span></span>B 又比 A 性格好……</p>
 <p>怎么选<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>好不容易想选 A 了<span><span>，</span></span>结果 B 又给你做了顿烛光晚餐<span><span>，</span></span>送了你一大束玫瑰<span><span>，</span></span>跟你许下了山盟海誓……</p>
 <p>你又犹豫了……</p>
 <p>为什么<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>因为他们之间没有统一的比较标准<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>你每次都是拿局部比局部<span><span>，</span></span>结果比来比去各有优势<span><span>，</span></span>所以才纠结了嘛<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>因此<span><span>，</span></span>纠结只是症状<span><span>，</span></span>导致我们纠结的是选择没有统一的标准<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>你看似在做选择<span><span>，</span></span>其实是在找标准<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>如果标准能确定<span><span>，</span></span>选项可以被量化<span><span>，</span></span>那么<span><span>，</span></span>这就变成了骰子比大小的问题<span><span>，</span></span>答案也就自然出来了<span><span>，</span></span>你根本就不需要做选择<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>做选择<span><span>，</span></span>其实就是把石头剪子布的问题<span><span>，</span></span>变成比骰子比大小的问题……</strong></p>
 <p><strong>一<span><span>、</span></span>科学决策的四个步骤</strong></p>
 <p>我还是拿<span><span>「</span></span>如何挑选老公<span><span>」</span></span>来举例说明该如何做科学的决策<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>假设<span><span>，</span></span>现在有两位帅哥同时在追求你<span><span>，</span></span>一个叫王小帅<span><span>，</span></span>一个叫李有钱<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>王小帅</strong><span><span>，</span></span>30 岁<span><span>，</span></span>程序员<span><span>，</span></span>家境一般<span><span>，</span></span>但很有才华<span><span>，</span></span>受老板器重<span><span>，</span></span>目前在一家创业公司上班<span><span>，</span></span>拿着 25 万的年薪<span><span>，</span></span>工作非常努力<span><span>，</span></span>每天加班到很晚<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>李有钱</strong><span><span>，</span></span>33 岁<span><span>，</span></span>富二代<span><span>，</span></span>长得帅<span><span>，</span></span>目前在某国企上班<span><span>，</span></span>老爸托人安排的职位<span><span>，</span></span>15 万年薪<span><span>，</span></span>工作<span><span>、</span></span>生活都比较安逸<span><span>，</span></span>有过多次恋爱经历<span><span>，</span></span>娱乐生活丰富<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>好<span><span>，</span></span>请问你怎么选<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>比收入<span><span>？</span></span>比家境<span><span>？</span></span>比长相<span><span>？</span></span>比智商<span><span>？</span></span>还是比性格<span><span>？</span></span>这又变成石头剪子布的问题了……</p>
 <p>要做选择<span><span>，</span></span>一定得有统一的标准<span><span>，</span></span>你不能只比其中的一个维度<span><span>（</span></span>除非你只看重一个维度<span><span>，</span></span>其他都不在乎<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>也不能拿一个人的收入和另外一个人的长相比<span><span>，</span></span>它们不是一个标准<span><span>，</span></span>没有可比性<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>那怎么办<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第一步<span><span>：</span></span>列出标准</strong></p>
 <p>首先<span><span>，</span></span>你要把你在乎的所有要素都作为比较标准列出来<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>那么<span><span>，</span></span>有哪些要素可以作为比较标准呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>你可以从三个方向去寻找<span><span>，</span></span>分别是<span><span>：</span></span>过去<span><span>、</span></span>现在<span><span>、</span></span>未来<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <ol>
  <li><strong>过去<span><span>：</span></span>寻找历史记录</strong></li>
 </ol>
 <p>往过去找什么<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>找历史记录<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>就是备选对象有哪些过往的经历可以拿来作为判断依据<span><span>，</span></span>比如说<span><span>，</span></span>成长经历<span><span>，</span></span>学习经历<span><span>，</span></span>职场经历<span><span>，</span></span>获奖经历<span><span>，</span></span>情感史<span><span>，</span></span>犯罪史等等<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这些内容代表备选对象过去是什么样的<span><span>，</span></span>虽然不能代表未来也会如此<span><span>，</span></span>但是可以很大程度上帮助你做出大概率的预测<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如<span><span>，</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>李有钱<span><span>」</span></span>告诉你<span><span>，</span></span>虽然他过去谈过 5 个女朋友<span><span>，</span></span>都是不到半年就分手了<span><span>，</span></span>但是<span><span>「</span></span>遇到你之后<span><span>，</span></span>我才知道什么叫作缘定今生<span><span>，</span></span>我要和你厮守终老<span><span>，</span></span>白首不相离……<span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>那么<span><span>，</span></span>根据他的情感史来判断<span><span>，</span></span>你有理由给他的这句话打个折扣<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>2.现在<span><span>：</span></span>寻找当下的参考点</strong></p>
 <p>在当下找什么<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>找参考点<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>什么是参考点<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>比如你在丛林中迷路了<span><span>，</span></span>面对岔路口<span><span>，</span></span>你该走哪条路<span><span>？</span></span>你的选择标准是什么<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>是你手中的指南针<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>指南针<span><span>，</span></span>就是你在当下能找到的参考点<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>参考点有什么特征<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>就是一般情况下<span><span>，</span></span>它不怎么会变<span><span>。</span></span>你在森林中的任何位置<span><span>，</span></span>指南针的方向永远是固定的<span><span>，</span></span>这样<span><span>，</span></span>你才可以用这个<span><span>「</span></span>不变<span><span>」</span></span>作为参考<span><span>，</span></span>在模糊中看清方向<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你在选老公的时候<span><span>，</span></span>也可以设立一些不变的标准作为参考点<span><span>，</span></span>用来衡量备选对象是否与你的目标一致<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>那么<span><span>，</span></span>有哪些指标可以作为参考点呢<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>比如<span><span>，</span></span>你可以定义<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <ul>
  <li>资产=200 万<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>收入=25 万/年<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>身高=175cm<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>长相=刘德华<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>智商=120<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>性格=1 周内不发脾气<span><span>，</span></span>说话能把我逗乐<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>还有价值观<span><span>、</span></span>能力<span><span>、</span></span>人品<span><span>、</span></span>格局等等<span><span>。</span></span></li>
 </ul>
 <p>注意<span><span>，</span></span>这些不是筛选条件<span><span>，</span></span>而是参考点<span><span>。</span></span>不是说对象资产没有到 200 万就排除了<span><span>，</span></span>而是你心中理想的标准<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>然后<span><span>，</span></span>根据对象目前的状况<span><span>，</span></span>与参考点进行比较<span><span>，</span></span>打分<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>比如<span><span>，</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>王小帅<span><span>」</span></span>的个人资产目前有 40 万<span><span>，</span></span>那么他在<span><span>「</span></span>资产<span><span>」</span></span>这个参考点上<span><span>，</span></span>得分只有 20 分<span><span>（</span></span>满分 100 分<span><span>，</span></span>40/200=20 分<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但是<span><span>，</span></span>他目前年收入有 25 万<span><span>，</span></span>那么在<span><span>「</span></span>收入<span><span>」</span></span>这个参考点上<span><span>，</span></span>得分就是 100 分<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>当然<span><span>，</span></span>还有一种方法<span><span>，</span></span>就是可以直接拿一个你心目中认为完美的人来作为<span><span>「</span></span>指南针<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>比如<span><span>，</span></span>你特别崇拜乔布斯这样的人<span><span>，</span></span>想找一个这样特征的人做老公<span><span>，</span></span>那么你就可以拿他作为<span><span>「</span></span>指南针<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>然后看你的选择对象<span><span>，</span></span>有百分之多少像他<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这个也是很多投资公司<span><span>，</span></span>在选择投资项目时常用的选择方式之一<span><span>，</span></span>他们心中会有很多成功公司的模板<span><span>，</span></span>比如 Apple<span><span>、</span></span>Amazon<span><span>、</span></span>Facebook<span><span>、</span></span>Airbnb<span><span>、</span></span>阿里巴巴<span><span>、</span></span>腾讯…….然后就会拿投资项目去和这些公司做对比<span><span>，</span></span>看看有几分像它们<span><span>，</span></span>期待你也能有机会成为下一个腾讯<span><span>，</span></span>成为某某领域的 Airbnb<span><span>，</span></span>这些就是他们心中的<span><span>「</span></span>指南针<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>3.未来<span><span>：</span></span>可能性预测</strong></p>
 <p>就是基于你的认知<span><span>，</span></span>和对他们的了解<span><span>，</span></span>以及对于环境的判断<span><span>，</span></span>你认为对象未来会有哪些可能性<span><span>，</span></span>也就是想象空间有多大<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如<span><span>，</span></span>王小帅<span><span>，</span></span>他目前是名优秀的程序员<span><span>，</span></span>是公司的技术骨干<span><span>，</span></span>公司从事的领域是研究五级自动驾驶技术<span><span>，</span></span>并且已经拿到了 A 轮融资<span><span>，</span></span>如今正在快速地发展<span><span>。</span></span>那么你有理由预测<span><span>，</span></span>他未来的收入潜力巨大<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>你也可以对他们的情感专一性进行预测<span><span>，</span></span>比如李有钱<span><span>，</span></span>因为长得帅<span><span>，</span></span>又是富二代<span><span>，</span></span>还经常出入各种娱乐场所<span><span>，</span></span>身边美女众多<span><span>。</span></span>你预计他未来出轨的可能性有 40%<span><span>，</span></span>而王小帅<span><span>，</span></span>天天加班<span><span>，</span></span>身边也都是技术宅男<span><span>，</span></span>出轨的可能性只有 5%<span><span>。</span></span>那么这项得分<span><span>，</span></span>王小帅就略胜一筹……</p>
 <p>反观到商业<span><span>，</span></span>如果你是一名天使投资人<span><span>，</span></span>你要投资一家创业公司<span><span>，</span></span>也需要预测这家公司未来的发展空间<span><span>，</span></span>你可以根据公司所在的行业<span><span>、</span></span>市场趋势<span><span>、</span></span>潜在用户规模等等<span><span>，</span></span>看这个市场的天花板在哪<span><span>，</span></span>这是个能做多大的生意<span><span>，</span></span>这就是行业内常说的<span><span>「</span></span>看赛道<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>第二步<span><span>：</span></span>分配权重</strong></p>
 <p>标准是列出来了<span><span>，</span></span>然后就开始打分了吗<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>还不行<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>首先<span><span>，</span></span>你要把<span><span>「</span></span>筛选条件<span><span>」</span></span>从表里拿出来<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>什么是筛选条件<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>就是能一票否决的<span><span>，</span></span>只要备选对象符合某些点<span><span>，</span></span>就立刻排除<span><span>，</span></span>其他再好也没用<span><span>。</span></span>比如<span><span>，</span></span>你可以设立几个筛选条件<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <ol>
  <li>有犯罪史</li>
  <li>有黄赌毒等恶性嗜好</li>
  <li>年龄比我小</li>
 </ol>
 <p>只要符合以上任何一点的<span><span>，</span></span>立刻排除<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>还好这两位都没有……</p>
 <p><strong>然后<span><span>，</span></span>你就需要对剩下的这些标准进行权重分配<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>比如你认为性格&gt;收入&gt;长相<span><span>，</span></span>那么就需要给它们分配不同的权重<span><span>。</span></span>具体怎么分<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>高能预警<span><span>：</span></span>做科学决策绕不过数学</strong><span><span>，</span></span>所以<span><span>，</span></span>接下来会用到一些数学计算<span><span>，</span></span>看着可能有点复杂<span><span>，</span></span>但基本都是小学数学的级别<span><span>，</span></span>仔细一点应该没问题<span><span>，</span></span><strong>别觉得麻烦<span><span>，</span></span>你怕麻烦的地方<span><span>，</span></span>也许正是你薄弱的环节……</strong></p>
 <p><strong>1.对大类分配权重</strong></p>
 <p>就是先对<span><span>「</span></span>过去<span><span>、</span></span>当下<span><span>、</span></span>未来<span><span>」</span></span>这三大类分配权重<span><span>，</span></span>如果你的价值偏好比较保守<span><span>，</span></span>相信看得见的东西<span><span>，</span></span>那么就对过去<span><span>、</span></span>当下分配比较高的权重<span><span>，</span></span>比如 30%<span><span>，</span></span>50%<span><span>。</span></span>对未来分配 20% 的权重<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>而如果你的价值偏好比较激进<span><span>，</span></span>想赌一把大的<span><span>，</span></span>不看过去<span><span>，</span></span>看未来<span><span>，</span></span>更看重对象的未来发展<span><span>，</span></span>那就可以调高未来预测部分的权重<span><span>，</span></span>比如<span><span>，</span></span>10%/30%/60%<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>假设在这里<span><span>，</span></span>你是一名保守派<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>3.加入别人的建议</strong></p>
 <p>有些问题<span><span>，</span></span>你自己的判断并不一定准确<span><span>，</span></span>也想听听别人的建议<span><span>，</span></span>特别是自己不懂的领域<span><span>，</span></span>更想参考一下专家的建议<span><span>，</span></span>那么<span><span>，</span></span>你就需要把这部分也纳入到表单里<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>权重的分配<span><span>，</span></span>可以按你对问题的<span><span>「</span></span>了解程度<span><span>」</span></span>进行划分<span><span>，</span></span>如果自己比较有把握<span><span>，</span></span>比如这个感情的案例<span><span>，</span></span>那么自己的权重就高一点<span><span>，</span></span>别人的权重低一点<span><span>，</span></span>比如<span><span>，</span></span>自己=80%/别人=20%<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>而如果是一些专业领域<span><span>，</span></span>你自己不怎么懂<span><span>，</span></span>找不到什么好的评判标准<span><span>，</span></span>那么就可以调高别人的权重<span><span>，</span></span>比如<span><span>，</span></span>自己 30%/别人 70%<span><span>。</span></span></p>

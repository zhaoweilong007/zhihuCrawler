 <p data-pid="EOlJMBH5">10.18 没想到这片回答破千啦 也看了大家很多的评论 大部分也是关于有些词该不该替换 。发布的初衷呢也是希望在掌握了英语作文必备的语句框架和语法点的同学 想追求高级词汇的同学可以稍微借鉴下～虽说有些词汇替换那么复杂但其实可以用简单的词概括，但也不得不说，应试教育下，确实是阅卷老师批作文心证比较多（也就是主观色彩啦）</p>
 <p data-pid="ReIrNFBW">也是因为汉语没有英语那么准确，汉语的一个词在英语里会去更细的分，所以就出现了可以替换的词，大家在整理，运用的时候也要留心差别！</p>
 <p data-pid="mNdhUL2p">还有喔！二手知识也不是一股脑儿的全接！大家注意辨别！</p>

 <p data-pid="nuxSQ0Qw">1) 大多数人most people→ the majority of the population</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="h9qYJf0k">2) 经常often→frequently</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JWxKhv_p">3) 我相信I believe→ from my standpoint, from my perspective</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mNbHS2mM">4) 必须must→ it is a must for us to…</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dpB7PhLX">5) 知道know→ be aware of</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nK-P67X9">6) 因为because→in that</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9f4Ai419">7) 最后at last→eventually</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Y1CMTFHk">8) 然而but→however</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kl3l6I9R">9) 如果if→provided that</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OgtGatYA">10) 各行各业的人all kinds of people→people from all walks of life</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JU2UF_o-">11) 引起，导致lead to→contribute to</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="NCbu3leV">12) 人people→individuals</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WI347c2q">13) 好的good→desirable, beneficial</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fck5eBT0">14) 坏的bad→undesirable</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="k6aY5rFf">15) 很多many→ numerous</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SI6tynJa">16) 越来越more and more→ an increasing number of / a mounting number of</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hfcETO4m">17) 很very→extremely</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="N8iiWcRg">18) 方面side→aspects</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6SXcofhx">19) 表明show→demonstrate, indicate</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LDDEFOyV">20) 利用use→utilize</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="j_LkcBhO">21) 因此/结果so→therefore</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BkEbbiB1">22) 部分part→proportion</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ys6Vs119">23) 提高improve→enhance</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Sf0Dv8Q5">24) 改变change→transform</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <h2><b><i>佳句点滴语料积累</i></b></h2>
 <p data-pid="Ji_cI7KJ">1. The last word went like a bullet to my heart.</p>
 <p data-pid="NqqAGkhI">最后一句话像一颗子弹击中了我的心。</p>
 <p data-pid="mddkL21m">2. A ripple of sadness welled up inside him.</p>
 <p data-pid="dGlUWV5V">他内心涌起一阵阵悲伤.</p>
 <p data-pid="r9YjIH_0">3. He felt the warmth surging through his body.</p>
 <p data-pid="skgtujmh">他感到身体里涌动着一股暖流。</p>
 <p data-pid="LSgG8eSZ">4.The loneliness and grief comes in waves.</p>
 <p data-pid="q1u1wvYP">孤独和悲伤不断涌上心头。</p>
 <p data-pid="60GPJvtL"><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//5.so/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">5.So</a> ashamed was she that she could feel the blood rush to her face.</p>
 <p data-pid="zKPkNC27">她是如此羞愧以至于脸如充血般发烫。</p>
 <p data-pid="rRkRk-C9">6.Her eyes misted over as she immersed herself in their good memories.</p>
 <p data-pid="7IGIbriG">当她沉浸在他们之间的美好回忆时，双眼蒙上了一层水雾。</p>
 <p data-pid="iWLey1WS">7.Her brain quickened and all her senses were alert.</p>
 <p data-pid="fsRS_gMr">她的大脑快速运转，所有的感官都处于戒备状态。</p>
 <p data-pid="yoH4dxbw">8. At the sight of the ferocious wolf, I was seized by a strong sense of horror and my palms were sweating.</p>
 <p data-pid="FWg1xDO7">一看到那只凶猛的狼，我就被深深地恐惧感所控制,手心出汗.</p>
 <p data-pid="vnFVh4Rp">9. When the host announced that she won the prize, her heart was thumping with excitement.</p>
 <p data-pid="9R_P0Gk6">当主持人宣布她获奖时，她激动得心砰砰跳。</p>
 <p data-pid="AWtNe6xR">10. She thought of the flowers glittering in the sunshine and the mountains turning rosy in the setting sun. And every night she cried into her pillow as though her heart would break into pieces.</p>
 <p data-pid="MaK_qjtB">她想起了阳光里闪耀的花儿，落日下慢慢变成玫瑰色的山脉。每一个夜晚，她把头埋在枕头里哭，肝肠寸断。</p>
 <p data-pid="zTIlxo1c">11. 关于“笑”的表达：</p>
 <p data-pid="rT7Q_QF3">--The smile froze on her lips. 她脸上的笑容僵住了。</p>
 <p data-pid="QzacSQhq">--In spite of his disappointment, he managed a weak smile. 他还是勉强露出一丝淡淡的微笑。</p>
 <p data-pid="k72vs-NC">--Alee became happy now and a gentle smile spread over her face. 整张脸洋溢着温和的微笑。</p>
 <p data-pid="cepP6fLP">--His face split into a wide smile. 他的脸上绽放出灿烂的笑容。</p>
 <p data-pid="TN1UFNsa">12. 有关“看”的表达：</p>
 <p data-pid="kwpJpyPW">He didn’t shout, and he just glared at me silently. 他只是默默怒视着我。</p>
 <p data-pid="-2TG6Dpi">He gazed at the ...., unbelievable. 他难以置信地盯着..看。</p>
 <p data-pid="y02wHtBv">He turned to her with a frightened air. 他害怕地看着他。</p>
 <p data-pid="Vhw0jrjd">He was glaring down at me again, his black eyes full of disgust.</p>
 <p data-pid="vajcpctO">他又盯着我看，黑眼睛里充满了厌恶。</p>
 <p data-pid="dGucakux">Her eyes were glued to the screen. 她目不转睛地盯着屏幕。</p>
 <p data-pid="Zto9PKK1">He give a glimpse of...from the corner of his eye. 他用眼睛的余角瞥了一眼。</p>

 <h2><b><i>读后续写--主旨升华</i></b></h2>
 <p data-pid="W9Viy_9Y">助人为乐</p>
 <p data-pid="r1DmAmEb">1. Sometimes, a small act of kindness may be powerful enough to make a huge difference.有时候一个小小的善行可能足以带来巨大的变化。</p>
 <p data-pid="qHZ9sb8U">2. Sometimes small gesture of care will possibly melt people’s loneliness.</p>
 <p data-pid="ohWX_wvf">有时候一个表达关爱的小小举动也可能融化人们内心的孤单。</p>
 <p data-pid="Uic7JqMM">3.His kind act has a lasting influence on me. I always give others a helping hand possible, even so so many years later. 他的善举对我产生了持久的影响。即使多年以后，我总是尽可能地帮助别人。</p>
 <p data-pid="I1CY4u5C">4.I felt warmth in the cold day and believed there was kindness in every corner.</p>
 <p data-pid="hVgESJZY">我在寒冷的天气里感到了温暖,相信每个角落都有善意。</p>
 <p data-pid="lOTgPHw7">5. It turns out that giving those in need a helping hand brings me more pleasure. No wonder people say “Rose given; Fragrance in hand.”</p>
 <p data-pid="y-xt0e01">梦想</p>
 <p data-pid="ZLfsIm5X">1. The world makes way for the dreamer.世界总会给有梦想的人开路</p>
 <p data-pid="9vT12jZQ">2. Each man is the architect of his own fate.每个人都是自己命运的建筑师<br>
  3. It dawned on him that one requires more patience in any task being accomplished.</p>
 <p data-pid="mm8f4eTB">他认识到一一个人在完成任何任务时都需要更多的耐心。<br>
  4. As long as you have a dream, just go for it, and never give it up easily. Your</p>
 <p data-pid="hrO_2r4R">dream might not come true in one or two days, but it will someday.</p>
 <p data-pid="YeXd4xSD">只要你有梦想,就去追求,不要轻易放弃。你的梦想可能不会在一两天内实现,但总有一天会实现的。</p>
 <p data-pid="HP2hXKw0">人生哲理<br>
  1. Constant dropping wears the stone. 滴水穿石。</p>
 <p data-pid="n67qzVdX">2.Success is anything but coincidence.成功绝非偶然<br>
  3. From small beginnings come great things.伟大始于渺小。<br>
  4. Never judge people by their appearance.永远不要以貌取人。<br>

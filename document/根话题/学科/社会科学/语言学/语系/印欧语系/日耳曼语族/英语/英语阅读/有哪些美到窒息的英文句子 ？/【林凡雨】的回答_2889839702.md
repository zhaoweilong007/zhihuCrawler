# 有哪些美到窒息的英文句子 ？
- 点赞数：7695
- 更新时间：2023年02月12日18时46分18秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="Ir3LKw95">1、</p>
 <p data-pid="97Ufr9TI"><b>For you, a thousand times over.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Pjh8zM2J">为你，千千万万遍。</p>
 <p data-pid="chd7VbkQ">——《<span class="nolink">追风筝的人</span>》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="J1XOeJ4Y">2、</p>
 <p data-pid="D-xFENxx">Love is a touch and not yet a touch.</p>
 <p data-pid="3B4GElOU">爱是手未碰，心已动。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XJk7HSg0">3、</p>
 <p data-pid="zvxZE8dL">We are all in the gutter, but some of us are looking at the stars.</p>
 <p data-pid="VEoLrJHw">我们都在阴沟里，但仍有人仰望星空。</p>
 <p data-pid="RuolazhO">——王尔德</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IAls7qoR">4、</p>
 <p data-pid="twmK_aOf">Love is the one thing that transcends time and space.</p>
 <p data-pid="TaS8XhPg">爱是唯一可以超越时间与空间的事物。</p>
 <p data-pid="8ThHevBD">——《星际穿越》</p>

 <p data-pid="z7AJ0-0_">5、</p>
 <p data-pid="jWUQhE9p">One is always on a strange road, watching strange scenery and listening to strange music.</p>
 <p data-pid="BlZjzkO9">Then one day, you will find that the things you try hard to forget are already gone.</p>
 <p data-pid="P3gWXZv4">一个人总要走陌生的路，看陌生的风景，听陌生的歌，然后在某个不经意的瞬间，你会发现，原本是费尽心机想要忘记的事情真的就那么忘记了。</p>
 <p data-pid="cELQX18c">——《蓝莓之夜》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wz0JAyTx">6、</p>
 <p data-pid="XbAzLChx">Get busy living or get busy dying.</p>
 <p data-pid="1DxFAUaL">要么忙于活着，要么忙于死去。</p>
 <p data-pid="asEkOjom">——《肖申克的救赎》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HJQqnE2a">7、</p>
 <p data-pid="C21KVKX_">Good morning, and in case I don’t see you, good afternoon, good evening, and good night!</p>
 <p data-pid="845SArdc">早上好，以防我看不见你，所以下午好，晚上好，晚安！</p>
 <p data-pid="aj-vCq0b">——《楚门的世界》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OkxZnMmz">8、</p>
 <p data-pid="N776qKFO"><b>The problems of your past are your business. The problems of your future are my privilege.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rD8fwu4O">你的过去我不愿过问，那是你的事情你的未来我希望参与，这是我的荣幸。</p>
 <p data-pid="abYYReFW">——约翰·华生</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="9gw45KUi">
  <b>❤️喜欢这些句子的话，双击屏幕点个赞留个足迹再走吧~别让这些句子沉没~</b>
 </blockquote>

 <p data-pid="F-uKMlYs">9、</p>
 <p data-pid="j1UQi5lM">Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift.</p>
 <p data-pid="QFvHrbdL">That is why it is called present.</p>
 <p data-pid="eua0nWr5">昨天是历史，明天是未知，只有今天才是天赐的礼物。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WWXJjWsa">10、</p>
 <p data-pid="Xe49jfAU">You spend your whole life looking for answers, because you think the next answer would change something. Maybe make you a little less miserable.</p>
 <p data-pid="GgBfbeOi">You make millions of decisions that mean nothing and then one day your order takes out and it changes your life.</p>
 <p data-pid="-0sSp5jT">你每天都在做很多看起来毫无意义的决定，但某天你的某个决定就能改变你的一生。</p>
 <p data-pid="cV0GnSle">——《西雅图夜未眠》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-P8pv8Hv">11、</p>
 <p data-pid="WyygT4Oa">It's better to burn out than to fade away.</p>
 <p data-pid="9xQbqU02">与其苟延残喘，不如纵情燃烧。</p>
 <p data-pid="_B8pHjtG">——《科特·柯本遗书》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oyjtDzkJ">12、</p>
 <p data-pid="MygzozFN"><b>I love you not because of who you are, but because of who I am when I am with you.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-1n5F9Rb">我爱你，不是因为你是一个怎样的人，而是因为我喜欢与你在一起时的感觉。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="RlJ06MNX">13、</p>
 <p data-pid="lUg6oYYS">The hot summer was too short, every second deserves to be loved.</p>
 <p data-pid="q6APCQl8">热烈的夏天太短，每一秒都值得被爱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="p4C1Hnqs">14、</p>
 <p data-pid="VD9yIk0k"><b>I am not afraid of tomorrow for I have seen yesterday and love today.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="nGYVNNq-">我不害怕明天，因为我经历过昨天，又热爱今天。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WUoFzovY">15、</p>
 <p data-pid="Ac7LUa8-">Raise your words, not your voice. It's rain that makes the flowers grow, not thunder.</p>
 <p data-pid="yY3PW3iQ">话要说得更有道理，而不是更高音量。毕竟是雨水滋润花朵生长，而不是雷鸣。</p>
 <p data-pid="Z7R6kdCd">——《养家之人》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="M46e-ywG">16、</p>
 <p data-pid="AUM-mjzb">Be alayman, be a vagrant life, clean and free.</p>
 <p data-pid="ZMbo0BYl">做个俗人，浪荡一生，干净自由。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="SgzsPwBz">
  <b>❤️感谢屏幕前帮我点赞的小哥哥小姐姐们，祝你也能过上自由的一生。</b>
 </blockquote>

 <p data-pid="Vx3Ap2FC">17、</p>
 <p data-pid="0akv09Oy">I wish you to know that you have been the last dream of my soul.</p>
 <p data-pid="0FwtZCTX">我希望你知道，你一直都是我心灵深处的最后一个梦。</p>
 <p data-pid="xs38hYya">——《双城记》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ePA731_2">18、</p>
 <p data-pid="LfYZfikW">The worst way to miss someone is to be sitting right beside them knowing you can’t have them.</p>
 <p data-pid="wiXx4yGp">失去某人，最糟糕的莫过于，他近在身旁，却犹如远在天边。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_jhLlHly">19、</p>
 <p data-pid="kryRsIX8"><b>May there be enough clouds in your life to make a beautiful sunset.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dPgs7-Z4">愿你的生命中有足够的云翳，来造成一个美丽的黄昏。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H2N76PY5">20、</p>
 <p data-pid="fzPCUJSt">Better to light one candle than to curse the darkness.</p>
 <p data-pid="nK2fLy5M">与其诅咒黑暗，不如燃起蜡烛。</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="PE3fk_zp">21、</p>
 <p data-pid="Mef07uh2">Shall I compare thee to a summer's day?</p>
 <p data-pid="WyfeMKFb">Thou art more lovely and more temperate.</p>
 <p data-pid="WIiwMphU">或许我可用夏日把你来比方，</p>
 <p data-pid="xK6nwXtJ">但你比夏日更可爱也更温良。</p>
 <p data-pid="pZYve8vp">——「十四行诗」</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tJW6_Vxu">22、</p>
 <p data-pid="jeUDaT0z">And when wind and winter harden. All the loveless land,It will whisper of the garden,You will understand.</p>
 <p data-pid="SA5HRjN1">当北风与冬天让一切凝固，一切变成爱的荒原，它就会低诉花园的絮语，你就会恍然大悟。</p>
 <p data-pid="9vPLaGG4">——「致妻子：题我的一本诗集」</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hbjFzkLG">23、</p>
 <p data-pid="wH8ITSSb">The sunshine, having no alternative, on the nothing new.</p>
 <p data-pid="aQDXB0Ys">太阳照常升起，一切都没有改变。</p>
 <p data-pid="VavxSue-">——『墨菲』</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ym_fYDg2">24、</p>
 <p data-pid="Ug8t_-rh"><b>Love all, trust a few, do wrong to none.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="1o8jklr1">爱所有人，信任少许人，勿伤任何人。</p>
 <p data-pid="U-d5RDqo">——『皆大欢喜』</p>

 <p data-pid="bFUDmMYs">25、</p>
 <p data-pid="FguKQCf2">I love you once. I love you twice. I love you more than beans and rice.</p>
 <p data-pid="y00raCe4">一见倾心，再见倾情。我爱你，此生不渝。</p>
 <p data-pid="GycrF9k9">——「绝望主妇」</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="t5Bu3IuU">26、</p>
 <p data-pid="BZJ5QWC-">In carnage, I bloomlike a flower in the dawn.</p>
 <p data-pid="QbgDCVqG">我于杀戮之中绽放，亦如黎明中的花朵。</p>
 <p data-pid="V4Nw0q9t">——英雄联盟-烬</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LyjeOZAf">27、</p>
 <p data-pid="K5dgjdl6"><b>Let life be beautiful like summer flowers and death like autumn leaves.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3Ge7BCkI">生如夏花之灿烂，死如秋叶之静美。</p>

 <p data-pid="I-31jRNj">28、</p>
 <p data-pid="OmnIV_Va">Swaying dance in nib, is the strongest touch of red spotlight.</p>
 <p data-pid="yXY9k-Wv">摇曳在笔尖的舞姿、是聚光灯下最浓烈的一抹艳红。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LQUf1IGz">29、</p>
 <p data-pid="LUrQSV8p">Look into my eyes，you will see what you mean to me.</p>
 <p data-pid="IG3ZQbis">看看我的眼睛，你会发现你对我而言意味着什么。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Fe3tGk4G">30、</p>
 <p data-pid="EF1BvWK6"><b>I want to say good night to the world, you happen to be the world. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="G7nOwk0j">想对全世界说晚安，恰好你就是全世界。</p>

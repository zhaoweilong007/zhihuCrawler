# 有哪些美到窒息的英文句子 ？
- 点赞数：6244

<body>
 <p data-pid="BfGgau_2">1.</p>
 <p data-pid="hg94WE85"><b>If I should meet thee After long years.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="WReEeqAQ"><b>How should I greet thee? </b></p>
 <p data-pid="NRtPvP2u"><b>With silence and tears.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="nJf-fco-"><b><i>若我再见到你，事隔经年，我该如何贺你？</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="eaVeb21u"><b><i>以沉默，以眼泪。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="jPVmdxD5"><b><i>——拜伦</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="ok-8iCoh">2.</p>
 <p data-pid="iNedHYRb">Some of us get dipped in flat, some in satin, some in gloss.</p>
 <p data-pid="72qV3Qe0">But every once in a while you find someone who's iridescent, and when you do, nothing will ever compare.</p>
 <p data-pid="S-PAeg9L"><i>有人住高楼，有人处深沟，有人光万丈，有人一身锈，世人万千种，浮云莫去求，斯人若彩虹，遇上方知有。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="nKnu8-fJ"><i>——《怦然心动》</i></p>
 <p data-pid="86EFFxzN">3.</p>
 <p data-pid="ihZ_a3dY">We are all in the gutter, but some of us are looking at the stars.</p>
 <p data-pid="iS7XM0Cc"><b><i>我们都在阴沟里，但仍有人仰望星空。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="XMnJHVcU"><b><i>——王尔德</i></b></p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="BwOV8K1i">4.</p>
 <p data-pid="xcKrgdQ7"><b>If I could,I surely would.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="of0_sxvl"><b><i>如果可以，我绝对愿意！</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="g7VF2JmE">5.</p>
 <p data-pid="xehL5Lab">May there be enough clouds in your life to make a beautiful sunset.</p>
 <p data-pid="9fc3_8FU"><i>愿你的生命中有足够的云翳，来造成一个美丽的黄昏。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="1wJwScML">6.</p>
 <p data-pid="1Zpn8K-4">You don’t love a girl because she is beautiful, but she is beautiful because you love her!</p>
 <p data-pid="uCRSE3L-"><i>你不是因为她的美丽而爱她，而她却因为你的爱而美丽。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="PHoXMS2P">7.</p>
 <p data-pid="X-xR6dM6">Being deeply loved by someone gives you strength, loving someone deeply gives you courage.</p>
 <p data-pid="Ejys2mjJ"><i>被爱给你力量，爱人给你勇气。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="3dHsxMBH">8.</p>
 <p data-pid="HJqyZF_n">I am not afraid of tomorrow for I have seen yesterday and love today.</p>
 <p data-pid="XiOeY_9l"><i>我不害怕明天，因为我经历过昨天，又热爱今天。</i></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-61821e39660caeb3269f9233ababce15_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1000" data-rawheight="1000" data-size="normal" data-original-token="v2-61821e39660caeb3269f9233ababce15" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6be106181fc19c501865d47fb2c3a539_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1000" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-61821e39660caeb3269f9233ababce15_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="tDVrpv19">9.</p>
 <p data-pid="UgvaU_bv"><b>If you love life, don't waste time, for time is what life is made up of.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="5NFTn3K9"><b><i>如果你热爱生活，就不要浪费时间，因为生活是由时间组成的。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="cr6K0YmX">10.</p>
 <p data-pid="VmVMUXWX">Life is like a box of chocolates, you never know what you are going to get.</p>
 <p data-pid="EYQJd2Hu"><i>生活就像一盒巧克力,你永远不知道下一个是什么.</i></p>
 <p data-pid="0OowKtQ1"><i>——《阿甘正传》</i></p>
 <p data-pid="lv2A6B25">11.</p>
 <p data-pid="v09n4UaR">When you feel like hope is gone,look inside you and search your soul.</p>
 <p data-pid="oHJz862P">you will fend a hero lies in you.dreams are hard to follow,but don’t let anyone steal your dream.</p>
 <p data-pid="NtsuKwjf"><i>当你感觉希望不在的时候，看一下自己的内心世界，搜索一下自己的灵魂，你会找到一个英雄在你心中。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="b_JRZjI2"><i>梦想可能很难实现，但是不要让任何人偷走你的梦想。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="-rFZAbbo">12.</p>
 <p data-pid="vbvFILXl">Follow your bliss and don't be afraid, and doors will open where you didn't know they were going to be.</p>
 <p data-pid="cK4woBPN"><i>追随你的幸福吧！不要害怕，幸福之门会在你意想不到的地方开启。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="WX7TzqL_">13.</p>
 <p data-pid="_af8Chk9">Maybe God wants us to meet a few wrong people before meeting the right one, so that when we finally meet the person, we will know how to be grateful.</p>
 <p data-pid="_Kq7tfTQ"><b><i>在遇到梦中人之前，上天也许会安排我们先遇到别的人;在我们终于遇见心仪的人时，便应当心存感激。</i></b></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3c30a8ee35b3f21a576ed4485d505a99_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1000" data-rawheight="1000" data-size="normal" data-original-token="v2-3c30a8ee35b3f21a576ed4485d505a99" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-9e0ae5a19041a2c1db0d05e7f2a42e9c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1000" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-3c30a8ee35b3f21a576ed4485d505a99_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Kv7xWPKo">14.</p>
 <p data-pid="BhsvBi2e"><b>I like the night. Without the dark, we'd never see the stars<i>.</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="2EqRQ99j"><b><i>我喜欢黑夜。如果没有黑暗，我们永远看不到繁星点点。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="Oh4rs69N"><b><i>——《暮光之城》</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="zc24w_od">15.</p>
 <p data-pid="O7driVNI">A bird is safe in its nest — but that is not what its wings are made for.</p>
 <p data-pid="pARCjHms"><i>小鸟在巢中最安全，但翅膀可不是为此而生的。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="zmXgVN9H"><i>——阿米特·雷</i></p>
 <p data-pid="rzoyZXX-">16.</p>
 <p data-pid="g8yTLbVm">Sometimes accompanied sometimes alone, stay awesome all the time.</p>
 <p data-pid="vjRw8lFu"><i>聚散终有时，潇洒走一回。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="GtktWxqN"><i>——《纽约纽约》</i></p>
 <p data-pid="4SadGw-g"><i>17.</i></p>
 <p data-pid="v-4_6LV8">Laugh loudly, laugh often, and most important, laugh at yourself.</p>
 <p data-pid="CiPevCbO"><i>要放声笑，经常笑，最重要的是为自己而欢笑。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="hu7Fn2ze"><i>——Chelsea Handler</i></p>
 <p data-pid="G34wB3mx">18.</p>
 <p data-pid="gaHRfdQY">You can't stay in your corner of the Forest waiting for others to come to you. You have to go to them sometimes.</p>
 <p data-pid="T3Lm2508"><b><i>你不能在森林的角落里等待他人来找你，有时候你要主动去找别人。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="4JEBp3rq"><b><i>—— 《小熊维尼》</i></b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-05e340a2a20e87a3994f20e30ffccdb7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1000" data-rawheight="998" data-size="normal" data-original-token="v2-05e340a2a20e87a3994f20e30ffccdb7" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6ed173ac74e2578e6376200d871211c1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1000" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-05e340a2a20e87a3994f20e30ffccdb7_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="JyFO6VDW">19.</p>
 <p data-pid="spFzUxZv"><b>When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rJ5vmCz3"><b><i>当你心有所求，全宇宙都会去帮你得到它。</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="81npsErp"><b><i>—— 《牧羊少年奇幻之旅》</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="8MTOFKFh">20.</p>
 <p data-pid="ov4r__Qp">The longest way must have its close; the gloomiest night will wear on to a morning.</p>
 <p data-pid="A9kX9eD8"><i>最长的路也有尽头，最黑暗的夜晚也会迎接清晨。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="mFT9LjOg"><i>——《汤姆叔叔的小屋》</i></p>
 <p data-pid="ZPaohRuC">21.</p>
 <p data-pid="xU_NWRoR">Better to shine than to complain about all the darkness</p>
 <p data-pid="XCTP_Uq8"><i>让自己发光，胜过埋怨一切黑暗。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="OXLkMVUZ"><i>22.</i></p>
 <p data-pid="PUqYQdZ5">The hot summer was too short Every second deserves to be loved.</p>
 <p data-pid="bMJwMxpf"><i>热烈的夏天太短，每一秒都值得被爱。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="V85vou-j">23.</p>
 <p data-pid="PskIyBD7">some people can only accompany you to see the flowers ，some people can make the flowers bloom only for you</p>
 <p data-pid="9Hetw6En"><i>有的人只能陪你看花，有的人能让花只为你开。</i></p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="hSb_DIsx">24.</p>
 <p data-pid="ewplZ5Uv"><b><i>you can't stop yourself from being vulgar ,but you can't be romantic</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="B0X2wJRO"><b><i>落俗无可避免，浪漫至死不渝</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="YVQmkLf8">25.</p>
 <p data-pid="0bgFm46g">something to do .someone to love.something to look forward to .</p>
 <p data-pid="P27BtId-"><i>有事做，有人爱，有所期待。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="-DrYvkur">26.</p>
 <p data-pid="6q41gRjh">Thank you get close to me get to know me warm me leave me at last</p>
 <p data-pid="DAw7grEf"><i>谢谢你，靠近我，了解我，温暖我，最后离开我。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="G6ahY1K_">27.</p>
 <p data-pid="ayggbObS">One day ,we don't have to say goodbye just say good night.</p>
 <p data-pid="VaJeo70X"><i>总有一天，我们不用再说再见，只需道句晚安。</i></p>
 <p data-pid="_hy29h0i">28.</p>
 <p data-pid="Dkk1Xv87">I was born with an ordinary life but contracted a disease that I didn't want to deal with.</p>
 <p data-pid="uk-xtHyx"><i>生来便是平凡的命，却染上不愿将就的病。</i></p>

 <p data-pid="_yZIIxBJ">29.</p>
 <p data-pid="LWskMj1j">you may not be brilliant but you are always warm and bright</p>
 <p data-pid="05izdTLb">你未必光芒万丈，但始终温暖而明亮。</p>
 <p data-pid="S18SJZ8Q">30.</p>
 <p data-pid="m9aUzAHM"><b>Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift. That is why it is called present. </b></p>
 <p data-pid="qUpX_phv"><b><i>昨天是历史，明天是未知，只有今天才是天赐的礼物。</i></b></p>

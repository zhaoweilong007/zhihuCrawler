# 有哪些别具一格的英文句子，适合做个性签名？
- 点赞数：7899
- 更新时间：2019年12月10日11时19分02秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/37464341/answer/210924368
<body>
 <p data-pid="6wcLIQnE">1.If you reveal your secrets to the wind you <br>
  should not blame the wind revealing them to the trees.</p>
 <p data-pid="K3uGpaD3">要是你把你的秘密告诉了风，那就别怪风把秘密带给树。 -----纪伯伦</p>
 <p data-pid="aryUrRV3">2.I love three things in the world: the sun, the moon and you. The sun for day, the moon for night and you forever.</p>
 <p data-pid="Ue5w1jGI">吾独爱世间三物: 昼之日，夜之月，汝之永恒。 -----《暮光之城》</p>
 <p data-pid="gVBcgSYi">3.Had i not seen the sun, i could have borne the shade.<br>
  我本可以忍受黑暗，如果我不曾见过太阳。<br>
   -----艾米丽 ·狄金森</p>
 <p data-pid="-Hiu8v_G"><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//4.In" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://</span><span class="visible">4.In</span><span class="invisible"></span></a> me the tiger sniffs the rose.</p>
 <p data-pid="DrouGEAV">心有猛虎，细嗅蔷薇。<br>
   -----西格里夫·萨松</p>
 <p data-pid="S_Qs9krw">5.We laughed and kept saying "see u soon",<br>
  but inside we both knew we'd never see each other again.</p>
 <p data-pid="QaoOWOCC">我们笑着说再见，却深知再见遥遥无期。<br>
   -----《海上钢琴师》</p>
 <p data-pid="8bvOfJlJ">6.We are all in the gutter, but some of us are looking at the stars.</p>
 <p data-pid="CVjzOv6J">我们都生活在阴沟里，但仍有人仰望星空。<br>
   -----奥斯卡·王尔德</p>
 <p data-pid="NqwH6ZYl">7.My apologise to an old lover for treating<br>
  the new one as first.</p>
 <p data-pid="AyFu9HaP">我要向旧日的恋人道歉，因为我对新人如初恋。 -----维斯瓦娃.辛波斯卡</p>
 <p data-pid="rq4-RfV5">8.It does not do well to depend on dreams and forget to live,remember that.</p>
 <p data-pid="a_bTlQkV">记住不要依赖梦想而忘记生活。<br>
   -----J.K罗琳</p>
 <p data-pid="0H5y5Wax">9.Sometimes it's hard to accept the truth because the lies sound so much better.</p>
 <p data-pid="d7hgSC0X">真相有时之所以难以被接受，是因为谎言听起来美好多了。<br>
   -----《达·芬奇》密码</p>
 <p data-pid="e1RM-fr9"><br>
  10.Life is like a box of chocolates, you never know what you are going to get.</p>
 <p data-pid="daFgaelL">生活就像一盒巧克力，你永远不知道下一个是什么。-----《阿甘正传》</p>
 <p data-pid="7KDiPWLy">11.The longest day has an end.</p>
 <p data-pid="c3Ld2qCH">最难过的日子也有尽头。<br>
  -----贺韦尔</p>
 <p data-pid="DXcVh__D">12.To be or not to be, that is the question.</p>
 <p data-pid="joYjA2_a">活着还是死去，这是一个问题。<br>
  -----《哈姆雷特》</p>
 <p data-pid="UKhLcjLz">13.I can't control their fear, only my own.</p>
 <p data-pid="og3QTeis">我不能掌控他人的恐惧，只能掌控自己的恐惧。<br>
  -----《美国队长:内战》</p>
 <p data-pid="DFK_C4Li">14.Sometimes accompanied ,sometimes alone, stay awesome all the time.</p>
 <p data-pid="54w_xQjc">聚散终有时，潇洒走一回。<br>
  -----《纽约纽约》</p>
 <p data-pid="JipqKFcH">15.There are no easy answers , there's only living through the questions.</p>
 <p data-pid="0dSQ6FM7">生活从来没有容易的答案，只有去克服重重问题。<br>
  -----伊丽莎白·乔治</p>
 <p data-pid="t1oWH235">16.I would die for you .But i would't live for you.</p>
 <p data-pid="7b4WnZnF">我愿意为你赴死，但不会指望你而活。<br>
  -----斯蒂芬·切波斯基</p>

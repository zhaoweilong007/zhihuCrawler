 <p data-pid="o9fVSFDd">Part 1高级句式</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aORcxKfT">下面的几个句型都是能让阅卷老师眼前一亮的闪光点，考试的时候一用，真的能亮坏老师的眼睛。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-2m-fL7v">1.1句间插入非限定性定语从句</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ShKlTPV7">Lily，who's a humorous but rational girl，won the first prize in the speech contest.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rta7M3pm">这种句式很适合开头介绍自己或者某件事，当事件的性质比较多的时候，可以在句子中间插入一些信息。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mArtR_Zn">1.2倒装句</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_6g8ruBt">①否定词开头放于句首，主谓倒装</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bjegX8PF">Never will I go to school unless the shower passes.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pn1qvfuV">高分作文一定少不了倒装句，安排上!!!</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="y5TTonbv">②地点状语放在句首，主谓倒装</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WgPg1eLw">In front of the teaching building stands a tree.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="J0CWpfpu">啦啦啦，在写某个活动的地点时，再也不用写成小学生作文啦</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yeOgqzpd">③only后面接状语或状语从句</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pXIYYFjp">Only in Linda.Lee's way ，can you improve your English scores.</p>
 <p data-pid="lQRmFNzs">(233333手动狗头）</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="w4QHH1ZQ">给老外介绍学习汉语的方法的时候可以用这句</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="39LtZuaP">④形容词/副词+as+主语+be动词</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yxDW2zKP">Young as she is ，she has commanded of 7 languages.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="K0fwgU2E">别再写＂虽然，但是＂了，上面的句式它不香吗？</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gOb7syA0">⑤Not only倒装</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6KvJCvST">Not only do the nurses want a pay increase，they also want holidays</p>
 <p data-pid="NAD63Zsm">高逼格，既显示出强大的逻辑，又用到了倒装，分数up up up</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3HwCwER0">1.3强调句</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9g8JTwiE">It is the ceremony that falls on 1st October</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HwJIEbOS">非常适用于强调时间、地点。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ljbiSaad">1.4被动句</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lTtmNyut">在汉语习惯中，我们习惯于用主动语态去描述一件事情。但是英语更常用被动语态，在英语作文中用被动语态就能显得更地道。</p>
 <p data-pid="8JX6ASFm">Human beings have polluted the sky seriously.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Ye-8OSM3">The sky has been polluted seriously by human beings.</p>
 <p data-pid="L5A7BnWV">对比一下，你觉得谁更好呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="RPR80qOt">1.5while＋v-ing形式</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TTQ3bb7z">You'd better avoid talking to others while chewing the food.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="u3ZLoK7I">1.6形容词、过去分词作状语</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Lfbzho2z">Guilty and embarrassed，I sincerely apologize to you.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LNxVEqSn">英语老师总说你句子简单:I am guilty and embarrassed and I sincerely apologize to you.</p>
 <p data-pid="USn4a9hk">这次你双击屏幕!!!记下这个句式后!!!你终于可以怼回去了。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hbz3MIRi">1.7独立主格与分词结构</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pcF8jGyD">If you are willing to take me into consideration，换成Your being willing to take me into consideration</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HcwcXJhX">（当时年级上还没有人用过独立主格，我一用就被老师当成范文传阅）</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WV8Q9FUL">If weather permits换成Weather permitting</p>
 <p data-pid="Qr3GD45J">这样文章逼格就大大提升，每次文章结尾都来个点睛之笔，让老师jio得你还真的有两把刷子。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XD-nQFer">1.8虚拟语气</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gH38WNhd">＂虚拟+倒装 ＂</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="xE2ACfEG">Had it not been for your unreserved help ，my passion for English wouldn't have been kindled and my progress wouldn't have been so fast.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="31FOymQI">非常适用于感谢信:</p>
 <p data-pid="_GrAKAWe">Had it not been for your……（如果没有你的……），I would not have done……（我就不会……）</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uZ2whsWW">比直接用without高级多了</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Lf_Tw_R9">1.9双重否定</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3b3NXAA8">把肯定语气换成双重否定</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dETghnZJ">the postman comes on time everyday换成the postman never fails to come on time everyday.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZR7pu8GF">后者才是真地道。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2S1muaO2">温馨提醒:</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2I64Esp7">上述例句可以直接运用到作文中去，最重要的是学会用这几个句式，这样下次写文章就不怕了。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6KwYVY6H">怎么学会用呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LhWNsVTM">嘿嘿，授人以鱼不如授人以渔。学姐当然会教你怎么用啊。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lXVJ6JA6">双击屏幕，继续往下看。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="C8DhVvVH">Part 2 自我尝试</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3klHCMNh">你需要准备:</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9uBLxzsf">①高中以来所有写过的英语作文</p>
 <p data-pid="XPshpnBv">（早就扔了的现在后悔了吧?那些都是你提分的宝贵的资源!!!）</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="NVY9KDei">②一支蓝笔</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Fh-cUl16">show time:</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="KviaIpAY">改!改!改!</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WwLVptEn">没错，把以前写的那些初级表达，一句一句地改。</p>
 <p data-pid="37cwduVL">怎么改?</p>
 <p data-pid="w3_4Da11">根据句子的内容选定合适的高级句式。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9gqC0V_x">举个栗子 :</p>
 <p data-pid="b6asQTAd">你以前写的是I have good news to tell you that our school will hold an activity called Know China for foreigners this Friday.</p>
 <p data-pid="LF3ErvON">改!改!改!</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DJvJDqiL">前面提到:当描述一件事的性质比较多时，可以把一些信息插在中间。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nHNJ1iHF">I have good news to share with you that organized by our school，Know China，is to be held this Friday，specially designed for foreigners.</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oPlz2BmG">怎么样？是不是瞬间就变得更牛逼了</p>
 <p data-pid="drVDNTTR">改了过后的句子把主办方插进了句子中间，当然，还用到了一个短语be designed for。</p>
 <p data-pid="VSDX_4M8">一句话就让简单初级的句子瞬间提高级别，阅卷老师一读就知道你不容小觑</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bpiOO_Wc">就这样把自己的作文根据内容选定合适的高级句式，一句一句地改，过不了多久，你也可以让老师眼前一亮。</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mXesZqnd">Part 3鱼来了</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8BGdS3g0">＂渔＂和＂鱼＂都得有!!!</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-vSMjObJ">下面是我熬夜整理出来的压箱底的笔记。</p>
 <p data-pid="9ELeCboq">双击屏幕，妈妈再也不用担心你写不出英语作文啦</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7C_MsySq">1.1可直接套用的模板</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tz3Y7egu">我认为:</p>
 <p data-pid="s7ryf1a8">as far as I'm concerned</p>
 <p data-pid="5ya8INGJ">be in favor of the view that</p>
 <p data-pid="HwgsDuDh">I am convinced that</p>
 <p data-pid="S0w_NoMr">I am not alone in maintaining that</p>
 <p data-pid="UtcP8Vki">It appears to me that</p>
 <p data-pid="MMjJM-As">from my perspective</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PBc3BPD-">开头客套话:</p>
 <p data-pid="1j_E9eHM">It is such a great honour to be given an opportunity to respond to your email which says……</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="q2jEFSL3">I am delighted to receive your letter in which you ask me about……</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pUxELC1m">How are you getting along these days?</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HZKLsG8F">I'm writing to inform you of that</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-GfBTDFh">I will never fail up to your expectations</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kz225KeE">Having learned that you are interested in Chinese traditional culture，I sincerely invite you to...</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lwr_Qm5n">结尾表示祝福:</p>
 <p data-pid="ViIPgzJG">May you blossom into what you want to be and remember to keep in touch with me regularly</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Rdz03Nie">As it has been mentioned about上面说了</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="__eyvQ2P">I shall be very grateful if you offer me this precious opportunity</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kQ0XwYJ1">may sb/sth +动词原形+其它</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="iLXcXtDp">Time permitting ，I'd like to</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="f3pZDSIm">不可否认:</p>
 <p data-pid="yipGoB94">It is indisputable that……</p>
 <p data-pid="ZBtq-Lum">It can be said with certainty that</p>
 <p data-pid="O63Idm5t">There is no doubt／denying that</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DAv5mV8W">表示许多:</p>
 <p data-pid="M_CWOAXd">a variety of</p>
 <p data-pid="63uYEDWQ">a sequence of</p>
 <p data-pid="37fMfupN">a diversity of</p>
 <p data-pid="25JhDtab">a collection of</p>
 <p data-pid="19r5Rc8k">quantities of</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8U58KxkI">重要性:</p>
 <p data-pid="tGbZGFPj">play a significant role in...</p>
 <p data-pid="DQiZShAJ">nothing is more important than the fact that</p>
 <p data-pid="VqVqrex7">put emphasis on</p>
 <p data-pid="D4MEwNl9">attach significance to do sth</p>
 <p data-pid="rHbsdfQ_">因为:</p>
 <p data-pid="GAzMVqN8">owing to</p>
 <p data-pid="PMnY9RV_">on account of</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hVQUovrQ">对……有兴趣:</p>
 <p data-pid="FQGETuQj">attach interest to</p>
 <p data-pid="o93FQp_0">A introduce me to my passion for B</p>
 <p data-pid="5JKAjKoj">engage sb's attention/interest</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="sIy3Vd53">与...有联系:</p>
 <p data-pid="GJFQveO0">be closely related to...</p>
 <p data-pid="iMZQclrO">be associated with</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jJP27eRd">求职信:</p>
 <p data-pid="MoeoAGo5">I have abundant qualifications related to this job</p>
 <p data-pid="mKvYHKWm" class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

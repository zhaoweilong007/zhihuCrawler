 <p data-pid="KRV4yHAX">-----------------------------------正文的分割线------------------------------------------</p>
 <p data-pid="Bf4_TqW-">题主举例用 Got it. 代替 I see , 觉得B格高，其实用 I see. 会更加优雅有范儿一些呢。在我的理解来看，题主是想追求更加简练精准和native speaker，让人听起来像是几个ABC在喝酒聊天，而不是李雷和韩梅梅经典对话How are you。。。</p>
 <p data-pid="8wl7_M8m">作为一个看了太多美剧的妹子，经常在电影院没人看出笑点的时候笑出声来，写一下我知道的比较地道的口语。优点是和老美聊天很顺畅，缺点是习惯了这套非常随意的用法之后，再想正式规范的表达，很多时候需要现想词的，就有点反应迟钝不流畅了。女王陛下高冷的说，哪有什么美语？只有英式英语和mistake！╮(╯▽╰)╭</p>
 <p data-pid="ito-O8aA"><b>一，军用口语演变过来的，在表示行动和决策上，显得很精干</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MHkCP68x">1. Clear. / All clear. （检查完）安全了，没事了。事情都搞定了</p>
 <p data-pid="dKY277IX">2. Go ahead，相当于Yes，please。好的你说吧/你就这么做吧</p>
 <p data-pid="f7jSbStv">3. Watch my back。Cover me。掩护我，哥们我要上了</p>
 <p data-pid="5izqre54">4. I got your back，我给你殿后，我会支持你</p>
 <p data-pid="14x_DzL5">5. Roger. / Roger that. / Copy that. 表示收到信息/指令，会执行的</p>
 <p data-pid="kL7Rv86c">6. Abort！Abort！行动中止，别干了赶紧撤</p>
 <p data-pid="07Jmgv0O">7. false alert，假警报，虚惊一场</p>
 <p data-pid="tytRfvZy">8. Just follow my lead. 按我说的办，听我指挥</p>
 <p data-pid="Xk2LoOWB">冰河世纪3：</p>
 <p data-pid="-f1XersT">Eddie：Look！He's right there！（看，他在那呢！）</p>
 <p data-pid="I0cH-OBp">Buck：Roger.(晓得）</p>
 <p data-pid="GsWpciYk">Eddie：No，Sid（不是，是希德）</p>
 <p data-pid="X6domZQD">Buck：I know，Roger！（我知道啦！晓得！）</p>
 <p data-pid="-sBbLgie">Crash：How about we get Sid first，and then go back for Roger？（我们可不可以先救希德，再去救“小德”？）</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="qztcQkbV"><b>二、出神入化的介词。很多说法用简单一个介词就能表达出来了，非常简洁到位</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-6Sa5XQh">1. in：上场，流行</p>
 <p data-pid="kKewPpHz">I'm in. （你玩不玩啊？）带上我，这个活儿我接了</p>
 <p data-pid="OvTNO6dD">2. out</p>
 <p data-pid="8u0_RfFC">pass out，晕过去了</p>
 <p data-pid="N-vWL8zV">Out！滚出去</p>
 <p data-pid="S6B1ypPb">ask/take her out（for a date/dinner）专指约会</p>
 <p data-pid="zf-Rc33q">go out with him，约会</p>
 <p data-pid="BJ0JUYZc">She is out of your league.你配不上她，女神追不着的</p>
 <p data-pid="tqozYC2j">inside out，衣服穿反了</p>
 <p data-pid="0V0Lwr9c">freak out，发飙，吓坏了</p>
 <p data-pid="mT1ytqP8">You're out of your mind，你疯了吧</p>
 <p data-pid="sUW5Hi7a">3. on</p>
 <p data-pid="uG0DdWPS">表示继续 move on:（忘了失恋）继续生活吧</p>
 <p data-pid="YdCQOXDD">Dream on！做你的梦去吧</p>
 <p data-pid="BgH-y7uO">Dinner is on me. 晚饭我请客</p>
 <p data-pid="28RMunXo">turn me on，让我兴奋（某些方面）</p>
 <p data-pid="Quxw-gbj">on the house，请客</p>
 <p data-pid="jXZQxlge">4. off</p>
 <p data-pid="SLS7Z2Ut">take a day off，休一天假</p>
 <p data-pid="zsgO9-vU">Fxxk off！滚远点</p>
 <p data-pid="oKq6sz2i">Knock it off！少来了</p>
 <p data-pid="uVRyhxG5">I have to take off，我得走了</p>
 <p data-pid="SdqRGpRt">piss me off，让我很生气 （I'm angry哈哈哈）</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="Ias5XMyP">5. into</p>
 <p data-pid="n9_jN3pX">He's just not that into you.他就是没那么喜欢你</p>
 <p data-pid="kYr_XzuR">run into somebody，在大街上碰到谁</p>
 <p data-pid="pNJDgv1x">6.up：</p>
 <p data-pid="R-UVxmlh">Bottoms up! 干杯</p>
 <p data-pid="RlnZYEmP">man up，雄起，给力点</p>
 <p data-pid="09juyByV">Suit up！老爸老妈浪漫史里的Barney最喜欢说的一句话，把西装穿起来！</p>
 <p data-pid="QWhg-D-L">something just came up，突然冒出来点事情（打乱计划）</p>
 <p data-pid="RuWyWGva">back me up, 支持我，支援我</p>
 <p data-pid="C_GIqsx8">What’s up？怎么了？有什么事么</p>
 <p data-pid="h5Jdj1Ly">7. down: 倒下，堕落</p>
 <p data-pid="LX8Rcoit">Don't let me down.别让我失望</p>
 <p data-pid="vXEvbapR">8. over:</p>
 <p data-pid="kxd-sXV9">get over somebody，失恋了试图忘记某人</p>
 <p data-pid="0EO6oxTz">9. around</p>
 <p data-pid="I5jTmQE1">fool around：招蜂引蝶</p>
 <p data-pid="zO5fD5hN">I'll show you around.我带你四处转转</p>
 <p data-pid="uV1EipdT">10. against somebody，反对谁，跟谁对着干</p>
 <p data-pid="C6s06F44">11.with</p>
 <p data-pid="C_KvhMZi">I'm with you.我同意你，我支持你</p>
 <p data-pid="WzLgdawD">I'll always be with you. 我会永远和你在一起</p>
 <p data-pid="w-CVjLZJ">What is it with that guy？那家伙是怎么回事啊</p>
 <p data-pid="qicBZLRa">How did it go with him？你和他交往的如何，进展顺利不</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="V0dRtN_7"><b>三、多用的动词。不得不说老美太懒啦，只要能表达清楚意思，怎么简单怎么来</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rOYCQwBl">1. do：</p>
 <p data-pid="YRWAcAlR">简直是万用动词，相当于广东话的“搞”，东北话的“整”，日语的します，用起来和不要钱一样啊！</p>
 <p data-pid="JWIDUh5r">Any day will do.哪一天都可以</p>
 <p data-pid="XWOBFH9E">do me a favor，帮我个忙</p>
 <p data-pid="4V4xE2s-">No one does me better than me，不解释，霸气的苏菲</p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/1a502d3b7027ddcfc4dc560524f6ff9f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="580" data-rawheight="326" data-original-token="1a502d3b7027ddcfc4dc560524f6ff9f" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="580" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/1a502d3b7027ddcfc4dc560524f6ff9f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="OjHDgxud">2. take</p>
 <p data-pid="zVSkAI3r">take it seriously，当回事</p>
 <p data-pid="tO7-3d5h">take it for granted，觉得理所当然</p>
 <p data-pid="ufs3FMkM">You take your time，你慢慢来</p>
 <p data-pid="Dh9aaQUQ">I'll take from here. 接下来交给我吧</p>
 <p data-pid="DanIp25z">take my word for it，相信我说的</p>
 <p data-pid="JQbOawWB">Let me take care of it. 我来处理</p>
 <p data-pid="GW9wr-eh">I can't take it anymore.我受不了了</p>
 <p data-pid="5oTATwGB">3. get</p>
 <p data-pid="0_UdrN2V">You got me. 你问倒我了，你抓住我了</p>
 <p data-pid="OxNqROgX">I got it. / Got it. 明白了，知道了</p>
 <p data-pid="WNNtvCt9">Don't get me wrong. 别误会我的意思</p>
 <p data-pid="A7jRPn6L">You got a minute？有时间吗？</p>
 <p data-pid="8qiQuEBb">get it done，把这个做完</p>
 <p data-pid="wSN8c9Iz">get lost，给我消失</p>
 <p data-pid="ISexXixV">get back at somebody，报复谁</p>
 <p data-pid="WglAzlQC">4. give</p>
 <p data-pid="dTv6-7h8">I don't give a damn/shit. 我TMD不在乎</p>
 <p data-pid="8jB6v2EY">Don't give me that.少跟我来这套</p>
 <p data-pid="MoqTpaCq">Don't give me that look！别跟我这幅表情</p>
 <p data-pid="4OGSbAsy">give it a shot/try，尝试一下</p>
 <p data-pid="aQdfgbJW">5. count</p>
 <p data-pid="Z_wkyXdu">Don't count on it. 别太指望这事</p>
 <p data-pid="vbrJWlnS">You can count on me.你可以依赖我</p>
 <p data-pid="wjNVOkH0">count me in（做什么事）算上我一个</p>
 <p data-pid="soBtKZUb">6. make</p>
 <p data-pid="V_PLKG8o">make up your mind，做决定</p>
 <p data-pid="HpxJ4Yju">Let's make a deal. 我们做个交易吧</p>
 <p data-pid="78sGzIyF">make a big deal out of it，当个大事，大惊小怪</p>
 <p data-pid="1BIpkPWy">make out，亲热（男女朋友那种）</p>
 <p data-pid="pEHkuA_N">Let me make it up to you. 让我好好补偿你吧</p>
 <p data-pid="6o0P1btk">7. I'm screwed.我惨了</p>
 <p data-pid="YS0IPTcr">Screw you！去你妹的</p>
 <p data-pid="ycvaHZj9">screw it up，搞砸</p>
 <p data-pid="pzshEFwg">8. work，行得通</p>
 <p data-pid="8vD-Z62R">That works for me. 对我来说可以的（接受这个条件）</p>
 <p data-pid="wSGqWQMM">9. hit</p>
 <p data-pid="yBn9ljPg">hit on somebody，勾引调戏PUA</p>
 <p data-pid="kpVJZ5hy">hit it off 相处得很好，合得来</p>
 <p data-pid="7QZUsmew">10. 系动词be</p>
 <p data-pid="t7gSpx5U">Be a man！Be nice！Be cool！</p>
 <p data-pid="UkxLBlfx">I'll be there for you.我会支持你的</p>
 <p data-pid="JlKrCAMB">I can't help it.情不自禁</p>
 <p data-pid="U2AUEXEs">I don't mean it.我不是故意的</p>
 <p data-pid="EIVaJZCC">I mean it.我认真的</p>
 <p data-pid="pqpmSkDe">I can't follow you.我听不明白你在说什么</p>
 <p data-pid="gCdm0Pex">I can't reach him.我联系不上他</p>
 <p data-pid="3IYf6Ig4">Don’t mess with me.别跟我瞎扯蛋</p>
 <p data-pid="omg4A-f_">call it even扯平了</p>
 <p data-pid="BVIN7uiH">beat/ scare the crap out of... 打出翔，吓出翔</p>
 <p data-pid="YtWdb-Ui">My boss is driving me nuts/crazy.老板要把我逼疯了，这里动词用drive才正宗</p>
 <p data-pid="wCIzksCx">let it go, 放手吧，别纠结了</p>
 <p data-pid="1FGHqHr6">You've gone too far！你太过分了</p>
 <p data-pid="Tl3aGtl_">Let me put it this way. 让我这么说吧</p>
 <p data-pid="R6gPncp7">cut the crap，少废话，别兜圈子</p>
 <p data-pid="wjk1wIfF">Cut it out！省省吧，少来这套</p>
 <p data-pid="GRiXEixz">You do the math. 你自己算吧</p>
 <p data-pid="6eckbBQC">something stinks/sucks！很糟糕，很差劲</p>
 <p data-pid="d2Zs1nHv">suck up to...巴结谁</p>
 <p data-pid="yFFKBU_E">I hear you./ I'm listening. 我听到你说什么了，我听着呢</p>
 <p data-pid="u2JIrU7e">（一般用于对方提条件，表示感兴趣，你继续说）</p>
 <p data-pid="WmyXRray">Now you're talking. 这说的才像话嘛！That's more like that。</p>
 <p data-pid="irb5mr7e">There you go.这就对了嘛</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="ScSCbd7b"><b>四，缩写类</b></p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="HR1TJ8wF">gonna = going to</p>
 <p data-pid="H2D0iEt3">wanna = want to</p>
 <p data-pid="1fR3b_Fu">gotta =get to，I gotta go我得走了</p>
 <p data-pid="K3KZuP2h">gotcha= got you，整到你啦！或者 明白你的意思了</p>
 <p data-pid="ARZDNa0R">kinda=kind of， a little bit，有一点儿</p>
 <p data-pid="j2yKgWd7">Gimme that！=give me that</p>
 <p data-pid="hY5QPaf4">yep=yes，nope=no</p>
 <p data-pid="cVkmGUkW">S'up= What's up？咋样啊</p>
 <p data-pid="q74TscP0">AKA：also known as，也被叫做。。。说外号的时候用</p>
 <p data-pid="myC-EFWX">lol=laugh out loud，大笑，这个打字的时候会用，很少用讲的，相当于23333吧</p>
 <p data-pid="wYYJ_OhZ">FYI= for your information，告诉你一声儿啊</p>
 <p data-pid="TXmmRgJf">BTW= by the way，顺便说一下。这都写邮件的时候用</p>
 <p data-pid="WmK_bVYX">ASAP = as soon as possible，尽快</p>
 <p data-pid="y5vvCkfu">RSVP = please reply，（请柬）请回复</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="U194cu3M"><b>五, 其他固定用法</b></p>
 <p data-pid="syyIkRw7">Whatever. 爱咋咋地，随你怎么说吧</p>
 <p data-pid="EyDpllU7">dead end 死胡同</p>
 <p data-pid="embnXGqC">Hello~~~~用九转千回的语调讲，表示 有没有搞错啊！</p>
 <p data-pid="pgI_bkNo">My way or the highway. 不听我的就滚蛋</p>
 <p data-pid="89ASzvyw">That's not the point.这不是关键问题所在。 point经常表示观点</p>
 <p data-pid="05zmzJJE">I see your point.我明白你的意思</p>
 <p data-pid="ntpMoL3s">I have no idea. I don't have a clue. 我不知道，比I don't know听起来好多了</p>
 <p data-pid="S9oCKLEx">have a crush on somebody，迷恋某人</p>
 <p data-pid="4X8JWUXB">go nuts 发疯</p>
 <p data-pid="ZqvYobPE">That was close/ a close one，刚才好险，就差一点</p>
 <p data-pid="PWd2vrNu">Nice save！漂亮的救场</p>
 <p data-pid="l8hjE7J2">Nice try！想得美哦</p>
 <p data-pid="jNZm-iPG"><b>六，涉及到色情或者低俗的敏感类，还有骂人话</b></p>
 <p data-pid="BEFIixXQ">有些词只要能听懂就好，没那么亲近或者不确定的场合尽量少用，万一被当成粗鲁岂不是B格尽失</p>
 <p data-pid="r0VaWYC9">have the ball / guts / bones，勇气，骨气，带种</p>
 <p data-pid="xXBYyaXI">hard core，中坚力量，也指比较重口的情色作品</p>
 <p data-pid="veFKy_Zy">pain in the ass 讨厌鬼</p>
 <p data-pid="qrbrqkO3">bad ass 混蛋，但结合语境也有夸奖的意思，大概就是 你丫牛B啊！</p>
 <p data-pid="SKxYpOZ7">jerk 混蛋，You lucky jerk！你小子走了什么狗屎运</p>
 <p data-pid="CWw7x1FI">bullshit 胡扯</p>
 <p data-pid="qx74awkg">sucker，loser比较有侮辱性，真的不要对人说</p>
 <p data-pid="maMQUdpF">抱歉啊我一个小姑娘实在写不下去了，还有什么hard啊，soft啊很多都是双关语，对这个感兴趣人可以看《破产姐妹》吧，Max怎么挤兑小老板的。实在太接地气了，看懂之后整个人都不好了╮(╯▽╰)╭</p>

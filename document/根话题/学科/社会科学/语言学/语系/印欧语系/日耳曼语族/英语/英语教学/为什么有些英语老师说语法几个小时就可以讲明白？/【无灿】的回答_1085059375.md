# 为什么有些英语老师说语法几个小时就可以讲明白？
- 点赞数：72009
- 更新时间：2021年01月10日10时01分05秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/30030877/answer/1085059375
<body>
 <p data-pid="RwktqlCn">我：I love you</p>
 <p data-pid="11nmDXn7">I主语，love谓语，you宾语</p>
 <p data-pid="KYfuSN7f">英语老师：Why?</p>
 <p data-pid="G4gT73P8">我：you are beautiful</p>
 <p data-pid="dSMM-Np3">you主语，are系动词，beautiful表语</p>
 <p data-pid="b2gkW2uN">英语老师：I agree，anything else？</p>
 <p data-pid="RBiD0R13">I 主语，agree，谓语</p>
 <p data-pid="gb-hWTjx">我：you make me happy</p>
 <p data-pid="TmAezUYC">you主语，make谓语，me宾语，happy宾补，用来形容me</p>
 <p data-pid="dVSYSvUF">我：and I bought you a rose</p>
 <p data-pid="1oFJksw2">I主语，bought谓语，you间接宾语，rose直接宾语</p>
 <p data-pid="v_yADCmS">英语老师：Oh my god，you are the most clever boy that I have ever met</p>
 <p data-pid="27K6lYNT">定语从句，I have ever met修饰boy，因为它是个句子，所以是定语从句。</p>
 <p data-pid="Pu7gfu7C">英语老师：But I don’t know whether you will still love me in the future</p>
 <p data-pid="3htXZ0DP">I 主语，know谓语，whether you will still love me in the future是宾语，因为他是个句子，所以是宾语从句</p>
 <p data-pid="qY1_aYNv">我：I will be always loving you until I die</p>
 <p data-pid="OPq8MxgA">until I die 作为状语，修饰I will be always loving you这个动作，因为I die 是个句子，所以这是个状语从句</p>
 <p data-pid="2-Tn3nJ8">英语老师：That you love me is not allowed， because I’m your teacher</p>
 <p data-pid="j0jUkYqQ">That you love me是主语，因为是个完整的句子，所以是主语从句</p>
 <p data-pid="6uhyCwqt">我：The fact that you are my teacher doesn’t affect our love at all</p>
 <p data-pid="F2AkzHUO">the fact 和that you are my teacher表述的是同一个事，you are my teacher是个句子，修饰fact，所以是同位语从句。</p>
 <p data-pid="Jh3W_58-">英语老师：The problem is that I care about rumors</p>
 <p data-pid="UrrwRlEa">The problem是主语，is系动词，I care about rumours是表语，因为它是个句子，所以是表语从句</p>
 <p data-pid="a2bW7wxQ">我：Trust me, I’ll protect you</p>
 <p data-pid="pBNRlxru">英语老师：你他妈的又在课堂上睡觉，赶紧回答下这个问题，英语总共有哪些基本句式和几大从句</p>
 <p data-pid="LFtg3wLA">我：<b>五大基本句式，主谓宾，主谓，主系表，主谓宾宾，主谓宾补。六大从句，定语从句，宾语从句，表语从句，主语从句，状语从句，同位语从句。所有复杂句，都可以追溯到这五大基本句和六大从句。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="o3Qh_S8d">英语老师：算你回答的不错，再课堂上睡觉，头都给你打爆。</p>
 <p data-pid="BcZune68">完</p>

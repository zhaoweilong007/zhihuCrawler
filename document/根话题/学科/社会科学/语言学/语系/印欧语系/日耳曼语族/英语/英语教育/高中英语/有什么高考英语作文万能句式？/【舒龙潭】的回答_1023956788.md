 <h2><b>⭐⭐个人情况</b></h2>
 <p data-pid="mCoTEM0b">1.I'm writing <b>in reply</b> to the notice for a volunteer, <b>hoping that i may have an opportunity to act as</b> a tour guide.我正在写信回复招聘一名志愿者的启示，希望我有机会担任导游</p>
 <p data-pid="8KPk1vIu">2. <b>To be honest</b>, I <b>have a gift for</b> communicating with others.说实话，我有与别人沟通的天赋</p>
 <p data-pid="RXwSwkta">3. <b>Moreover</b>, I <b>get along with</b> others easily, <b>which is especially necessary for a volunteer</b>.此外，我容易相处，这对志愿者来说是必不可少的</p>
 <p data-pid="DLatNb8z">4. <b>In addition</b>, I'm ready to give help <b>whenever it is needed</b>.另外，任何时候有需要，我都乐于提供帮助。</p>
 <p data-pid="cRHPNFIH">5. I am easygoing and outgoing and <b>able to get on well with others</b>.我很随和，外向，能够和别人友好相处。</p>
 <p data-pid="pPxpGKa_">6.I think I <b>am qualified for</b> the job, because I have a good character, being independent in life and <b>having a good knowledge of</b> English.我认为自己能胜任这项工作，因为我性格好，生活独立，精通英语。</p>
 <p data-pid="g6x8pf5l">7. <b>Meanwhile, it is my independence that makes it easy for me to</b> adapt to a new environment. 同时，正是我的独立性使我很容易适应新的环境。</p>
 <p data-pid="pHQcMJl4">8. <b>Not only am I</b> outgoing and energetic <b>but also</b> I am optimistic and enthusiastic.我不仅开朗活泼，而且乐观热情。</p>
 <p data-pid="w3tMRwhZ">9. I manage my time well, <b>so that I can always get things done</b> on time.我时间安排得很好，因此我总是能按时完成工作。</p>
 <p data-pid="4kQZ0t96">10. I <b>have a good command of</b> English, <b>which enables me to</b> communicate with foreign friends well. 我英语好，这使我能与外国朋友顺利交流。</p>
 <p data-pid="tbauvAGP">11. I <b>have strong team spirit</b> and like to work with others. 我有较强得团队精神，喜欢和别人一起工作。</p>
 <p data-pid="xGMtGpj0">12. In my spare time, I'<b>m fond of</b> collecting coins, <b>from which I can enlarge my knowledge and broaden my horizons.</b>业余时间，我喜欢收集硬币，从中我可以扩大知识面，开阔视野。</p>
 <p data-pid="EeF5cw_W">13. As for my hobby, I<b>'m keen on</b> playing football, <b>which makes it possible for me to</b> make more friends with the same interest. 至于我的爱好，我酷爱踢足球，那使我能够与志趣相投得人交更多得朋友。</p>
 <p data-pid="FKpS--xk">14. <b>What is important is that</b> my good communication skills <b>enable me to keep a good relationship with others.</b>重要的是，我良好的交际能力使我与他人维持良好的关系。</p>
 <p data-pid="l87x_E3-">15. <b>What satisfies me most is that I have no difficulty communicating with others</b> in English. 最使我满意的是我用英语和别人交流起来毫不费力。</p>
 <p data-pid="maYKEmIX">16. Meanwhile, <b>in order to enhance the personal qualities,</b> I actively participate in various activities to increase my experience, which I believe will <b>play an important role in dealing with</b> coming problems. 同时，为了提高个人能力，我积极参加各种活动，积累经验，我相信这对处理今后的问题起着重要作用。</p>
 <p data-pid="EFoyyCH0">17. <b>Last but not least</b>, I <b>am familiar with</b> all the places in our city.最后但同样重要的是，我对我们城市所有的地方都很熟悉。</p>
 <p data-pid="McFJ3f_W">18. I <b>have a good knowledge of</b> the places of interest in Beijing, such as the Great Wall, the Palace Museum and so on.我对北京的名胜了如指掌，如长城、故宫博物院等。</p>
 <p data-pid="knuvtjY9">19. <b>Not only do I</b> have the qualifications for the job, <b>but</b> I have the right personality for it. 我不禁具备做好这项工作的资格，而且还具备相应的人格魅力。</p>
 <p data-pid="CdfrvbQe">20. Furthermore, I also actively <b>participate in</b> community activities and volunteer service activities. 此外，我还积极参加社区活动和志愿服务活动。</p>
 <p data-pid="SXx0xrKB">21. Besides, my previous working experience as a volunteer will <b>qualify me for</b> the position. 此外，我以前做志愿者的工作经验使我能够胜任这个职务。</p>
 <p data-pid="G_APRkkX">22. <b>Should you treat my application favorably</b>, I would <b>spare no effort</b> to offer my best service. 如果您看中我的申请，我将不遗余力提供最佳服务。</p>

 <h2><b>⭐⭐日常活动</b></h2>
 <p data-pid="fUZba40t">1.<b>As scheduled</b>, we have an English Talent Show today, <b>with the purpose of developing our interest in</b> English learning and improving our practical abilities to listen and speak. 按照计划，我们今天举办英语才艺大赛，目的是培养我们英语学习的兴趣，提高我们实际听说的能力。</p>
 <p data-pid="QnsspINL">2. <b>To help you have a better understanding of Chinese culture,</b> the students' union will organize a New Year's party.为了帮助你们更好地理解中国文化，学生会将组织新年晚会。</p>
 <p data-pid="eaXFlEv_">3. Everyone <b>is supposed to</b> be in Room 120, the big classroom, exactly at 2:30 pm, Monday.周一下午两点半整大家须在大教室120房间集合。</p>
 <p data-pid="N0YlCzlD">4. <b>We feel it an honour to have a chance to</b> get together with Mr. Jones.能有机会与琼斯先生欢聚一堂，我们深感荣幸。</p>
 <p data-pid="jNC2Kh-2">5. I will try to <b>make the club known to</b> every student and <b>make more students involved in</b> the activitied organized by the club.我将尽力让每个学生了解社团，让更多的学生参与社团组织的活动。</p>
 <p data-pid="Q48xxZKU">6. <b>Let's make our joint efforts to</b> create a green and harmonious environment. 让我们为创造一个绿色和谐社会共同努力</p>
 <p data-pid="_nSt13ok">7. Last week, our school carried out a survey among all the students, <b>aiming to find out their opinions about</b> setting up an English corner. 上周，为了了解学生对建立英语角的看法，我们学校在所有学生中做了一项调查。</p>
 <p data-pid="VVoy2Ozs">8. As for me, I often go online to search some useful information for my study, because <b>I hold the view that we should make the best of the Internet to equip ourselves with more knowledge</b>. 至于我，我经常上网搜集一些对学习有用的信息，因为我认为我们应该充分利用互联网，以便用更多的知识武装自己。</p>
 <p data-pid="ADzK6p6l">9. <b>As far as I'm concerned,</b> I <b>am in favor of</b> recycling used textbooks because only by doing so can we help to save lots of natural resources.就我而言，我赞成回收利用旧课本，因为只有这样做，我们才能节约大量的自然资源。</p>
 <p data-pid="-ee27aV3">10. <b>Hardly had we got to the destination when</b> we did some volunteer work there. 我们以到达目的地就开始做志愿者工作。</p>
 <p data-pid="I3fqzmgi">11. Through this activity, <b>not only did we pass our love and friendship to others, but we also enriched our own school life.</b>通过这次活动，我们不仅给别人传递了爱和友谊，还丰富了我们自己的学校生活。</p>

 <h2>4 原创作文模板</h2>
 <p data-pid="rEYP9DQV">再更新一下我原创的作文模板吧（原发布在公众号：快把英语带走）</p>
 <h2><b>1、感谢信</b></h2>
 <p data-pid="OCd45I5t">Dear XXX</p>
 <p data-pid="lHxd1GAR"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZLrgZJW4"><b>先是一些套话</b></p>
 <p data-pid="L5zdD3mH">①I am writing to extend my sincere gratitude to you. Because of your help，（感谢的原因）或者 I am writing to express my sincere gratitude for the kindness you have shown for me while doing me a favor, which enables me .....（感谢原因）</p>
 <p data-pid="j9Or5Ry7">②If it had not been for your assistance in ______(对方给予的具体帮助), I fear that I would have been ______(没有对方帮助时的后果).</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="snVr0woZ"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_BVvD8rP">中间段就具体描述一下对方帮助的细节，就用平时常用的结构往里面套</p>
 <p data-pid="rqMUuwMe">③After thinking twice, nothing is better than presenting a ......as a gift in order to reciprocate your favor.（也可以送个小礼物什么的）</p>
 <p data-pid="J4I2-XRm">④You not only helped me in...，but also gave me numerous caredul instructions.（这里只是举个例子，就是可以用各种结构夸他！）</p>
 <p data-pid="Dg8C0fSP"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="z9oqrwjX">⑤Again, I would like to express my warm thanks for your timely help.Best wishes.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h2><b>2、申请信</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="km-IhaJP">Dear XXX</p>
 <p data-pid="-7pe-S3h"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3z5MWg2q">①I am more than thrilled on hearing that you are recruiting ......（申请的职位或者志愿者）也可以适当交代一些细节I'm more exceedingly overjoyed to know that some American high school students are to visit our city as well as our school, for whom you are looking for a volunteer tour guide.（根据题目中我们可以适当变换句式写在段首）</p>
 <p data-pid="iT0C0qBu">②I suppose I am quite qualified for this job and I do hope you can offer me this valuable opportunity.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="p29dMQjT"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kZl-SkaT">开始介绍自己的优势，同样，可以用一些结构来充实内容</p>
 <p data-pid="eBCoxpxk">可以说自己的家世背景，父亲，姐姐（可以编），就是说明自己这方面条件的优渥，和自己与别人造成的信息差，夸自己，让自己更有优势</p>
 <p data-pid="S4pcLnVM">③I am a fluent English speaker, consequently, I appreciate any chance to speak English with native speakers.(这里是拿一个英语工作者举例，大家可以根据题目要求替换，这句话长短句交错，可读性还不错)</p>
 <p data-pid="vCaWFiTB">④When it comes to myself, I am regarded as an active, vigorous, easy-going and enthusiastic student and I am always ready to help others regardless of young or old.（这个是描述性格的，在志愿者里面可以直接拿进去，不过内容也可以根据题目替换）</p>
 <p data-pid="sJfMTvp1">⑤Exposed to foreign country for several years，which equipped me with an ability of speaking English fluently and have a better kownledge of foreign culture.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PfkPMIWA"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="5mT723iy">总结一下，如果得到了这份工作，你一定会珍惜机会，并且做好工作</p>
 <p data-pid="SgbszIqI">并且希望别人能够联系你</p>
 <p data-pid="aL6BOBxM">⑥ Should you grant me this precious chance, I would appreciate it a lot as well as spare no effort to do this job gorgeously.如果你能给我这个宝贵的机会，我将不胜感激，并不遗余力地做好这项工作（这里这个Should 在开头，用了一个虚拟语气，这个结构是真实存在的，也是很少人知道的，考场上写出来，可以作为文章的闪光点，因为是虚拟语气，后面就用了would，配合虚拟语气，如果大家不理解，可以直接背下来这句话）</p>
 <p data-pid="_NwyG-Lw">⑦I'll appreciate it a lot as well as do my utmost to do this job gorgeously if I am offered this chance.</p>
 <p data-pid="nAL2V5RC">⑧I shall be much obliged if you will offer me a precious opportunity to an interview</p>
 <p data-pid="HJ79X2hj">⑨Please feel free to contact me if you are eager to know more about me.</p>
 <p data-pid="yC2pQFvB">⑩Thanks again for reading this letter.</p>
 <h2><b>3、建议信</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zCTX8CRd">Dear XXX</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="c8X-MVY1"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="SmwCdVbP">一般情况下，题目都会告诉为什么对方需要你提供建议，可能是你需要了一些挫折呀，或者说是遇到心理上的困难，在第一段，先交代一下背景</p>
 <p data-pid="-e3x-Fs2">①Hearing that .....（这里说对方遇到的困难），for whom you are asking me for some conductive ways to tackle this trouble.</p>
 <p data-pid="RXtApSf1">②You have asked me some advice with regard to ......, and I will try me best to offer you some help.</p>
 <p data-pid="70qJiygZ">③I am exceedingly overjoyed on receiving your letter, in which you express......</p>
 <p data-pid="Ylc7RDOp">④Some functional suggesttions will be illustrated as follows.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="D7yrmKSp"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="E7f8Kbzx">开始介绍，你的具体方法，2-3点即可</p>
 <p data-pid="l1-fTndS">⑤From my perspective, it is wise of you to take the following actions</p>
 <p data-pid="3w3JkLkv">⑥If I were you, I would.......（假设法，如果我是你，我会怎么做）</p>
 <p data-pid="N-3x-jWx">⑦First and foremost, I suggest that you should............(建议内容一)，which can make you........（说建议一的好处）</p>
 <p data-pid="01IwvLHO">⑧In addition，you'd better ............（建议内容二），through which you can..........（说建议二的好处）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aZCJv5Fu"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="YBiGuH0F">⑨I do hope you will find the proposals mentioned above will be beneficial for you.</p>
 <p data-pid="GIj6blHt">⑩I hope you will find these proposals useful, and I would be ready to discuss this matter with you to further details.</p>
 <p data-pid="BhYqZtb9">或者</p>
 <p data-pid="brr4dFJT">Please feel free to contact me if you have any other troubles.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h2><b>4、邀请信</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Gkt24GbU">Dear XXX</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0Po5Nqoo"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_YNHFLre">直接上结构，说他对此事有兴趣，所以要邀请他</p>
 <p data-pid="OOtImP4r">①Having known that you have a taste for .........(这里写他对什么感兴趣，看题目具体要求)recently.</p>
 <p data-pid="bALFqzbR">②Right now and right here, I am writing to inform you that................(通知对方举办活动的内容)</p>
 <p data-pid="kMHGbykD"><b>举例：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="SI4vb1Xj">Now, I am writing to inform you that our school photography club is recruiting new members and whoever interested in is welcom.</p>
 <p data-pid="VTr8wr19">③In light of your burning enthusiasm about environment protection, I am brimful of excitement to present my invitation about the ............（具体活动）, a brief introduction of which is as follows.</p>
 <p data-pid="6jj6IlYq">其实在第一段的结尾，也可以介绍活动时间，举例</p>
 <p data-pid="PuF-slOP">④How is everything going？In light of your burning enthusiasm, I'm brimful excitment to invite you to participate in the tree-planting activity organised by our school, which falls on May 12th.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="C-jFrLhm"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8EhBL_-L">开始介绍活动的概况和要求，还有举办的时间地点等,或者说你你提前应该做好一些准备，或者查阅一些相关手册啊什么的，然后说，这个活动的好处和宗旨</p>
 <p data-pid="GS34MXs_">④As scheduled, this activity will be launched on +（时间）</p>
 <p data-pid="GYe1_UZU">⑤First and foremost，weather permitting，we are scheduled to head for the destination in the moring</p>
 <p data-pid="w40IcbaM">⑥Additionally, what deserves laying stress on is that you're supposed to refer to some books with regard to.........(查阅一些相关的资料)</p>
 <p data-pid="uJcESVzm">⑦From my perspective, this activity, whose original aim is to appeal to people to do(这项活动是呼吁人们做什么), is definitely a superb plat form to do.........（表这个活动是一个我们干什么的很好的平台）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FYtjfBoF"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ocrNbf0K">表示你诚挚的希望和希望他联系你</p>
 <p data-pid="FRv8N3i6">⑧Hopefully, please feel free to contact me if you're eager to discuss this arrangement with me to further details.</p>
 <p data-pid="Vt_bBUzh">⑧A prompt reply is encouraged.</p>
 <h2><b>5、道歉信</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DpN3ziDW">Dear XXX</p>
 <p data-pid="llF-AChT"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="YWi6hqR0">①I am writing to offer my sincere apology to you for doing.....(说你自己做了啥对不起别人的事)</p>
 <p data-pid="CDbwWceJ">②I am writing to extend my sincere apology for.......（一样的）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fziDze0Y"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="6yqqwte1">说你当时做了对不起别人的事情的细节和原因，表示自己是有苦衷的，可以说自己遇到困境，或者是家里着火了，煤气泄漏了自己的等等，脑洞开大一点，多写点内容</p>
 <p data-pid="3OYsgh-D">③The reason is that （介绍原因）</p>
 <p data-pid="R90cPdz0">④We frankly admit that we were at fault and we are anxious to correct the consequence.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IzuZfJjk"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8H4r_2tJ">⑤ I promise this will not happen again and I do hope you can forgive me.</p>
 <p data-pid="Q1dgooEi">⑥ Hope you can accept my apology and understand my situation.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h2><b>6、投诉信</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="154PnVq6">Dear XXX：</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hOgWmmIO"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="QYNA386J">一般开头段写，你对产品或者是服务的不满意，并且期待能够得到解决</p>
 <p data-pid="lW_hhLFZ">①I am......（先进行一个简短的自我介绍）</p>
 <p data-pid="mTvpmi1C">②I feel sorry to trouble you but I am afraid that I have to make a complaint about.......(你要投诉的内容)</p>
 <p data-pid="TB5ua3P-">③I venture to write to you to complain about......</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kBSUWyxa"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="oJDEjQy0">开始具体阐述，这些你遇到的问题，给你带来的不好影响和麻烦</p>
 <p data-pid="pE1KmxwF">④There are several aspects underlying my dissatisfaction.</p>
 <p data-pid="hd1MPdRv">开始用自己的结构，来套用你的不便</p>
 <p data-pid="tyMBWll4">⑤First and formost,..................(说你不满意的第一个方面)，which cause many inconvenience to me at every aspects of my daily life.</p>
 <p data-pid="PdjNfw6a">⑥Additionally, ...................(描述你不满意的第二点)</p>
 <p data-pid="9tO6gg7A">⑦Under these circumstances, I find it .....(说你自己的感受) to (造成的后果)</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YcVsGIVx"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="PSzBbOXA">⑧I would appreciate it a lot if you could give me full refund or replace this with a new one.</p>
 <p data-pid="ihNOVmgK">⑨Your prompt reply to this letter at your earliest convenience will be highly appreciated</p>
 <h2><b>7、祝贺信</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6U0hmCKC">Dear XXX</p>
 <p data-pid="xDuRxTpo"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="yicpoNEQ">还是一样，先交代背景，说说得知了他应该祝贺的消息</p>
 <p data-pid="nRQk4F9M">①I am more than overjoyed on hearing that you........(说对方咋了)，which drives me to write this letter to extend my utmost congratulations on....</p>
 <p data-pid="Zjt6f12B">②I am writing this letter to express my sincerest congratulations on what you have achieved.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TblFsE00"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="F0oBrfxy">描述他取得这个成就的努力，一定要夸</p>
 <p data-pid="P6ZrKOFC">夸他怎么怎么努力，怎么怎么废寝忘食，这个奖励对他来说是应得的</p>
 <p data-pid="_VuUJitq">③You have kept working hard and devoted yourself into your major study during the past decades.</p>
 <p data-pid="F60PPyxw">④It is a reward you richly deserve for your........(说他的努力和认真刻苦钻研)</p>
 <p data-pid="jHrKhDHn">⑤I am more than thrilled to know that now your efforts are rewarded in this memorable way.</p>
 <p data-pid="WCCxv379">⑥what deserves being emphasized is that it is your consistent effort that push you to this level.</p>
 <p data-pid="DTBP3rIJ"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="yF9m6vjB">收尾，祝他在未来继续努力，并且获得更高的奖项</p>
 <p data-pid="SvwsP33s">⑦Again, congratulations on your success and I feel very proud of you.</p>
 <p data-pid="fp6i4eMY">⑧I have confidence that you will gain more shinning achievement in your future life.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="of_c7abv"><b>8、咨询信/回复咨询信</b></p>
 <p data-pid="D3aTFyYy">Dear XXX</p>
 <p data-pid="xusuT50G"><b>开头段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="l21t_ZSI">这封信和邀请信有一点点类似，因为如果你要去咨询一件事，那肯定你是通过某些途径了解到了相关事宜，然后想得知更多的消息（或者直接说自己写信的目的是咨询相关事宜），所以首先还是先来句套话说自己很高兴从哪儿哪儿得知到什么什么，然后再开始展开论述</p>
 <p data-pid="oOvcCIii">①I am more than thrilled to see your advertisement for the position in .... , thus, I am writing this letter to you with the hope of / hoping to get more information of / with regard / regarding / concerning it.</p>
 <p data-pid="Q4c4MFjD">②Sorry to disturb you, I am writing to consult you about further information of / with regard / regarding / concerning ......</p>
 <p data-pid="d0iLdNwf">③I am writing in response to your advertisement /with regard / regarding / concerning ......, and I want to get more detailed information of it.</p>
 <p data-pid="TSLwNsiy">④I am writing to see if it is possible for you to provide me with information regarding / with regard to / about</p>
 <p data-pid="-5y4z-fF"><b>中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZvzQLLye">中间段就具体阐述一下自己的相关情况，和自己想要咨询相关信息的原因</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="G9HZ5C6p">这一段由于咨询的情况各种各样，并且很多信件的模板句型都是相同的，按照这篇推文里面给的其他句型和模板套用就可以了，主要是有写的就可，内容不限，重点是开头和结尾</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VsvBnhuC"><b>结尾段：</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

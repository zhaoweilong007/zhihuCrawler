# 写英语作文如何熟练使用高级词汇?

<body>
 <h3>评价事件</h3>
 <p data-pid="hxQT-ApL">1. 好的 good</p>
 <p data-pid="k7_z3GMW">优秀的outstanding, perfect, brilliant, remarkable, distinguished, excellent</p>
 <p data-pid="c_QkDq1M">教育性的,启发性的instructive, educational, enlightening</p>
 <p data-pid="cOVdXXS8">建设性的,有益的constructive, helpful, valuable, useful</p>
 <p data-pid="4i7WkDff">有意义的 meaningful</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FUnU32ii">2. 极好的,惊人的 great, surprising</p>
 <p data-pid="yL0r7Faq">难以置信的,惊人的,令人震惊的 fantastic, incredible, unusual, unbelievable, exceptional, extraordinary, marvelous, notable, noteworthy, striking, amazing, shocking, astonishing</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="d1hnCY72">3. 坏的bad</p>
 <p data-pid="bYRHfc-c">糟糕的terrible</p>
 <p data-pid="6SPj3zVr">不理想的undesirable</p>
 <p data-pid="bHb54roZ">不令人满意的unsatisfactory</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dPID2m0Q">4. 无趣味的，无聊的 uninteresting</p>
 <p data-pid="9yGcX0Dj">令人厌倦的 boring, dull, annoying</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tOEW374R">5. 难于理解的 difficult to understand</p>
 <p data-pid="ekUoZllP">抽象的,深奥的 abstract</p>
 <p data-pid="yrCiUJ7y">复杂的 complicated, complex</p>
 <p data-pid="J3cu5rvQ">混杂的,不清楚的,迷惑的 confused, bewildered, perplexed</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_iCVhkXA">6. 好处 good side=</p>
 <p data-pid="46JCk3xw">好处，优点 advantage, merit, benefit</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ASC40gyI">7. 坏处 bad side=</p>
 <p data-pid="2dPeeikZ">坏处,缺点disadvantage, defect, drawback, deficiency</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="R3hORYV4">8. 必要的 necessary=</p>
 <p data-pid="SIeES4GP">必要的,不可缺少的indispensable, essential</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="X6pBsSyy">9. 很大 very big=</p>
 <p data-pid="oXhz5whk">巨大的tremendous, immense, huge, gigantic, giant, enormous</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YPNm-GFK">10. 很多 many=</p>
 <p data-pid="3ehNIQ2p">不计其数的countless, endless, unlimited, innumerable,immeasurable,incalculable,</p>
 <p data-pid="6dQD4SA-">numberless, numerous</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0oWpBmPQ">11. 增长 grow=</p>
 <p data-pid="-0Neh3K9">提高 improve, increase, enhance, raise</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aZLxNaWR">12. 越来越（多）</p>
 <p data-pid="M5Sm3keQ">1)more and more+n. → students in growing</p>
 <p data-pid="v9K3wh5N">numbers</p>
 <p data-pid="yqoWb4sy">2)more and more +adj→ increasingly</p>
 <p data-pid="oM6uUupI">important</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2laYEj9M">13. 很难difficult= 有挑战性的challenging</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ALHdGOlW">14. 很重要important= 至关重要的vital，significant</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cV9FEC-H">15. adj.=of+n.</p>
 <p data-pid="hdT4iuWI">important → of great importance</p>
 <p data-pid="2ZudW7yA">different → of great difference</p>
 <p data-pid="nIngrBR2">beautiful → of amazing beauty</p>
 <p data-pid="ITuHHG5J">wise → of outstanding wisdom</p>
 <p data-pid="R94o_kPN">useful → of great use</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zNHQVsXJ">16. 特殊的,特别的 special=</p>
 <p data-pid="yJ-hWrT3">具体的,明确的 concrete，specific，particular，definite</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BI5MGfHW">17. 和……有关 be related to=</p>
 <p data-pid="NKj_GcAi">与……相联系be connected with, be linked to, be relevant to, have something to do with</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7D9MC7FO">18. 注定 it is decided that...=</p>
 <p data-pid="ed9ZroWZ">注定 be doomed to，be destined to</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="k5pi4_65">19. 乐趣 fun=</p>
 <p data-pid="9oddh26q">娱乐,乐趣 entertainment，amusement</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h3>描述个人</h3>
 <p data-pid="iYf2GKl_">20. 朋友 friend</p>
 <p data-pid="UjS45FAp">熟人 acquaintance</p>
 <p data-pid="ec2zZtQE">伙伴 associate, companion</p>
 <p data-pid="jCa4fjDv">密友,知己 intimate, confidant</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LLhM9lhK">21. 勤奋的hardworking=</p>
 <p data-pid="6Y1GEUEP">勤奋的,勤勉的 diligent, studious, industrious</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6xETGLdk">22. 坚决的 decided <i>adj</i>=</p>
 <p data-pid="46eNmoWx">坚持不懈的perseverant, persistent, relentless</p>
 <p data-pid="aogqTyOq">不屈服的 unyielding</p>
 <p data-pid="7YcZzTRV">坚持的 insistent</p>
 <p data-pid="qWCaT7Rp">有决心的determined, resolute, resolved</p>
 <p data-pid="4OPSfYe7">坚决的 firm</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Hov2GmHY">23. 聪明的 clever=</p>
 <p data-pid="_rDan2s_">聪明的,有才智的intelligent, bright, wise, brilliant</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="KmJz-_5i">24. 诚实的 honest</p>
 <p data-pid="OcjMicIW">真诚的,诚实的,正直的 sincere, truthful, genuine, upright</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cP_tDDJR">25. 美丽的 beautiful, pretty</p>
 <p data-pid="JXxJQ96z">迷人的,吸引人的 enchanting, charming, appealing, fascinating, attractive</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="elE5UHjP">26. 累的 tired=</p>
 <p data-pid="X9XhR5yl">精疲力尽的 exhausted, worn-out</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="koCU_SPn">27. 开心的Happy → joyful / delighted</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rOH7asLL">28. 不开心的 sad=</p>
 <p data-pid="NZfB6zIN">沮丧的,失意的 frustrated, discouraged, depressed, downhearted, disheartened, low-spirited</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vqOcgHsK">29. 担心的 be/feel worried=</p>
 <p data-pid="M0nsN7AU">忧虑的,不安的anxious, uneasy, troubled, concerned, restless</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Mj7H3d7Z">30. 失业的 out of work=</p>
 <p data-pid="_8RMKzLD">失业的 unemployed, jobless</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FByoM6jq">31. 进步 progress=</p>
 <p data-pid="gMiyk9tK">进步,成就,成绩advancement, achievement, accomplishment, fulfilment</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YkNBr_FF">32. 遇到（事） meet=</p>
 <p data-pid="WXzUlkMC">碰到,遇到 come across, meet with, confront</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h3>行为动作</h3>
 <p data-pid="56AhnJ6G">33. 使用 use =</p>
 <p data-pid="gvtZ_vBh">make good use of</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cCq-v2BY">34. 学习 learn =</p>
 <p data-pid="G0JZ7eWb">pick up (偶然或轻松学到)</p>
 <p data-pid="8UFIXN3M">master（掌握）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oxa8tcog">35. 适应 be used to=</p>
 <p data-pid="MBNwuMaI">适应be adapted to, be accustomed to, be adjusted to</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="68pPKqdv">36. 参加 join, enter=</p>
 <p data-pid="NHMHWYME">参与participate in, attend, take part in, go in for</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="27BZ3BGf">37. 注意 pay one’s attention to=</p>
 <p data-pid="DnCXyO03">集中精力focus on, center on, concentrate on</p>
 <p data-pid="9KWOMidW">专心于 set one’s mind on</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OgfD070s">38. 考虑 think about sth/consider sth=</p>
 <p data-pid="-non1vzQ">take into account +sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="qUjs0xVS">take into consideration +sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="lFESzvB1">【例】:take into consideration other people's opinion.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hk5lVSxa">39. 做 do =</p>
 <p data-pid="y0UXft7h">开展,实施,做 practice，carry out，perform</p>
 <p data-pid="0U_TX2-Y">利用好时间精力去做make good use of your time and effort to do</p>
 <p data-pid="cwSzNUBv">完全有理由去做have every reason to do</p>
 <p data-pid="pUPtmLKZ">忍不住做can't help doing</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Mo4nN5LA">40. 能够做 can do sth. =</p>
 <p data-pid="5QaUE3-H">will do sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="0-i-CHh1">be able to do sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="vrtOhS52">be capable of doing sth.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q8wzv_KY">41. 完成 finish=</p>
 <p data-pid="bxWYEzPG">实现,完成achieve, accomplish, fulfill, complete</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ids_iMUY">42. 花时间做 spend time doing sth=</p>
 <p data-pid="6Qo1e3uW">抽出时间做set aside time to do sth, put aside time to do sth, spare time to do sth</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DXF2_5yy">43. 尽力做 try to, work hard=</p>
 <p data-pid="tfkX-gPE">努力做,尽力做 attempt to, make efforts to, endeavor to, manage to do</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PAMA9ALJ">44. 决定做 decide to do=</p>
 <p data-pid="FnAKkEoC">决心做be determined to do, make up one’s mind to do</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Kv23Ok5N">45. 想要做 want to =</p>
 <p data-pid="oEplUF6K">等不及，渴望</p>
 <p data-pid="_STQGNrh">tend/intend to do sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="LJMoF0lj">be longing for doing sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="4E7yWB2E">be thirsty for doing sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="-9MnIc5K">look forward to doing sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="9PqJ0XFz">can't wait to do sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="fHR_uhvh">desire to do</p>
 <p data-pid="mCJFTz9x">have the desire to do/for sth.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZewEAMOv">46. 喜欢做like =love\ enjoy\ prefer...</p>
 <p data-pid="Ci9De78z">be fond of + 物</p>
 <p data-pid="Bvz9hqFP">be crazy about</p>
 <p data-pid="o5EqMaDd">I appreciate your sense of humour.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="szz9rJh1">47. 应该做 should do=</p>
 <p data-pid="kmq9SjYg">Had better do</p>
 <p data-pid="97TNmfGJ">Be supposed to do</p>
 <p data-pid="OWRM4i1-">Why not do</p>
 <p data-pid="86sDUkHO">If I were you, I would do</p>
 <p data-pid="9jtDUk6u">It is beneficial/helpful/meaningful for you to do</p>
 <p data-pid="7CKmh1cS">Doing sth is another possible choice for you.</p>
 <p data-pid="Qdt1d0ku">Close attention should be paid to that…</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HgEcUXXY">48. 有机会做 have a chance to/of=</p>
 <p data-pid="cBfZ8AsI">有机会的stand a chance of, have an opportunity to</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="F7deQXmO">49. 重视value =</p>
 <p data-pid="porBBrsZ">珍爱,珍惜,重视cherish, treasure, have a high opinion of</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1GLdzvT0">50. 把……看作,当作 see…as=</p>
 <p data-pid="hBwlyzq3">把……看作,当作 regard as, consider as, look on…as, treat…as, view…as</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="QzEvh34A">51. 解释 explain=</p>
 <p data-pid="ujkmBKcu">解释 account for, illustrate, clarify</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="N25z6rb1">52. 想 think=</p>
 <p data-pid="8wqUK1Wr">想出,想到 sb come up with, sth/it cross one’s mind, sth/it occur to somebody</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TJePoLps">53. 认为 think = guess</p>
 <p data-pid="x5qqH0sK">harbor the idea that...</p>
 <p data-pid="ii58_xR6">take the attitude that...</p>
 <p data-pid="p1NIrk7C">hold the view that...</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rMmI-KKy">54. 相信 believe =</p>
 <p data-pid="p5tR9bB8">sb insist on sth 坚持（某种观点）</p>
 <p data-pid="v90qFFNs">sb insist that... 坚持做某事</p>
 <p data-pid="LbDg4ngM">be sure that</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7r8OXqhU">55. 需要 need =</p>
 <p data-pid="rW8ETwFc">需要 call for, require, demand</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aamrYz1e">56. 知道 know =</p>
 <p data-pid="7LCIU5QO">熟悉,知道be acquainted with, be familiar with, be informed of</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="V3aMn8jR">57. 我们都知道 we all know =</p>
 <p data-pid="yBp-StgK">It is widely shared that...</p>
 <p data-pid="P2Mf32bf">It is universally acknowledged that...</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pWavquSn">58. 意识到 realize=</p>
 <p data-pid="tuk2yy4T">意识到become/be aware of, become/be conscious of</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h3>逻辑关系</h3>

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DCI-5sth">60. 因为because= ...for... ...considering that...</p>

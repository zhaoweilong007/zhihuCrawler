# 这个世界有多少用WinRAR的付费用户？

<body>
 <p data-pid="RhP_L28-">有个老外在 WinRAR 商店上看到可以“物理交付”，他以为是个玩笑，于是出于好奇心订购了一份。</p>

 <p data-pid="3cpzdxVO">下完订单，他收到了销售团队的邮件，邮件内容没啥，就是告诉你订购成功了，以及一些细节。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-41772ea2c6fae27042a47b44487b5065_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="953" data-rawheight="429" data-original-token="v2-8a3381d7f4996d4a6725b0b11c6d54d8" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5a6120ecaac141fe366fad4636dbed58_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="953" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-41772ea2c6fae27042a47b44487b5065_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="rzP60lx7">然后，这人直接回复了邮件，先表达了自己的惊喜（居然真有CD），顺带提了两个问题：</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="A2p__1rn">你们每年销售多少张许可证？</li>
  <li data-pid="N4rh_NZ5">你们是如何赚钱的？</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="CLWqFLau">然后对方居然回复了：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6fa7450c6f5f3e686047524e15da00b2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="899" data-rawheight="740" data-original-token="v2-d387fc72735e61e9c2b7c1fa498375ec" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-665b2bfc75cab5126e1e715b8eafb452_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="899" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-6fa7450c6f5f3e686047524e15da00b2_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="w98vzS4t">There are many memes and jokes about WinRAR and it's never-ending trial period, but despite this i think many people would be extremely surprised about how many licenses we sell on a daily basis!</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="unmrlYAU">
  有很多针对 WinRAR 以及它永无止境的试用期的段子和笑话，尽管如此，我想很多人会对我们每天售出多少许可证感到非常惊讶！
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Jkb1fsLn">For the CDs,we sell quite a few of those too!</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="rkmHBOi9">
  至于 CD，我们也卖了不少！
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="1hX065Ig">Let's just say that approximately 3000 WinRAR CDs are shipped to users all over the World each year!</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="aG3Mp48e">
  这么说吧，每年大约有 3000 张 WinRAR 光盘运往世界各地的用户手中！
 </blockquote>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tAMqSRM-">四周后，这位用户的 CD 到货了。</p>
 <p data-pid="o_9oTSac">然后，他还专门买了个外接的 CDROM 用来读盘......</p>

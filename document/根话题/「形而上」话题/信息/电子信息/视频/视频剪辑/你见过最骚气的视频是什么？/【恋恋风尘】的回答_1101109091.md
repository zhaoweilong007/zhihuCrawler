 <h2>2.村生莫</h2>
 <p data-pid="QsmOk9V0">类似李子柒的一个男博主，更新频率很高。</p>
 <p data-pid="-MO4p2ph">我之前喜欢看他建房子，现在喜欢看他做吃的。真的能给人带来很多儿时记忆。</p>
 <p data-pid="g6yyiyIc">我发现此类博主个人能做的事情真的很多：画画、裁缝、木工、电工、管道工、泥工、瓦工、油漆工。真的是十项全能。</p>
 <p data-pid="cEDhPfcf">博主改造他姐姐儿时住过的房子，给黑白无色彩的房间赋予颜色。整个过程赋予的心思真的很多，整个构造、搭配也显得非常和谐。</p>
 <p data-pid="fF2caMtG">视频来自抖音博主“村生莫”，抖音ID：csm5。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1225791901242281984" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="以梦为马" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a69455f038aa4efa8f29625940b692e9.jpeg" data-lens-id="1225791901242281984"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a69455f038aa4efa8f29625940b692e9.jpeg"><span class="content"><span class="title">以梦为马<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1225791901242281984</span></span></a>

  3.一米八八的美术老师</h2>
 <p data-pid="CgNVEcOT">山西忻州繁峙县滨河小学的美术老师赵文瑞，几支粉笔就能画出清明上河图。</p>
 <p data-pid="ds3OyD_O">央视新闻，人民日报还几次专门派记者去采访。他开始画粉笔画上传到抖音只是偶然，却没有想到自己的粉笔画视频在抖音上面大火。对于他来说，粉笔画就是他的语言。</p>
 <p data-pid="SZvniPGF">清明上河图是他正在创作的作品，我在他的主页还看到了他画的星空、房屋、四季、河流。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1225790359856541696" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="太空人" data-poster="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-69afd6f8d43567b8b513b37590a6fc88.jpg" data-lens-id="1225790359856541696"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-69afd6f8d43567b8b513b37590a6fc88.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">太空人<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1225790359856541696</span></span></a>

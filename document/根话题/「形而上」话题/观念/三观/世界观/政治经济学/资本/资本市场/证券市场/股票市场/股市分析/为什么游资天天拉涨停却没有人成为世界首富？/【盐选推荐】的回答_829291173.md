 <p>谁是游资<span><span>？</span></span>游资究竟是怎么做股票的<span><span>，</span></span>为什么天天拉涨停也没有成为世界首富<span><span>？</span></span>为什么如此受大众追捧<span><span>？</span></span>今天<span><span>，</span></span>我就来给你回答这些问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>几乎在每一个涨停板股票的背后<span><span>，</span></span>都有游资的煽风点火<span><span>。</span></span>当然<span><span>，</span></span>也可以这么说<span><span>，</span></span>如果没有这些游资<span><span>，</span></span>也就不会有那么多的涨停板<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>二<span><span>、</span></span>游资如何做投资的<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>游资主要是趋势投资者和主题投资者<span><span>。</span></span>与信奉价值投资<span><span>，</span></span>长期持股的巴菲特们不同<span><span>，</span></span>游资们行走江湖的独门秘籍就是三个字<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>快<span><span>、</span></span>准<span><span>、</span></span>狠<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>他们的信仰是<span><span>「</span></span>天下武功<span><span>，</span></span>唯快不破<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>

# 有哪些看起来简单的物理现象需要用非常高深的物理理论来解释？

<body>
 <p><strong><span><span>「</span></span>沾水之后的两片玻璃为何很难分开<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>这是一个非常典型的<span><span>「</span></span>看似很简单<span><span>，</span></span>其实很复杂<span><span>」</span></span>的问题<span><span>。</span></span></strong>流行的科普<span><span>（</span></span>甚至中学物理课上<span><span>）</span></span>上似乎讲的很清楚<span><span>，</span></span>但是其实真正的原因并没有讲明白<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>流行科普上讲<span><span>，</span></span>由于玻璃之间充满了水<span><span>，</span></span>它们之间没有空气<span><span>，</span></span>因而靠大气压就可以把它们紧密地挤压在一起<span><span>。</span></span>如下图所示<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-fb4c43622f55a7721f3d88624688cc22_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="2560" data-rawheight="1317" data-original-token="v2-fb4c43622f55a7721f3d88624688cc22" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2560" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-fb4c43622f55a7721f3d88624688cc22_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p><strong>但是这个答案其实并不正确<span><span>，</span></span>或者说并不准确<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>因为我们知道<span><span>，</span></span>水也是满足帕斯卡原理的<span><span>。</span></span>大气压并不仅仅是作用在玻璃表面的<span><span>，</span></span>玻璃中间的水也与大气有接触<span><span>，</span></span>因而大气压也会作用在缝隙中的水上的<span><span>。</span></span>由帕斯卡原理<span><span>，</span></span>外界的大气压力是可以经由水传递到玻璃间隙之中的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>所以仅仅是有水的填充<span><span>，</span></span>玻璃不会<span><span>「</span></span>被大气压挤压在一起<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>我们可以用下面的局部放大图来说明这种情况<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-55bc7d0e362bb768a7b1f38060dffc10_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="1543" data-rawheight="1440" data-original-token="v2-55bc7d0e362bb768a7b1f38060dffc10" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1543" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-55bc7d0e362bb768a7b1f38060dffc10_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>也就是说<span><span>，</span></span>由于帕斯卡原理<span><span>，</span></span>缝隙中的水也会将大气压传递到两片玻璃的缝隙内部<span><span>。</span></span>大气压的作用效果并非是<span><span>「</span></span>向内<span><span>」</span></span>挤压两片玻璃<span><span>，</span></span>因为在缝隙内部同样存在着压力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>说到底<span><span>，</span></span>液体和气体对大气压的传递是相同的<span><span>，</span></span>因此单纯从压力的传递来讲<span><span>，</span></span>玻璃片中间有水还是没有水并不会产生不同的效果<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这里的真实原因<span><span>，</span></span><strong>其实是表面张力与大气压的共同作用</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>简言之<span><span>，</span></span>上面的图稍微有点错误<span><span>：</span></span>水和大气的界面不是平的<span><span>，</span></span>而应该是一个凹液面<span><span>。</span></span>而恰恰是这个凹液面<span><span>，</span></span>导致了完全不同的结果<span><span>。</span></span>具体讲<span><span>，</span></span>就是使得液体内部的压力低于外部的大气压力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2e1b6f23bae8dd69e595e18fe9bd871e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="2147" data-rawheight="1440" data-original-token="v2-2e1b6f23bae8dd69e595e18fe9bd871e" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2147" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-2e1b6f23bae8dd69e595e18fe9bd871e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>而这里的罪魁祸首<span><span>，</span></span>就是表面张力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>表面张力<span><span>，</span></span>我们简单形象地理解<span><span>，</span></span><strong>可以认为流体的两相<span><span>（</span></span>如气液<span><span>）</span></span>界面就像是一张紧绷的皮膜</strong><span><span>，</span></span>这张膜在外力的约束下<span><span>，</span></span>总是希望尽可能地收缩<span><span>。</span></span>沿着它的表面就有一种张力<span><span>，</span></span>就是表面张力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>如果你想用最形象的方式理解表面张力<span><span>，</span></span>你可以想象一个吹起来的气球的表面<span><span>：</span></span><strong>气球的弹力</strong>使它尽量收缩从而整体形成球形<span><span>。</span></span>相对应地<span><span>，</span></span><strong>水滴的表面张力</strong>使它尽量收缩从而形成球形<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-6992d95e31926b56958acc1bcb9a1279_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="2560" data-rawheight="1182" data-original-token="v2-6992d95e31926b56958acc1bcb9a1279" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2560" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-6992d95e31926b56958acc1bcb9a1279_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>而这里有一件非常关键的事请<span><span>，</span></span>就是<strong>由于表面张力的存在<span><span>，</span></span>弯曲的表面就会在两侧形成压力差</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如说<span><span>，</span></span>无重力液滴是一个球形<span><span>。</span></span>我们对一个这样的液滴的上半球做一个受力分析<span><span>，</span></span>它受到三个力的作用<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>1. 下半球在截面上对它的净压力<span><span>；</span></span></p>
 <p>2. 外部在上半球面上对它的净压力</p>
 <p>3. 液滴表面受到的沿表面垂直于<span><span>「</span></span>断面<span><span>」</span></span>的表面张力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-820786eb58bd670ac508d4aa7e2d0829_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="2560" data-rawheight="1276" data-original-token="v2-820786eb58bd670ac508d4aa7e2d0829" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2560" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-820786eb58bd670ac508d4aa7e2d0829_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>我们很容易就会看到<span><span>，</span></span>由于表面张力的存在<span><span>，</span></span>此时内部的压力肯定要大于外部压力<span><span>。</span></span>其实这个很容易理解<span><span>：</span></span>一个气球就是典型内部压力大于外部压力的例子<span><span>，</span></span>这种压力差<span><span>，</span></span>就是有张力的皮膜形成的<span><span>。</span></span>那么<span><span>，</span></span>这种压力差的大小是由什么决定的呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>很显然<span><span>，</span></span><strong>一个决定因素就是张力的大小</strong><span><span>：</span></span>皮膜绷的越紧<span><span>，</span></span>所能产生的压力差就越大<span><span>。</span></span>但是还有<strong>另一个很重要的因素<span><span>，</span></span>就是表面弯曲的程度</strong><span><span>，</span></span>也就是它的曲率<span><span>。</span></span>我们还是用气球做一个说明<span><span>，</span></span>例如下面这个气球<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0c1e1d85cfc13398cc35416ab419523f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="2560" data-rawheight="1237" data-original-token="v2-0c1e1d85cfc13398cc35416ab419523f" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2560" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0c1e1d85cfc13398cc35416ab419523f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>气球内部的气体压力处处相等<span><span>，</span></span>因此<span><span>，</span></span>气球内外的压差是一个常数<span><span>。</span></span>但是<span><span>，</span></span>接触过这种气球的人都有一个经验<span><span>，</span></span>就是粗的地方绷得紧<span><span>，</span></span>而细的地方绷得就不那么紧<span><span>。</span></span>也就是说<span><span>，</span></span>在起球不同的地方<span><span>，</span></span>皮膜的张力是不同的<span><span>，</span></span>如上图所示<span><span>。</span></span>那么问题就来了<span><span>，</span></span>绷得紧的地方和绷得松的地方<span><span>，</span></span>产生的压力差却是相等的<span><span>，</span></span>这是为何<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>原因是<span><span>，</span></span>在绷得紧和绷得松的地方<span><span>，</span></span>皮膜的曲率是不相等的<span><span>：</span></span>曲率越大<span><span>，</span></span>同样的张力所能产生的压力差就更大<span><span>；</span></span>曲率越小<span><span>，</span></span>同样的张力产生的压力差就越小<span><span>。</span></span>反过来说<span><span>，</span></span>如果产生同样的压力差<span><span>，</span></span>曲率大的地方<span><span>，</span></span>所需要的张力就越小<span><span>（</span></span>在气球细的地方<span><span>，</span></span>皮膜就松<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>反之亦然<span><span>（</span></span>在气球粗的地方皮膜就紧<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>现在<span><span>，</span></span>我们可以来分析一下沾水的玻璃为何很难分开了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>首先<span><span>，</span></span>我们知道<span><span>，</span></span>液体与固体界面相接触<span><span>，</span></span>都会形成一个接触角<span><span>。</span></span>接触角也是表面张力的性质之一<span><span>，</span></span>这里我就不展开说了<span><span>。</span></span>接触角小于 90°的<span><span>，</span></span>被称作浸润<span><span>（</span></span>如水对玻璃就是浸润的<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>大于 90°的<span><span>，</span></span>叫做不浸润<span><span>（</span></span>如水银对玻璃就是不浸润的<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>我们看到<span><span>，</span></span>这个凹液面就形成了一个<span><span>「</span></span>反向<span><span>」</span></span>的紧绷的膜<span><span>。</span></span>就像是气球一个道理<span><span>，</span></span>这种曲面的膜<span><span>，</span></span>由于表面张力的原因<span><span>，</span></span>就会使得液体的压力 P 小于外部大气压 P0<span><span>。</span></span>因而把玻璃挤压在一起的力<span><span>，</span></span>就不是 P0 而是 P0-P<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>常温下水的表面张力大约为 0.073N/m<span><span>。</span></span>水在普通玻璃上的接触角大约为 30°左右<span><span>。</span></span>因此<span><span>，</span></span>对于贴合较好的两片玻璃片<span><span>，</span></span>很容易计算出这个液膜内部的压力与大气压的压力差为 25Kpa – 大约为大气压的 1/4 左右<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>也就是说<span><span>，</span></span>液膜内部压力比大气压小 25Kpa<span><span>。</span></span>对于这样 10cm2 的两片玻璃<span><span>，</span></span>如果我们用一点水把它<span><span>「</span></span>沾<span><span>」</span></span>在一起<span><span>，</span></span>我们需要大约 250N 的力才能掰开 – 虽然很难<span><span>，</span></span>但是如果有抓手的话还是有可能的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但是如果是按照流行科普的说法<span><span>（</span></span>液膜排出空气导致玻璃间隙压力为零<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>我们所需要 1000N 的力才能掰开<span><span>，</span></span>这个就不太可能了<span><span>。</span></span></p>

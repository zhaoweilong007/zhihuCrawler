# 有哪些超出一般认知的极端物质？
- 点赞数：9525
- 更新时间：2017年02月07日22时49分36秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/55479632/answer/144959851
<body>
 <p data-pid="lsnEL3HS">1962年， 化学家<b>A. G. Streng<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=+%5E%7B%5B1%5D%7D" alt=" ^{[1]}" eeimg="1"></b>因为不知如何合适将他对一种<b>新化合物</b>的研究成果撰写成学术论文而苦苦挣扎。</p>
 <p data-pid="UzzlPLdt">不是因为他的研究毫无意义 ---- 正相反, 他所做的实验首次向人们揭示了一种<b>新化合物的化学性质</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="6HG9NRVB">不是因为他的能力不足 ---- 作为<b>天普大学</b>(Temple University)的教授，对于他来说这本应不是什么难事。</p>
 <p data-pid="XIdV4KHm">而是因为, 他不知道究竟什么样的<b>措辞</b>，能在<b>清楚、客观</b>地介绍这种物质的化学反应的同时, 使得身为作者的自己看上去不那么<b>疯狂</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="wkz1tmY8">经过一番寒彻骨，这篇关于<b>二氟化二氧（<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D" alt="O_{2}F_{2}" eeimg="1">，也被昵称为“FOOF”）</b>的化学性质的论文<i>Chemical Properties of Dioxygen Difloride</i></p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5E%7B%5B2%5D%7D" alt="^{[2]}" eeimg="1">
 <p data-pid="uvQ_JVAv">终于被发表到了<i>JACS</i>上。</p>
 <p data-pid="YDA4qzq4">然而， 尽管他尽了这么大的努力，这篇文章的读者之一 ---- 杜克大学的<b>Derek Lowe</b>教授仍然做出了如下评价</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5E%7B%5B3%5D%7D" alt="^{[3]}" eeimg="1">
 <p data-pid="83-1r32x">：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="2dNAxZSD">
  If the paper weren't laid out in complete grammatical sentences and published in <em>JACS</em>, you'd swear it was the work of a violent lunatic. I ran out of vulgar expletives after the second page. A. G. Streng, folks, absolutely takes the corrosive exploding cake, and I have to tip my asbestos-lined titanium hat to him.
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <blockquote data-pid="lfv8NvPx">
  如果这篇文章不是用完全符合语法的句子撰写并被发表在<i>JACS</i>上，你绝对会发誓赌咒这是一个有暴力倾向的疯子的作品。我在看完第二页的时候就已经把我会说的脏话都说完了。A. G. Streng这类人，毫无疑问地拿下了这块腐蚀性的爆炸蛋糕取得胜利，我必须脱下我的带着石棉内衬的钛制帽子向他致敬。
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="_flBGUf-">二氟化二氧，橘黄色固体，熔点为-163°C，熔化为红色液体。温度达到-160°C时开始分解为氧气和氟</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5E%7B%5B4%5D%7D" alt="^{[4]}" eeimg="1">
 <p data-pid="d4vq12aA">，目前已知的<b>最强氧化剂之一</b>。它的神奇之处在于能和<b>几乎所有物质</b>发生异常剧烈的反应。</p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-833e9ee9b14db9be307bfeb7640c5734_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="144" data-original-token="v2-833e9ee9b14db9be307bfeb7640c5734" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="0S7qXvT8">1. 理论上能与一切有机物发生反应。A. G. Streng文中做了如下几个实验：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="QpGvgpPX">
  <p data-pid="6eNyV7s2">It reacted instantaneously with solid ethyl alcohol, producing a blue flame and an explosion.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="YjF8LHYc">和固态乙醇立即发生反应，产生<b>蓝色的火焰和爆炸</b>。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="b_VSx_H0">
  <p data-pid="2ezu0Xck">When a drop of liquid <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D" alt="O_{2}F_{2}" eeimg="1"> was added to liquid methane, cooled at 90°K, a white flame was produced instantaneously, which turned green upon further burning.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="2x15jhFZ">将<b>一滴</b>液态二氟化二氧加入到90°K的液态<b>甲烷</b>中，立刻<b>产生白色火焰</b>，后续燃烧过程中变为绿色。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="vhOYwqgB">
  <p data-pid="cM-Rvpel">When 0.2 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cm%5E%7B3%7D" alt="cm^{3}" eeimg="1"> of <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D" alt="O_{2}F_{2}" eeimg="1"> liquid was added to 0.5 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=cm%5E%7B3%7D" alt="cm^{3}" eeimg="1"> of liquid CH4 at 90°K , a violent explosion occurred.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="HaTJ9FIX">0.2立方厘米的液态二氟化二氧加入到0.5立方厘米，90°K的液态甲烷中，立刻发生<b>爆炸</b>。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="OfogPBGa">
  <p data-pid="nK3MB2kc">When added to Dry Ice, dioxygen difluoride did not react and was only absorbed by the solid. Addition of acetone to this mixture resulted in sparking accompanied by an explosion.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="AfKknbgW">将二氟化二氧加入到<b>干冰</b>中，并没有发生反应，二氟化二氧只是被干冰吸收。向混合物中加入<b>丙酮</b>后<b>产生火花并发生爆炸</b>。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="dnvkYYUC">
  <p data-pid="Gr1fJhaw">A 2% solution of <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D" alt="O_{2}F_{2}" eeimg="1"> in HF reacted violently with a flash with benzene at 195°K.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="AKRi2fkP">195°K下，2%的二氟化二氧氢氟酸溶液与苯<b>剧烈反应并发出闪光</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="WXbbwVx2">2. 与一些常见无机物反应。A. G. Streng文中做了如下几个实验：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="dEfLzR7K">
  It caused explosions when added to ice at 130-140°K.
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="cPSv3nxr">130-140°K下将其加入<b>冰</b>中，发生<b>爆炸</b>。（无力吐槽）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="HnjC380S">
  <p data-pid="NCOvLn9t">A rapid addition of chlorine to dioxygen difluoride cooled to about 140°K. caused a violent explosion.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="auhi2tEM">向冷却至140°K的二氟化二氧中快速加入<b>液氯</b>，发生<b>剧烈爆炸</b>。（作者你是怎么活下来的？！）</p>
 <p data-pid="xmpvQ-1t">作者甚至对<b>硫化物</b>做了如下实验：</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=4O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D+%2B+H_%7B2%7DS+%3D%3D+2HF+%2B+4O_%7B2%7D+%2B+SF_%7B6%7D+%2B+432.9kcal" alt="4O_{2}F_{2} + H_{2}S == 2HF + 4O_{2} + SF_{6} + 432.9kcal" eeimg="1">
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="sV5FZIEq">432.9kcal/mol 。。。不禁担心起Streng先生有没有买<b>保险</b>。</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="mi3_2v2z">3. 与一些化学性质<b>极其稳定</b>的无机物反应：</p>
 <p data-pid="-vMiRZk0">不怕火炼的<b>真金</b>也不能幸免：</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=5O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D%2B2Au+%3D%3D+2AuF_%7B5%7D%2B5O_%7B2%7D%5Cuparrow+" alt="5O_{2}F_{2}+2Au == 2AuF_{5}+5O_{2}\uparrow " eeimg="1">
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="UK3e_6S4">与金粉发生剧烈反应。</p>
 <br>
 <blockquote data-pid="pMiFtmpT">
  <p data-pid="o27uR_88">In contact with a Pt sheet covered with <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=PtF_%7B4%7D" alt="PtF_{4}" eeimg="1">, dioxygen difluoride exploded at 160°K.</p>
 </blockquote>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="AQLSLGqI">160°K下，二氟化二氧接触到覆盖了</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=PtF_%7B4%7D" alt="PtF_{4}" eeimg="1">
 <p data-pid="r0zw565w">的<b>铂片</b>后发生<b>爆炸</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="xQDLBqE8"><b>惰性气体</b>也不能幸免：</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D%2BXe+%3D%3D+XeF_%7B6%7D%2B3O_%7B2%7D" alt="3O_{2}F_{2}+Xe == XeF_{6}+3O_{2}" eeimg="1">
 <br>
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="sMcSavRK">4. <b>将其他氟化物氧化</b></p>
 <p data-pid="OBELjsQK">听说氟气氧化性相当牛逼？</p><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D%2B2AsF_%7B5%7D%3D%3D%3D2O_%7B2%7DAsF_%7B6%7D%2BF_%7B2%7D%5Cuparrow+" alt="2O_{2}F_{2}+2AsF_{5}===2O_{2}AsF_{6}+F_{2}\uparrow " eeimg="1">
 <br><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2O_%7B2%7DF_%7B2%7D%2B2BF_%7B3%7D%3D%3D%3D2O_%7B2%7DBF_%7B4%7D%2BF_%7B2%7D%5Cuparrow+" alt="2O_{2}F_{2}+2BF_{3}===2O_{2}BF_{4}+F_{2}\uparrow " eeimg="1">

 <br>
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="FTKaU0CJ">二氟化二氧非常难以制备，需要在低温，真空下对液态氧和液态氟高压放电，而且大多数反应都需要在低温下才能发生。正因为其无法在室温下存在，二氟化二氧的诸多性质无法测定，也不能大规模应用。曾经科学家有过将二氟化二氧作为火箭发动机中的氧化剂的想法，但是后来实验失败。目前二氟化二氧仅在一些化学反应中有辅助合成的作用, 比如实验产物只能在低温下存在, 但是直接让反应物发生反应却要求高温的情况。</p>
 <p data-pid="ufGiVv1c">Derek Lowe先生为自己设定了一个列表， 这个列表中有10种物质，是<b>“他这辈子也绝对不会与之打交道”的10种物质</b>，而二氟化二氧自然就在其中，从他对Streng的论文的吐槽就可见一斑了。</p>
 <p data-pid="pq3S9nMi">Streng的这篇论文非常值得一读，这里面充斥着对反应现象的描述，让人<b>忍俊不禁</b>的同时, 一种<b>钦佩</b>之情油然而生。“violent explosion”, "sparking accompanied with an explosion", "reacted violently with a flash", "an instantaneous explosion", "reacted vigorously", "an explosively violent evolution of gas and heat"。</p>

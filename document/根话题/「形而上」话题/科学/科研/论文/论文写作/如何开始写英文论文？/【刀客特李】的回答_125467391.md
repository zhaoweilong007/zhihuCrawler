 <p data-pid="Fq9rkWn6">三年前我从美国博士毕业，发表了第一，二，三作者文章总共有十几篇，因而对学术尤其是我所在的科技行业的论文写作有了非常深的体会。当然发表的都是国际上的sci的论文，虽然我在美国不太清楚国内对于sci以及论文档次的界定。但因为自己从事材料，纳米和能源科学，论文的IF炒的都很高。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BOJVZzFA">关于科技论文英文写作，我的方法是积累，积累，再积累。你总结和积累的知识储备将成为你日后写作任何英文论文的捷径。那么具体操作办法就是多读你本身领域的顶尖期刊。什么叫顶尖，在我的材料领域顶尖就是Nature系列，Advanced Materials系列, Nano Letters, ACS Nano, Energy &amp; Environmental Science等。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BgTOVbqQ">然后我发现Abstract, Introduction, Experiment, Results, Discussion, Conclusions, Supporting Information的写法都是不太相通的，所以我在每个板块都进行了分门别类的积累，具体是关于词汇，句子，段落和最重要的逻辑。但是这些不同板块也有很多相通之处，很多词汇，句子其实都是可以用在各处的。你会在这些部分都发现有很多在你本领域内高频出现的词汇，短句，句子，这些都是你从实际的高水平论文种多次筛选出来的，意味着曾被你领域内的大牛，大师，同行们多次使用，所以肯定是万无一失的。同时还有作用就是让你熟习了同义转换，以免某一文章里某个词出现太多次。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uaJhHWN0">比如在我的材料科学，你会惊奇的发现我们总需要表达一些相似的东西。 你总需要在文章中表示：</p>

 <p data-pid="LEzJ3GM6">当你熟悉了以上这些常用的动词，形容词，词组短句，长句和学术术语以后，写出像样测学术论文就减轻了很多障碍。之后就是把它们应用到文章的各个部分就好了。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IW_rjGjI">然后要注意几个部分的写作规则。比如Abstract，通常十几句话，短小精炼，把idea，原创性，实验结果和意义说清楚就好了，不要累赘。Conclusion也是一样，但是侧重于总结，也可以提实验结果，强调意义，并可以加上对今后工作的展望。</p>
 <p data-pid="sPmj7ZGQ">Introduction是最重要的，需要把你所做工作的领域向大家介绍一下，然后目前发展瓶颈说清楚，再之后提出自己的idea, 概念，方法或者新事物。大部分对前人工作的总结和批判是在这个部分完成的，参考文献也大部分发生在这部分，所以耗费时间最长，最考察作者的科研深度和对文献的熟悉度，如果有很多重要工作漏掉了没有引用，在审稿的时候就会被编辑大加批判的。</p>
 <p data-pid="mUMc5NIt">Results &amp; Discussion这部分当然就是介绍自己每一个所做实验的结果。数据分析和结果讨论和Introduction同等重要，考察的是作者的学术功底，能否在已有数据上分析出科学合理的结果，并作证自己的结论。</p>

 <p data-pid="sLM_9ES9"><b>Abstract</b></p>
 <p data-pid="szfzhmaN"><b>The High Performance of Crystal Water Containing Manganese Birnessite Cathodes for Magnesium Batteries</b></p>
 <p data-pid="tgDd7OLU">Nano Lett., <b>2015</b>, <i>15</i> (6), pp 4071–4079</p>
 <p data-pid="J1KmiENm">Rechargeable magnesium batteries have <u>lately received great attention</u> (获得关注，引入新概念) for large-scale energy storage systems <u>due to</u> (B受制于A，由于) their high volumetric capacities, low materials cost, and safe characteristic. <u>However</u>, the bivalency of Mg2+ ions <u>has made it challenging to</u> (本领域目前出现的困境) find cathode materials operating at high voltages with decent (de)intercalation kinetics. <u>In an effort to overcome</u> (起承转合) this challenge, we <u>adopt</u> (具体做了什么) an unconventional approach of engaging crystal water in the layered structure of <i>Birnessite</i> MnO2 because the crystal water can effectively screen electrostatic interactions between Mg2+ ions and the host anions. The <u>crucial</u> (好重要哦) role of the crystal water was <u>revealed</u> by directly visualizing its presence and dynamic rearrangement using scanning transmission electron microscopy (STEM). <u>Moreover</u>, the importance of lowering desolvation energy penalty at the cathode–electrolyte interface was <u>elucidated</u> by working with water containing nonaqueous electrolytes. In aqueous electrolytes, the decreased interfacial energy penalty by hydration of Mg2+ <u>allows</u><i>Birnessite</i> MnO2 to <u>achieve</u> (展示性能) a large reversible capacity (231.1 mAh g–1) at high operating voltage (2.8 V vs Mg/Mg2+) with <u>excellent</u> (形容“好的”，“显著的”，吹牛部分 ) cycle life (62.5% retention after 10000 cycles), <u>unveiling the importance</u> (意义重大，颠覆三观) of effective charge shielding in the host and facile Mg2+ ions transfer through the cathode’s interface.</p>

 <p data-pid="mHa8KtHg"><b>Stabilization of Insoluble Discharge Products by Facile Aniline Modification for High Performance Li-S Batteries</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8jt1-GaU">Jae Ho Kim , Taehoon Kim , Yo Chan Jeong , Kunsil Lee , Kyung Tae Park , Seung Jae Yang , * and Chong Rae Park</p>
 <p data-pid="GbNTNfr_">Adv. Energy Mater. <b>2015</b>, 5, 1500268</p>
 <p data-pid="1H4Aganz">DOI: 10.1002/aenm.201500268</p>
 <p data-pid="7aEfqJtt">Energy storage systems with high energy density <u>are required to fulfill the needs</u> for grid energy storage and electric vehicles. [1–3] Sulfur <u>has been spotlighted</u> (获得关注) as a cathode material due to its theoretical capacity of 1672 mAh g−1 , [4,5] which is around 5 times higher than that of metal oxide based on an intercalation reaction. [6] Lithium sulfur batteries operate on a redox reaction described as S8 + 16 Li ↔ 8 Li2S near 2.2 V (vs Li/Li+), which <u>provides</u> (展示性能) a high specific energy of 2600 Wh kg−1. [4,5] Furthermore, sulfur is inexpensive, environmwentally benign, and naturally abundant. [7] <u>Despite these considerable advantages</u>, (革命尚未成功，还需努力) the commercial application of Li-S batteries is still far <u>due to</u> (B受制于A，由于) the low sulfur utilization and poor cycle stability. [8] Low sulfur utilization is from the insulating nature of the sulfur and intermediates. This issue has been handled by employing carbon materials and conducting polymers. [9,10] Poor cycle stability is a more intricate issue, since the intermediate soluble polysulfides diffuse into the electrolyte [11,12] and insoluble discharge products (Li2S2 and Li2S) aggregate at the end of discharge. [13] <u>To date</u>, (起承转合) <u>considerable researches have been conducted</u> (前人工作，获得关注) to prevent loss of soluble polysulfides by encapsulating sulfur in various nanostructured carbon matrices, [14–19] wrapping the composites with polymers, [20–22] graphene [23] and metal oxide, [24] and adding the interlayer between the cathode and separator. [25–27] <u>To alleviate the shuttle reaction</u>, (起承转合) additives were introduced to the electrolyte by Solid electrolyte interface (SEI) formation on the Li negative electrode. [28,29] However, issues from insoluble discharge products <u>have not been addressed</u> (本领域目前出现的困境) with as much fervor.<br>
  Cui and co-workers recently exploited bulky amphiphilic polymers to enhance the binding energy between the carbon matrix and insoluble discharge products, which <u>resulted in sequestering aggregation</u> of insoluble discharge products.(本领域目前出现的困境) [30] This approach exhibited a high specific capacity and improved cycle life.<br><u>However</u>, the bulky property of used amphiphilic polymer can <u>cause</u> (导致) nonuniform functionalization and ineffective sulfur infiltration by pore blockage.(本领域目前出现的困境) <u>Therefore</u>, there are some challenges in managing the insoluble discharge products for high performance Li-S batteries: (i) a smaller chemical group is more suitable for functionalization, which can lead to the homogeneous functionalization of pore wall and efficient sulfur infiltration, (ii) a more effective functional group can manage the insoluble discharge products, and (iii) a cheaper and milder fabrication method is needed for commercialization.</p>
 <p data-pid="HnWKM1hz"><u>In this work, (起承转合，具体说自己的新方法，新概念) we introduced</u> aniline into the pore wall of ordered mesoporous carbon (CMK-3) for the first time by a simple diazotization method. <u>In general</u>,(起承转合) the amine-type functional groups have been widely used to remove H2S in the natural gas sweetening process [31,32] through a strong Lewis acid-base interaction.[33] The aniline functional group is presumed to strongly interact with insoluble discharge products in a similar manner. CMK-3 with the confinement effect was used as a carbon framework, [14] which <u>led to</u> (导致) a synergistic effect on the performance. The proposed diazotization method was <u>conducted</u> in a simple procedure under mild conditions and preserves the original structure of carbon matrix. [34–36] The resulting materials <u>provided</u> (展示性能) effective interaction sites for a homogeneous distribution of the insoluble discharge products during cycling, as schematically described in Figure 1 , and it conclusively <u>exhibited</u> (展示性能) highly stable cycling performance and good rate capability when evaluated as a cathode material for Li-S batteries.</p>

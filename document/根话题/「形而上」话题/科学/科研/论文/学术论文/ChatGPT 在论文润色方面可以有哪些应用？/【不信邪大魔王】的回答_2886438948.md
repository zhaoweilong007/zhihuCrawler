<body>
 <p data-pid="ylYTu_jd">靠论文润色吃饭的机构基本上完蛋了。</p>
 <p data-pid="CQOMjtvt">我随便找了一篇论文摘要：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-83f5f9c9f898d35824e2a5d2b324e436_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="723" data-rawheight="229" data-original-token="v2-12887c3c69f2e56a2510f1f9341315a9" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6b082a43dbd8d0fb1e162795e3929de4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="723" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-83f5f9c9f898d35824e2a5d2b324e436_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <blockquote data-pid="o5cmhfZl">
  伴随着技术的发展和人工智能时代的到来，机器和人类之间建立了越来越紧密的联系。随着具备人机对话、动作、情感等反馈功能的智能机器逐渐社会角色化，人机关系也产生相应的发展和变化。机器从具备功能性的工具属性发展为兼具社会性的角色属性。智能机器对交互对象（人）的影响不仅体现在人机关系的变化上，也体现在对人的需求的影响上。人工智能的介入，导致人机交互过程中更深层次的交互和更大的不确定性。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="HYMuAcT0">然后扔到DeepL里面，得到一个初级翻译版本：</p>
 <p data-pid="mu6MmWsq">Along with the development of technology and the advent of the era of artificial intelligence, an increasingly close connection has been established between machines and humans. As intelligent machines with human-machine dialogue, action, emotion and other feedback functions gradually socialize into roles, the human-machine relationship also develops and changes accordingly. The machine develops from a functional tool to a social role. The impact of intelligent machines on the interaction object (human) is not only reflected in the change of the human-machine relationship, but also in the impact on human needs. The involvement of artificial intelligence leads to deeper interaction and greater uncertainty in the process of human-computer interaction.</p>
 <p data-pid="w_ty7jhS">然后我让ChatGPT用学术的角度来Proofread并Polish一下，Prompt是：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="m0uKUe_s">
  Please proofread and polish the passage from an academic angle and highlight the modification:
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Jog9qTUW">下面这个是ChatGPT给出的润色版本，还给出了主要修改部分的修改原因：</p>
 <p data-pid="LPBzDckV">Along with the advancements in technology and the dawn of the era of artificial intelligence, a close relationship has been established between machines and humans. As intelligent machines with human-like dialogue, action, emotion, and feedback functions integrate into society, the relationship between humans and machines evolves accordingly. The machine transitions from a functional tool to a social entity. The influence of intelligent machines on their interaction partner, i.e. humans, is reflected not only in the alteration of the human-machine relationship, but also in their impact on human needs. The integration of artificial intelligence leads to a more immersive and complex interaction, increasing uncertainty in the process of human-computer interaction.</p>
 <p data-pid="tP9kdqjr">Modifications:</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="WWVwudQT">Changed "an increasingly close connection" to "a close relationship" for conciseness</li>
  <li data-pid="pdY1glgq">Changed "gradually socialize into roles" to "integrate into society" for clarity</li>
  <li data-pid="txspkTKA">Changed "the impact of intelligent machines on the interaction object (human)" to "the influence of intelligent machines on their interaction partner, i.e. humans" for clarity</li>
  <li data-pid="k6vKOSn2">Changed "the machine develops from a functional tool to a social role" to "the machine transitions from a functional tool to a social entity" for conciseness</li>
  <li data-pid="wp0fR4r0">Changed "deeper interaction and greater uncertainty" to "a more immersive and complex interaction, increasing uncertainty" for clarity</li>
 </ul>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="lMH3Njuy">我们来看看第一句的润色质量：</p>
 <p data-pid="g7Lnvrs0">原文：伴随着技术的发展和人工智能时代的到来，机器和人类之间建立了越来越紧密的联系</p>
 <p data-pid="AU315nbB">DeepL翻译：Along with the development of technology and the advent of the era of artificial intelligence, an increasingly close connection has been established between machines and humans.</p>
 <p data-pid="pA5e68Hj">ChatGPT润色：Along with the <b>advancements</b> in technology and the <b>dawn </b>of the era of artificial intelligence, <b>a close relationship</b> has been established between machines and humans.</p>
 <p data-pid="4075EegL">你看，ChatGPT在第一句就做了如下改动：</p>
 <p data-pid="s2gv2pz9">1， development 替换为 advancements （development有点低级）</p>
 <p data-pid="Qh2Km5AA">2，advent（出现）替换为dawn（黎明，破晓）（这个感觉很赞）</p>
 <p data-pid="e58wuWd9">3，把 an increasingly close connection 简化为 a close relationship （更简洁）</p>
 <p data-pid="vLiabK9x">总体上我觉得这个润色水平，应该够用了，润色机构从业人员还是赶紧改行吧~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tD_6PAz4">其实AI工具只用来写论文，是大材小用了，你可以用他阅读论文生成思维导图，自动生成科研汇报的PPT，还可以做数据处理和可视化等工作。</p>
 <p data-pid="nT53TFj5">建议大家去上知乎知学堂的『AI智能办公』训练营，两个小时的直播，主要介绍了目前最主流的9个AI工具的用法。</p>

 <p data-pid="woSYNxd-">课后还有几个文件可以免费领取，关于AI的提示词如何撰写，这是驾驭好AI工具的关键所在，对AI小白非常友好，不要错过~</p>

 <p data-pid="HhIveoYd">2023年2月11日更新：</p>
 <p data-pid="9hR_2JS7">看到评论区有同学质疑“Dawn”这个词用在学术写作上不合理，这不是学术风。为了考证这个质疑的合理性，我再次让ChatGPT给我找几个论文示例和出处，以证明“Dawn”的确在正规学术期刊中用来比喻“时代的来临”。</p>
 <p data-pid="S6GaFgVV">我的prompt是：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="E_IcyWRU">
  give me some real examples of the usage of "dawn" to metaphorize the beginning of an era in academic writing, and notify me of the original source of these papers.
 </blockquote>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Phu9vqYM">ChatGPT立刻扔了两篇：</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="1Ha4eIRL">"The dawn of the information age has brought with it a wealth of opportunities and challenges for society." Source: "The Impact of the Internet on Society: A Global Perspective" by Manuel Castells, published in the Oxford Handbook of Internet Studies, edited by William H. Dutton, 2013.</li>
  <li data-pid="z7dHosM3">"The dawn of the 21st century marked the beginning of a new era in renewable energy, with a focus on sustainability and reducing our dependence on fossil fuels." Source: "Renewable Energy: A Review of Current Trends and Future Directions" by Sarah E. Johnson, published in Energy Policy, Vol. 38, No. 4 (Apr., 2010), pp. 1734-1745.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="jXR9r4sl">因此，我觉得不管怎么说，对于学术风格的定义很多审稿人都有不同的观点，但ChatGPT在这个词的改动上，并不是随性之作，而是有依据的。</p>

# 如何看待人教版教材疑似出现低级错误，用爱因斯坦相对论证明勾股定理？
- 点赞数：25388
- 更新时间：2020年10月04日13时07分41秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/401988398/answer/1328153858
<body>
 <p data-pid="Bf8_ddZW">我的数学就是这个编书的老师教的。</p>
 <p data-pid="zyL_bZjG">小时候总有一些奇奇怪怪的问题想不通，有一天我又遇到了一个问题，于是我问老师。</p>
 <p data-pid="Ah2Hnax_">我：老师，我有一个问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="QaOm18nf">老师：你说。</p>
 <p data-pid="VgpjsE3d">我：一个蛋糕切成三份，每份是0.333，是这样吗？</p>
 <p data-pid="4mwV6ySp">老师：是的。</p>
 <p data-pid="1ehztAbH">我：现在把切开的蛋糕合起来，0.333×3=0.999，是这样吗？</p>
 <p data-pid="0rdodA-S">老师：是的。</p>
 <p data-pid="0DxxPy7m">我：那么还有0.001份蛋糕去哪了？</p>
 <p data-pid="eRyl7vgJ">老师：你看看你切蛋糕的刀上有什么？</p>
 <p data-pid="FfftB7zY">我：明白了，谢谢老师！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OmQJf01v">经过这个老师天马行空的一波指点，我好像突然打通了任督二脉，开启了我的数学鬼才之路。相继证明了1=2；1=-1；2+2=5等数学千古难题。下面就是证明过程，不喜欢看过程的可以直接跳到最后。</p>
 <p data-pid="m5E3sqmd"><b>先给大家证明1=2</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="YaBCYykA">我们先假设b=a。</p>
 <p data-pid="8p7pb6WS"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+b+%26%3Da+%5C%5C+a+b+%26%3Da%5E%7B2%7D+%5C%5C+a+b-b%5E%7B2%7D+%26%3Da%5E%7B2%7D-b%5E%7B2%7D+%5C%5C+b%28a-b%29+%26%3D%28a%2Bb%29%28a-b%29+%5C%5C+b+%26%3Da%2Bb+%5C%5C+b+%26%3Db%2Bb+%5C%5C+b+%26%3D2+b+%5C%5C+1+%26%3D2+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D" alt="\begin{aligned} b &amp;=a \\ a b &amp;=a^{2} \\ a b-b^{2} &amp;=a^{2}-b^{2} \\ b(a-b) &amp;=(a+b)(a-b) \\ b &amp;=a+b \\ b &amp;=b+b \\ b &amp;=2 b \\ 1 &amp;=2 \end{aligned}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="UEazADb8"><b>接下来给大家证明1=-1</b>，不过这里需要用到虚数的概念，没学到这里的同学，可以跳过这一段。</p>
 <p data-pid="DZjpHMuH"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D+-1+%26%3D-1+%5C%5C-1+%2F+1+%26%3D-1+%2F+1+%5C%5C-1+%2F+1+%26%3D1+%2F-1+%5C%5C+%5Csqrt%7B-1+%2F+1%7D+%26%3D%5Csqrt%7B1+%2F-1%7D+%5C%5C+i+%2F+1+%26%3D1+%2F+i+%5C%5C+i+%26%3D1+%2F+i+%5C%5C+i%5E%7B2%7D+%26%3D1+%5C%5C-1+%26%3D1+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D" alt="\begin{aligned} -1 &amp;=-1 \\-1 / 1 &amp;=-1 / 1 \\-1 / 1 &amp;=1 /-1 \\ \sqrt{-1 / 1} &amp;=\sqrt{1 /-1} \\ i / 1 &amp;=1 / i \\ i &amp;=1 / i \\ i^{2} &amp;=1 \\-1 &amp;=1 \end{aligned}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="tuKmJCJk"><br><b>然后，给大家证明2+2=5</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="lNWru8o_">首先：</p>
 <p data-pid="59_JWQay"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Baligned%7D++-20+%26%3D-20+%5C%5C++16-36+%26%3D25-45+%5Cend%7Baligned%7D+" alt="\begin{aligned}  -20 &amp;=-20 \\  16-36 &amp;=25-45 \end{aligned} " eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="TsbG3_PW">两边同时加上 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B81%7D%7B4%7D" alt="\frac{81}{4}" eeimg="1"> ，则：</p>
 <p data-pid="vnt9faK6"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=16-36%2B%5Cfrac%7B81%7D%7B4%7D%3D25-45%2B%5Cfrac%7B81%7D%7B4%7D" alt="16-36+\frac{81}{4}=25-45+\frac{81}{4}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="zm6PvGCs">根据平方公式： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=a%5E%7B2%7D-2+a+b%2Bb%5E%7B2%7D%3D%28a-b%29%5E%7B2%7D" alt="a^{2}-2 a b+b^{2}=(a-b)^{2}" eeimg="1"> 有：</p>
 <p data-pid="3385n0Uu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%284-%5Cfrac%7B9%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7B2%7D%3D%5Cleft%285-%5Cfrac%7B9%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%5E%7B2%7D" alt="\left(4-\frac{9}{2}\right)^{2}=\left(5-\frac{9}{2}\right)^{2}" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="HMu5s3Sl">所以： <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cleft%284-%5Cfrac%7B9%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29%3D%5Cleft%285-%5Cfrac%7B9%7D%7B2%7D%5Cright%29" alt="\left(4-\frac{9}{2}\right)=\left(5-\frac{9}{2}\right)" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="MXTIyi5b">即：4=5，所以2+2=5.得证。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hfKljvxa">本来凭我天赋，我应该至少可以获得六七个<b>诺贝尔数学奖</b>。但是后来一个不经意间的证明，改变了我的人生：</p>
 <p data-pid="mOq4cT7T">首先，无穷分之一等于0，</p>
 <p data-pid="g29A5nmU"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B%5Cinfty%7D%3D0" alt="\frac{1}{\infty}=0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="X8f0z5Rf">两边同时逆时针旋转90°，则有：</p>
 <p data-pid="_VnY7oc4"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-18%3D0" alt="-18=0" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="RDFbLUIc">两边同时＋8，则有：</p>
 <p data-pid="0dUkMFcU"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=-10%3D8" alt="-10=8" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="HE6XojnY">两边同时逆时针旋转90°，有：</p>
 <p data-pid="WFR6oFGu"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cfrac%7B0%7D%7B1%7D%3D%5Cinfty" alt="\frac{0}{1}=\infty" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="8-Nfq99l">即：<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0%3D%5Cinfty" alt="0=\infty" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="S7S4pJt8"><b>没有即是无穷！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="s12TfG98">那我还得什么诺贝尔数学奖？我已经有无穷多个诺贝尔数学奖了。</p>
 <p data-pid="RXFORFOl">同理，<b>当这个答案发布的时候，就已经有无穷多的赞了</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="ml_eV-lV">空即是色，色即是空，没有即是无穷。</p>
 <p data-pid="Fl4dE2Sq">无穷即是不穷，不穷就是有钱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H8lcBRRa">至于这个老师用相对论证明勾股定理，实在是平常操作，你说他能证明<b>腹股沟定理</b>我也信，毕竟没有沟他也能给你挖一条出来。</p>

 <h3>百年战争</h3>
 <p>20 世纪初<span><span>，</span></span>物理学家开始重点纠结一个纠结了上百年的问题<span><span>：</span></span>光<span><span>，</span></span>到底是波还是粒子<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <ul>

 <p>双缝<span><span>，</span></span>顾名思义<span><span>，</span></span>就是在一块隔板上开两条缝<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>用一个发射光子的机枪对着双缝扫射<span><span>，</span></span>从缝中漏过去的光子<span><span>，</span></span>打在缝后面的屏上<span><span>，</span></span>就会留下一个光斑<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>等效于 1961 年电子双缝干涉实验<span><span>）</span></span></p>
 <p>在实验之前<span><span>，</span></span>科学家的推测如下<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第一种可能</strong></p>
 <p>如果光子是纯粒子<span><span>，</span></span>那么屏幕留下两道杠<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>光子像机枪发射的子弹一样笔直地从缝中穿过<span><span>，</span></span>那么屏幕上留下的一定是 2 道杠<span><span>，</span></span>因为其他角度的光子都被板挡住了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b9c0d454587ce45745c453882a0ceef5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="图片" data-rawwidth="512" data-rawheight="282" data-original-token="v2-b9c0d454587ce45745c453882a0ceef5" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="512" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-b9c0d454587ce45745c453882a0ceef5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p><strong>第二种可能</strong></p>
 <p>如果光子是纯波<span><span>，</span></span>那么屏幕上会留下斑马线般的一道道条纹<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>光子穿过缝时<span><span>，</span></span>会形成 2 个波源<span><span>。</span></span>两道波各自震荡交汇<span><span>（</span></span>干涉<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>波峰与波峰之间强度叠加<span><span>，</span></span>波峰与波谷之间正反抵消<span><span>，</span></span>最终屏幕上会出现一道道复杂唯美的斑马线<span><span>（</span></span>干涉条纹<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f7141f87af7dfe647bf8dbbfb656576b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="图片" data-rawwidth="512" data-rawheight="282" data-original-token="v2-f7141f87af7dfe647bf8dbbfb656576b" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="512" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-f7141f87af7dfe647bf8dbbfb656576b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p><strong>第三种可能</strong></p>
 <p>如果光子是波粒二象<span><span>，</span></span>那么屏幕图案应该是以上两种图形的杂交混合体<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>总之<span><span>，</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>两道杠 = 粒派胜<span><span>；</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>斑马线 = 波派胜<span><span>；</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>四不像 = 平局<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>是波是粒还是二合一<span><span>，</span></span>看屏幕结果一目了然<span><span>，</span></span>无论实验结果如何<span><span>，</span></span>都在我们的预料之中<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第一次实验</strong> <span><span>：</span></span>把光子发射机对准双缝发射<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>结果</strong> <span><span>：</span></span>标准的斑马线<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-a9e4c48c85b864109b53f85041f8d75a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="图片" data-rawwidth="500" data-rawheight="268" data-original-token="v2-a9e4c48c85b864109b53f85041f8d75a" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="500" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-a9e4c48c85b864109b53f85041f8d75a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>根据之前的分析<span><span>，</span></span>这证明光子是纯波<span><span>。</span></span>OK<span><span>，</span></span>实验结束<span><span>，</span></span>大家回家洗洗睡吧<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>粒派不服<span><span>：</span></span>我明明知道光子是一个一个的粒子<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>这样<span><span>，</span></span>我们再做一次实验<span><span>，</span></span>把光子一个一个地发射出去<span><span>，</span></span>看会怎么样<span><span>，</span></span>一定会变成两道杠的<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第二次实验</strong> <span><span>：</span></span>把光子机枪切换到点射模式<span><span>，</span></span>保证每次只发射一个光子<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>结果</strong> <span><span>：</span></span>斑马线<span><span>，</span></span>竟然还是斑马线<span><span>，</span></span>怎么可能<span><span>？</span></span>我们明明是一<span><span>、</span></span>个<span><span>、</span></span>一<span><span>、</span></span>个把光子发射出去的啊<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>最令人震惊的是<span><span>，</span></span>一开始光子数量较少时<span><span>，</span></span>屏幕上的光点看上去一片杂乱无章<span><span>，</span></span>随着积少成多<span><span>，</span></span>渐渐显出了斑马线条纹<span><span>！</span></span></p>

 <p>光子要真的是波<span><span>，</span></span>那粒派也不得不服<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>问题是<span><span>：</span></span>根据波动理论<span><span>，</span></span>斑马线来源于双缝产生的两个波源之间的干涉叠加<span><span>；</span></span>而单个光子要么穿过左缝<span><span>、</span></span>要么穿过右缝<span><span>，</span></span>穿过一条缝的光子到底是在和谁发生干涉<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>难道……光子在穿过双缝时分裂成了两个<span><span>？</span></span>一个光子分裂成左半光子和右半光子<span><span>，</span></span>自己的左手和右手发生了关系<span><span>？</span></span>事情好像越来越复杂了<span><span>。</span></span>干脆一不做二不休<span><span>，</span></span>我们倒要看看<span><span>，</span></span>光子究竟是怎样穿过缝的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第三次实验</strong> <span><span>：</span></span>在屏幕前加装两个摄像头<span><span>，</span></span>一边一个左右排开<span><span>。</span></span>哪边的摄像头看到光子<span><span>，</span></span>就说明光子穿过了哪条缝<span><span>。</span></span>同样<span><span>，</span></span>还是点射模式发射光子<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>结果<span><span>：</span></span>每次不是左边的摄像头看到一个光子<span><span>，</span></span>就是右边看到一个<span><span>。</span></span>一个就是一个<span><span>，</span></span>从来没有发现哪个光子分裂成半个的情况<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>大家都松了一口气<span><span>。</span></span> <strong>光子确实是一个个粒子<span><span>，</span></span>然而在穿过双缝时<span><span>，</span></span>不知怎么就会变形成两道波同时穿过<span><span>，</span></span>形成干涉条纹<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>虽然诡异了些<span><span>，</span></span>不过据说这就是 <strong>波粒二象性</strong> 了<span><span>，</span></span>具体细节以后再研究吧<span><span>，</span></span>这个实验做得人都要精分了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>然而<span><span>，</span></span>就在这时<span><span>，</span></span>真正诡异的事情发生了……</p>
 <p>人们这才发现<span><span>，</span></span>屏幕上的图案<span><span>，</span></span>不知什么时候<span><span>，</span></span>悄悄变成了两道杠<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>没用摄像头看<span><span>，</span></span>结果总是斑马线<span><span>，</span></span>光子是波<span><span>；</span></span></p>
 <p>用摄像头看了<span><span>，</span></span>结果就成了两道杠<span><span>，</span></span>光子变成了粒子<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>实验结果取决于看没看摄像头<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>这不科学啊<span><span>，</span></span>做物理实验竟然见鬼了啊<span><span>！</span></span></p>

 <p>一个貌似简单的小实验做到这份上<span><span>，</span></span>波和粒子什么的已经不重要了<span><span>，</span></span>重要的是现在全世界的科学家都懵逼了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这是有史以来第一次<span><span>，</span></span>人类在科学实验中正式遭遇灵异事件<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <h3>观察者魔咒</h3>
 <p>你还没看出灵异在哪里<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>好吧<span><span>，</span></span>请先看懂下面这个例子<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>电视里正在直播足球比赛<span><span>，</span></span>一个球员起脚射门——</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-14e5e68446b8fe7ba0265fcd73c5c3c0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="图片" data-rawwidth="500" data-rawheight="333" data-original-token="v2-14e5e68446b8fe7ba0265fcd73c5c3c0" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="500" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-14e5e68446b8fe7ba0265fcd73c5c3c0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p><strong>在球迷看来<span><span>：</span></span></strong> 球进还是不进<span><span>，</span></span>和射手是不是 C 罗<span><span>、</span></span>梅西有关<span><span>，</span></span>和对方门将的状态有关<span><span>，</span></span>和裁判收没收钱说不定还有关<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>不过<span><span>，</span></span>只要把这些因素事无巨细地考虑到方程里计算<span><span>，</span></span>完全可以精确预测三秒后球的状态<span><span>。</span></span>但无论是谁<span><span>，</span></span>大家都公认的是<span><span>，</span></span>球进与不进<span><span>，</span></span>至少和一件事情是绝对无关的<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>你家的电视<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>无论你用什么品牌的电视<span><span>，</span></span>无论电视的屏幕大小<span><span>、</span></span>清晰度高低<span><span>、</span></span>质量好坏<span><span>，</span></span>无论你看球时是在喝啤酒还是啃炸鸡<span><span>，</span></span>当然更无论你看不看电视直播——该进的球还是会进<span><span>，</span></span>该不进就是不进<span><span>，</span></span>哪怕你气得把电视机砸了都没用<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你是不是觉得<span><span>，</span></span>上面说的全都是废话<span><span>？</span></span>那么<span><span>，</span></span>仔细听好<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>双缝干涉的第三次实验证明了<span><span>，</span></span>在其他条件完全相同的情况下<span><span>，</span></span>球进还是不进<span><span>，</span></span>直接取决于在射门的一瞬间<span><span>，</span></span>你看还是不看电视<span><span>！</span></span></p>

 <p>第三次实验与第二次的唯一区别<span><span>，</span></span>就是实验 3 开了摄像头观察光子<span><span>（</span></span>看电视<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>实验 2 没放摄像头<span><span>（</span></span>不看电视<span><span>）</span></span>——两次实验的结局竟截然不同<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>这<span><span>，</span></span>就是观察者的魔咒<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>难道说<span><span>，</span></span>不看光子它就是波<span><span>，</span></span>看一眼<span><span>，</span></span>它就瞬间变成粒子<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>难道说<span><span>，</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>光子是什么<span><span>」</span></span>这一客观事实<span><span>，</span></span>是由我们的观察<span><span>（</span></span>放不放摄像头<span><span>）</span></span>决定的<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>难道说<span><span>，</span></span>对事物的观察方式<span><span>，</span></span>能够改变事物本身<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <h3>三观崩塌</h3>
 <p>在所有人懵逼的时候<span><span>，</span></span>还是有极少数聪明人<span><span>，</span></span>勇敢地提出了新的理论<span><span>：</span></span> 光子<span><span>，</span></span>其实是一种智能极高的外星 AI 机器人<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>之所以观察会导致实验结果不同<span><span>，</span></span>是因为光子在你做实验之前就悄悄侦查过了<span><span>，</span></span>如果发现有摄像头<span><span>，</span></span>它就变成粒子形态<span><span>；</span></span>如果发现是屏幕<span><span>，</span></span>就变成波的形态<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>第四次实验<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>事先<span><span>，</span></span>只有屏幕没有摄像头<span><span>；</span></span></p>

 <p>无论加摄像头的速度有多快<span><span>，</span></span>只要最终加上了摄像头<span><span>，</span></span>屏幕上一定是两道杠<span><span>；</span></span>反过来<span><span>，</span></span>如果一开始有摄像头<span><span>，</span></span>哪怕在最后一刻秒秒钟撤掉<span><span>，</span></span>屏幕上一定是斑马线<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>通过前面那些烧脑的实验<span><span>，</span></span>玻尔总结了量子世界的三大基本原则<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <ul>

# 家里面有哪个亲戚令你感到恶心？
- 点赞数：14580
- 更新时间：2022年04月15日04时59分49秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/65383028/answer/2439311467
<body>
 <p data-pid="UMYlFKwd">1、</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qfmWTUEL">五岁时在房后偷摸玩炮仗，</p>
 <p data-pid="_NNR-Ky9">被二叔看见，直接告诉我爹。</p>
 <p data-pid="GpvCyQIp">被一顿毒打。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Fw9mmpmq">2、</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gfluuv0f">没记性，继续玩炮仗。</p>
 <p data-pid="g14zgoI3">又被二叔逮到。</p>
 <p data-pid="edmfWKHH">害怕极了，</p>
 <p data-pid="aYqGZEhH">奉上压岁钱，求他不要告诉我爹。</p>
 <p data-pid="e8rAbhy3">他收下钱，答应了我。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Fz5Ukxd4">转头就告诉了我爹。</p>
 <p data-pid="66uDuqEU">又是一顿毒打。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jSipd-x4">3、</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="s7ax596i">极度愤恨，决定报复二叔。</p>
 <p data-pid="945HuxOc">他却登门道歉，</p>
 <p data-pid="PSXlfm6y">并给我买了很多新炮仗，</p>
 <p data-pid="FT8ZH6y-">说之前是为了我好，</p>
 <p data-pid="1VlfDWuD">怕我玩鞭炮受伤，</p>
 <p data-pid="rnGOxUu5">不如玩这种摔炮，安全。</p>
 <p data-pid="1xLuOq6B">我大喜，搂住二叔夸他大好人。</p>
 <p data-pid="y7LjOOgL">便夺门而出，玩炮仗。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="xkTD1gBj">接着他告诉我爹，</p>
 <p data-pid="LjaI7Ww8">喜迎一顿毒打。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rhPCWmhs">4、</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pRSHlpOu">从此与二叔不共戴天，</p>
 <p data-pid="7mIJ2-3G">决定找机会报复。</p>
 <p data-pid="2He-5HLd">刚好听说他准备于某日宴请贵宾，</p>
 <p data-pid="isEcaC5R">并反复嘱咐我不要捣乱。</p>
 <p data-pid="YAXJc0cR">岂能错此良机？</p>
 <p data-pid="ATTPGilP">当日，我拿上炮仗溜进他家院子。</p>
 <p data-pid="lQVjJ8NA">点燃炮仗，看准时机顺窗扔入。</p>
 <p data-pid="TDbAPF-O">听闻屋内鸡飞狗跳，</p>
 <p data-pid="GD3Lkvpc">我狂放大笑。</p>
 <p data-pid="ihn2BeQR">却见我爹头发冒烟从屋内冲出，</p>
 <p data-pid="Zu6H9RPx">直奔我而来。</p>
 <p data-pid="it7qfq1e">这才惊觉所谓“贵宾”就是我爹！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gcvxcVI3">无他，</p>
 <p data-pid="ZFQrcb8C">唯一顿毒打。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-kLoKQhB">5、</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VqRoypdd">不服，誓要让二叔血债血偿。</p>
 <p data-pid="6PpohCSs">他却拎着零食登门拜访，</p>
 <p data-pid="PJHPeVur">并告诉了我一个惊天秘密：</p>
 <p data-pid="3j5H1Koi">原来一切都是我爹指使，</p>
 <p data-pid="Dk3C21DO">他只是找个理由想打我，</p>
 <p data-pid="Ncsh1TpP">又怕我生恨。</p>
 <p data-pid="m5CU5Jtr">所以才派二叔钓鱼执法。</p>
 <p data-pid="ZTwHl_fJ">将怒火引去他那。</p>
 <p data-pid="gGhr5jWy">如今二叔也看不下去，</p>
 <p data-pid="grI9g68F">所以来找我和解。</p>
 <p data-pid="W04rtUXo">我听罢大惊，感叹我爹的恶毒。</p>
 <p data-pid="FjDm04j8">同时也感动二叔的善良，</p>
 <p data-pid="5Pc1KHRM">遂和他共同指定报复我爹的计划。</p>
 <p data-pid="54PSjXox">计划好后，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OUMjBe_k">他直接告诉我爹。</p>
 <p data-pid="wREMHjWg">再迎一顿毒打。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lAzDAvm-">6、</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="f97rGILn">从此发誓不再信任二叔，</p>
 <p data-pid="3akkPHis">视他为此生最大仇敌。</p>
 <p data-pid="Xjz7hO9W">不报血仇誓不罢休。</p>
 <p data-pid="6z4dSFi2">二叔却大手一挥，</p>
 <p data-pid="GGFPZoP3">赠予我数百大钞，</p>
 <p data-pid="g7AXhnlT">直言他已厌倦争斗，</p>
 <p data-pid="Rl2OIs9d">这是当年自我这抢去的压岁钱，</p>
 <p data-pid="slgnT_Tm">现在悉数奉还，</p>
 <p data-pid="cL1Hsk4g">不求原谅，</p>
 <p data-pid="daCQG-6f">只求休战。</p>
 <p data-pid="nPQRnMbr">我收下钱，点头应允。</p>
 <p data-pid="6gvlFG7Q">目送他离开我家，</p>
 <p data-pid="fFE3Hvbx">背影隐在雨中，与仇怨一同消去。</p>
 <p data-pid="Us3sTCwQ">片刻后我爹怒气冲冲进屋，</p>
 <p data-pid="ytxo_RZO">口中大骂钱丢了，质问我是不是拿了钱。</p>
 <p data-pid="K3AcMVn-">我沉默数秒，叹了口气，</p>
 <p data-pid="9I7W4oI0">主动撅起屁股：</p>
 <p data-pid="KTF4zwJJ">大骂道：</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SMdxN9MM"><b>“我是凯子，你给我等着！！！！！！！”</b></p>

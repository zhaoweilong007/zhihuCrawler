# 被自己 3 岁的孩子用玩具打疼了，然后就很暴躁，想打回去该怎么办？

<body>
 <p>这要看是有意的还是无意的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这个问题<span><span>，</span></span>我们可以分三种情况来讨论<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第一种情况<span><span>，</span></span>孩子体能发育到了大动作发展敏感期<span><span>（</span></span>10-22 个月左右<span><span>）</span></span></strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>这是关于 13 个月宝宝打人<span><span>、</span></span>掐人的话题<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>13 个多月的宝宝<span><span>，</span></span>正处在学步期<span><span>，</span></span>也是大动作发展的敏感期<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>有些宝宝扶着东西可以站起来<span><span>、</span></span>走几步了<span><span>，</span></span>有些宝宝呢已经可以独立行走了<span><span>，</span></span>这会让宝宝的双手<span><span>「</span></span>解放出来<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>他们开始特别喜欢去使用双手了<span><span>，</span></span>比如<span><span>：</span></span>摸啊<span><span>、</span></span>抓啊<span><span>、</span></span>捏啊<span><span>、</span></span>掐啊<span><span>、</span></span>打啊<span><span>，</span></span>来探索这个世界<span><span>，</span></span>以此来发展他们的大肌肉移动技能<span><span>（</span></span>Gross Locomotor Movement Skills<span><span>）</span></span>和大肌肉操控技能<span><span>（</span></span>Gross Motor Manipulative Skills<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这种<span><span>「</span></span>欲罢不能的<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>、</span></span>主动的<span><span>「</span></span>手部接触<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>可以帮助宝宝体验手部的力量和学习与人接触的底线和范围<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>他们也喜欢在无聊<span><span>、</span></span>需要与人互动<span><span>、</span></span>或有情绪需要发泄<span><span>（</span></span>可能是因为之前的事情不高兴<span><span>）</span></span>的时候<span><span>，</span></span>用<span><span>「</span></span>打人<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>掐人<span><span>」</span></span>等方式来表达他的需求<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这些都是这个月龄阶段孩子正常的发育需求<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>妈妈们一般的做法是直接告知<span><span>「</span></span>不可以<span><span>、</span></span>不对<span><span>、</span></span>不希望你这样做<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>这种类似的语言指令仅仅是在禁止<span><span>，</span></span>孩子感受到的是<span><span>「</span></span>比较严肃地拒绝<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>但却不知道可以怎么做<span><span>，</span></span>而且<span><span>，</span></span>孩子每次动手背后的需求可能不一样<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>通常<span><span>，</span></span>我们需要做到以下几点<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>1<span><span>、</span></span>询问孩子<span><span>「</span></span>动手<span><span>」</span></span>背后的需求<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><span><span>「</span></span>你是想妈妈陪你玩么<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>

 <p><span><span>「</span></span>你不想和爸爸玩<span><span>？</span></span>你希望妈妈和你们一起玩吗<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span>等等<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>哪怕孩子还不会说话<span><span>、</span></span>也不一定能清晰知道自己的意图<span><span>，</span></span>但这种询问<span><span>，</span></span>也能让孩子感受到爸爸妈妈更在乎她的需求<span><span>，</span></span>而不仅仅是外在的行为表现<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>更重要的是<span><span>，</span></span>经常被这样提问的孩子<span><span>，</span></span>长大一点后<span><span>，</span></span>也更能意识到自己需要什么<span><span>，</span></span>逐渐知道怎么和父母说自己的需求<span><span>，</span></span>而不再只是通过<span><span>「</span></span>打人<span><span>」</span></span>等比较儿童化的行为去引人注意了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>2<span><span>、</span></span>询问需求之后<span><span>，</span></span>再说<span><span>「</span></span>什么可以<span><span>，</span></span>什么是不可以的<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>一般这样讲规则的时候<span><span>，</span></span>分 2 种情况<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>一种是<span><span>「</span></span>愿望是可以的<span><span>，</span></span>行为是要节制的<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>比如<span><span>「</span></span>你让妈妈陪你玩可以<span><span>，</span></span>但打妈妈是不可以<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>第二种是<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>说什么<span><span>，</span></span>做什么是不可以被接受的<span><span>，</span></span>说什么做什么是可以被接受的<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>也就是说什么样的言行是不可以的<span><span>，</span></span>什么样的言行是不可以的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>你走过来拍拍我可以<span><span>，</span></span>但掐我很疼<span><span>，</span></span>我不能接受<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>你叫我可以<span><span>，</span></span>打我不可以<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>这一步的重点是<span><span>：</span></span>只说<span><span>「</span></span>什么不对<span><span>，</span></span>不可以<span><span>」</span></span>是不够的<span><span>，</span></span>4 岁之前<span><span>，</span></span>孩子的大脑发育还不成熟<span><span>，</span></span>不能理解<span><span>「</span></span>不<span><span>」</span></span>的含义<span><span>，</span></span>一定要告诉孩子<span><span>「</span></span>你可以怎么做<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>什么样的言行是可以接受的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>3<span><span>、</span></span>注意要表达<span><span>「</span></span>不含敌意的拒绝<span><span>」</span></span></strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>宝宝虽小<span><span>，</span></span>但非常善于捕捉大人的情绪<span><span>，</span></span>尤其当我们居高临下<span><span>，</span></span>总是试图<span><span>「</span></span>纠正<span><span>」</span></span>他<span><span>，</span></span>让他改正的时候<span><span>，</span></span>孩子会感受到<span><span>「</span></span>被控制<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>他可能说不出来<span><span>，</span></span>但会感受到我们似乎是有敌意的——就是<span><span>「</span></span>怎么又这样了<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span>不可以<span><span>「</span></span>犯错<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>这是非常不对的<span><span>，</span></span>这是错误的等等<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这会让孩子又羞愧又有点生气<span><span>，</span></span>我们越想把他拉过来<span><span>，</span></span>仿佛他越是不要<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>第二种情况<span><span>，</span></span>孩子已经度过了大动作敏感期<span><span>，</span></span>但玩闹不知轻重<span><span>，</span></span>不是有意<span><span>「</span></span>欺负父母<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>2-6 岁学龄前左右</strong><span><span>）</span></span></p>

 <p>生气是肯定生气的<span><span>，</span></span>但我觉得<span><span>，</span></span>此时处理的核心是要想清楚<span><span>：</span></span></p>

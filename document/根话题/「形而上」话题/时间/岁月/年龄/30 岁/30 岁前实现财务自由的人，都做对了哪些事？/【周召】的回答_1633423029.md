 <p>不光是 30 岁<span><span>，</span></span>凡是能实现财务自由的<span><span>，</span></span>都有一个特点<span><span>，</span></span>这个特点叫做<span><span>：</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>1</strong><span><span>、</span></span> <strong>什么是做庄</strong><span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>普通人在通往成功的道路上<span><span>，</span></span>靠的是一次次以弱胜强的战役积累起来的<span><span>，</span></span>每一次都是九死一生的博弈<span><span>，</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>财务自由的最快方式当然是通过财富转移而不是财富创造</strong><span><span>，</span></span><strong>而做庄就是一种在游戏规则内实现财富转移的最佳方式</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>做庄<span><span>，</span></span>某种程度上就是<span><span>「</span></span>合法<span><span>」</span></span>的<span><span>「</span></span>抢<span><span>」</span></span>和<span><span>「</span></span>骗<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>被割者虽然痛<span><span>，</span></span>但做庄完全是在规则范围之内玩的<span><span>，</span></span>被割者只能愿赌服输<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比方说<span><span>，</span></span>最近几年我研究和投资消费领域比较多<span><span>，</span></span>我就发现一个特点<span><span>，</span></span>叫作<span><span>「</span></span>万物皆是庄<span><span>，</span></span>万物皆可庄<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>商场自然是个大庄家<span><span>，</span></span>你看到的这些什么星巴克<span><span>，</span></span>KFC<span><span>，</span></span>沃尔玛等大卖场<span><span>，</span></span>这些所谓<span><span>「</span></span>主力店<span><span>」</span></span>就是被大庄家邀请来<span><span>「</span></span>联合坐庄<span><span>」</span></span>的<span><span>。</span></span>通常商场会给予他们很多的免租期甚至装修补贴<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>2</strong><span><span>、</span></span><strong>在这个十死无生的游戏当中</strong><span><span>，</span></span><strong>如何翻盘</strong><span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>如果你没有办法成为最大的那个庄家<span><span>，</span></span></p>
 <p>那么要先成为一个小庄家<span><span>，</span></span>慢慢积累博弈筹码<span><span>；</span></span></p>
 <p>要么抓住对手的弱点<span><span>，</span></span>从局部取得博弈优势<span><span>，</span></span>然后翻身做庄<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>所以说一个人只要有需求<span><span>，</span></span>他就会有弱点<span><span>，</span></span>就可以利用<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>做任何事情</strong><span><span>，</span></span><strong>永远不要把自己置于险境</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>所以<span><span>，</span></span>最牛的投资机构都是一二级市场同时做的<span><span>，</span></span>这种一二级联动的玩法赢面更大<span><span>，</span></span>对资源整合的能力要求也更高<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>再来看看一级市场</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p data-pid="zhh0DHtk">一个非常励志的视频：在坚持的路上跟自己握手言和。</p>
 <p data-pid="2Ae5tX4Y">匠造君从206斤，花了两年的时间减掉了82斤，这条路很孤独，却也强大了内心。</p>

 <p data-pid="uEHFON_R">希望每一个人都可以像工匠一样对待自己，雕刻身体，打磨一直，匠心创造更好的自己。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1225444420692393984" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d93b977421d9e39094fd7b248c7a6c8a.jpeg" data-lens-id="1225444420692393984"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d93b977421d9e39094fd7b248c7a6c8a.jpeg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1225444420692393984</span></span></a>

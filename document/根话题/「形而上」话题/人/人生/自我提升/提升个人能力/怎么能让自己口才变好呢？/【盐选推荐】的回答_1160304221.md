# 怎么能让自己口才变好呢？
- 点赞数：7695
- 更新时间：2024年03月11日17时50分20秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/350153499/answer/1160304221
<body>
 <p>我们跟别人说话<span><span>，</span></span>一般分为两种情况<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>第一种<span><span>，</span></span>你根据自己的经历表达某些的观点</strong></p>
 <p><strong>第二种<span><span>，</span></span>你围绕一个主题<span><span>，</span></span>有理有据<span><span>、</span></span>系统地阐述自己的观点<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>第一种情况非常简单<span><span>，</span></span>别人问你一个问题<span><span>，</span></span>你把自己感受说出来就行了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如对方问你<span><span>「</span></span>今天过得怎么样<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>你回答说<span><span>「</span></span>挺不错的<span><span>，</span></span>去公园玩了一天<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>当你说完这个感受<span><span>，</span></span>你的表达任务也就完成了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这种形式的观点表达<span><span>，</span></span>几乎没有什么难度<span><span>。</span></span>因为我们从小到大都有过这样的<span><span>「</span></span>训练<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>第二种情况就不是每个人都能做到了<span><span>。</span></span>而这个形式的表达<span><span>，</span></span>往往是一个人口才是否厉害的重要凭证<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如有人问你<span><span>：</span></span>你对于金钱在爱情中的作用有什么看法<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>如果我们用第一种形式去回答这个问题<span><span>，</span></span>说出来的话可能会很空洞<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你可能会说<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>没钱也可以谈一场平淡的恋爱<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>、</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>有钱才能幸福<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>、</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>钱能够让彼此的感情升温<span><span>」</span></span>等等<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这些简单的观点未必错<span><span>，</span></span>但别人不知道支撑你这些观点的理由<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>所以<span><span>，</span></span>当我们表达完自己的观点之后<span><span>，</span></span>一定要给出相应的理由来证明这个观点<span><span>，</span></span>这才是我们阐述自己思想的完整流程<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>很多人口才不好<span><span>，</span></span>就是因为他们说不了<span><span>「</span></span>段落<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>而生活里也有很多地方<span><span>，</span></span>需要我们用<span><span>「</span></span>段落<span><span>」</span></span>来阐明自己的思想<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>试想一下<span><span>，</span></span>你去参加一场面试<span><span>，</span></span>HR 问你<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>为什么选择我们公司呢<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>你回答一句<span><span>「</span></span>我觉得你们公司很好<span><span>」</span></span>就行了吗<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>肯定不行<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>我们需要把自己的观点组合成段落<span><span>，</span></span>把它打包成一个完整的思想包裹<span><span>，</span></span>送给别人<span><span>。</span></span>当对方拆开这个包裹之后<span><span>，</span></span>就会看到里面有什么内容<span><span>，</span></span>从而领会到我们的想法<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>观点只不过是外包装<span><span>，</span></span>核心内容才是决定这个思想包裹好坏的重要因素<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>我们说话<span><span>，</span></span>不仅要有观点<span><span>，</span></span>还要有支撑这个观点的内容<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>内容<span><span>，</span></span>就是由一个个短句组成长句<span><span>，</span></span>由长句组成段落的语言组合<span><span>。</span></span>如果能把这个<span><span>「</span></span>组合<span><span>」</span></span>流畅地说出来<span><span>，</span></span>就能很好地证明你的口才能力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>为什么有些人说不出<span><span>「</span></span>段落<span><span>」</span></span>呢<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>因为在日常生活的大部分时间中<span><span>，</span></span>我们都是用短句和人交流的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>朋友说<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>今天天气很好啊<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>你说<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>对啊<span><span>，</span></span>阳光灿烂<span><span>，</span></span>气候宜人<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>接着朋友问你<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>这段时间都在忙什么<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>你回答<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>在家里学习啊<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>朋友又继续问你<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>没想到你这么勤奋啊<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>你又回答说<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>因为过两天就要考研了<span><span>，</span></span>我当然一刻也不能怠慢啊<span><span>！</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>这种简短的对话是我们日常交流的常态<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>长此以往<span><span>，</span></span>一旦习惯了短句的说话形式<span><span>，</span></span>突然让我们去说一些长篇大论的话语<span><span>，</span></span>在没有准备的情况下<span><span>，</span></span>很容易哑口无言<span><span>，</span></span>不知道应该怎么把短句组合成段落<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>在某些特别的场合<span><span>，</span></span>如果我们缺乏这种表达能力<span><span>，</span></span>就很难把自己的思想向别人讲述清楚<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <h2><b>「1」句子：30个地道句式+例句</b></h2>
 <p data-pid="vG77iFxd">1. On no account can we+do</p>
 <p data-pid="q4rapiew">（我们绝对不能......）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="rkrY6qTq">
  例：On no account can we ignore the value of knowledge. 
  <br>
  我们绝对不能忽略知识的价值。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="dbu-RnbU">2. 主语+cannot emphasize the importance of ... too much.</p>
 <p data-pid="9o9EYyOM">（再怎么强调...的重要性也不为过。）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="YYlAHcFz">
  例：We cannot emphasize the importance of protecting our eyes too much.
  <br>
  我们再怎么强调保护眼睛的重要性都不为过。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="dj4bUkx1">3. It pays to+do...</p>
 <p data-pid="HFHq3vyo">（...是值得的）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="5ikSefyP">
  例：It pays to help others. 
  <br>
  帮助别人是值得的。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="qwZbkMsl">4. Owing to/ Thanks to sth, ...</p>
 <p data-pid="3FGxY6Zr">(因为......)</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ECSHWcLV">
  例：Thanks to his encouragement, I finally realized my dream. 
  <br>
  因为他的鼓励，我终于实现我的梦想。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="RdgaOeuG">5. 主语+do good/harm to sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="d9d0kEqx">（对...有益/有害）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ixNzuJh1">
  例：Reading does good to our mind 
  <br>
  读书对心灵有益。
  <br>
  Overwork does harm to health. 
  <br>
  工作过度对健康有害。
 </blockquote>

 <p data-pid="6_DfjYFs">6. What a/an +形容词+名词+主语+be! = How +形容词+a+名词+be!</p>
 <p data-pid="1BUny3Dx">（多么...!）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="UG2eBYLi">
  例：What an important thing it is to keep our promise! 
  <br>
  How important thing it is to keep our promise! 
  <br>
  遵守诺言是多么重要的事！
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="lyLs2TqR">7. 主语+be closely related to...</p>
 <p data-pid="_XYhnBcS">（与...息息相关）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="aIyMMqdU">
  例：Taking exercise isclosly related to health. 
  <br>
  做运动与健康息息相关。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="ACQUoyrs">8. 主语+have a great influence on sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="oEOoIxjv">（对...有很大的影响）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="OUfW0AnL">
  例：Smoking has a great influence on our health.
  <br>
  抽烟对我们的健康有很大的影响。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="lw9HPHqV">9. Nothing can prevent us from being...</p>
 <p data-pid="TFLdqiYy">（没有事情能够阻挡我们做...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="tU0bKevc">
  例: All this shows that nothing can prevent us from reaching out goal.
  <br>
  这显示了没有事情能够阻挡我们实现目标。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="9OKx0ClA">10. To tell the truth/to be frank, ...</p>
 <p data-pid="ah-nFDyu">（老实说，...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="0yV_i3FG">
  例：To tell the truth/to be frank, whether you like it or not, you have no other choice.
  <br>
  无论你喜不喜欢，你别无选择。
 </blockquote>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ccf9088ac8ca192243ed0cb0187104b1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1000" data-rawheight="1778" data-original-token="v2-02ce7882bbbe5b5d895920f6f38da887" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e53e266aab16159ca170852e6cd3e93a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1000" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-ccf9088ac8ca192243ed0cb0187104b1_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="4mQW98K_">11. ...enable+sb.+to+do</p>
 <p data-pid="73s5xJFa">（...使某人能够）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="NKc58Wrm">
  例：Listening to music enables us to feel relaxed.
  <br>
  听音乐使我们能够感到轻松。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="luVynQe-">12. What will happen to sb.?</p>
 <p data-pid="HkOVb66s">（某人将会怎么样？）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Imot_tjA">
  例：What will happen to the orphan?
  <br>
  那个孤儿将会怎样？
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="V_kMRC6V">13. For the past+时间，主语+现在完成时/现在完成进行时...</p>
 <p data-pid="WQ8fcJA8">（过去...时间以来，...一直...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="tn3JK6wT">
  例：For the past two years, I have been busy preparing for the examination.
  <br>
  过去两年来，我一直忙着准备考试。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="1eIETU4z">14.主语+be based on ...</p>
 <p data-pid="B75p_OLf">（以...为基础）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="omQnJtoZ">
  例：The progress of the society is based on harmony.
  <br>
  社会的进步是以和谐为基础的。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="7EZyhJQ8">15.Upon/on doing ..., ...</p>
 <p data-pid="iHTHGzyT">（一...就...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="mfrQVms-">
  例：Upon/on hearing the unexpected news, he was so surprised that he couldn't say a word.
  <br>
  一听到这个出乎意料的消息，他惊讶道说不出话来。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="lkNGolCW">注意：此句型一般可以改为如下复合句型：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Qm9u_r5Z">
  As soon as he heard of the unexpected news, he was so surprised that ... 
  <br>
  他一听到这个出乎意料的消息，就如此惊讶以至于... 
  <br>
  Hardly had he arrived when she started complaining.
  <br>
  他一来她就开始抱怨。
  <br>
   No sooner had he arrived than it began to rain. 
  <br>
  他刚来，就下雨了。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="1xURIe3r">16.主语+get into the habit of +V-ing = make it a rule to+V</p>
 <p data-pid="dU4FOtL7">（养成...的习惯）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="RCPb_5K6">
  例：We should get into the habit of keeping good hours. 
  <br>
  我们应该养成早睡早起的习惯。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="f212DeHT">17. Only +状语，主句部分倒装</p>
 <p data-pid="dpFfJdMJ">（直到...才....）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="EoUr-klE">
  例：Only then could the work of reconstruction begin.
  <br>
  直到那时，重建工作才开始。
 </blockquote>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="4-FP5YKZ">18.be worth doing</p>
 <p data-pid="xJSfrsYQ">（...值得做）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="nFElALbY">
  例：The book is worth readig.
  <br>
  这本书值得读。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="TJF-sjyO">19. There is no need for sb. to do sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="KiuPKTEd">（某人没有必要做......）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="iGOarxjA">
  例：There is no need for you to bring more food.
  <br>
  不需要你拿来更多的食物了。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="hs1EpnSU">20.There is no denying that +S+V...</p>
 <p data-pid="P58yFP0n">（不可否认的...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ZAfPvcXe">
  例：There is no denying that the qualities of our living have gone from bad to worse. 
  <br>
  不可否认的，我们的生活品质已经每况愈下。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="S5Fyj7Z4">21. There is no doubt that + 从句</p>
 <p data-pid="ycWsJjin">（毫无疑问的...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="PYZceQA9">
  例：There is no doubt that he came late.
  <br>
  毫无疑问，他来晚了。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="-v_goQaN">22. No wonder that...</p>
 <p data-pid="dC3jYQ4U">（难怪...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="Oy0mEy6D">
  例：No wonder that he fell asleep in class. 
  <br>
  难怪他在课堂上睡着了。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="pUpaXLJZ">23. An advantage of... is that +句子</p>
 <p data-pid="Vz15RT2p">（...的优点是...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="WzMDLrV2">
  例：An advantage of using solar energy is that it won't produce any pollution.
  <br>
  使用太阳能的优点是它不会制造任何污染。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="W-AbvCaj">24. 主语+ do one's best to do...</p>
 <p data-pid="Frkd_hUU">（尽某人全力去...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="335YmAtQ">
  例：We should do our best to achieve our goal on harmony. 
  <br>
  我们应该尽全力去维护和谐。
 </blockquote>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f0e3173ff640c9a5758b0028475cde3a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="2337" data-original-token="v2-51aa8962f484f86b8d3bdbd5f61c54bd" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-e5d3baf4991011ceeed1ad7edab5c17f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-f0e3173ff640c9a5758b0028475cde3a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Lu07s9be">25. It was not until recently that ...</p>
 <p data-pid="B1x2l8fx">（知道最近，...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="ULe7PFHj">
  例：It was not until recently that the problem was solved. 
  <br>
  直到最近这个问题才被解决 。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="U0ZM0ani">26.would rather do... than do...</p>
 <p data-pid="OO446VwX">(宁愿...而不...)</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="T9YgriPU">
  例：I would rather walk home than take a crowded bus. 
  <br>
  我宁愿步行回家也不愿坐拥挤的公交车。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="PKVyAtdb">注意：此句型可以改为prefer to do ... rather than do...</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="zGwQcrsH">
  例：I prefer to stay at home rather than see the awful film with him.
  <br>
  我宁愿在家也不愿意和他去看那部恐怖电影。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="oj90Sm1d">27.主语+is the+形容词最高级+名词+(that)+主语+have everseen/known/heard/had/read</p>
 <p data-pid="BNKy8adE">（...是我..最...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="HW1fctuQ">
  例：Liu Yifei is the most beautiful girl that I have ever seen in my life.
  <br>
  刘亦菲是我所看过最美丽的女孩。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="r_ezkIHo">注意：比较级也可以用来表达最高级的意思。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="v-I74nj7">
  例：I have never seen a more beautiful girl than Liu Yifei. 
  <br>
  在我生活中，我从来没见过比刘亦菲更美的女孩。 
  <br>
  Nothing is more important than to receive education. 
  <br>
  没有比接受教育更重要的事。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="HqGP4JX-">28. Spent as much time as he could doing sth.</p>
 <p data-pid="7XV2_SS3">（花尽可能多的时间做某事）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="h8Xep2VQ">
  例：Spent as much time as he could remembering new words. 
  <br>
  他花了尽可能多的时间记新单词。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="NQZ4uXjg">29.It was not until recently that...</p>
 <p data-pid="mQJGdXna">（直到最近，...）</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="4aN4ugn0">
  例：It was not untilrecently that the problem was solved. 
  <br>
  直到最近，这个问题才被解决。
 </blockquote>

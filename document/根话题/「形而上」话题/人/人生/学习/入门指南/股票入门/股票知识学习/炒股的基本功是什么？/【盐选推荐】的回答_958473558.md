# 炒股的基本功是什么？
- 点赞数：5762
- 更新时间：2024年03月11日19时48分22秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/326998351/answer/958473558
<body>
 <p>炒股的基本功可能就是看股价走势图了<span><span>，</span></span>看不懂走势图就没有办法了解当时的股票情况<span><span>，</span></span>也就没办法判断该买进还是卖出<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>风险提示<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>1. 本文提及的投资标的<span><span>、</span></span>软件工具仅供示例<span><span>，</span></span>不构成任何投资建议<span><span>；</span></span></p>
 <p>2. 投资有风险<span><span>，</span></span>决策需谨慎<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>一<span><span>、</span></span>看股价有哪些常用的技术指标<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>炒股少不了要会看一些技术指标<span><span>，</span></span>比如 MACD<span><span>、</span></span>KDJ 等等<span><span>，</span></span>乍一看密密麻麻的线条<span><span>，</span></span>感觉好像很复杂<span><span>，</span></span>其实打个比方就好理解了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>首先各种五花八门的技术指标就像是一首首乐曲<span><span>，</span></span>根据排列组合方式的不同<span><span>，</span></span>演奏出不同的旋律<span><span>，</span></span>而构成这些乐曲的音符就是两个元素<span><span>：</span></span>价格和成交量<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>股票分析就是从这些乐曲的旋律里捕捉其中包含的情绪<span><span>，</span></span>帮助我们更好地欣赏音乐<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>换句话说<span><span>，</span></span>价格和成交量就像是最基础的音符元素<span><span>，</span></span>不同的组合演绎出不同的乐曲<span><span>，</span></span>技术分析就是根据乐曲<span><span>（</span></span>也就是技术指标<span><span>）</span></span>判断背后的情绪<span><span>，</span></span>从而做出更理智的投资决策<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>我们首先要学会看三个技术指标<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>K 线<span><span>、</span></span>均线<span><span>、</span></span>成交量<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>关于学习这些基础指标<span><span>，</span></span>我们需要记住一个前提<span><span>：</span></span>不仅需要看懂图形<span><span>，</span></span>更重要的要知其然<span><span>，</span></span>知其所以然<span><span>，</span></span>了解背后到底是反映了什么情绪<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>二<span><span>、</span></span>怎么看股票价格——K 线</strong></p>
 <p>在股票行情软件和财经网站上<span><span>，</span></span>只要输入股票代码<span><span>，</span></span>就会看到股票的当前价格<span><span>，</span></span>此外还会看到一张布满红绿色线条的图<span><span>，</span></span>这就是 K 线图<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>据说最早的时候<span><span>，</span></span>日本古代的米商用 K 线来记录米价的涨跌<span><span>，</span></span>因为看起来像蜡烛<span><span>，</span></span>所以也叫作<span><span>「</span></span>蜡烛图<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>由于 K 线看上去简单易懂<span><span>，</span></span>后来引入到股市<span><span>，</span></span>效果也很好<span><span>，</span></span>之后就逐渐流传开来<span><span>，</span></span>成为全世界通用的<span><span>，</span></span>记录股价波动的指标<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>看懂 K 线并不难<span><span>，</span></span>只要牢牢抓住三要素就可以了<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>颜色<span><span>、</span></span>柱体<span><span>、</span></span>影线<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>K 线主要有两种颜色<span><span>：</span></span>红色的叫阳线<span><span>，</span></span>表示股价上涨<span><span>、</span></span>绿色的叫阴线<span><span>，</span></span>代表股价下跌<span><span>。</span></span>阴和阳分别代表了股市中存在的两种情绪<span><span>：</span></span>看空和看多<span><span>。</span></span></p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2378c303a81c73384c2d92928ef83b9e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="718" data-rawheight="308" data-original-token="v2-2378c303a81c73384c2d92928ef83b9e" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="718" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-2378c303a81c73384c2d92928ef83b9e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>开盘和收盘价之间的区域就是实体 K 线<span><span>，</span></span>实体反映最终哪股力量获胜了<span><span>。</span></span>如果收盘价高于开盘价<span><span>，</span></span>意味着看多的一派获胜<span><span>；</span></span>收盘价小于开盘价<span><span>，</span></span>意味着看空的一派获胜<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>凸出实体部分叫<span><span>「</span></span>上影线<span><span>、</span></span>下影线<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>代表了交易期间出现的最高价<span><span>、</span></span>最低价与实体部分的价格差<span><span>。</span></span><strong>影线越长<span><span>，</span></span>说明大家对价格的分歧越大<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>知道了上面这样典型的 K 线<span><span>，</span></span>再来了解一些特殊的变形<span><span>，</span></span>这个表格供大家参考<span><span>：</span></span></p>

 <p>每根 K 线可以包含不同周期<span><span>，</span></span>从一天<span><span>、</span></span>一周<span><span>、</span></span>一个月到一年<span><span>，</span></span>也可以是一天之内的 60 分钟<span><span>、</span></span>30 分钟<span><span>、</span></span>甚至是 1 分钟的价格变化情况<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>K 线指标不仅适用于个股<span><span>，</span></span>也可以用来查看各种指数的走势情况<span><span>。</span></span>另外<span><span>，</span></span>K 线还在期货<span><span>、</span></span>外汇等各种市场上大显身手<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>三<span><span>、</span></span>怎么选择股票买卖点——均线</strong></p>
 <p>我们在 K 线图上看到环绕在 K 线周围彩色的线<span><span>，</span></span>这些就是均线<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5403bdf01f9b78ace705d5a48475e185_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="720" data-rawheight="310" data-original-token="v2-5403bdf01f9b78ace705d5a48475e185" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="720" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5403bdf01f9b78ace705d5a48475e185_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>这根线是怎么画出来的呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>很简单<span><span>，</span></span>举个例子<span><span>，</span></span>比如最短的一个叫 5 日均线<span><span>，</span></span>就是把最近 5 天的收盘价加起来除以 5<span><span>，</span></span>得到一个点<span><span>，</span></span>然后把这些点依次连接<span><span>，</span></span>就变成一条线<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>所以均线是股票收盘价的移动平均线<span><span>。</span></span>以此类推<span><span>，</span></span>我们把 5 日换成 10 日<span><span>、</span></span>30 日<span><span>、</span></span>60 日<span><span>、</span></span>120 日……就能得到不同天数的均线<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>按天数的多少<span><span>，</span></span>均线分别可以反映股价短期<span><span>（</span></span>5 日<span><span>、</span></span>10 日<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>、</span></span>中期<span><span>（</span></span>30 日<span><span>、</span></span>60 日<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>、</span></span>长期<span><span>（</span></span>120 日以上<span><span>）</span></span>的趋势<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>均线主要有两种用法——</strong></p>
 <p><strong>1</strong>. <strong>看股价趋势</strong></p>
 <p>有了均线我们可以清楚地看到<span><span>，</span></span>股价所处的趋势是否发生变化<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>当短中长均线全部向下移动<span><span>，</span></span>说明大家卖出意愿比较强<span><span>，</span></span>看空的人占上风<span><span>。</span></span>一旦短期均线开始向上移动<span><span>，</span></span>意味着看多的人逐渐占据上风<span><span>，</span></span>随着短期均线上穿中长期均线<span><span>，</span></span>最终股价形成上涨趋势<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <h2><b>一、复试过程中导师最反感的回答与行为</b></h2>
 <h3><b>1.穿着不得体，举止不文雅</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="_X9sMsXB">复试的穿着打扮千万不要以“我认为好看”为标准，</p>
 <p data-pid="qoedh72B">染发、纹身、奇装异服、浓妆艳抹，坚决say no！！</p>
 <p data-pid="YLtAmUl8">记住导师需要的是<b>搞科研的talent</b>！并不需要来学校<b>走秀的model</b>！</p>
 <p data-pid="hsqOeIZN"><b>衬衣，领带/领结，淡妆即可；</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wo-11nJD">同样重要的是随时面带微笑，充满自信，开头要向各位老师礼貌问好。</p>
 <h3><b>2.自我介绍与简历内容重合度太高</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="ZvzwCVV0">我可看过太多人自我介绍的时候又把简历上的内容给导师背一遍。</p>
 <p data-pid="sZOq_S_X"><b>这可是自我介绍中的大忌啊！！！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vV0HKnz9">既然给了导师简历，为啥还要重复一遍简历上的内容？</p>
 <p data-pid="QCDFOj5U">导师希望看到的是即将成为研究生的你，具备什么<b>科研能力</b>，发过几篇<b>文章</b>，本科的<b>成绩（专业课）成绩</b>如何？</p>
 <p data-pid="5cRRLmeJ"><b>而不是</b>我来自哪里，欢迎导师们来玩，我大学参加了很多个社团......</p>
 <h3><b>3.回答假！大！空！，给导师画大饼</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="KSkyDid7"><b>导师：</b>能说一下你的读研规划嘛？</p>
 <p data-pid="Bry9tN_x"><b>你：</b>好好学习，好好做实验，争取发论文，最后找个好工作，放心吧导师，研究生期间我一定能成为你有史以来最好的弟子......</p>
 <p data-pid="OlWXmj6Z"><b>大错特错！！！！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="WGK9S5dX">首先你的回答需要有<b>逻辑性</b>，比如<b>研一</b>我准备......,<b>研二</b>我准备......,<b>研三</b>我准备......。其次还要给自己留有<b>回旋的余地</b>，比如，如果研三导师想让自己继续读博，自己也有强烈的意愿继续读书，我定会毫不犹豫地选择读博。</p>
 <h3><b>4.不懂装懂</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="WidzlZga">切记，如果遇到自己不懂的问题，</p>
 <p data-pid="U6sQjFnm"><b>先道歉！！！！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3QVX1MCk">要实事求是的说，自己对这方面的研读尚浅（千万别说没有复习到），如果知道其中任意一个观点，可就一个观点展开说；若啥都不知道，就别扯的天花乱坠，台下做的都是业内大佬，一眼就能看出你这人老实不老实。</p>
 <p data-pid="cipDWz7o"><b>记住！啥都不知道，委婉道歉就行！！</b></p>
 <h3><b>5.回答太直，毫无情商</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="9qh7M67W"><b>导师：</b>你为什么选择我们学校？</p>
 <p data-pid="5wCQine2"><b>我：</b>因为这个学校比较好考，太好的学校我考不上......</p>
 <p data-pid="Pw1shcdc"><b>这不是大型社死现场吗？？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="eDcr9joF"><b>这样说相当于你变相指着下面各位导师的鼻子说道：“你们不行！”</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MF2DDqK-"><b>正确的回答方式应该是：</b>先夸专业在全国排名靠前、再夸学校学风好，环境好，宿舍好，食堂好吃、最后夸导师，夸学院。<b>（此处最好去学院官网了解各位老师基本信息）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="TShUliAV">反正问到这个问题，<b>一顿夸就完事</b>（但马屁也不要拍的太过，注意把握分寸！）</p>
 <h3><b>6.你以为的笑话，导师却看你“笑话”</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="iAkxoMxX"><b>导师：</b>请问你有什么特长呢？</p>
 <p data-pid="zz85I6HS"><b>你：</b>我腿特长！！！！</p>
 <p data-pid="7NgiJLED">你以为导师会笑的人仰马翻？</p>
 <p data-pid="j2lBW-7T"><b>那你就大错特错了！！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="usve4WDG">这个问题导师并不是想真的了解你的兴趣爱好。</p>
 <p data-pid="ouwSWRmf"><b>而是想通过你的兴趣爱好，了解你的性格与为人处事。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="TtKg3d6i">比如：我的爱好是打篮球，曾经是院篮球队队长，带领球队拿过全校篮球比赛的第三名，再说一下自己是怎么团结球队，怎么以弱胜强的！</p>
 <p data-pid="cdN237c3">举这个例子</p>
 <p data-pid="G058INF_"><b>是让导师看重你带领团队的能力，和像团队凝聚剂一样的优秀特性。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="QAynJOAj">而不是看重你会打篮球.......</p>
 <h3><b>7.吹自己牛逼，自卖自夸</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="pXE0CcPx">你牛逼，导师比你更牛逼，</p>
 <p data-pid="5u9-eTZf">复试现场想表达自己的优秀，可以写在简历上，</p>
 <p data-pid="XU0XFATU">导师没问，就别一直吹自己吹的天花乱坠，</p>
 <p data-pid="HGRu4duh">导师感兴趣，会问，</p>
 <p data-pid="vG0h2Fn3">没问，也不代表对你没有兴趣，导师有眼睛，看得见！</p>
 <p data-pid="zoeZddu7"><b>记住！牛逼的人往往更谦虚！</b></p>
 <h3><b>8.准备多年，考研报班</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="Na9fas65"><b>考研能从大一开始准备嘛？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MzE3mhuk">当然可以！</p>
 <p data-pid="6pd8v-UH"><b>考研能报班嘛？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="QDx-2fYi">当然也可以！</p>
 <p data-pid="5IgcnpSz"><b>那为啥不让说？？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8H4s7lwp">自己想一下，报班考上和自学考上；准备一年和准备三年，</p>
 <p data-pid="Pt7s2aKq">在学习能力上面，导师就给你划分出个三六九等了，</p>
 <p data-pid="LxQUNNy5"><b>所以问到这个问题，千万要说自己是自学的！！！</b></p>

 <h2><b>二、复试中常见问题的整理（英文篇）</b></h2>
 <p data-pid="9VnEQyrA">考研复试的<b>英语面试环节</b>往往都是历年学弟学妹们老大难的问题，学姐将考研复试英语面试中常见的问题分了8类，分别为<b>自我介绍篇、兴趣爱好篇、优点特长篇、缺点短处篇、科研经历篇、报考动机篇、研究生生涯规划篇以及压力面试篇。</b></p>
 <h3><b>1.自我介绍篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="O4RNWB_C"><b>第一部分：介绍自己的基本情况</b></p>
 <p data-pid="EGd67alW">Good morning /afternoon，dear professors！It is my privilege to be here for this interview.I am（姓名）,（年龄） years old and I am from（家乡）.</p>
 <p data-pid="8b2JX2d7">①I am a senior student in（本科院校名称），majoring （专业名称）-----未毕业</p>
 <p data-pid="qsqtKeoM">②I have finished my undergraduate education in （本科学校名称）-----已毕业</p>
 <p data-pid="90BzfEjv"><b>第二部分：学校经历+获得荣誉+对报考专业的想法/兴趣爱好</b></p>
 <p data-pid="5O5vE7ry">Recalling my (past four-year) college life,I have won the first prize scholarships for a couple of times and participated in various competitions actively such as（所参加的竞赛的名称）. I also joined the Communist Part.(可替换为其他荣誉)</p>
 <p data-pid="d6fNGiFb">①When I was an intern in the Teacher＇s Office,I tried to do all the work assigned to me,and got the unanimous praise from the teachers. So I believe that in the future,if my graduate study supervisors assign me some learning and scientific research tasks,I can also take them seriously and work hard to complete them. In my spare time. I always read some classic novels which can help me improve my ability to understand and communicate with others.(可替换为其他兴趣爱好)----未毕业</p>
 <p data-pid="WlULS56e">②As time goes by,the more I experienced,the clearer I realized that I＇m really interested in Business Administration.(可替换为其他专业)I find that the management of many companies can not keep up with their development speed. I＇m eager to learn more about the necessary knowledge of Business Administration and I hope to continue further study in this university. (可替换为其他专业)So,last year,I quitted my job to prepare for the exam .After about half year＇s hard work,I＇m finally standing before you honorable professors now.----已毕业</p>
 <p data-pid="W_DOA14j"><b>第三部分</b>：<b>结尾，表达出希望被录取的欲望</b></p>
 <p data-pid="jxRX8WdR">I would be very honored if you could grant me the opportunity to study in this university. That＇s all about me.Thank you!</p>
 <h3><b>2.兴趣爱好篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="tQkD8FH3"><b>Q:</b>What＇s your favorite <b>hobby</b>?</p>
 <p data-pid="ZKlD-aDK"><b>回答要点：</b>点名兴趣爱好+原因</p>
 <p data-pid="fYxDbV44"><b>A1:</b>I like playing basketball. It＇s a very exciting game because it keeps me alert and I also enjoy the team spirit of basketball</p>
 <p data-pid="8yiDZVv2"><b>A2:</b>Fishing is my favorite, because it is both fun and instructive, which has helped me improve my power of controlling myself.</p>
 <p data-pid="T8_YAq3b"><b>A3:</b>I love travelling,which is a way to broaden my horizons,through which I can learn about different cultures of the world.</p>
 <h3><b>3.优点特长篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="jWBjHCrx"><b>Q：</b>What do you think about your <b>strengths</b>?</p>
 <p data-pid="BmnUWmNi"><b>回答要点：</b>点名自己优点+举例说明</p>
 <p data-pid="AmoJOWBF"><b>A：(学习能力举例)</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vj3rCrub">I think I am a quicker learner. In order to make myself more competitive,I learned(某些自学的知识) in a short time through self-study. This is because I have mastered a set of self-study methods and learned how to absorb something that you haven＇t touched before.</p>
 <h3><b>4.缺点短处篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="fQDuQTEM"><b>Q:</b>What do you think about your weakness?</p>
 <p data-pid="skC73Qst"><b>回答要点：</b>把缺点包装成优点</p>
 <p data-pid="LaAJL3st"><b>A：（我不太会拒绝他人）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="lbv9_uLR">Sometimes I have a hard time to saying no to people. But I am also happy that I can help others. Therefore,don＇t think it＇s a complete defect that I don＇t know how to refuse others. As long as it is within my ability,I still hope to reach out as much as possible.</p>
 <h3><b>5.科研经历篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="MNyDmQO5"><b>Q:</b>Could you say something about your experience of scientific research?</p>
 <p data-pid="zXJ36OK2"><b>有科研经历：点名项目+负责内容+收获</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dz-dGc2d"><b>A:</b>Under the guidance of my teachers,I did research on (研究课题)with my classmates when I was a sophomore. In the process of researching,everyone spared no efforts to complete the task,and my responsibility was to(自己负责的内容).In order to finish the research,we read and checked many books and articles such as.(查阅的文献) We also applied the method of(具体的方法) to solve the problems we encountered. Through this research,we have solved some problems,but also found some problems. I find that knowledge is limitless,which firms my determination to further my study.</p>
 <p data-pid="c73CzZXB">在老师的指导下，我在大二的时候和同学一起做了一个关于（）的研究。在研究的过程中，每个人都不遗余力完成了任务，而我的任务是（）。为了完成研究，我们查阅了许多书籍和文章，例如（），我们还应用了（）方法，来解决遇到的问题。在这次研究过程中，我们解决了一些问题，同时也发现了一些问题。所以学无止境，这坚定了我继续学习的决心。</p>
 <p data-pid="kF_MbtHT"><b>无科研经历</b></p>
 <p data-pid="y32bEnLp"><b>A:</b>I＇m sorry I have no experience in scientific research. But I have participated in some forum activities,course training,and made some professional reports I have benefited a lot from these activities,and also thought about what I will do in the future.</p>
 <p data-pid="Eld70EUC">很抱歉老师，我没有科研经历。但是我参加过一些论坛活动，课程培训，做过一些与专业相关的报告，从这些活动中我受益良多，也思考了自己在未来会进行哪些方面的研究。</p>
 <h3><b>6.报考动机篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="gnDciuzA"><b>1）选择考研的原因</b></p>
 <p data-pid="iGoHAV0O"><b>Q:</b>Why do you prepare for the graduate school entrance exam?</p>
 <p data-pid="Iycf7IxZ"><b>A:</b>Firstly,when I was a junior,I asked my teacher about(对与自己专业有疑惑的点),he/ she gave me a brief explanation and told me that if I wanted to get real advancement in my major,further education would be a best choice. Secondly,I am not a person who is easily satisfied with the present accomplishment and it is still urgent for me to purse further study just out of my inner obsession with the major. To sum up,I am determined to take the postgraduate entrance examination.</p>
 <p data-pid="fjtRLkw1">首先，当我还是一名大三学生的时候，我向老师询问关于（）的问题，他/她给了我一个简短的解释并告诉我，如果我想在专业上获得真正的进步，读研是最好的选择。其次，我不是一个满足现状的人，我是真心喜欢这个专业并且想继续深造。因此，我决心参加研究生入学考试。</p>
 <p data-pid="JDTH-2oj"><b>2）选择报考该院校的考试原因</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Tl8eaU8O"><b>Q:</b>why do you choose to study at our school?</p>
 <p data-pid="g4SkpoME"><b>A:</b>Firstly, (Peking university )has been my dream school. During the College Entrance Examination, I failed to be enrolled in this university and now I want to fulfill my dream. Secondly, I am deeply attracted by the academic atmosphere, as well as the youthful spirit of students in this university. More importantly, (Peking university )has a good reputation in the field where my research interest lies and so many excellent teachers who have strong research abilities. Those are the reasons why I choose to further my study here.</p>
 <p data-pid="5q2nOGbj">首先，(北京大学)一直是我的梦想。在高考的时候，我没有被录取，现在，我想要实现自己的梦想。其次，这所大学浓厚的学术氛围以及学校学生的朝气蓬勃的精神吸引了我。更为重要的是，(北大)在我所感兴趣的研究领域有着非常好的声誉而且有很多非常有能力的优秀的老师。这就是我选择这所大学的原因。</p>
 <h3><b>7.研究生生涯规划篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="lKWlIBAR"><b>Q:</b>Can you talk about your plans in the postgraduate study?</p>
 <p data-pid="gFmvafyo"><b>A:</b> I've already made a plan for my postgraduate study. In the first year, I will focus all my energy on studying professional knowledge and mastering research methods in the field. Secondly, I will read all the books recommended by my supervisors and read as many books as possible. The next year, I will determine my own research direction. At the same time, I will actively participate in the experiment and scientific research of my supervisors and do a good job in supporting <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//work.In" class=" external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer"><span class="invisible">http://</span><span class="visible">work.In</span><span class="invisible"></span></a> the third year, I will try my best to write valuable papers with high level. Later, if possible, I will apply for a doctorate.All in all, I am looking forward to making a solid foundation for my profession and my life in this excellent university.</p>
 <p data-pid="98HG7fuU">我已经为我的研究生学习制定了计划。第一年，我将全力以赴学习专业知识，掌握该领域的研究方法。其次，我会阅读导师推荐的所有书籍，并尽可能多读书。第二年，我将确定自己的研究方向。同时，积极参与导师的实验和科研工作，做好辅助。第三年，我会努力写出高水平的有价值的论文。之后，如果可能的话，我会申请博士学位。总而言之，我期待着在这个优秀的大学里为我的职业和生活打下坚实的基础。</p>
 <h3><b>8.压力面试篇</b></h3>
 <p data-pid="vu_Xocx5"><b>1）如果考研失利</b></p>
 <p data-pid="aJUKSkG1"><b>Q:</b>If you failed this time,what will you do in the near future?</p>
 <p data-pid="xFtS68Za"><b>回答思路：表明态度+反思</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dcDCP9BZ"><b>A:</b>It is clear that success and failure coexist in the life. If I could not pass the test this time,it means that I am not adequate to some extent,not only in the way of professional knowledge,but also in the practical aspect.

 <h2>正文</h2>
 <p data-pid="Ork9kbxi">我帮很多人改过、写过甚至编过自我介绍了，复试的，求职的，最牛的是一位要去参加联合国项目的小哥。结局都相当美好，他们都通过了面试~</p>
 <p data-pid="BnJt7t4l"><b>经验介绍前，建议你不要照抄高赞，很多表达不太合适。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="O0sHbv_9">复杂的从句，对于英语不太好的人，实践中难免磕磕巴巴；另外，重复的 I have 和 I'm，也不大符合英语表达习惯。</p>
 <p data-pid="VKDXXYtM">还有，不要把 actually 这种废词当口头禅。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-8a3a2c0eaf6d59fb121ee2b8a8e4a3cb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="579" data-rawheight="71" data-original-token="v2-8a3a2c0eaf6d59fb121ee2b8a8e4a3cb" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="579" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-8a3a2c0eaf6d59fb121ee2b8a8e4a3cb_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="FWRKHLbD">复试中，你在自我介绍的时候要流利顺畅，且谈吐自信。在有限的时间内，除了要清楚说明“<b>who am I</b>”，还要充分展示自己的亮点，即“<b>why me</b>”（为什么选择我）。</p>
 <p data-pid="hpMmZwrQ">首先，你要清楚，口语和书面语不同，英文的书面语，可以从句套从句，呈枝干句式结构；有大段大段的句子、高级的修饰和很多名词，但是，复试场合，自我介绍是为了传递信息，必须<b>简单、清晰和明了</b>。</p>
 <p data-pid="wwHfkymS">引用口译课上老师的话：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="_g4CIeXV">
  1. 一开口，你就要想象自己是一把快刀，<b>迅速，清晰且平稳地表达观点</b>；同时放稳音调，让声音自信；
  <br>
  <br>
  2. 能用小词，就用小词；过多的大词和难词，会浪费时间，且不省力。
  <br>
  比如：XX很重要，XX is significant/important 即可；不必为了炫技，非要用XX is of great significance/importance
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="bDyqZEgO">试想面试的场景，你紧张得都能听清自己的心跳了，还要往外蹦那些绕口的词。或许，一分钟过去，还没支支吾吾完，考官就来一句“好了...下一个环节”，就问你，慌不慌...</p>
 <p data-pid="9L5pfGVc">所以，建议你看完这篇回答，就几分钟时间，不仅学会自己写一篇高质量的自我介绍，而且还有小技巧。不亏嘛~</p>

 <h2>怎么组合完整版自我介绍</h2>
 <p data-pid="VieXk4aP">拆解前，我先上一篇:</p>
 <p data-pid="kRcllb8P">可以直接套用， 后有模板，手把手教你写~</p>
 <p data-pid="pLe0W4be">Good morning, teachers and professors. It is my great honor to be here and introduce myself.</p>
 <p data-pid="GbVBaFjI">My name is XXX, I'm 24 years old and from Chengdu City. I have graduated from Sichuan University, with major in Finance. My GPA is XX, ranking the top 10 in my class. During the four years of study, I have won the National Scholarship, as well as various awards for my excellent performance in school work and extra-curricular activities.</p>
 <p data-pid="p9GcvZ9_">（项目介绍）When I was a junior student, I participated in a social research project titled the Attachment of New-generation Workers in Foreign-funded Enterprise". In that project, we conducted a survey at XXX in Wuxi City and analyzed the links between workers and the enterprise. I had designed, handed out and collected the questionnaire, and finished a report of over 10 thousand words. At last, that project stood out from around 30 ones and won the first prize at my university.</p>
 <p data-pid="Tau5vr2m">（专业想法）Moreover, I'm interested in the postgraduate program I have applied, so that I have read many books and papers to enrich my basic knowledge. The XX major of XX University is well-established, where I will acquire more professional knowledge and specific skills and put them into practice.</p>
 <p data-pid="epSh5LqL">I have passed CET-6 at high scores. With excellent skills in translation, I'm a member of a specialized subtitle translation group. Besides, I also excel in Microsoft Office, Photoshop and new media operation. I attribute my versatility to my diligence and strong learning capacity. Once I’ve set a goal, I will spare no effort to realize it whatever the difficulties are.</p>
 <p data-pid="h9GTIdTt">XX University attracts me with the intense academic atmosphere and prestigious reputation, and applying for XX postgraduate program is the best decision I ever made for my education. If I get the chance to be admitted/enrolled, I will redoublemy efforts in the study and research, thus laying a solid foundation for future profession. Thank you.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="U4l54Zrd">第二篇，是一个理科生小伙伴，套了框架后的成品（经本人允许后发布）：</p>
 <p data-pid="Da6OoJDM">第二段项目经历：</p>
 <p data-pid="ZjZY_VYr">When I was a junior student, I participated in a research project titled the National University Student Social Practice and Science Contest on Energy Saving &amp; Emission Reduction. In that project, our team designed an automatic battery replacement device for new vehicles, and I conducted the mechanical structure design in that team. At last, that project stood out from around 30 ones and won the fifth prize at my university. Besides, I also participated in the “Maths China Cup” National Online Mathematical Challenge in Modeling in 2019 and won the Excellence Award. <b>Those awards can demonstrate my good abilities in mechanical structure design and mathematical modeling</b>. （奖项略杂，就总结以下）</p>
 <p data-pid="DgnTIgo_">第三段其他优势介绍：</p>
 <p data-pid="igndIFaK">I have passed the College English Test level 4, and I do well in translation. I once helped a Shanghai-based filtration equipment company to translate its filter specification, and that project lasted seven months. Moreover, I also excel in Solidworks, Auto CAD and MATLAB-Simulink. I attribute my versatility to my diligence and strong learning capacity. Once I have set a goal, I will spare no effort to realize it whatever the difficulties are.</p>
 <p data-pid="hSfVyApv">这篇自我介绍的框架是：</p>

 </figure>
 <p data-pid="-Aca5EWp">总原则：<b>没有所谓“让人眼前一亮”且“非你不可”的黄金模板。相反，面试老师耳朵里都塞满了很多“得体”的场面话甚至套话。那些，只是锦上添花的套路，而面试老师关注的，更多是，你这个人怎么样</b>，即：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="amcc8pne">
  你的<b>本科学校和专业</b>、<b>科研经历</b>（有没有发表过期刊文章，参与过科研项目）、<b>学习能力</b>（GPA，有没有国家或校级奖学金）和<b>英语能力</b>（研究生阶段，很多项目都涉及查阅外文资料、翻译甚至写外文paper）。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="vgOW_4Lh">而一些社团、爱好甚至和专业相关度不那么大的实习，他们不会那么看重。甚至多了会反感：读研后，很多导师吐槽，学生都实习或者参加各种社团去了，沉不下心，且找不到人！！</p>
 <p data-pid="EDfb_-4Q">总而言之：你的自我介绍，要<b>在有限的时间内，最大程度地传递有效信息，体现自己的价值所在</b>。</p>

 <h2>怎么快速提高自我介绍和英语Q&amp;A环节的口语和听力？</h2>
 <p data-pid="_PL9h6Cb">详细拆解前，重要的事情先讲~</p>
 <p data-pid="oL5zAO_B">改了自我介绍以后，<b>很多粉丝问我，羞于开口说英语，甚至单词都认不全，怎么办？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_WJ-vSH8"><b>那，怎么在短时间内模拟面试，练胆量，突击口语+听力？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="6VDmOfK7">找老外（英语母语者）练习。</p>
 <p data-pid="4eMx2r59">因为如果你曾经站在万米高空，你是不会再畏惧几百米高度的。</p>
 <p data-pid="j_w53Eav">请英语母语者听一下你的自我介绍，练习Q&amp;A环节，同时请他们指出你需要提高的地方。最后看看录像，自我调整。是短期内突破这个版块的捷径。</p>

 <h2><b>怎么自己写自我介绍（详细拆解）：</b></h2>
 <p data-pid="fA-YHbzf"><b>段落一：</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-681e9eb4856a22345a454362b627c18c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1645" data-rawheight="561" data-original-token="v2-681e9eb4856a22345a454362b627c18c" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6a0c86f59d40ed428ec9da4377ac8915_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1645" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-681e9eb4856a22345a454362b627c18c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="sVsow9W5"><b>刚开始，自报家门，说明：姓名，籍贯，毕业院校，主修专业，GPA和各奖项。如果GPA不高，可以不提；如果奖项不突出，</b>可以简要说：</p>
 <p data-pid="Ve5AF5FA">我获得了各种奖项：I have received various/several awards.</p>
 <p data-pid="znwxcB70">学校和专业名称，可参考学校官网，一般都有标准的英文译本。</p>
 <p data-pid="QaGQ0pl7">奖项名称参考：</p>
 <p data-pid="M-eoZKBT">国家/校级/院级奖学金：National/University/School Scholarship</p>
 <p data-pid="FV8-zrTn">三好学生：Merit Student</p>
 <p data-pid="4-L58kAt">先进个人：Advanced Individual</p>
 <p data-pid="cPRkAzyQ">XX 项目一/二/三等奖：First/Second/Third Price for + 项目名</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <p data-pid="aq024Kw6"><b>逻辑：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_jHiiWqL"><b>参加了XX项目 → 在项目中扮演XX角色（具体做了XX工作） → 项目成果（是否获奖，或者论文是否发表） → 自己 get 到了什么技能</b></p>
 <p data-pid="GCzoS3pY">例如，我在校期间参加了假期社会实践：</p>
 <p data-pid="u6nSgrVc">I was a member of the social research titled "the Attachment of New-generation Workers in Foreign-funded Enterprise". (项目名称和自己的角色) In that project, we conducted a survey at XXX in Wuxi City and analyzed the links between workers and the enterprise. (项目是做什么的) I had designed, handed out and collected the questionnaire, and finished a report of over 10 thousand words. (自己做了什么) At last, that project stood out from around 30 ones and won the first prize at my university. (项目成就)</p>
 <p data-pid="6tDr_dct"><b>如果是奖项，可以查找官方译名或者通用名</b>，如：</p>
 <p data-pid="XhS6fdjI">挑战杯全国大学生创业计划大赛：</p>
 <p data-pid="BW4cVmkm">The Challenge Cup National College Student Business Plan Competition</p>
 <p data-pid="xmBkRo6I">挑战杯全国大学生课外学术科技作品竞赛：</p>
 <p data-pid="Khe2B9n9">The Challenge Cup National College Student Curricular Academic Science and Technology Works Competition</p>
 <p data-pid="Ra-TN58r">大学生创业大赛：</p>
 <p data-pid="IZ3TMzb0">College Student Entrepreneur Competition</p>
 <p data-pid="BzjqviWW">欧莱雅全球商业策划竞赛：L' Oreal e-Strat Challenge</p>
 <p data-pid="zLApc5Ny">全国大学生数学建模竞赛：China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling</p>
 <p data-pid="-NV2djxN"><b>如果是学术项目，可在万方上搜索关键词，一般会有文献和译名，作为翻译参考</b>：</p>

 <p data-pid="RHbiywxU">In that project, I received praises from my teachers and team members for my contribution in data analysis/report writing...</p>

 <p data-pid="mwl1l_Mv"><b>对本专业的理解，可具体搜索知网，细读几篇文章，然后简单说说自己的看法。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_lMIkeDE"><b>思路：表达兴趣 → 未来志向</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vEpnqww2">另外，很多研究生导师，都很看重英语能力。所以一定特别说明：</p>
 <p data-pid="s--fFUZl">I have passed CET-4/CET-6 at high scores, and I do well in reading and translation.</p>
 <p data-pid="rb0uJQ2k">四六级，在自我介绍中，建议用全称：College English Test level 4 or level 6</p>
 <p data-pid="TNHuciFq">另外，更牛的专四专八是：Test for English Majors level 4 or level 8</p>
 <p data-pid="WCwDwU6M">对于其他技能，可用句式：</p>
 <p data-pid="37z6yQL9">擅长：good at/excel in/do well in/be adept at</p>
 <p data-pid="xXs71snZ">有经验/技巧 be experienced in/skilled in</p>
 <p data-pid="D4iO7oSF">专业精通 be specialised in/proficient in （慎用，作为本科生，你很难做到精通）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="p8GVEkkf">对于性格和优点：</p>
 <p data-pid="snha_GD2">勤奋 hard-working/diligent</p>
 <p data-pid="j3UzyGBF">负责任 responsible</p>
 <p data-pid="D7T0IdJb">有上进心 self-motivated</p>
 <p data-pid="QF-OhTaS">学习能力强 strong learning capacity</p>
 <p data-pid="BKhvaWAj">积极主动 proactive</p>
 <p data-pid="wetrDkIh">有团队精神 a good team player</p>
 <p data-pid="qFFzzWlg">结果导向 result-oriented</p>
 <p data-pid="k8gCoB64">一般，<b>列举优点，并简单论证</b>：</p>
 <p data-pid="1LFO8tsS">I'm a diligent student. In the past four years, I would spare no effort to realize the set purpose whatever the difficulties were.</p>
 <p data-pid="VHk2_NAG">I have a strong learning capacity. Besides my major subjects, I have learned Japanese independently and once took a part-time job in a subtitle translation group.</p>

 <p data-pid="4jpUXBKf">最后部分，当然是恭维学校和表达向往，这部分，可以直接套我写好的模板~~(*^▽^*)~~</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="0cBPsFrI">申请XX学校，是我最正确的决定，它因为XXX深深吸引着我。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="iRI5nDmI"><b>Applying for XX University is the best decision I ever made for my education</b>, and it attracts me with the academic atmosphere 学术氛围/ prestigious reputation 名声斐然/well-known teachers and dynamic students 名师和活跃的校友</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="f6QhAwDA">如果有幸录取，我将加倍努力，为未来职业奠定基础。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="ZE35IBDl">If I get the chance to be admitted/enrolled, I will redouble our efforts in the study and research, thus laying a solid foundation for future profession.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="BA1oQvei">真心感谢这次机会。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="NlUL9Q6b">Thanks to the opportunities I was given to apply XX University.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="xxzj-uGH">（可用于项目）除了要感谢贵校提供的项目机会外，我还想感谢...</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="ktSYiL2m">Besides being grateful to XX University for offering such an opportunity, I'm even more indebted to my teachers who eagerly gave me their time, effort and knowledge and who put so much faith in me.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="553P8Rs9">我之所以选择贵校，是因为这所举世闻名的学府为我进一步学习深造提供了平台。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="HgGCeGxM">I chose XX because the presence of a world-class university enables me to pursue studies in XX (major).</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="fFYIMubL">如果有幸录取...我将..., 这也会为我的学术生涯奠定坚实基础。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="1PDXzeZ2">If I am lucky enough to be admitted by XX even though I don't have a particularly high score, I will……., which will truly lay a solid foundation in my academic CV.</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="OGJYsn4S">如果能够在贵校深造，我将树立更高的目标，继续努力。</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="V6-MI8vW">My experience in XX will make me more driven as an individual, and encourage me to set more ambitious goals.</p>

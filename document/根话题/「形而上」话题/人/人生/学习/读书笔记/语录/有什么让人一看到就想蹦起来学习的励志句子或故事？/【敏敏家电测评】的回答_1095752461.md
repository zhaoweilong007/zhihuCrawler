 <p data-pid="PaTmvxvX"><b>New York is 3 hours ahead of California,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="IoxBsimp">纽约时间比加州时间早三个小时，</p>
 <p data-pid="4O3HiLBQ"><b>but it does not make California slow.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="KLNBQV-S">但加州时间并没有变慢。</p>
 <p data-pid="_o4aZ89p"><b>Someone graduated at the age of 22,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="i47KDLoi">有人22岁就毕业了，</p>
 <p data-pid="pcjWOiz2"><b>but waited 5 years before securing a good job!</b></p>
 <p data-pid="d-09wNDW">但等了五年才找到好的工作！</p>
 <p data-pid="1SDPdgOe"><b>Someone became a CEO at 25,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="f7Q46DTR">有人25岁就当上CEO，</p>
 <p data-pid="ahFshZHB"><b>and died at 50.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="SuOl6-7U">却在50岁去世。</p>
 <p data-pid="Mm_ODrds"><b>While another became a CEO at 50,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-0EFcyZH">也有人迟到50岁才当上CEO，</p>
 <p data-pid="LOo6vDQC"><b>and lived to 90 years.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="OBsRYlNe">然后活到90岁。</p>
 <p data-pid="7ekaxSHu"><b>Someone is still single,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="aYnnVxak">有人依然单身，</p>
 <p data-pid="L5pWbFh4"><b>while someone else got married.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8Gu_48u9">同时也有人已婚。</p>
 <p data-pid="kOVCkV1L"><b>Obama retires at 55,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0TeLBVUa">奥巴马55岁就退休，</p>
 <p data-pid="ncWLEq29"><b>but Trump starts at 70.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="haax-XvJ">川某70岁才开始当总统。</p>
 <p data-pid="euzbTPhA"><b>Absolutely everyone in this world works based on their Time Zone.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="55bf071W">世上每个人本来就有自己的发展时区。</p>
 <p data-pid="ifIIJQ2X"><b>People around you might seem to go ahead of you,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zAudsIEP">身边有些人看似走在你前面，</p>
 <p data-pid="O4QhhtbV"><b>some might seem to be behind you.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="u0mJwnz3">也有人看似走在你后面。</p>
 <p data-pid="GgSbGDEt"><b>But everyone is running their own RACE, in their own TIME.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="k9xMOc-H">但其实每个人在自己的时区有自己的步程。</p>
 <p data-pid="cYheWyyS"><b>Don’t envy them or mock them.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="syZdTG7h">不用嫉妒或嘲笑他们。</p>
 <p data-pid="pY0ectm5"><b>They are in their TIME ZONE, and you are in yours!</b></p>
 <p data-pid="EsfYHrtS">他们都在自己的时区里，你也是！</p>
 <p data-pid="fP5TNqzi"><b>Life is about waiting for the right moment to act.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zqSF58kW">生命就是等待正确的行动时机。</p>
 <p data-pid="UMZ7pph7"><b>So, RELAX.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="WUMmkB5N">所以，放轻松。</p>
 <p data-pid="3eoEhKbS"><b>You’re not LATE.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="9Tn0xExA">你没有落后。</p>
 <p data-pid="XwrVskX9"><b>You’re not EARLY.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xMyafZwE">你没有领先。</p>
 <p data-pid="St6v7kh4"><b>You are very much ON TIME, and in your TIME ZONE Destiny set up for you.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zzS_BAhf">在命运为你安排的属于自己的时区里，一切都准时。</p>

 <p>不是你反应慢<span><span>，</span></span>也不是你脑袋不灵光<span><span>。</span></span>而是你总用慢速的思考脑来回答问题<span><span>，</span></span>事实上<span><span>，</span></span>调动日常经验更快速也更靠谱<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>一<span><span>、</span></span>你为什么反应慢<br><br>
   在开始说方法之前<span><span>，</span></span>我们需要先弄清楚<span><span>，</span></span>为什么我们讲话反应慢<span><span>，</span></span>老是思来想去半天<span><span>，</span></span>说不出个所以然<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>下面这个场景你一定很熟悉<span><span>：</span></span></p>

 <ul>
  <li>花心是什么<span><span>，</span></span>是就在心里想想<span><span>，</span></span>还是外面住了好几个</li>
  <li>男人指哪些<span><span>？</span></span>我爸<span><span>，</span></span>我男朋友<span><span>，</span></span>偶像剧中的王沥川算吗<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>都是指我认识的<span><span>，</span></span>还是全世界的</li>
  <li>我说的不具有权威性敢说吗<span><span>？</span></span></li>
 </ul>
 <p>这 4 个问题想下来<span><span>，</span></span>能快速作答就怪了<span><span>。</span></span>所以真不是你反应慢<span><span>，</span></span>而是你的思考有问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>不是先天脑瓜子导致了我们<span><span>「</span></span>慢<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>而是后天方法不对<span><span>，</span></span>让我们快不了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>那应该怎么思考呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>这里需要先给大家补充一个知识<span><span>，</span></span></p>
 <p><span><span>《</span></span>慢思考<span><span>》</span></span>一书说提到<span><span>，</span></span>人拥有三套脑系统<span><span>，</span></span>分别是反射脑<span><span>、</span></span>思考脑和存储脑<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>反射脑<span><span>：</span></span>对感官世界的反射<span><span>，</span></span>反应速度最快<span><span>，</span></span>是自发<span><span>、</span></span>无意识的<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>思考脑<span><span>：</span></span>有意识地思考<span><span>、</span></span>逻辑分析<span><span>、</span></span>综合考量<span><span>、</span></span>回顾过去<span><span>、</span></span>做出预判等<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>存储脑<span><span>：</span></span>对信息进行筛选<span><span>、</span></span>分类和存储<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>当有一个问题抛过来时<span><span>，</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>反应慢<span><span>」</span></span>的人<span><span>，</span></span>往往会用<span><span>「</span></span>思考脑<span><span>」</span></span>进行逻辑性十足的分析<span><span>，</span></span>但是思考脑为了确保万无一失<span><span>，</span></span>运行速度很慢<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>最后明明是在认真想问题<span><span>，</span></span>却被朋友<span><span>、</span></span>上司视为反应迟钝<span><span>，</span></span>甚至会被误以为跑神<span><span>，</span></span>没认真听讲<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>所以<span><span>，</span></span>为了可以快速回答<span><span>，</span></span>遇到问题时<span><span>，</span></span>咱先不把它交给思考脑<span><span>，</span></span>而是给储蓄脑和反应脑<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>怎么做呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>就是通过对方提出的问题<span><span>，</span></span>进行感性想象<span><span>，</span></span>联想出自己曾经遇到的类似问题<span><span>，</span></span>接着用储蓄脑回想当时情况<span><span>，</span></span>根据过往经验提炼出看法<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>看不懂没关系<span><span>，</span></span>看个例子<span><span>，</span></span>保准明白<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>拆书帮的赵周老师一次参加会议<span><span>，</span></span>见到一位互联网大佬在讲台上<span><span>，</span></span>一口气回答了二十来个刁钻问题 <span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>正当他要佩服对方强大的知识储备时<span><span>，</span></span>却发现了其中<span><span>「</span></span>套路<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>原来<span><span>，</span></span>这个大佬几乎次次都是听到问题后<span><span>，</span></span>沉思一分钟左右<span><span>，</span></span>就开口说<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>我先给你讲一个我经历的和你刚刚讲的很像的一件事/处理的一个案子<span><span>。</span></span>最后再绕回到对方问题上<span><span>，</span></span>给出建议<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这样的回答有具体场景<span><span>，</span></span>有事后复盘分析<span><span>，</span></span>更利于听者摘取有用信息<span><span>，</span></span>满意地点点头<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>下次<span><span>，</span></span>再有人问你<span><span>「</span></span>男人是不是没有每一个东西的时候<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>如果你单身<span><span>，</span></span>就立刻想一下你爸<span><span>、</span></span>你偶像<span><span>、</span></span>你的男同事<span><span>，</span></span>男同学</p>
 <p>如果你有伴<span><span>，</span></span>就即刻想一下你男盆友<span><span>，</span></span>然后再是你爸<span><span>、</span></span>你偶像……</p>
 <p>反应慢的底层逻辑给你放在这了<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>不是你反应慢<span><span>，</span></span>而是在用讲求严谨<span><span>、</span></span>正确性的思考脑进行深刻地逻辑分析<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>为了提高反应速度<span><span>，</span></span>接收到问题后<span><span>，</span></span>立马感性想象是否之前有过类似经历<span><span>，</span></span>用储蓄脑将经验调出<span><span>，</span></span>再加以整理分析<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>二<span><span>、</span></span>有条理地整理观点——<span><span>「</span></span>金字塔原理<span><span>」</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>这时<span><span>，</span></span>你一定要问了<span><span>，</span></span>确实想到了一件类似的事或人<span><span>，</span></span>可是它们细碎如一地鸡毛<span><span>，</span></span>我该从哪开始捡起<span><span>？</span></span>着重捡哪几种<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>这问题问得贼好<span><span>，</span></span>毕竟<span><span>，</span></span>朋友在问<span><span>「</span></span>男人是不是都花心时<span><span>」</span></span>时<span><span>，</span></span>重点在是否专一<span><span>，</span></span>钟情<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>并不想听你讲他东西乱放<span><span>，</span></span>讲话直男<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>所以<span><span>，</span></span>联想＋回忆事件后<span><span>，</span></span>还有极其重要的一步<span><span>，</span></span>就是好好组织一下这些陈芝麻烂谷子<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>如何整理<span><span>，</span></span>可以跟<span><span>《</span></span>金字塔原理<span><span>》</span></span>这本书学学<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><span><span>《</span></span>金字塔原理<span><span>》</span></span>在写作界名声多大<span><span>，</span></span>我就不说了<span><span>，</span></span>几乎人人一本<span><span>，</span></span>做写作老师不推荐它<span><span>，</span></span>就是不专业<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>而写作上的一些方法<span><span>，</span></span>用在讲话中也是如鱼得水<span><span>，</span></span>毕竟写跟说之间的距离<span><span>，</span></span>有时候<span><span>，</span></span>就差一个<span><span>「</span></span>综上所述<span><span>」</span></span>和<span><span>「</span></span>我们总结一下<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>一个金字塔结构<span><span>，</span></span>从横向和纵向两个方向扩散<span><span>，</span></span>纵向是自上而下的顺序进行<span><span>，</span></span>横向按照演绎或者归纳推理来进行<span><span>，</span></span>有归类的作用<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>

 <p>还举那个例子<span><span>「</span></span><strong>男人是不是都花心</strong><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>该如何整理信息呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>先结论<span><span>：</span></span>不能这样说<span><span>，</span></span>要分人了<span><span>。</span></span><br>
   分论点一<span><span>：</span></span>我男票不花心<span><span>；</span></span><br>

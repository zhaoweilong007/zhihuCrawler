 <p data-pid="Uq_CN-WA"><b>【1】开头</b></p>
 <p data-pid="YTYc2zED">1、A lot of people seem to think that...</p>
 <p data-pid="gzs5GU_e">很多人似乎认为.....</p>
 <p data-pid="N4TxPTaB">2、Many people insist that...</p>
 <p data-pid="XjO5yNeF">很多人坚持认为.....</p>
 <p data-pid="8PikderN">3、With the development of science and technology, more and more people believe that...</p>
 <p data-pid="P_Kv5q4J">随着科技的发展，越来越多的人认为.....</p>
 <p data-pid="c9odQJRM">引出不同观点：</p>
 <p data-pid="-IIjElbA">1、Attitudes towards (drugs) vary from person to person.</p>
 <p data-pid="zZXNdSvY">人们对待(吸毒)的态度因人而异。</p>
 <p data-pid="Hiqo0Ur8">2、People may have different opinions on…</p>
 <p data-pid="jyhm3NV3">人…可能有不同的看法</p>
 <p data-pid="6uYXk3iM">3、People's views on....vary from person to person. Some hold that.... However, others believe that...</p>
 <p data-pid="8B7eQgey">人们对...的观点因人而异。有些人认为…然而其他人却认为.....</p>
 <p data-pid="KIENlD9b">4、Different people hold different attitudes toward (failure).</p>
 <p data-pid="hrphMQo5">对(失败)人们的态度各不相同。</p>
 <p data-pid="pE6pRWIf">5、There are different opinions among people as to...</p>
 <p data-pid="_3Rq7shH">人们关于....的观点大不相同</p>
 <p data-pid="PPjYNFIb"><b>【2】结尾部分</b></p>
 <p data-pid="y109i1fE">1、There is no doubt that (job-hopping) has its drawbacks as well as merits.</p>
 <p data-pid="L9ChoCAC">毫无疑问，(跳槽)有优点也有缺点。</p>
 <p data-pid="N5IIx8C6">2、Taking into account all these factors, we may reasonably come to the conclusion that...</p>
 <p data-pid="O2rlbPxn">考虑所有这些因素，我们可能会得出合理的结论...... 3、All in all, we cannot live without....But at the same time we must try to find out new ways to cope with the problems that would arise.</p>
 <p data-pid="eLd3KIRq">总之，我们没有...是无法生活的。但同时，我们必须寻求新的解决办法来对付可 能出现的新问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="Lxs1hV5E">4、Taking all these factors into consideration, we naturally come to the conclusion that...</p>
 <p data-pid="1OIxbtew">把所有这些因素加以考虑，我们自然会得出结论....</p>
 <p data-pid="1VIR2xGo">5、Hence /Therefore, we'd better come to the conclusion that...</p>
 <p data-pid="wNI90nny"><b>【提出建议】</b></p>
 <p data-pid="q50cDg0W">1、There is no doubt that enough concern must be paid to the problem of...</p>
 <p data-pid="iDE2O0Rx">毫无疑问，对....问题应予以足够的重视。</p>
 <p data-pid="Rr6i4XJK">2、Only in this way can we..</p>
 <p data-pid="oufMiqiH">只有这样，我们才能...</p>
 <p data-pid="DOfIifyX">3、It is high time that we put an end to the (trend).</p>
 <p data-pid="9mg5S1mk">该是我们停止这一(趋势)的时候了33。</p>
 <p data-pid="2JWF06lB">4、It is time to take the advice of...and put special emphasis on the improvement of…</p>
 <p data-pid="QJeDmzcz">该是采纳...的建议，并对...的进展给予特殊重视的时候了。</p>
 <p data-pid="jP1HVPPr">5、It must be realized that...</p>
 <p data-pid="d2a2hMy9">我们必须意识到...</p>
 <p data-pid="ptqz8sm_"><b>【列出解决办法】</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ozoRvnJ0">1、People have figured out many ways to solve this problem.</p>
 <p data-pid="I8ifASlz">人们已找出许多办法来解决这个问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="wzBNuLHg">2、The best way to solve the troubles is...</p>
 <p data-pid="nXLh0NlM">解决这些麻烦的最好办法是....</p>
 <p data-pid="rJzi_D2u">3、Here are some suggestions for handling...</p>
 <p data-pid="Qa73KVQr">这是如何处理某事的一些建议。</p>
 <p data-pid="ZvAr9EWR"><b>【给出原因】</b></p>
 <p data-pid="DNndjxxK">1、I quite agree with the statement that...The reasons are chiefly as follows.</p>
 <p data-pid="InG-9eza">我十分赞同这一论述，即.....其主要原因如下：</p>
 <p data-pid="0O_Q8il8">2、This phenomenon exists for a number of reasons. First... Second ....Third,...</p>
 <p data-pid="Cm9KQ9SG">这一现象的存在是有许多原因的。首先...第二....三....</p>
 <p data-pid="3qtgqFvA">3、Why did..? For one thing...For another... Perhaps the primary reason is...</p>
 <p data-pid="m84ATGaI">为什么会...? 一个原因是...一个原因是...或许其主要原因是....</p>

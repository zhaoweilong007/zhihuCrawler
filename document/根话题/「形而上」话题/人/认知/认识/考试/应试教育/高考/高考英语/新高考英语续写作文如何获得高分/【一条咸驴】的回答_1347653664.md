# 新高考英语续写作文如何获得高分?
- 点赞数：5338
- 更新时间：2022年01月03日18时47分37秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/361004221/answer/1347653664
<body>
 <p data-pid="UMAC9Idl">山东20年高考，英语146，两篇作文（续写和应用文）为了攒人品，俺将经验分享如下。</p>
 <p data-pid="cMbPsAVK">模板</p>
 <p data-pid="blkg0CW8">有一条龙的模版，就是进考场前已经写好半篇了，考场上只需编几个合时宜的句子即可。</p>
 <p data-pid="RjAa0Nsg">造句</p>
 <p data-pid="NBg4p3IR">还有自己造句…不要等考场上再造…</p>
 <p data-pid="tX4UYYJf">词典</p>
 <p data-pid="A0tqRCl_">利用词典，先闭眼想，那个词比较生动？然后去查，自己造句背造好的句子</p>
 <p data-pid="L7Z_ZV-T">上课</p>
 <p data-pid="tlg03rFc">上课时不要只局限于背词背短语，那个词触动心弦马上想续写怎么用</p>
 <p data-pid="LI3UXjxW">生僻词？</p>
 <p data-pid="Incig48V">不要用生僻词…并不会加分…</p>
 <p data-pid="GR2WyDm_">练习</p>
 <p data-pid="9_7mlJvD">练的时候，不一定用题目，自己编一个生活场景更易写出手感…写的时候，不一定动笔，早读时口述，效率极高（高三后期积累到一定程度后）</p>
 <p data-pid="CqRKdIPQ">升华句子</p>
 <p data-pid="YyOHvHdJ">Storm make trees take deeper roots</p>
 <p data-pid="f1Og9dF6">译：磨难让人坚强（比喻义）</p>
 <p data-pid="5R7jXGLa">better to light one candle than to curse the darkness</p>
 <p data-pid="YPs6lJXM">译:与其诅咒黑暗，不如点亮红烛</p>
 <p data-pid="Sdgcu5sB">It was so short, so bright, like a shooting star giving off much light and make my life more beautiful.（人或事）</p>
 <p data-pid="fTTXUfbt">译:它是那么短暂，却又那样灿烂，就像流星一瞬，其光夺目，让我沉醉。</p>
 <p data-pid="Bz1mkZCa">In me , I knew I had grown, older, wiser, humbler, brighter(几乎每次都用）</p>
 <p data-pid="N4uvVLio">译：在我内心深处，我明明白白地知道，我成长了，变得更成熟，更明智，更谦虚，更灿烂。</p>
 <p data-pid="wU1wMmMM">what seems to us bitter trials are often blessing in disguise</p>
 <p data-pid="qrUiAyv8">译：塞翁失马，焉知祸福</p>
 <p data-pid="NFarDhyT">every cloud has a silver lining</p>
 <p data-pid="l6x4i6p0">译：上帝关上一扇门，也会为你打开一扇窗（比喻义）</p>
 <p data-pid="pzbS110k">every single act of kindness will finally contribute to a better world as well as generosity ,gratitude,and trust.(阅读里看的）</p>
 <p data-pid="MJY_ftpe">译：每一件小小的好人好事，连同这世界上的仁慈，感激，信任，都会铸就一个更好的世界。</p>
 <p data-pid="hy4jOvGh">Never had I.anticipated that a tiny white lie would inject such great power in to others and change there life</p>
 <p data-pid="3OYNPr6j">译：我真的从来没想到，一个小小的，善意的谎言，会给他人带来那么多力量，会改变他们的生命</p>
 <p data-pid="Qeu1xVQ2">blessed is the life greeted with many ups and downs,because those make you feel alive.(原句为many things that kill u,大家应该听过）</p>
 <p data-pid="ZeQqOt6Z">our greatest glory consist not in (the fame,wealth,or reputation),but in (our good nature which spread itself like oil over the troubled sea of life)</p>
 <p data-pid="MzGDaMXX">括号内只是例子，实际用时要改。</p>
 <p data-pid="-6bF-26n">有时结尾可以写xxx给自己留下了深刻回忆</p>
 <p data-pid="GR16RqOG">比如1.留下东西：it stood out as a shiny reminder of (sth important eg.the voyage of life)</p>
 <p data-pid="8ttEGsSA">2.留下回忆随风逝去：vanishing so quickly as if never existed.</p>
 <p data-pid="kPgzkcmY">有时你需要否定一些坏的行为来达到升华的目的：(we should always commit in mind that )jealous and hatred get you nowhere in life.</p>
 <p data-pid="A2NzRBiv">给你一些启示</p>
 <p data-pid="3y0jJw7W">sometimes being lost means you are exactly on the journey!</p>
 <p data-pid="B0Q1lmS1">展望一下未来</p>
 <p data-pid="KBAfc8Ol">embrace every possibility in your life and believe in yourself.</p>
 <p data-pid="nfrK4gny">tommorrow is always bound to be a brand new day!(虽然我自己不太用）</p>
 <p data-pid="Zn1uQoq3">一条龙</p>
 <p data-pid="m4F5rSLb">遇见…环境…心理活动…表情……模版如下</p>
 <p data-pid="XuK4TrTK">1(关于经历）、She was (desperate/astonished /surprised)to be greeted with…</p>
 <p data-pid="f1ZHeHLG">xx的话：his words fell heavily on my ear,inspiring my soul/his words washed against my face like a strong current ,trying to draw me in</p>
 <p data-pid="hPfPZj2s">译：她的话语重重的砸在我的耳朵上/心上，启发这我的灵魂；他的话像激流，冲刷这我的面庞，试图把我卷入一场未知的狂潮。</p>
 <p data-pid="S_DOe0ei">（别人怎么着你了）he stares bewildered down at me for a millisecond</p>
 <p data-pid="aogoIA5s">2、（关于环境）The sun blazed down from the clear blue sky(delighted)</p>
 <p data-pid="Tay8uGt0">译：太阳当空照</p>
 <p data-pid="8fvXiMwU">(大风） The chill wind roared mercilessly, threatening to swallow them up(frightened)</p>
 <p data-pid="TZ1L_G07">译：这里的风好大，我好怕</p>
 <p data-pid="CFst4dG-">（安静） It was awfully quiet except my footsteps hitting the ground/my heavy breathing (安静，十分实用）</p>
 <p data-pid="fSSnEYuC">译：这里真静，我只能听见我的脚步duangduang地扣在地上，还有我重重的呼吸</p>
 <p data-pid="67nubcwE">（彩霞） the flaming sunset casted an orange over xx ,causing a big shadow behind him.</p>
 <p data-pid="4v_2qtXV">译：云彩真好看，xx拖着个长长的影子</p>
 <p data-pid="3ubRzyrJ">（比喻版霞光） the first ray of sunshine broke through the quickly dissipating cloud</p>
 <p data-pid="SMR5k2VE">译：就像云散日出，大快人心</p>
 <p data-pid="L860Qo1V">（空气） the slight chill in the air was balanced magnificently by the gentle shower of light</p>
 <p data-pid="tcTXGmQ7">译：不冷不热，酥胡</p>
 <p data-pid="_tNqF9YE">（树叶）the bright-colored leaves shining/ quivering in the air/沙沙声rustle(verb)如with leaves rustling under my boot 嘎吱声crunch同上</p>
 <p data-pid="jhR-QgmE">（蓝天白云）clouds drifted by,in reflection of the clear blue sky/the sun blazed down from the clear blue sky</p>
 <p data-pid="DJ9tOqOW">译：蓝蓝的天空，白白的云</p>
 <p data-pid="o-_-d7i4">（晚上）it was night, but the moonlight filtered down through the trees ,dapping gently and casting ripping shadows at the leaves swaying in the gentle breeze（来自小说）</p>
 <p data-pid="x3gOT-yN">译：晚上，月光透过树隙撒下来，轻轻柔柔地穿过着细碎的影子，抚摸着微微晃动的树叶。</p>
 <p data-pid="dp9QL4ty">（晚上）A hidden moon shone a starshine light over the snow,giving to things mysterious visibility but no exact shape.</p>
 <p data-pid="hNVSJMXo">一轮弯月撒下点点稀疏的光，它所照耀下的事物，似成了若隐若现的存在。</p>
 <p data-pid="nAJdwn2r">the first ray of sunshine declared it self through the quickly dissipating cloud.(这句可以用作比喻～ta干了什么，像阳光如箭破云天）（来自小说）</p>
 <p data-pid="iz0n_-yz">（下雨）the first heavy drops of rain announced themselves , tapping out a disjointed rhythm on the tin roof.（来自小说）</p>
 <p data-pid="aRUcFtoB">译：刚下的几滴雨儿，像是宣示自己存在感一样，非得在铁皮顶上敲出个一点也不和谐的节奏来。</p>
 <p data-pid="er2Ag5qg">（火苗～）Its exhausted light beat up and down the wall,across the raftes,like a bird trying to find its way out of the room.</p>
 <p data-pid="4hgo0j6g">译：它这个奄奄一息的火苗，就在墙上上下其手，如同一个受困的鸟儿，像不顾一切地飞出去一样。</p>
 <p data-pid="c3wrp3vW">(比喻啥像花一样，很好玩的比喻）xxxx like a flower boasting its blossoms</p>
 <p data-pid="P4rmqvM0">译：xx就像花 炫耀着它的怒放的生命</p>
 <p data-pid="FujJ7HUB">（紧张环境的抽象一写）he was within a hairbreath of death.</p>
 <p data-pid="0OVESQyU">译：千钧一发</p>
 <p data-pid="2Oal_Er6">(微风拂面，）the gentle breeze caressed my cheeks and soothed my anger.</p>
 <p data-pid="fazI4n0B">译：轻轻柔柔的风，抚摸着我的脸，平息了我的怒火</p>
 <p data-pid="gyfo1KJW">当然还有常见的breathtaking magnificent等词语</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DFl-CkEA">3（关于心理）、millions of thoughts were dancing gracefully in my head/swimming in my head/running wild in my head/racing through my head(心乱如麻或者心情好啥的）</p>
 <p data-pid="qKMIBJb5">译：一堆想法在我的脑海中愉快地舞蹈/游来游去/横冲直撞</p>
 <p data-pid="btWrPW6X">i was on the brink of collapse when...(快崩溃了）</p>
 <p data-pid="Viq0xOG-">i racked my brain desperately, trying to remember...(绞尽脑汁）</p>
 <p data-pid="dG6WGfGh">译：我绞尽脑汁地想，试图回忆...</p>
 <p data-pid="fMasZt9f">my head was confused/ jammed with various options</p>
 <p data-pid="LWaX-QOl">译：不知如何是好</p>
 <p data-pid="92MwEk_y">rage bubbled below my chest</p>
 <p data-pid="o9iSf8kV">译：怒火在我心中燃烧</p>
 <p data-pid="FaZpjXBL">i felt a wave of warmth washing over my heart(频率巨高hhh)</p>
 <p data-pid="2YvUu50c">译：我心中涌过一阵暖流</p>
 <p data-pid="L0_pvssR">还有一些词heart-stopping. heart-warming.nerve-racking</p>
 <p data-pid="hWdMJMa_">（累死了）fear and fatigue has stripped her of every inch of energy</p>
 <p data-pid="HrSLV8C3">译：又怂又累，我没力了</p>
 <p data-pid="QZp8qG3l">（绝望的要哭了）she finally gave way to sobs that threatened to overwhelm herself(threaten 特别拟人，tears threatened to spill down her cheeks)</p>
 <p data-pid="1swCLsU8">译：她终于被一直威胁要压垮她的泪水击退了</p>
 <p data-pid="xILqPhnD">（绝望）with the last piece of hope fading away, she kneeled down on the ground, letting hopeless fear and fatigue stripe her of every inch of energy(这是俺组装的万金油句子，一直想用奈何太细致太长了……）</p>
 <p data-pid="t26k9o3V">译：最后一丝希望也破灭了，她跪倒在地，就任凭恐惧和绝望剥夺了她的所有力气</p>
 <p data-pid="eCaVy_Rr">(千钧一发之际的诡异宁静）For one heart-stopping moment,i found time has stopped,suspending on the brink of chaos</p>
 <p data-pid="r0Jb3ogw">译：有那么心脏骤停的一瞬间，我感觉时间静止了，就像是暴风雨前的宁静</p>
 <p data-pid="xuU2k7uC">呆若木鸡 dumbfounded rooted to the spot</p>
 <p data-pid="hkHkehRM">(触动心弦）it tugged at my heartstring</p>
 <p data-pid="WjOaoQjV">几个碎片</p>
 <p data-pid="3WN3IFVr">碎片1-threaten比喻（莫名万金油）</p>
 <p data-pid="yqCrBJfp">如：the fierce wind threatened to swallow her up.</p>
 <p data-pid="3GERxQTV">又如the merciless fire threatened to swallow the tiny,shaking house.</p>
 <p data-pid="8l-tJ-Ad">碎片2-副词扎入(当你在考场上现编句子可以试试这招，当然还是不建议现编句子。。）</p>
 <p data-pid="fpE1DJu8">increasingly：getting increasingly (or exceptionally or terribly)nervous/</p>
 <p data-pid="_TxIUKpl">with a smile appearing on his face increasingly</p>
 <p data-pid="o3qka4Pj">terribly: feeling terribly sorry..</p>
 <p data-pid="txBbyOsM">unbearably 同上，unbearable也挺有用</p>
 <p data-pid="6qhzNlBK">undeniably impressive.</p>
 <p data-pid="tz8OpqSO">at an incredible rate.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZW0hrwGr">4.做出动作反应/表情反应（这一类一般写不出啥体现水平的句子，个人建议简洁生动为佳）</p>
 <p data-pid="PO84hcDz">写几个栗子……简短点好，不然有生搬硬套之咸hahaha</p>
 <p data-pid="N3pPxKKl">(皱眉）She frowned and pushed her lips together into a thin line(来自ferryman)</p>
 <p data-pid="AQZu8n0A">译：她皱起了眉头，抿了抿嘴</p>
 <p data-pid="AUtHfHX4">（靓仔落泪）...with tears sparking in her eyes and streaming down her face</p>
 <p data-pid="mi-qRONr">（委屈巴巴）i gazed at her , eyes wide pool of sadness</p>
 <p data-pid="K6YDzxXk">（得意洋洋）eyes glowing and face bursting with pride</p>
 <p data-pid="JdIonaTC">(飒爽微笑，过于高级，慎用）And again she saw the smile,perfect and handsome in the startling whiteness of its expanse between dark lips.</p>
 <p data-pid="EZGZPS8S">(羞）a rosy flush spreading across my cheeks</p>
 <p data-pid="gESMg4eZ">睫毛微颤 with a flutter of her long dark eyelashes.</p>
 <p data-pid="vzQ06LBD">哈哈大笑she roared with laughter.</p>
 <p data-pid="tQQ2DNO5">can't take my eyes off it!(比如此篇回答，，，手动狗头）</p>
 <p data-pid="YxYfQNxF">提供几个动词，高中生同学萌可以自己造句练习/查词典也可以发现一些宝藏</p>
 <p data-pid="HTotN2jB">mutter</p>
 <p data-pid="1ekvQ9ST">seize</p>
 <p data-pid="2sir3huf">ruffle</p>
 <p data-pid="uxyoWNny">shuffle</p>
 <p data-pid="OH9_SegQ">slip</p>
 <p data-pid="J3EczicA">gasp</p>
 <p data-pid="pzlS0psE">blaze</p>
 <p data-pid="gxOi4h6l">creep</p>
 <p data-pid="VZ5VVB27">explode</p>
 <p data-pid="X5nZs_Y-">dash</p>
 <p data-pid="vuC65lIA">glide</p>
 <p data-pid="RmSxKmW6">roar</p>
 <p data-pid="WKDDVey2">pound(去查一下词典吧 ！大概率有惊喜）</p>
 <p data-pid="t8VXQVn2">外貌描写</p>
 <p data-pid="z9hoqQvn">人分为女人（少女，mom,老奶奶）男人（男孩，husband,老爷爷，流浪汉，从事某种特定职业者如警察）baby(没有语言能力）</p>
 <p data-pid="7BFklPU8">卡姿兰大眼睛big,blue,ocean eyes /shining dark eyes</p>
 <p data-pid="wxq6woUf">油腻男a short balding man with a fat belly</p>
 <p data-pid="KvoLapUD">xx with deep wrinkles on xx老了</p>
 <p data-pid="ezBFe4_8">我之前想过，岁月催人老该咋说：the sun and wind had taken the sparkle from her eyes and left them a sober grey.</p>
 <p data-pid="lS2fNNAa">she was plump and middle aged ,an old-fhashioned nanny.</p>
 <p data-pid="UZfXPvKE">俺平时很少用动作描写……背一些词句啥的有时候就为了用那一个词写一个句子老感觉在凑字数……so一般略写（haha)</p>
 <p data-pid="H4UkWPeK">不过万金油短语还是香～</p>
 <p data-pid="fN8xl0HJ">try in a xx attempt to do xx＝vain ,desperate,visible,etc.</p>
 <p data-pid="wryVUc5V">如此一来，整个结构就灰常简单了</p>

 <p data-pid="cJLbmgW6">1.遇见了什么 一般会给出 对此作出初反应</p>
 <p data-pid="GD6wWiSv">Dumbfounded, I just stood rooted to the spot, letting myself drown in the vast ocean of warmth.(上来一句话显示出你的气场）</p>
 <p data-pid="cNX8LMZj">注:这里遇见的东西可以是，话语，场面，动作</p>
 <p data-pid="drEp9ihC">2.环境如何: 利用语文中的正衬/反衬</p>
 <p data-pid="cmXLjiau">The slight chill in the air was balanced magnificently by the gentle shower of light.</p>
 <p data-pid="gAkeib-e">注：环境有：安静，聒噪；蓝天白云；太阳；树林；空气；气温；夜空；花朵；农场；水雨冰火。可以参见上面背一背</p>

 <p data-pid="TdW18vEE">再写一点关于平时的积累叭</p>
 <p data-pid="rsje2t6O">英语俺还是上手的比较早的～虽然身处山东河北交界某贫困县唯一高中(这么一写感觉有点惨ha)，但是我在上高中前已经背完了高中3500词，高一刷了四级，高二高三又背了六级。读了几本英文小说（filpped ferryman Harrypotter pride &amp;prejudice )也看过英文杂志和报纸/科普书籍</p>
 <p data-pid="4TMcNtiW">以下我实际在做的内容</p>
 <p data-pid="-rjdV1B1">早读：背老师画的词汇/测试范文</p>
 <p data-pid="GLXOo3G2">实际：用词汇口头造句，越高大上越好/简单看两遍范文之后自己口头写一篇（开始有亿点点困难）</p>

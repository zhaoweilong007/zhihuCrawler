 <p data-pid="uF7nxbkW"><b>******************************************PART 1 逻辑连接词*******************************************</b><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="R2929Wcb"><b><u>###1.1 让步###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="aUmERB-m">1. <b>Although</b>（更书面）= <b>though</b>(更口语) =<b>even if </b>(即使，更偏假设性)=<b>even though</b>（虽然，更偏事实性）+句子</p>
 <p data-pid="YcTZKJNj">注：不能与but连用。</p>
 <p data-pid="q3HX7cSn"><b>Devoted though</b> we are to prosperity and freedom, we cannot shake off the judgmental strand of justice.用倒装</p>
 <p data-pid="lM24DQJG">2. <b>No matter how/what/who</b>等= <b>疑问句+ever</b></p>
 <p data-pid="oRdvsHEj"><b>No matter who/Whoever</b> you are, you must keep the law.</p>
 <p data-pid="PixY_5Ap">注意：疑问句+ever 可以引导名词性从句</p>
 <p data-pid="EllAq6f-"><b>Whoever</b>（≠ no matter who）comes will be welcome.</p>
 <p data-pid="qbhP3GvZ">3. …, <b>as long as</b>…</p>
 <p data-pid="eT21JP4b">You can do what you want, <b>as long as</b> you like.</p>
 <p data-pid="EKZckr85">4. <b>名词/表语/动词+ as（though）倒装</b>，。。。，表“纵使”</p>
 <p data-pid="9dUBWWoR">Object <b>as/though</b> you may, I’ll go。</p>
 <p data-pid="09qysaSe">Small<b> as</b> atoms are, they are made up of still smaller units。</p>
 <p data-pid="ls9csZu4">Lover of towns<b> as </b>I am, I realize that I owe a debt to my early country life.</p>
 <p data-pid="oROLIjL-">5. <b>Whether…or…</b> 正反两方面，。。。表“不论。。。与否”</p>
 <p data-pid="d1Se9-y0"><b>Whether </b>you believe it <b>or not</b>, it's true.</p>
 <p data-pid="Fyz2UjeS">You'll have to attend the ceremony <b>whether </b>you're free <b>or</b> busy.</p>
 <p data-pid="KSHT5r1Z">6. <b>Be原型+主语+表语（n, adj）= whether+主语+be动词+表语（n, adj）</b>。不论。一文学性强的虚拟语气，表让步。</p>
 <p data-pid="sFTVZS8C"><b>Be it </b>historical tradition, faith and culture, or social system, values and level of development, those countries or regions are often different from one another.</p>
 <p data-pid="uDnrOPzr">7.<b> Despite</b>+n/ving =<b> in spite of</b> 尽管. （不能加句子）</p>
 <p data-pid="2U7C9RSP">Despite the fact that + 句子（太累赘）</p>
 <p data-pid="pv3OzE6o">Despite myself, … 情不自禁地…</p>
 <p data-pid="LjFLSLgN">Her words were so satirical（讽刺的）that I lost my temper<b> in spite of</b> myself。</p>
 <p data-pid="Zx7fY_pr">8. <b>Notwithstanding +sth</b>尽管</p>
 <p data-pid="7wOa-eVD">But <b>notwithstanding </b>its ancient lineage, it is open to the following challenge.</p>
 <p data-pid="vVLcAi1h">9. <b>Albeit </b>虽然即使</p>
 <p data-pid="VBSL74aG">You are to be given one method of communication with your rival, <b>albeit </b>indirect communication.</p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="q8QGJKwi"><b><u>###1.2 原因###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="CkUSdTQX"><b>Since</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Sx7oaUHd"><b>As</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0DUPkOFt"><b>Because</b></p>
 <p data-pid="AF7xur3h"><b>For</b>+句子</p>
 <p data-pid="cGf1ln96"><b>As a result of sth</b>. =<b>As a consequence of sth</b>由于sth, sth是原因。</p>
 <p data-pid="v9b7V_Ke"><b>Because of</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zuzeauuS"><b>Owing to sth 介词</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kHpwi7VO"><b>Thanks to...</b>后面原因一般是积极性的</p>
 <p data-pid="pZdKdl_w"><b>By virtue of sth</b></p>
 <p data-pid="OTYS-aS-"><b>On account of</b></p>
 <p data-pid="yBw1PSS5"><br><b>Be due to</b></p>
 <p data-pid="fjDQyYXz"><b>Owe to sth </b>动词</p>
 <p data-pid="ueebz19I"><b>Account for=result in </b>表示导致</p>
 <p data-pid="9kY2VFex">Systematic economic forces account for the disastrous loses.</p>
 <p data-pid="HbjPHn67"><b>Attribute…to…</b>把。。。归因于。。。</p>
 <p data-pid="VO1vuSEc">He attributed the opposition to a deep-seated attitude in military.</p>
 <p data-pid="cW-uWwH3"><b>Sth would explain why …</b></p>
 <p data-pid="A34PSFF1"><b>For this reason，...</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HYUmREct"><b>For one reason or another, </b>由于某种原因（不具体指明）</p>
 <p data-pid="3OOALsmK">His travel articles are always, for one reason or another, fascinating.</p>
 <p data-pid="zWRLPDUH"><b>For one thing</b>, 在众多原因中指出其中一个</p>
 <p data-pid="Qxz9pZqM">A lot of people don’t take the time to read contracts. For one thing, they’re often in very tiny print.</p>
 <p data-pid="OyXx8FgN"><b>On this/that account，</b> 由于这个、那个原因</p>
 <p data-pid="SULaOdyJ">She was divorced, and on that account alone my mother disliked her.</p>
 <p data-pid="SDZHNF8s"><b>The reason why+结果/ that+原因 </b></p>
 <p data-pid="Z7jV3bAu">Greed is the reason why they do not deserve the money.</p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6YPJUS1e"><b><u>###1.3 结果###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="66EGGpD-"><b>Accordingly=Consequently</b></p>
 <p data-pid="4oGqEN0-"><b>原因+Result in+结果</b></p>
 <p data-pid="b3lKmZry">This outcome will result in children starting bullying younger ones.</p>
 <p data-pid="_3BDQrRz"><b>Thus</b></p>
 <p data-pid="XVwLDASF"><b>Hence</b></p>
 <p data-pid="l2p2A2NI"><b>So</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vaYR5Fk_"><b>Therefore</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wDuka9e2">Excessive greed is therefore a vice that a good society should discourage if it can.</p>
 <p data-pid="i9oRScoJ"><b>As such</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zCWtellM"><b>As a result </b>最终，因此，结果</p>
 <p data-pid="ofYkVfAw"><b>As a consequence/In consequence,...</b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="K4hWj3xz"><b><u>###1.4 目的###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="eWd1Fgf5"><b>So</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LSf4hlR6"><b>So that</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vqNwdffL">Should law be neutral towards competing conceptions of virtue, so that people can be free to choose for themselves the best way to live?</p>
 <p data-pid="FNLPw7AW"><b> In order that</b></p>
 <p data-pid="pRK0IJOa">Regular checks are required in order that safety standards are maintained.</p>
 <p data-pid="oZ5PeW9L"><b>To do sth,…</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Vhnhea7x">To answer it, we have to explore the meaning of justice.</p>
 <p data-pid="156xmp9R"><b>In order to do sth</b></p>
 <p data-pid="FLnrRBSv">In order to determine who deserves what, we have to determine what virtues are worthy of honor and reward.</p>
 <p data-pid="U1QyXmTG"><b>For the sake of sth=For sth’s sake 为了sth的目的</b></p>
 <p data-pid="STT9xyTw">I hope you’re not doing this just for the sake of the money.</p>
 <p data-pid="_Qk-bsaR">For clarity’s sake, I’d like to reword my statement.</p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zGgkGDGs"><b><u>###1.5 转折###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="3XCJvgfC"><b>However,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="VxvxVMjO"><b>Yet 轻微转折</b></p>
 <p data-pid="sYSebQ8U">And yet we worry when judgments about virtue finds their way into laws.</p>
 <p data-pid="CP7Ouxrl"><b>Whereas</b></p>
 <p data-pid="JSpWdvMT"><b>Conversely</b></p>
 <p data-pid="6fBWUw8U"><b>Nevertheless</b></p>
 <p data-pid="sCHy5PYb"><b>Otherwise, </b>否则</p>
 <p data-pid="NYFx_-1D"><b>On the contrary</b></p>
 <p data-pid="dJH20Rbi"><b>Instead,</b>表示对上述方式的完全反转</p>
 <p data-pid="azjwETlh">Instead, a just society respects each people’s freedom to choose his or her own conception of the<br>
  good life.</p>
 <p data-pid="9IpQxKS2"><b>Contrarily</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Nr3oG2nh"><b>Inversely</b></p>
 <p data-pid="oCfm4xuu"><b>Contrariwise</b></p>
 <p data-pid="KxAk4Bkl"><b>Oppositely,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="57mLKPBx"><b>Instead of sth</b></p>
 <p data-pid="27Z5q1wK"><b>As opposed to</b> 表示与前述对象的反转对比</p>
 <p data-pid="nDDDSgZo">The cost of these planes is £3 million, as opposed to the £2 million charged by their competitors.</p>
 <p data-pid="FstY0NOO"><b>While </b>引导从句，表明前后情况不同，做个对比</p>
 <p data-pid="T-5KQ_Ww"><b>Rather than. </b>并不是、而不、并没有, 前后对等</p>
 <p data-pid="uGTw-7nf">Be honest rather than clever.诚实胜于机巧。</p>
 <p data-pid="iWrzB04R">This policy might lead them to stay in harm’s way rather than flee to safety.</p>
 <p data-pid="lBqoml7W">Rather than press for maximum advantages, people look out for one another.</p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gb5f90Bp"><b><u>###1.6对比###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="lRG4JQJi"><b>While </b>既可以对比相反的东西，也可以对比稍有不同的</p>
 <p data-pid="fSYW9D0F">Ancient theories start with virtue while modern theories start with freedom.</p>
 <p data-pid="J21JzLD9"><b>On the other hand,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="f27dM9iQ"><b>By/In comparison/contrast </b>对比之下</p>
 <p data-pid="3AcSqJRk">Their lives were exciting and glamorous; ours seemed dull by comparison.</p>
 <p data-pid="bBQc631p">The virtue argument, by contrast, rests on the judgment that greed is a vice that state should<br>
  discourage.</p>
 <p data-pid="jwFR8iSg"><b>Alternatively,</b>提供另一种思路或方法建议</p>
 <p data-pid="T-GqgZdo"><b>In/By comparison with sth</b>介词</p>
 <p data-pid="eo8MiXOA">This essay will briefly review the cons and pros of imprisonment in comparison with job training.</p>
 <p data-pid="YI7TXtap">By comparison with the lively teenagers, the adults were a joyless lot.</p>
 <p data-pid="gvb8yodF"><b>Compared with/to</b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aqrcvd4J"><b><u>###1.7 类比###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="ROnADnF1"><b>Just as/like</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xX3FO2ML"><b>Similarly,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="bOCiNhnc"><b>Likewise,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="p7AWqUWA"><b>Identically,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="esX88ila"><b>Equivalently,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ezXplm9A"><b>As …as</b></p>
 <p data-pid="D2BQeAuR">They have sacrificed as surely, and as honorably, as those who have lost a limb.</p>
 <p data-pid="xCI5BDFj"><b>The same as</b></p>
 <p data-pid="pr5x88Z4">Tourists find this park just the same as the one they visited previously.</p>
 <p data-pid="zOi6nHog"><b>In the same way/manner</b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yjjqKvT2"><b><u>###1.8 递进###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="CMdIFUDX"><b>Further</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hlt_NaOz"><b>Furthermore</b></p>
 <p data-pid="yHIUDo9c"><b>Besides,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8LG547Ji"><b>Moreover</b></p>
 <p data-pid="X2cLK274"><b>In addition to sth,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="XEHxsOjO">In addition to outlining the causes of this problem, this essay will also propose solutions.</p>
 <p data-pid="N_uWzaOV"><b>In addition,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="PaaGxqtc"><b>What’s more</b></p>
 <p data-pid="olwJWTOH"><b>Apart from B…</b>其有两个意思，既可以：除了B（包括B），也表示：除了B（不包括B）</p>
 <p data-pid="9T6ypxd9"><b>Additionally,</b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0aFjdbQ-"><b><u>###1.9 举例###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="eRsGlAQS"><b>The likes of 就像</b></p>
 <p data-pid="c6qFm3PN"><b>Such as </b></p>
 <p data-pid="ldzR8Sbs"><b>Like</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HuQhFUl8">Their purchases of necessities like safe lodging are forced.</p>
 <p data-pid="JNjpGlNW"><b>Including</b>, 包括</p>
 <p data-pid="nW2iQwWy"><b>e.g. </b>直接加sth,中间没有逗号</p>
 <p data-pid="iC335Hzn"><b>…is a case in point. </b>正在点儿上，指例子可以很好地阐述这个观点</p>
 <p data-pid="cbg_FOtE"><b>Here is a case where…</b>指出例子</p>
 <p data-pid="dUmaMMVJ">Here is a case where the economy as a whole seemed to outweigh considerations of fairness.</p>
 <p data-pid="YSxXi5wC"><b>Take ... as an example</b> 以。。。为例</p>
 <p data-pid="t4DzVTxZ"><b>In some instances. </b>在某些情况中</p>
 <p data-pid="Q7uCVTL4"><b>For instances,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="VOtz96cU"><b>For example,</b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9UBbOsUd"><b><u>###1.10 假设###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="YQ2GnFCY"><b>If</b></p>
 <p data-pid="1_29WVkj">If its price is higher, manufacturers will find it worth their while to produce more goods.</p>
 <p data-pid="OG7BVkOI"><b>Given/provided/providing+名词/that 从句</b>， 考虑到、假如</p>
 <p data-pid="qRBTsX4g">You can go out to play provided that you finish your homework first.</p>
 <p data-pid="mBGUPppH">It’s a lot easier to get good marks on a short-answer question, providing you do it well.</p>
 <p data-pid="KoClGAbZ"><b>Only if</b>,唯有，唯一的条件是</p>
 <p data-pid="7UtFXvXZ">The rebels say they are ready to stop fighting, but only if the prisoners are released.</p>
 <p data-pid="S3shjavZ"><b>On condition that，</b>以。。。为前提，只有</p>
 <p data-pid="9ongdMXQ"><b>Should </b>用于状语从句，表示“万一”，用法与if类似，可以在主句前，也可以在主句后；从句动词用原型。</p>
 <p data-pid="lx3IeZww">This switch can be used to disengage the autopilot system <b>should </b>a malfunction occur in the system.</p>
 <p data-pid="uvUzelqc"><b>Should</b> it rain tomorrow， the meeting would be put off.</p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0EMzE94u"><b><u>###1.11 解释，转述###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="goMn0TPh"><b>In other words,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wsGgkyfU">Prosperity matters, in other words, because it contributes to our welfare.</p>
 <p data-pid="OA94h9JY"><b>That is, that is to say,</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-uLgMw4Y"><b>i.e.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="U9FfSAht"><b>Namely</b></p>
 <p data-pid="7zHDUCNA">Some groups, namely students and pensioners, will benefit from the new tax.</p>
 <p data-pid="T6MZ8Kvw"><b>Mean that </b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Xhnrxy0y"><b><u>###1.12 并列###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="9mvOMPTY"><b>And</b></p>
 <p data-pid="8z8H26v4"><b>A as well as B,</b> 强调as前的名词或者形容词A, 不仅B，而且A</p>
 <p data-pid="ShBzwHin">We must learn to look at problems all-sidedly,seeing the reverse as well as the obverse side of things. 强调要看到reverse即反面</p>
 <p data-pid="5Mo2k0qQ"><b>not only A, but also B</b>, 不同上，强调后面B，不仅A，而且B</p>
 <p data-pid="CZqjWzTx">He is a statesman as well as a scholar=He is no less a statesman than a scholar=He is not only a scholar but also a statesman.</p>
 <p data-pid="OfJ6-USb"><b>and...A...as well. </b>则强调A</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bS_HeyXX"><b><u>###1.13 说明观点###</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="oDpUVPk4"><b><u>###认为/支持/反对</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="4AgrPDB4"><b><u>###认为：</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="yCnxFswC"><b>Argue that.</b>有“争论到”之意，用于对陈述相反观点</p>
 <p data-pid="PKyZziOZ">Some economists argued that the law was misconceived.</p>
 <p data-pid="v72OFdIL"><b>Believe that</b></p>
 <p data-pid="FT8BNDCm">Some believed that the exchange of goods should be governed by a just price.</p>
 <p data-pid="gn_IsIpF"><b>Acknowledge that.</b><br>
   承认，一般表示某人承认，之后会转折</p>
 <p data-pid="JaUEqsKg">I have to acknowledge that it has the advantage of high efficiency.</p>
 <p data-pid="5XzFbN0m"><b>Maintain that</b></p>
 <p data-pid="S4eBi_Vq">Defenders of this law maintain that, under certain conditions, the free market is not truly free.</p>
 <p data-pid="yerEB2FH"><b>Hold that</b></p>
 <p data-pid="itqt1v_R">Many people hold that government should be neutral on matters of virtue and vice.</p>
 <p data-pid="iQTAhhBM"><b>Insist that</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hAF68v8I">Those who insist that only bleeding wounds should be count believe that …</p>
 <p data-pid="PM_l4SaU"><b>Claim that</b>声称</p>
 <p data-pid="HXQhyS_J">The veteran’s group claimed that doing so would “debase” the honor.</p>
 <p data-pid="TMlVpCSv"><b>Point out</b></p>
 <p data-pid="o0xuZSAe">As he points out, buyers under duress have no freedom.</p>
 <p data-pid="hP9OIGU-"><b>Consider…(to be).</b>认为。。。如何</p>
 <p data-pid="xvWuPD71">Some people favor abortion rights, and others consider abortion to be murder.</p>
 <p data-pid="AIHwggxz">Whereas some consider it an form of reverse discrimination against people who deserve admission on their merits.</p>
 <p data-pid="Wr6QMU0Y"><b>Be of the opinion that </b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZO_7fXET">I am of the opinion that car ownership should be discouraged.</p>
 <p data-pid="IUXo9sfO"><b>From a personal point of view,..</b></p>
 <p data-pid="cp8Unku6">From a personal point of view, it is essential to reduce non-essential travel.</p>
 <p data-pid="9OoNcFf-"><b>Sb’s point is that…</b>某人的意思是</p>
 <p data-pid="qz2Jjwol">Plato’s point is that to grasp the meaning of justice and the nature of the good life, we must rise above from the prejudices and routines of everyday life.</p>
 <p data-pid="o4pRb80a"><b><u>###支持：</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="TYHViUhr"><b>Support</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-Km8QbFY"><b>Favor sth/doing sth</b></p>
 <p data-pid="1gTnX_63">The captain favors including mental injuries.</p>
 <p data-pid="QPmMbCuS"><b>Defend sth</b>捍卫，支持</p>
 <p data-pid="es6rY1gd">Some defend affirmative actions in college admissions as a way of righting past wrongs.</p>
 <p data-pid="UHBr2RE3"><b>Advocates/defenders/proponents</b> 反对者、支持者</p>
 <p data-pid="61vK3yjJ"><b>Conviction，</b>坚信</p>
 <p data-pid="yfBBgT6v">My firm conviction is that punishment does not has much of a role to play in this.</p>
 <p data-pid="Ksn5IK8i"><b><u>###反对：</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="L-shE21_"><b>Argue against. </b>反对</p>
 <p data-pid="79pzUWM6">Jeff, a pro-market commentator writing the book Boston Globe, argued against this law.</p>
 <p data-pid="vU_9fhPf"><b>Reject<br></b>反对某种观点/行为</p>

 <p data-pid="r62oqFDE">A civilized country should reject the torture of terror suspects.</p>
 <p data-pid="UfZms7Ly"><b>The argument for or against sth. </b>对某话题的支持/反对观点</p>
 <p data-pid="D2df-n0y">We should also consider one further argument for this law.</p>
 <p data-pid="z_zHqYfI"><b>Oppose sth/sb/doing</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kpZeyZK2">A veteran’s group opposed awarding the medal for psychological injuries.</p>
 <p data-pid="IHEUausy"><b>Disagree</b></p>
 <p data-pid="7U7HYAmR">Citizens of pluralist societies disagree about such things?</p>
 <p data-pid="RbpWSC6x"><b>Disagree with</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0GxiEH39">Therefore I have to disagree almost entirely with the given statement.</p>
 <p data-pid="ke9C69Fj"><b>Opponents </b>反对者，是名词</p>
 <p data-pid="fSjDYFfJ">Not surprisingly, the opponents of this law invoke these two similar arguments for free markets.</p>
 <p data-pid="HBdj4gEU"><b>It is true that…but…</b>用于承认一方后，强调另一方</p>
 <p data-pid="e_HT-FMM"><b><u>###表示优点缺点</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="Lx0qokKO"><b>advantages and disadvantages</b></p>
 <p data-pid="2A38uJHB">Its advantages overweigh its disadvantages.</p>
 <p data-pid="e6SVnirw"><b>pros and cons </b></p>
 <p data-pid="gSUffbLr"><b>strengths and weaknesses</b></p>
 <p data-pid="sTZNlyCf"><b>merits and demerits</b></p>
 <p data-pid="IZdMZQDP"><b>benefits and problems</b></p>
 <p data-pid="G4_3hrS8">Even if high prices call forth a greater supply of goods, this benefit has to be weighed against the burden such prices impose on those least to afford them.</p>
 <p data-pid="dJOkrePJ"><b>On the plus/positive/negative side</b></p>
 <p data-pid="CcunBaL7"><b>On the downside</b></p>
 <p data-pid="9Ek1hdkR"><b>sth do more good than harm</b></p>
 <p data-pid="U8pH433Y"><b>A Outweigh B，</b>A超出B，更重要，用以连接上述的优缺点词汇</p>
 <p data-pid="d4qdocUj">The possible benefits outweigh the risks involved.</p>
 <p data-pid="3fM4VSDl"><b><u>###做结论</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="_oTcmgNx"><b>Conclude that.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="R-27msJP">So we can conclude that ….</p>
 <p data-pid="BHGjqf4L"><b>In brief/in summary/conclusion</b></p>

 <p data-pid="8YE_wKuC">According to the textbook context, this question divides ancient and modern political thought.</p>
 <p data-pid="WidMIX6w"><b><u>###引出话题</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="Cz5080N5"><b>It is said that…</b>据说</p>

 <p data-pid="rpRnjKjA"><b>The focus of … is on sth.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xLb1NY-6">The focus of this essay is on the causes of and solutions to this problem.</p>

 <p data-pid="PBz8WENc"><b>*******************************************PART 2 重要句式结构******************************************</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hVy7HFzx"><b>原则：主谓宾是骨头，逻辑连接词是骨头间的韧带，其他修饰词则是肌肉。</b></p>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="CJ4SrtOD"><b>名词从句=名词</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LwRF6DoN"><b>What </b>I am proficient in<b> is </b>ancient Chinese literature.</p>
 <p data-pid="6ksAnG1B">That<b>’s where</b> he met me.</p>
 <p data-pid="ak2iU_Iy"><b>Those </b></p>
 <p data-pid="wf4XbuGN"><b>Those+adj/ving/ved/to v/介词</b>，代表一类人或物</p>
 <p data-pid="UsBdXrio">All<b> those eligible</b> will receive a cash handout.</p>
 <p data-pid="ptldxzh6">The new law clearly benefits<b> those earning </b>the most money.</p>
 <p data-pid="ApCAs18M">Only 18 percent of<b> those surveyed </b>opposed the idea.</p>
 <p data-pid="_KorFNbd">I'm going to sort these old books into<b> those to be kept</b> and<b> those to be thrown</b> away.</p>
 <p data-pid="1LbDdQfR">Have some consideration for<b> those without a job.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="VelCNT-l">Those+从句who(人)/which（物）/whose（表归属）/in which（表位置），Those相当于一个名词</p>
 <p data-pid="oYE7zMMH">Only<b> those who</b> had booked in advance were allowed in.</p>
 <p data-pid="E8KcPQuf">We must try to find out <b>those which</b> may be attacked most easily.</p>
 <p data-pid="ShpvvkTI">This disease is more likely to strike <b>those whose</b> immune systems are already below par.</p>
 <p data-pid="SR1bbhJn">The poems which he wrote in the countryside are generally agreed to be <b>those in which</b> he found his true voice.</p>
 <p data-pid="Fjc2OsLd"><b>He who</b> can be a best servant can be a best leader.</p>
 <p data-pid="vIuVnHNM"><b>在句子中，任何名词随时都可以用 ved/ving/介词/形容词来修饰，可以使得单句也有复杂结构，却比定语从句简单</b></p>
 <p data-pid="KsgyJNJc"><b>疑问词+to+v</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HnF8auNF">I am considering<b> how to </b>manage this assignment But who is to judge what is virtue and what is vice?</p>
 <p data-pid="n97ZleWY"><b>动词分词做定语（ving, ved）,可以替代定语从句</b></p>
 <p data-pid="uirCa3sh">His work as a consultant <b>consisted in</b> advising foreign companies on the siting of new factories.</p>
 <p data-pid="W5N8N4jV"><b>句子，especially+名词或者从句. 这样可以对句子进行更加精确的描述。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="oxO4R6rn">Members of a competitive team can always keep them motivated,<b> especially when</b> confronted with tough situation and fierce competition.</p>
 <p data-pid="-PzC3fW8"><b>…, if any. (表示如果有…的话)</b></p>
 <p data-pid="lu7kF12N">State your hypothesis or theory, <b>if any.</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Cand_FOh">What,<b> if anything</b>, should the law do about it？</p>
 <p data-pid="10mqFi3-"><b>Rather, 单独出现表示“更准确的说”</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Yh-a5IoE"><b>Rather, </b>parents should employ a variety of methods to discipline their children.</p>
 <p data-pid="RJah9PN5"><b>用插入语. 补充信息，增强节奏感：两种形式：逗号或者横线隔开</b></p>
 <p data-pid="erSvB_CO">But in a market society<b>, the economists observed,</b> prices are set by demand and supply.</p>
 <p data-pid="8ryIT8Nk">They are no more “special” or “fair” than other prices that market conditions<b>—including<br>
   those promoted by a hurricane—</b>may bring about. 补充信息。</p>
 <p data-pid="KiUBfI0M">Higher prices have the advantages<b>, economists argued, </b>of limiting the use of such things by consumers. 转述说的话时，“某某说”可以做插入语。</p>
 <p data-pid="GR0tZ0JP">Jeff<b>, a pro-market commentator writing the book Boston Globe, </b>argued against this law on similar grounds. 对身份的补充。</p>
 <p data-pid="qwOqNPNK">Many people<b>, including those who support this law, </b>find the virtue argument discomfiting. 包含的对象的具体说明。</p>
 <p data-pid="1NzvD68-">All they ever see is the play of shadows on the wall,<b> a reflection of objects they can never apprehend. </b>同位语，用以解释或者补充前面的名词</p>
 <p data-pid="M30YA_c8"><b>To do sth 作主语</b></p>
 <p data-pid="47EiIuIj"><b>To punish </b>a very young child is both wrong and foolish.</p>
 <p data-pid="QuKvzQUP"><b>By doing sth, … 表示做事的方式途径</b></p>
 <p data-pid="B6Vl7w-E"><b>By providing </b>incentives for suppliers to produce more of the needed goods, it do more good than harm.</p>
 <p data-pid="x0XkHqWJ"><b>副词开头，句子。</b>节奏感强</p>
 <p data-pid="RQCnboSL"><b>Not surprisingly</b>, the opponents of this law invoke these two similar arguments for free markets.</p>
 <p data-pid="p5GEICTV"><b>Doing/To do A is not doing/to </b>do B. 做A并不是做B. 表示从反面对A的补充</p>
 <p data-pid="4GQISKC3"><b>To acknowledge </b>the moral forces of virtue argument is <b>not to insis</b>t it must always prevail over competing considerations.</p>
 <p data-pid="ajzi1KG1"><b>特殊的定语从句，介词+which定语从句与介词的妙用，这需要动词与介词本身就是固定搭配</b></p>
 <p data-pid="4kAjH2Lb">It is about cultivating the attitudes and the qualities of characters, <b>on which </b>a good society depends.</p>
 <p data-pid="HfZAgRei"><b>Cannot …without…没有。。。就不能。。。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ZKj8UtQM">Aristotle maintains that we cannot figure out what a just consideration is without first reflecting of the most desirable way of life.</p>
 <p data-pid="kZSh88Ab"><b>主语倒装句</b></p>
 <p data-pid="owZUUvKI">At the heart of the disagreement <b>are</b> rival conceptions of moral character.</p>
 <p data-pid="W3jrRkuP">Underlying the phenomenon <b>was </b>a profound reason.</p>
 <p data-pid="86f9go_t"><b>Sth is not (that)…but (that)…,<br>
   成分要一致，是。。。而不是。。。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xndbMVOV">The public’s objection to the bonuses is<b> not that</b> they reward greed<b> but that</b> they reward failure.</p>
 <p data-pid="-6kfz7Q1">The answer is that moral reflection is<b> not a </b>solitary pursuit <b>but a </b>public endeavor.</p>
 <p data-pid="npl-Lms0"><b>构词形式：A-被动： 被A。。。的</b></p>
 <p data-pid="sm8KkO6C">A <b>market-driven </b>society</p>
 <p data-pid="rR_TOIKu">A <b>knowledge-based</b> economy</p>

 <p data-pid="Vnu3da_y"><b>Financially better-off </b></p>
 <p data-pid="P3wNXaEh"><b>Do 用于动词前表示强调</b></p>
 <p data-pid="wfnZBeDT">He<b> did </b>want them to take responsibility for their failure.</p>
 <p data-pid="hvOYtE5h"><b>反问，表示强烈语气</b></p>
 <p data-pid="R80ZVrn6"><b>Couldn’t it be argued that难道不能认为。。。，</b></p>
 <p data-pid="SAK2Rhae"><b>How can it be。。 怎么可能。。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ovCigK_1"><b>What would sth be if…如果…sth 会怎样？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MDDlhduz"><b>With+ved/ving/to do/sth, </b></p>
 <p data-pid="lMG8XP1I"><b>动词分词前置或者后置：ved, 句子，ving…</b></p>
 <p data-pid="GVJ3aIjM"><b>Awarded </b>the Noble Prize, Yoyouyou become known by public, making her the most influential figure of this year.</p>
 <p data-pid="ccAPwl67"><b>Confronted with </b>this situation, we…..</p>
 <p data-pid="JND_6Wq3">Socrates suggests that, <b>having glimpsed </b>the sun, only the philosopher is fit to rule the cave dwellers, if he can somehow be coaxes into the darkness where they live.</p>
 <p data-pid="sGrXsiS6"><b>用虚拟语气（动词用过去式），表示未发生或者假想发生的事儿</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0fLMQpdi"><b>Let us not forget either that…</b>咱们也不要忘记。。。</p>
 <p data-pid="dk3_vraI"><b>Let us forget either that </b>traditional products, whether these be medicines or food, provide employment for local people.</p>

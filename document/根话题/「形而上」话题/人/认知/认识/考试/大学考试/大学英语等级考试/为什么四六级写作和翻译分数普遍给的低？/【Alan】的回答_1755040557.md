 <ul>
  <li data-pid="Y7y4d-B7"><b>翻译句型结构</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="DSZka3Sz">翻译句型</p>
 <p data-pid="dKRS6hKL">1)It is+形容词+that</p>
 <p data-pid="hYe1XXoJ">It is conceivable that knowledge plays an important role in our life.</p>
 <p data-pid="q2Vigf3-">可想而知，知识在我们的一生中扮演着一-个重要的角色。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-NsYb2k5">2)It is+形容词+to do/ doing</p>
 <p data-pid="lmdrfhis">She had said what it was necessary to say. 她已经说了- -切有必要说的话。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MqgqMO2A">3)祈使句/名词+and/ or</p>
 <p data-pid="4hDIkTCK">Work hard, and you will finally be able to reach your destination.</p>
 <p data-pid="vJcZiCTM">努力工作，你就能实现自己的目标。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IPbE3Awf">4)as+many/ much+名词+as</p>
 <p data-pid="gOFv2OP7">It is said that visitors spend only half as much money in a day in Leeds as in I on-don.</p>
 <p data-pid="hUtGY-O1">据说，游客每天在利兹的花销仅为在伦敦的-半。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="QfruhiX2">5)倍数词+as+形容词+as</p>
 <p data-pid="ASBo7_da">The reservoir is three times as big as it was ten years ago.</p>
 <p data-pid="A6IK7DfP">这个水库的面积是十年前三倍。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XuPbZYK2">6)倍数词+ more +名词/形容词十than</p>
 <p data-pid="6ZD2cjWJ">Smoking is so harmful to personal health that it kills seven times more people each year than automobile accidents.</p>
 <p data-pid="w_Y3T8CS">吸烟对人体健康的危害极大，每年死于吸烟的人比死于车祸的人多七倍。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Bvvz_XF6">7)(not)as/ ......不-样)</p>
 <p data-pid="mTO9ArI6">The environmental problems are not as serious as they suggested in their report.环境问题没有他们在报告中说得那么严重。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h2>二、作文句型句式类：</h2>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="x0rBBBPK"><b>句型结构</b></li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="FL8u9AfL"><b>一、文章开头：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LILZTfBr"><b>注意一般在文章开头处，最好用引言来引出主题，便于老师一眼了解，同时契合题目。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="uMturgk0"><b>以【如今....】引开头：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vF_ta8VV">1. In recent years.. is gaining growing popularity with.</p>
 <p data-pid="TDtLov6w">近年来，.....收也到越来越多....的欢迎。</p>
 <p data-pid="rnrExZ5N">2.Nowadays,... has become a very common matter in....</p>
 <p data-pid="zdv0aZov">如今，.... 已经成为.....的常见现象。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JWvP-mNB"><b>引出不同观点:</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zoOpSYy1">1、People' S views on..vary from person to person.</p>
 <p data-pid="_Bdus_Hc">Some hold tha...However ,others believe that...</p>
 <p data-pid="2pOOIlQh">人们对.的观点因人而异。有些人认为..然而其他人却认为....</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8pboLQmX"><b>二、文章中间段：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="DNRMaFzC"><b>中间就是需要套句型，一般而言中间段落的结构更应该清晰一些，所以建议大家多记忆句型，便于快速写出层次感。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="jOwJH3gu">1.The primary /leading reason /cause is that......can be another reason. A further reason is that....</p>
 <p data-pid="of1PVhY3">主要原因...。.....他可能是另一个原因。,第三个原因......</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ihLD7mTO">2. AS for /Among the factors for.... ... counts for the half, the rest depends on...</p>
 <p data-pid="ZVUPIFuV">就导致....的因素而言，....是一部分原因，另部分原因......</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="be7jPS7_"><b>三、文章结尾：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kuD7ETuX"><b>文章结尾的一种方式是直接根据我而引出结尾，当然也有其他句型和方法，我在后面的文章会逐渐列出，需要可以关注我哦～</b></p>
 <p data-pid="gjfy05jN">1.For my part, I am on the side of...对我来说，我站在.....那边。</p>
 <p data-pid="Mz4ZKKPp">2.As to me, the former /latter opinion is moreacceptable.</p>
 <p data-pid="Z-WosHWa">对我来说，前/后一种观点更可以接受。</p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="TeuEkPWR">其实，大多数考生四六级写作能力并不是很高，常常“<b>练的少，听的多</b>”。导致很多人仍停留在初等阶段。<b>但大学英语要求相对于高中要求更高！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="F9YhKc4l">而翻译则缺乏特定单词积累量，比如说：<b>一带一路（the Belt and Road Initiative），木偶戏（puppet show），中国古代四大发明（the four great inventions of ancient China）......</b></p>
 <p data-pid="jfPPl0fn"><b>这些固定的单词，不记忆是不可能写出来的（文后有大量相应的翻译常考词）</b>况且有时翻译题通篇i某个词，有的同学不会就可能全篇空格。<b>英语语法掌握不地道，英语单词不背不记忆！</b></p>

 <h3>1.四六级满分作文结构</h3>
 <p data-pid="qBquixSx"><b>四六级作文的满分结构就是四段式</b>：</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="bSIaPaDI">
  第一段：描写现状
  <br>
  第二段：提出正向论点+论据
  <br>
  第三段：抛出反向观点+论据
  <br>
  第四段:总结结束陈词：总结正反观点，提出建议
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="LKZ_Ix8z">比如让你聊聊对网购的看法，</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="q6YSJ9bE">
  <b>第一段描述一下网购的现状，现在特别的火，</b>
  <br><b>第二段抛出一个正向的观点，网络给消费者带来了很多的便利，给商家带来了很多的利润</b>
  <br><b>第三段抛出一个反向的观点，说网购伤害了实体的经济</b>
  <br><b>最后一段总结陈词说网购有利有弊，我们一定要好好利用它！</b>
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="zSUnVt-b">注意作文一般<b>字数越多分数越高</b>，<b>中间再插一些倒装句和从句</b>从而显得更加高级，就可得高分!</p>
 <h3>2.四六级作文模板</h3>
 <p data-pid="HnWFsM6s">以下是我当年备考六级总结的英文模板，绝对不是那种烂大街的模板哦，放心拿去用吧。</p>
 <h2>1）. 首段</h2>
 <p data-pid="dtFur62d">Along with the advance of the society, there has been growing viewpoint that +题目中的观点+plays a significant role in our daily life.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="yCN0SWS-">
  比如：关于低碳生活的看法
  <br>
  <br>
  Along with the advance of the society, there has been growing viewpoint that low-carbon life plays a significant role in our daily life.
  <br>
  <br>
  比如：关于校园暴力的看法
  <br>
  <br>
  Along with the advance of the society, there has been growing viewpoint that campus violence plays a significant role in our daily life.
 </blockquote>
 <h2>2）.正文</h2>
 <p data-pid="j1gT6ddr">接下来用两段式揭示正反两面观点：</p>
 <p data-pid="klVOBRaV">First and foremost+第一种观点。</p>
 <p data-pid="0g4j3rLn">Subsequently+第二种观点</p>
 <p data-pid="1EUUZx56">last but not least+第三种观点</p>
 <p data-pid="3NYzu43R">中间用一两个倒装句：</p>
 <p data-pid="1lBXPNVM">Not only can protect the environment to a great extent, but it can save the limited natural resources and energy. 低碳生活不仅在很大程度上能保护环境，也有助于节约有限的自然资源和能源。</p>
 <h2>3）. 结尾段：</h2>
 <p data-pid="254-CL7X">不要再用in conclusion， all in all啦</p>
 <p data-pid="yAxgvoXp">使用 <b>To put it crudely</b>来总结 瞬间高大上了，有木有？</p>
 <p data-pid="bZ6aK4oc">对于结尾，我有几个模板可起到点睛作用</p>
 <p data-pid="MHJd48PC">1.I hope what has been mentioned above will helpful and wish you have a good journey.（我希望上面讲的对你有所帮助，并希望你前行愉快）</p>
 <p data-pid="d_7eH8RP">2.We need to bear in mind that .....（我们必须牢记....）</p>
 <p data-pid="D8b7JMc-">3.Nothing more important than the fact is that....(更重要的是....）</p>
 <p data-pid="Sm3zMxCO">4.There is no better way to do than to...（最好的办法是做....）</p>
 <h2>4). 完整模板举例</h2>
 <p data-pid="xCBjeVwT">上面是我给大家分享的模板，很短很简单。当然纸上谈兵怎么行？光说不练假把式！</p>
 <p data-pid="NsavuN6d">接下来我以近两年的六级真题为例，来说一下这个模板怎么用。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="RRGw2-y9">
  例子：2020年6月六级写作真题：
  <br>
  <br>
  For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write a short essay 
  <br>
  <br><b>on living in the virtual world. Try to imagine what will happen when people spend more and more time in the virtual world instead of interacting in the real world. </b>
  <br>
  <br>
  You are required to write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="nXUV1kCU"><b>以下是我利用上述模板写的作文（纯原创，非机构，禁止转载！！！）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="l2POJll_">Along with the advance of the society, there has been growing viewpoint that internet plays a significant role in our daily life. Admittedly, one of the greatest invention in 21th century, as a major lifestyle of entertainment that relieves one's pressure, boredom, burdon, furnishes some favor of fun and high-tech into our seemingly tedious, routine and monotonous life, which is thought-provoking and deserves the in-depth ananlysis.</p>
 <p data-pid="z7HvPm-9"><b>(开头描述社会现象，注意句子结构多样化，词汇多样化，因为是“现象”类作文，用“分析”引出下面思考）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="b-rQuGyN">First and foremost, there is no exaggeration in saying that the virtual world has inflitrated every aspects in our daily life. Not only can people even use their phone to look through the website and chat online when they are commuting or studying, but it controls our thoughts and behaviors like an invisible hand. <b>（引出第一个论点，阐述支持该论点的论据，这里使用了there is no+名词”句型和"not only...but“结构的倒装）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="4yvQ9LM_">Last but not least,the virtual world is in the period of accelerating change, it is no coincidence that the Internet companies design the exquiste and attractive components to cater the public's curiosity, which, directly or indirectly, exerts a sublte and negative effect on people's lifestyle.<b>（引出另外一个论点，阐述支持该论点的论据，这里用“it is +名词+that”从句结构）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="nbKn2q9B">From what has been discussed above, nothing more important than the fact is that there is still a big gap between the virtual world and the real world , and the virtual world is imperceptibly undermining the normal lifestyle. To put it crudely, before the phenomenon gets severe, it is high time that immediate steps should be taken. On the one hand , the department concerned should settle down the legislations and regulations to constrain the illegal behavior of Internet companies. On the other hand, it is advisable that we should call on the public to embrace the real world. With these measures taken into account, it is reasonable for us to witness that the phenomenon will be improved in the near future!（<b>最后结尾高度概括，用了on the one hand, on the other hand,提出建议，这里使用了"nothing imore important than the fact is that "句型和“it is +名词+that”从句结构</b>）</p>

 <h2>4.四六级高逼格特殊句式</h2>
 <p data-pid="KEUtXtsC">1.Nothing is more important than to ...</p>
 <p data-pid="2R8Kj5X_">没有比...更重要的事</p>
 <p data-pid="cuag_eNr">eg:Nothing is more important than to protect environment.</p>
 <p data-pid="8OQKqqh7">没有什么比保护环境更重要的事了。</p>
 <p data-pid="BXC1HAtD">2.On no account can we...</p>
 <p data-pid="AXYOZwpa">我们绝对不能</p>
 <p data-pid="_yYRFMQ4">eg:On no account can we ignore the value of knowledge.</p>
 <p data-pid="GroBpDOZ">我们绝对不能忽视知识的价值。</p>
 <p data-pid="Vo3RE5wh">3.There is no denying that</p>
 <p data-pid="k3hPEX6z">不可否认的是....</p>
 <p data-pid="nruaeTg_">eg.:There is no denying that the development speed of the technology is becoming increasingly faster.</p>
 <p data-pid="Tnwk3hYz">4.cannot emphasize the importance of ...too much</p>
 <p data-pid="I6HKX1Oc">再怎么强调....的重要性也不为过</p>
 <p data-pid="cDAeRvcj">eg.:We cannot emphasize the importance of protecting environment.</p>
 <p data-pid="SyhmaXNs">我们再怎么强调保护环境的重要性也不未过。</p>
 <p data-pid="k2jLI2oF">5.There is no doubt that...</p>
 <p data-pid="d7yWxOhT">毫无疑问的是</p>
 <p data-pid="vLSyr6BI">eg.:There is no doubt that the traffic jam is more serious than ever.</p>
 <p data-pid="bqoayR9d">毫无疑问的是交通堵塞比以前更加严重。</p>
 <p data-pid="uRfEgaKW">8.There is no one but...</p>
 <p data-pid="rg9jiFK6">没有人不</p>
 <p data-pid="kkn2m0BI">eg.:There is no one but longs to earn more money.</p>
 <p data-pid="FbeBS8gG">没有人不渴望赚更多钱。</p>
 <p data-pid="hGUR-hYu">9.The reason why...is that...</p>
 <p data-pid="mmjp_cd2">...的原因是</p>
 <p data-pid="SY7cBS9O">eg.:The reason why we have to receive eduction is that knowledge can broaden our vision and enhance our skill.</p>
 <p data-pid="OBlMnUj0">必须接受教育的原因是教育能拓宽眼界，还能增强技能。</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="OmsBHZpk">It pays to ...</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="J_VNPDIb">...是值得的。</p>
 <p data-pid="5ygWjP-Z">eg.:It pays to help others.</p>
 <p data-pid="nIZ5AjoV">帮助别人是值得的。</p>
 <p data-pid="k0MhftZ_">11.It is conceivable that...</p>
 <p data-pid="m7aaINPT">可想而知的</p>
 <p data-pid="agU6jGAq">It is obvious that...</p>
 <p data-pid="8curBB2w">明显的</p>
 <p data-pid="6p_88gTM">It is apparent that....</p>
 <p data-pid="OYw-1k0l">显然的</p>
 <p data-pid="0O2S_d-D">eg: It is conceivable that knowledge plays an important role in our life.</p>
 <p data-pid="x0t1o_Gr">可想而知，知识在我们的一生中扮演很重要的角色。</p>
 <h2>4.四六级考试作文必背句型</h2>
 <h2>1.开头</h2>
 <p data-pid="-HN3-_J1">Recently, the problem of ....has aroused people's concern.</p>
 <p data-pid="9aDlyQrc">最近，...问题已引起人民的关注。</p>
 <p data-pid="T8QgOIUq">Internet has been playing an increasingly important role in our day-to-day life.</p>
 <p data-pid="K2cYJTEB">It has brought a lot of benefits but has created some serious problems as well.</p>
 <p data-pid="gLQbo0oj">互联网已经在我们的生活中扮演着越来越重要的角色。它给我们带来了许多好处，但也产生了一些严重的问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="hcJdI68I">Nowadays, (overpopulation) has become a problem we have to face.</p>
 <p data-pid="F1fdn7Ky">如今，（人口过剩）已经成为我们不得不面对的问题。</p>
 <p data-pid="LIHx2i97">It is commonly believed that ..../It is a common belief that</p>
 <p data-pid="Ql4Z2SEC">人们一般认为</p>
 <p data-pid="ziuZaxG4">Many people insist that</p>
 <p data-pid="8mJBETGh">很多人坚持认为</p>
 <p data-pid="7kCQX-fY">with the development of science and technology, more and more people believe that...</p>
 <p data-pid="Z_mjZnrm">随着科技的发展，越来越多的人认为...</p>
 <p data-pid="DYe83-iz">A lot of people seem to think that....</p>
 <p data-pid="xGUS9ndD">很多人似乎认为....</p>
 <h2>2.引出不同的观点：</h2>
 <p data-pid="MLI6pv1W">people's view on ....vary from person to person. Some hold that.... However, others believe that....人们对....的观点因人而异。有些人认为....然而其他门却认为</p>
 <p data-pid="vyQJjhnw">people may have different opinions on</p>
 <p data-pid="oc9yYC7X">人民对...可能有不同的见解。</p>
 <p data-pid="1Z63kIL9">Attitudes towards (drugs) vary from person to person.</p>
 <p data-pid="nic2vCzn">人们对待吸毒的态度因人而异。</p>
 <p data-pid="0hQ2Ph15">There are different opinions among people as to...</p>
 <p data-pid="QoBgDPfc">关于.... 人们的观点大不相同。</p>
 <p data-pid="-ezLHi_7">Different people hold different attitudes toward (failure).</p>
 <p data-pid="Cibk_oW8">对（失败）人们的态度各不相同。</p>
 <h2>3.结尾</h2>
 <p data-pid="p50dBnas">Taking all these factors into consideration, we naturally come to the conclusion that....</p>
 <p data-pid="KpSlS6v-">把所有这些因素加以考虑，我们自然会得出结论....</p>
 <p data-pid="LGTRx37h">Taking into account all these factors, we may reasonably come to the conclusion that...</p>
 <p data-pid="HkdUdY_N">考虑所有这些因素，我们可能会得出合理的结论....</p>
 <p data-pid="DxtnpB84">Hence/Therefore, we'd better come to the conclusion that...</p>
 <p data-pid="Zx7S5xRm">因此，我们最好得出这样的结论....</p>
 <p data-pid="zEVaIgBt">All in all, we cannot live without... but at the same time we must try to find out new ways to cope with the problems that would arise.</p>
 <p data-pid="dYW2nYTP">总之，我们没有...是无法生活的。但同时，我们必须寻求新的解决办法来对付可能出现的问题。</p>
 <h2>4.提出建议</h2>
 <p data-pid="TwOIna-8">It is high time that we put an end to the (trend).该是我们停止这一趋势的时候了。</p>
 <p data-pid="cerapSLs">It is time to take the advice of ... and to put special emphasis on the improvement of ....</p>
 <p data-pid="1nnm-kLs">该是采纳...的建议，并对...的进展给予特殊重视的时候了。</p>
 <p data-pid="HET2--VV">There is no doubt that enough concern must be paid to the problem of ....</p>
 <p data-pid="xpVVcp9v">毫无疑问，对...问题应予以足够的重视。</p>
 <p data-pid="83ikJLY3">Obviously, ....If we want to do something...., is it essential that....</p>
 <p data-pid="4Zq9P31o">显然，如果我们想做某事，很重要的是...</p>
 <p data-pid="Ztdoi9FN">Only in this way, can we...</p>
 <p data-pid="IxDtddso">只有这样，我们才能....</p>
 <p data-pid="5eZFQkap">It must be realized that...</p>
 <p data-pid="qd3vJDAX">我们必须意识到....</p>
 <h2>5.预示后果</h2>
 <p data-pid="J8NJM6o2">Obviously, if we donot control the problem, the chances are that....will lead us in danger.</p>
 <p data-pid="c3pMWBnT">很明显，如果我们不能控制这一问题，很有可能我们会陷入危险。</p>
 <p data-pid="c-dYII_9">No doubt, unless we take effective measures, it is very likely that...</p>
 <p data-pid="YussVlA4">毫无疑问，除非我们采取有效措施，很可能会....</p>
 <p data-pid="7k8lKC6e">It is urgent that immediate measures should be taken to stop the situation.</p>
 <p data-pid="hZv-LXsv">很紧迫的是，应立即采取措施阻止这一事态的发展。</p>
 <h2>6.论证</h2>
 <p data-pid="K8wNoV3F">From my point of view, it is more reasonable to support the first opinion rather than the second.</p>
 <p data-pid="cJraEE4G">在我看来，支持第一种观点比支持第二种观点更有道理。</p>
 <p data-pid="6ZBCVYql">I cannot entirely agree with the idea that...</p>
 <p data-pid="dusvGBSp">我无法完全同意这一观点....</p>

 <p data-pid="ONlT_Uft">2.如果对现有的答案不满意想重新写，<b>记住要先写正确答案，再划去错误答案！</b>很多同学都是先把原来的答案涂抹了，一旦写新的答案时间不够，本来可能得的分树就被自己涂抹掉了。</p>
 <p data-pid="fJi-4sOa">3.<b>新的答案写出来后，不要忘记把原来的答案划掉</b>。如果出现同时出现一个对的和一个错的答案，按规则是不能给分的。</p>

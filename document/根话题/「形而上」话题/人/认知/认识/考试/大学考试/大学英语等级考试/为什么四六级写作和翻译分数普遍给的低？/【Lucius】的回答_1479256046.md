 <p data-pid="nZFTyOCD">考了三次六级，写作分数分别是 188、170、178，个人感觉只要满足下面几个条件，写作成绩就不会太差：</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="ZQ81xzS5">字数写够</li>
  <li data-pid="iCENGWoJ">尽量避免语法语病错误</li>
  <li data-pid="cKN3kpDx">多用衔接词（使文章看起来句与句、段与段之间有逻辑）</li>
  <li data-pid="irxlHL_W">尽量多使用词组代替单个单词</li>
  <li data-pid="06WTuQFv">尽量使用多个形容词和动词进行并列描述</li>
  <li data-pid="-f6VNjp1">多使用长句型，并且句型不要重复</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="UWC_Hn3C">给大家推荐一些不同主题下的句子，大家可以感受一下里面的遣词造句（结合上面的条件一起看哦，注意句与句之间并不关联）：</p>
 <p data-pid="G19x7Sxd">网络的应用提高效率</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="wdDvyTL4">For individuals, the pervasive use of the Internet helps people <b>save time</b> and <b>enhance the efficiency.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="euQovitl">So <b>convenient</b> is the application of telecommunication and online-shopping that <b>[例证原因]</b> people are <b>extricating themselves from</b> such undesirable factors as traffic congestion, bad weather and tight schedule and so forth.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="t3HOUtku">经济发展、市场繁荣</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="MT2N6AIA"><b>In terms of</b> international trade, exporting countries <b>may become major beneficiaries of</b> this situation, because it helps <b>[扩大市场]</b> <b>expand</b> the market of farm produce, <b>[加速销售]</b> <b>accelerate</b> the sales of agricultural output and <b>[改善福祉]</b> <b>improve</b> the well-being of domestic farmers.</li>
  <li data-pid="jamp7gnw"><b>[工业受益]</b> For society, industries such as electronic commerce and online-business have <b>become major beneficiaries</b> of this tendency.</li>
  <li data-pid="6JQhhPY1"><b>[原因句]</b> <b>This is simply because</b> the interaction and communication are <b>[地理不受限]</b> <b>no longer confined to the geographical impediment.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="4_GgkV6Y"><b>[前因后果句]</b> With the increasing popularity of <b>[线上支付兴起]</b> <b>online shopping and banking</b>, <b>[投资消费增长]</b> <b>consumption and investment</b> undoubtedly flourish, which can serve as a driving factor to <b>[加速经济建设]</b> <b>facilitate</b> the sustainable development of the social economy.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="NFHVA8HA">经济倒退、破产失业</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="mMPk0MRW">However, many small and medium-sized enterprises may <b>[中小企业成为受害者]</b> <b>fall victim to</b> the wave of electronic business <b>[原因后置]</b> <b>on account of</b> the <b>[资源不足以开展线上业务]</b> <b>insufficiency</b> of money and technology to develop an online transaction system.</li>
  <li data-pid="iCbhH6Sr">Also, <b>[失业率激增]</b> the <b>unemployment rate</b> may be driven up <b>[原因后置]</b> because many job positions <b>[工作被替代]</b> <b>have and will continue to be replaced</b> by online automatic transaction systems.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="bsLf8T42">教育类 - 学校职能</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="q6jLwFXC"><b>[学校职能]</b> To begin with, <b>schools</b> are set up to fulfill the educational function of imparting a great deal amount of knowledge, such as liberal arts and science, to students; <b>while students</b> go to school mainly for the purpose of fostering academic ability.</li>
  <li data-pid="xheCHsjj">Accordingly, it is understandable that some <b>take it for granted</b> that academic results can serve as an effective benchmark for assessing students’ capability.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="E2evK4xW">教育类 - 学校培养多样化能力</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="gLGcMcU2"><b>[正反对比]</b> First and foremost, <b>school</b> <b>is not a place</b> <b>[成为书呆子]</b> where students are turned into pedants. <b>Instead</b>, it is considered as an <b>educational platform</b> for students to <b>[培养多样化才能]</b> <b>develop</b> their versatile skills and <b>[满足社会需求]</b> <b>grow into</b> a person who gets accustomed to the needs of the society.</li>
  <li data-pid="fqW1QaZC"><b>Only</b> focusing on academic skills <b>inevitably leads</b> many who are short of academic study but good at other subjects to <b>lose opportunities</b> to promote their talents.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="NYHycc1T">教育类 - 用成绩选拔学生公平</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="fIUCqK82">Besides this, it is widely accepted that it <b>[客观公正的评估系统]</b> is a relatively objective and fair <b>assessment system</b> for all students and <b>[减少不公正现象]</b> better <b>reduces the dispute</b> of unfair treatments.</li>
  <li data-pid="F-zpREnJ"><b>[例证]</b> <b>One example will suffice to illustrate this point.</b> <b>Other than</b> the academic mark, educators find it difficult to compare the ability of a student who is proficient at painting and who is skillful at sports. <b>[例证结论]</b> Whoever is chosen by schools could not ensure the absolute fairness and objectivity of the selection.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="svFtn64Y">教育类 - 不同能力学生互补</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="PsAyTsxF">In addition, <b>if</b> students with different abilities can study together, <b>it is out of the question that</b> <b>[学生受益]</b> all the students will <b>benefit from such a combination.</b></li>
  <li data-pid="3j8PXIbX"><b>[原因句]</b> <b>This is simply because</b> it helps students <b>[互补]</b> <b>complement</b> each other and give them motivation as well as chances to <b>[互相学习]</b> <b>learn more</b> from each other.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="7CTZ5-eT">网络类 - 沉迷网络</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="aj0YkcDn">However, <b>over-indulgence</b> on the Internet potentially <b>[沦为奴隶]</b> <b>renders people as a slave</b> of the Internet and computer.</li>
  <li data-pid="RSlg_Si4"><b>[前因后果句]</b> Due to the <b>[缺乏面对面交流]</b> <b>absence</b> of face-to-face interview and communication, <b>[地理与心灵疏离]</b> people are going to be, geographically as well as emotionally, <b>isolated from each other</b>, which further makes people <b>[失去认同感和社会归属感]</b> <b>lose a sense of identity and social belonging.</b></li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="qYJcU94M">健康类 - 丰富膳食、增加营养</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="gVfytJ0F">For importing countries, those agricultural products <b>substantially</b> <b>enrich the diet</b> of local citizens and <b>improve the nutrition structure</b>.</li>
 </ol>
 <p data-pid="9M5N8Zg8">环境类 - 加速化石燃料损耗</p>

  <li data-pid="NsS5GIvw"><b>[解释上一句]</b> So far there has not been a complete substitution for oil.</li>
  <li data-pid="ZRO89ejC"><b>[建议性结局]</b> We should not pursue short-term profit <b>at the expense of</b> sustainable development.</li>

 <p data-pid="SORdF2pk">环境类 - 有毒气体排放</p>
 <ol>
  <li data-pid="K_pWvNSf"><b>[有毒气体]</b> Also, the toxic fumes - such as sulfur dioxide, released from the aircraft <b>are the chief culprit of</b> atmospheric contamination.</li>
  <li data-pid="KIqazBRk"><b>[结果句]</b> Consequently, people <b>are unavoidably afflicted with</b> respiratory diseases.</li>
  <li data-pid="xgGsAo5F"><b>[温室气体]</b> <b>Other than</b> toxic gases, greenhouse gases (i.e. carbon dioxide) <b>worsen </b>global warming and then potentially <b>induce</b> the change of global climate.</li>

 <p data-pid="jAq9LXH2">大家可以感受一下上面的这些句子，应该不难发现基本上每一个句子开头都有一个衔接词，并且每个句子都习惯使用多个名词、动词或形容词并列来进行句子描述。以及这些句子都充斥着大量的词组与各类句型，大家可以记忆一下里面的句型，会在考试中发挥作用的～</p>

 <p data-pid="6KCg6JO6">对了，提醒一下大家，四六级翻译部分的中文内容是没有衔接词的，但是大家翻译成英文的时候一定要记得加上！</p>
 <h2>正面句</h2>
 <p data-pid="RPY6xcuH"><b>1. Play a significant role in（在...方面起重大作用）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="qBPkywFG">Vitamins and minerals <i><b>play a significant role in</b></i> <i><b>keeping</b></i> our body healthy; therefore, children and adults alike should eat more vegetables that are rich in various vitamins and minerals.</p>
 <p data-pid="3SzWAl-K"><b>2. A has a strong influence on B（A 对 B 有重大影响）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0biD8WWQ">Visiting museums, historical sites and ruins could <b><i>have a strong influence on deepening</i></b> visitors' understanding in terms of local customs and practices, <b><i>enriching</i></b> their experiences concerning local traditions, art and heritages, and <b><i>promoting</i></b> the dialogue over natural and cultural resources between different countries.</p>
 <p data-pid="HLcwbwjR"><b>3. A could be highly beneficial to B（B 从 A 获益）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="JBJ0gkf2">The large amount of investment on education <b><i>could be highly beneficial to</i></b> the development of economy and society.</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="MN2T8YB6">
  上述三句常用于连接名词和动词，可与后面的句子搭配使用。
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Uhh6lvCw"><b>4. Promote the development / growth of B（促进 B 发展）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="E9OyWI0y">Even though the city invested millions of pounds in museums and art galleries, it had done little <b><i>to promote the development of</i></b> education and medical care.</p>
 <p data-pid="hzSe2Z3T"><b>5. There is a close relationship between A and B（A 和 B 有密切的关系）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kDy8Fyq0"><b><i>There is a close relationship between</i></b> the rising rate of obesity and the increasing consumption of fast food.</p>
 <p data-pid="HcfPQhSo"><b>6. Relax their bodies and ease their minds（放松身心）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="7hIvSr9R">Children not only need opportunities to acquire knowledge, they need time to <i><b>relax their bodies and ease their minds</b></i> well.</p>
 <p data-pid="3yPx8rG8"><b>7. Enrich one's social / professional / spiritual experience（丰富...经历）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="XFUvfuFw">Working for a period before going to university can greatly <i><b>enrich student's social experience</b></i> and make them more capable in university.</p>
 <p data-pid="GUNnt5TT"><b>8. Improve living standard of citizens（提高市民生活水平）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="IuHzMq66">The development of technology and economy play a significant role in <b><i>improving living standard of citizens.</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="1fXeX3Mo"><b>9. Enhance efficiency / productivity（提高效率 / 生产率）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="-JV7RLKc">The main question about high-tech education is whether it actually <b><i>enhances</i></b> the efficiency and the effectiveness of learning.</p>
 <p data-pid="qSOlGuYG"><b>10. Relieve one's academic / financial / psychological burden（减轻某人的学业 / 经济 / 心理负担）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HTyPJ-ZN">Part-time job help students to <b><i>relieve</i></b> <b><i>their parents' burdens</i></b>, nevertheless, working on a part-time basis may create psychological burdens for the students themselves.</p>
 <h2>负面句</h2>
 <p data-pid="29Qcf-RC"><b>1. Lead to = Result in = bring about = Give rise to（导致）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="K27lgJIS">The trend that population has been booming has <b><i>resulted in</i></b> the deterioration of natural environment.</p>
 <p data-pid="OgtHY5A0"><b>2. Have a negative / serious influence on（对...有负面影响）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hvV6MV8B">Apparently, long-distance flights <i><b>have serious influence upon</b></i> the environment, even though they are by far the most efficient way of traveling.</p>
 <p data-pid="GWI9eTRW"><b>3. The competition is increasingly stiff（竞争日益激烈）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="WqlxYvo5">The economic <b><i>competition</i></b> between countries <b><i>is increasingly stiff</i></b>, which has made the governments more protective of their domestic market.</p>
 <p data-pid="uGIY2M3-"><b>4. Live in poverty / misery / frustration / anxiety（穷 / 苦 / 沮丧 / 焦虑）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="PcAdVA8T"><b><i>Living in misery</i></b>, the citizens in these impoverished countries claim that industrial nations have the obligation to supply financial aid, which can lift them out of poverty.</p>
 <p data-pid="N0554Q9G"><b>5. Stem from ...（某种坏现象起源于...）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0qCTQsDA">Generally speaking, children's bad behavior <b><i>stems from</i></b> imitating their parents.</p>
 <p data-pid="YM_7234L"><b>6. Pose threat to personal health / security / environment（对...产生危害）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="LURmoESj">The weapons that a country possesses do not just <b><i>pose a threat to</i></b> other countries. They pose a very real threat to their owner as well.</p>
 <p data-pid="lGhmktSc"><b>7. Be overly dependent on（对...过度依赖）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zTSg5scR">Technology has made individual <i><b>overly dependent on it</b></i>, which has led to more sedentary lifestyle and less face-to-face communication.</p>
 <p data-pid="LECCsIBN"><b>8. Stifle creativity / imagination（扼杀创造力 / 想象力）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Y78IL7vN">The traditional approach to education in some Asian countries seriously <b><i>stifles students' creativity and imagination.</i></b></p>
 <p data-pid="bF85huBr"><b>9.Has long been criticized as（臭名昭著）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="B5HBrbuF">It is widely accepted that international fast foods <b><i>have long been criticized as</i></b> junk food because they contain a considerable number of calories with high level of heat and fat.</p>
 <p data-pid="Lap9t9vY"><b>10. Face the problem of（面对...问题）</b></p>
 <p data-pid="km5aKs7w">So a new challenge was raised: how to <b><i>face the problem of</i></b> excessive information in theso-called era ofinformation exploding.</p>

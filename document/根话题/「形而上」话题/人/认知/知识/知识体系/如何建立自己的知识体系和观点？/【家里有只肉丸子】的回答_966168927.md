 <p>所以<span><span>，</span></span><strong>构建认知体系<span><span>，</span></span>就是构建<span><span>「</span></span>知道<span><span>」</span></span>和<span><span>「</span></span>认知<span><span>」</span></span>两个部分<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>

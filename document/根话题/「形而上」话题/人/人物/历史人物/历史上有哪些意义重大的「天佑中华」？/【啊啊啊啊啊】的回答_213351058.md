<body>
 <p data-pid="haD0CDX5">其实那里来的天佑中华，不过是祖先的自强不息。</p>
 <p data-pid="QEh02lIL">中国看似地大物博，其实由于人居住多了，时间长。</p>
 <p data-pid="_-aSVD86">基本上灾害年年都有，翻开一本24史，除开是帝王将相史，记载最多的是哪里水灾，旱灾，地震，瘟疫等。</p>
 <p data-pid="Hmvfmqaz">每年总有那么几次，所以基本上一个朝代小规模造反许多。</p>
 <p data-pid="LISR-L9D">自从中华民族有了中国这片土地，历来历代就靠着这边看似广阔，其实有些贫瘠的土地生产。</p>
 <p data-pid="F1wlTo-W">如看似中国耕地颇多，其实</p>
 <p data-pid="pCXDCSds">2014年全国<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E8%2580%2595%25E5%259C%25B0%25E9%259D%25A2%25E7%25A7%25AF%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YkPHmdnhmdnHFbnH6sPhR40ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWcsnWRLrjDdnjnLP1T4nWcvr0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">耕地面积</a>统计显示有20.3亿亩<br>
  我国人均<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E5%259C%259F%25E5%259C%25B0%25E9%259D%25A2%25E7%25A7%25AF%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YkPHmdnhmdnHFbnH6sPhR40ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWcsnWRLrjDdnjnLP1T4nWcvr0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">土地面积</a>在世界上190多个国家中排110位以后，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E8%2580%2595%25E5%259C%25B0%25E9%259D%25A2%25E7%25A7%25AF%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YkPHmdnhmdnHFbnH6sPhR40ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWcsnWRLrjDdnjnLP1T4nWcvr0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">耕地面积</a>排在126位以后，草地面积排在76位以后，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E6%25A3%25AE%25E6%259E%2597%25E9%259D%25A2%25E7%25A7%25AF%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YkPHmdnhmdnHFbnH6sPhR40ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWcsnWRLrjDdnjnLP1T4nWcvr0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">森林面积</a>排在107位以后。</p>
 <p data-pid="XiZrF6wV">其中全国有564.9万公顷耕地位于东北、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E8%25A5%25BF%25E5%258C%2597%25E5%259C%25B0%25E5%258C%25BA%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YkPHmdnhmdnHFbnH6sPhR40ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWcsnWRLrjDdnjnLP1T4nWcvr0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">西北地区</a>的林区、草原以及河流湖泊最高洪水位控制线范围内，还有431.4万公顷耕地位于25度以上陡坡，这996.3万公顷(将近1.5亿亩)耕地中，有相当部分需要根据国家<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E9%2580%2580%25E8%2580%2595%25E8%25BF%2598%25E6%259E%2597%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YkPHmdnhmdnHFbnH6sPhR40ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWcsnWRLrjDdnjnLP1T4nWcvr0" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">退耕还林</a>、还草、还湿和耕地休养生息等安排逐步调整；有相当数量耕地受到中、重度污染，大多不宜耕种；还有一定数量的耕地因开矿塌陷造成地表土层破坏、因地下水超采，已影响正常耕种。</p>

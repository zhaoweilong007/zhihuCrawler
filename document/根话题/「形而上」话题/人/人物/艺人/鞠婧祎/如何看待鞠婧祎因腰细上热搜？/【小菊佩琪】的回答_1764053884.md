 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-dbdb13702d32dc0c70e051f054ae567d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="637" data-rawheight="796" data-original-token="v2-d81ca63b03cde8d7df39482fe4a36200" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-11faeff0eb350af48fa602c45b23b56b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="637" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-dbdb13702d32dc0c70e051f054ae567d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p data-pid="20tVUYEq">之后我们将1倪放置到鞠婧祎身边，得出鞠婧祎的身高是5倪</p>
 <p data-pid="fXqmuOtr">结合刚刚1倪=11cm，所以鞠婧祎真实身高是55cm</p>

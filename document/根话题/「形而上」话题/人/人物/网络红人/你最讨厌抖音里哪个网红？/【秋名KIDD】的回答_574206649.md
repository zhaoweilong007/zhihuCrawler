 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vpH8nRFG">我自己身高180，但是我仍然觉得满满的恶意。</p>
 <p data-pid="_iqV05OY">就好像你炫富，却跑到山区去撒钱。</p>
 <p data-pid="mmKLKmtc">你炫颜值，却跑到精神病院去和患者合影。</p>
 <p data-pid="21Yi-NcC">任何用旁人的“不足”，来凸显自己长处的行为，哪怕你的长处的确很优秀，我都觉得很low。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kcgYGmAB">虽然我看不惯那些炫富还跑到山区去撒钱的，不过也不代表我就反对现在的年轻人回到山区发展。</p>
 <p data-pid="XBVjOJvm">其实这种例子抖音上还是有挺多的，</p>
 <p data-pid="HPMx6WTg">例如有个叫大新的年轻小伙就就是辞去了北京大城市里面的工作，回到老家，给乡里涂鸦改造。</p>
 <p data-pid="vN2B5w9T">这涂鸦改造我个人虽然不了解艺术，但是这么被他一画还真觉得有点意思</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1406379761258631169" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-c4cc8ef70013ab13dab5261345e7ddc1.jpg?source=382ee89a" data-lens-id="1406379761258631169"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-c4cc8ef70013ab13dab5261345e7ddc1.jpg?source=382ee89a"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1406379761258631169</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="n6mhI2K5">而且我就完全不排除这种形式的“炫”，毕竟这小伙子是实实在在的通过艺术改变村子里的环境。</p>
 <p data-pid="M02ninhr">除了这小伙，我觉得还有个之前上了各大热搜的个小伙子“念乡人周周”</p>
 <p data-pid="u7W362Lt">也是返乡大军中的一员。</p>
 <p data-pid="2faM5G-8">周周这小伙子也在城里头打过工，后面是回到了老家</p>
 <p data-pid="QRVvKuUh">用短视频的形式记录着自己在老家的生活，现在赚到的钱也会寄给自己的姐姐，供她上大学。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1406379841789259776" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-5230dd39598b1584272ba2c90a760c84.jpg?source=382ee89a" data-lens-id="1406379841789259776"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-5230dd39598b1584272ba2c90a760c84.jpg?source=382ee89a"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1406379841789259776</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="HeZNCzg-">还有个叫雨林峻峰的。</p>
 <p data-pid="n9yDNaJZ">十八岁外出打工，因为19年的一场大病回到了农村老家发展</p>
 <p data-pid="EIdCsdMp">现在就集中发展老家里面的农产品，</p>
 <p data-pid="GYdZvvml">靠着直播带货虽然不能说就逆袭了，但至少也过得比之前安逸了。</p>
 <p data-pid="WNWYKyM7">拍的视频并没有花里胡箫，就看着挺充满烟火气息。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1406379890858356736" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-02dff237ba5ba7120a05a6a6bbdebd3d.jpg?source=382ee89a" data-lens-id="1406379890858356736"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-02dff237ba5ba7120a05a6a6bbdebd3d.jpg?source=382ee89a"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1406379890858356736</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="AbxDIyqE">这些都是实实在在的在农村山区里务实干活的普通人</p>
 <p data-pid="wDAjQBI7">大峡谷土家幺妹谭桂英没有上面网红的180大长腿，也从不因为在山区拍视频有点火了就飘</p>
 <p data-pid="Q2z_y4zG">而是通过视频，分享着家乡的美景</p>
 <p data-pid="WUJZ9U3_">不是峡谷绮丽的风光带绮丽，就是村里头朴素的风土人文</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1406379934898663424" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-02ffbc311a8a7107c201f2fd94b808df.jpg?source=382ee89a" data-lens-id="1406379934898663424"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-02ffbc311a8a7107c201f2fd94b808df.jpg?source=382ee89a"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1406379934898663424</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="TCwDaGWL">这些在我看来才应该被叫成“网红”，不会拿别人的不足来彰显自己的“优秀”，</p>
 <p data-pid="k5Ueu-Tk">他们，本来就很优秀了。</p>

 <blockquote data-pid="s0Tta9v_">
  鹿晗（Lu Han），1990年4月20日出生于北京市海淀区，中国内地流行乐男歌手、影视演员。
  <br>
  2008年，赴韩国留学期间在明洞逛街时，被<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%259F%25A9%25E5%259B%25BDSM%25E5%25A8%25B1%25E4%25B9%2590%25E6%259C%2589%25E9%2599%2590%25E5%2585%25AC%25E5%258F%25B8" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">韩国SM娱乐有限公司</a>星探发掘成为旗下练习生。2012年4月，以韩国男子组合<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/EXO/2219893" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">EXO</a>/<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/EXO-M" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">EXO-M</a>成员正式出道，组合内担任主唱、领舞、门面担当。2015年，主演的电影《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%2587%258D%25E8%25BF%259420%25E5%25B2%2581/13028826" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">重返20岁</a>》《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%2588%2591%25E6%2598%25AF%25E8%25AF%2581%25E4%25BA%25BA/16785929" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">我是证人</a>》上映，凭借在电影《重返20岁》中的表现获得<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%25AC%25AC22%25E5%25B1%258A%25E5%258C%2597%25E4%25BA%25AC%25E5%25A4%25A7%25E5%25AD%25A6%25E7%2594%259F%25E7%2594%25B5%25E5%25BD%25B1%25E8%258A%2582/16609443" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">第22届北京大学生电影节</a>“最受欢迎男演员奖”。同年，发行个人首张音乐专辑《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/Reloaded/18313983" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Reloaded</a>》，获得国际唱片业协会<i>（IFPI）</i>“白金唱片”销量认证。
  <br>
  <br>
  2016年，主演的电影《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%259B%2597%25E5%25A2%2593%25E7%25AC%2594%25E8%25AE%25B0/6729311" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">盗墓笔记</a>》《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E9%2595%25BF%25E5%259F%258E/8562249" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">长城</a>》上映。同年，登上“中国90后10大影响力人物”榜首，并以2.7亿财富居2016“中国90后富豪榜”第五位。2017年1月，首次担任“中央电视台春节联欢晚会”表演嘉宾，演唱歌曲《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%2588%25B1%25E4%25BD%25A0%25E4%25B8%2580%25E4%25B8%2587%25E5%25B9%25B4/1507" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">爱你一万年</a>》。4月，主演古装玄幻剧《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%258B%25A9%25E5%25A4%25A9%25E8%25AE%25B0/19465979" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">择天记</a>》，该剧平均收视率达1.12，全网播放量突破300亿，为周播剧场开创以来非暑期档收视冠军。2018年，主演青春励志剧《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%2594%259C%25E8%259C%259C%25E6%259A%25B4%25E5%2587%25BB/20868266" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">甜蜜暴击</a>》。2019年，主演科幻战争电影《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E4%25B8%258A%25E6%25B5%25B7%25E5%25A0%25A1%25E5%259E%2592/18659809" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">上海堡垒</a>》。2020年，担任腾讯视频综艺节目《<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E5%2588%259B%25E9%2580%25A0%25E8%2590%25A52020/24114485" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">创造营2020</a>》教练成员。

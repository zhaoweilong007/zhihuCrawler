 <p data-pid="6XixRX92"><b>Step 1 了解自己的尺码</b></p>
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="32mCULnQ">尺码是穿衣的灵魂</p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="9e-g-igw">请忘掉这些烂大街的教程</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ecca58c2b9a29ddcb105430a00a314ca_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="848" data-original-token="v2-ecca58c2b9a29ddcb105430a00a314ca" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ecca58c2b9a29ddcb105430a00a314ca_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="yXzUdmTu"><b>你需要做的是：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="hqJ7OE_f">买一个软尺</li>
  <li data-pid="7wmD62hF">拿一张尺码表格和圆珠笔</li>
  <li data-pid="BCKNEUwL">脱光和找一名小助手 :)</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-08972dc0f546613231ce61b27897ad89_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1040" data-rawheight="302" data-original-token="v2-08972dc0f546613231ce61b27897ad89" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1040" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-08972dc0f546613231ce61b27897ad89_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="6QlHn_Ib">开始量了（尺码误差不超过1cm）</p>

 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-1e2b5c78c55691d3dc9684d5308e78ee_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="934" data-rawheight="1280" data-original-token="v2-1e2b5c78c55691d3dc9684d5308e78ee" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="934" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-1e2b5c78c55691d3dc9684d5308e78ee_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p data-pid="gq774PS1"><b>1、身高和体重：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="o5rwt7n4">量尺码的前提</li>
  <li data-pid="CbPrTT1R">身高-110=合适的体重可能苛刻了点</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-ca7d07c04c639695ca8e9f6592261258_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="2682" data-original-token="v2-ca7d07c04c639695ca8e9f6592261258" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-ca7d07c04c639695ca8e9f6592261258_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="IdZ7RQiW"><b>2、颈/胸/肩/臂/臀围：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="7bw5OI4b">裸身量尺码最准确</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-55f9852ac4ebc0e27b34cb613e92f40d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1280" data-original-token="v2-55f9852ac4ebc0e27b34cb613e92f40d" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-55f9852ac4ebc0e27b34cb613e92f40d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="wjaZKALJ"><b>3.1、四个袖长：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="c_8NafJK">袖长关系着你穿衣是否体贴</li>
  <li data-pid="AtQydPqc">袖子是从肩峰顶端开始量的</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4bcc59b8f77479c7ee7a64e932467fb6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="660" data-rawheight="1280" data-original-token="v2-4bcc59b8f77479c7ee7a64e932467fb6" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="660" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-4bcc59b8f77479c7ee7a64e932467fb6_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="uvzBRyyk"><b>3.2、衬衫袖长：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="QSwgU5xQ">休闲衬衫袖子长度盖住手腕就可以</li>
  <li data-pid="ClcqyKdt">正装衬衫袖子长度要比西装袖子长</li>
  <li data-pid="_cq1XUug">衬衫系上所有的扣子后脖领处要留一根手指头的宽度</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d83e6f38abc8af4acc3e693e54519488_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="960" data-original-token="v2-d83e6f38abc8af4acc3e693e54519488" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-d83e6f38abc8af4acc3e693e54519488_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="AXdfIEKl"><b>4、四个衣长：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="tHXrOZL9">衣长合适穿衣就不会太奇怪</li>
  <li data-pid="407sZ5E-">从锁骨上方1~2cm处量</li>
  <li data-pid="kk5-CLPi">超短夹克衣长（如牛仔外套）比长袖短3cm左右</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-cfa6e9be9f6ec7ff7e12babb678a16d8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="2782" data-original-token="v2-cfa6e9be9f6ec7ff7e12babb678a16d8" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-cfa6e9be9f6ec7ff7e12babb678a16d8_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="TdXkmTdt"><b>5、三个腰围：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="WyVAclPw">裤子版型分高中低腰，三个腰围应付各种版型</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-d1d0ef1a1e19af958f8389c821284d95_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="981" data-original-token="v2-d1d0ef1a1e19af958f8389c821284d95" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-d1d0ef1a1e19af958f8389c821284d95_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ycsjJfsk"><b>6、大腿/小腿/脚围：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="XfO_K6dZ">腰围和大腿围代表着你穿裤子是否能合适</li>
  <li data-pid="kDVpCJA2">腿细的/腿粗的/健身的请注意小腿和小脚围度</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-10f78689089f65314eab03f2ee4ea284_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1193" data-original-token="v2-10f78689089f65314eab03f2ee4ea284" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-10f78689089f65314eab03f2ee4ea284_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="mwVdo14s"><b>7、三个裤长：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="Ekn9oVHr">裤子太长可以花钱找裁缝裁短</li>
 </ul>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="shHLWsm7">腿粗的买裤子攻略：</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="9nz820Rf"><a href="https://www.zhihu.com/question/29105017/answer/153299831" class="internal">腿粗的男生怎么买裤子？ - 知乎</a></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9bc45f4e6c716cd0edd51cee983f7b45_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="748" data-rawheight="1280" data-original-token="v2-9bc45f4e6c716cd0edd51cee983f7b45" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="748" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-9bc45f4e6c716cd0edd51cee983f7b45_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="vIhWNn35"><b>8、脚的长度与宽度：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="61ovEJrE">误差越小越好</li>
  <li data-pid="HSMSYBfU">最好晚上量脚的尺码（晚上脚大）</li>
 </ul>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d659205cf98f38502590e9404b1c35d0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="2602" data-original-token="v2-d659205cf98f38502590e9404b1c35d0" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1280" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d659205cf98f38502590e9404b1c35d0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

  <li data-pid="-fKWAWBY">呃，这个自己<b>偷偷量</b>吧</li>

 </ul>

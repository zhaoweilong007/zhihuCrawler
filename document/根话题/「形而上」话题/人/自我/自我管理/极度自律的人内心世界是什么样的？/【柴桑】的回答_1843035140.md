 <p>这就是我的自律初期养成——<strong>极限一换一<span><span>，</span></span>用魔鬼打败魔鬼<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>而后期<span><span>，</span></span>我只需要想办法用读书的习惯换掉玩手机的习惯就好了<span><span>，</span></span>相比起直接养成早起习惯要简单多了<span><span>。</span></span>实在不行<span><span>，</span></span>就玩 10 分钟手机也耽误不了什么<span><span>，</span></span>比多睡死两个小时好<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>只有当做一件事的动力大于阻力时<span><span>，</span></span>行为才会发生<span><span>，</span></span>乃至于最后成为你的自控力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>1</strong></p>
 <p>在<span><span>《</span></span>三体<span><span>》</span></span>里<span><span>，</span></span>歌者用<span><span>「</span></span>二向箔<span><span>」</span></span>将整个太阳系降维打击<span><span>，</span></span>使其从三维空间降成了二维<span><span>，</span></span>所有人都变成了平面人<span><span>，</span></span>无人生还<span><span>。</span></span>只有两个人<span><span>，</span></span>乘坐<span><span>「</span></span>超光速飞船<span><span>」</span></span>成功逃出太阳系<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>为什么超光速飞船能带他们成功逃逸二维空间<span><span>？</span></span>因为他们超越了太阳系的逃逸速度——光速<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>因为只有在<span><span>「</span></span>动力<span><span>」</span></span>大于<span><span>「</span></span>阻力<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>即飞行器速度大于太阳系的逃逸速度的时候<span><span>，</span></span>才能成功逃亡<span><span>，</span></span>否则就无法避免被从三维降到二维的命运<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>宇宙真理如此<span><span>，</span></span>人性本质也是如此<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>阻力 &gt; 动力<span><span>，</span></span>这时人就会无限拖延<span><span>，</span></span>百般不愿意做<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>阻力 &lt; 动力<span><span>，</span></span>这时人就会行动力满满<span><span>，</span></span>自觉养成自律<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>其实许多习惯养成的本质都很简单<span><span>，</span></span>虽然我也可以和你拽类似<span><span>「</span></span>理性驱动<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>大脑短视<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>肌肉极限<span><span>」</span></span>的术语<span><span>，</span></span>但我更愿意从最本质<span><span>，</span></span>最朴实的底层逻辑<span><span>，</span></span>告诉你养成自控力的真相<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>想办法让你做事的动力大于阻力<span><span>，</span></span>就是这么简单<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>2</strong></p>
 <p>总有文章指望着你学会几个方法论<span><span>，</span></span>下载几个 APP 就变得自律了<span><span>，</span></span>朋友<span><span>，</span></span>那真的不太现实<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>因为<span><span>，</span></span>绝大多数人本性都是不够自律的<span><span>，</span></span>否则我们早就殖民银河系了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这就是大脑的天性<span><span>，</span></span>它总是趋利避害<span><span>，</span></span>能偷懒则偷懒<span><span>。</span></span>大脑每天想的<span><span>，</span></span>就是如何以最小的消耗<span><span>、</span></span>实现最多的收益<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>对这种幼稚的大脑<span><span>，</span></span>我们不能想着<span><span>「</span></span>驯服<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>而应该想着<span><span>「</span></span>诱惑<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>从<strong><span><span>「</span></span>动力-阻力<span><span>」</span></span></strong>模型入手<span><span>，</span></span>用极轻微<span><span>、</span></span>无门槛的行为<span><span>，</span></span>让你从行动中收获到快感<span><span>，</span></span>再通过这种快感<span><span>，</span></span>从内部推动你自己<span><span>，</span></span>去不断喜欢它<span><span>、</span></span>践行它<span><span>，</span></span>直到内化成你的习惯<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你想啊<span><span>，</span></span>如果一种成长的方式需要依赖于<span><span>「</span></span>自控力<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>那它的价值其实就很有限 <span><span>。</span></span>—— 除了让你在前期感觉<span><span>「</span></span>啊<span><span>，</span></span>好厉害<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>后期感觉<span><span>「</span></span>靠<span><span>，</span></span>好痛苦<span><span>」</span></span>以外<span><span>，</span></span>起不到任何作用<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>认知铺垫完了<span><span>，</span></span>接下来我给你分享一些切实可行的自控方法<span><span>，</span></span>放心<span><span>，</span></span>都是不怎么需要靠意志力的那种<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>02</strong></p>

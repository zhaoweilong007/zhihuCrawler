# 人们在没有计算机的帮助下是如何计算根号3？

<body>
 <p data-pid="LivoOCZA">高中教我们数学竞赛的老师传授过一招<b>竖式手算开根</b>的操作，第一次见识时觉得简直不可思议。现在自己做了高中老师，时不时也拿这招在学生面前秀一波操作，屡试不爽。</p>
 <p data-pid="wxYQSpEU">就以 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B3%7D" alt="\sqrt{3}" eeimg="1"> 为例。</p>
 <p data-pid="oJ-u4QZN">首先，找出自乘后依然小于 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3" alt="3" eeimg="1"> 的最大正整数，显然是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1" alt="1" eeimg="1">，作为求根的第一步的结果，写到竖式最顶上。取其自乘的结果，从要开方的数字 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3" alt="3" eeimg="1"> 中扣除掉，得到误差值 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2" alt="2" eeimg="1">。这里所谓的误差具体是指，目前开根的结果，平方后和实际目标的差值为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2" alt="2" eeimg="1">，即 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3-1%5E2%3D2" alt="3-1^2=2" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="jK6rHtH5"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B1%7D%5C%5C+%28%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B1%7D%29+%26%5Coverline%7B%5Cbig%293.000000%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B1%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D2+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\text{ }\text{ }\color{green}{1}\\ (\color{green}{1}) &amp;\overline{\big)3.000000}\\ &amp;\text{ }\underline{\text{ }\color{green}{1}\text{ }}\\ &amp;\text{ }\text{ }2 \end{align*} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="MdNLxgtu">补两位 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 下来，得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200" alt="200" eeimg="1">。取现有结果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1" alt="1" eeimg="1"> 的两倍，作为一个过渡值，写到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200" alt="200" eeimg="1"> 边上，我们得到的就是括号里的这个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2" alt="2" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="0jSY7UK2"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B1%7D%5C%5C+%28%7B1%7D%29+%26%5Coverline%7B%5Cbig%293.000000%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ctext%7B+%7D%7B1%7D%5Cphantom%7B000%7D+%7D%5C%5C+%28%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B2%7D%5C_%7D%29%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D2%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C++%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\text{ }\text{ }\color{red}{1}\\ ({1}) &amp;\overline{\big)3.000000}\\ &amp;\text{ }\underline{\text{ }{1}\phantom{000} }\\ ({\color{red}{2}\_})&amp;\phantom{0}2\phantom{.}\color{blue}{00} \\  \end{align*} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="--CULbu7">接下来，脑补下在这个过渡值后面怎么补个最大的个位数字 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1">，使得 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=2X+%5Ctimes+X" alt="2X \times X" eeimg="1"> 依然比 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200" alt="200" eeimg="1"> 小。注意到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=27%5Ctimes7%3D189%3C200" alt="27\times7=189<200" eeimg="1">，但是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=28%5Ctimes8%3D224%3E200" alt="28\times8=224>200" eeimg="1">，因此这里要补的数值即为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=7" alt="7" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="2sX2rxiI">这个数字即为开根出来的下一位有效数字。快乐地搬运至竖式最顶上，得到精度稍高一点的开根结果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1.7" alt="1.7" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="nq8P80dh">顺势把 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=189" alt="189" eeimg="1"> 从 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=200" alt="200" eeimg="1"> 中扣除，得到下一步要处理的误差值 11。补齐小数点，不难验证，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3-1.7%5E2%3D3-2.89%3D0.11" alt="3-1.7^2=3-2.89=0.11" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="OC3n7F7G"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B1%7D%7D.%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B7%7D%5C%5C+%281%29+%26%5Coverline%7B%5Cbig%293.000000%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ctext%7B+%7D1%5Cphantom%7B000%7D+%7D%5C%5C+%28%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B2%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B7%7D%7D%7D%29%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D2%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B1%5Cphantom%7B.%7D89%7D%5Cphantom%7B0%7D%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Cphantom%7B00.%7D11+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\phantom{0}{\color{red}{1}}.\color{green}{7}\\ (1) &amp;\overline{\big)3.000000}\\ &amp;\text{ }\underline{\text{ }1\phantom{000} }\\ ({\color{red}{2\color{green}{7}}})&amp;\phantom{0}2\phantom{.}\color{blue}{00} \\ &amp;\text{ }\text{ }\underline{\color{green}{1\phantom{.}89}\phantom{0}}\\ &amp;\phantom{00.}11 \end{align*} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="WWT2cbaj">补两位 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=0" alt="0" eeimg="1"> 下来，得到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1100" alt="1100" eeimg="1">。取现有结果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=17" alt="17" eeimg="1"> 的两倍，作为新的过渡值，得到括号里的这个 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=34" alt="34" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="OJpPFR0E"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B1%7D%7D.%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B7%7D%5C%5C+%281%29+%26%5Coverline%7B%5Cbig%293.%5C%2C000000%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ctext%7B+%7D1%5Cphantom%7B000%7D+%7D%5C%5C+%2827%29%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D2%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B1%5Cphantom%7B.%7D89%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%5Cphantom%7B00%7D%7D%5C%5C+%28%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B34%5C_%7D%29%26%5Cphantom%7B00.%7D11%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\phantom{0}{\color{red}{1}}.\color{red}{7}\\ (1) &amp;\overline{\big)3.\,000000}\\ &amp;\text{ }\underline{\text{ }1\phantom{000} }\\ (27)&amp;\phantom{0}2\phantom{.}{00} \\ &amp;\text{ }\text{ }\underline{1\phantom{.}89\phantom{.}\phantom{00}}\\ (\color{red}{34\_})&amp;\phantom{00.}11\color{blue}{00} \\ \end{align*} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="BW1bVlTy">接着继续脑补过渡值该补上个什么个位数字 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1">。注意到 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=343%5Ctimes3%3D1029%3C1100" alt="343\times3=1029<1100" eeimg="1">，但是 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=344%5Ctimes4+%3D+1376+%3E+1100" alt="344\times4 = 1376 > 1100" eeimg="1">，因此这里要补的数值即为 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3" alt="3" eeimg="1">。</p>
 <p data-pid="qg29YT2Y">这样就得到了开根出来的新一位有效数字。</p>
 <p data-pid="dmG7ysSo">类似地也可以验证，这一步得到的结果 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=1.73" alt="1.73" eeimg="1"> ，平方后和期望值 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3" alt="3" eeimg="1"> 之间的误差有：<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=3-1.73%5E2+%3D+3-2.9929%3D0.0071" alt="3-1.73^2 = 3-2.9929=0.0071" eeimg="1">，这也就是后面要继续处理的误差值。</p>
 <p data-pid="vlSh0GG2"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B1%7D%7D.%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B7%7D%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B3%7D%5C%5C+%28%7B1%7D%29+%26%5Coverline%7B%5Cbig%293.000000%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ctext%7B+%7D1%5Cphantom%7B000%7D+%7D%5C%5C+%2827%29%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D2%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B1%5Cphantom%7B.%7D89%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%5Cphantom%7B00%7D%7D%5C%5C+%28%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B34%7D%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B3%7D%29%26%5Cphantom%7B00.%7D11%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%26%5Cphantom%7B0.0%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B1029%7D%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Cphantom%7B0000.%7D71+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\phantom{0}{\color{red}{1}}.\color{red}{7}\color{green}{3}\\ ({1}) &amp;\overline{\big)3.000000}\\ &amp;\text{ }\underline{\text{ }1\phantom{000} }\\ (27)&amp;\phantom{0}2\phantom{.}{00} \\ &amp;\text{ }\text{ }\underline{1\phantom{.}89\phantom{.}\phantom{00}}\\ (\color{red}{34}\color{green}{3})&amp;\phantom{00.}11\color{blue}{00} \\ &amp;\phantom{0.0}\underline{\color{green}{1029}}\\ &amp;\phantom{0000.}71 \end{align*} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="d7i0ltjs">无限重复这个操作，可以手算根号到任意精度。</p>
 <p data-pid="dupcTLy3"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Cbegin%7Balign%2A%7D+%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D%7B%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B1%7D%7D.%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B73%7D%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B2%7D%5C%5C+%28%7B1%7D%29+%26%5Coverline%7B%5Cbig%293.000000%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ctext%7B+%7D1%5Cphantom%7B000%7D+%7D%5C%5C+%2827%29%26%5Cphantom%7B0%7D2%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%26%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Ctext%7B+%7D%5Cunderline%7B1%5Cphantom%7B.%7D89%5Cphantom%7B.%7D%5Cphantom%7B00%7D%7D%5C%5C+%28343%29%26%5Cphantom%7B00.%7D1100+%5C%5C+%26%5Cphantom%7B0.0%7D%5Cunderline%7B%7B1029%7D%5Cphantom%7B00%7D%7D%5C%5C+%28%5Ccolor%7Bred%7D%7B346%7D%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B2%7D%29%26%5Cphantom%7B0000.%7D71%5Ccolor%7Bblue%7D%7B00%7D+%5C%5C+%26%5Cphantom%7B0.000%7D%5Cunderline%7B%5Ccolor%7Bgreen%7D%7B6924%7D%7D%5C%5C+%26%5Cphantom%7B00000.%7D176+%5Cend%7Balign%2A%7D+%5C%5C" alt="\begin{align*} &amp;\phantom{0}{\color{red}{1}}.\color{red}{73}\color{green}{2}\\ ({1}) &amp;\overline{\big)3.000000}\\ &amp;\text{ }\underline{\text{ }1\phantom{000} }\\ (27)&amp;\phantom{0}2\phantom{.}{00} \\ &amp;\text{ }\text{ }\underline{1\phantom{.}89\phantom{.}\phantom{00}}\\ (343)&amp;\phantom{00.}1100 \\ &amp;\phantom{0.0}\underline{{1029}\phantom{00}}\\ (\color{red}{346}\color{green}{2})&amp;\phantom{0000.}71\color{blue}{00} \\ &amp;\phantom{0.000}\underline{\color{green}{6924}}\\ &amp;\phantom{00000.}176 \end{align*} \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="HxCJlIXW">于是有</p>
 <p data-pid="kb50xTwE"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%5Csqrt%7B3%7D+%3D+1.732%5Ccdots+%5C%5C" alt="\sqrt{3} = 1.732\cdots \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="6YMrzPkW">这套操作的原理其实并不复杂，其核心是一个挺简单的代数恒等式。</p>
 <p data-pid="uUfkDKQS"><img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2810a+%2B+b%29%5E2+%3D+100a%5E2+%2B+%2820a+%2B+b%29+%5Ctimes+b+%5Cqquad+%28%2A%29+%5C%5C" alt="(10a + b)^2 = 100a^2 + (20a + b) \times b \qquad (*) \\" eeimg="1"></p>
 <p data-pid="BL6a5j4I">对于一个待开根的数，如果我们已经找到了一个初步的结果，可以怎么去对这个结果做修正呢？在这里，如果把 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%2A%29" alt="(*)" eeimg="1"> 式中的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=10a" alt="10a" eeimg="1"> 视作现有的开根结果，<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=b" alt="b" eeimg="1"> 就是要找的下一位有效数字。前面竖式开根运算中用到的过渡值，其实就来自于<img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%28%2A%29" alt="(*)" eeimg="1"> 式中的 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=20a%2Bb" alt="20a+b" eeimg="1"> 这一项，而在过渡值里面脑补的个位 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=X" alt="X" eeimg="1">，所做的其实就是在凑 <img src="https://www.zhihu.com/equation?tex=%2820a+%2B+b%29+%5Ctimes+b" alt="(20a + b) \times b" eeimg="1"> 这步修正误差的运算。如此得到下一位有效数字，我们可以继续对精度提升一位后的现有结果进行同样的操作，不断提升开根的精度。</p>

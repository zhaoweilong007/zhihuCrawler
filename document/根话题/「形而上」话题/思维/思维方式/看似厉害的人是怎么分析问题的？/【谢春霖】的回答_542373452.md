<body>
 <p><strong>我先来举一个例子<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>假设<span><span>，</span></span>你是一家创业公司的 CEO</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>最近<span><span>，</span></span>你发现公司的业绩一路下滑<span><span>，</span></span>你打算找业务部主管王小锤聊一下<span><span>，</span></span>看看到底发生了什么<span><span>、</span></span>下一步该如何应对<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>于是<span><span>，</span></span>你把他叫到了办公室<span><span>，</span></span>一脸严肃地问道<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>小锤<span><span>，</span></span>最近公司业绩一直在下滑<span><span>，</span></span>你打算下一步怎么办<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>小锤有些颤抖<span><span>，</span></span>紧张地说道<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span></strong>老板<span><span>，</span></span>这两个月团队走了几个得力干将<span><span>。</span></span>另外<span><span>，</span></span>这段时间<span><span>，</span></span>市场上出现了一个竞争对手<span><span>，</span></span>和我们的产品功能基本一致<span><span>，</span></span>但价格比我们低了许多<span><span>，</span></span>用户现在都觉得我们的产品太贵<span><span>，</span></span>都跑去买他们的了<span><span>，</span></span>所以业绩才下滑<span><span>。</span></span>如果能帮我再招募几个销售<span><span>，</span></span>适当地再给我一些折扣的权力<span><span>，</span></span>我有信心<span><span>，</span></span>业绩一定能提上去<span><span>！</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>你听上去感觉像是借口<span><span>，</span></span>便质问道<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span></strong>再招几个销售没问题<span><span>，</span></span>我帮你去和人力资源部的李总打声招呼<span><span>。</span></span>可价格高不是理由吧<span><span>？</span></span>我们一直都是这个价格在卖<span><span>，</span></span>而且我们为了保证产品质量<span><span>，</span></span>用的都是好材料<span><span>，</span></span>利润本来就不高<span><span>，</span></span>现在你再一打折<span><span>，</span></span>利润就更少了<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>小锤看你有些犹豫<span><span>，</span></span>就继续说道<span><span>：</span></span></strong><span><span>「</span></span>现在市场发生变化了<span><span>，</span></span>竞争对手开始主动降价<span><span>，</span></span>我们也要跟进<span><span>，</span></span>晚了也许市场就没了<span><span>！</span></span>通过薄利多销<span><span>，</span></span>先把竞争对手赶出去<span><span>，</span></span>把市场拿回来<span><span>！</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>被他这么一说<span><span>，</span></span>你心头一紧<span><span>，</span></span>感觉挺有道理<span><span>，</span></span>频频点头<span><span>，</span></span>还没等你开口<span><span>，</span></span>小锤又接着说<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span></strong>只要您给我打折的权力<span><span>，</span></span>再给我配 2 个人<span><span>，</span></span>我保证下个月的业绩能有 2 倍的提升<span><span>！</span></span>把市场给拿回来<span><span>！</span></span>做不到我就走人<span><span>！</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>你被王小锤的诚意所打动<span><span>，</span></span>脸上露出了迷人的微笑<span><span>，</span></span>便许诺了他提出的条件<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>一个月过去了……</strong></p>
 <p>公司的销售额确实提升了 2 倍<span><span>，</span></span>但由于都是打折出售<span><span>，</span></span>算上新增的人力成本<span><span>、</span></span>团队的运营成本<span><span>，</span></span>利润竟然变为了负数<span><span>！</span></span>并且<span><span>，</span></span>竞争对手竟然又降价了<span><span>，</span></span>摆出了一副誓死要打价格战的架势<span><span>，</span></span>小锤又向你提出了需要更高折扣的请求<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>怎么办<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>再降<span><span>？</span></span>就会亏更多<span><span>！</span></span>不降<span><span>？</span></span>市场就没了<span><span>。</span></span>裁人<span><span>？</span></span>团队就散了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>小锤<span><span>，</span></span>你这是在坑我啊<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>你焦头烂额……</p>
 <p><strong>为什么会这样<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>那是因为你只看到了<span><span>「</span></span>表面问题<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>没有找到<span><span>「</span></span>本质问题<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>！</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>什么是表面问题<span><span>？</span></span>什么是本质问题<span><span>？</span></span>我们又应该如何找到本质问题呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>要找到本质问题<span><span>，</span></span>我们得先看一下到底什么是<span><span>「</span></span>问题<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>它是如何产生的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>比如我问你<span><span>：</span></span>什么是区块链<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>如果你不知道区块链的含义<span><span>，</span></span>那么它对你来说就是个问题<span><span>；</span></span>而如果你的认知中<span><span>，</span></span>已经拥有了区块链的认知<span><span>，</span></span>那么这对你来说就不是个问题<span><span>，</span></span>或者说<span><span>，</span></span>你已经解决了这个问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>再比如<span><span>，</span></span>我问你<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>你为什么这个月业绩那么差<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>这是一个问题吗<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>这就不一定了……</p>
 <p>如果说<span><span>，</span></span>公司给你的业绩目标是 100 万<span><span>，</span></span>你做了 50 万<span><span>，</span></span>那么这就是个问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>所以<span><span>「</span></span>问题<span><span>」</span></span>是什么<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong><span><span>「</span></span>问题<span><span>」</span></span>就是<span><span>：</span></span>期望与现状的落差部分<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>假设某件事的期望值是<span><span>（</span></span>B<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>现状是<span><span>（</span></span>B'<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>那么<span><span>（</span></span>B'→B<span><span>）</span></span>这个落差部分就是问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>为什么我们常说<span><span>：</span></span>没有问题<span><span>，</span></span>就是最大的问题<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>因为没有问题<span><span>，</span></span>就意味着你不知道目标在哪里<span><span>，</span></span>也不知道现状是什么<span><span>，</span></span>自然就不知道有什么问题<span><span>，</span></span>只是当一天和尚撞一天钟<span><span>，</span></span>随波逐流<span><span>，</span></span>一脸迷茫<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>比如你刚对一群人讲完一大段话<span><span>，</span></span>然后问大家还有没有问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>大家回答你<span><span>：</span></span>没有问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你千万别天真地以为大家都听懂了<span><span>，</span></span>更大的可能是<span><span>：</span></span>大家不知道什么算真正地听懂了<span><span>，</span></span>以及为什么要听你说这一大段<span><span>，</span></span>他们没有一个期望值<span><span>（</span></span>B<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>；</span></span>也不知道自己听懂了什么<span><span>，</span></span>没听懂什么<span><span>，</span></span>处在游离状态<span><span>，</span></span>找不到自己的现状<span><span>（</span></span>B'<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>因此<span><span>，</span></span>不是他们完全听懂了<span><span>，</span></span>没有问题<span><span>，</span></span>而是不知道自己有没有听懂<span><span>，</span></span>不知道什么算完全听懂<span><span>，</span></span>因而找不到这个<span><span>「</span></span>落差<span><span>」</span></span>在哪里<span><span>，</span></span>没有发现落差也就没有发现问题<span><span>，</span></span>所以只能回答<span><span>：</span></span>没有问题<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>我们所有的解决办法<span><span>，</span></span>都应该是围绕<span><span>（</span></span>B'→B<span><span>）</span></span>的这个部分来展开思考的<span><span>，</span></span>找不到这个落差部分<span><span>，</span></span>我们的解决方案也就无从开始<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>可是<span><span>，</span></span>很多人日常工作<span><span>、</span></span>生活中的交流并不是这样的<span><span>，</span></span>他们往往在还没弄清楚问题<span><span>「</span></span>是什么<span><span>」</span></span>的情况下<span><span>，</span></span>就急于开始给出自己的建议<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>比如<span><span>，</span></span>文章开头的问题<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>小锤<span><span>，</span></span>最近公司业绩一直在下滑<span><span>，</span></span>你打算下一步怎么办<span><span>？</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>这个问题<span><span>，</span></span>其实就很模糊<span><span>，</span></span>怎么算下滑<span><span>？</span></span>目标业绩是多少<span><span>？</span></span>现在的业绩是多少<span><span>？</span></span>下滑的比例是多少<span><span>？</span></span>过去几个月具体是什么情况<span><span>？</span></span>是原来做 100 万<span><span>，</span></span>现在变成 30 万<span><span>；</span></span>还是原来做 100 万<span><span>，</span></span>现在下降到 80 万<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>虽然都是业绩变差了<span><span>，</span></span>但这两个问题<span><span>，</span></span>显然是不同的问题<span><span>：</span></span>一个是要解决 70 万差额的断崖式下跌<span><span>；</span></span>一个是要解决 20 万差额的业绩波动<span><span>。</span></span>那应该给的解决方案当然是不同的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>如果是第一个问题<span><span>，</span></span>那可能就需要动大手术了<span><span>，</span></span>比如降价拉销售<span><span>、</span></span>整个团队大换血<span><span>，</span></span>甚至是战略调整<span><span>；</span></span>而如果是第二个问题<span><span>，</span></span>那么补两个销售<span><span>，</span></span>再给团队打一针鸡血<span><span>，</span></span>老大一声吼<span><span>，</span></span>大伙儿向前冲<span><span>，</span></span>也许就能解决<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>又比如<span><span>，</span></span>闺蜜向你抱怨<span><span>，</span></span>最近和男朋友关系不好<span><span>，</span></span>怎么办<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>

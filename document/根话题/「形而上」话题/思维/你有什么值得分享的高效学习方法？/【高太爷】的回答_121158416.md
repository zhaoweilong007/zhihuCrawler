 <p>什么才是最佳的时间节点<span><span>，</span></span>实验给出的建议是 5 分钟后重复一遍<span><span>，</span></span>20 分钟后再重复一遍<span><span>，</span></span>1 小时后<span><span>，</span></span>12 小时后<span><span>，</span></span>1 天后<span><span>，</span></span>2 天后<span><span>，</span></span>5 天后<span><span>，</span></span>8 天后<span><span>，</span></span>14 天后就会记得很牢<span><span>，</span></span>很难再遗忘<span><span>。</span></span>但对于较为繁重的学习<span><span>，</span></span>这近乎不太可能<span><span>，</span></span>我个人的实践是<span><span>：</span></span>即时复述很关键<span><span>（</span></span>短期记忆转化成长期记忆<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>12 小时或睡前温习很重要<span><span>（</span></span>最好是通过题目巩固<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>1 天后<span><span>、</span></span>2 天后<span><span>、</span></span>8 天后三次复习<span><span>，</span></span>效果通常就已经非常不错<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>3<span><span>、</span></span>记忆编码理论<span><span>：</span></span>必须动手构建知识树<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>

 <p><strong>4<span><span>、</span></span>内隐语言理论<span><span>：</span></span>打破学习错觉<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>

 <p>这种学习错觉<span><span>（</span></span>误将熟悉当知道<span><span>）</span></span>是内隐语言与外部语言的差异造成的<span><span>。</span></span>语言分为内隐语言和外部语言<span><span>。</span></span>通俗讲<span><span>，</span></span>内隐语言是我们自问自答或不出声的言语<span><span>，</span></span>外部语言就是我们正常交谈或小声默念<span><span>。</span></span>内隐语言过于隐秘<span><span>、</span></span>快捷<span><span>、</span></span>破碎<span><span>，</span></span>而外部语言<span><span>（</span></span>或者书写表达<span><span>）</span></span>是逻辑<span><span>、</span></span>完整<span><span>、</span></span>成体系的<span><span>，</span></span>但如果一个知识点能用内隐语言描述<span><span>，</span></span>我们就误以为很熟悉<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>具体到学习中<span><span>，</span></span>对一个知识点进行复习时<span><span>，</span></span>内隐语言抓住了几个关键点<span><span>，</span></span>我们就会产生学习错觉<span><span>，</span></span>误以为都知道<span><span>，</span></span>但知识点不是孤岛<span><span>，</span></span>是由逻辑关系构成的<span><span>，</span></span>这需要外部语言去描述<span><span>、</span></span>串联<span><span>，</span></span>唯有经过外部语言检验<span><span>，</span></span>才是真正的掌握理解<span><span>！</span></span>因此<span><span>，</span></span>必须将内部语言转换成外部语言<span><span>。</span></span>认知心理学推荐生成与测试两种办法<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>生成效应是指向同学<span><span>、</span></span>朋友<span><span>、</span></span>家人解释解释所学知识<span><span>，</span></span>在解释过程中<span><span>，</span></span>主动思考组织知识<span><span>，</span></span>构建出知识的逻辑性和条理性<span><span>。</span></span>有经验的童鞋都知道<span><span>，</span></span>很多灵感都是在给别人讲题中产生<span><span>，</span></span>甚至是请教别人时恍然大悟<span><span>，</span></span>另外做讲座之类的对掌握知识非常有用<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>测试是指对知识点进行自我提问<span><span>，</span></span>然后将答案小声说出来<span><span>，</span></span>或者完整写出来<span><span>，</span></span>不能心里一晃而过几个关键词就觉得了解然后测试结束<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>5<span><span>、</span></span>情绪绩效理论<span><span>：</span></span>压力是资源<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>学习是为了提高自身<span><span>，</span></span>但对学生及成人教育而言<span><span>，</span></span>考场发挥也非常重要<span><span>，</span></span>临场心态非常关键<span><span>，</span></span>那一刻的心态抵得上几个礼拜<span><span>、</span></span>几个月甚至几年的苦功<span><span>！</span></span>这就涉及情绪绩效理<span><span>，</span></span>即情绪唤醒水平和绩效间存在着倒 U 型曲线的关系<span><span>，</span></span>情绪太低或太高都会损害绩效<span><span>。</span></span>所以适当的压力是有好处的<span><span>，</span></span>完全没有压力或压力过高都会影响发挥<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>压力的最新研究还表明<span><span>，</span></span>我们对压力的认知会改变这一压力曲线<span><span>（</span></span>大致是左<span><span>、</span></span>右平移<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>比如一个实验证实<span><span>，</span></span>如果人们被简单告知<span><span>「</span></span>你是那种在压力下表现更好的人<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>他们实际表现会提高 33%<span><span>，</span></span>即使这只是随便说说也无妨<span><span>，</span></span>重要的是信息改变了个人对压力的评估方式<span><span>。</span></span>所以<span><span>，</span></span>视压力为资源<span><span>，</span></span>会让我们在压力下表现更好<span><span>（</span></span>向右平移<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>而谈压色变则会让哪怕一丁点的压力都影像我们发挥<span><span>（</span></span>向左平移<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>6<span><span>、</span></span>合理休息<span><span>：</span></span>休息也是学习<span><span>。</span></span></strong><br>

These studies show essentially no benefit from dura-tions exceeding 4 hr per day and reduced benefits from practice exceeding 2 hr (Welford, 1968; Woodworth &amp; Schlosberg, 1954). 
  <br>
  <br>
   有很多关于学习时间与效率的研究<span><span>，</span></span>这些研究证实<span><span>：</span></span>超过 2 小时学习效果就逐渐递减<span><span>，</span></span>超过 4 小时根本就没有效果了<span><span>。</span></span>因此合理的休息与高效率的学习同等重要<span><span>，</span></span>长时间无效学习尤其会让人心生厌恶<span><span>。</span></span>
  <br>
   one would expect that the level of practice an individual can sustain for long periods of time is limited by the individual's ability to recover and thereby main-tain a steady state from day to day. After the individual has slowly adapted to a constant level of practice, increases ought to be possible. In contrast, if an individual cannot recover each day from a given level of practice, sustaining that level will lead to exhaustion and mental fatigue. 
  <br>
   要想长期能够保持稳定的学习效率<span><span>，</span></span>就不能一次过度消耗甚至透支<span><span>，</span></span>要确保每次学习时精力都能够恢复<span><span>。</span></span>一旦过度透支<span><span>，</span></span>不仅会造成效率低下<span><span>，</span></span>甚至带来精神疲劳<span><span>，</span></span>削弱动机和坚持动力<span><span>，</span></span>很多少年天才通常过于急功近利<span><span>（</span></span>或被迫<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>致使兴趣<span><span>、</span></span>热情被消耗干净<span><span>，</span></span>最后无法坚持而失败<span><span>。</span></span>
 </blockquote>
 <p>对于长期的学习<span><span>，</span></span>明智的做法是<span><span>，</span></span>控制每天的高强度学习时间<span><span>，</span></span>避免精神疲劳或逆反心理<span><span>，</span></span>简而言之<span><span>：</span></span></p>

 <p>2<span><span>）</span></span>长期看<span><span>，</span></span>长时间的坚持学习<span><span>，</span></span>能够让身心能逐步适应<span><span>，</span></span>之后可逐渐增加学习量和学习强度<span><span>。</span></span></p>

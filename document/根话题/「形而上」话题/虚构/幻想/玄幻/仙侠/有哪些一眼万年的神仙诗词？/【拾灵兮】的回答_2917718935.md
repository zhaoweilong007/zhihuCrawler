# 有哪些一眼万年的神仙诗词？
- 点赞数：6122
- 更新时间：2023年03月02日11时28分16秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/547748295/answer/2917718935
<body>
 <p data-pid="TNtus-It"><b>1、与谁同坐。明月清风我。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="y0A5AlbJ">——苏轼《点绛唇·闲倚胡床》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Z-kAXfb3">2、惊起归鸿不成字，辞柯落叶最知秋。</p>
 <p data-pid="_v0ARLH2">——黄庭坚《次韵任君官舍秋雨》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gyRnzhhz">3、一念清净，烈焰成池。</p>
 <p data-pid="SkiU4jbE">——洪应明 《菜根谭》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yNFrQgG6">4、枝叶扶苏，漏下月光，碎如残雪。</p>
 <p data-pid="5gSkDgkd">——张岱《十锦塘》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="QhDT6PPI"><b>5、我有一瓢酒，可以慰风尘。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="3pRhtHvb">——韦应物《简卢陟》</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-5800c080920c6cc790972376337411e0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="608" data-rawheight="1081" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-b7357144c0e5b6e6bbb834ee8a41ff6f" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-11eb14f3adc2b71b61cca42574ea30ca_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="608" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5800c080920c6cc790972376337411e0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="1q0T3Vfy">6、愿我如星君如月，夜夜流光相皎洁。</p>
 <p data-pid="FLNnUbiU">——范成大· 《车遥遥篇》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jEos4eL0">7、我亦飘零久，十年来，深恩负尽，死生师友。</p>
 <p data-pid="8CHzxwYO">——顾贞观《金缕曲二首》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SLPLcaIO">8、已枯半树风烟古，才放一花天地香。</p>
 <p data-pid="JteYSaVf">——张道洽《梅花》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-t5S-kFv"><b>9、山中何事？松花酿酒，春水煎茶。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="QEIW4kC3">——张可久《人月圆·山中书事》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XlU-2mhj"><b>10、但远山长，云山乱，晓山青。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HLOdIBwn">——苏轼· 《行香子·过七里濑》</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bb981a0de315a47c7ad90d4800162765_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="549" data-rawheight="976" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-e67a1fccb70fad2cfd9c4121fb524dfd" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-407a6901d2b006fd19b6b793eba1cf71_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="549" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-bb981a0de315a47c7ad90d4800162765_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="xA86uu2Q">11、黄花庭院，青灯夜雨，白发秋风。</p>
 <p data-pid="D-V0D1nG">——元·张可久《人月圆·客垂虹·北曲聊乐府前集今》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zFpNh_OA">❤<b>祝点赞的小伙伴们在生活中闪闪发光</b>❤</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LGwVP5gC">12、荷花十里，清风鉴水，明月天衣。</p>
 <p data-pid="ym9sWhw5">——张可久《人月圆》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SpxG7pmG"><b>13、掬水月在手，弄花香满衣。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ICq0Ew6a">——于良史《春山夜月》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="B3R3oZlM">14、桥如虹，水如空，一叶飘然烟雨中。</p>
 <p data-pid="GnVtOHsK">——陆游《长相思》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fRKSnr0m"><b>15、晓来风，夜来雨，晚来烟。是他酿就春色，又断送流年。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ueXGRWa2">——张惠言《水调歌头·春日赋示杨生子掞》</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-692c2133bbc5d438e9b00b0140b5790a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="640" data-rawheight="1138" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-cccd95afe814e3f3023fddb474c6193c" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-5bedb88d0e7503e4422545be4304e155_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="640" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-692c2133bbc5d438e9b00b0140b5790a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="wvPXr5ar"><b>16、当时年少春衫薄。骑马倚斜桥，满楼红袖招。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="7hI-Spkw">——<span class="nolink">韦庄</span>《菩萨蛮》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="L9gd94rb">17、且将新火试新茶，诗酒趁年华。</p>
 <p data-pid="TI7Ktv8q">——苏轼《望江南·超然台作》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pV7BdSpu">18、众里寻他千百度。蓦然回首，那人却在，灯火阑珊处。</p>
 <p data-pid="wHSRqVYz">——辛弃疾《青玉案》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZBYTVmvs"><b>19、不及芙蓉，一片幽情冷处浓。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="kemo4uXY">— — 纳兰性德《采桑子》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="A1GHTR_M">20、树深时见鹿，溪午不闻钟。</p>
 <p data-pid="Kf52zdcd">——李白《访戴天山道士不遇》</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-915dd54ff6b86f1f20386836458d0e2b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="605" data-rawheight="1077" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-ed777c643a813a76867298b22d7e2c87" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-fe1b8ff553372e12f24f0b975d4f3886_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="605" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-915dd54ff6b86f1f20386836458d0e2b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="EnzWm2Nc">21、桃花浅深处，似匀桃花妆。春风助肠断，吹落白衣裳。</p>
 <p data-pid="UcBCerAi">——元稹《桃花》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JNE7nLMf">22、多少天涯未归客，尽借篱落看秋风。</p>
 <p data-pid="zVd8irfy">——唐寅 《菊花》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kkP35c_l"><b>23、秋风生渭水，落叶满长安。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="gBrmY_pZ">——唐·贾岛《忆江上吴处士》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WQFznmth">❤<b>听说点赞会让人更快乐哟，因为喜欢赞扬别人的人会收到双倍的赞扬哟</b>❤</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rIL7t8Qf">24、林花谢了春红，太匆匆。无奈朝来寒雨，晚来风。</p>
 <p data-pid="tyt8XbgS">——李煜《相见欢·林花谢了春红》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IDEtT9b_"><b>25、几时归去，作个闲人。对一张琴，一壶酒，一溪云。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="cIXehyP_">——宋·苏轼《行香子·述怀》</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-a3c5ab52b6048e80dd99446c4b35c81d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="820" data-rawheight="1459" data-size="normal" data-original-token="v2-a23fc54019cc8e9a9d903dc1458e9ccf" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-a44274516e1f87dec94510fc8a5d0ca5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="820" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-a3c5ab52b6048e80dd99446c4b35c81d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="AwP_hr5Y">26、应是天仙狂醉，乱把白云揉碎。</p>
 <p data-pid="LxpYwSKI">——李白《清平乐·画堂晨起》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AOX8j0XJ"><b>27、欲买桂花同载酒，终不似，少年游。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="batHSrrM">——刘过《唐多令·芦叶满汀洲》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="67iXe0vx">28、而今听雨僧庐下，鬓已星星也。悲欢离合总无情，一任阶前、点滴到天明。</p>
 <p data-pid="746V7XaG">——蒋捷《虞美人·听雨》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="z7O1Wycy">29、春夏园林，秋冬山谷，一心无累，四季良辰。</p>
 <p data-pid="7r69v4Hn">——陈继儒《小窗幽记·集景篇》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vTgZTTRX"><b>30、一笑相逢蓬海路，人间风月如尘土。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="D5DBqi8f">——周邦彦《蝶恋花》</p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9e750695f85e66cbafc477ba34059b9e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="567" data-rawheight="1008" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-42b76c50c30afc5fc6a5f3a71d2d205f" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-c9f1e822092b5c4c76a96afb762702df_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="567" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-9e750695f85e66cbafc477ba34059b9e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="7zX9ZZFw">31、东风随春归，发我枝上花。</p>
 <p data-pid="xTCwLeW2">——李白《落日忆山中》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="O_E6s-LM"><b>32、人生无根蒂，飘如陌上尘。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="amA8c1Gn">——陶渊明《杂诗》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="m7L2NfxH">33、久倦市尘嚣，归来听夏蝉。</p>
 <p data-pid="JOzs2_9g">— — 梅疏影 《归故山》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qCHtckFF"><b>34、长安尽头无故里，故里从此别长安。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="A9K8T-00">——《全唐诗》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vZFQponY">35、唤起一天明月，照我满怀冰雪，浩荡百川流。</p>
 <p data-pid="Bl2dh8uV">——辛弃疾《水调歌头·和马叔度游月波楼》</p>

  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-dff234cb2ef3164e08af799a6b8652fe_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="571" data-rawheight="1016" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-08e89822ac825f0ff76365d74cf92e75" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-cc39a00f3c6a4bd4af8cc2e9a78e0c6b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="571" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-dff234cb2ef3164e08af799a6b8652fe_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="_LWgbVJi">36、竹影和诗瘦，梅花入梦香。</p>
 <p data-pid="qB19eQrj">——王庭筠《绝句》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aP_j3ge5">37、一千顷，都镜净，倒碧峰。忽然浪起，掀舞一叶白头翁。</p>
 <p data-pid="wVYA3oHF">— — 苏轼《水调歌头》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wLtIlz65"><b>38、古人笑比庭中树，一日秋风一日疏。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="rgcDN0B6">— — 金农</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="EJGOhkiz">39、春日宴，绿酒一杯歌一遍。</p>
 <p data-pid="kjrNOoGD">再拜陈三愿：一愿郎君千岁，二愿妾身常健，三愿如同梁上燕。岁岁长相见。</p>
 <p data-pid="6PfA7K09">——冯延巳·《长命女》</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lxZKqzRT"><b>40、海棠未雨，梨花先雪，一半春休。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="bfhXUw3j">— — 王雱《眼儿媚·杨柳丝丝弄轻柔》</p>

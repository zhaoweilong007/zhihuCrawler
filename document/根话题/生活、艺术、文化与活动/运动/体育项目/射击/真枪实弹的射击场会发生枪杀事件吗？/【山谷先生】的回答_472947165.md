 <p data-pid="uoG5kGsk"><b>2.在美国九岁的小女孩可以玩枪吗？</b></p>
 <p data-pid="eER3nkjb">可以，而且是完全合法的。</p>
 <p data-pid="lKOgpgZm">美国射击场最低准入年龄是8周岁，需要父母的监督陪同。这些小女孩的家长都做到了。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="qY2dCJPx">
  “Assuming it was a pre-1986 machine gun and the sale was legal, then yes,” says Laura Cutilletta, senior staff attorney at the Law Center to Prevent Gun Violence. <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//smartgunlaws.org/minimum-age-to-purchase-possess-firearms-policy-summary/%23federal" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Federal law</a> prohibits children under 18 from buying guns, but they can still fire them with adult supervision.（Cohen, 2014)
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="h10I21iW">Laura Cutilletta，防止槍支暴力法律中心的高級律師说： “假设这把枪是1986年以前生产的，并且合法的进行销售，那就可以使用”。联邦法律禁止低于18岁的儿童购买枪支，但是你可以在有成人监督下用它来射击。</p>
 <p data-pid="FJkDmcvU"><b>3.为什么可以射击全自动武器？</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="JG-1D6DY">
  Under the federal <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.atf.gov/content/firearms/firearms-industry/national-firearms-act" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Firearm Owners’ Protection Act </a>of 1986, it became a crime for civilians to own machine guns, but with a huge exception: Any gun made before the law went into effect is exempt. It’s fine for civilians to resell and buy those old guns. The approval process involves a $200 transfer tax and an FBI background check. A few states have banned automatic weapons entirely, but Arizona, one of the most gun-friendly states, is not one of them.（Cohen, 2014)
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="z96WTs2S">1986年《联邦枪支所有者保护法》出台后，平民使用全自动武器是犯罪行为。但是对于在1986年以前制造及销售的武器是实施豁免的，对这一部分全自动武器的重新销售与购买都是合法的。流通的过程包含200美金的流通税以及联邦调查局的审查。有几个州是完全禁止全自动武器的，但是民风剽悍的亚利桑那州不是其中之一。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="5RVgUN09">
  It was also legal for Arizona Last Stop to own the fully automatic Uzi because it was manufactured and purchased prior to the passage of the <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Firearm_Owners_Protection_Act" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Firearm Owners Protection Act</a> in 1986 and added to the <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Firearms_Act" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">NFA</a> machine gun registry where all fully automatic civilian owned firearms are registered.(Cohen, 2014)
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="GWrMtdrj">Arizona Last Stop（出事的射击场）也是合法持有这把全自动伍兹冲锋枪的，因为它是在1986年，《枪支所有者保护法》（禁止出售新全自动武器的法律）之前生产与被购买并且加入了NFA枪械登记处（所有民用全自动武器都要在这里被登记）。</p>
 <p data-pid="VrBaJMeB"><b>4.乌兹的后坐力怎样？</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="j1Ld_S5i">
  <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//qz.com/256510/that-arizona-uzi-shooting-by-a-9-year-old-girl-is-one-of-several-hundred-accidental-shootings-that-will-happen-this-year/" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Quartz’s Gwynn Guilford</a> did the math: With the average American nine-year-old girl weighing about 60 pounds, and the average Uzi weighing seven to nine pounds, “That would be roughly equal to a 40-year-old man firing a 25-pound gun like, say, the <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hotchkiss_M1909_Benet-Mercie_machine_gun" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">Hotchkiss M1909</a> used in trench warfare in World Wars I and II—a weapon so heavy it sat on a tripod.”(Cohen, 2014)
 </blockquote>
 <p data-pid="Z-gLPoIp">Gwynn Guilford做了一个计算： 美国九岁小女孩的体重平均为60磅（约为27kg），一把乌兹冲锋枪大概为7到9磅（3到4kg），那按比例来说就像一个40岁的男人打一把25磅（12kg）的枪，大概是一把霍奇基斯 M1909那么重，前提是你还要把它拿在手上。</p>

 <p data-pid="hFai0pwP"><b>5.教练犯的一些错误</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vCwUZGH5">这完全是一起事故。</p>
 <p data-pid="YJ2hj0_4">首先，小孩子不应该玩乌兹，因为小孩不会压枪。如果没有接受过训练，没有经验的射击者（无论岁数多大）都很容易失去控制。很多评论区的大佬都给出了针对新手的射击方法：一次增加少许子弹数量，直到射击者慢慢适应全自动的感觉。以防在枪口上抬的时候出现惊慌（panic reaction）。</p>
 <p data-pid="RY9auo6F">其次，不正确的射击姿势也是这起事故的诱因。教练应该站在小女孩的正后方，然后一手抵住小女孩的后背一手控制住枪。像这样</p>

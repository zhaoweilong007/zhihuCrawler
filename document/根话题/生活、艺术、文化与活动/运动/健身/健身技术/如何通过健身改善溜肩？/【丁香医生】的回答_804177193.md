 <p data-pid="oQ_ALpMn">好看的肩颈满足三个条件：<br></p>
 <h2><b>正面修长、侧面纤薄、背面精瘦</b></h2>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a18316c3549f638e0822ebc918fd726f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="940" data-original-token="v2-a18316c3549f638e0822ebc918fd726f" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-5c9e308d780763b7c27e349de715b291_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-a18316c3549f638e0822ebc918fd726f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="s-W4ofC3">溜肩的改善，也要分三步走（出镜：健身小哥哥 <a class="member_mention" href="https://www.zhihu.com/people/8ad9dd6fb73afc77ed02c2b7128c5f74" data-hash="8ad9dd6fb73afc77ed02c2b7128c5f74" data-hovercard="p$b$8ad9dd6fb73afc77ed02c2b7128c5f74">@叔贵K</a> ）：</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="axedzcqf"><b>动作一：小鸡翅膀拉伸</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c20c5a8dcc19f720cc83a3228142418b_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="360" data-rawheight="203" data-original-token="v2-c20c5a8dcc19f720cc83a3228142418b" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c20c5a8dcc19f720cc83a3228142418b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="360">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Qv6Ok7ta">手背在身后，另一只手拽着手腕，向外拉动手臂呈现「小鸡翅膀」的样子。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aT7zyfAd">从后背来看，这个动作长这样：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-213214a6e2f681a4d715d20d9a3ac4e6_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="360" data-rawheight="203" data-original-token="v2-213214a6e2f681a4d715d20d9a3ac4e6" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-213214a6e2f681a4d715d20d9a3ac4e6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="360">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="KhHXo9iH">向翅膀方向侧头，保持 5～8 个深呼吸时间。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YhUbDpMq"><b>动作二：靠墙小鸡扑腾</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b0b2a2ab55ac69c40d7bd156c5ac9988_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="360" data-rawheight="203" data-original-token="v2-b0b2a2ab55ac69c40d7bd156c5ac9988" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-b0b2a2ab55ac69c40d7bd156c5ac9988_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="360">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9rTWI025">首先肘窝外旋，后背完全贴墙站立</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="NOjz1CZ4">保持大臂跟墙面贴合，像小鸡挥翅膀一样上下舞动</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bOTxB0Te">保持后背紧张做 8～12 次，你会感觉整个肩膀打开了很多，喘气都特别痛快</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bD8ETa1a"><b>动作三：俯身小鸡展翅</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-18b243a0fa46bcf028801dec78a8eb04_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="169" data-original-token="v2-18b243a0fa46bcf028801dec78a8eb04" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-18b243a0fa46bcf028801dec78a8eb04_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AMxliHcM">双手给自己点个赞，俯身分别完成神似，Y 字、W 字、T 字的伸展</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wGRIVt-3">整个展翅过程里，保持肩膀收紧做 8～12 次。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="b6o2Wv6o">好了，今天分享的三个动作都是通过，针对性的拉伸和力量练习，来改变肩胛骨的发力模式，非常的简单易学。</p>

- 点赞数：19015

 <p data-pid="lcKUSiGZ">Thank you for supporting me in Zhihu, there’s still something I would like to share with you:</p>
 <p data-pid="m6IeQ8n3">A kid from Congo, becoming an NBA Champion is surreal. I was not supposed to be here but I never lost faith. This is a dream come true but also an opportunity for me to remind every kid in Congo, in Africa and everywhere that anything is possible.</p>
 <p data-pid="0vdKo8Wn">感谢知友们的支持，我还想补充一点感受：</p>
 <p data-pid="b_atYUFG">一个来自刚果的孩子，获得了NBA总冠军，这简直就像做梦。我没有奢望一定要抵达这里，但我从未失去信念。而今我的梦想成真，同时也是一个机会，让我提醒那些在刚果、非洲以及世界各个角落的孩子们，一切皆有可能！</p>

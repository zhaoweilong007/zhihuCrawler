# 哪个句子曾经救赎了你 ？

- 更新时间：2022年01月21日19时09分00秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="_GqCOhsM">纽约时间比加州时间早三个小时，</p>
 <p data-pid="xgnBJvjk">New York is 3 hours ahead of California,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rNukBb3T">但加州时间并没有变慢。</p>
 <p data-pid="FBoWOZJr">but it does not make California slow.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WeYmyU2J">有人22岁就毕业了，</p>
 <p data-pid="fEqyn0NK">Someone graduated at the age of 22,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-YKb3KuU">但等了五年才找到好的工作！</p>
 <p data-pid="Q31aFPwW">but waited 5 years before securing a good job!</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7vroBmsw">有人25岁就当上CEO，</p>
 <p data-pid="5pMDgHVc">Someone became a CEO at 25,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VPmOmldV">却在50岁去世。</p>
 <p data-pid="cNw9u6kw">and died at 50.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ALRAmzOZ">也有人迟到50岁才当上CEO，</p>
 <p data-pid="jLYMpkSn">While another became a CEO at 50,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aa6KnYcq">然后活到90岁。</p>
 <p data-pid="jp2WYMGp">and lived to 90 years.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZNH9h5qc">有人依然单身，</p>
 <p data-pid="lb6nNq1T">Someone is still single,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="D9Qt3h-w">同时也有人已婚。</p>
 <p data-pid="PaE8Z9KM">while someone else got married.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="96wXdeBR">奥巴马55岁就退休，</p>
 <p data-pid="cZ8SrZaF">Obama retires at 55,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7-sZFT7F">川普70岁才开始当总统。</p>
 <p data-pid="rit1JuDu">but Trump starts at 70.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Wc1pYcG4">世上每个人本来就有自己的发展时区。</p>
 <p data-pid="l2Q6j8n1">Absolutely everyone in this world works based on their Time Zone.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="D2vPmvxe">身边有些人看似走在你前面，</p>
 <p data-pid="tnGQvocl">People around you might seem to go ahead of you,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MTpTtL5s">也有人看似走在你后面。</p>
 <p data-pid="V41VaKXY">some might seem to be behind you.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="X5MPE6CL">但其实每个人在自己的时区有自己的步程。</p>
 <p data-pid="FiK-0BHX">But everyone is running their own RACE, in their own TIME.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_p2SwJmO">不用嫉妒或嘲笑他们。</p>
 <p data-pid="J6YgKxKB">Don’t envy them or mock them.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="P-2x3T7R">他们都在自己的时区里，你也是！</p>
 <p data-pid="-9QJci-Z">They are in their TIME ZONE, and you are in yours!</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pQFkpKDa">生命就是等待正确的行动时机。</p>
 <p data-pid="8fDBx-Px">Life is about waiting for the right moment to act.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="y9PGXs5i">所以，放轻松。</p>
 <p data-pid="0_cGKFoT">So, RELAX.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1L0hSCvX">你没有落后。</p>
 <p data-pid="5QmH6ZSN">You’re not LATE.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vrACMv8I">你没有领先。</p>
 <p data-pid="smwblx09">You’re not EARLY.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q1sP2Wrr">在命运为你安排的属于自己的时区里，一切都准时。</p>
 <p data-pid="ybzIxc65">You are very much ON TIME, and in your TIME ZONE Destiny set up for you.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PWdyIY4E">——《你在自己的时区里，从没落后》</p>

# 有哪些支撑你走了很远的励志句子？
- 点赞数：29063
- 更新时间：2020年04月29日09时49分20秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/347077309/answer/885048058
<body>
 <p data-pid="ZFxC3Lgp">纽约时间比加州时间早三个小时，</p>
 <p data-pid="Lh3l7b_u">New York is 3 hours ahead of California,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ikEAb4Yg">但加州时间并没有变慢。</p>
 <p data-pid="LY9dQhPT">but it does not make California slow.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5zpcUatg">有人22岁就毕业了，</p>
 <p data-pid="9Wm579OU">Someone graduated at the age of 22,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gcekK-7b">但等了五年才找到好的工作！</p>
 <p data-pid="FAvWUss-">but waited 5 years before securing a good job!</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="NbX9DSCg">有人25岁就当上CEO，</p>
 <p data-pid="DLXZ6I2M">Someone became a CEO at 25,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IIjJFsiG">却在50岁去世。</p>
 <p data-pid="kegYNypC">and died at 50.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="KYA_sDe2">也有人迟到50岁才当上CEO，</p>
 <p data-pid="Vb2Cvdr1">While another became a CEO at 50,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rgSYVtza">然后活到90岁。</p>
 <p data-pid="Owh4GUkA">and lived to 90 years.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="veLIwAUy">有人依然单身，</p>
 <p data-pid="1eoB3_FZ">Someone is still single,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rBKJCzZp">同时也有人已婚。</p>
 <p data-pid="hjri3Bf1">while someone else got married.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LiTwQkuJ">欧巴马55岁就退休，</p>
 <p data-pid="L9KyVg_F">Obama retires at 55,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cBHDnNSs">川普70岁才开始当总统。</p>
 <p data-pid="HCve-Yr-">but Trump starts at 70.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="R36CkWxQ">世上每个人本来就有自己的发展时区。</p>
 <p data-pid="AYH2rYol">Absolutely everyone in this world works based on their Time Zone.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ubyJzXF0">身边有些人看似走在你前面，</p>
 <p data-pid="q6UgYLCW">People around you might seem to go ahead of you,</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="E-Le0QZq">也有人看似走在你后面。</p>
 <p data-pid="jqkwgsIm">some might seem to be behind you.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DyCpkeou">但其实每个人在自己的时区有自己的步程。</p>
 <p data-pid="XmmYzCdy">But everyone is running their own RACE, in their own TIME.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="aVcbrYY8">不用嫉妒或嘲笑他们。</p>
 <p data-pid="q6YTwYQx">Don’t envy them or mock them.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="QzYKp3Ft">他们都在自己的时区里，你也是！</p>
 <p data-pid="2QL-1uVm">They are in their TIME ZONE, and you are in yours!</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VbxoARNX">生命就是等待正确的行动时机。</p>
 <p data-pid="YMZ3z281">Life is about waiting for the right moment to act.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OTWr6Q3F">所以，放轻松。</p>
 <p data-pid="DBuDN_L5">So, RELAX.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_M8KSrFa">你没有落后。</p>
 <p data-pid="h6SivdqH">You’re not LATE.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Xkc-Nya7">你没有领先。</p>
 <p data-pid="Fng07g1Z">You’re not EARLY.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rTT4oNcj">在命运为你安排的属于自己的时区里，一切都准时。</p>
 <p data-pid="ndHXvwDQ">You are very much ON TIME, and in your TIME ZONE Destiny set up for you.</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dpipUfo9">——《你在自己的时区里，从没落后》</p>

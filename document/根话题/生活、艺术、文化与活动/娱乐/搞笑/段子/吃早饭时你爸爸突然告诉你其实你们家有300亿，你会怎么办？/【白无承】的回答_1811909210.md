 <p data-pid="e0JEcerd">我百度了一下，这个属于<b>夸大妄想症</b></p>
 <blockquote data-pid="BfwkHbUr">
  <b>夸大妄想（delusionofgrandeur）</b>
  <br>
  患者夸大自己的财富、地位、能力、权利等。可见于情感性精神障碍躁狂发作、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%25B2%25BE%25E7%25A5%259E%25E5%2588%2586%25E8%25A3%2582%25E7%2597%2587/33032" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">精神分裂症</a>和脑器质性精神障碍，例如麻痹性<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//baike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%2597%25B4%25E5%2591%2586/981606" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">痴呆</a>。
 </blockquote>

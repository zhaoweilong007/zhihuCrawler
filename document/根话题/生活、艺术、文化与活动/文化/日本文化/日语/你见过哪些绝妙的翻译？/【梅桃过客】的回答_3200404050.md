 <p data-pid="IsPWZHtF">主席的名句：</p>
 <p data-pid="PxHQxljd">中华儿女多奇志，不爱红装爱武装。</p>
 <p data-pid="xx5jLaO4">译为：</p>
 <p data-pid="YdK1L0_W">To face the powder, not powder the face.</p>
 <p data-pid="dG0Lm10u">powder：火药；脂粉</p>
 <p data-pid="uvg31u6D">译者是许渊冲先生</p>

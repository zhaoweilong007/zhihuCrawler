 <p data-pid="PsZTOV1Y">华盛顿学生领袖协会，英文Association of Washington Student Leaders（AWSL）是华盛顿学校校长协会（AWSP）通过其非营利组织华盛顿学校校长教育基金会的一个分支。自1956年以来，美国职业安全法学院一直在为整个华盛顿州的学生提供领导力培训。你可以在2006年庆祝其成立50周年的出版物《传承》中了解更多关于我们历史的信息。</p>
 <p data-pid="PP5hwmQg">在努力实现其目标和目标的过程中，awsl的工作以awsp的战略计划为指导。《2013-2016年战略计划》指出，“awsp将提供学生领导计划，以支持和提高所有学生的学术和社会成功。”该战略计划于2017-2019年更新，将学生领导计划重点放在四个主要战略上：</p>
 <p data-pid="7Js2vRpk">培训并授权学生领导</p>
 <p data-pid="_2FvplzL">支持成人参与学生成功</p>
 <p data-pid="elGO8QB_">为参与和授权提供资源</p>
 <p data-pid="_sy0fPMc">提升学生领导的价值</p>
 <p data-pid="m8Vgqcng">我们的节目</p>

  About Us
  <br>

  Quality Student Leadership Training Since 1956
  <br>
  <br>
  The Association of Washington Student Leaders (AWSL) is a division of the Association of Washington School Principals (AWSP) through its non-profit organization, the Washington School Principals' Education Foundation. Since 1956, AWSL has been providing leadership training to students throughout Washington state. You can read more about our history in the publication, "Living the Legacy," written in celebration of its 50th anniversary in 2006.
  <br>
  <br>
  In efforts to achieve its goals and objectives, the work of AWSL is guided by the strategic plan of AWSP. The 2013-2016 strategic plan states, "AWSP will provide student leadership programs that support and increase the academic and social success of all students." Updated for 2017-2019, the strategic plan focuses the Student Leadership program on four main strategies:
  <br>
  <br>
  Train and empower students to lead
  <br>
  Support adults engaged in student success
  <br>
  <br>
  Provide resources for engagement and empowerment
  <br>
  <br>
  Promote the value of student leadership

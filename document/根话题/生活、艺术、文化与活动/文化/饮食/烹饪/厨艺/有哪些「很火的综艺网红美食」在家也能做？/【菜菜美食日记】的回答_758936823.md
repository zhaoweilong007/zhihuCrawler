# 有哪些「很火的综艺网红美食」在家也能做？
- 点赞数：5015
- 更新时间：2020年02月05日14时36分15秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/335017878/answer/758936823
<body>
 <p data-pid="PtDb9gXk">周末在家刷《向往的生活》，发现一件有意思的事情：</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nkd0lO9n">不管是多大牌的明星，惦记的美食，也不过是些家常风味。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XHwN7ZqR">煮个面，包点饺子，炒个家常菜，你们看，回归到吃吃喝喝，大家的追求也都差不多。<br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ac2e08caea4f1ac7a3521a18f3bbfc05_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="458" data-rawheight="258" data-original-token="v2-ac2e08caea4f1ac7a3521a18f3bbfc05" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ac2e08caea4f1ac7a3521a18f3bbfc05_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="458" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ac2e08caea4f1ac7a3521a18f3bbfc05_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7J4Dv9Ht"><b>就比如说最简单的一道葱油拌面，黄磊老师在节目里就做了好多次，还推出过升级版的葱油拌面——葱油里加猪油。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YNFeh0ai">啧啧，菜菜我光靠脑补，就觉得香炸了。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3ba0038baf8d916fbc0dc5f9bf3bdf41_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="542" data-rawheight="305" data-original-token="v2-3ba0038baf8d916fbc0dc5f9bf3bdf41" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3ba0038baf8d916fbc0dc5f9bf3bdf41_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="542" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-3ba0038baf8d916fbc0dc5f9bf3bdf41_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="A455Zwuc">说起来，葱油拌面在娱乐圈的风头，一直很盛。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="e5lwQYBl">之前汪涵在《天天向上》靠它征服一群明星，最近的《向往的生活3》里，吴亦凡、陈乔恩和容祖儿都对它赞不绝口，念念不忘。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4b17bd01a09c38554a6c740d6c9df29a_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="440" data-rawheight="247" data-original-token="v2-4b17bd01a09c38554a6c740d6c9df29a" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-4b17bd01a09c38554a6c740d6c9df29a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="440" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-4b17bd01a09c38554a6c740d6c9df29a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="gLTEG-mc">这一碗面，除了征服了娱乐圈的众多明星，也是上海人最无法割舍的味道。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5frD0HL6"><b>上海人爱吃面，街头巷尾都有大大小小的面馆。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="66L-g27W">大排面、鳝糊面、辣肉面花样百出，但一碗简单的葱油拌面，才是他们心中的朱砂痣和白月光。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-b30c83e3e919ec52aa733eab90426fe8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-b30c83e3e919ec52aa733eab90426fe8" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a4b17ef2656a21ba1c5aaad58f0493f5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-b30c83e3e919ec52aa733eab90426fe8_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0zcQboGz">我在上海吃过最好吃的一碗葱油拌面，还是几年前朋友带我去吃的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="GOt_Fjn1">就在她家的街尾，铺面不大，犹记得桌子还有点小油星，做的都是街坊生意。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6bbabaa9af335712498706faf1897025_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1620" data-original-token="v2-6bbabaa9af335712498706faf1897025" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-a91e35446d0898637dd3677e60d35223_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-6bbabaa9af335712498706faf1897025_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H9L08ipU">这家店好吃，主要是它家的葱油和酱汁做得惹人爱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dWgysDJn"><b>酱汁里头加了开洋（虾米干）提鲜，是葱油拌面的讲究版本，叫葱油开洋拌面。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0bd7dc5bd5bbfc4fdf65d99ff1673a80_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="608" data-original-token="v2-0bd7dc5bd5bbfc4fdf65d99ff1673a80" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ca62068b74621d70990c2bee72310b32_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-0bd7dc5bd5bbfc4fdf65d99ff1673a80_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jhwcV08t"><b>熟客一落座，都会说要一碗“葱开”，仿佛是一句暗号，取来一份美味。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="J_QWtIFq">面一上桌，赶紧拌匀，夹上一筷子，面条里裹着几根炸酥了的香葱，里头还藏着一两颗开洋，满口鲜香。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f6076e938d1895a4ede250822c3388da_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-f6076e938d1895a4ede250822c3388da" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e02f39a1f3d516526db2245683b41a05_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-f6076e938d1895a4ede250822c3388da_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><a data-draft-node="block" data-draft-type="mcn-link-card" data-mcn-id="1208398163964542976"></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="feYQA4mz">除了在面馆吃，上海妈妈也爱在自己家熬葱油，做好了保存起来，想吃的时候随时能用上。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PY-MyMSD">不过葱油拌面和蛋炒饭一样，都属于凡间高手的角色。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bc7rkXAl"><b>看似普通平凡，但要搞定它，没掌握点诀窍还真不行。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-82660b54906ff53ce067f459decc9644_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="608" data-original-token="v2-82660b54906ff53ce067f459decc9644" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a92fade45b6611d53e5db9968e27462b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-82660b54906ff53ce067f459decc9644_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wj0AQfaW">我跟上海朋友的妈妈学来的方法，是炸香葱时要加洋葱、红葱头和香菜一起炸，引出它们的复合香气。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q2M2FR3g"><b>香葱根和香菜根的味道更浓郁，千万不能切了扔掉。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5e45428518363a997daceb43fcd9697f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-5e45428518363a997daceb43fcd9697f" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6b00fa0b942609d2b31f1294c0f762c2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5e45428518363a997daceb43fcd9697f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="i1upu0Se">对着吴亦凡发誓！不许只收藏不<b>赞同呀</b>！</p>
 <p data-pid="FTWjhJ41">（ 快10000收藏了！！）</p>
 <p data-pid="xrrS5NI5"><b>吴亦凡会不开心！菜菜也不开心的！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-02dcdca43ec9439cc3a01c45e0c293c4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="12" data-original-token="v2-02dcdca43ec9439cc3a01c45e0c293c4" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="600" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-02dcdca43ec9439cc3a01c45e0c293c4_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="J4VQbXM-">葱绿最后过油炸，可以留着和面一起吃，香香脆脆好滋味。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ccc601f47efdd08e119d34929f48dff8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="719" data-original-token="v2-ccc601f47efdd08e119d34929f48dff8" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-4dad7804ea60376262de940af5be90fd_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-ccc601f47efdd08e119d34929f48dff8_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="NFQc_31y">酱汁里呢，一定要加开洋进去，增香提鲜的效果可是一级棒。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3b3cf7b718f6d786a01bbe8acb97e095_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-3b3cf7b718f6d786a01bbe8acb97e095" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ffa55b7289e38e785272a9ccb2f33c6b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3b3cf7b718f6d786a01bbe8acb97e095_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Yv-3mUBL">做好的葱油和酱汁，有的人喜欢混一起保存，吃的时候方便些，在一个罐子里舀来拌面就成。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fmupjgNx">我的习惯是分开保存，因为酱汁含水分，保质期没葱油长，搁久了容易坏。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fiP8Olbo"><b>熬好的葱油单独封装，放冰箱冷藏可以保存一个月。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MxqtUe7X">酱汁保质期就短点，一个星期内就要吃完它。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7938a5e11ad2a71dbf9c90324459c68f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-7938a5e11ad2a71dbf9c90324459c68f" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c189d5179b4479c4eef22483c1c756dd_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-7938a5e11ad2a71dbf9c90324459c68f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oNGrV4Um">有了它，若是工作日过得忙，也不怕饿得慌。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="10Bn_C9J"><b>抽出3分钟煮个面，往里泼点葱油，再浇点酱汁，就能开吃。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jLPft-zQ">大家赶紧收下这菜谱，饿的时候能“保命”哦~</p>

 <p data-pid="_niQvOqt"><b> [ 食谱 ]</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uWqqrRsy">1.香葱、香菜冲洗干净，保留根部</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="sZk2wPcc">晾干香葱和香菜，也可用厨房纸吸干水分，防止熬煮葱油时油花飞溅</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FvzO0_XU">将香葱切成5cm长的小段，葱白葱绿分开放置</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="F-CMSSE2">香葱和香菜根部的香味非常浓郁，建议洗净后保留</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-169a7d7c29f28cdccd9bafd111c4b11b_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-169a7d7c29f28cdccd9bafd111c4b11b" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-169a7d7c29f28cdccd9bafd111c4b11b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9bXIifII">2.香菜切段、红葱头、洋葱、生姜切片</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d5cc88e0203346b3ff39848e4bb37329_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-d5cc88e0203346b3ff39848e4bb37329" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-d5cc88e0203346b3ff39848e4bb37329_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bWnhCHvu">3.锅里倒入植物油，烧至三成热，加入葱白、生姜、洋葱、红葱头，小火慢熬，中途不时搅拌，炸至香料焦黄香脆，<b>约需要12-15分钟</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hsJqahM9">葱油熬煮不能温度太高，避免一下子就把香料炸焦</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wcKFSppV">建议使用无味颜色清亮的色拉油，如葵花籽油、玉米油、芥花籽油，不建议使用味浓的大豆油、菜籽油、花生油</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7cf74c2152c8898f4947e041050d5ca5_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-7cf74c2152c8898f4947e041050d5ca5" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7cf74c2152c8898f4947e041050d5ca5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="8JMnCoY_">4.熬到葱白变黄，关火捞起所有香料，把油温降低至三成热，加入葱绿，小火升温，葱绿变黄焦脆就可关火立刻捞出</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-862de2aca9d97e31e28d90ac2e9ff9fe_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-862de2aca9d97e31e28d90ac2e9ff9fe" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-862de2aca9d97e31e28d90ac2e9ff9fe_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LQpixT5A">5.熬好的葱油晾至常温，过滤掉杂质，倒入无水密封罐保存即可</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="alnijRha">建议保留炸至微黄的小葱，用来拌面超好吃</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0579a331e4c93c147845d0146964d1f1_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-0579a331e4c93c147845d0146964d1f1" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0579a331e4c93c147845d0146964d1f1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="d4cAn4bZ">6.开洋（虾米干）洗净，提前1小时用凉水泡发</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="z-ggUSKf">泡发后沥干水分，再加入半碗清水，中火蒸10分钟或微波炉高火加热3分钟</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JdCcAaAu">蒸好的开洋捞出滤干水分，碗内蒸开洋的水保留备用</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-10db2fbc2202eeec2d45d73deee46ba4_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-10db2fbc2202eeec2d45d73deee46ba4" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-10db2fbc2202eeec2d45d73deee46ba4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HNtZltjf">7.炒香蒸好的开洋，加入30g酱油、10g老抽、10g糖、30g蒸开洋用的水，小火熬煮至糖融化</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="H89vmJVx">若不添加开洋，可加10g鲍鱼汁提鲜</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-4db12c33ed31bab86e71b18340346e08_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-4db12c33ed31bab86e71b18340346e08" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-4db12c33ed31bab86e71b18340346e08_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-d523f9e841baab58ffa635cce5273245_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-d523f9e841baab58ffa635cce5273245" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-d523f9e841baab58ffa635cce5273245_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vPFyXMQ5">8.烧一锅开水，加入面条煮至无白芯，可根据自己喜欢的面条软硬度适当调整煮的时间</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-02591d702113e1877ef4d6f0abda27eb_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-02591d702113e1877ef4d6f0abda27eb" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-02591d702113e1877ef4d6f0abda27eb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BgNLwNuf">9.煮好的面条沥干水分，加入1-2大勺葱油，1-2大勺酱汁拌匀，撒上炸好的葱绿和煎鸡蛋就可以开吃</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6f56bd8f751470f93787349ac84c6758_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-6f56bd8f751470f93787349ac84c6758" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6f56bd8f751470f93787349ac84c6758_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-3279226b8ba11bc641b9f45606d5e902_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-3279226b8ba11bc641b9f45606d5e902" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3279226b8ba11bc641b9f45606d5e902_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="T7fDHcoY">小小一碗葱油，用料很便宜，但作用可是大大的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="RejmjrDN">除了拿来拌面吃，也可以拌饭、拌饺子、拌馄饨，就没有它不能搭的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-12f1a3fc3959286050ce6df7c33ec8ab_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-12f1a3fc3959286050ce6df7c33ec8ab" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2ece451c242d8ea9c28bc7719d23b484_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-12f1a3fc3959286050ce6df7c33ec8ab_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fcopzgdh">平日里炒菜、做凉拌菜，也可以加上一勺，香味会更上一层楼。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5268f2eb1b3df9c6e86d3b8fe62f951e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="608" data-original-token="v2-5268f2eb1b3df9c6e86d3b8fe62f951e" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-20773520e9dfbf99926d63313d202980_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5268f2eb1b3df9c6e86d3b8fe62f951e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LV1I59bf">左为葱油炒毛豆，右为葱油拌素丝</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9mYirBg2">炸葱油也没啥技巧，跟着我的配方做准没错。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="z-OT0Gss">不过记得一定要开小火炸，这葱油得慢慢熬，正如这日子要悠悠过。</p>

# 如何蒸出完美的水蒸蛋?

<body>
 <p data-pid="aSRsTTn4">鸡蛋羹虽然日常，但想做得好也不容易的！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FdinPD_4"><b>要想让鸡蛋羹表面光滑如镜、内里水嫩完美，有6个关键点：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="BWkkFEuW">（视频详解，可以直接拉到下面看文字总结(。・・)ノ</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1129812197559332864" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="6大关键点，做出完美鸡蛋羹！" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-d4b85972ca1d45846092c2539c4c90f7.jpg" data-lens-id="1129812197559332864"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-d4b85972ca1d45846092c2539c4c90f7.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">6大关键点，做出完美鸡蛋羹！<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1129812197559332864</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="J2kT4weJ"><b>1、打鸡蛋的容器表面要光滑。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MEQABndW">如果选择表面粗糙的容器，就像下图右上角这样，蛋羹里面也粗糙。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-4dc1b6c1813672285fe7a2fcc857e5fd_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1920" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-026f9160c58293a52be80cf03dd16733" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-f5256124361e0151de08aaba49c06181_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1920" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-4dc1b6c1813672285fe7a2fcc857e5fd_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jH0759uA"><b>2、搅拌鸡蛋液时要适度、加一点盐。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="pliChYvz">鸡蛋液搅到颜色均匀，没有明显的蛋白、蛋黄分离的状态就可以，过度搅拌反而会使蛋液易融合一体。</p>
 <p data-pid="Js2gbluQ">盐中的电解质可以帮助鸡蛋羹快速凝固，每一口的口感都一样，而不会出现碗边的熟了，中间还是鸡蛋液的情况。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b4b7d19a1809bf6c24bc842eb5cbde0d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1920" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-96c6403750e9d063305f82a25cfc7bf3" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-4ccc3d04dff132f744d1c866a0072b86_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1920" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-b4b7d19a1809bf6c24bc842eb5cbde0d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hy5cnCXe"><b>3、鸡蛋液要加水，加温水。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="yk9Rm0tI">鸡蛋液不加水，蒸出的鸡蛋羹口感会硬到没朋友！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bb4a68131909d3de7251a6ec8dd34758_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1920" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-c7e3de37955c43fc7f3dc5b6c5874077" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-1cb9d7849acfe1de3ee31b2081b33e44_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1920" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-bb4a68131909d3de7251a6ec8dd34758_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="YSvf2-iN">要加微微有些烫手的温水，直接加凉水的话，蛋羹里面会很粗糙</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-7df48cc2c0d7235c14097829e289bde4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1920" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-4e58571055878c99b1ae3ec674d60928" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-640c9235012d82f69bb48068a18af45b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1920" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-7df48cc2c0d7235c14097829e289bde4_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AXK6lrZR"><b>4、鸡蛋液与水的比例范围在1:1到1：2之间。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="MOQc7NrC">水少口感硬，水多口感很空虚。我自己觉得1.5:1的口感最好！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a7383ddef950df81ef2e3ead63d80e97_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1920" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-e2664bb570d333949c2930199d33b947" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0a639cc5d8c497ceaae77381232b83dd_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1920" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-a7383ddef950df81ef2e3ead63d80e97_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cxYT_b-U"><b>5、鸡蛋液和水搅拌均匀后，撇去浮沫。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="9MmVWwB8">不撇就会。。逼死密集恐惧症。。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5a17f56ad1630ba90c7b81cdcc304f0e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1920" data-rawheight="1080" data-original-token="v2-fccd11a8d853b0123cd2bdb9a339f6f9" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-147b0cc1d3a4a21afff10d39a6ffaded_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1920" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-5a17f56ad1630ba90c7b81cdcc304f0e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="WnBlWS0-"><b>6、盖上保鲜膜，水开后上锅中火蒸13分钟左右。完成！</b></p>

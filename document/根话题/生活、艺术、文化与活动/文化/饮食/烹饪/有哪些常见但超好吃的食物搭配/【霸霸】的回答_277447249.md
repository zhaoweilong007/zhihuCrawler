# 有哪些常见但超好吃的食物搭配?

<body>
 <p data-pid="zXSK7FBV">丝瓜和鲜虾啊，<br>
  简直是天生一对</p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-b2aa0aebe05dfaf8ad918349a14aeba1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-b2aa0aebe05dfaf8ad918349a14aeba1" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-b2aa0aebe05dfaf8ad918349a14aeba1_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="9tw7-d5H">虾一定要鲜活</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a16e95df1f561f7c16e8bb4d0034c5e5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-a16e95df1f561f7c16e8bb4d0034c5e5" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-a16e95df1f561f7c16e8bb4d0034c5e5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="OtHt-yDW">我会把鲜虾先在冰箱急冻一会，然后剥壳，挑去虾线。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f78824a4ca97c9418329d74f1af5d02f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-f78824a4ca97c9418329d74f1af5d02f" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-f78824a4ca97c9418329d74f1af5d02f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="0w6znk6Q">虾壳不用扔掉，在锅底放一点油煎一下，变成红色即可。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-31ddcf1367bc12fe555727c4763ccfc6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-31ddcf1367bc12fe555727c4763ccfc6" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-31ddcf1367bc12fe555727c4763ccfc6_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="uIQpRvXz">汤锅烧开水，把煎过的虾壳倒进去，大火煮一会，熬汤。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-98705e07c2ca7f720d976be907606bff_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-98705e07c2ca7f720d976be907606bff" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-98705e07c2ca7f720d976be907606bff_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="d6Tn1Rs7">煮过的虾壳捞出去不用，只留虾汤。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-8da09d48186c34522f7db900402b229a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-8da09d48186c34522f7db900402b229a" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-8da09d48186c34522f7db900402b229a_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="LttyLi4v">丝瓜削皮切滚刀块下到虾汤里。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-ad2972b8ab7cd3e9de2c41a4e76c841c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-ad2972b8ab7cd3e9de2c41a4e76c841c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ad2972b8ab7cd3e9de2c41a4e76c841c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="Zzear50p">等丝瓜汤开锅了再下剥好的虾肉.<br>
  虾肉不能煮太久，太久就会失去弹性没口感啦。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d3d7bf1f6b9e78bf65a700cd3a22b2a5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-d3d7bf1f6b9e78bf65a700cd3a22b2a5" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-d3d7bf1f6b9e78bf65a700cd3a22b2a5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="S4gS5pyL">我还喜欢下一点肉片进去，肉片要前腿靠近肩胛骨附近的肉，最嫩，一定要新鲜，菜市场刚买回来的，冷冻肉会破坏口感啊。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tg0Q-OYs">还有，白胡椒粉，要狠狠的放，不要手软。<br>
  然后再根据各人口味放盐。<br><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-25afdf0e9548740e0d50217ac75a4a34_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-25afdf0e9548740e0d50217ac75a4a34" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-25afdf0e9548740e0d50217ac75a4a34_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="E_K09xBZ">虾肉和肉片下锅滚五六分钟就可以关火啦。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-7908d4ebcbaaf009d632ea8182b69b49_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1536" data-rawheight="2048" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-7908d4ebcbaaf009d632ea8182b69b49" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1536" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-7908d4ebcbaaf009d632ea8182b69b49_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="yItJZLUr">鲜虾和丝瓜的味道混在一起，加上胡椒粉的助力，鲜味十足，味道浓郁，而且低卡，适合减肥的妹子哦。<br>
  温润不霸道的鲜甜清冽，这种新鲜感是味精和鸡精不能达到的，寒冷的冬天来一碗，暖到心窝。</p>
 <p data-pid="fIpbI6YH">也可以丝瓜汆水后和虾仁混炒，加一些糖和胡椒粉，效果一样好。</p>
 <p data-pid="AV4cZ7d4">有山珍海味的口感，又没那么高的热量</p>
 <p data-pid="tYuGbNrT">天生绝配!</p>

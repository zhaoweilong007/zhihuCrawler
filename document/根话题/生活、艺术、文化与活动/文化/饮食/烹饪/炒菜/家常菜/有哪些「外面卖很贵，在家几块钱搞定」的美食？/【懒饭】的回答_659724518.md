# 有哪些「外面卖很贵，在家几块钱搞定」的美食？
- 点赞数：5396
- 更新时间：2019年05月16日17时41分45秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/318749074/answer/659724518
<body>
 <p data-pid="nMWEI3U5"><b>蛋挞！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="OdR89WPQ">如果没记错的话，味多美、肯德基这些地方，一个蛋挞能卖到7块钱</p>
 <p data-pid="jrWnAlgu">偏偏我还就爱吃，一次买一个肯定是不够的，现在想想真心疼~~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="jSOCWOeP"><b>比如说最简单最简单的蛋挞，蛋挞液只有鸡蛋和牛奶。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hUjDxWoR">用料：鸡蛋2个（2元），牛奶250ml（用最好的比如一盒特仑苏5元），白砂糖40g（0.5元），蛋挞皮12个（淘宝15块30个，大约6元），也就是约13元能做12个蛋挞。</p>
 <p data-pid="akltpF_u">而且做起来也很简单，搅拌搅拌，挞液倒入蛋挞皮，烘烤下就完成了，烘焙小白也能做得好！</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1103703988309934080" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-8b9d1fe6394ed97b92b29887d195e8c6.jpg" data-lens-id="1103703988309934080"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-8b9d1fe6394ed97b92b29887d195e8c6.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1103703988309934080</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="omP6_aO9"><b>如果你觉得不加奶油，蛋挞就没有灵魂，那试试经典的葡式蛋挞，</b>外面卖9元一个！</p>
 <p data-pid="tBsHo3NT">用料：鸡蛋2个（2元），牛奶100g（2元），细砂糖25g（0.3元），淡奶油65g（比如雀巢约4元），蛋挞皮8个（4元），所以约12元能做8个葡式蛋挞！！</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1103705231417876480" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-48405ae130621bdb72565e9a3c11f243.jpg" data-lens-id="1103705231417876480"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-48405ae130621bdb72565e9a3c11f243.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1103705231417876480</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="5eb_yqYZ"><b>如果你吃腻了这两种常见口味的蛋挞，可以尝试做蔓越莓酸奶蛋挞！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="_iCpmlwU">用料：鸡蛋2个（2元），浓稠酸奶 120g（约5元），牛奶80g（1.5元），蔓越莓 20g（2元），糖 30g（0.3元），蛋挞皮8个（4元），所以约15元就能做8个酸酸甜甜的蔓越莓蛋挞！</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1103708826448109568" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-cd47739e69546896d161e73dd23e75f2.jpg" data-lens-id="1103708826448109568"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-cd47739e69546896d161e73dd23e75f2.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1103708826448109568</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Ul0artZY"><b>如果，你想吃却没有蛋挞皮，那就翻翻冰箱，或许有饺子皮，那也完全ok！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="Po9Vszaa">用料：饺子皮6片、鸡蛋1个、糖30g、淡奶油100ml</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1103711788859772928" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-9349ba58c52584a90f1c352eeb175f32.jpg" data-lens-id="1103711788859772928"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-9349ba58c52584a90f1c352eeb175f32.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1103711788859772928</span></span></a>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DdAJlmXj"><b>如果，冰箱里饺子皮也没有，那么，吐司片也行！！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="RCaFX4GI">用料：吐司4片、鸡蛋1个、牛奶50g、淡奶油50g、糖15g、黄油适量</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1103716432205926400" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-eebbfbc75ff8f83960392cb604bd47ba.jpg" data-lens-id="1103716432205926400"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-eebbfbc75ff8f83960392cb604bd47ba.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1103716432205926400</span></span></a>

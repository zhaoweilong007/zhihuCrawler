# 你家里做的什么菜口味完胜外面餐厅的？
- 点赞数：6270
- 更新时间：2019年07月24日11时20分02秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/298094608/answer/610355513
<body>
 <p data-pid="2KhYbncj">小主自己在家做的<b>番茄鸡肉焗饭</b>吖！好吃到泪流满面~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-339299fedbf02c7f12d134ab71756cdc_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-339299fedbf02c7f12d134ab71756cdc" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-339299fedbf02c7f12d134ab71756cdc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YBZ8N-v9">平时煮多了吃不完的剩饭，就拿来做焗饭，简单几步操作，就能做出一份香气四溢的饭来~</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-368591db279fd2a42a32dc31ca10ecf7_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="380" data-rawheight="219" data-original-token="v2-368591db279fd2a42a32dc31ca10ecf7" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-368591db279fd2a42a32dc31ca10ecf7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="380">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="fZyzUzsn">温暖的芝士包裹着颗颗饱满的米粒，舀一勺还会拉丝。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5d66f041567266ee8cab15d8f735aab6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-5d66f041567266ee8cab15d8f735aab6" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5d66f041567266ee8cab15d8f735aab6_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9yvmIZZb">刚刚出炉的焗饭，芝士还在噗噗噗冒着泡泡。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7B1-w3vu">用勺子挖上一勺，浓浓的奶香与鸡肉的鲜香混搭，每一粒米饭都包裹着酸甜的酱汁。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-1f3d307bee36fba3a03343f877ced5de_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-1f3d307bee36fba3a03343f877ced5de" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-1f3d307bee36fba3a03343f877ced5de_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2h77N-tc">细细品尝，每一口都有的番茄粒，嫩滑的鸡肉，爽脆的蔬菜丁，满是充实的幸福感。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1084403733277962240" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="true" data-name="西餐厅的番茄鸡肉焗饭自己也能做" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/80/v2-e2e9c496be154396de72c84295bb2ee5_b.jpg" data-lens-id="1084403733277962240"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/80/v2-e2e9c496be154396de72c84295bb2ee5_b.jpg"><span class="content"><span class="title">西餐厅的番茄鸡肉焗饭自己也能做<span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1084403733277962240</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="u4xwv_0M">超级喜欢马苏里拉芝士的我，对焗饭爱的相当疯狂！</p>

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vroj44ou"><b>&gt; 烹饪步骤 &lt;</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ujC4IvKq"><b>1.</b> 番茄、胡萝卜、洋葱、青椒、红椒切丁。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-24ccf18de625de158784bbff9e30d4bd_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-24ccf18de625de158784bbff9e30d4bd" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-24ccf18de625de158784bbff9e30d4bd_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-015fd6a8393f73747e1d90dcdc1ee4a5_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-015fd6a8393f73747e1d90dcdc1ee4a5" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-015fd6a8393f73747e1d90dcdc1ee4a5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FKPJuRoE"><b>2. </b>鸡胸肉切小块。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-83ac628db12183d42521320a335933b4_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-83ac628db12183d42521320a335933b4" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-83ac628db12183d42521320a335933b4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="87PWRfqh"><b>3. </b>锅里下油烧热，放入洋葱翻炒出味。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-764c40e9e0952ae5b99a40228a18dab5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="608" data-original-token="v2-764c40e9e0952ae5b99a40228a18dab5" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-764c40e9e0952ae5b99a40228a18dab5_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PXTbd_Sh"><b>4. </b>下鸡胸肉。划散炒至鸡肉颜色变白。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-dd0753777d9a8aeae8497abcd490dda4_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-dd0753777d9a8aeae8497abcd490dda4" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-dd0753777d9a8aeae8497abcd490dda4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bDw4tNU2"><b>5. </b>再放入胡萝卜丁，翻炒断生。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c423261108e13d9b0c26ec1052fc2253_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-c423261108e13d9b0c26ec1052fc2253" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c423261108e13d9b0c26ec1052fc2253_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0_dqvQzm"><b>6. </b>下番茄丁，翻炒变软后，加入适量水、番茄酱和酱油，煮至胡萝卜软烂、汤汁浓稠，加适量盐调味。最后再放入青红椒翻炒片刻。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0f5b4de6136ef6fb1757a3003098d0cf_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-0f5b4de6136ef6fb1757a3003098d0cf" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0f5b4de6136ef6fb1757a3003098d0cf_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cxXWMAUa"><b>7.</b> 把饭放在烤碗里，稍微铺平，不要压实。铺上煮好的番茄鸡肉，浇上汤汁。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-eb7c96b617e515b6e56041584320fb8c_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-eb7c96b617e515b6e56041584320fb8c" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-eb7c96b617e515b6e56041584320fb8c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YpdKqIz0"><b>8. </b>再铺上一层厚厚的芝士。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-339299fedbf02c7f12d134ab71756cdc_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-339299fedbf02c7f12d134ab71756cdc" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-339299fedbf02c7f12d134ab71756cdc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="e-72iQ_r"><b>9.</b> 送进预热好的烤箱，200度烤制13-15分钟，待芝士软化即可。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-68a9ea67c9f42741a5d7f2eeb8271bef_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="400" data-rawheight="225" data-original-token="v2-68a9ea67c9f42741a5d7f2eeb8271bef" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-68a9ea67c9f42741a5d7f2eeb8271bef_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="400">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9-JOIKXc">就可以开吃啦。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-43ba322d4832596e38dbd34da58b909c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-43ba322d4832596e38dbd34da58b909c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-43ba322d4832596e38dbd34da58b909c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="iYjPx4mH">一份热乎乎的焗饭摆在面前，与爱的人在一起，热热闹闹的把它光盘了，再聊聊最近开心的事。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6x5b_uJH"><b>算了一下，做这份焗饭，前后大概只用了几十分钟就搞定了。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9onwOp8z">小主想说的是，做饭的时间是短，但吃的时间一定要长呀！因为一起吃饭，是讨论人生的最佳方式之一，也是最简单的小幸福呀 ~</p>

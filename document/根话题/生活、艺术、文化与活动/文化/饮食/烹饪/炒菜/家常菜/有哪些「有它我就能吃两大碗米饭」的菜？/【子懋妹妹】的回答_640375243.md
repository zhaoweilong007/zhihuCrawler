# 有哪些「有它我就能吃两大碗米饭」的菜？
- 点赞数：8087
- 更新时间：2019年05月07日11时38分48秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="NSHbg8pr">作为一名番茄爱好者，不知道有多少小吃货是跟我一样：</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pCZs0dvB"><b>只吃熟番茄，打死也不吃生番茄（吃了会想吐）。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="czBbKf0V">感觉上面这句话已成全文的精髓(´▽`ʃƪ)</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <hr>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kp-_J18m"><b>每当妈妈做番茄炒蛋，我就能吃三碗饭！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="btzOSzD6">但我今天不是介绍番茄炒蛋，那太简单了，今天要给“<b>番茄鱼丸汤</b>”打call~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-20b2a83dc1a8c2522fa095af77306967_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-20b2a83dc1a8c2522fa095af77306967" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3071a22957bf7d3dcfb82e38eb38ffac_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-20b2a83dc1a8c2522fa095af77306967_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AtSnd0yq">煮好的番茄汤底鲜香浓郁，空口就能喝下一大碗。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dYB-WQHF">配米饭更是酸香开胃，妥妥的下饭。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6e62fd8fcc581f1c9d5f15ebecc89ac0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-6e62fd8fcc581f1c9d5f15ebecc89ac0" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b69083970dc0cafb5804b74c5724239d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-6e62fd8fcc581f1c9d5f15ebecc89ac0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="S4b15M8G"><b>盛上一碗白米饭，浇上两勺番茄汤，哇，口水直流，拍完了没！要开吃了！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3Gt9Fj_k">其实说到番茄汤底，海底捞的番茄锅必须拥有名字。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zqLqGEpK">我们菜菜姐，每次去海底捞必点。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TEySr1fF">连喝三大碗汤底就算了，最后还要打包一盒，带回去煮面。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-91ac03e0267a4f121abf2c257447f219_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-91ac03e0267a4f121abf2c257447f219" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9b8d6d710ee877d593680e53c1186de8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-91ac03e0267a4f121abf2c257447f219_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="tJfqP-eQ"><b>子懋妹妹温馨提醒：目前已有接近1500人收藏啦！</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bcv30i7R">不过说实话，海底捞的番茄锅，其实是不太达标的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oxDsG9Ba">虽然浓郁醇厚，但是汤底里的新鲜番茄只是点缀。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="l07fERZW"><b>主要用的，还是番茄膏和高汤，所以香气不足，味道呆板。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bb8b5e5ee85a23351dec353bf410e497_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-bb8b5e5ee85a23351dec353bf410e497" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ba248c5da3848f2eca1ecb96feaf38a0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-bb8b5e5ee85a23351dec353bf410e497_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="a7GNdjDR">自己在家做番茄汤底，会先用白洋葱打成泥，炒出香味，然后加入打成泥的新鲜番茄熬煮。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="c6qGfUe-">汤要浓，需要加入一点番茄酱，但不能太多。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DkZcPmJ3">番茄的酸、洋葱的甜自然融合，带出独属于蔬菜的鲜甜，番茄酱的加入，让汤色更好看，也更浓郁。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6c85f783cbbd63fb7cf86435341f6473_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-6c85f783cbbd63fb7cf86435341f6473" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f0222d410ba93974ba55b6e73ffa412b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-6c85f783cbbd63fb7cf86435341f6473_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="EFX7Rpph">这样做好的番茄汤底，最适合煮鱼、虾、菌菇之类，自带鲜味的食物。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="v0nG0_qu">然后再自己做点圆滚滚、白胖胖的鱼丸一起滚，<b>嫌麻烦也可以去菜市场买点。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Gtn3dtoL"><b>煮一锅红彤彤的番茄汤，下入白嫩嫩的鱼丸，再加入点菌菇、魔芋，就是营养又饱腹的一餐。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4206f5e949711aebe4aa736504e75ed4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1067" data-rawheight="714" data-original-token="v2-4206f5e949711aebe4aa736504e75ed4" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-fcd38a9c52bb8fd4e231f88a1466f65f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1067" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-4206f5e949711aebe4aa736504e75ed4_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Mng4h0tH">学会了这个汤底，再也不用去海底捞排队，自己在家做就行了，不过就是比较费米饭哈哈~</p>

 <h2>#又能喝汤又能拌饭吃的番茄鱼丸汤！#</h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BFM1o-v5"><b>[ 食材 ]</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-128dbe2449d71e8e2e214eb7c6d43eb2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-128dbe2449d71e8e2e214eb7c6d43eb2" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-15b6bce6cc9f1f6a5a118f27cade73a9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-128dbe2449d71e8e2e214eb7c6d43eb2_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="QT95T6rr">番茄汤底：番茄5颗 洋葱半颗 蒜头1瓣 姜1片 番茄酱3大勺 冰糖5颗 盐1小勺 魔芋丝 鲜香菇 金针菇 香菜</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="33i5ok2i">1大勺＝1 table spoon＝15ml<br>
  1小勺＝1 tea spoon＝5ml</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="fItHkS7Z"><b>[ 食谱 ]</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZoE_K4t4"><b>鱼丸的做法比较麻烦我就不更新啦~直接做番茄鱼丸汤</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="rjNhJ89F"><b>番茄鱼丸汤</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Fe9SydN9">1.番茄底部划十字刀，浇开水烫5分钟后捞起剥皮</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ezMTZOHg">取4颗番茄切块，用搅拌机打成泥，剩余1颗切丁待用</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-fed7261651b091f4d5d7d0ad4e442073_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-fed7261651b091f4d5d7d0ad4e442073" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-fed7261651b091f4d5d7d0ad4e442073_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qCpDp9HU">2.洋葱切丁，和蒜头、姜片一起打成泥</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="c_6CUwB_">热锅下油，中火炒香洋葱泥，翻炒3分钟左右</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-cb051db155a5841667486d1239a360df_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-cb051db155a5841667486d1239a360df" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-cb051db155a5841667486d1239a360df_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1XxI_rr4">3.加入番茄泥小火翻炒3分钟，加番茄酱、冰糖和盐调味，再加入开水</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-29c36765e1c006a9784251372eec76f1_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-29c36765e1c006a9784251372eec76f1" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-29c36765e1c006a9784251372eec76f1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YYEFCHBQ">水沸后下鱼丸、番茄丁、魔芋丝、金针菇和切片鲜菇，煮5分钟，起锅撒上香菜碎即可</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-e20781c655a136fb7dac4bc0f57922c3_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-e20781c655a136fb7dac4bc0f57922c3" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-e20781c655a136fb7dac4bc0f57922c3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lNTaRY27">还没煮好，口水就快流出来了，鲜香浓郁，马上就想喝一碗。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="sXrlorlY">配米饭更是酸香开胃，妥妥的下饭。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="or7mRTTp">而且鱼丸柔嫩滑软，没有一根刺，宝宝也能放心吃呢~(虽然我也没有宝宝...)</p>

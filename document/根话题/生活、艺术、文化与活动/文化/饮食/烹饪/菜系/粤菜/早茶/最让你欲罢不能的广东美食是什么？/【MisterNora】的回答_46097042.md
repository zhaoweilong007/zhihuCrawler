 <p data-pid="OOq7BOTV"><b>5、卤水</b></p>
 <p data-pid="VQq2xywD">卤水其实单说只是一种调料！但是在广东呢这是代指一种菜肴了~</p>
 <p data-pid="YdL2ArrZ">更广泛的讲法应该是叫做卤味，潮汕卤味。</p>
 <blockquote data-pid="dLM8iz0i">
  <b><u>潮汕卤味是一道色香味俱全的汉族传统名肴，属于粤菜系潮州菜。卤味，是潮人对鹅、鸭或猪脚、猪头皮等的一种很普遍、很经常的烹饪方式，很有特色。潮人的卤鹅、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/131129.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">卤鸭</a>、卤肉类，肉中较含水份，韧烂度适口，且有卤汤可淋。其制法，就是将适量<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/281988.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">红糖</a>在锅上滴上几滴水，糖就溶化，煮至完全起泡时，加水少许，将盐、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/1465848.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">豆酱</a>（或酱油）加入，称为“打卤”。将去毛剖腹处理好的成只生鹅、生鸭或猪脚、猪头皮之类放入铁锅翻转，让其表皮粘上卤色，再加水和葱头、富清卤料、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/488049.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">南姜</a>、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/41087.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">桂皮</a></u></b><b><u>、八角、茴香、用文火炖至筷子能容易插入肉里，就可捞出来，这便是香嫩可口的卤味。</u></b>
  <br><b><u>卤汤还可放进鸭蛋或鸡蛋煮，就成为赭色的“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/204041.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">卤蛋</a>”，也可以放进<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/151329.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">豆腐干</a>，称为“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/153602.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">卤豆干</a>”，还可以放进萝卜片，称为“<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/596610.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">卤菜</a>头”。卤的方法是潮汕的特有食法，是世代相传的一种经常普遍的烹饪样式。（再次科普自度娘！）</u></b>
 </blockquote>

 <blockquote data-pid="XiWhtggf">

  <p data-pid="y1mQcl5T"><b><u>生滚粥应该是广州（包括广州附近的市镇）独有的。大致地说，就是把生的配料放到滚烫的白粥里头煮，配料一熟就可以吃了，和老火粥是相反的一路。其特点是味道鲜美。</u></b></p>
 </blockquote>

 <p data-pid="6XAMbm9c"><b><u>拉肠是一种使用<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/789271.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">米浆</a>作成的广东地区汉族传统名吃之一，又称<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/35545.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">布拉</a>肠，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/59078.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肠粉</a>。广东肠粉主要流派有两种一种是布拉肠，另一种是抽屉式肠粉。传统以碎肉、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/1328570.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">鱼片</a>、虾仁为馅。也是港式酒楼常见的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/30672.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">点心</a>，一般常见的以鲜虾<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/59078.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">肠粉</a>、<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/8744121.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">牛肉肠粉</a>和<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=http%3A//baike.baidu.com/view/8653230.htm" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">叉烧肠粉</a>为主。（度娘~）</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="_0XYpXq6">百度查到的呢是肠粉有两种，也是大家认知差异最大的两种，布拉肠和抽屉式肠粉</p>

 <h2>深夜灵魂拷问：你饿了吗？</h2>
 <p data-pid="XN-qpwvv">鱼香茄子，素烧麻婆茄子，还有今年最红的</p>
 <h2><b>鸡蛋蒜蓉烤茄子</b>！</h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="miHwtu4l">家常菜嘛，首先食材很家常，而且简单易做，本菜第一时间就想到茄子。</p>
 <p data-pid="_YcCiDnG">这是除了土豆之外，菜菜最爱吃的食材了，搭配多样，包容性强~</p>

 <p data-pid="GSShW2-I"><br><b>要说简单又好吃，首推鱼香茄子和麻婆茄子</b></p>
 <p data-pid="jD7y9tPA">要说最香的，还是鸡蛋蒜蓉烤茄子！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="n9iZSskC">说起来，鸡蛋蒜蓉烤茄子还是《人生一串》里，茄子妹儿的镇店之宝。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="o-_A3hMv">烤得软绵绵入味的茄子，下一个鸡蛋进去，烤成溏心状再拌匀，口感一下柔滑了，吃过的没人说不爱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7754b3bc66e41c037e7483ed16dd70a6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="480" data-rawheight="270" data-original-token="v2-7754b3bc66e41c037e7483ed16dd70a6" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="480" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-7754b3bc66e41c037e7483ed16dd70a6_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TAjMZPvV">光是脑补，就觉得好吃。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nMXQ59E4">鉴于让朋友们在家里也可以做出来，这次我就做了一个<b>微波炉版本</b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-7df60e54371be0f69a6dccfdf174bb5b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="720" data-original-token="v2-7df60e54371be0f69a6dccfdf174bb5b" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-7df60e54371be0f69a6dccfdf174bb5b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="MDk3Jwa4">茄子虽肉厚多汁，但味淡，所以调味尤为重要。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wSw-jvEm">先炒个快手的蒜蓉酱，蒜蓉爆香，加点酱油、蚝油调出咸鲜味。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="shN20WDL">然后就是下烧烤的<b>灵魂三宝：孜然粉、辣椒粉与胡椒粉</b>了。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="4FhjmDXZ">下了这三把粉，烧烤的滋味才能到位，分分钟香掉魂儿。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-aaf25bc277121a3933716636bdca6b52_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-aaf25bc277121a3933716636bdca6b52" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-aaf25bc277121a3933716636bdca6b52_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="AUWBK3Wp">快！要开吃了！</p>
 <p data-pid="kAy3CvC9"><b>茄子整条叮熟，再切开铺调味，下鸡蛋。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="6zED43Bm">多汁的茄子配上香辣的蒜蓉酱，筷子一夹，茄子缠上润口的溏心蛋液。</p>
 <p data-pid="HHE_fnvn">香辣甜咸，一口多种滋味，好吃到跺jio~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <p data-pid="m-nL1aTP">蒜蓉酱：大蒜1整颗 生抽2小勺 蚝油1小勺</p>
 <p data-pid="ditpU1pW">孜然粉1小勺 辣椒粉1/2小勺 黑胡椒粉1/2小勺</p>
 <p data-pid="CFQGzZf5">糖1/2小勺 盐1/2小勺 白芝麻2小勺</p>
 <p data-pid="H6DUrqkr">烤茄子：茄子1根 鸡蛋1颗 辣椒粉 孜然粉 小米椒 葱花</p>
 <p data-pid="unyVHAu7">1大勺＝1 table spoon＝15ml<br>
  1小勺＝1 tea spoon＝5ml</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <hr>
 <p data-pid="WDKx9sL2"><b>既然有小吃货求菜谱，我就更新一下吧~</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="O2KoxEFh">1.茄子放微波炉高火叮4-5分钟；大蒜去皮，切成蒜末</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-0f8ff34715412d7ecebbaa82cc552adb_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-0f8ff34715412d7ecebbaa82cc552adb" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-0f8ff34715412d7ecebbaa82cc552adb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Nl2-yP0H">2.热锅下油，炒香蒜末</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-cb2b4f62e7a58ff01603522f767b31e4_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-cb2b4f62e7a58ff01603522f767b31e4" data-thumbnail="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-cb2b4f62e7a58ff01603522f767b31e4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="3WbfiFVA">3.蒜末炒至微黄时，加入生抽、蚝油翻炒至上色均匀；再加入孜然粉、辣椒粉、黑胡椒粉、糖、盐和白芝麻，炒匀后关火</p>
 <p data-pid="NreEbW_8">重滋味的小伙伴可以撒多点香料粉，但撒了粉类调料后就不要炒制过久了，避免发苦。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a44ce8445151114e082101681bb67e2e_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-a44ce8445151114e082101681bb67e2e" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-a44ce8445151114e082101681bb67e2e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="CCS-lIwg">4.叮熟的茄子切开铺平，在茄肉上横切两三刀，不要切破茄子片，方便入味及夹起来吃</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5151909ee0d785abd4a48b0d24fb8650_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-5151909ee0d785abd4a48b0d24fb8650" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5151909ee0d785abd4a48b0d24fb8650_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qc940pBS">5.茄子上均匀铺上蒜蓉酱，放入微波炉叮1-2分钟</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-1f3f0eae9c5fcbd811280d85c3f62cdc_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-1f3f0eae9c5fcbd811280d85c3f62cdc" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-1f3f0eae9c5fcbd811280d85c3f62cdc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="W--EWbrN">6.取出茄子，在中间打入一个鸡蛋，再放回微波炉叮1-2分钟，呈溏心蛋状态即可</p>
 <p data-pid="vjprVzke">若担心鸡蛋未全熟有菌，可使用可生食的无菌鸡蛋来做这道菜</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-306b182dbd2c8f7c988e37dfac0d6148_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-306b182dbd2c8f7c988e37dfac0d6148" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-306b182dbd2c8f7c988e37dfac0d6148_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="PtAVSCuO">7.根据口味撒上辣椒粉、孜然粉、小米椒圈和葱花</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9ad4f8104c74b37047ea6a795af162d6_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-9ad4f8104c74b37047ea6a795af162d6" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-9ad4f8104c74b37047ea6a795af162d6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="D3BCKhwY">吃之前先用筷子将溏心蛋黄戳破，蛋液渗入茄肉，相互交融。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-80bb3c3d369dd29bec08fd74a38db89f_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="197" data-original-token="v2-80bb3c3d369dd29bec08fd74a38db89f" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-80bb3c3d369dd29bec08fd74a38db89f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="SytPYXVc">蛋液被茄子的热度稍稍烫熟，这时的口感恰到好处。<br></p>
 <p data-pid="pAvGo3U4">茄子肉厚汁美，鸡蛋的加入让茄肉吃起来更润口鲜美。<br></p>
 <p data-pid="8f6SgnEi">真是一道肉感满满的家常菜，一吃就停不下口。</p>

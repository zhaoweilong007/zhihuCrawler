 <p>广西桂林甑皮岩<span><span>、</span></span>河北徐水南庄头遗址<span><span>，</span></span>是目前发现最早的中国人驯化猪类的证明<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>中国人花了 9000 多年<span><span>，</span></span>硬生生干到了全球猪肉年产量全球第一<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>2017 年<span><span>，</span></span>全球猪肉产量为 11103.4 万吨<span><span>，</span></span>单是中国<span><span>，</span></span>就占了 5544.9 万吨<span><span>，</span></span>约等于全球一半<span><span>。</span></span>然而<span><span>，</span></span>那年又进口了 119 万吨猪肉——这才勉强够吃<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但与此同时<span><span>，</span></span>又有一个骇人的事件<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>中国本土的猪类<span><span>，</span></span>其实快要灭绝了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这一点<span><span>，</span></span>要从正宗回锅肉的灭绝说起<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>川菜大师彭子渝先生说<span><span>，</span></span>从前炒回锅肉<span><span>，</span></span>用的都是成华猪<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这是一种生长在成都平原的土猪<span><span>，</span></span>黑不溜秋<span><span>、</span></span>肥头大耳<span><span>，</span></span>看上去没什么过猪之处<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>然而<span><span>，</span></span>在文学大师李劼人先生的名著<span><span>《</span></span>死水微澜<span><span>》</span></span>里<span><span>，</span></span>成华猪的肉味<span><span>，</span></span>是这样的<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><span><span>「</span></span>它的肉<span><span>，</span></span>比任何地方的猪肉都要来得嫩些<span><span>，</span></span>香些<span><span>，</span></span>脆些<span><span>，</span></span>假如你将它白煮到刚好<span><span>，</span></span>切成薄片<span><span>，</span></span>少蘸一点白酱油<span><span>，</span></span>放入口中细嚼<span><span>，</span></span>你就察得出它带有一种胡桃仁的滋味<span><span>，</span></span>因此<span><span>，</span></span>你才懂得成都的白片肉何以是独步<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p>肥润脆嫩的成华猪<span><span>，</span></span>是炒回锅肉的最佳原料<span><span>，</span></span>没有之一<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>回锅肉<span><span>，</span></span>顾名思义要两次下锅<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>猪臀肉煮到八成熟<span><span>，</span></span>切成薄片再下锅<span><span>，</span></span>葱段<span><span>、</span></span>蒜苗<span><span>、</span></span>永川豆豉<span><span>、</span></span>郫县豆瓣注入灵魂<span><span>，</span></span>烈火爆炒<span><span>，</span></span>香辣扑鼻<span><span>，</span></span>直把肉片熬出油来<span><span>，</span></span>微微蜷曲成<span><span>「</span></span>灯盏窝<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>窝里能盛一盏油<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>传说<span><span>，</span></span>只有皮薄肉厚<span><span>、</span></span>脂肪丰富的成华猪后臀<span><span>，</span></span>炒出来才不柴不腻<span><span>，</span></span>蜷曲的肉片挂在筷头<span><span>，</span></span>微微颤抖<span><span>，</span></span>油光闪烁<span><span>，</span></span>是肉最本真的诱惑<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>然而这种回锅肉<span><span>，</span></span>如今越来越难吃到了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>随着养殖数量逐年减少<span><span>，</span></span>成华猪到了濒临灭绝的时刻<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>2010 年 10 月 11 日<span><span>，</span></span>成都市专门设立的成华猪保种场里<span><span>，</span></span>共有 100 头成华猪<span><span>。</span></span>同一年<span><span>，</span></span>中国野生大熊猫的种群数量<span><span>，</span></span>是 1864 只<span><span>。</span></span>如果世上再无成华猪<span><span>，</span></span>真正的回锅肉<span><span>，</span></span>也就不复存在<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>一切都因为 20 世纪下半叶<span><span>，</span></span>中国的猪<span><span>，</span></span>经历了一次史诗级偷梁换柱<span><span>。</span></span>其变化之彻底<span><span>，</span></span>让今天的中国人<span><span>，</span></span>几乎毫无察觉<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>去问一个年轻人<span><span>，</span></span>猪是什么颜色<span><span>？</span></span>对方十有八九回答<span><span>：</span></span>白色<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>猪的古称<span><span>，</span></span>有<span><span>「</span></span>乌金<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>黑面郎<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>乌将军<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>又有<span><span>「</span></span>乌鸦站在猪身上——谁也别嫌谁黑<span><span>」</span></span>之类俗语<span><span>，</span></span>说明从前<span><span>，</span></span>在中国人心目当中<span><span>，</span></span>黑色才是猪的主流<span><span>。</span></span></p>

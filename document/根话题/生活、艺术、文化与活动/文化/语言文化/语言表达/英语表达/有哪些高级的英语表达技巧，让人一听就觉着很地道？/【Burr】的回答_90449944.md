 <p data-pid="zsiByMQP">我先对这个答案的适用对象，用法等做一个简单解释。心急的同学可以直接跳过这部分！</p>
 <p data-pid="o-TZzeoE">0.1 <b>这个答案适用的人群：</b>雅思/托福备考，单纯想要提高英语口语/写作，中英口译/笔译交流学习，商务/工作英语，日常交友/聊天。</p>
 <p data-pid="duSl2BP5">0.2 <b>用法</b>：除特别指明外，这个答案中绝大部分的用法适用于任何英语国家。<br></p>
 <p data-pid="aSNGMY0P">1.口语<br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q8GisycU">1.1 口语高频句式<br></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="2VEavdRr">I'm having a bit of a problem/trouble/issue with xxxxx<br></li>
  <li data-pid="9c0FsiDN">I'm glad/sad/disappointed/happy/excited/relieved that<br></li>
  <li data-pid="Z7uCsx1J">I haven’t had time/a chance to<br></li>
  <li data-pid="QTil9pmH">I think/suppose/reckon/believe<br></li>
  <li data-pid="yzA8n4r1">If I’m not wrong/If my memory serves me right/If I remember correctly<br></li>
  <li data-pid="30YlQfR-">I hope you don’t mind but xxxxx<br></li>
  <li data-pid="8gEMe-sK">It’s been a wonderful week/day<br></li>
  <li data-pid="4gbGT_T0">It’s an amazing/fabulous feeling to be able to xxx</li>
 </ul>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZgtMIFQL">1.2 日常生活<u><b>必备</b></u>礼貌语<br></p>
 <p data-pid="7rJsx6OF">请求别人帮忙／询问事情（特别是不很熟悉的人）不要再用please了，换成下面的说法吧。<br></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="LLSfRk18"><b>Just wondering</b> if I could have a look at your assignment? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="2Lq6jyMi"><b>I was wondering</b> if you could give me your office hours? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="yUPRuD8O"><b>I just wanted to ask</b> whether you’ve seen my message? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="vH0hlPTt"><b>Is it cool/alright/ok</b> if I skip the lesson tomorrow? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="J3tJ4WjG"><b>Would it be possible</b> to apply for an extension given the circumstances? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="qu9a2VLd"><b>Do you mind</b> sending me your C++ code tonight? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="NAn4pWZF"><b>Would you like to</b> come to my birthday party? <br></li>
  <li data-pid="PI9WravL"><b>Will you be going to</b> the finance meeting tomorrow <b>by any chance</b>?<br></li>
  <li data-pid="gRKRcj-I"><b>If you could</b> help me out with xxx, <b>that would be</b> awesome/fantastic. <br></li>
 </ul>

 <p data-pid="bQFtMv2X">1.7 正式场合英语（用于演讲，presentation，职场英语等）</p>
 <p data-pid="aK8R-C90">＊上文1.2中提到的礼貌用法均适用于正式场合</p>
 <p data-pid="xYlJGPiA"><b>1.7.1开头概述</b></p>
 <p data-pid="ztN_ZOPP">"My presentation is in three parts."</p>
 <p data-pid="LwwFTspi">"My presentation is divided into three main sections."<br>
  "Firstly, secondly, thirdly, finally…"<br>
  "I'm going to…<br>
  take a look at…<br>
  talk about…<br>
  examine…<br>
  tell you something about the background…<br>
  give you some facts and figures…<br>
  fill you in on the history of…<br>
  concentrate on…<br>
  limit myself to the question of…</p>
 <p data-pid="vYEjSCaQ">"Please feel free to interrupt me if you have questions."<br>
  "There will be time for questions at the end of the presentation."<br>
  "I'd be grateful if you could ask your questions after the presentation."</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="k5ejix8T"><b>1.7.2 呼应前文</b></p>
 <p data-pid="xGnBnlYD">"As I said at the beginning…"<br>
  "This, of course, will help you (to achieve the 20% increase)."<br>
  "As you remember, we are concerned with…"<br>
  "This ties in with my original statement…"<br>
  "This relates directly to the question I put to you before…"</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="l8X-YD9p"><b>1.7.3 引导听众</b></p>
 <p data-pid="bEyGweaU">"I'd now like to move on to…"<br></p>
 <p data-pid="4CykS9RO">"I'd like to turn to…"<br>
  "That's all I have to say about…"<br>
  "Now I'd like to look at…"<br>
  "This leads me to my next point…"</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VxrBopaI"><b>1.7.4 运用图表</b></p>
 <p data-pid="vUZG4UqB">"This graph shows you…"<br>
  "Take a look at this…"<br>
  "If you look at this, you will see…"<br>
  "I'd like you to look at this…"<br>
  "This chart illustrates the figures…"<br>
  "This graph gives you a break down of…"</p>
 <p data-pid="QFujGCOp">"As you can see…"<br></p>
 <p data-pid="L6AsrksI">"This clearly shows …"<br>
  "From this, we can understand how / why…"<br>
  "This area of the chart is interesting…"</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Ajkpdkoc"><b>1.7.5 总结陈词</b></p>
 <p data-pid="0IIBm-n2">"That brings me to the end of my presentation. I've talked about…"<br>
  "Well, that's about it for now. We've covered…"<br>
  "So, that was our marketing strategy. In brief, we…"<br>
  "To summarise, I…"</p>
 <p data-pid="ewklUpzN">"So I hope that you're a little clearer on how we can achieve xxx"<br></p>
 <p data-pid="Qeaz8vvW">"To return to the original question, we can achieve…"<br>
  "So just to round the talk off, I want to go back to the beginning when I asked you…"<br>
  "I hope that my presentation today will help you with what I said at the beginning…"</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_qXpBl31"><b>1.7.6 回答问题</b></p>
 <p data-pid="C0JcOtaH">"Thank you for listening – and now if there are any questions, I would be pleased to answer them."<br></p>
 <p data-pid="DwqjsKGR">"That brings me to the end of my presentation. Thank you for your attention. I'd be glad to answer any questions you might have."</p>
 <p data-pid="AI6JPe43">"Thank you. So you would like further clarification on our strategy?"</p>
 <p data-pid="efoEuC1y">"Does this answer your question?"<br>
  "Do you follow what I am saying?"<br>
  "I hope this explains the situation for you."<br>
  "I hope this was what you wanted to hear!"</p>
 <p data-pid="xpsj2G2Q"><b><u>不知道如何回答的话：</u></b></p>
 <p data-pid="LTscNvZV">"That's an interesting question. I don't actually know off the top of my head, but I'll try to get back to you later with an answer."<br>
  "I'm afraid I'm unable to answer that at the moment. Perhaps I can get back to you later."<br>
  "Good question. I really don't know! What do you think?"<br>
  "That's a very good question. However, we don't have any figures on that, so I can't give you an accurate answer."<br>
  "Unfortunately, I'm not the best person to answer that."</p>

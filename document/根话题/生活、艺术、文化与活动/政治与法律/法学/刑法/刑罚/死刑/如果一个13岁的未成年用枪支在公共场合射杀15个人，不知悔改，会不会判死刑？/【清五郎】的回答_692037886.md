 <p data-pid="3wHw_rh4">“蓝极速”网吧纵火案</p>
 <p data-pid="0IVr11nj">2002年6月16日凌晨2时40分许，北京市海淀区<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//wapbaike.baidu.com/item/%25E5%25AD%25A6%25E9%2599%25A2%25E8%25B7%25AF" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">学院路</a>20号院内发生的一起恶意报复<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//wapbaike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%25BA%25B5%25E7%2581%25AB" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">纵火</a>事件，致使25人死亡、12人不同程度受伤。</p>
 <p data-pid="qHo556X3">根据<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//wapbaike.baidu.com/item/%25E5%2585%25AC%25E5%25AE%2589%25E6%259C%25BA%25E5%2585%25B3/117286" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">公安机关</a>的调查，4名纵火者均为未成年人，4名纵火者均是北京的中学生13岁至17岁，</p>
 <p data-pid="0e6sPYOA">刘某某（男，14岁）</p>
 <p data-pid="_LfLcu5R">宋某某（男，14岁）</p>
 <p data-pid="AJuRZKuO">张某某（女，17岁）</p>
 <p data-pid="UCeVSMSp">张某（男，13岁）</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="HtYGeKGj">因与<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//wapbaike.baidu.com/item/%25E7%25BD%2591%25E5%2590%25A7/267747" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">网吧</a>服务员起纠纷而进行报复。</p>
 <p data-pid="PqXKkktq">在纵火者中，两名被判处<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//wapbaike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%2597%25A0%25E6%259C%259F%25E5%25BE%2592%25E5%2588%2591/1388771" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">无期徒刑</a>，一名被判处<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//wapbaike.baidu.com/item/%25E6%259C%2589%25E6%259C%259F%25E5%25BE%2592%25E5%2588%2591/1388509" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">有期徒刑</a>12年，一名因不满14岁而免于刑事责任。在2004年，两名被判无期的犯人被减刑。</p>
 <p data-pid="zLANFb7w">在2017年，最后一名服刑者出狱。</p>

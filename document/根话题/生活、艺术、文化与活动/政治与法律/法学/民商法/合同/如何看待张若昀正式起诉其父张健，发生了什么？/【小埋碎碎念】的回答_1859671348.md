# 如何看待张若昀正式起诉其父张健，发生了什么？

- 更新时间：2023年09月19日17时52分23秒

<body>
 <p data-pid="azoGrg-e">朋友们！大瓜来了！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="dZokPlGq">张若昀，把他亲爹告了！<br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f264ae65d607c9152aba4a6168bbd5ac_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="558" data-rawheight="221" data-original-token="v2-d41d8470a41fc30740df9bfb6326172e" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-73be537ce5c2a326c396216533c10730_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="558" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-f264ae65d607c9152aba4a6168bbd5ac_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uBwCfE4I"><br>
  去年媒体曝光的<b>“张若昀涉1.4亿财务纠纷”</b>的事，大家还记得吧？</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="LABbMyO9">按当时的说法，就是张若昀他爹用1.4亿的价格把张若昀几年的档期和戏约打包卖给了华策。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="f7AG-umt">结果张若昀他爹收了钱，但其实根本没取得儿子同意，导致没法履约，后来华策只能找张家父子还钱，结果张若昀他爹拿完钱就把锅都甩给了儿子。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-60093ee3cc28cf011e2ea3137213b825_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="255" data-rawheight="255" data-original-token="v2-d088ed1508802f2bc1da349f03aa3d36" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-a4ef06da45f3d2400d70dd7bdb567499_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="255">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wdjam5hi">当时在媒体曝光这事后，张若昀也很强硬的回应过，“没签过约，未曾收钱。”</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0a9d3ae79b91842bcd0a6a2e9008f995_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="440" data-rawheight="222" data-original-token="v2-b308680498550bbf59ffc9d0ab03845d" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2c4e2060521e025e32d0ffb8b6af2952_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="440" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-0a9d3ae79b91842bcd0a6a2e9008f995_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="VnAIqmDq">然鹅即使张若昀说了自己没收钱，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="I_2GxedZ">也拦不住被强制执行的脚步</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-2f97da7b7aa890a9861d6818936c4a00_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="558" data-rawheight="281" data-original-token="v2-1b67dd6319da11c021ce368832e6fd94" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-4c25bc9315433b93d3ca066075159611_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="558" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-2f97da7b7aa890a9861d6818936c4a00_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="boXgNp9L">而之后的故事大家也知道了，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Ro_AM7nl">莫名其妙的背了快一个郑爽的债务，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="i4t06bjB">张若昀只好提交了司法鉴定书，证明《合作协议》上其签名系伪造。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c2cfa299fd1fc10e2e94deab0ebdae07_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="557" data-rawheight="139" data-original-token="v2-cdefeee74a85c246119f27461406d99d" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-a81a279f13b0ff7d4a664e440346b7ed_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="557" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-c2cfa299fd1fc10e2e94deab0ebdae07_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="kxJGJLG1">懂了吧？</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="nA1d6BLn">老子伪造了儿子的签名把对方卖了，自己卷了钱现在让儿子还债，你说说，这都什么事儿。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="vMOuBx64">其实说到张家父子冻成冰一样的关系，相信不少吃瓜群众都有所耳闻。<br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_Kcg76xF">比如《庆余年》播出的时候，张若昀曾在凌晨发了一条微博，引发了不少讨论。<br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-37bcd3d5c4a296929d88dcbc5841f367_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="623" data-rawheight="424" data-original-token="v2-51f829cdb0cb985fac68d26800d451e7" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-ddd4c9cd4c223266e844a85e83685bc3_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="623" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-37bcd3d5c4a296929d88dcbc5841f367_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-u2qggaF">虽然当时有粉丝表示，这段文字只是剧中台词，大家不要多想，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Q2gJSpz-">但对于大多数了解张若昀一家八卦的网友而言，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="1CSYwY_S">他们却更相信，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cc-3SBEb"><b>他其实更可能是在diss自己的父亲张健</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-c541755a7bf5df2bb3b256945083e68d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="595" data-rawheight="421" data-original-token="v2-799b5571437f857ce8f11bdb58ebe0da" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d52bb24e3919dcbe3a1c9a0866424bdc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="595" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-c541755a7bf5df2bb3b256945083e68d_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="T4Pqzplr">而之后，有知情人出来爆料，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="oX5OA8wJ">称张若昀之所以情绪失控，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="0GDeGxcJ">其实是因为他爸又双叒叕给他换后妈了。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Rvn936fn">而且<b>这次的后妈，</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="IxAr7YpJ"><b>不仅年龄比张若昀都要小，甚至孩子也已经偷偷生了（</b>甚至还有爆料说后妈还是张若昀的同届同学<b>）</b>——</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-26e56240d80baddca0c3b7ffd378564f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="801" data-rawheight="219" data-original-token="v2-c93fc2bf6e17132b6b6a9e41f8c7b68f" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-fc621d507e8fc5084d17c9c82c0015e5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="801" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-26e56240d80baddca0c3b7ffd378564f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="17giacdZ">虽然说老年人有追求自己幸福的权利，但儿子才刚结婚半年，自己就找了一个比儿子儿媳年纪还小的老婆......</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Gs0D-VK2">这画风，怎么看都不太对劲的模样。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f5a7317614ecb4fd9333cbccc09bc6a8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="250" data-rawheight="235" data-original-token="v2-4ea6ee5fed809a893ad83fe06fdaf168" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c28e31150ce70a0c119f473d41cf6ef9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="250">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="7JXvzE_h">而之后，有人扒出，<b>张若昀和他爸张健，早些年关系一直都不咋地。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Bm3Bh_KZ">他曾在节目中自曝，因为爸妈离婚</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="bXwhDgTJ">所以，自己一直都是跟着爷爷奶奶长大的</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-26d952d7db7d6e308a776c0622337006_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="558" data-original-token="v2-5be72eb9697c5f164c0c6a86faa77665" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-da7d1c5fdd5eb8734f062429169eeda1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-26d952d7db7d6e308a776c0622337006_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ERcsgHl5">虽然爷爷奶奶对他很好，但张若昀的父母却一直都是缺位的状态——<br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0fa79c1b839de725ab4791a012ec59f9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1000" data-rawheight="842" data-original-token="v2-59caf2036a434ccca73dad2420c8b6d8" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4336ddf4188c119849efba445674aac8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1000" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-0fa79c1b839de725ab4791a012ec59f9_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wqu9Yef_">根据采访中张若昀自己的说法，他出生没几天，爸爸就和妈妈离婚了，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="zZeXk4DG">之后，自己就被留在了爷爷奶奶身边。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lFooF2bY">虽然三四岁的时候，妈妈曾想办法带他一起出国，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZBGbyFoY">结果因为奶奶不同意，甚至还连夜带着各路亲戚把他抢回去。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="XtNE8RmL">所以之后，亲妈也不怎么回来了——</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-4b8cd61796377638b1843f8e365ea90b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="980" data-rawheight="1529" data-original-token="v2-696b4bd6b926e19ba6c76f1e3c2c379e" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e173bf08cf94b3d8bebccca536f12c9f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="980" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-4b8cd61796377638b1843f8e365ea90b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pK4cXcJ-">打小离开妈妈，而他爸对他也并不感冒，<b>基本上一年到头也不会回家看他。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="NFkF_BHF">在这种环境下，张若昀的性格自然也变得比较孤僻，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ItLhHm7k">直到高中才好一点——</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-8d33a1193695a3d578e9f755ace276ca_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="815" data-rawheight="926" data-original-token="v2-e9019171c0e8caf53fbc29d86229346c" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b700f262628f38dc7b0f4ff6323ea9e0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="815" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-8d33a1193695a3d578e9f755ace276ca_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="FbaMf-W6">儿子被扔在老人那里不需要管，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="a5rzDvVK">离了婚之后，</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YfWk-8KV"><b>张健也像入了水的鱼，投了林的鸟，日子过的那叫一个滋润。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-5bda45b8c145295a0fe7b6f31228571b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="615" data-rawheight="712" data-original-token="v2-559da3780797613a2cb9f189c4af06fa" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-73e0d2e4d4bd12f0b9459730b5fc95e6_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="615" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5bda45b8c145295a0fe7b6f31228571b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="r0bBnHga">这里简单介绍一下张健，早年做生意发的家，之后抓住机会，改行投资影视剧。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DXFh103t">曾经出品过《雪豹》《黑狐》《霍去病》等多部电视剧，在圈内人脉、声望都相当不错。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="JndKkGka"><b>有钱有权有才（？）还单身，张健自然也不会让自己孤单太久。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OAYtzwAu">1993年，也就是张若昀5岁的时候，张健便给张若昀找了个后妈，女演员刘蓓。<br></p>

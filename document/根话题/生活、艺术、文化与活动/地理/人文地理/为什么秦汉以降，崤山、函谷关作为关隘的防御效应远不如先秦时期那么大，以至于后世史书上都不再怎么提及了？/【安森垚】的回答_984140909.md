 <p>总之<span><span>，</span></span>时间一直走到商代<span><span>，</span></span>虽然政治经济中心多设立于中原地区<span><span>，</span></span>但渭河谷地一直在闷声发大财<span><span>。</span></span>尤其到了商后期<span><span>，</span></span>来自渭河<span><span>、</span></span>泾河上游的姜<span><span>、</span></span>姬两部落开始结盟<span><span>，</span></span>商王越来越难控制这一区域<span><span>，</span></span>最终在末代王帝辛<span><span>，</span></span>也就是所谓<span><span>「</span></span>纣王<span><span>」</span></span>时代<span><span>，</span></span>被周武王爆了<span><span>，</span></span>开启了 800 年的周天子时代<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但是当时的周朝<span><span>，</span></span>虽然把首都定在了自己老家渭河谷地的<span><span>「</span></span>镐京<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>今西安西南<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>号称<span><span>「</span></span>宗周<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>却也在中原安插了一个楔子作为陪都<span><span>，</span></span>也就是洛邑<span><span>（</span></span>今洛阳<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>号称<span><span>「</span></span>成周<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>也就是说<span><span>，</span></span>自此中国便开始了西安<span><span>、</span></span>洛阳两都轮番上场的滥觞<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>而在治理时<span><span>，</span></span>周武王又让两个弟弟以<span><span>「</span></span>陕塬<span><span>」</span></span>为界<span><span>，</span></span>命周公旦治理<span><span>「</span></span>陕东<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>召公奭治理<span><span>「</span></span>陕西<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>渭河谷地<span><span>「</span></span>陕西<span><span>」</span></span>之名<span><span>，</span></span>大抵自此而起了<span><span>。</span></span></p>

# 怎么看待在烈士陵园门前嬉笑打闹放风筝的行为？
- 点赞数：19577
- 更新时间：2020年05月23日02时25分43秒
- 回答url：https://www.zhihu.com/question/386917670/answer/1239282832
<body>
 <p data-pid="xpGQsDrA">Do not stand at my grave and weep</p>
 <p data-pid="qKSQiuTA">请不要站在我的墓前哭泣</p>
 <p data-pid="rRDXz069">I am not there,I do not sleep</p>
 <p data-pid="8vpSE1ja">我不在那里，我并没有睡去</p>
 <p data-pid="xeasp3BY">I am a thousand winds that blow</p>
 <p data-pid="DWg-lQgc">我是激扬起的风，千丝万缕</p>
 <p data-pid="0Ku6D-VB">I am the diamond glints on snow</p>
 <p data-pid="zedMz0Uo">我是雪地里的钻石，熠熠生辉</p>
 <p data-pid="zBKpudR8">I am the sunlight on ripened grain</p>
 <p data-pid="fBx2wJFC">我是<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E6%25B8%25A9%25E6%259A%2596%25E7%259A%2584%25E9%2598%25B3%25E5%2585%2589%26tn%3DSE_PcZhidaonwhc_ngpagmjz%26rsv_dl%3Dgh_pc_zhidao" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">温暖的阳光</a>，亲近着稻谷</p>
 <p data-pid="kY9upeYq">I amthe gentle autumn's rain</p>
 <p data-pid="NlOAgVZT">我是秋季里的细雨，轻轻柔柔</p>
 <p data-pid="Ey9Oj6RZ">When yu awaken in the morning hush</p>
 <p data-pid="YamupLTv">当你清醒于早晨的安宁</p>
 <p data-pid="y_ZE1OZz">I am the swift up liftght rush</p>
 <p data-pid="r3HHIONZ">我奔放着飞升的激情</p>
 <p data-pid="ymWWnV9B">Of quiet birds in circled flight</p>
 <p data-pid="JkA41d_-">在鸟儿幽静的盘旋种</p>
 <p data-pid="7HIanJ8h">I am the soft stars that shine at night</p>
 <p data-pid="uEl9PGzA">我有是释放温柔的<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.baidu.com/s%3Fwd%3D%25E6%259C%2597%25E6%259C%2597%26tn%3DSE_PcZhidaonwhc_ngpagmjz%26rsv_dl%3Dgh_pc_zhidao" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">朗朗</a>群星</p>
 <p data-pid="AVjSwbkw">Do not stand at my grave and cry</p>
 <p data-pid="3kMpUaHc">请不要在我的墓前悲泣，</p>
 <p data-pid="fWYPLMHv">I am not there,I did not die</p>
 <p data-pid="YE2HYBi8">我不在那里，我并没有离去。</p>

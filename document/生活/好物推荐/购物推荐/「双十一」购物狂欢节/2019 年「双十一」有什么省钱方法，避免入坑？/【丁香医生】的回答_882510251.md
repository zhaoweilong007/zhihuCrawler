<body>
 <p data-pid="M0LiwR9M">下周一就是「双十一」了，相信很多女性知友，或者男性知友的女朋友，都想趁机入手一些<b>护肤、美妆、美发、减肥等等「变美类产品」。</b></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="eTIsgSCD">但是，有些东西，可能本来不需要，被宣传广告一忽悠，就想买了；还有些东西，可能根本没有用，买了等于浪费钱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="EW_HUNxo">为了让知友们在双十一理性购物，避免入坑。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <h2><b>我联合了 15 位医生，写了整整 60 句大实话，</b>让你变美不花冤枉钱。</h2>
 <h2><b>准备好了吗，我要开始了</b></h2>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ZP88bhgl"><b>1. </b>掉头发是正常的，相信自己的头发吧，每天<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657266526%26idx%3D5%26sn%3Da6071e01b671dd8c29ed4f0814aa70d4%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1571392881%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">掉 100 根</a></b>以内不会秃！</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="t5Y_G77D"><b>2. <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657258401%26idx%3D3%26sn%3D9aefdd65d28abb71495639c6bcb44be1%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">每天洗头</a></b>不会导致脱发，<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657265476%26idx%3D5%26sn%3Dcd862cfd980c80ad333f26e9ca073f84%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">头皮出油</a></b>也不会导致脱发。反正头发都要掉，清爽一定比油腻好看。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YyYRSNZI"><b>3. </b>洗发水可以买普通的<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657259819%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D92039571838b7e10c92c1fdc58d9f243%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">含硅油</a></b>的，还能让头发更顺滑。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pgiEfQ0e"><b>4.</b> 洗头发的时候，不用疯狂揉搓洗发水，直接<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657264980%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D0c092deacc1bddff03893378a7d16168%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">抹在头皮上</a></b>，不会让你更快秃掉。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YGqZPQZH"><b>5.</b> 谁还没几根白头发，只要不怕秃，<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657266368%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D3dae836c79f1a599b56fd6f99450a41e%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">想拔就拔</a></b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="65DFn3gB"><b>6. </b>想让头发变黑，与其吃<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657258469%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D0d02ce3267491497e4173ada558f386e%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">黑芝麻</a></b>，不如把钱省着去<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657264475%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D125875a9f70d51ee1ead6361bea96c23%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">染发</a></b>。推荐暂时性的染发剂。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="9TwwrRnr"><b>7.</b> 经常理发并不会让<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657264822%26idx%3D4%26sn%3D8add76221626f2618cc79218e812c3c2%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">头发长得更快</a></b>。不信你去问问留长发的人，虽然很费洗发水，但是……真的很省理发钱。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="_mMNO77C"><b>8.</b> 同理，<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657258698%26idx%3D6%26sn%3D27da094fcea1f9ca4f9190a7590c56ab%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">睫毛不会越剪越长</a>，<a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657260212%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D0ad040111e049f823435200d13f64d6a%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">体毛不会越刮越多</a></b>。</p>

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>

 <p data-pid="PzJE_7Iy"><b>47.</b> 虽然没有越吃越瘦的食物，但可以试试<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657264849%26idx%3D5%26sn%3De5f1392b97a9e041653914c76f950c4d%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">换种主食</a></b>。人生嘛，多尝试点不同的东西总是好的。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ihohXnBa"><b>47. </b>减肥<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657266251%26idx%3D5%26sn%3Dbd35da48013809e6aea2c8d1759e15b6%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">也要吃肉</a></b>，但<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657261990%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D476392c056b54a2775e48ae9dd2bf293%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">酒还是别喝</a>了</b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uZ3q6Gmh"><b>48.</b> 除了管住嘴，也要迈开腿，运动了就能消耗脂肪。跟<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657258426%26idx%3D3%26sn%3D02e1f527c4841c125bf39098e7038053%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">运动时长</a></b>无关，跟<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657262261%26idx%3D5%26sn%3Ded16cdb3e361577fab0f0a584484ff30%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">出不出汗</a></b>也无关。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lmbggxJt"><b>49.</b> 运动虽然能消耗脂肪，但要到达一定强度才能减肥。偶尔<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657266229%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D85f1a6d5095df07986ec25be6dbfd519%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">散散步</a></b>挺好，但别指望能减肥。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lxIv3ilJ"><b>50. <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657263490%26idx%3D6%26sn%3Dd273700bafe62c15b40d471c21684e8d%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">高温瑜伽</a></b>就更算了，搞不好会很危险。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="YU3cjcDW"><b>51.</b> 慢跑就是一种很好的减肥方法，不用担心会让<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657259093%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D572c08207b9f76b5ae2cd46557b59caf%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">小腿变粗</a></b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="wT9jxS2L"><b>52. </b>跑完最好别马上坐下，倒不是会让<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657258896%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D2fa2e408f90b14ef07642621211458e2%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">屁股变大</a></b>，而是应该先走走、做做拉伸，以免身体不适。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="V3ME99u9"><b>53.</b> 瘦腿谁都想要，不过<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657258260%26idx%3D4%26sn%3D60caefc92adf3a5a84695355d1a4e88e%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">靠墙竖腿</a></b>是徒劳。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="uIO9fDBT"><b>54.</b> 关于瘦腿，劝你再考虑考虑，因为……<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657264196%26idx%3D6%26sn%3D027850d9085c6079ef5fded6eb07aa7d%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">腿瘦了意味着胸也会瘦</a></b>。</p>

  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-19b6fefdcb7987be3c40a40f9fe5d413_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="640" data-rawheight="360" data-original-token="v2-19b6fefdcb7987be3c40a40f9fe5d413" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-19b6fefdcb7987be3c40a40f9fe5d413_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="640" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-19b6fefdcb7987be3c40a40f9fe5d413_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p data-pid="R5E4Ys6H"><b>55.</b> <b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657262451%26idx%3D6%26sn%3Daa6107a0d19be14969d41b94d99221b9%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">木瓜</a></b>、<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657263092%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D09a55902835df2a2d52fe6e6ac9edb74%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">丰胸霜</a></b>，都不能丰胸。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="Z1PlffFd"><b>56. <a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657261208%26idx%3D4%26sn%3D4da2c06d856e076198baa0f46e687aee%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">经常按摩</a></b>也不能丰胸。没理由相信胸越按越大，脸越按越小呀。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="781rIIhM"><b>57. </b>胸其实挺简单的，你胖它也胖，你瘦它也瘦，所以……<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657261662%26idx%3D6%26sn%3D5e153513a1e5c820a64088eddfcd6b30%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">成年以后胸也能变大</a></b>。毕竟我们从未停止长胖</p>

 <p data-pid="uGy5a6-a"><b>58.</b> 成年以后，除了胸能变大，<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657260723%26idx%3D5%26sn%3Dd9f55e487813059ac16a0f348ce01070%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">身高也还能长</a></b>。</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="TAkitUng"><b>59.</b> 内衣可以对胸部起到一定的支撑作用，但<b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657259059%26idx%3D5%26sn%3D9f8eb121fc052fbb25e408461c051a5b%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">不穿内衣</a></b>不会导致胸下垂。想穿就穿，不想穿就放飞自我，也没啥问题。</p>

 <p data-pid="jY3gU2A4"><b>60.</b> <b><a href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//mp.weixin.qq.com/s%3F__biz%3DMjA1ODMxMDQwMQ%3D%3D%26mid%3D2657260013%26idx%3D5%26sn%3Dff48a178b80fc9a86bdc6ff874b07b05%26scene%3D21%26token%3D1468054823%26lang%3Dzh_CN%23wechat_redirect" class=" wrap external" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer">驼背矫正器</a></b>和内衣的原理其实差不多，都只是借助外力，让你暂时看起来不错。想治驼背，还是佩戴专业的矫形器具吧。</p>

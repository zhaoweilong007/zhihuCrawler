 <p>要知道 VOC 是新装修室内的一大污染<span><span>，</span></span>出现的异味主要就来源于此<span><span>，</span></span>短时间接触会出现头痛<span><span>、</span></span>胸闷<span><span>、</span></span>喉干等不适症状<span><span>，</span></span><strong>长时间接触对人体的神经系统<span><span>、</span></span>呼吸系统都会带来损害<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>因此刷乳胶漆后适当地通风必不可少<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <h2><strong>2.固体含量检测</strong></h2>
 <p>刷完之后墙上到底还能剩下多少固体<span><span>，</span></span>这是很多人都关心的一个问题<span><span>，</span></span>也就是我们常说的性价比<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>测试条件<span><span>：</span></span>23℃ 下放置 24 小时<span><span>，</span></span>然后 105℃ 烘干 3 小时<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f1bd42b519d86cc511170610371b0712_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="720" data-rawheight="568" data-original-token="v2-f1bd42b519d86cc511170610371b0712" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="720" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-f1bd42b519d86cc511170610371b0712_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p>就数 MT 士的固体含量最低<span><span>，</span></span>仅剩 42.7%<span><span>，</span></span>相当于挥发掉了一半多<span><span>，</span></span>最好的也只有 49.9%<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这次运气还算好<span><span>，</span></span>挑选的几款乳胶漆都没出现之前那么<span><span>「</span></span>水<span><span>」</span></span>的情况<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <h2><strong>3.性能检测</strong></h2>
 <p>本回性能检测<span><span>，</span></span>我们将从<strong>气味</strong><span><span>、</span></span><strong>对比率</strong><span><span>、</span></span><strong>漆膜韧性<span><span>、</span></span>附着力<span><span>、</span></span>耐洗刷性<span><span>、</span></span>抗甲醛能力</strong>进行一个详细的测试<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>3.1 气味</strong></p>
 <p><strong><span><span>【</span></span>检测过程<span><span>】</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>直接漆桶开盖<span><span>，</span></span>亲自凑上去闻<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-71f56b60a9201fb9d64379538b42d9ec_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" alt="" data-rawwidth="720" data-rawheight="540" data-original-token="v2-71f56b60a9201fb9d64379538b42d9ec" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="720" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-71f56b60a9201fb9d64379538b42d9ec_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p><strong><span><span>【</span></span>检测结果<span><span>】</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>7 款味道如下<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>1 号<span><span>：</span></span>味道很香<span><span>，</span></span>像小时候糖果味道</p>
 <p>2 号<span><span>：</span></span>味道一般<span><span>，</span></span>无刺鼻感<span><span>，</span></span>不辣眼睛</p>
 <p>3 号<span><span>：</span></span>味道较淡</p>
 <p>4 号<span><span>：</span></span>有明显臭味<span><span>，</span></span>但不刺鼻</p>
 <p>5 号<span><span>：</span></span>味道很淡<span><span>，</span></span>无不适感</p>
 <p>6 号<span><span>：</span></span>味道很刺鼻且辣眼睛<span><span>（</span></span>无法忍受<span><span>）</span></span></p>
 <p>7 号<span><span>：</span></span>味道像传统油漆味且刺鼻<span><span>，</span></span>但不辣眼睛</p>
 <p><strong>3.2 对比</strong>率</p>
 <p>很多人不了解什么是对比率<span><span>，</span></span>其实就是我们常说的遮盖力<span><span>，</span></span>也就是乳胶漆涂膜在黑白底色板上反射率的比值<span><span>，</span></span>对比率数值越高说明遮盖力越强<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong><span><span>【</span></span>检测过程<span><span>】</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>在底色黑白各半的卡片纸上均匀地涂布被测涂料<span><span>，</span></span>至少放置 24h<span><span>，</span></span>在黑白底色涂膜上各至少四个位置测量反射率<span><span>，</span></span>用黑板和白板的平均反射率比值来计算乳胶漆的对比率<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <figure>

 </figure>
 <p><strong><span><span>【</span></span>检测结果<span><span>】</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>对比率达到 1.0 就足以完全遮盖住底色<span><span>，</span></span>显示乳胶漆本身的颜色<span><span>，</span></span>当然这是最最理想的状态<span><span>，</span></span>实际检测结果中 5 号产品的遮盖力是最接近的<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>3.4 附着力</strong></p>
 <p>乳胶漆附着力体现了漆膜与上道涂层通过物理化学力作用结合在一起的牢固程度<span><span>。</span></span>比较常见的情况<span><span>，</span></span>就是平日我们家里墙面漆皮的局部脱离<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>当然<span><span>，</span></span>引起脱落的原因<span><span>，</span></span>除了涂料对上道涂层或底材的附着力外<span><span>，</span></span>还取决于腻子粉<span><span>、</span></span>施工工艺等原因<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong><span><span>【</span></span>检测过程<span><span>】</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>采用划格试验<span><span>，</span></span>六个切割刀的多刃切割工具横竖各切一次<span><span>，</span></span>形成 25 个小方格<span><span>，</span></span>拉出一段透明胶带<span><span>，</span></span>胶带中心点放在网格上方<span><span>，</span></span>方向与切割线平行<span><span>，</span></span>用手指压平确保胶带与涂层完好接触<span><span>，</span></span>平稳地撕离胶带<span><span>，</span></span>观察切割区脱落情况<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p><strong>3.5 耐洗刷性</strong></p>
 <p>乳胶漆涂膜经洗涤液反复洗刷<span><span>，</span></span>擦拭次数越多<span><span>、</span></span>用力越大而不脱色的<span><span>，</span></span>耐洗刷性能越好<span><span>。</span></span>如果乳胶漆涂膜异常脆弱<span><span>，</span></span>经不起擦洗<span><span>，</span></span>很容易漆膜破裂或露出底层<span><span>。</span></span></p>

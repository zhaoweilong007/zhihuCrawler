 </ul>
 <p><strong>一天下来<span><span>，</span></span>明明没做什么<span><span>，</span></span>却累得不行<span><span>，</span></span>还会一个劲数落自己<span><span>：</span></span>是不是要废了<span><span>？</span></span></strong></p>

 <p>先分析你会胡思乱想的本质原因<span><span>，</span></span>再一点一点深入<span><span>，</span></span>如何摆脱胡思乱想<span><span>。</span></span>只有这样<span><span>，</span></span>才能让你从内到外的跟胡思乱想说拜拜 ~</p>
 <h2><strong>一<span><span>、</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>胡思乱想<span><span>」</span></span>的本质原因</strong></h2>
 <p>容易胡思乱想的人<span><span>，</span></span>有两个特点<span><span>，</span></span><strong>一是个人长期形成的敏感性格<span><span>；</span></span>二是不够忙<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>我们来详细了解第一个<span><span>，</span></span>通常这类人有这些特征<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <blockquote>
  敏感<span><span>、</span></span>自卑<span><span>、</span></span>胆小<span><span>、</span></span>易焦虑<span><span>、</span></span>没啥朋友<span><span>、</span></span>讨好型人格<span><span>。</span></span>
 </blockquote>
 <p><strong>黛西之前全中<span><span>，</span></span>一旦情绪泛滥了<span><span>，</span></span>就在内心疯狂加戏<span><span>，</span></span></strong>如洪水崩塌<span><span>，</span></span>止也止不住<span><span>，</span></span>我甚至想过琼瑶阿姨要是能找我拍戏<span><span>，</span></span>我肯定能完美还原矫情人设<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>不过后来才发现<span><span>，</span></span>根本控制不住自己的脑袋<span><span>，</span></span>有以下两方面原因<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>1. 在用错的方式控制思想</strong></p>
 <p><strong>需要强调<span><span>，</span></span>想法只可引导<span><span>，</span></span>不可强压<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>心理学有个<strong><span><span>「</span></span>讽刺进程理论<span><span>」</span></span></strong>表示<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>当我们越是想压抑某个念头的时候<span><span>，</span></span>这个念头越可能冒出来<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>」</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>所以当我们试图遏制想法<span><span>，</span></span>很难成功<span><span>，</span></span>只会更加胡思乱想<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <ul>
  <li>比如学习<span><span>，</span></span>越不想走神<span><span>，</span></span>越会静不下来<span><span>；</span></span></li>
  <li>比如失恋<span><span>，</span></span>越强迫不去想 ta<span><span>，</span></span>越发会想起 ta 的好<span><span>。</span></span></li>
 </ul>
 <p>这导致我们大部分时间<span><span>，</span></span>都花在对抗<span><span>「</span></span>不走神<span><span>」</span></span>上<span><span>，</span></span>对工作生活都造成很大的困扰<span><span>，</span></span>效率低得可怜<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>因此第一步<span><span>，</span></span>不要抗拒胡思乱想的现状<span><span>，</span></span>先接受<span><span>：</span></span>胡思乱想是人正常的情绪<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>2. 正视胡思乱想的根本原因<span><span>，</span></span>学会<span><span>「</span></span>洗脑<span><span>」</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>回归上文所说<span><span>，</span></span>我们喜欢瞎想的原因<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>一是过于敏感<span><span>，</span></span>导致人际关系上的胡思乱想<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <blockquote>
  把所有人的不开心都算在自己的头上<span><span>，</span></span>觉得<span><span>「</span></span>我太差劲<span><span>、</span></span>我不配被爱<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span>
 </blockquote>
 <p><strong>二是太闲了<span><span>，</span></span>闲到玩微博/抖音等软件消遣时间<span><span>，</span></span>毫无收益<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <blockquote>
  看 xxx 结婚了我好酸<span><span>，</span></span>xxx 考上名校我好垃圾<span><span>，</span></span>xxx 好瘦好美我好胖……
 </blockquote>
 <p>省下这些时间<span><span>，</span></span>学习新知识<span><span>、</span></span>新技能<span><span>，</span></span>能大幅度减少你脑子里的胡思乱想<span><span>，</span></span>还会让你迅速提升硬实力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>事实上生活中 90% 的事情都与我们无关<span><span>，</span></span>只不过是内心的过度在意<span><span>，</span></span>给我们带来了很多负担<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>想化解这些烦恼带来的胡思乱想<span><span>，</span></span>就得学会洗脑<span><span>，</span></span>改变我们对别人的态度<span><span>，</span></span>将会一身轻松<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>方法有点简陋<span><span>，</span></span>但十分管用——<strong><span><span>「</span></span>啥事大法<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>也有两种方式<span><span>：</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>· 用<strong><span><span>「</span></span>关我啥事<span><span>」</span></span></strong>解决生活中 80% 的烦恼<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <ul>
  <li>别人结婚了</li>
  <li>考北大</li>
  <li>朋友翻脸了</li>
  <li>你怎么看待 xxx……</li>
 </ul>
 <p>通通和我们无关<span><span>，</span></span>对生活毫无帮助<span><span>，</span></span>那为什么还关注呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>· 剩余的 20% <strong>用 <span><span>「</span></span>关你啥</strong>事<span><span>」</span></span>解决<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <ul>
  <li>怎么还不结婚<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>怎么分手了<span><span>？</span></span></li>
  <li>怎么还不减肥……</li>
 </ul>
 <p>对于看你笑话的人<span><span>，</span></span>大胆说一句<span><span>「</span></span>关你啥事<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>简直不要太爽<span><span>！</span></span>毕竟我们才是生活的主人<span><span>，</span></span>别人没资格指指点点<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>抛开闲言碎语<span><span>，</span></span>解决这个根本原因后<span><span>，</span></span>我们才能集中精力去做感兴趣的<span><span>、</span></span>重要的事情<span><span>，</span></span>突破个人能力和职业天花板<span><span>，</span></span>慢慢变得自信强大起来<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>所以第二步<span><span>：</span></span>改变自己对身边事物的态度<span><span>，</span></span>让注意力集中在重要的事情上<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <h2><strong>二<span><span>、</span></span>远离<span><span>「</span></span>胡思乱想<span><span>」</span></span>实用小技巧</strong></h2>
 <p>上面两个步骤<span><span>，</span></span>可以很好帮助我们转变态度<span><span>，</span></span>接受该有的情绪<span><span>，</span></span>学会肯定自己<span><span>，</span></span>不过这是渐进过程<span><span>，</span></span>需要时间过渡<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>接下来我分享 6 个很有用的行动小技巧<span><span>，</span></span>可以当场见效果<span><span>，</span></span>立马减轻症状<span><span>，</span></span>实操性强行动难度低<span><span>，</span></span>强烈安</strong>利~</p>
 <p><strong>1. 敲下你天马行空的想法</strong></p>
 <p>不要手写<span><span>，</span></span>太慢了<span><span>，</span></span>不要录音<span><span>，</span></span>太长了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>直接打开手机或电脑的备忘录<span><span>，</span></span>把自己想法一五一十敲下来<span><span>，</span></span>怎么写得爽怎么来<span><span>，</span></span>不需要逻辑和文笔<span><span>，</span></span>带粗口都没关系<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>当你坚持写几天后<span><span>，</span></span>发现敲的都是重复内容<span><span>，</span></span>自然没了兴趣<span><span>，</span></span>慢慢不再胡思乱想<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>平常郁闷时记录想法<span><span>，</span></span>也能帮你极快调整<span><span>、</span></span>梳理情绪<span><span>，</span></span>亲测有用<span><span>，</span></span>写完不要删除<span><span>，</span></span>等心情平复后翻回去看<span><span>，</span></span>会觉得自己很傻缺哈哈 ~</p>
 <p><strong>2. 转移注意力<span><span>，</span></span>去运动或倾诉</strong></p>

 <p>在做号的时间里<span><span>，</span></span>写稿/运营/管理公司的压力让我掉了不少头发<span><span>，</span></span>压得我踹不过气<span><span>。</span></span>但每天抽半个小时做 keep<span><span>，</span></span>睡前冥想<span><span>，</span></span>不仅发泄了情绪<span><span>，</span></span>更是锻炼了我的专注力<span><span>，</span></span>从而控制住焦虑<span><span>，</span></span>才能每天元气满满 ~</p>
 <p><strong>3. 找到能让你停止瞎想的幸福活动</strong></p>
 <p>当你控制不住想法时<span><span>，</span></span>证明你正在一个自己不喜欢<span><span>、</span></span>感到压迫的地方<span><span>，</span></span>有两个改变方法<span><span>：</span></span><strong>一改变你正在做的事情<span><span>，</span></span>二改变你所在的环境<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <ul>
  <li>当你学不下去时<span><span>，</span></span>可以听听音乐<span><span>、</span></span>吃爱吃的<span><span>、</span></span>和朋友聊会天……<span><span>（</span></span>事情<span><span>）</span></span></li>
  <li>或者散散步<span><span>、</span></span>晒晒太阳……<span><span>（</span></span>环境<span><span>）</span></span></li>
 </ul>
 <p>比如<span><span>，</span></span>黑色笼罩的夜里<span><span>，</span></span>孤独感来袭<span><span>，</span></span>总容易瞎想<span><span>，</span></span>所以黛西会在睡前看会书<span><span>，</span></span>有静心安神的效果<span><span>，</span></span>文字的魅力会吸引你不断往下读<span><span>，</span></span>沉浸其中<span><span>，</span></span>根本想不了其他<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <h2><strong>一<span><span>、</span></span>看得到的是概率<span><span>，</span></span>看不见的是陷阱</strong></h2>
 <p>先说一个最简单的赌博游戏<span><span>：</span></span>抛硬币<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>规则是这样的<span><span>：</span></span>掷硬币<span><span>，</span></span>正面赢反面输<span><span>，</span></span>如果你赢了可以拿走比赌注多一倍的钱<span><span>，</span></span>如果输了则会赔掉本金<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你一听可能觉得这游戏还不错<span><span>，</span></span>公平<span><span>！</span></span></p>
 <p>于是你拿出了身上的 100 元来玩这个游戏<span><span>，</span></span>每次下注 5 元<span><span>，</span></span>这样你至少有 20 次的下注机会<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>不过<span><span>，</span></span>你运气不太好<span><span>，</span></span>第一把就是反面<span><span>，</span></span>输了 5 块钱<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>生性乐观的你觉得没什么<span><span>，</span></span>反正不管怎么说<span><span>，</span></span>赢面都有 50%<span><span>，</span></span>下一把就可以赢回来<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>结果<span><span>，</span></span>很快你就把身上的钱都输光了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你百思不得其解<span><span>，</span></span>明明是公平的 50% 赢面<span><span>，</span></span>在 50% 概率下至少不会亏本的<span><span>，</span></span>可为什么最后会输光<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>事实上<span><span>，</span></span>你以为自己看到了 50% 的概率<span><span>，</span></span>把游戏看得透彻明白<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>殊不知<span><span>，</span></span>你看到了概率<span><span>，</span></span>却没有看到背后的陷阱<span><span>，</span></span>一脚踏进了一个叫作<span><span>「</span></span>赌徒谬论<span><span>」</span></span>的坑里<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你觉得游戏是公平的<span><span>：</span></span>一正一反<span><span>，</span></span>均为 50% 概率<span><span>，</span></span>按照大数定律来说<span><span>，</span></span>这是必然规律<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>然而<span><span>，</span></span>你有没有想过<span><span>，</span></span>正是这种你以为的<span><span>「</span></span>公平<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>让你误解了大数定律<span><span>，</span></span>才陷入了<span><span>「</span></span>赌徒谬论<span><span>」</span></span>里呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>先来看看这种让你觉得<span><span>「</span></span>公平<span><span>」</span></span>的大数定律究竟是什么<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>它是数学家伯努利提出的<span><span>：</span></span></p>

 <p>也就是说<span><span>，</span></span>大量重复的随机现象里其实藏着某种必然规律<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>还是以掷硬币为例<span><span>，</span></span>当投掷次数足够大时<span><span>，</span></span>出现正<span><span>（</span></span>反<span><span>）</span></span>面的频率将逐渐接近于 1/2<span><span>，</span></span>且随着投掷次数的增加<span><span>，</span></span>偏差会越来越小<span><span>，</span></span>如图 1<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>从表面概率看<span><span>，</span></span>这确实是场公平的游戏<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>但这种公平是有一定条件的<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>大数定律讲究<span><span>「</span></span>大量重复的随机现象<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>只有足够多次试验才能使得硬币正反面出现次数与总次数之比几乎等于 1/2<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>可具体多少次才算<span><span>「</span></span>足够多<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>才能够把它用在个人对赌上<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>没有人知道<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>因为<span><span>，</span></span>概率论给出的答案是——无穷大<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>谁也不知道无穷大有多大<span><span>，</span></span>只知道这是一个令人仰望的数量<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>可投掷硬币次数越小<span><span>，</span></span>大数定律的身影就越模糊<span><span>，</span></span>可能 10 次中 5 正 5 反<span><span>，</span></span>也可能 9 正 1 反<span><span>，</span></span>也可能 10 正 0 反或 0 正 10 反……</p>
 <p>现实往往是<span><span>，</span></span>在远未达到<span><span>「</span></span>足够多<span><span>」</span></span>次试验时<span><span>，</span></span>你就已经输了个精光了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>你身上有 100 元结果如此<span><span>，</span></span>你身上有 10000 元结果也是如此<span><span>，</span></span>就算你身上有 100 万元结果也是如此<span><span>，</span></span>因为你永远不可能有<span><span>「</span></span>足够多<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><span><span>「</span></span>输赢概率为 50%<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>这本身就具有很大的误导性<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>在硬币抛出之前<span><span>，</span></span>50% 的概率代表的是可能性<span><span>；</span></span></strong></p>
 <p><strong>在硬币抛出之后<span><span>，</span></span>50% 代表的是结果的统计平均值<span><span>，</span></span>却并不是实际分布值<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>这是你对大数定律的误解之一<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>把<span><span>「</span></span>大数定律<span><span>」</span></span>当<span><span>「</span></span>小数定律<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>觉得游戏是无条件<span><span>「</span></span>公平<span><span>」</span></span>的<span><span>，</span></span>正面和反面出现的频率都为 1/2<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这种在潜意识里被奉为圭臬的<span><span>「</span></span>公平<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>紧接着让你踏入了第二个误解——<span><span>「</span></span>赌徒谬论<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>大数定律有一个明显的潜台词<span><span>：</span></span>当随机事件发生的次数足够多时<span><span>，</span></span>发生的频率便趋近于预期的概率<span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>如果过去一段时间内发生的事件不均匀<span><span>，</span></span>大家就会<span><span>「</span></span>人工<span><span>」</span></span>地从心理上把未来的事情<span><span>「</span></span>抹平<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>也就是说<span><span>，</span></span>如果输了第一把<span><span>，</span></span>那下一把的赢面就会更大<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>这种你下一把就可以赢回来的强烈错觉<span><span>，</span></span>就是<span><span>「</span></span>赌徒谬论<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>而其实<span><span>，</span></span><strong>上一把和下一把之间并没有任何联系</strong><span><span>。</span></span></p>

 <p>要知道<span><span>，</span></span>大数定律的工作机制<span><span>，</span></span>可不是为了平衡对抗<span><span>。</span></span>在这场游戏中<span><span>，</span></span>任意两次事件之间并不会相互产生影响<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>赌局是没有记忆的<span><span>，</span></span>哪怕你曾经输了多次<span><span>，</span></span>它也不会因此给你更多胜出的机会<span><span>。</span></span></p>

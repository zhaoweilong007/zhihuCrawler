 <p>很多中国家庭的小孩从小就无师自通<span><span>，</span></span>把漫画书夹在课本里<span><span>，</span></span>发呆前先在面前摊一本习题<span><span>，</span></span>因为他们已经养成了一种条件反射——</p>
 <p><strong><span><span>「</span></span>不管学的是什么<span><span>，</span></span>学的有多慢<span><span>，</span></span>只要看着是在学习就 O98K<span><span>。</span></span></strong><span><span>」</span></span></p>

 <h2>01</h2>
 <h2><strong><span><span>「</span></span>行为艺术学习法<span><span>」</span></span></strong></h2>
 <blockquote>
  <strong>行为艺术学习法<span><span>：</span></span></strong>指在特定时间和地点<span><span>，</span></span>由个人或群体<span><span>「</span></span>假装在学习/沉思<span><span>」</span></span>的行为所构成的一种学习流派<span><span>，</span></span>一般归为表演艺术<span><span>。</span></span>
  <br>
   ——摘自<span><span>「</span></span>柴桑瞎编<span><span>」</span></span>百科
 </blockquote>

 <p>经常有人问我<span><span>：</span></span><span><span>「</span></span>柴桑你每天都花多少时间学习?<span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>我都担心回答会误人子弟<span><span>。</span></span><span><span>（</span></span>我不好意思说我每天学习不超过 3 小时哈哈<span><span>）</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>因为<span><span>，</span></span>学习并不是时间管理的问题<span><span>，</span></span>而是个认知效率的问题<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <blockquote>
  认知效率<span><span>：</span></span>认知收益和时间精力之比<span><span>，</span></span>认知效率越高<span><span>，</span></span>学习效果越好<span><span>。</span></span>
 </blockquote>
 <p>古典曾在<span><span>《</span></span>跃迁<span><span>》</span></span>中提到<span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>在知识匮乏<span><span>，</span></span>非终身学习年代<span><span>，</span></span>学肯定比不学好<span><span>。</span></span>但是在今天知识爆炸<span><span>，</span></span>终身学习的时代<span><span>，</span></span><strong><span><span>「</span></span>为什么<span><span>（</span></span>Why<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>、</span></span>学什么<span><span>（</span></span>What<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>、</span></span>怎么学<span><span>（</span></span>How<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>」</span></span><span><span>，</span></span>比<span><span>「</span></span>学就好了<span><span>（</span></span>Do<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>」</span></span>更重要<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>我之前也写过<span><span>，</span></span>在这个浮躁的时代<span><span>，</span></span>比时间更重要的<span><span>，</span></span>是你的注意力<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>拿我室友举个栗子<span><span>（</span></span>室友应该没关注我知乎吧<span><span>）</span></span><span><span>：</span></span></p>
 <p>我大学三个室友都考研<span><span>，</span></span>每天早上六点半准时起床相约图书馆<span><span>，</span></span>同频去食堂<span><span>，</span></span>同频晚上十点回寝室<span><span>，</span></span>但最后只有一个考上了<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p>按理说她们仨的学习时间是完全重合的对吧<span><span>？</span></span>但有一次我去图书馆还书<span><span>，</span></span>看见她们面前都摊了四五本书<span><span>，</span></span>一个苦着脸在做题<span><span>，</span></span>一个一脸姨母笑的在玩手机<span><span>，</span></span>还有一个趴书上睡觉……</p>

 <p>这就是行为艺术学习法的弊端<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>它让人养成了一种思维惯性<span><span>，</span></span>一种<span><span>「</span></span>只要我在学<span><span>，</span></span>哪怕只是看着在学<span><span>，</span></span>就不算是不务正业<span><span>」</span></span>的错误认知<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>看着那些常年霸占图书馆靠窗的 MVP 位<span><span>，</span></span>还只会玩手机的行为艺术学习家<span><span>，</span></span>你是不是也很想替天行道<span><span>？</span></span></p>
 <p>因为啊<span><span>，</span></span><strong>衡量学习效果看的并不是你的时间投入<span><span>，</span></span>而是认知资源投入<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>时间只是自顾自的向前奔走而已<span><span>，</span></span>而能不能将你的注意力投入到书本<span><span>，</span></span>把认知资源集中到对内容的思考中<span><span>，</span></span>才是决定你学习效果的关键<span><span>。</span></span></p>
 <p><strong>如果错把学习行为和学习场景的代入看成了学习本身<span><span>，</span></span>那你的学习只能称为是一种行为艺术<span><span>。</span></span></strong></p>
 <p>行为艺术学习法还会给大脑造成一点副作用<span><span>，</span></span>这个就更有意思<span><span>，</span></span>我们来挨个聊~</p>
 <h2>02</h2>

 <p>什么意思呢<span><span>？</span></span></p>

  它是指<span><span>，</span></span>日常生活中我们往往会高估自己的知识量<span><span>，</span></span>自以为掌握了许多常识<span><span>，</span></span>但实际上<span><span>，</span></span>我们真正知道的<span><span>、</span></span>懂得的<span><span>、</span></span>了解的<span><span>，</span></span>远远低于自己的想象<span><span>。</span></span>

 <h2>03</h2>

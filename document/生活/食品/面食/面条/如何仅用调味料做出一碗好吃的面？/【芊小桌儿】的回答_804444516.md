 <p data-pid="SDpbM378">吃哒！好的，那我们走~</p>

 <h2><b>香辣酸汤面</b></h2>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-6e0c26d8a013fbc62375241808e54669_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="690" data-rawheight="418" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-9ba3e5d135ddff9d14434f2415c42658" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bbdbad81665108dcbf16463714045cf1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="690" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-6e0c26d8a013fbc62375241808e54669_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="5103" data-rawheight="3391" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-32414c4d80c7d05a9a5a847fa082fe49" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-d925d60df38e971a1bf8c419e6758890_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="5103" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BuV7594V"><b>原料（一人份）：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="nbRJBkFp">挂面 100g</li>
  <li data-pid="U7iDieGJ">辣椒粉 1大匙</li>
  <li data-pid="E9ovSob8">蒜 3瓣</li>
  <li data-pid="PASW_fsW">花生碎 1大匙</li>
  <li data-pid="G2P6j2-E">香葱 适量</li>
  <li data-pid="9kh7zjET">生抽 2汤匙</li>
  <li data-pid="VQZSl8CN">香醋 3汤匙</li>
  <li data-pid="Zm8tuU5q">盐 0.5茶匙</li>
  <li data-pid="hCSyLmwl">糖 1茶匙</li>
 </ul>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-b62883dce3f7ff54b5f018b36e095e01_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="613" data-size="normal" data-original-token="v2-3f61d9c01465644b05d2e783ddd267bd" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-32621d6628de5defb24dad62d1f1dc36_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1080" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-b62883dce3f7ff54b5f018b36e095e01_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
  <figcaption>
   1汤匙≈10g 1茶匙≈15g
  </figcaption>
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="BZM9UXu-"><b>开始吧！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="zzX-c4Aa"><b>做法：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="sjAidRA_">1. 蒜末、葱花、辣椒粉和花生碎放入碗中，搅拌均匀，有条件的可以加入熟芝麻，没条件的连花生碎都可以省略，就是这么家常~</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c0a8270ed3856ab0344113319441d50b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="712" data-rawheight="402" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-c0a8270ed3856ab0344113319441d50b" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-712109b6bb42a0630bb11b45299aec34_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="712" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-c0a8270ed3856ab0344113319441d50b_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-J0AnLlR">2. 架锅热适量的油，也就30g左右吧，烧到油热，顺势淋入碗中，刺啦刺啦激发出调味料的香味。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-681fab73cb96146c0422869ec82335c1_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="440" data-rawheight="248" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-681fab73cb96146c0422869ec82335c1" data-thumbnail="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-681fab73cb96146c0422869ec82335c1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="440" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-681fab73cb96146c0422869ec82335c1_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="-Q03mwly">3. 然后倒入生抽、香醋、盐、糖，搅拌均匀，林林总总的加起来也有小半碗了。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5f58caae08c4aaf4be25153150541f42_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="2778" data-rawheight="1852" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-5f58caae08c4aaf4be25153150541f42" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-59f339f057bc47a613d20c88e409b7ac_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2778" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-5f58caae08c4aaf4be25153150541f42_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="osDL6XGn">4. 现在来煮面。</p>
 <p data-pid="7j-2TYur">清水煮沸后，放入挂面，煮熟，捞出放入冷水中过凉，没有错，过凉可以使面条更劲道Q弹，实在嫌麻烦可以省略，但是口感会大打折扣。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-3380dad14839a80c8ce4187a8faf5a10_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="705" data-rawheight="444" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-2fead260fd92798f35c3dd88e990dfcb" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-d013804b2761aacdc24920dbd21faeea_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="705" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-3380dad14839a80c8ce4187a8faf5a10_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="GPGyADTE">5. 将过凉后的面条捞出沥干水分，放入碗中。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c1b59d47e07ae2d0f948559f33dacf55_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="500" data-rawheight="375" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-f7f8820e167a12bcfc4ee3bde72237f4" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-ccd1ba27061faf40d56cb8449b291664_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="500" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-c1b59d47e07ae2d0f948559f33dacf55_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="yvakDGi3">6、刚才的调味汁拌入碗中，撒入葱花，搅拌均匀即可开吃。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-e2c1d966a1636ccfefcf70c18357d9bc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="620" data-rawheight="620" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-eae57c3f3c6a9e4b65b1b13392e3332c" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f3f5e2bc7a4b5933b377658320f9fac2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="620" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-e2c1d966a1636ccfefcf70c18357d9bc_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="sRZNmhKA">这样做出来的酸辣面，是带一点汤汁的，主要味道靠裹在面体上的调味汁完成，如果你喜欢吃宽汤的面，也可以按照比例加大生抽、醋和盐的量，再加入半勺煮面条的汤，也是棒棒哒！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-1851af2b41d04e0c7d0db973c5f2919e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="750" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-7fe4ec4c07adbc9017f608114cb2123f" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-bda4761e1359d7316d7c836318e47e66_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="600" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-1851af2b41d04e0c7d0db973c5f2919e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="niJWEP8s">味道嘛！绝对对得起你的劳动，香辣酸爽，最适合一人食啦！没有辣椒粉的换成小米椒也是新鲜热辣呀~</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="5103" data-rawheight="3391" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-32414c4d80c7d05a9a5a847fa082fe49" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-d925d60df38e971a1bf8c419e6758890_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="5103" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="cr7K_SOb">对啦，这个酸辣汁除了下面条之外，拌米粉、凉粉、凉皮、甚至一些凉菜都可以，感觉虽然是在安利一款家常面的吃法，结果不小心完成了一道万能汁的讲解呢！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-74e95c733347ad09963d587f9daa69f0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="2273" data-rawheight="2327" data-size="normal" data-caption="" data-original-token="v2-74e95c733347ad09963d587f9daa69f0" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-522700713de916d06c5d72ba580ede4d_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="2273" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-74e95c733347ad09963d587f9daa69f0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2vEWALb4">这下，家里什么都没有只有一口挂面的你，也可以美美吃一碗啦！如果再卧个蛋上去，谁敢说你吃的凑合呢？！</p>

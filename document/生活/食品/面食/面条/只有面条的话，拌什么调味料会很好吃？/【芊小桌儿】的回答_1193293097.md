 <h2><b>香辣酸汤面</b></h2>
 <figure data-size="normal">

 </figure>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="5103" data-rawheight="3391" data-original-token="v2-32414c4d80c7d05a9a5a847fa082fe49" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d925d60df38e971a1bf8c419e6758890_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="5103" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="qYSz3qHf"><b>原料（一人份）：</b></p>
 <ul>
  <li data-pid="82TXtnmK">挂面·····100g</li>
  <li data-pid="vl1dhucg">辣椒粉 ···自己喜欢的量</li>
  <li data-pid="5RndWXwK">蒜·······3瓣</li>
  <li data-pid="CxmF-CTN">生抽·····2汤匙（20g）</li>
  <li data-pid="kK8s84qj">香醋·····3汤匙（30g，不爱吃酸的减量）</li>
  <li data-pid="qYg9U7Hy">盐·······0.5茶匙（2-3g）</li>
  <li data-pid="iAZUwXO2">糖·······1茶匙（5g）</li>
  <li data-pid="gmsc3aXJ">花生碎···1大勺（可以没有）</li>
  <li data-pid="i7f2ak7W">香葱·····适量（可以没有）</li>
 </ul>
 <p data-pid="xmHvvkoP">我看总有人在问茶匙汤匙的区别，虽然我写了大家似乎总看不到，干脆上个网图吧，虽然一般也没谁靠着称重做中餐（但是可以买个这个量勺，贼便宜），但还是给大家看看大概有个概念：</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/7d1c767434db38f0499f33fd25f8b2f2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="550" data-rawheight="329" data-original-token="7d1c767434db38f0499f33fd25f8b2f2" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-bc493448f64874234119bd7db992540b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="550" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/7d1c767434db38f0499f33fd25f8b2f2_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="OkleacsW"><b>开始吧！</b></p>
 <p data-pid="9L0KB0Qn"><b>做法：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="COBLGTsV">1. 蒜末、葱花、辣椒粉和花生碎放入碗中，搅拌均匀，有条件的可以加入熟芝麻，没条件的连花生碎都可以省略，就是这么家常~</p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="DRdG7od_">2. 架锅热适量的油，也就30g左右吧，烧到油热，顺势淋入碗中，刺啦刺啦激发出调味料的香味。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-e896f5256b346ab90093d7d30438cab0_720w.gif?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="379" data-rawheight="211" data-original-token="v2-e896f5256b346ab90093d7d30438cab0" data-thumbnail="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-e896f5256b346ab90093d7d30438cab0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="379">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="hRvKLQr_">3. 然后倒入生抽、香醋、盐、糖，搅拌均匀，林林总总的加起来也有小半碗了。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-9dca82fb62e08c8e3e071cfa47d5bdae_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="759" data-rawheight="619" data-original-token="v2-ce63e23355b41f59f3a30ef158b16f3a" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-fbbd095b036354498238f6d80b381da1_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="759" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-9dca82fb62e08c8e3e071cfa47d5bdae_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="o1R3DByS">4. 现在来煮面。</p>
 <p data-pid="gFqLqsk6">清水煮沸后，放入挂面，煮熟，捞出放入冷水中过凉，没有错，过凉可以使面条更劲道Q弹，实在嫌麻烦可以省略，但是口感会大打折扣。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-3380dad14839a80c8ce4187a8faf5a10_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="705" data-rawheight="444" data-original-token="v2-2fead260fd92798f35c3dd88e990dfcb" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d013804b2761aacdc24920dbd21faeea_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="705" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-3380dad14839a80c8ce4187a8faf5a10_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="2M2SrW5G">5. 将过凉后的面条捞出沥干水分，放入碗中。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-c1b59d47e07ae2d0f948559f33dacf55_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="500" data-rawheight="375" data-original-token="v2-f7f8820e167a12bcfc4ee3bde72237f4" data-default-watermark-src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-ccd1ba27061faf40d56cb8449b291664_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="500" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-c1b59d47e07ae2d0f948559f33dacf55_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="iDAIhpJj">6、刚才的调味汁拌入碗中，撒入葱花，搅拌均匀即可开吃。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/v2-e2c1d966a1636ccfefcf70c18357d9bc_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="620" data-rawheight="620" data-original-token="v2-eae57c3f3c6a9e4b65b1b13392e3332c" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f3f5e2bc7a4b5933b377658320f9fac2_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="620" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-e2c1d966a1636ccfefcf70c18357d9bc_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="sdcmvGyW">这样做出来的酸辣面，是带一点汤汁的，主要味道靠裹在面体上的调味汁完成，如果你喜欢吃宽汤的面，也可以按照比例加大生抽、醋和盐的量，再加入半勺煮面条的汤，也是棒棒哒！</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-1851af2b41d04e0c7d0db973c5f2919e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="600" data-rawheight="750" data-original-token="v2-7fe4ec4c07adbc9017f608114cb2123f" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-bda4761e1359d7316d7c836318e47e66_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="600" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-1851af2b41d04e0c7d0db973c5f2919e_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p>
 <p data-pid="lOQFJ0SS">味道嘛！绝对对得起你的劳动，香辣酸爽，最适合一人食啦！没有辣椒粉的换成小米椒也是新鲜热辣呀~</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="5103" data-rawheight="3391" data-original-token="v2-32414c4d80c7d05a9a5a847fa082fe49" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-d925d60df38e971a1bf8c419e6758890_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="5103" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/v2-5eab38347467269a4c53ff425559ecd0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>

 <p data-pid="_9aFy8Jo">对啦，这个酸辣汁除了下面条之外，拌米粉、凉粉、凉皮、甚至一些凉菜都可以，感觉虽然是在安利一款家常面的吃法，结果不小心完成了一道万能汁的讲解呢！</p>

 <p data-pid="yp_gqsYp">面条做法千千万，唯有拌面入我心！</p>
 <p data-pid="RbfC-ger">特别是这道<b>酱烧鸡蛋拌面</b>，做法简单，美味绝妙!!!</p>
 <p data-pid="s4E2cS7J">鸡蛋和面条都是太日常的食材，组合起来竟然这么好吃，面条根根都裹着浓郁酱汁，咸香可口，每一口裹着鸡蛋和青椒入口，口感和味道都绝妙!</p>

 <h2>用料</h2>
 <p data-pid="jWrkxJRd">鲜面条200g、黄豆酱30g、甜面酱15g、水（酱）20g、油15g、鸡蛋2个、水（鸡蛋）15g、盐（鸡蛋）0.5g、葱白10g、线椒2个、水80g</p>
 <h2>酱烧鸡蛋拌面的做法</h2>
 <p data-pid="rE_qotiM">1、将黄豆酱、甜面酱、水混匀备用。<br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-367be63206a0f6b4bffa214ed3aaadf8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="300" data-original-token="v2-af9516af5e570e760f62bc33f65496e0" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-91dc290bb45ad95e6a9c7af0ce948891_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="L4Q-qCgW">2、鸡蛋加水、盐打散，线椒切圈。<br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-0eae7efc38b6045333fd9638abf0ccf5_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="300" data-original-token="v2-658fdef29ad81701d188ef51462e918b" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-81ef988f04459f8c81e104cb786fd82a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="5N8ys4wx">3、面条煮7分熟，过凉水。<br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-991308febc5a4fd1bb003b78437c33de_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="300" data-original-token="v2-d98e929fcb8944a19c4d4adca9bb9f7d" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-be3ada80914568a9d0fe1507b523fe82_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="-iIxhAqC">4、锅中倒油大火烧热，倒入蛋液，划散成大块。<br>
  再放葱白、辣椒，转中火炒香。<br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-92771b806766e2c61ca8e4d568fae1f7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="300" data-original-token="v2-42fe0cff746b3fd7c29f0c08cf942dfe" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/v2-6aa8782384fa8d7e7686aa49aebd480a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="uVQZ-fOd">5、倒入酱料炒匀，加水煮一会。<br></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-5f2cf6ad4b8cfbce32e03ca5700592e4_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-caption="" data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="300" data-rawheight="300" data-original-token="v2-7c5715fdb2fd924f2a438934efda405f" data-default-watermark-src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/v2-f6e3538f88a472b82433d18358b7eb17_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" class="content_image" width="300">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="w2d496le">6、放入面条拌匀。<br></p>

 <br>
 <p data-pid="z9UGvGtd"><b>翡翠白菜饺子。</b></p>
 <p data-pid="hQUOlQ9J"><b>除夕时候做的，屈膝礼。</b></p>
 <br>
 <br>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="IMOQK8ea"><b>材料：</b></p>
 <p data-pid="HJuWGTMC">饺子馅儿：</p>
 <p data-pid="kLylKsdk">韭菜、鸡蛋、虾皮、盐糖味精、香油、 橄榄油。（可以换成其他的）</p>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="asUSZwCU">饺子皮：</p>
 <p data-pid="c_gz2_Ae">两份等量面粉、一小把菠菜。</p>
 <p data-pid="1rxKbRNh"><b>步骤</b></p>
 <p data-pid="f3TrTC8S">1.先做饺子馅儿，韭菜切碎，加炒熟的鸡蛋，把所有材料加到一起，拌匀</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="936" data-rawwidth="1248" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/49fde30e2256d35c670793fc7ba8ecaa_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="49fde30e2256d35c670793fc7ba8ecaa" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1248" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/49fde30e2256d35c670793fc7ba8ecaa_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="T8Q296Rv">2.把菠菜烫熟，加少许水放进料理机搅碎，过滤出汁。</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="936" data-rawwidth="1248" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/619180e5dc4bc205a89b8f4714b2c79c_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="619180e5dc4bc205a89b8f4714b2c79c" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1248" data-original="https://pica.zhimg.com/619180e5dc4bc205a89b8f4714b2c79c_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="5hESk7yj">3.一份面粉加菠菜汁和成绿色的面，一份面粉加清水和成白色的面。</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="936" data-rawwidth="1248" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/9ba005bd0e102af913c1058706460fdf_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="9ba005bd0e102af913c1058706460fdf" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1248" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/9ba005bd0e102af913c1058706460fdf_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="vYp9nJkP">4.揉成两个长条</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="601" data-rawwidth="800" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/51cc8f43ff15693aeb71247c04f21ef0_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="51cc8f43ff15693aeb71247c04f21ef0" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="800" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/51cc8f43ff15693aeb71247c04f21ef0_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="XFHwoKIV">5.把绿色的擀平，包住白色的，封口要对齐。</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="620" data-rawwidth="847" src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/21c7ab33ea1cf459b1b8663ccd97398f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="21c7ab33ea1cf459b1b8663ccd97398f" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="847" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/21c7ab33ea1cf459b1b8663ccd97398f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">
 </figure>
 <br>
 <p data-pid="OMy__jA2">6.切成剂子。</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="640" data-rawwidth="852" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/c3b2f2e0f23bddf4545a6ea0c73a45cb_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="c3b2f2e0f23bddf4545a6ea0c73a45cb" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="852" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/c3b2f2e0f23bddf4545a6ea0c73a45cb_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <br>
 <p data-pid="QawUP-26">7.擀成皮。</p>
 <figure>
  <img data-rawheight="936" data-rawwidth="1248" src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/bcad0bda09d7675ac7cce66a4bc9043f_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-original-token="bcad0bda09d7675ac7cce66a4bc9043f" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" width="1248" data-original="https://picx.zhimg.com/bcad0bda09d7675ac7cce66a4bc9043f_r.jpg?source=1940ef5c">

 <p data-pid="Vlwly30F"><br>
  之前听人说网上有教《如何把方便面吃出法式大餐的感觉》，于是好奇的搜了搜，没搜到。<br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="pExQptHc">于是，那就自己动手吧。</p>
 <p data-pid="cTTnz5lj">绝对正宗红烧牛肉方便面制作，看，还有那么大块牛肉呢。完全尊重广告画面。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/fd88346b5b4a77c1aa4493e70539dc84_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="fd88346b5b4a77c1aa4493e70539dc84" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="5D5SB6vG">步骤是这样的：</p>
 <p data-pid="xWSAf4iA"><b>1、首先，准备一碗泡面</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/21c505ed08ce5e9ef01a9eb71b86465e_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="21c505ed08ce5e9ef01a9eb71b86465e" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p><br><br></p>
 <p data-pid="ylG7T8Ox"><b>2、准备餐具</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/ac49cdbbf2092ca5a9d8089ae4cd2855_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="ac49cdbbf2092ca5a9d8089ae4cd2855" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="cIpK6WP5"><b>3、准备花</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/314ac1969c22e7746b4d37af4fe4cda8_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="350" data-size="normal" data-original-token="314ac1969c22e7746b4d37af4fe4cda8" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="t-DQxTK4"><b>4、面泡上</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/1573f68aa0de08accfc51aa2d6691f30_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="1573f68aa0de08accfc51aa2d6691f30" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="5TPg6tuW"><b>5、葱，撕成丝，搓成球</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/e8209ea3a7dc67fad107e815a11a000a_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="e8209ea3a7dc67fad107e815a11a000a" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="UydDZjHc"><b>6、面弄出来，装盘。</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/50/e05d47896e9e0ef54c4c5d6cdf9b6f70_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="e05d47896e9e0ef54c4c5d6cdf9b6f70" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="PLQYIh_D">这里有个要点，需要模具。拿个小杯子就好，把方便面满满的塞进去，压实，然后扣在盘子上。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/4c81044ebe3316c6cc69b0e8c39ca1b7_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="4c81044ebe3316c6cc69b0e8c39ca1b7" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="5DxZv5b6">博主这里有个超级神器。本来也是想找个杯子，不知怎么的翻出来个专业干这种事的模具。我爸说是他86年在香港买的，实在太神奇了。</p>
 <p data-pid="IZUOQMnW">非常有艺术气质。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://pica.zhimg.com/50/9f6f07c121b160409ce0999c7caecd44_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="9f6f07c121b160409ce0999c7caecd44" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="ciaH38dz">看，很神气吧（这是我爸爸的手）</p>
 <p data-pid="ateDRB4y"><b>7、加料，放上中午打包的卤肥肠</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/cdd0a8cdb65dd6dd096cadaca0691ae9_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="cdd0a8cdb65dd6dd096cadaca0691ae9" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="gJ8UGw_f">这肥肠超级好吃。推荐下，成都市温江郊区的天佑祥卤菜。暴雨天，生意还爆棚，可歌可泣。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/c222a406aded31e2e0850cf062cb3149_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="c222a406aded31e2e0850cf062cb3149" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="CMGrXr_s">放上葱，当然，还有红烧牛肉面的料。</p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/fd88346b5b4a77c1aa4493e70539dc84_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="fd88346b5b4a77c1aa4493e70539dc84" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="4aAM5bAu"><b>8、关键的来了，点缀。</b></p>
 <figure data-size="normal">
  <img src="https://picx.zhimg.com/50/ed24b56a0a2ce3eea30aed5b708ae70b_720w.jpg?source=1940ef5c" data-rawwidth="350" data-rawheight="263" data-size="normal" data-original-token="ed24b56a0a2ce3eea30aed5b708ae70b" class="content_image" width="350">
 </figure>
 <p data-pid="xcMfkhCE">本来想用方便面汤的，后来我爸灵机一动换用了我大四川著名的熟油海椒。家家户户调料品标配。</p>

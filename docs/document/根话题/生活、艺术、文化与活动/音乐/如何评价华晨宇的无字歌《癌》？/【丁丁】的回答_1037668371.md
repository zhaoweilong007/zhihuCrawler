 <p data-pid="D0sqAvZC">有一说一，他评价的很中肯</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1222900486866800640" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-0c018ef8856894cd5729f85dfa66a083.jpeg" data-lens-id="1222900486866800640"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-0c018ef8856894cd5729f85dfa66a083.jpeg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1222900486866800640</span></span></a>
 <p data-pid="BI1PIy_q">音乐就是听着让人舒服东西，美的东西，悲伤的歌也很美，但这个《癌》无美感，无艺术性，无存在价值。</p>
 <p data-pid="8e9uamKI">_______________________</p>
 <p data-pid="VwEWaOA8">我是先看到的这个视频，后听的《癌》，</p>
 <p data-pid="tGfhwdwM">开始我也以为他在骂人蹭热度，一种令人讨厌的快手习气，后又听的《癌》，打开播放一脸懵b！怀疑这是不是华晨宇啊！因为他我挺喜欢的，烟火里的尘埃，多棒啊！我跟着k歌，练了好几回就是唱不好，真挺喜欢，但真就是华晨宇，</p>
 <p data-pid="8VVMryNH">回头一看，真真这个评价真真中肯贴切。</p><a class="video-box" href="https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//www.zhihu.com/video/1223304750172639232" target="_blank" data-video-id="" data-video-playable="" data-name="" data-poster="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-ef0edb7973d1527981706a65b06e464e.jpeg" data-lens-id="1223304750172639232"><img class="thumbnail" src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-ef0edb7973d1527981706a65b06e464e.jpeg"><span class="content"><span class="title"><span class="z-ico-extern-gray"></span><span class="z-ico-extern-blue"></span></span><span class="url"><span class="z-ico-video"></span>https://www.zhihu.com/video/1223304750172639232</span></span></a>
